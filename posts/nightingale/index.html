Game Description#

Nightingale takes you on a dangerous journey through the fantastical kingdoms of the fairies. As a Realmwalker, you’ll explore, craft, build, and fight across a variety of environments, alone or with friends. This open world survival game takes you through a labyrinth of beautiful but treacherous kingdoms. Stranded after the destruction of a network of portals, you must reach Nightingale, the last human bastion. Open portals and travel through wild forests, treacherous swamps and shimmering deserts. Gather resources, cook food and build shelters to survive in harsh conditions.

Craft tools and find patterns to create the necessary equipment and weapons. Give your equipment magical properties using special ingredients and arcane technologies. Design and build an impressive estate in a variety of styles. Upgrade buildings, create communities, and even hire NPCs to help manage your homestead. Travel freely across vast kingdoms, encountering stunning visuals and remnants of past expeditions. Explore mysterious faerie architecture, explore shadowy caves and explore abandoned ruins. Beauty and danger lurk behind every portal.

Combine strengths, skills and resources to survive and thrive. Build communal estates, uncover the secrets of the kingdom, and overcome challenges together. Craft melee weapons and firearms to face fearsome creatures, including the Bound, twisted fey creatures that mimic humans. Gather your strength to defeat the colossal primates that inhabit the deepest worlds. Determine your next destination by collecting kingdom maps. Each realm offers unique dangers, discoveries, and surprises, and you influence its design, including hostility, abundance of resources, and even the environment itself.
