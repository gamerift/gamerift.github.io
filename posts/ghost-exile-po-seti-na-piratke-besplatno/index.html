Game Description#

Ghost Exile is a horror game with survival elements that can be played over the Internet online by up to four people. For free on the pirate Ghost Exile, you can join a team of professional exorcists to perform a secret ritual and expel the ghost from the building. You can play online with a friend or connect with random people from all over the world. It is very important to interact with your partners and act as coordinated as possible. Any mistake can cost the lives of team members.

Online play and a single player campaign with bot partners are available. When performing a particular task, you will first need to determine the type of ghost the heroes are dealing with. Each type of cast has its own unique characteristics. In total you will face 20 different enemies. Once the type of enemy is determined, you will need to perform a special ritual that can expel evil spirits into the other world.

Each group member has an individual role within the team. One participant searches for special items, the second deals with equipment, the third is assigned the role of observer, and the fourth protects everyone from enemies. Coordinated work guarantees success in completing the task. In the locations you can find about 10 different clues that tell you what kind of ghost the players are dealing with.

By successfully completing assignments, experience points are awarded. They can be spent on new equipment and abilities. A special feature of the gameplay is a microphone through which you can communicate with the ghost. Exorcists use EMF sensors, a voice recorder, monitor the enemy’s movement, use a laser projector, etc. There are secondary tasks available that allow you to gain more experience and special rewards.
