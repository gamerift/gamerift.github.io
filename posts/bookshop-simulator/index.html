Game Description#

In Bookshop Simulator, the game process is to order goods, their arrangement on shelves and maintenance at the cash desk for revenue. Earned funds are directed to decorating the premises, its physical expansion and subsequent hiring of personnel. Employees can be entrusted with the duties of cashiers or merchandisers, freeing time for the management of other aspects of their business. The player’s main task is to turn a small shop into a large trading network. In addition, in the institution you can have a cat that makes the company visitors.

The accumulated profit in Bookshop Simulator allows you to customize the interior of the outlet, offering the choice of various walls, floors and decorative elements like carpets and plants. The space is equipped to create thematic departments for specific genres or cozy reading zones for cartridges. The company is allowed to expand for new locations around the world, including areas created in the image of France and Japan. The revenue from already working points goes to the opening of fresh branches, and between them you can move to optimize control.

This game allows you to purchase boxes with used publications, among which there are rare, mythical specimens. Such volumes endow the gamer with abilities that increase income and expand operational capabilities. The cat living in the shop requires care, including the purchase of food, bed and other items to maintain its activity. The range is not limited to literature and is able to include comics, table entertainment and other products. A similar variety of goods helps to attract a wider circle of customers to different branches of the network.
