Game Description#

The pirate version ofFirst Strike: Classic allows you to play online for free with a friend, controlling one of 12 nuclear superpowers. On the Internet online you can unite in alliances or play in deathmatch mode. Online play allows you to play as the United States or North Korea. Each country has its own unique capabilities, units, weapons and much more. The task is simple - to survive in a nuclear war. Before going into confrontation, you need to find a way to protect your people from disaster.

You need to expand your power, develop bombs, dismantle old developments and update your arsenal. Scientific research helps obtain blueprints for new nuclear technologies, improve intelligence and defense systems. Free online play in First Strike: Classic allows you to trade or sign alliances. Russia, China, the States or North Korea have both their advantages and disadvantages.

You can develop 12 unique missiles, including the Trident or the Tsar. Ballistic projectiles can be developed. A 3D globe is available, which is a global map. Alliances are concluded through Non-Aggression Treaties. There is a system for changing weather conditions, the weather changes and many other factors affecting the gameplay. Multiplayer sessions last for half an hour or up to an hour in real time.
