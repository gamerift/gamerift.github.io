Game Description#

Come in the role of a harsh mercenary in a harsh, ruined war, kingdom. Take part in step -by -step battles and examine the RPG elements of Stoneshard, available in the gameplay. You can perform contracts, secondary and plot missions. Heal the wounds, purchase equipment and improve the protagonist as soon as you wish.

Freely move around the big and seamless world. Explore the villages lying in the ruins, go down to the dungeons or look into abandoned tracts. Find your adventures in Aldore and learn more about the past of this kingdom. As for any mercenary, you will need to earn gold. Sign the contract to fight the enemy and get a reward. Perform quests or bargaining with merchants found around the world. Sell unnecessary items to them, or buy the best equipment. Find the ingredients and materials to use knowledge in alchemy and cooking.

The main character can pump more than 100 abilities. Experiment with more than 200 subjects with your attributes and characteristics. Create a unique fighter who is able to fight with the strongest warriors of the kingdom. In the battles, you need to think in advance and try to prevent mistakes in your and so limited moves.

Heal the wounds with alcohol or drugs. Create special decoctions, potions, or squeeze the wound with something hot. The character is subjected to moral attacks, loss of reason or vice versa, adrenaline tributary, which will help to defeat opponents in battle. Create your own caravan and return to it to patch your wounds and sit around the fire. Worship the deity, having received abilities, but having given the price for them.
