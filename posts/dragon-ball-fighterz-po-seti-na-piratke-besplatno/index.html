Game Description#

The fighting gameDRAGON BALL FighterZ allows you to play online, competing with a friend, or gives you the opportunity to challenge players from all over the world to fight online online. All fighters and game modes are available on Pirate, including the story campaign and multiplayer modes. Some characters are unlocked immediately and for free, while others must be unlocked as you progress. Choosing a team of three unique fighters, you will have to crush all the enemies in your path.

The gameplay of DRAGON BALL FighterZ is inspired by several other games in the genre. Tag mechanics are borrowed from the Marvel vs. series. Capcom. The online game is also built on MvC principles. By assembling a team of three fighters, players control only one unit at a time, while the rest act as support. You can change the hero at any time.

The fighters fight using several regular attacks and super attacks. In order to carry out an enhanced attack, you need to spend Ki points. The Vanishing Attack is available to all fighters. It allows you to teleport and stab in the back. However, this ability requires energy. Characters can also use Dragon Rush, Super Dash, Ki Charge and many other abilities.

Feature Come on Shenron! allows you to collect dragon balls during battle to gain experience points. Random Dragon Balls are awarded for performing the simplest combo. With Shenron, you can revive an ally, restore health, or perform an enhanced attack. The roster includes 20 characters, such as Gohan, Goku, Nappa, Kid Buu, Android 16, Bardock, Kefla and others. Some need to be unlocked during the storyline. The ability to play online for free speeds up the discovery of new heroes, as more victory points are awarded for participating in online tournaments.
