Game Description#

On the pirateSatellite Reign you can play online for free with a friend or team up online with up to 4 people to go to the city of the future and prevent an evil mega-corporation from seizing power over the country. Multiplayer allows you to create your own group of four well-trained agents, where each team member performs his own individual function in the squad. Together with your partners, you will have to penetrate buildings, kill enemies, commit sabotage and bring down the largest monopoly in history.

A huge open world in the form of a neon metropolis of a gloomy cyberpunk future is available for exploration. The city consists of several districts. In each area you will have to fight unique enemies and complete a number of special tasks. The possibility of free online play in Satellite Reign allows you to individually customize your character: select skills, hyperprosthetics, choose a genetic code and much more. A wide selection of weapons is available, ranging from various melee weapons to futuristic guns.

Each task can be completed in several ways. They depend on the teamâ€™s teamwork and the chosen passing style. The gameplay provides a whole platform for experimenting with tactics and action strategies. For example, you can enter a building through ventilation shafts or turn off all the generators so that the doors open. With the right skills, you can capture the minds of the guards or simply act ahead.
