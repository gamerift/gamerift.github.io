Game Description#

Project Arrhythmia is a rhythm game in which you can play online for free as a pirate with a friend. The adventure is presented with incredible dynamics, momentum and bellet hell. Up to four people can team up online via the Internet. The online game Project Arrhythmia features local co-op, allowing you to go through the story campaign together. The gameplay is distinguished by its high dynamics of action. Reaction and focus are important.

Actions take place in the future. The year is 205X. People are suffering from a new disease called Tokyo Flu. Programmer Anna Schroeder is trying to find a cure using the supercomputer HAL. She manages to discover that treatment can be carried out using pulse and high rhythm. The possibility of free online play allows you to compete in musical duels. You can perform in teams.

A wide variety of bright and loud stages are presented. You can challenge up to four people at the same time. You can fight in a mode where it’s every man for himself, or you can join forces. Story campaign available. The adventure consists of 20 levels. Additional modes are presented by Party - an endless adventure, Snake - collecting balls and Battle Royale - a battle to the last survivor. There is a level editor that allows you to create your own unique challenge using a huge toolkit, including adding music.
