Game Description#

Sword Art Online FRACTURED DAYDREAM gives gamers an unexpected call. This new part of the popular franchise is not a standard VR adventure. Here, familiar landscapes are spinning and turning, temporary lines collide, and past glory is mixed with new problems. Fasten the belts because the system failure in the main game program, Galaxia, throws characters from different stories together. In this project there is no mentality of a lone wolf.

This is a team game in which up to 20 players form strategic detachments. Forget about loneliness - here is power in quantity. The raids become the main theme where users together destroy powerful enemies that would crush any single adventurer. Each player has his own role, his own set of skills, which contributes to the general success of the team. Imagine that this is a symphony of avatars, each part of which plays its melody, creating a beautiful - and, we hope, victorious - harmony.

So, Sword Art Online Fractured Daydream is not just the passage of levels and the grabbing of the Luta. She is about the connections created in battle, about joint triumphs in overcoming, seemingly, irresistible difficulties. This is a team of teamwork, a reminder that in the huge online worlds that we explore, sometimes the greatest victories come in a battle with the comrades that you just met.
