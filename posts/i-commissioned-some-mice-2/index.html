Game Description#

I commissioned some Mice 2 offers the search for numerous mice and cheese on 15 fantasy canvases. Gamers scrupulously examine detailed, hand -drawn worlds to find in the amount of 1,500 disguised objects. The main gameplay is to find and click on targets, forcing them to disappear from the image. Each work of art is a separate puzzle that sets a person to find all elements within the entire collection.

Navigation within each scene in I Commissioned Some Mice 2 is carried out by a mouse for movement and zoom. The control from the keyboard by means of WASD and the shooter also provides an alternative way to study stylized images. If the searches come to a dead end, an unlimited tips are available, directing to not yet found items. This game opens all 15 levels from the very beginning, allowing you to choose a starting point and pass them without any restrictions.

The progress of the player is automatically monitored and preserved in one of the three available slots for future sessions. For those who want to start hunting, the function of complete discharge of all found objects is reduced. Each canvas of this project is accompanied by unique music and embosses, creating a special atmosphere for each search. There is also a timer fixing the time spent on the completion of a separate work of art.
