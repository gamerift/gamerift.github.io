Game Description#

Yet Another Zombie Survivors is an action roginal for those who are ready to fight with the horde of undead! Humanity can be on the verge of extinction, but this does not mean that you will fall without a fight. This is not a typical game with long scenes and complex management - here you need to immediately join the battle and see how sparks fly! Regardless of what your level of game experience you have, you will enjoy this game. So sit down, relax and plunge into an exciting, similar to playing the survival of the gameplay. Gather your team and choose improvements to create the most effective synergy against the inexorable horde of zombies. Your detachment will develop on the basis of your individual decisions and preferences, so whether you want to focus on auxiliary skills or specialize in your favorite character - the choice is yours.

Do you lead a lonely special forces fighter, next to whom there will be only a faithful guard dog, or a whole team of mercenaries armed with flamethrower, katana and rocket installation? Every time you play, it all depends on you! Create your detachment to your liking with constant improvements and watch how it turns into a perfect murder machine. Want to change the situation? No problem - just reboot and try something new. Increase the strength of the virus to affirm your superiority over the enemy and make the Horde stronger and faster.
