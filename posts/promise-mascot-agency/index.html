Game Description#

Promise Mascot Agency offers the gamer to plunge into the life of Kaso-Machi, a damned Japanese town. The main character, Michi, is in exile and must establish the work of the agency of urban masks. Key tasks include hiring these creatures, resolving the problems that arise due to their actions, and clarifying the causes of the protagonist link. Communication with a kick, a living finger with a complex character, is also part of the gameplay while you are trying to make your agency the best in the region through the study of the world and plot events.

Mascot in Promise Mascot Agency is living creatures with your dreams, fears and periodic crises. It is necessary to deal with characters such as Tororo, a cat with a pronounced interest in adult videos, or to-fu, who constantly cries. For success, it is necessary to make friends with these figures, direct them and help perform tasks, trying to avoid catastrophes. It is not easy to send wards to work: they can get stuck in the doorway or arrange a fire in the kitchen during advertising shares.

When the wards go awry, the cards of the heroes received for friendship with the inhabitants of Kaso-Machi come to the rescue. Among the allies can be the captain of the sign, a local superhero in the style of road signs, or mother-san, the mistress of the bar with a remarkable collection of latex. These personalities help out in critical situations. It will be moved to the town of Michie on its cow track; Initially, this is an old car, but it can be improved by adding nitro, wings-planners, and even a mechanism for launching a Pinki assistant.
