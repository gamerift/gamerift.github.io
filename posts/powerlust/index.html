Game Description#

Powerlust throws you into the open world, where you yourself form your fate. Create a unique character not connected by strict class restrictions. Become a magician of fire, owning a colossal sword, or an archer-nonsense, shedding death from afar. A huge selection of spells, weapons, abilities and skills will allow you to create your own game style and experiment with countless builds. But raw power is not all. A fight based on skills requires an exact choice of time and awareness. Determine from enemy attacks, strike with mortal accuracy and master the intricacies of various camera angles - isometric, from top to bottom, from the third and even from the first person. Explore the bright world abounding in tasks - from the search for lost treasures to helping bizarre NPC in their strange endeavors.

Thirst for hardcore sensations? Activate a permadeite and feel what the real consequences are. But do not be afraid, casual players will also be able to enjoy the trip. Reigracy reigns as a procedurally generated dungeons, so each adventure will be new. Outside the battle, a bright world awaits you. Travel noisy cities, help bizarre NPC and open hidden treasures. Strength depends only on you, whether it be the fiery sword of the-usclester or the rain of the arrow of the archer-nocromancer.

This solo project, the passion of its creator, offers a unique experience. Plunger in the procedurally generated dungeons, each of which is a new challenge. Accept the optional permadeath to get the truly hardcore experience of Roguelike. Communicate with the community supporting you in Discord, share your reviews and help form the future of the game. In Powerlust, you will find an open world that needs to be conquered, a huge arsenal that needs to be mastered, and freedom to create your own legend. Do you answer the call to the authorities?
