Game Description#

InGhost of Tsushima DIRECTOR’S CUT the twilight of the 13th century begins. The Mongol Empire massacred many peoples as part of a campaign whose goal was to conquer the East. Between mainland Japan and the mighty Mongol fleet, commanded by the legendary General Khotun Khan, lies only the island of Tsushima. And he is conquered, small villages are destroyed. Samurai Jin Sakai remained the last of the great clan. He intends to do everything to stop the enemy and protect the territory. It’s time to put aside all the principles as a warrior, and pave a new path that can help liberate the island.

This open-world adventure game allows you to travel through vast areas, meet various characters, discover ancient monuments, and capture the hidden beauty of Tsushima. The player will enjoy carefully designed regions, from overgrown fields and peaceful temples to ancient forests, villages and harsh mountain landscapes. In quiet moments of harmony with nature, you will be able to find peace, and in moments when you manage to help people living on the island, you will find relief.

Jin was raised and trained as a samurai. After the clan’s defeat at the hands of the Mongols, its world was destroyed. He faces the most difficult decision in his life: respect traditions and customs and continue the fight, but without a chance, or stray from the path of the samurai in order to protect the island and its inhabitants by all means necessary. To return Tsushima, Jin will turn to both old acquaintances and amazing new allies for help and support. The enemies from Mongolia are unpredictable and their martial arts and weapons are amazing. Gain an advantage by adjusting Jin’s skills and learning different tactics.
