Game Description#

Rugby League Team Manager 4 puts you at the head of the regional club. The circle of your tasks is covered by financial management, negotiations with sponsors and the selection of the state of assistants, mentors and medical staff. You have to improve infrastructure, including the stadium, gym and training fields. This project covers 226 teams from the Northern and Southern Hems, and over 7,000 adult players are available for choice.

In the Rugby League Team Manager 4, the formation of the composition occurs through viewing youth groups and interaction with agents to sign new athletes. The transfer mechanism allows the athletes to make proposals in the last year of their contract, and successful transactions are closed at the end of the current season. The administrative interface is equipped with a dragging function for the selection of the composition, which displays the characteristics of the rugbites with the possibility of filtering. This title also allows you to delegate the responsibilities of mentors for specific positions within the framework of an updated training process.

During the competitions, the gamer interacts with his squad in real time through a two -dimensional or three -dimensional engine. The option of a tactical reference book makes it possible to organize prepared combinations and assign them for specific sections of the field. The tactical arsenal includes the choice of short, medium or long initial blows and the conduct of the conversation with the team during the break. For deeper tuning, intra -game editors allow you to change the parameters of athletes and coaches, and the database editor in this game supports the creation of new competitions, managing the calendar using algorithms or importing files and the use of personal photos of rugbites and emblem clubs.
