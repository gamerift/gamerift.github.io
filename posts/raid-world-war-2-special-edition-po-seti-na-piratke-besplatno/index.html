Game Description#

RAID: World War 2 - Special Edition is a cooperative shooter that allows you to play online with a friend, fighting with each other or with other players online online. The pirate store includes all the additional content for the special edition for free, including 100 gold bars, a hero jacket, melee weapons, avatars and much more. Events take place during the Second War. The story of RAID: World War 2 - Special Edition centers on four prisoners of war: Sterling, Rivet, Kurgan and Wolfgang. The possibility of free online play allows you to fight for any of the presented heroes. Depending on the choice of fighter, the gameplay changes, since each soldier performs an individual role for the team.

Each character has its own unique abilities and characteristics. Along with Mrs. J. White, John Colins acts as support. Together they give orders and send them to important operations. The goal of the tasks is simple and straightforward - to destroy all enemies and return peace to your country. The fighting will take place in Flakturm, in the center of Berlin, allowing players to go through the Elbe River and many other historical events. Mechanics and features of the project: single-player campaign and multiplayer; 4 classes of heroes: scout, stormtrooper, rebel and demolition; protagonists with different tactics, weapons, abilities and battle cry; ability to activate Var Edge - berserker mode that does not require reloading weapons; individual character customization to suit personal preferences; battles in the UK, USA, Germany and Soviet lands; numerous modifications, improvements, leveling up abilities, etc.
