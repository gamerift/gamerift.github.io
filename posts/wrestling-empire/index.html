Game Description#

In Wrestling Empire the player becomes a participant in the World Lightweight Championship. It is necessary to manage an athlete in the ring, apply his abilities and win rivals. The battles are presented in real time with extensive visual effects. This part includes a smooth frequency of personnel and modern graphics, while leaving the atmosphere of retro-style.

This version has more functions and capabilities in pumping and battles. The user will face unique opponents with the original characteristic and special skills. Wrestling fights will occur on large arenas with spectators and in unexpected adrenaline places. The project is focused on conducting multiplayer matches. The gameplay includes over 50 characters, hundreds of objects to change the appearance of the ward and methods to improve the fighter.

News reports appear weekly, talking about rising stars and interesting cases in the world of sports. The career begins with how the user takes control over a newcomer in big sports. Before passing, you will need to create a hero by choosing his mask, physique, face, floor, skin color and secondary clothes. Gradually, the athlete improves such abilities: strength, wrestling skill, dexterity, energy supply and behavior. For unsuccessful and good battles, an in -game currency is accrued, which allows you to pump the character and buy new items.
