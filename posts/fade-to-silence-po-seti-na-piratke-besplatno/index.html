Game Description#

On the pirateFade to Silence you can play online for free with a friend, surviving together in a cold post-apocalyptic world online online. The world is in eternal winter. The planet is under a layer of snow and ice. The heroes will have to fight with demons and creatures of evil. Free online play offers the opportunity to join forces with a partner to overcome obstacles, difficult enemies and powerful bosses.

The combat is built on the principles of the Souls series. The plot part tells about a hero named Ash. He is trying to collect various materials and resources that will help him survive the frosty winter. You need to build a shelter and survive. Online play allows you to join forces, solving various problems together, improving equipment and fighting fierce opponents, one blow of which can instantly kill the protagonists. The main mechanics of Fade to Silence: tense and frosty atmosphere; exploration mode and open world; the ability to travel in wolf teams; companions, allowing you to fight monsters together; true survival during the apocalypse; co-op mode for two.

Open plains, mountains, dungeons, abandoned temples, etc. are presented for exploration. Players must constantly fight the cold. A snowstorm can be worse than any bloodthirsty monster. Each character has unique skills and abilities. When leveling up, you need to gain access to higher level equipment. One player can collect only the simplest materials, but if you get together as a team, you can build an impregnable fortress. Footprints are left in the snow, so you can track hunting grounds and stalk your victims.
