Game Description#

Revengeful Ghost introduces Kayiti Tsujisava, a second grade student of a high school, whose twin sister Akane disappears after a message on his phone-scrap. This communication contained a video that became central in subsequent events. Soon after the loss of Akan, another student from his school takes scores with life, jumping off the roof. This second incident forces Cayiti to go to the educational institution to clarify the connection between these cases.

The investigation begins with the left device containing the initial clues to the unfolding situation. The plot of Revengeful Ghost develops as the main character of a video that revives the event of a 25-year ago is studying. Then they put an ultimatum to him: to find the location shown on the record, otherwise he will die. Throughout the passage, the player reveals the details of the curse and truth, hidden for decades. Such a development of events is exposed to fear and origins of cruelty, affecting the characters during the merger of the past and the present.

The gameplay is presented in the first person in this horror adventure. The storyline unfolds mainly through text messages to the smartphone, and the system itself operates in real time. The intra -game clock goes at the same pace as the real ones, and the messages from other actors come with appropriate intervals. These communications serve as a mechanism that launches new events and advance the narrative, plunging the gamer deeper into the unfolding secret.
