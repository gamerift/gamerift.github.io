Game Description#

Broken Sword - Shadow of the Templars: Reforged follows the curious and slightly unlucky George Stobbart, an American tourist who never seems to have a dull day, and Nico Collard, a witty French journalist with a knack for riddles. The story begins in Paris, a city known for its rich culture and cafe scene, but for George this time it means dodging an explosion outside a charming little cafe. This incident kicks off an investigation that unfolds faster than you can tell a conspiracy, taking George and Nico on a wild ride that spans Europe and the Middle East. What begins as a simple explosion soon develops into a deeper mystery involving the Knights Templar, a group shrouded in mystery and history.

Broken Sword - Shadow of the Templars: Reforged is structured around point-and-click mechanics, a proven method that allows gamers to navigate different environments and interact with the world in front of them. The design encourages exploration by asking users to click on objects, people and places to find clues. Dialogue plays a central role in the story, and there is always a sense that every word may contain a clue to solving the mystery. This is the kind of game where attention to detail can mean the difference between moving forward and spending hours scratching your head over a puzzle. But it’s not just about collecting objects and listening to people talk; The puzzles George and Nico face range from easy, thought-provoking puzzles to more challenging ones.
