Game Description#

Untitled Goose Game is a stealth action game about a goose, which you can play online with a friend or compete online on the Internet. All online modes are available for free on the pirate site, giving players the opportunity to play as mischievous geese. Taking control of the animal, you will need to go to the expanses of an English village to terrorize the local population. It takes creativity to get a person to lose their temper.

You have to complete a huge number of different quests, which are real puzzles on interaction with the environment. The online game is completely devoid of violence. The game mechanics of Untitled Goose Game include the need to play tricks on people by stealing items that are important to them, moving their tools, house keys, etc. The project contains a huge amount of crazy humor.

The possibility of a free online game allows you to use various abilities of geese: run, cackle, flap their wings, peck others, etc. The maps are very interactive and allow you to interact with almost every item in the location. People react very violently to any changes and may start chasing the bad guy. If they manage to catch up with the bird, then they take the object that he stole from them. Once at a new location, a list of tasks opens that must be completed in order to advance further in the plot. Additional quests are built around various time-limited challenges, so you have to act as quickly as possible!
