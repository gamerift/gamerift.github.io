Game Description#

Pure Rock Crawling is an SUV simulator, the main concept of which is built around a realistic simulation of tires and suspension. The developers set a goal to create the most realistic ride on rocky paths, deserts, hills, etc. A meditative ride through the stones is one of the best ways to experience the adventure of the wild lands of the entire planet.

At first glance, the task in Pure Rock Crawling is extremely simple: drive through 10 gates consisting of cones to get along a designated route from one point on the map to another. However, this will not be so easy. An incredible road of obstacles lies ahead, running through mountains, rocks, forests, various difficult trails, etc. Points will be awarded for each cone passed. If you catch an object, the points are deducted.

Precision and concentration are required to overcome the difficult path in the shortest possible time. Each stage takes 5 minutes. Watch your throttle! You can overdo it or underdo it, which will cause your failure. As you progress, new tracks, challenges, and additional modes of transport will open up. Just remember one thing: you definitely won’t be able to complete the route like in arcade races. The game requires attention and patience.
