Game Description#

Coffin Dodgers can be played online with a friend for free, and you can also compete with people from all over the world online. Each participant chooses their own vehicle and character to destroy the Grim Reaper and his hordes of zombies. All players are armed with a variety of homemade weapons and auxiliary devices. Free online play allows you to compete on 13 unique interactive tracks. Coffin Dodgers gameplay includes: 7 characters with their own unique weapons; customization of wheelchairs; quirky ragdoll and physics; open world to explore; competitions for up to 4 people; single player mode and time trials.

Events take place in a quiet and cozy village where elderly people live. A mysterious resident has settled in the town and decides to collect the souls of all the people in the area. A group of grandparents decide to stand up for their home. The online game features 7 charismatic characters. They use wheelchairs to get around. With this type of transport it is necessary to confront the Grim Reaper. Racing requires true survival. The championship includes 13 races, where you need to use a variety of boosts, enhancements and weapons, confronting an army of the living dead.

The Sunny Pines community is divided into 4 unique areas. Each area is represented by its own design and offers a variety of competitions to compete in. If you finish last, the Reaper takes the elderly soul. However, he brings the character back as a living dead. Gradually, the Lord absorbs the town, making it much darker. At the same time, the complexity of the gameplay increases.
