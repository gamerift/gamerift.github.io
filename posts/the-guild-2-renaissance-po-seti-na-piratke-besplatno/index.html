Game Description#

The Guild 2: Renaissance is a global addition to the colonial simulator, which you can play online with a friend, completing cooperative missions, or take part in confrontations online online for free on a pirate. The setting immerses you in Renaissance Europe. This period is remembered for the fact that society began to emerge from the power of the church and feudal lords. In fact, modern civilization was formed during this era. People began to pay more attention to technology, science, art, etc.

The dark ages are gone. The 14th century brought a new order and foundations. Society has changed forever. The network game offers a plunge into the world of trade and commerce, when the middle class began to radically change society. Alone or in a company, you need to go through a chain of various tasks, gaining freedom and throwing off the shackles of feudal rulers. Together with your partners, you will have to go through the period when reading and writing ceased to be the prerogative of only the rich and wealthy. It is necessary to become a pioneer on the path to class equality and global education. Key features of The Guild 2: Renaissance: free online play and single player campaign; gameplay immersing you in the beginning of the Renaissance; 8 new professions: mercenary, gravedigger, mason, magician, innkeeper, banker, miller and gardener; many new ones goods, additional tasks and a huge number of innovations; unique scenarios: Transylvania, Rhineland and the Alps; improved AI and increased map scales; difficulty settings and a special measure of God.
