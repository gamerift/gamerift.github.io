Game Description#

Lost Luminosity begins with how Sam inherits the mother’s apartment after her death. Recently discharged from a psychiatric hospital, she never revealed the causes of her mental disorder. The neighbors complain about strange sounds and inexplicable phenomena coming from an empty dwelling. Intrigued by these stories, the hero decides to personally find out the source of anxiety and find out the truth about the past of his family. The whole investigation is focused on the study of a closed space to understand the events that occurred there.

The gameplay is tied to the study of the house in the first person. Interaction with the environment helps to restore the picture of the past and clarify the fate of the father. During the passage of the linear history of Lost Luminosity, details about the state of the mother are opened through objects discovered in the apartment. The narrative also affects the social topics of family relations, offering to look at the main character from the outside. An important part of the gameplay is a physically correct interaction with objects that allows you to directly contact the situation.

The whole game is pierced by a oppressive and alarming atmosphere. Sam directly faces the presence of supernatural forces that create a constant feeling of anxiety. The central conflict forces the protagonist to confront these entities and the crushing load of family history. This journey is built as a thriller combining psychological elements with direct paranormal phenomena. The emotional plot unfolds through tense moments and secrets revealed by the hero in the walls of the house.
