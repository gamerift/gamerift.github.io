Game Description#

Blood of Mehran takes the legendary warrior Mehran to Ancient Mesopotamia, where his desire for a peaceful life is destroyed by the ambitions of others. Inspired by the Arabian Nights, the story revolves around themes of love, loss and loyalty. The game world includes sprawling palaces, bustling bazaars, and endless sand dunes to explore. Gamers can travel on horseback, exploring locations in search of secrets and collectibles hidden in cities and in the wild.

Key features of the Blood of Mehran saga:

A deep story of love and fidelity, inspired by oriental tales. Flexible combat system: from slashing attacks to archery and stealth maneuvers. Exploring the colorful world of Ancient Mesopotamia on horseback and on foot. Develop your hero through the skill tree to enhance his legendary abilities.

The gameplay offers several approaches to battles. Direct combat is done in the hack-and-slash genre, and the arsenal includes a combination of a sword and a shield or paired blades. To hit distant targets, a bow and arrow is provided, allowing you to eliminate threats from a distance. The third-person combat mechanics also allow for combat on horseback while galloping across open terrain.

In situations that do not involve open confrontation, a well-thought-out stealth system is an alternative. The player can use cover to move unnoticed past enemies, remaining a real ghost in the location. All actions affect character development, helping Mehran become stronger. In a special skill tree, the player distributes points to unlock and improve the hero’s abilities, adapting his skills to the chosen playing style.
