Game Description#

Try to Drive sets two players the task of simultaneously piloting a tandem bicycle on a vertical highway. One gamer controls the gas, setting the speed of movement and evading the traps. At the same time, his partner is responsible for the wheel, laying the route through the environment. Successful ascent to the top directly depends on the coordination and connection in the duet. The entire gameplay is built on this joint control scheme, turning cooperation into a key element for advance forward.

Try to Drive is based on the story of two friends who went to the tandem race, which takes place at a great height. Their route to the competition is the main scene for events, being completely unpredictable. Players lead their bike through office buildings, lively highways and existing construction sites. Each area offers obstacles that check the coherence of the actions of the two riders. Landscapes replace each other, from entertainment parks to industrial zones, forcing the duet to constantly adapt to new conditions.

Four complexity modes allow you to configure the degree of test. Light offers passing with frequent checkpoints to maintain progress. On normal difficulties become more significant, and points of conservation are less common. The complex mode provides only a few control marks on the entire map, requiring long segments of a continuous game. The final option, madness, removes all the checkpoints, forcing to make the only full rise from the very beginning to the end.
