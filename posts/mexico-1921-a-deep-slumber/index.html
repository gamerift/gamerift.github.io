Game Description#

**Mexico, 1921. A Deep Slumber. **begins with gamers stepping into the shoes of Juan Aguirre, a young and ambitious photojournalist making his way through the streets of post-revolutionary Mexico City. At this time, the dust from the Mexican Revolution was just beginning to settle, and the political landscape was still in flux. Here users find themselves in the thick of things, following Juan, who is trying to uncover the truth about the murder of President Alvaro Obregon. The task is not easy, because this incident shook the entire city. However, amid the chaos, Juan’s lens becomes his weapon.

The film centers on Mexico, 1921. A Deep Slumber. interweaves political intrigue with the art of journalism. The city is alive with characters, each of whom has their own secrets and plans. Juan doesn’t just take photographs, he interacts with people from different walks of life. Some of them are politicians, others are street vendors, and some - well, some - you wouldn’t want to meet in a dark alley.

Every interaction provides a piece of the puzzle, every conversation and every photo brings the player closer to the truth. Rates? Tall. Answers? Gamers themselves must find them. Here users are not just walking along the streets of Mexico. They capture them, literally. With Juan’s camera, players capture key moments, recording the story as it unfolds. But it’s not just about taking pictures.
