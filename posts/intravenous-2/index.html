Game Description#

Intravenous 2 immerses you in the world of cruel intrigues and intense action. Play for Sean, desperately trying to save his abducted father, and Steve, a grumbling former military with a dark past. Their unlikely union is based on a common goal: to pass the labyrinth of the underworld, complete secrets, lies and mortal danger.

But how you can handle this insidious Odyssey depends only on you. Will you be a ghost, fluttering in shadows and manipulating environment in your interests? Or will you release a lead storm by destroying your opponents with a weapon? Intravenous 2 gives you the opportunity to determine your game style, rewarding both a cunning strategist and shooter. Secretity or firepower - the choice is yours. Get in the dark, strategically turning off the light and hacking the locks, or give free rein to your finger, releasing a hail of bullets. The game is dynamically adjusted to your approach, so each passage will be fresh and exciting.

Paying the path through this mixing world ambiguous from a moral point of view, you will not only solve the truth about the disappearance of Father Sean, but you will also learn the confusing story of Steve himself. Their intertwined stories are woven into the exciting tapestry of betrayal, revenge and, ultimately, atonement. Hit your skill, whether it is honing with deadly accuracy with the help of 29 tuned types of firearms or mastering the art of silent captures. Take advantage of Sean’s unique ability to “bend” time in tense situations to get a tactical advantage over formidable opponents.
