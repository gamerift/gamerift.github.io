Game Description#

Call of Duty 2 provides the opportunity to compete online online, fighting with a friend or random players from around the world. You can play the pirate game for free on the Internet online or go through all the single-player campaigns. The online game is aimed at confrontations in which you need to score the maximum number of points. Sometimes there are additional goals. Points are awarded for one kill. For the returned flag, 2 points are awarded, for the captured banner and for the destruction of the headquarters - 10. For suicide and the murder of allies, one point is deducted.

Three sides to the conflict are represented: Soviet troops, the American and British armies. They are fighting against the Germans. The interface stands out for its minimalism. When your health reaches a critical level, the screen turns red. The possibility of free online play allows you to fight with a wide arsenal of weapons, but fighters can only carry two types of guns and 4 grenades. There are stationary machine guns such as the Browning M1919 and MG-42, as well as various anti-aircraft guns, artillery pieces, etc. Call of Duty 2 campaigns: Soviet: Winter War, Not a Step Back and Stalingrad Fortress; British: Battle of El Alamein, Tank detachments, Defeat of Rommel and Battle of Caen; American: D-Day, Hill 400 and Crossing the Rhine.

All three campaigns take place between 1941 and 1945. Campaigns are divided into episodes, and these in turn are divided into missions. A total of 10 episodes are presented with a total of 27 missions. The plot tells about the dramatic events of World War II. The game characters are Vaslily Ivanovich Kozlov, John Davis, David Welsh and Billy Taylor. Each hero is represented by an individual character and an individual set of skills.
