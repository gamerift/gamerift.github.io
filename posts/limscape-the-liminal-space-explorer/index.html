Game Description#

Limscape: The Liminal Space Explorer offers the first -person trip to quiet, terrible environment. In this project, gamers move according to locations that demonstrate signs of linearity, striving for the exit. This gameplay develops from simple movement, including actions such as swimming, jumping, squats and climbing. Such activity translate the study from horizontal routes to vertical obstacles, increasing the player’s immersion and its interaction with unique conditions.

Gamers also select and throw objects, further strengthening their influence on the world. Limscape: The Liminal Space Explorer contains 5 official levels, each of which creates familiar, but alarming impressions. These levels cover the images of hotels, hydraulic structures, metro, pools, office buildings and city fragments. Each level hides a message that reveals the essence of Title. Promotion depends on simple movement: players must detect and follow the specified goal.

This initial task lays the basis for further understanding the reasons for the continuous search for such guidelines and the general architecture of the project. In addition to study, this project offers a design mode that allows gamers to convert the environment. This game gives complete freedom of action. This sand system simplifies the placement of a variety of objects through direct input. Users control lighting, shadows and physical properties in the spaces formed, observing changes in real time.
