Game Description#

Metal Gear Solid δ: Snake Eater is a remake of a classic stealth explosion, telling the story of the origin of the legendary soldier Big Boss. The action unfolds in 1964, during the Cold War, and the main character Naked Snape penetrates the Soviet Union to stop the nuclear threat and resist his former mentor Boss, who went over to the side of the enemy. There is a rich storyline with memorable characters, such as Ozelot, Eve and The End, which throw various challenges. The game also introduces concepts such as camouflage, survival and CQC (close battle), which allow us to adapt to various conditions and situations. The action has several endings, depending on the actions and the choice of the player throughout the passage.

Users at Metal Gear Solid Delta: Snake Eater will have to become in the place of an elite soldier who is entrusted with an extreme importance task: to get into deep jungle to prevent the development of a weapon that can plunge the world into a catastrophic war. The bets are high, and secrecy becomes the key to survival. The gameplay is an exciting mixture of espionage and survival, where the player has to outwit the enemy, using tactics such as masking, close battle, persecution, interrogation, climbing, hunting and even treatment of wounds on the fly. The jungle themselves is a formidable adversary, full of traps, into which enemies and prey can fall. Here the danger lurks in the shade, and each sound can be a warning about a hidden predator. The surrounding environment gives the game additional complexity, making every step and decision critical for the success of the mission.
