Game Description#

Race Driver: GRID is an arcade car simulator aimed at playing online with a friend or players from all over the world, competing online in various tournaments and championships. You can take part in multiplayer races for free on a pirate or go through a single-player campaign. In solo mode, you can pump up your car by tuning any element of the car or repairing the body.

As a racer, you need to move up the career ladder, attracting the attention of sponsors and advertisers. Having earned a reputation, many firms and manufacturers will want to sign a contract and offer endorser terms. Each employer in Race Driver: GRID sets its own conditions, so you will need to familiarize yourself with the contracts.

Competitions, regardless of whether it is a single-player campaign or an online game, are represented by three continents: circuit racing and derby are in the USA, Formula 1 races are transferred to Europe, and the Japanese prefer to watch drifting and dueling battles. Free online play includes competition in all disciplines and on all continents represented. Each territory has 3 professional leagues and one super final.

The off-season period includes an additional race - the La Mans Series. Each player can choose his own team, sponsors, symbols, colors and even set a name. The team’s performance differs from that of the driver himself. In this regard, you need to carefully study the cards of your employees. Opponents also make mistakes on the tracks, so you should always be on guard to take advantage of your opponents' mistakes.
