Game Description#

Talented is an arcade roguelite shoot ‘em up that throws gamers into a chaotic world where survival is key. The project revolves around the character Ed, who will spend 20 nights on the infamous Demon Conveyor Belt (D.C.B). Each night presents him with new challenges as waves of enemies come inexorably, and Ed can only adapt and use a combination of abilities and upgrades to stay alive.

The randomness-focused structure makes each session feel different, as no two playthroughs are the same. Once immersed, users quickly realize that survival depends not only on skills, but also on choosing the right combination of talents and strategies. What sets Talented apart from other games in the genre are the randomly generated skill trees that accompany each play. Due to the ever-changing nature of these skill trees, users never settle into a predictable rhythm.

Each time you start a new run, you’re presented with different abilities, talents, and upgrades, forcing you to think on your feet and adapt quickly. The title offers a wide choice: from spells that incinerate enemies in the blink of an eye, to abilities that allow you to manipulate time itself. The array of customization options is wide enough to suit a variety of styles, and the randomness keeps the game unpredictable, forcing you to be on your guard. With six different character classes, the title offers gamers an extra layer of challenge.
