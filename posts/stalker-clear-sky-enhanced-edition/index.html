Game Description#

S.T.A.L.K.E.R.: Clear Sky - Enhanced Edition transfers a gamer to the role of a mercenary named Scar, forced to survive among warring groups, unstable abnormalities and deadly emissions. The action takes place to the events of the previous title of the series, demonstrating the origins of the territorial split in the zone. The basis of the mechanics is the system of wars of fractions - with its help, the user is able to change the alignment of forces between armed formations.

Each clash affects control over key points and the arrangement of the parties in the alienation zone. In Stalker: Clear Sky - Enhanced Edition, a lot is tied to decisions, on which not only the outcome of the missions depends, but also the attitude of the fractions to the main character. Fighting scenes develop unpredictably: ambushes can be expected at any time, and aggressive emissions turn the landscape into a deadly trap. The unique stability of the scar to such phenomena attracts the attention of stalkers, scientists and the military.

These situations are not attached to the script - they are a consequence of the constant conflict and activity of AI. An improved edition of Title makes a number of improvements without changing the foundations of the concept. The updated weapon model and review adjustment enhance the interaction with the environment. Water behavior, a change in the sky and weather effects form a lively reaction to what is happening. All changes are aimed at increasing gameplay immersion. This shooter creates a dynamic environment where each player’s action leaves a mark.
