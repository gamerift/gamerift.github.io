Game Description#

Echoes of the end puts the player under the control of Rin, the west that owns magic. The central plot tells about her campaign to save her brother from the totalitarian empire, which can prevent the war. It is united with Abram Finley, scientists, and together they are trying to reveal a conspiracy that can light up an ancient conflict. The narrative affects the topics of trust, atonement and self -sacrifice against the backdrop of the Time of Troubles in the world of AEM. History is presented using the technology of capturing movements.

The gameplay in Echoes of the End is built on an action from a third person, combining the battles on the swords and the use of magical skills. Gamers use the arsenal of Rin forces to overcome many opponents and clashes with large -scale bosses. Its possession of magic develops during the adventure, and this action provides opportunities for combining forces with Abram in combat combinations. This game with an emphasis on characters contains scenes of violence and bloody reprisals.

The study and solving of puzzles form another key component of the project. Moving mechanics allow you to move through a variety of areas with the help of such skills as a double jump, jerk and gravity management, and each chapter introduces new tests. The riddles associated with the environment require the use of Rin forces, including gravity manipulations, the destruction of objects and the creation of illusions. Overcoming these difficulties often depends on interaction with Abram to promote the world, which includes locations from lava fields to mountain peaks inspired by the landscapes of Iceland.
