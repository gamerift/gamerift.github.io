Game Description#

TheCarX Street car simulator allows you to build your own car and hit the streets to conquer the whole city. The metropolis is presented on a huge scale, including its surroundings: city streets, industrial area, picturesque highways, etc. CarX Streets features drifting, high-speed chases, heavy traffic and intense competition in online races.

The main feature of the game is the possibility of deep car customization and wide tuning possibilities. You can customize the exterior of the car, including choosing a body, tires, spoilers, glass, etc., as well as upgrade the interior, choosing enhanced engines, transmissions, suspensions, and much more.

The racing is heavily tied to realistic physics, using CarX Technology, specially written for the game. Matchmaking is represented by the selection of opponents in accordance with the skills and level of the driver, placing cars of similar “filling” and class in one row. The city’s nightscapes will challenge only the best street racers to race for the title of greatest street racer, testing drivers' reflexes and alertness through sharp turns and dangerous mountain slopes.
