Game Description#

Desecrators offers Gamer the role of the cosmic marauder, operating in the Galaxy Milky Way in 4096. Humanity has erected numerous large structures in mastered star systems, from deep processing stations to orbital complexes, often containing valuable technologies, weapons and resources. On board the Nightingale FTL Cracer, the character acts as a well-known defiler, making raids on the selected objects.

The task is to accumulate a destructive arsenal and sufficient technologies to try to storm the legendary storage facilities of the Galactic Treasury, but this path runs through the galaxy full of dangers for all prey hunters. The gameplay in DESECRATORS includes hovercraft piloting with six degrees of freedom, demanding skill control for navigation in confusing locations and survival in contractions. It is necessary to dodge weapons fire and missile attacks when maneuvering inside complexes.

The battle requires the use of a variety of inventory of 25 units of weapons, missiles and mines, such as plasma throwers for close combat or rail to destroy remote targets. This shooter offers a campaign with procedural generation, creating changed levels of levels filled with enemies, traps and potential finds during research missions. The opponents under the control of AI, from basic drones to advanced combat vehicles using joint actions, have to be opposed. These enemies are coordinated during collisions to provide agreed resistance to the action of the user.
