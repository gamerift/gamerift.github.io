Game Description#

Knightfall: A Daring Journey is a fast-paced first-person shooter in which you can play online with a friend on a pirate for free, controlling two knights. The network game is represented by cooperative mode and confrontation. Multiplayer is available online, and co-op allows you to play locally. Heroes are connected by a common goal. You will need to take part in the race for the rose. You need to move between cities during the day, and when night falls you need to hide behind castle walls.

You will need to travel during the daytime. With each subsequent day there are fewer cities, but you constantly need to make a pass. To keep the hero of Knightfall: A Daring Journey from freezing, it is very important to get into cover. Free online play is available for up to 24 players at the same time. The versus mode features classic battle royale elements.

You can divide into teams or fight alone. If at least one team member reaches the city, he gets the right to be the first to choose from a huge list of weapons and heals. Members of his group can also choose equipment for themselves. The remaining knights take whatever is left. When the next day comes, you need to mount your horse and go on a journey again to pick the legendary rose. The higher the connection between the hero and the horse, the better it drifts and overcomes obstacles. Enemies need to be shot with pistols. You can throw grenades at your opponents.
