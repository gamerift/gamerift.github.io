Game Description#

The anime adventureCRIMESIGHT tests your deduction skills and allows you to play online as a pirate for free, competing in logic with a friend or random players online online. You have to enter into a real battle of wits, trying to unravel the mystery of the crimes committed. The online game CRIMESIGHT invites you to both join forces and compete with each other in solving puzzles. The free online play feature includes a wide variety of multiplayer modes. The single-player adventure pits players against an advanced artificial intelligence called Moriarty. Helps in passing AI Sherlock.

The adventure involves 6 characters. Everyone has their own unique abilities and deductive skills. Characters interact with each other using a variety of patterns and signals. After the heroes arrive at the crime scene, it is necessary to carefully explore the premises, collecting evidence and clues available in the location. One of the six characters is a murderer. It is necessary to track him down before he kills his next victim. It is required to interact with each other in order to solve assigned tasks as quickly as possible.

Actions take place in the future. London, 2075. All the characters find themselves in a huge mansion in a snowstorm. All heroes are experienced detectives using a high-tech system called Foresight AI. It allows you to quickly analyze the situation and predict further events based on the facts obtained. With its help, it was possible to reduce the crime rate by as much as 90 percent.
