Game Description#

On the pirateAliens Colonial Marines you can play online with a friend, or go through the story campaign alone. For joint battles, an online Internet connection and a LAN connection are available. All DLC included for free: Stasis Interrupted, Lisbeth Hutchins, Samuel Stone and Richard Levy. The online game Aliens: Colonial Marines allows you to go with your friends through numerous locations from the franchise of the same name, gives you the opportunity to visit the planet LV-426, and will delight true fans with references to famous heroes and events.

The story starts from Sulaco orbit on a ship called Sephora. The gameplay is a classic corridor FPS, where you need to kill Aliens in order to gain access to new equipment and guns. A total of 15 types of weapons are available. Each barrel can be improved and modified. To monitor monsters, a motion sensor is introduced that determines the location of the xenomorph. It not only represents enemies, but also shows allies. A special item is a plasma cutter that allows you to open jammed doors. Features of Aliens Colonial Marines: the possibility of free online play; co-op for up to 4 people, allowing you to go through the campaign together; a battle in which two teams fight in 2 rounds of 5 minutes each, and after time the teams change sides; extermination - a mode with a confrontation between marines and aliens, where it is important to score as many victory points as possible; rescue - a game where, in the role of aliens, you cannot allow people to reach the evacuation point; survival - holding back the onslaught of xenomorphs, where the latter’s goal is to completely exterminate people; hunting beetles - a team of four players not only fights off alien monsters, but also carries out a list of important assignments along the way.
