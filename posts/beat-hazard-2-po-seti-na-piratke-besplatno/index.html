Game Description#

InBeat Hazard 2, you can play online with a friend, even on a pirate. Free online, this arcade shooter offers a wild ride through space, destroying enemies to the beat of its own tunes. Online play synchronizes gameplay with your personal music library, making each track a unique battlefield. Enemy waves and power-ups pulse to the beat as you dodge bullets and deliver devastating blows as your ship grows stronger to the beat of the music.

However, the game is not limited to local files. It has an ace up its sleeve - the Open Mic feature, which allows you to connect to premium streaming services such as Spotify or YouTube. This opens up a whole universe of possibilities, turning almost any song into your own personal space opera. Who hasn’t dreamed of saving the galaxy while listening to their favorite playlist?

But wait, there’s more! Beat Hazard 2 features procedurally generated boss ships. These big bads are freshly prepared for each track, giving you a unique enemy to fight each time. It’s like having a personalized nemesis for every song in your library. Talk about freshness!

Speaking of fresh, your own ship gets in on the action too. For each track, the game creates a one-of-a-kind vessel - from a nimble little fighter to a huge hulk. It’s like the space lottery - you never know what you’ll get, but you can be sure that it will be a lot of fun to fly. Plus, if you come across a particularly awesome ship, you can share the love and tell your friends how to unlock it.
