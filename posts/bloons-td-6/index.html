Game Description#

Bloons TD 6 (Bloons Tower Defense 6) is a 2018 action strategy game where the player will have to defend a fortress with the help of monkeys. The goal of the game is to destroy a stream of fast-moving balloons. The fewer goals missed, the greater the chances of winning. The balls differ in appearance and characteristics - new ones have been added in this part: purple, reinforced and impenetrable.

They move along a certain trajectory, sometimes from all sides, so you will have to use all the talents of a strategist in order not to lose. You can test your abilities on 30 cards. Some of them are particularly difficult and contain areas with no vision that the monkeys will have to shoot blindly. The user has 2 types of monkeys at his disposal: Towers. There are 20 types in total and 2 new ones: alchemist and druid. Each monkey tower has 3 development branches and 5 levels of pumping. Heroes. Each game can accommodate 1 monkey hero with 20 special skills and 2 active abilities. As you progress through the levels, you gain access to new skills.

The player can choose the appropriate difficulty level - from beginner to expert, and each opens up new modes: with a limited number of monkeys, double life of balls or the rough “Champanzee”. In this part of the series, the developers pleased with colorful three-dimensional graphics and pleasant visual effects. In addition, you can now change the appearance of fighters.
