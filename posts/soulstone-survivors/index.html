Game Description#

Soulstone Survivors events take place in the gloomy fantasy universe, and the main characters will be the abyss hunters who strive to capture the unique stones of the soul. They have an incredible force capable of turning a carrier into a creature with which only a deity can compare by capabilities. However, it is extremely difficult to get them - for this it will be necessary to first defeat the master of the void. These are huge bosses with colossal power. They are the guardians of these ancient artifacts.

In Soulstone Survivors there is a developed combat system that allows you to pump an impressive number of skills - about 150 active and hundreds of passive. There are also special runes that can be combined with passions to enhance their properties. At the initial stages of passage, users gain all access to a pair of characters, however, performing complex tasks, it is possible to unlock as much as 14. They are all unique in their own way and have their own set of perks and the style of battle.

The Persian is allowed to be in other ways, including the craft of objects - weapons and armor. For the passage of especially complex tests, special craft items are given to create legendary weapons. Before the start of each test, users can somewhat complicate the gameplay, activating one of the many curses.
