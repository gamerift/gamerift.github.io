Game Description#

On the piratedLEGO Batman 2 DC Super Heroes you can play online for free, taking control of one of the characters from the Batman and DC-comics universes with a friend. Network play is available locally and online online. The plot follows billionaire Bruce Wayne against the villain Lex Luther. The possibility of free online play allows you to control Batman and his partner Robin. The adventure starts at Arkham Asylum. The opponents are local madmen and Two-Face along with the Joker. Lex Luthor uses the Deconstructor to free the Joker from prison.

The gameplay is an arcade game in Lego style. You will need to fight enemies, use gadgets and break block buildings. Various parts that make up the construction set fall out of them. New designs can be created from collected blocks. Gotham City is available for exploration. Various secrets are hidden in every nook and corner of the metropolis: characters, collectible cubes, coins, etc. The story of LEGO Batman 2 DC Super Heroes can be played with a partner. Each hero has its own unique features and gadgets.

The plot features characters such as Superman, Wonder Woman, Green Lantern and many others. There are a total of 50 superheroes available. Each of them can be played. Everyone has their own individual abilities. For example, Superman can fly and heat blocks with a laser, Batman uses an energy suit, etc. There are vehicles for movement, including the Batmobile, Batwing, Batboat, etc.
