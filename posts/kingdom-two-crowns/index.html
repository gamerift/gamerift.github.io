Game Description#

Kingdom Two Crowns is a cooperative microstrategy that tells about the brave, who has become a king and now he needs to gather his army. Having saddled the right horse, he goes on a long journey to expand the kingdom. He will be opposed by the army of greed, trying to take everything from him that he has set up with his own labor. The game offers to rush not only in European medieval landscapes, but also to see many radically new ones. The dead lands are a dark kingdom, the monarchs of which moved to the game straight from the Gothic hit Bloodstained. Many characters, including Miriam, appeared in Kingdom Two Crowns, preserving both their names and opportunities.

The king has a choice of several faithful horse, among which:

Giant beetle - allows you to overcome traps;

Undead -kon - knows how to create barriers;

Hamijin is a demonic beast, effective against a crowd of enemies.

The shogunate is feudal Japan, with which you can enlist the support of the ninja and the faithful cylin. In total, the game has 3 fractions, of which the player is free to choose the side. The warriors of greed are a semblance of little Gremlins. They captured 5 possessions of the king, from which they need to be driven away.

The protagonist can use technological improvements, open new types of weapons and find secrets. For players craving serious tests, there is an island of skull, where the gameplay proceeds according to their own rules. If you lose the crown, then it will not disappear in the main game, but the loss will drop to the very first island.
