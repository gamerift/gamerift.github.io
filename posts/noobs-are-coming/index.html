Game Description#

Noobs Are Coming puts the player in the place of the final boss in the arena, where he will have to fight off the waves of opponents. The main task is to withstand the gradually intensifying attacks of attackers called nobs for a 30-minute round. A list of characters-bosses, which includes a giant worm, queen of spiders or seductive succubus, is offered to choose from. After that, it is necessary to create a unique assembly, equipping the hero with special skills and combined dozens of different attacks for constructing protective tactics.

The gameplay in Noobs Are Coming is focused on creating special assemblies and finding effective combinations between equipment and skills. You can call the minions to help in battle, arrange automatic turrets and interact with physical objects, such as spiked balls on chains or large destructive nuclei. Opportunities include the cultivation of plant allies, the use of spells to turn enemies into sheep or gold statues, and even the mining of ore on locations.

Title mechanics allow you to combine the same items several times, opening access to specialized configurations that change the course of the battle. Souls fall out of the defeated opponents, which serve as a currency for pumping and buying improvements in the store between rounds. The universe of this game is a parody of role-playing settings and contains meta-yumor. As you pass, access to new content and additional tests open. This action-rogal can pass both alone and in the local cooperative mode.
