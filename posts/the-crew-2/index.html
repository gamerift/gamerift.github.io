Game Description#

In The Crew 2 you can ride anything, as long as you have money for this equipment. The game includes several game modes in the genre of a sports racing simulator from a third/first person: free, cooperative, career, multiplayer mode. A free race usually takes place on a separate map. We choose one unlocked vehicle element and try to win the race. In this simulator you can sail on a boat, drive a fast, high-speed car, or fly on an airplane.

Cooperative involves completing a story campaign - a career. We are creating a gang of racers from experienced, professional athletes who have dedicated their lives to driving a huge number of laps on karts, or racing cars, or from people who have dedicated their lives to water or air transport. There are riders from all over the world that you can take on your team and reach the top in all disciplines.

Multiplayer mode allows you to compete with other players in any discipline. The entire world of The Crew is perfectly drawn for the player. The open blue ocean is ready to be conquered by boat or speedboat; American canyons look as realistic as possible. Flights from the heights of which you can enjoy the view of New York, Montana, Arizona and any place in the game, because all of America is open to us!

The only drawback of the game is the lack of advanced car upgrades. We take a car, boat or plane in a stock version, and we can visually pump it up a little, and just a little upgrade the engine or tires, brake pads or automatic transmission.
