Game Description#

The pirate gameHammerting allows you to play online for free with a friend to build colonies or fight online in intense duels. Players control a nome mining colony. The possibility of free online play is aimed at both co-op and confrontation. The gameplay is a colony simulator and includes elements of strategy, role-playing and time management.

Hammerting takes place in the Mara Mountains in the Big World. The kingdom is at war. Dwarves must explore mines, forge weapons, form alliances, and fight enemies. The main task of the gnomes is to provide the allied troops with advanced weapons and repair their equipment in a timely manner. You can forge legendary armor or create epic swords. There is a single player campaign and online play.

As you delve deeper into the mines and explore new tiers, new opportunities and mechanics open up. When the clan begins to grow, they learn about the gnomes in the Main Land and order improved weapons and armor for their troops. Creating such items requires more rare resources. It is necessary to develop elevators so that you can quickly move between the tiers of the cave. Dwarves are divided into clans, among which there are, for example, the Steelheads. Each faction has its own unique characteristics. All resources and elements have physics, especially liquids and solids. The processes must be carried out linearly: pumping lava into the eyes, forging weapons, sharpening swords, etc. You can brew ale and go down the wells to explore.
