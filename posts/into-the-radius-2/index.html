Game Description#

Into the Radius 2 throws users with their heads into the chilling world. Regardless of whether you prefer single research or exciting adventures in the detachment, Radius promises danger and secrets equally. A project that will allow gamers to feel the anxious atmosphere first -hand. Forget about elaborate graphics and pomp. Into The Radius 2 reduces everything to the naked bones of survival. The radius itself is a character, mysterious and hostile force, dictating to you every step. Studies are thrown by anomalies, artifacts and otherworldly phenomena. The minimalist narration of this project gives free rein to the imagination, inspiring a sense of anxiety and surprise as it moves along the main story.

Resource management is of paramount importance in this inexorable environment. Cartridges, food and medicine in deficiency, which forces to make a difficult choice. Advance deeper to get potential benefits, or to play up and retreat? The tension is created not in explosive shootings, but in quiet moments: the rustle of foliage, distant steps, the constant feeling that invisible eyes are watching you. Title departs from the traditional template. This is not about the passage of levels or pursuit of high points, but about how to stay alive, to explore the unknown and experience the thrill of uncertainty.
