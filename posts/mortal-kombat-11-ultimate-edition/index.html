Game Description#

Despite the average ratings for the 10th part of the franchise,Mortal Kombat 11 became a continuation of the story, and the developers tried to improve and add a lot of new things to players. The eleventh fighting game in the Mortal Kombat universe has new gameplay, plot, graphics, and character relationships that reveal the story as it progresses. We arrive in the world after the defeat of Shinnok, who was defeated by Cassie Cage. Before us is Dark Raiden in a single guise. He plans to defend Earthrealm by forcing all opponents to flee or die. However, Kronika appears on the horizon.

The Keeper of Time decides to restore order to the entire Universe, and to do this she will have to get rid of Raiden. The latter intervened in the past, thereby upsetting the balance of power between good and evil. The final footage of the 2011 game perfectly captures these events, while we continue to correct the consequences. Unlike the 10th part, we will not have to resolve conflicts between characters, destroying traitors along the way and everyone who cannot take the final side. The plot of Mortal Kombat 11 is simple and clear: to correct the situation in the world and come to final harmony, but it is incredibly difficult to do this. The fatal change in characters is especially noticeable in the face of Raiden. While he was purifying the energy source of Earthrealm, dark energy seeped into his being, and now we do not see his peacekeeping views, but only an aggressive policy regarding the purification of the earth.

The gameplay of Mortal Kombat 11 hasn’t changed much. This is a 2.5D fighting game, where fatalities and brutalities remained, but were transformed due to new graphics. Fatal Blow and Krushing Blow have also been added. Killing Strike is a special move that deals a lot of damage to an enemy, provided your opponent has less than 30% life left. This is the actual replacement for X-Ray. Krushing Blow is a cinematic blow that plays out a cutscene of the blow after performing several combinations. Equipment has been added to the project, but it only affects the appearance of the characters.

The developers went further and made specialized tournaments in the MK universe. Ratings for competitive matches have been introduced within championships. One tournament lasts 4 weeks, and in the end you receive one of 9 ranks, each of which gives a unique reward. The matchmaking system is designed to match you with a player of your level. In addition to this, a special editor has been developed in which you can customize the appearance of the character the way you like. This is added to the advanced graphics engine that imbues the game with reality.
