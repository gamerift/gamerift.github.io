Game Description#

Melty Blood Type Lumina allows you to play pirate with a friend online. Multiplayer is available both locally and online online. This is a 2D fighting game. Its graphics are drawn in anime style.

This is the fifth part of the series. Melty Blood Type Lumina is also considered a reboot of the franchise. The re-release boasts updated graphics, redesigned combat participants, and redesigned visual effects. The netcode has also been reworked to provide a more stable connection during online battles. In the reimagining, the roster of fighters includes 14 heroes and heroines. Moreover, not all characters made it into the new game from previous titles. The possibility of free online play allows users to arrange fights while sitting together at one computer and using a shared screen. Battles with bots are also available. The combat system has been simplified by adding the Rapid Beat mechanic. With its help, you can perform combo attacks by repeatedly pressing one attack button. New lunar systems also appeared.

The modes include story, arcade, missions, time battles and online play. In addition, the menu has a gallery where you can listen to music or admire pictures, and an option for easy customization. The plot is presented in the form of dialogue scenes with large sprites of the protagonists. At such moments, the project becomes similar to visual novels. Each fighter has his own storyline. The product is based on the Tsukihime universe. However, the world is different here. It’s just based on the old setting.
