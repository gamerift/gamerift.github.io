Game Description#

InGrand Theft Auto: San Andreas (GTA Rumble Coop) you can play online for free as a pirate with a friend, go through a story campaign in co-op, or explore the large open world of the state of San Andreas online online. Online play allows you to participate in confrontations, moving between three large cities: Los Santos, San Fierro and Las Venturas. Any vehicle can be stolen. A variety of cars, trucks, motorcycles, helicopters, planes, boats and bicycles are available. The possibility of free online play offers each player to acquire their own transport. Cars in Grand Theft Auto: San Andreas (GTA Rumble Coop) can be customized. There are several clothing stores available where you can purchase custom outfits for your character.

The action takes place in 1992. In the center of the storyline is Carl Johnson, who will have to confront the Ballos and Vagos groups, as well as Frank Tenpenny, the head of the CRASH division. In every city you can find a huge number of activities and secrets. Los Santos consists of 32 districts, including business centers and ghettos. San Fiero includes many similar real-life locations such as Hashbury, Queens, Gantt Bridge, and Las Venturas is an entertainment destination. There are casinos, many strip clubs, the Egyptian pyramid The Camel’s Toe and much more. In gun stores you can buy many types of weapons: knives, pistols, machine guns, rocket launchers and various exploding devices.
