Game Description#

Retimed is a vibrant physics-based platformer that you can play online with a friend or compete online against both other players and artificial intelligence. 4 players can take part in the pirate game for free. Network play allows you to split into two teams, or take part in the classic deathmatch mode. Not only is there a free online game available, but also a single player mode.

By controlling your character, you can slow down time in the most critical situations. Rapid allows you to dodge projectiles and react to enemy shots. Fights take place in interactive arenas. Each location is distinguished by its individual environment and style. There are many different modes to choose from. Each has its own unique mechanics and offers to achieve an individual goal.

You can fight with players from all over the world, as well as with friends on the same computer. Retimed’s single-player mode allows you to fend off waves of enemies as you progress through different stages. A special difficulty mode called Sniper is available. In 8 arenas, different opponents and tasks await you. All 4 heroes have a unique appearance and individual abilities. The characters' names are Binten, Violet, Psycho and Yowie. In addition, each character is presented with a personal character and an interesting backstory.
