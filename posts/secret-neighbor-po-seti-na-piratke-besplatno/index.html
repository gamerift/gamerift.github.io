Game Description#

Secret Neighbor is a cooperative horror game that allows you to play online with a friend using an online Internet connection. The story follows a group of young children who break into their strange neighbor’s house to get to the basement, where they believe their friend is imprisoned. All network capabilities of the project are presented free of charge on the pirate site. There are rumors that the Neighbor is kidnapping children and keeping them captive. But what adds to the complexity is that one of the friends is a neighbor in disguise, so you never know who will stab you in the back.

The action takes place in the Hello Neighbor universe, covering events between the first and second acts. Network play allows you to play for one of the sides. The gameplay for a maniac is that you need to win the trust of a group of guys in order to strike at enemies at the most unexpected moment. A maniac is capable of beating children and locking them in the basement. If you play for the other guys, you need to overcome obstacles and collect keys to secret rooms. The possibility of free online play allows for constant interaction between participants, achieving the goal.

Children need to constantly interact with each other, exchanging objects, overcoming obstacles and traps together. Victory in Secret Neighbor is awarded if the guys manage to infiltrate the dungeon where the madman is keeping the players' friends locked up. The neighbor can use different gadgets, scare children, separate them to track them down one by one and much more. If you manage to throw all the kids into the dungeon, then the maniac wins the match.
