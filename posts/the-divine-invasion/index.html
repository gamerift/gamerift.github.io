Game Description#

The Divine Invasion is an adventure action that uses a third -person camera, which allows you to better consider dynamic fights with crowds of enemies and increases the view around the hero. The setting of this project, developed by a small indie studio, becomes a science fiction future with multiple elements of a popular direction-cyberpanus. Thanks to the main task of the gamer, which consists in the extermination of enemies, the game can be considered a simulator of a bond hunter, which serves as mysterious employers for a huge monetary reward. Their goals and initiatives seem to the protagonist illogical, but the generosity in the issue of remuneration relieve questions about issued instructions.

The plot starts after 2000 years from today. His message is that despite the serious leap in the development of technologies, he changed the world and human civilization, but the main thing remains unchanged - this is a heart that will not allow injustice and enables everyone to live according to conscience. Developers characterize their own project as an adventure study of locations with several elections affecting further events. In secret places, collectible objects are scattered, antagonists steal museum exhibits, which also need to be collected.

The gameplay focuses on the combat system, which uses cold weapons and various abilities, including hand -to -hand combat. Most missions must be held with stealth, because there will be too many enemy units. No one forbids to try their chances in an open conflict, but this is fraught with complex and long battles.
