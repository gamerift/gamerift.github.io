Game Description#

OSTRIV, this is a city -building simulator that makes you the ruler of the monarch of the 18th century. The player will have to work hard and show his ability to the manager, as well as creative abilities. The goal of the game to transfer the gameplay of simulators of this kind to a new level, by means of adding a huge number of opportunities and abandoning the usual restrictions. In this game, a really organic model of the structure of cities without already boring nets and specially allocated areas.

The simulator has cut the ability to create a very similar society. You will also have to pay attention to trade with neighboring cities, they are not close to nearby or distant. Not a little attention is paid to the tax system and the policy of the board. It is worth noting that the game world reacts very realistically to any changes. The seasons have been worked out with high detail, the weather change and the environmental impact is clearly noticeable, people behave even more naturally.

Each citizen of settlements is individual of his own prejudices, acts in accordance with his desires, chooses the profession he likes, creates a family and climbs up the career ladder. Two games are offered to choose from. The first is “history” and it needs to build and lead to prosperity the city of those times, passing the script. The second mode is called the “sandbox”, it allows you to simply enjoy gameplay without any tasks.
