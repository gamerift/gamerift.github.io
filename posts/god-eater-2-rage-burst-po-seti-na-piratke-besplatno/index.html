Game Description#

GOD EATER 2 Rage Burst is an action role-playing game with the ability to play online for free on a pirate’s account with a friend, fighting online against supernatural creatures over the Internet. The characters in the adventure act as god eaters who have sworn an oath to ancient powers. Heroes are fighters of the Blood squad, carrying out tasks from the Fenrir corporation. The online game GOD EATER 2 Rage Burst is presented as a cooperative game. You can join forces to fight huge monsters.

Heroes team up with up to 4 people to explore huge locations. You will need to fight opponents, collect loot and complete various quests. There are additional tasks and a central storyline. The possibility of free online play is represented by customization. Each player can personalize their character by choosing armor and guns. Both male and female heroes are available to choose from.

Units are represented by different classes, fight with unique weapons and have a number of special abilities. The enemies are quite strong and require an individual battle strategy. There are powerful bosses. Weapons include a variety of guns and deadly swords. There is a crafting system that allows you to create unique items. Collected items are stored in inventory. Both equipment and weapons are available for improvement. Heroes can be upgraded using one of the available skill branches. Battles take place in real time.
