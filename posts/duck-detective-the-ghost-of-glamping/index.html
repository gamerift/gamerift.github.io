Game Description#

Duck Detective: The Ghost of Glamping introduces Eugene McCryaklin, a duck detective who is currently experiencing depression, the consequences of a recent divorce and documented traction to bread, noticeably complicating its everyday life. His mission is to understand the situation in the camping territory, a place with the alleged paranormal activity, where there was no murder. In the process of completing this task, McRryakclina will have to study important subjects, find connections between facts and deal with episodes of her own past.

The storyline is revealed gradually, as it strives to comprehend everything that is happening. In Duck Detective: The Ghost of Glamping, players interact, conducting interrogations of a number of personalities; Each of them has unique information and its own motives, often concealing key details, and all dialogs are presented with complete voice voice acting. In this part, Macryaklin has a new partner, which makes certain changes to the usual methodology of the investigation and the principles of joint work.

The key lesson is to collect the testimonies and results of observations to form an integral picture of circumstances. This process requires a consistent analysis to clarify all the missing details. This detective project is designed for 2-3 hours of passage and is an independent continuation of the previously presented story. Gamers have to plunge into the intricacies of hidden deeds and difficult interpersonal relationships unfolding in this location.
