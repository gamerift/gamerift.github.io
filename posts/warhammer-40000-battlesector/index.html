Game Description#

The step-by-step strategy Warhammer 40,000: Battlesector transfers to the world of the future of the 41st millennium. It is required to collect their military units, build up strength, develop leaders' abilities and enter into a stressful confrontation, controlling destructive weapons. It is necessary to go through a rich plot campaign of 20 unique tasks. History is devoted to the devastation of the Vaal continent. It is required, together with Sergeant Carleon and his allies, cleanse Baal for a second of the influence of tyranids. In addition, it is necessary to maintain the legacy of the bloody angels.

In addition to the campaign regime, a fight is presented, where one of the three fractions will need to be taken under its control. You can control such cult detachments as a bloody priest, a librarian, dreadnought and a tyrant of the hive. In Warhammer 40,000: Battlesctor, more than 60 abilities and about 50 types of weapons are available. Each side has a Momentum system, which is used to appear Surged Unit. Superhuman abilities are combined with a powerful weapon to apply critical damage to enemies.

When earning command glasses, you will need to use aviation. In each fraction, it is represented by unique units. A storm raven can destroy xenos with missiles or apply a deep blow to the enemy’s rear. Harpy Tiranid breaks through the defense with the help of ultraviz and scatters spore mines across the field. Multiplayer in Varhammer 40000: Battlesctor is represented by an asynchronous regime and allows you to take part in Hotseat fights.
