Game Description#

Baldur’s Gate 2 Enhanced Edition gives users the atmosphere of classic RPG projects and at the same time allows you to play online with a friend on a pirate. Here the adventure continues, which began in part 1. In addition, there are enough additional missions, quests and campaigns that drag on for many hours. A squad of heroes going in search of adventure falls under the control of the user. And from the very beginning he can choose their composition by combining the proposed classes. In total, 11 classes are available to take online on the Internet, which have many branches.

The number of locations in Baldur’s Gate 2 Enhanced Edition has decreased, but in return they have become more detailed, and now gamers will need more time to explore the area in detail. While in one or another part of the map, the squad will encounter side units, and everyone will want to offer their own quests to complete. These tasks differ in duration and complexity, so each of them should be approached very carefully at the selection stage. Fortunately, for this, a detailed description of everything that needs to be done in a given situation is provided. The effectiveness of characters during the passage is determined by the degree of leveling up of skills, as well as the presence of the necessary combinations of items. And if the first ones can be pumped up along the way, then the second ones should be carefully selected. Multiplayer allows you to assemble a set of ammunition for each unit, so players will have enough time to do this. Also, the possibility of free online play allows you to save your characters and use their progress during a new playthrough. This is important, because sometimes some segments turn out to be much more difficult than the previous ones, and to achieve results you need to have the appropriate level of pumping.
