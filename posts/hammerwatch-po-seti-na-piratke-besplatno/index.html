Game Description#

The top-down adventureHammerwatch gives you the opportunity to join forces with a friend or random people online for free on a pirate. You can play online or use local co-op, going through the storyline on one device. Alone or using the free online play option, you need to destroy hordes of monsters over the course of four chapters. Each environment contains unique biomes, traps, secrets, puzzles and enemies.

Network play supports team play for up to four people. Hammerwatch features 6 hero classes. They all have individual skills and abilities. There are 3 difficulty levels to choose from: easy, medium and brutal. In addition, you can configure various modifiers that transform the passage. The easy mode can be made more difficult, or vice versa - the difficult mode can be simplified a little. Players themselves set the priority for each individual element. Dungeons are divided into blocks. Strong bosses await here. Killing them opens up access to upgrades. They can also be unlocked from merchants by paying for gold upgrades.

As a bonus, the developers have added an extensive editor. With its help you can create your own levels. A wide selection of environmental assets and opponents is presented. All objects on the level or the geography of the map are created using the author’s vision. Finished works can be shared with other players, challenging the most experienced adventurers. The gameplay is a direct descendant of Gauntlet from the 80s.
