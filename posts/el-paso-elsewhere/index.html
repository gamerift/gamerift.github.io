Game Description#

El Paso, Elsewhere immerses players in dynamic shootings with werewolves, vampires and other evil spirits that make his way to the surface of the planet. Events transfer to the territory of a small Texas settlement under the name El Passes, where he will have to fight terrible opponents. The main character finds himself in a small three -story hotel hiding the secret deep underground. Players will have to go down to dark basements to go through 46 combat missions and destroy hellish creatures. A single campaign consists of gradually becoming more complicated levels made in the style of old -school projects.

A protagonist in El Passes, Elsweair will advance through gloomy locations, faced with fallen angels, demons and victims of the insidious Count of Dracula. It is necessary to save people, as well as to replace the creatures into their native dimension. The game is an action-player from a third person, where it is necessary to plunge into the mysterious atmosphere of Neonuar, saturated with dangers in the form of terrible creatures from the underworld. Users will listen to the hip-hop soundtracks against the backdrop, moving and encountering enemies in the combat sloopers-core scenes. Users will be able to interact with the environment and destroy interactive interior items, shooting from firearms. It will turn out in the battles to rely on evasion, rifts and jumping in a slowdown in order to aimed to take shots with two hands with the help of pistols. Also, the hero will be able to throw Molotov cocktails to set fire and blow up opponents with blue fire.
