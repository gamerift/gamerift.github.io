Game Description#

Trinity Survivors offers the ability to play online. In this online game you can play online with a friend over the Internet, even if you are playing on a pirate. Create your own apocalypse-ready squad for free by choosing from a growing roster of three unique characters. Main? Strategy. Experiment with different combinations to find complementary skill sets and create an unstoppable trio. Witness amazing synergy as your well-oiled machine shreds your enemies.

Do you want to double the carnage? Trinity Survivors features online co-op for two players. Team up with a friend, develop a joint strategy and unleash torrents of destruction on the hordes of orcs. With bragging rights on the line, can the two of you conquer increasingly challenging levels and emerge with the highest score?

The beauty of Trinity Survivors is its depth. Each character has a specific set of skills, as well as powerful leadership abilities that can turn the tide of battle. Master the art of combining melee, ranged and area of ​​effect attacks. Find artifacts scattered throughout the world and watch your characters' abilities grow in power. No playthrough of the game is the same - a constant stream of new challenges awaits you. So gear up, gather your trinity and go into battle!
