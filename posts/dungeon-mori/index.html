Game Description#

Dungeon Mori begins with the return of monsters to the world, which has long expelled them. The main task of the player is to get rid of the lands of these creatures and the victory over the great eternal dragon. The research process is built on a choice system that determines the movement and events in the dungeons. The structure is based on step -by -step mechanics combined with solutions during the gameplay. The main cycle is tied on immersion in dangerous locations for the sake of treasures and prey, as well as on the confrontation of threats awaiting there.

At the heart of Dungeon Mori is a step -by -step combat system offering battles with a wide variety of monsters. In each of the 10 unlike each other, the dungeons will have to make a choice: get to the end and challenge the boss or leave the location earlier, preserving the obtained trophies. Any dungeon is inhabited by unique types of creatures, and the total number of opponents exceeds 50. Information about enemies can be found and entered in complete bestiary for further study.

The creation of the character begins with the choice of one of the 9th grades and 7 races. This role -playing game allows you to obtain rare equipment, arm yourself with powerful weapons to increase combat efficiency or change its appearance to your taste. You can take a pet with you, helping your master in battles. Such a system opens the scope for the formation of an individual combat strategy through combinations of equipment found during the study of the world and its underground labyrinths
