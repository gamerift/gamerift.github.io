Game Description#

Under a New Sun begins after a catastrophic failure of the ship, who left the player on an unknown planet. Awakening from cryosn occurs among fragments, completely solitude under a stranger sky. The mission to search for a new house for humanity failed, and the fate of the crew remains a mystery. The path begins in the middle of the wreckage of the vessel, in a world where the earthly flora has taken roots next to local views.

In the gameplay Under a New Sun, survival requires constant monitoring of hunger, thirst and physical wounds, while the days are replaced. The environment itself is fraught with threats: from weather conditions to the local fauna and old mechanisms patrolling the area. Studies become a necessity, encouraging to move between the islands and plunge into underwater locations.

Key mechanics for conquering a new world:

The fight against hunger, thirst and hostile fauna. The study of numerous islands and flooded locations. Tactile interaction with production stations. The construction of your own refuge and the technological base. Search for answers to questions about the crash of the ship.

Further development includes the collection of materials from the environment and their processing in useful components. This title offers a tactile process of creating objects: a gamer physically interacts with the workstations to perform specific tasks. It is necessary to crack the wood onto the boards, weave the canvas on the machine, make skins in suitable skin and melt ore to create tools. The search for parts from the old ship will generate electricity, providing energy for advanced buildings and constant refuge on the planet.
