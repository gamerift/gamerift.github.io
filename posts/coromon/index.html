Game Description#

Coromon is a role-playing indie adventure about taming animals for their further fights among themselves, according to the type of Pokemon. This project uses a rare pixel visual design as a tribute to the game industry of the last century. The camera is at an angle to create an isometric view of the world around the world, which is much more examined in this position. The single -user campaign of this game will take a gamer not for one day, and a thorough study will require even more free time. There is support for gamepads of any manufacturers.

The plot line occurs in the kingdom of the velia, located in the fictitious universe. The nameless protagonist enters into the ranks of Lux Solis - a technologically advanced organization for traveling around the world in order to collect his powerful army of Koromons. The pumping system allows you to improve the skills of both the protagonist and his detachment of animals of different types (fire, water, air, and so on) and outside the earthen workers, turtles, white bears, birds with 4 wings and many other variations). There are 120 of them in total, which creates scope to create various combinations. Own army is replenished as the territories advanced by capture and establishing friendly relations.

The combat system has a step -by -step system, and does not require the player to move the crums across the battle of the battle, but only choose the action: to attack one of the enemies, apply the damage to the spell, apply a buff or defend themselves. In addition to battles with animals of other tamers or species, there are boss files, which will be at least 6 pieces. In addition to the battles, the player will have to show his mind and solve the puzzles to open the path further.
