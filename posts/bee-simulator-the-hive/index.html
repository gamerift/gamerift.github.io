Game Description#

Bee Simulator: The Hive puts the player in the role of an insect with the task of protecting his colony from danger. A single story is focused on the actions of one bee, on which the survival of the entire swarm depends. Gamers have to move around the half-open world created on the model of the New York Central Park. Key activity includes the collection of pollen from plants, interaction with other insects and confrontation with aggressive Osami. The fate of the bee house depends on the actions of this individual worker.

The Bee Simulator project: The Hive is supplemented by mechanics of erection and management of housing. It is necessary to collect certain resources from the environment to build a functional and personalized hive. This system allows you to place both practical and decorative structures, making it possible to design the base of its community. The construction component is integrated into the gameplay, during which it is necessary to look for materials on the map, simultaneously opening mini-games and secrets on ponds, picnic sites and zoos.

In addition to a single campaign, this game includes a local multiplayer component for 4 people with a divided screen. This mode offers specially designed cards that support both cooperative and competitive goals. You can unite in teams or challenge each other in these special scenarios. The full range of bee classes, such as racing in air flows and the performance of communicative dances, is presented in a gameplay, offering a variety of tests for loners and groups.
