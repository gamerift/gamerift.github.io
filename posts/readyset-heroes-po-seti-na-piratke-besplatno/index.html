Game Description#

Teaming up with a friend on the pirateReadySet Heroes, you can clear the dungeon together in a colorful dungeon crawler that offers play online or on one PC. Regardless of how you go through the adventure, be it a co-op game online or a solo adventure, you will have to go into the fantasy world of anthropomorphic animals. ReadySet Heroes is an opportunity to plunge into an incredible story of valor and courage for free, assembling your party from a raccoon, a toad, a bird and a hare. The adventure will allow up to four people to cooperate on one sofa with a split screen into 4 parts.

Each hero has unique abilities, character and battle tactics. Having gone on a journey and using the opportunity of a free online game, you need to divide into 2 teams, where each person is responsible for a specific hero. Having created a party of two heroes, both factions descend together into the dungeon, where they fight powerful monsters and collect loot, while simultaneously pumping up their abilities. The group that reaches the center of the dungeon first wins.

The caves contain many traps, obstacles, puzzles and ferocious enemies. Bosses introduced. You will have to fight them in large interactive arenas. There are many weapons, equipment, armor and spells available. Network play is aimed at constant interaction between characters: you need to cover your partner, take turns making moves, delivering powerful attacks or combining skills. A real hardcore test awaits!
