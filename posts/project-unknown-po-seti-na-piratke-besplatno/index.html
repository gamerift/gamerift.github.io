Game Description#

Project Unknown immerses players in a world of paranormal dangers, inviting players to play online with a friend and other users. This online co-op horror game for 1-4 players pits you against spirits haunting a property available for free on the pirate market. You can team up with friends online to work as a team of Paranormal Property Solutions (P.P.S.) employees, driving out evil entities and increasing the value of real estate. But be prepared—haunted houses hide something creepier than just the ghosts of the night.

Each character brings unique abilities to the Ghostbusters team. There’s Eirin, a risk-taker with the tarot, and Zey, a reluctant teenager with a fearless canine companion. Don’t forget about Luther, the game’s priest, and Nyx, who wields an authentic grimoire. You will need every trick to survive the horrors that await you.

The game throws players into a deep pool of the supernatural. You will encounter a number of ghosts, each of which has its own chilling appearance and behavioral characteristics. Journal your observations to stay one step ahead of these ethereal enemies. Finding the cursed objects is the key to exorcism success, but staying alive is the real challenge.

These haunted houses are all dark. With limited light sources, you will have to carefully monitor your power consumption or risk tripping the breaker. And trust me, you don’t want to be in the dark - it’s a completely different beast compared to regular garden shadows. Oh, and did I mention the miasma? This creepy phenomenon can turn your exorcism into a disaster if you don’t take action.
