Game Description#

In Vampire: The Masquerade - Bloodlines 2, the player will move to Seattle and feel himself in the shoes of a “thin-blooded” vampire who was brought to trial and caused a war between clans. The main character was turned during a mass transformation of people, because of this he received only minor superpowers. Now he will have to figure out clan alliances and join one of them in order to survive. Dade Tully, the local hermit, will help the character adapt to the World of Darkness. As you progress, the hero will hunt and become more familiar with the dark side of the big city, which has always been ruled by bloodsuckers.

Blood merchants are also in an eternal confrontation and will have to weigh all the pros and cons when entering into dangerous alliances, because the war will not bypass anyone. Vampire: The Masquerade - Bloodlines 2 gives you the opportunity to choose your vampire character. The character can try on the role of a cruel and cold monster or appear as a generous tempter. In order to climb to the top of vampire society, the hero must work on developing cunning, charm, menace and a steely will. Without these qualities, it is impossible to survive in the shadow world. The gameplay will be presented in the form of close combat from the first person, and it will also be possible to shoot. Receipt of bonuses and their features will be affected by the emotional state of the victim.
