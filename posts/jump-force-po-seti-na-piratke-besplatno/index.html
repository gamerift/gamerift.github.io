Game Description#

JUMP FORCE is an anime fighting game for fighting with a friend, which you can play for free online on a pirate, fighting both with your comrades and random players from all over the world. When playing with partners, you can fight on the same screen, and an online Internet connection is available to confront random people. A huge variety of characters from famous anime cartoon series are presented. The possibility of free online play includes creating your own avatar with further choice of tactics and abilities.

You have to go through a story adventure or fight in the versus battle mode. The network game has its own lobby, which allows you to join already created sessions or create your own, inviting other participants to join you. The fighters are represented by such franchises as DRAGON BALL Z, ONE PIECE, NARUTO, BLEACH, YU-GI-OH!, SAINT SEIYA and many others. By creating his own hero, he can fight together with his favorite anime characters. A total of 40 heroes from 16 series are available: Ichigo Kurosaki, Son Goku, Sasuke Uchiha, Monkey D. Luffy, Kenshiro, etc. As a bonus, there are 14 additional unlockables in JUMP FORCE.

Each unit has its own unique fighting style and abilities. You should choose a Persian based on personal preferences and tactics. In the menu you can customize abilities, change clothes, accessories, etc. Items drop out as you progress or are awarded for leveling up. You can use several types of attacks, carry out combo attacks, grab or block an opponent’s attacks. To perform an enhanced technique that can take away a significant part of an opponent’s health, you need to accumulate a special rage scale.
