Game Description#

Endzone 2 throws you into the thick of the post -apocalyptic world. Humanity died, and you remained to collect fragments - literally. Each decision that you make as if you can send your scattered colony by the line. Lack of resources? Eat. Danger to the environment? Do not hesitate. Ruthless raiders eager to steal your lunch? Undoubtedly. This is not a simple walk. This is the management of your nascent civilization. You will juggle with resources as a professional, to pretend to be further expanding the settlement, and monitor the constantly changing environment.

This is a constant dance between your people are full, covered and, in general, alive, and at the same time rebuild the society from scratch in the shadow of the fallen world. Do you think you have everything you need? Title throws crooked balls, like a champion Pitcher. In one minute you are trying to conquer the last shreds of land suitable for life, and in the other - come across a strange sandstone that threatens to destroy your crops. The only constancy? Waiting for surprises.

This game checks your leadership qualities as a quiz, forcing you to adapt to any new conditions. But there is some gloomy satisfaction in watching how your colony slowly rebels from the ashes, right? Endzone 2 is not just a game of survival, it is Adventure about an unbending human spirit. This is a story about a community that returns from the edge of the abyss and intends to build a new future for itself. So, are you ready to rebuild humanity from scratch? The project is waiting for you. Just remember that hope is a valuable product in this wasteland. Use it wisely.
