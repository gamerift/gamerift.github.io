Game Description#

Dragonloop is a metro-vanning that combines manipulations over time with the design of the map, enclosing a player in a repeating 5-day cycle. The main character is a woman who has lost her memory and trapped this temporary loop. Together with a satellite named Ouro, she travels around a two -dimensional world, where strange temporal changes take place. The basis of the gameplay is the study and battles in order to break out of the closed circle, change fate and reveal the truth about the situation. Each new round makes it possible to advance further and open other lines of the narrative.

The process in Dragonloop is presented in the form of a side-scroll and focused on moving in combination with battles. Gamers can perform a quick breakthrough with OURO, connecting this ability to other skills and attacks. Key game mechanics allows you to turn the defeated boss into an elf, which can then be developed to increase combat power. Office of the environment, such as flue fish, boxes and suction cups, have many applications. They serve as tools for solving riddles, means for fighting and platforms for moving, opening various passage options.

The time itself is a special resource that can be added to certain places on the map. This mechanics are the central part of the study in the framework of a 5-day loop. The use of time to different areas can change them, opening new routes and information. Management of this resource and making decisions throughout each cycle leads a story to several finals. The outcome of history depends on how the player interacts with the world and his repeating chronology.
