Game Description#

SCP: 5k - cooperative shooter in the first person with the elements of the horror. Play both alone and unite with friends. The game is represented by hardcore complexity. Particular attention is paid to the detailed environment and plot. The team can have up to four people. Go to a complex and saturated adventure!

The story of SCP: 5k talks about how a foreign creature was discovered in the human psyche, which is responsible for all negative features: pain, cruelty, etc. The SCP Foundation is trying to explore this area. Council 05 instructs the best SCP Foundation fighters to go to the study of secret dungeons, where experiments were conducted that can exterminate the race of people. You play for members of the global occult coalition, units of the United Nations. They fight abnormal entities. Stir the labyrinths where SCP funds conducted its terrible experiments and gave rise to many artificial entities that massively kill and infect people. Features of SCP: Pandemic:

PVE-gameplay designed for four people; the possibility of a single game; Immersive gameplay and atmospheric soundtrack; Find out the secrets about the Pneuma project; Elements of the classic sai-fi horror.
