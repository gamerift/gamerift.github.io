Game Description#

Among Us (Among As) is a multiplayer game designed for 4 to 10 people on the server at the same time for exciting gameplay with a variety of scenarios. The setting of the project is boundless outer space, in which maps with different structures of surrounding objects are located. Casual controls and the ability to play on a local network in cross-platform mode make it possible to invite a noisy group to spend time together on the Among Us project. There is a built-in text chat for communication during gameplay.

Customizing the protagonist in Among Us allows you to distinguish your virtual protégé from the crowd and demonstrate your own uniqueness. You can choose the color of the strange creature you control with an atypical body structure and appearance, and also choose a hat that matches the image. You can also modify the rules of the competition, the main goal of which is to successfully launch a spaceship. Impostors will interfere with this - villains who kill and sabotage the flight. Their task is to destroy every member of the opposing team, which has no idea who exactly the enemy crew members are. Team Opportunities to Achieve the Goal: Check security cameras to identify who is only pretending to be a good character. A corpse or broken mechanism can be the reason for a meeting to discuss who exactly the enemy is. The crew can hold votes to expel enemies, this is the only way for the forces of good to win. Skillfully pretending and creating the appearance of actively working for the benefit of the team are the main skills for impostors. You can only eliminate people one by one, so as not to arouse suspicion of your actions. Creating chaos will allow you to divide the team and catch a successful moment for murder.
