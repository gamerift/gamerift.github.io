Game Description#

Arrow Heads is an isometric adventure that lets you team up with a friend or a team of up to four people to take part in an archery competition. You can play the pirate Arrow Heads for free online, fighting crowds of enemies and overcoming many different obstacles. Co-op is available on a shared screen and online online. Allows you to fight with each other or go through the campaign together.

The main weapon is a bow. you need to shoot, dodge, deflect arrows and much more. Multiplayer allows you to team up in a group of up to four people to face an army of enemies. Each character has his own abilities: you can fire rockets, electric Tesla coils, set bear traps, etc. The possibility of free online play includes constant interaction between partners. You need to cover a friend, share ammunition, etc. By killing enemies, you accumulate experience points and gain experience, which can be spent on leveling up.

The heroes fight killer bears that come in waves. You need to overcome obstacles on the roof of a skyscraper, go through a deadly laser eye, dodge cobblestones and much more. Defeating special enemies rewards you with bird seed. It allows you to discover new species of birds, gives you access to new shells, arrows, etc. Tournament tables are presented that track the best results of players.
