Game Description#

Toybox Turbos is an arcade simulator in which you can take part in pirate races online with a friend, competing online for free in various multiplayer modes. The online game allows you to ride in 35 different cars. There are a total of 18 tracks available, made in unique styles. On the tracks, players will find various obstacles and boosters.

Boosts include mines, machine guns, hammers and many other types of weapons that help destroy your opponents. The possibility of free online play is presented in 6 modes. Four people can participate in the competition. Toybox Turbos can be played locally, competing on one computer. Each mode offers a unique challenge and sets different goals for players to win.

In addition to multiplayer modes, a single-player campaign is presented. It consists of 7 cups: Race, Overtake, Time Escape, Countdown, Destruction, etc. After completing one of the cups, you can open a new micro machine. There are two types of cameras: classic and updated. There is a huge amount of varied secondary content available, as well as many improvements and updates for different types of cars.
