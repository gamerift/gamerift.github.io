Game Description#

Blood And Zombies is a zombie survival game that you can play online with a friend on a pirate for free, fighting alone or with a partner online online against hordes of bloodthirsty zombies. The action takes place during a zombie pandemic. A deadly virus has struck the entire planet and now humanity will have to fight the living dead. The possibility of free online play allows up to four people to join forces. The gameplay features first-person shooter mechanics and tower defense elements. For example, you will need to install turrets to hold back the wave of zombies and pump yourself up, while improving your defense mechanisms.

There are four heroes to choose from. Each character has their own individual set of skills and fights with unique weapons. The online game Blood And Zombies invites you to team up for more effective defense against bloodthirsty monsters. With each subsequent stage, the waves of enemies become larger. At the same time, new opponents and bosses appear. Multiplayer is based on interaction between participants. By collecting resources at locations, you need to build and strengthen a fortress. From a small outpost you can build an impregnable bunker. More than 10 types of defensive structures are available for construction. For example, these can be either ordinary machine guns or powerful Tesla towers. Heroes are upgraded using a skill tree. You can equip yourself with dozens of guns. Barrels can be improved and customized.
