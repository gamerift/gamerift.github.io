Game Description#

Loot of Baal works from the side panel of the screen, allowing you to play at the same time with other computer tasks. Players collect and send a detachment of mercenaries for battles with demons and obtaining treasures on randomly generated combat locations. The project offers hundreds of fighters, each with unique skills, and a collection of more than 1000 different things for the search. This concept of gameplay facilitates the promotion, because the key adventure occurs in a separate area of the display and does not require constant attention.

In the Loot of Baal, a gamer forms a team of three heroes, making a choice of 4 accessible classes: a magician, a paladin, a ranger and a berserker. Tactical depth is achieved through experiments with 72 individual skills and a combination of class abilities to overcome various tests. For example, one group can rely on spontaneous magic, while the other will focus on enraging weapons or activating several AUS to strengthen the entire detachment. The selected composition of the team directly determines its effectiveness and affordable tactical techniques in battles.

Equipment mining is controlled by a Luta system with thousands of possible affixes that create unique properties for each falling thing. Equipment is divided into levels of rarity: magical, legendary and epic, and over 400 immortal artifacts make up a separate category. This role -playing game allows you to create more than 200 elements of ammunition from the materials obtained. Additional changes are available through mechanics inserts of runes into the nest to improve objects to immortal status, as well as through options for improving and synthesizing components.
