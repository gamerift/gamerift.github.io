Game Description#

Bullet Roulette VR is a Russian roulette simulator that you can play online with a friend on a pirate for free, sitting in a dark bar with participants from all over the world, connected online via the Internet. The online game is designed for four players. To win, you will need to use cunning, ingenuity and dexterity. The project was developed for virtual reality helmets. Valve Index, HTC Vive and Oculus Rift are supported.

Four suspicious people sit at one table. Each character has their own dark past. There is no doubt that no one is going to play fair. The opportunity for free online play offers you to become one of 6 unique characters. Before the start of the match, each participant receives a random hero. Before you start shooting, you need to take 2 cards into your hand. They determine further actions. If everything goes well, then 1 dollar is credited. It gives the right to shoot any player in the face during the round. By killing another character, the winner takes all the money. If you fail to kill your table neighbor, the other surviving participant takes the money. Gameplay of Bullet Roulette VR: real adrenaline in virtual reality mode; the ability to play alone against artificial intelligence; 6 charismatic characters with their own background; three non-playable characters available for interaction; annoying music (the pianist can be shot); various locations in the form of closed-off basements.
