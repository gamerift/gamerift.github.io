Game Description#

**R.E.P.O. **- this is a online cooperative in the genre of Horror, in which gamers are united to collect valuable objects surrounded by terrible dangers. In this intense adventure, up to six gamers can immerse it, each of which is equipped with a tool based on physics, designed to capture and transport objects. What is the difficulty? Take care of the cargo, moving through the enchanted spaces, and not allow something to break along the way. But the physical engine is needed not only by users - it also affects the monsters, making them even more unpredictable.

Moving through the anxious environment of the game, you will have to work together, sharing tasks and remembering the fragile objects that you have to extract. The voice chat near adds an element of surprise and urgency, since you will hear your friends (or enemies) the more clearly the closer they are. Communication is a key point, but sometimes silence is no less important. Loud sounds can attract attention, and this is completely inappropriate when you are trying to carry a piano on a shaky floor.

However, the whole point is to avoid danger. In R.E.P.O. There is an opportunity to improve your equipment. For money you can buy improvements for robots, tools and weapons. These improvements facilitate the work, increasing the chances of survival in conditions of increasingly dangerous tasks. But be careful - each solution can lead to different results, and not all improvements are the same.
