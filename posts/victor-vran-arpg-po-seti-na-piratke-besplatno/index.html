Game Description#

Victor Vran is a game that can be played online. You can play it for free and even pirated. You will find yourself in an isometric world infested with demons, where you can fight with a friend online online. Knowing how to use a controller is a must, but it’s equally important to build your character correctly for online play. Dodge, jump, deliver crushing blows - the fate of the world depends on your monster-slaying skills.

In this action RPG you will find mountains of loot: weapons, items, equipment, even demonic abilities and fate cards. The battle is very intense, enemies are attacking you from all sides. You’ll have to use all your abilities to defeat them! Victor Vran does not hold your hand. You decide your own playing style. Create your own Victor with a mind-blowing arsenal - weapons that pack a punch, game-changing outfits, evil demonic abilities, and destiny cards to tip the scales in your favor. Creatively combine skills and weapons to carve a path of destruction through hordes of nightmarish monsters and cunning bosses. Slaying demons is more fun with friends! Team up as an online team and go through the story together in full co-op mode. Local co-op is also an option if that’s your thing.
