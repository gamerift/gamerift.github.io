Game Description#

Playing online in Tactic Boxing allows you and a friend to experience a world of fights that are as strategic as they are drunk. On the pirate site you can enjoy free fights online on the Internet. Unlike other boxing games where punches are thrown with predictable precision, Tactic Boxing uses ragdoll physics, making your boxers look like wobbly puppets hitting the canvas with every move. This is not only hilarious, but also adds unpredictability to the combat.

Do you want to become a champion? Tactic Boxing has a career mode where you can take a rookie boxer from the bottom to the top. Train your fighter, hone his skills - strength, accuracy, health, the whole nine yards - and develop his signature fighting style. Will you be a one-punch puncher, a relentless slugger, or a wily outside boxer outflanking your opponent? The choice is yours.

But wait, there’s more! Tactic Boxing also has a manager mode. In it, you take control of a boxing gym, training and turning promising rookies into champions. To turn a newbie into a contender, you need talent and a strategic mind.

Do you want to test your strength in battle with an opponent? Tactical Boxing has local multiplayer. Grab a friend, grab a controller, and see who has the better boxing IQ (and reflexes). Just remember that there’s no crying in boxing, but it’s okay to joke a little when your friend’s knees are weak from your killer jab.
