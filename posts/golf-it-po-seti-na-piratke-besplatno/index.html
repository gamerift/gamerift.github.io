Game Description#

Golf It! is a three-dimensional mini-golf focused on competitions on the Internet online, fighting with a friend on a pirate or challenging other players from all over the world for free. You can play online or compete on one device. The gameplay is aimed at multiplayer competitions in various interactive locations. Each level features a unique art style and a variety of traps and obstacles that prevent you from hitting the ball into the hole.

There is a single mode, online play and an extensive editor that allows you to create your own levels. Using the possibility of free online play, you can join already created rooms or create your own. You can also let other participants try to play on a personally created field. Network play allows you to join forces with partners, competing in teams.

Battles take place on 6 unique maps with 18 holes: Grassland, Winterland, Graveyard, Mines, Pirates Cove and Jade Temple. All locations have their own set of traps, obstacles and aesthetics, due to which they greatly dilute the gameplay of Golf It! variety. In total, about 2300 objects are included, available even in the in-game editor. There is a unique ball hitting system based on mouse speed, impact angle and much more. Success depends not only on the game mechanics, but also personally on the participant himself.
