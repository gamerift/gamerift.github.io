Game Description#

The main character -Middle-earth: Shadow of Mordor - will fight Sauron and avenge his family. One day, Sauron kidnapped Talion to sacrifice him and summon Celebrimbor, the spirit of the main elf in the world of the Lord of the Rings. However, this spirit helps Talion to resurrect, gives him new abilities and sends him into battle. The protagonist’s enemies are Sauron and his dark lords: the Hammer, the Black Hand and the Tower. In a completely open world of a third-person slasher, the hero will perform not only story missions, but also side quests within the game universe.

Talion’s main weapon is a sword. The famous commander and excellent father falls into the hands of the main villain of the game and becomes a sacrifice for which Sauron calls upon Celebrimbor - he needs the ancient elf to remove the seal from the ring and completely subjugate the will of the other bearers of the ancient relic. But the Elf endows the hero with his spirit, which will take revenge on the whole world, remaining in the human mind.

The storyline is diluted with the presence of many characters from the original trilogy. For example, Golum, who finds in spirit and man a “bright master”, with whom he is united by the Ring of Omnipotence. As before, Golum is a skilled guide to the world of Mordor, since Celebrimbor does not remember anything, not even his name. Armed with revenge, he and Talion head to the ruins of this world - to Lava Mountain.

The RPG component of the game allows the character to upgrade his level and skills in order to quickly track down and kill the Uruk captains, who are the goal of almost every quest. In the third-person slasher you will find massive battles, an interesting plot about the One Ring, as well as a story told from the inside by a creature that has been in the world for 3000 years.
