Game Description#

MORDHAU is a hack and slash game. Fights take place online on the company’s servers; offline you can train your skills in fights against artificial intelligence. A training session is also available, in which you will learn the basic mechanics and get your initial gold capital.

The name refers to a special German grip on the weapon, which, by the way, is available for selection. The main, but not the only feature is a fencing system that is as close to reality as possible, created through consultations with experts. Changing sides of blows, alternative grips, shooting from crossbows and bows, active use of shields in defense, fist fights - this variety of medieval combat is available to MORDHAU players. You will take part in 6 modes: Duel - a battle between two gamers on a rating Invasion - the main game mode in a 32 by 32 format, one side defends, the other storms Frontline - a clash of 64 people divided into 2 sides with the capture of points and set in the process goalsDeathmatch - every man for himselfTeam Deathmatch - a team grinder without tasksHorde - a team of a couple of allies must defeat 21 waves of botsBattle Royale - a confrontation between 60 people on a huge open map with random equipment, only one wins.

Advanced character customization makes it possible to change everything, right down to the voice. Armor, weapons and perks can be improved using in-game currency, which can be farmed by participating in bloody battles. Abilities are passive bonuses for a fighter and give advantages over the enemy.
