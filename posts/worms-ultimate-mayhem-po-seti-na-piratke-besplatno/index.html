Game Description#

On the pirate version ofWorms Ultimate Mayhem all network functions are available for free, allowing you to play online with a friend on the same screen or fight online online with an army of other players. You can either join teams or fight in co-op against a common enemy. Worms: Slaughter offers to take control of an army of well-armed worms, whose goal is to destroy the enemy army. The project changed the two-dimensional perspective to full 3D.

You can create your own weapons and use binoculars to assess the situation. A team has up to 6 units armed with unique guns. You cannot fall into the water, otherwise the character will immediately die. Multiplayer and single-player campaigns are based on turn-based battles, where each player takes turns making a move. The arsenal of weapons consists of bazookas, grenades, sheep, banana bombs and much more. Free online play in Worms Ultimate Mayhem includes customization of matches: number of lives, rounds, weapon damage, speed, etc. Up to 4 people can take part in multiplayer battles. The modes are represented by such competitions as Ultimate Fight, Home, Protecting the Statue and Competition.

Having built a factory, you are allowed to assemble your own barrels. Locations are completely subject to deformation and destruction, allowing objects and the landscapes themselves to explode. There are barrels of oil and mines that can be detonated. Themes of the locations include the Wild West, industrial industry, the Middle Ages, Arab countries and the prehistoric world. The single-player campaign consists of a set of missions united by one storyline. At the center of the story is Professor Chervil and his students traveling through time.
