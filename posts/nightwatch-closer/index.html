Game Description#

Nightwatch: Closer is a first-person horror game that places gamers in a tense, closed environment. The goal of the game is to survive for ten minutes, preventing unknown anomalies from entering a small post. The user is left alone, he has only a flashlight and ingenuity to fight off these threats. The title creates an atmosphere of tension and isolation, forcing users to remain vigilant and resourceful.

Nightwatch: Closer’s mechanics revolve around managing various factors such as psyche level, noise level, room lighting, and time. Anomalies appear randomly, influenced by these elements, requiring gamers to constantly adapt their strategies. Users must use a combination of observation, interrogation and quick reaction to keep the anomalies at bay. The title tests the user’s ability to remain calm under pressure and make quick decisions under high stress.

The scene of action is a dark, narrow post, where the user must hold out until a replacement arrives. The enclosed space heightens the sense of claustrophobia and urgency. The gamer’s only companion, a senior worker, falls asleep, leaving him alone with the anomalies. This scenario increases the tension and immerses the player in a nightmare situation where every second counts. The design keeps gamers on their toes, making the game intense and immersive.
