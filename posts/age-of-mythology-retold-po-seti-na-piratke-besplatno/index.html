Game Description#

Age of Mythology: Retold takes players into a world where myth meets strategy, offering a unique free online experience. This reimagining of the classic game allows you to challenge your friend online. Immerse yourself in legend in online play where you can control mythical monsters and harness the power of the gods, even as a pirate.

The game cannot do without variety. Greek, Norse, Egyptian and Atlantean gods are all in the game, and each brings their own flavor to the battlefield. Want to unleash lightning on your enemies? Forward. Prefer to play cool with nourishing rain for your troops? This is also in the game. This is not just choosing a side - it is creating your own legendary story.

But let’s not forget the real stars of the show: the monsters. We’re talking about centaurs, trolls, mummies - everything under the sun. These are not ordinary units, they are walking, talking pieces of mythology, ready to wreak havoc at your command. And if you thought the one-eyed cyclops was scary, then wait until you see what else is in the game.

The campaign mode in the game is also no slouch. 50 missions scattered across a mythical world will allow you to travel the world faster than Hermes on tea leaves. One minute you are besieging Troy, the next you are fighting giants in Midgard. It’s a whirlwind tour through ancient legends, and you’re at the wheel.
