Game Description#

InMedieval Dynasty you can play online with a friend to build a thriving city together and explore a vast open world. On online pirates you can play with friends from anywhere in the world. Online play allows you to team up with other players to face the harsh conditions of the Middle Ages and the dangers of the wild. One of the main tasks is the fight against hunger and thirst. You will have to hunt wild animals, grow crops and trade resources to ensure your survival. Create tools and weapons that will help you in your endeavors, but do not forget about their wear and tear.

As you progress, you will develop your character and choose a specialization. Chat with NPCs, build relationships and even find love. Complete quests, explore lush landscapes and discover secrets hidden in this world. You are also responsible for managing the village. Create a layout, gather resources and produce goods to support your community. Trade with other settlements to gain new resources and speed up the growth of your village.

Medieval Dynasty offers two extensive maps to explore: The Vale (solo) and Oxbow (solo and co-op). Team up with up to four friends in co-op mode and create a thriving community together. Customize your character’s appearance and go on a unique adventure. Immerse yourself in the dynamic cycle of day and night and the four seasons. Build a variety of structures, from simple shacks to sturdy stone houses, and decorate them with furniture and decorative items. Craft over 300 items, encounter various wild animals, and satisfy your food and water needs.
