Game Description#

InAge of Wonders 4 you can play online as a pirate for free, fighting with a friend or participating in confrontations online online with players from all over the world. The gameplay is a classic 4X strategy with turn-based battles, where competent army command and tactical skills decide. The online game is based on the fact that participants need to create their own powerful kingdom, gather an army and engage in large-scale battles with opposing forces. You can customize the appearance of people, choose culture and magical abilities.

The plot tells about a fantasy world in which magician kings return to take over the world. Together with the hero, you need to learn knowledge from magic books, entering into an unequal battle with divine creatures. The free online play option in Age of Wonders 4 allows you to fight on the side of humans, cannibal halflings or elves. While exploring vast locations, you need to collect magic books to master combat spells. Strength, diplomacy and magic help you choose your play style.

You will need to fight ordinary monsters, siege castles, explore icy wastelands or arid deserts. The fate of the world depends on the decisions made. There is a huge variety of empires with their own unique characteristics and many different warriors fighting with unique weapons. The ruler may return in future matches as an ally or enemy.
