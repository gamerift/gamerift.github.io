Game Description#

Metaverse Keeper is a simplified top-down roguelike. You can play it online with a friend on pirate. Including online on the Internet. Locations are divided into rooms. When visiting a specific room for the first time, users will not be allowed out of it until they have defeated all opponents. Battles with enemies take place in real time.

The possibility of free online play makes the process of destroying enemies faster. The plot of Metaverse Keeper follows the adventures of four heroes from different dimensions. The government called on them for help so that they could find out the cause of the cataclysms occurring in the Metaverse. The adventurers went for answers to the Bastion - a giant spaceship. Warriors can be pumped up, increasing health, stamina, damage and everything like that. With the help of chips, you can improve melee and ranged weapons, enhance talents, and also acquire passive effects. The dungeons here are constantly changing with each new run. The order in which adversaries appear also changes. The network game contains not only online multiplayer, but also local co-op. There are 4 adventurers to choose from: Howard - a scientist and astronaut who can set up teleports and summon energy spheres; Wong - a hacker-programmer who uses the Matrix Shuttle and relies on hand-to-hand combat; Brooks - a rocker musician who can evade enemy attacks (his rolls consume self-replenishing charges); Zoe is a demolition girl who can make a rocket jump and throw bombs at monsters.
