Game Description#

Day Of Infamy dives into the era of World War II. Players will have to take part in online battles over the Internet, complete cooperative missions and much more. On the pirate Day Of Infamy you can play online for free with a friend, taking the side of the US Army, the British Commonwealth and the Wehrmacht. The possibility of free online play allows you to fight in hardcore matches, including 7 multiplayer confrontation modes and 3 cooperative games aimed at playing together.

Participants in military operations will have to fight on fortified bridgeheads, in villages, cities, forests, etc. The online game includes 14 levels, recreated with historical accuracy. More than 60 types of authentic weapons are presented, including Thompson machine guns, PIAT grenade launchers, flamethrowers, FG-42 automatic rifles and other guns, which the developers have transferred with scrupulous precision. Americans, British, Germans, Canadians, Australians, Scots, Indians and African-Americans are taking part in the war.

All barrels are subject to modifications. You can install additional clips, bayonets, belts, under-barrel grenade launchers, etc. During battles, you can request artillery support. The support provides smoke screens, incendiary shells, and can arrange bombing from Unekrs, typhoons or P-47 fighters. By gaining experience points, you can unlock 33 additional units, the 101st Airborne Division, the 2nd Special Forces Battalion and the 1st Fallschirmjager Division.
