Game Description#

October Panic is a platform in an arcade style that requires passing five levels in one continuous session. The plot of the game begins with Rita of the resuscitator, whose occult experiments create a break in reality, flooding the streets of a small city with supernatural entities. This chaotic event releases the Horde of Creation, among which witches, werewolves, mummies, vampires and walking dead.

The player will manage the young collector of sweets named October to pave the way through the captured streets to the old ghost mansion and put an end to the annual curse. To confront the threat, the protagonist in October Panic can find and use 5 different Hallowean masks of monsters that give unique abilities. These artifacts, personifying vampires, mummy, monster, his bride and creature from the lagoon, serve as the main tools for fighting and overcoming obstacles.

Throughout the adventure, Gamer will face more than 30 types of opponents. Enemies include revived pumpkin, skeletons, dead newlyweds, killer maniacs and references to characters from old films, providing a variety of threats in each individual zone. The structure of this project is completely linear and conducts the heroine after five consecutive locations without the possibility of choosing a route. The key feature of gameplay is the absence of a conservation or loading system. Any failure leads to the loss of all progress and the need to start passing from the very first stage.
