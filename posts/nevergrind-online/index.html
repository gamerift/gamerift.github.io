Game Description#

Nevergrind online immerses players in a multi -user role -playing game by dungeons, where the search for treasures and the extermination of monsters come to the fore. Players can climb the career ladder as usual or challenge to the hardcore regime, and all this without microtransactions. The game combines the classic RPG elements with unique turns, creating an experience that stands out from the crowd.

Once in the world of Wandamor, the players find themselves in the Kingdom of Edenburg, the once calm kingdom, which is now besieging the Dark Forces. The game offers a wide selection of options for creating a character: 14 races and 14 classes to choose from. Each combination brings its strengths and weaknesses, allowing you to create various styles of games and team compositions.

In Nevergrind Online, it is customary to unite in commands, although a single game remains an acceptable option for single wolves. Parties up to five players can combine their efforts to solve more complex problems and receive big awards. The game does not drive classes into the framework of hard roles, but encourages experiments and flexibility in the approach to battle and teamwork.

Before plunging into the dungeons, players can explore seven different buildings of Edenburg. Each of them - from a noisy tavern to the Academy of Arcana - serves to prepare adventure seekers for upcoming tests. When the players are equipped and ready for battle, it’s time to go down to the procedurally generated dungeons that are under the kingdom.

Moving along the winding corridors and insidious basements, the players will encounter the menagerie of the monsters - from ordinary orcs to formidable bosses with unique abilities. The Luta system in the game makes the treasures a fascinating treasure, because thousands of objects can be found in it, including magical, rare, typesetting and unique objects. For those who cannot find the perfect sword or shield, a reliable trading system is provided, which allows you to exchange with other players to collect perfect equipment.
