Game Description#

Ember Knights can be played for free online as a pirate, teaming up with a friend locally or online online to fight back against the mad sorcerer Praxis. You have to fight a huge number of demons, arm yourself with a wide variety of weapons. Free online play allows you to trade gear, items, and relics with powerful synergies.

Multiplayer brings together up to four heroes who must go explore the Primary Worlds, fighting all their enemies along the way. Each character plays a role in the adventure. A joint campaign is built on interaction. An evil magician drained all the energy from the Tree of Life, spreading corruption throughout the continent. Legendary warriors must hunt down the villain and rid the world of the curse. Dozens of weapons and armor are available for use. Locations and loot in Ember Knights are randomly generated.

Relics are of great importance, determining the character’s skills and tactics. You can play as an archer, fencer and other classes. Collected items allow you to build a variety of buildings. The enemies are animals, ghosts, sorcerers and other opponents. Alone or together with your partners, you will have to visit the Royal Phalanxes, where you will need to fight the Plague Carriers. Each enemy poses a danger, and the locations consist of a variety of obstacles and traps.
