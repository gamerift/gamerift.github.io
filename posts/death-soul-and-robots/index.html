Game Description#

Death, Soul and Robots is a new exciting game in the Metroidvania genre, which immerses users on an unusual trip on the planet, which is on the verge of disappearance. In the role of the main character - a charming cat - you have to find the soul of planet 42, which is the only chance to save the Earth from imminent destruction. However, you can’t cope with this task alone, because you will meet many companions and opponents on the way. Here is a bright and diverse world, consisting of six different biomes, abounding with unique enemies, complex puzzles and hidden treasures.

In addition, you have to fight with six formidable bosses that will subject your skills and reflexes to the most serious tests. For survival, it is necessary to own seven types of weapons, each of which has its own advantages and disadvantages. The performance of various tasks will open the deeper layers of the narrative and shed light on exciting characters. Death, Soul and Robots is beyond the scope of simple gameplay: it is an exciting story about life, death and the consequences of our choice.

History affects deep issues of morality and makes user think about his actions. It causes a variety of emotions: laughter, tears, thoughts. Even after the end of the game, its influence remains in the hearts and minds of those who have encountered it. This is a mandatory game for those who are looking for a comprehensive experience, which organically combine the action, study, solid solutions and the plot. Go to an unforgettable adventure that will light your imagination and will captivate you from beginning to end.
