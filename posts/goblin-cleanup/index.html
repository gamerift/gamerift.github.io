Game Description#

Goblin Cleanup will turn over the idea of ​​the classic Dungeon Crawler genre. Forget about the extermination of dragons and the plunder of treasures - here you yourself put things in order. Take the role of a professional cleaner of the dungeons, who is instructed to restore order after a group of violent adventure seekers arranged a pogrom here. This is not an ordinary cleaning simulator. Time management is a key moment. You must have time to put the dungeon in order before the next group of heroes arrived. Each level poses unique tasks for you, checking your strategic planning skills and problems solving.

Be inventive - use all the tools available at your disposal to put things in order, organize and, in fact, turn the time back in the chaotic state of the dungeon. Want to go through the game alone? No problem. But it’s always more fun to clean with friends! Combine in cooperative mode and go through the dungeon together. Communication and coordinated efforts - this is the key to obtaining this sparkling stamp about cleanliness. Good news? The environment is constantly changing.

Each level offers a new experience, so you will not get stuck, wiping the same mucus from the same walls. Goblin Cleanup is a sip of fresh air for gamers who are looking for something more than ordinary trips to the dungeons. This is a bizarre turn that will make you experience strategic thinking and cleaning skills. In addition, she offers a laughing look at the consequences of epic quests. So throw a sword and a shield, take a mop and a bucket and get ready for an unforgettable unique experience in passing the dungeons.
