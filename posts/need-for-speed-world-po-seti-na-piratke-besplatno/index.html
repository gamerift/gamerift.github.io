Game Description#

Need for Speed: World is a multiplayer arcade racing game that allows players from all over the world to compete in street races online. You can play pirate for free online with friends or challenge random racers from all over the world. By winning competitions, you can buy new cars and upgrades. The online game Need for Speed ​​World Online features police chases. Participants can play for both sides, participating in races as cops or street racers.

There are many passenger cars and supercars on display. There are both licensed cars and various prototypes. Allows you to customize cars by choosing body type, suspension, window tinting, transmission, brakes, tires, external attributes and much more. Need for Speed: The world is represented by 16 parts manufacturers. Depending on their choice, parameters such as maximum speed, acceleration, handling, etc. change.

The fleet includes such powerful cars as the BMW M3 GTR, Lamborghini Gallardo LP560-4 and Audi R8 4.2 FSI quattro. The possibility of free online play allows participants to compete in a variety of modes, including sprint, circuit races, team chases, meeting point, treasure hunt and other types of races. The city is a mixture of the cities from Most Wanted and Carbon. The project implements a dynamic system of changing days, weather conditions and seasons, which directly affect the gameplay.
