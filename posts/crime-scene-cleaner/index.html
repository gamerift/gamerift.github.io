Game Description#

Crime Scene Cleaner is an unusual simulator in which you clean up traces of a crime. As soon as the customer calls you, and usually this is the mafia boss, you need to rush to the crime scene and wield a brush, a mop and many other tools. If you find yourself in a room full of corpses and blood, pull yourself together and do the job professionally. Don’t give investigators a chance to find the killer. Try not to get caught by the police yourself.

Destroy any evidence that could lead the cops to the trail. Sweep away body parts, pick off dried blood, remove fingerprints, etc. Try to do all this before the police arrive. Make sure the police don’t find anything! Use various devices and gadgets: fingerprint powder, ultraviolet light, acid, thermal imaging, etc.

You need to act as quickly as possible, because the situation is heating up every minute. As soon as you hear the howl of sirens, hurry up, because you need to have time to leave the premises and not fall into the hands of law enforcement officers. As soon as you successfully complete the task, wait for the next order. Features of Crime Scene Cleaner: gameplay that makes you think like an investigator and a killer; many tools to eliminate evidence and blood; intense timed puzzles; competition with rival cleaners; try to find out if you managed to deceive the police; get paid for completing such a dirty job.
