Game Description#

The Karate Kid: Street Rumble is a beat ‘em up title that immerses you in the world of the cult trilogy. Users step into the shoes of characters such as Daniel LaRusso, Mr. Miyagi, Ali Mills and Kumiko as they battle through various levels inspired by the films. The game has a side-scrolling format in which players face waves of enemies, including famous antagonists such as Johnny Lawrence, John Croesus and Terry Silver. The Karate Kid: Street Rumble gameplay is based on martial arts, with players performing powerful combos and signature moves.

At each level, new challenges and opponents await you; in order to move forward, users will have to master the art of karate. There are intense boss fights that test gamers’ skills and strategies, making every encounter memorable. In addition to the single player game, there is a local co-op mode that allows up to four users to join forces. The cooperative aspect enhances the gameplay as gamers can team up to defeat enemies and complete levels together.

The title captures the spirit of the original films, providing a nostalgic trip for fans of the series. The title offers a variety of features, including unlockable characters, special moves, and power-ups. Throughout the game, gamers can collect coins to purchase upgrades and customization options. The title also features a challenging arcade mode in which gamers strive to score as many points as possible.
