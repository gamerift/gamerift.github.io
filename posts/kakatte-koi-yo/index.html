Game Description#

kakatte koi yo! - This is a two -dimensional action platform with the possibility of local multiplayer. This is a real cat’s battle, in which you have to take control of powerful cats and master even more powerful fish to defeat your opponents in a fierce 2D platform battle. This game with charming aesthetics in the Japanese style and irresistibly cute characters is an obligatory game for any cat lover.

Get ready to fight friends in exciting matches 1V1 and 2V2 or in a chaotic free struggle. Three different ways to win: to blow up the enemy’s sanctuary with a powerful bomb, to score glasses, throwing, shooting and throwing fish into the basket, or struggle the enemy in a brutal battle in the forehead - every match is a new opportunity to win. And if you want to hone your skills, single tests temporarily allow you to experience your strength abilities, because you will compete with time to earn the desired golden koi. So what are you waiting for? Take your controller and get ready to open the power of Murlyki!
