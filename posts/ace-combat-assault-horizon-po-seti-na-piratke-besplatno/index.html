Game Description#

Ace Combat: Assault Horizon is an arcade flight simulator that you can play as a pirate with a friend online. Here you can have fun with other users only online via the Internet. In this sequel, combat helicopters were added.

The low barrier to entry into Ace Combat: Assault Horizon and the possibility of free online play allows you to find live opponents for PvP battles or partners for joint development of PvE content. Network play supports up to 16 users in regular multiplayer and up to 3 gamers in cooperative mode. There is a campaign for fans of the single. It has varied gameplay. There you will be given the chance to perform aerobatic maneuvers in a fighter jet, fight in an Apache, sink enemy cruisers and shoot at enemies with a machine gun in the spirit of first-person shooters. The range of available equipment is very diverse. Among the combat aircraft there are even PAK FA and non-existent models armed with gauss cannons. Units here can be repainted.

In this part the setting has changed. If earlier events unfolded in an alternative dimension, now the action takes place in the real world, but according to a fictitious scenario. In the story, the US operation together with the Russians to eliminate rebels in Africa turns into a disaster. After that incident, traitorous and terrorist soldiers take over Russia, gain access to the powerful and deadly Trinity weapon, and create a state called the New Russian Federation. The 108th Squadron Warwolf, led by Colonel Bishop, is trying to retake the country from the militants.
