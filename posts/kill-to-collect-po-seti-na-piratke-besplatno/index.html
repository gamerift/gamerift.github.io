Game Description#

Kill to Collect is a top-down action game that can be played online online, competing with a friend or teaming up to complete a single-player campaign. All multiplayer content is available for free on pirate sites. You can play online or fight on one device. The setting is made in the cyberpunk genre. The free-to-play feature allows you to team up with up to four people to clear futuristic, procedurally generated maps.

Events take place in the city of Geoshelter Alpha. This is the last human stronghold on Earth. In this oppressive world, corporations rule everything. Only bounty hunters can go down into the slums and punish the culprits. Only they protect the lower strata of the population and are able to repel evil corporate officials. There are several hunters to choose from, each with their own unique characteristics. Multiplayer allows you to choose each of them. Kill to Collect Characters: Kate Katana - a female warrior who wields a sword; Ivan Iron Fist - a powerful raver with bionic arms; Riot Ray - a former officer of the Corporation; Shocking Shelly - a girl mechanic who can deploy turrets.

There are 3 modes to choose from: Storyline, Trials and Free Hunt. The first allows you to go through a rich story, full of corporate intrigue and secrets, alone or in a company. The tests consist of 8 floors. Each subsequent level presents a more difficult challenge. Free play allows you to travel around the city without any goal, just fighting random enemies.
