Game Description#

Out of Sight is a project where a young girl, deprived of direct vision, should find a way through the building, temporarily receiving the ability to see through her toy. She turned out to be locked inside and tries to get out at any cost. Those who keep her here are actively looking for her and seek to stop her, creating a constant direct threat of her freedom and life.

In Out of Sight, the gameplay is built around the need to act extremely carefully and carefully, studying the world around him. The player’s vision changes between how the heroine herself perceives weakly, and the picture that her satellite toy provides. Any careless movement, the slightest sound or random contact with the subject can instantly give its location to the pursuers. Each action bears the risk of being caught that will result in an unknown future. In the walls of the building itself there is a story about what happened here long before its appearance.

Studying the rooms, finding hidden passages and abandoned objects, you can gradually find hints about the horrors of the past. These findings show how the house has become a place marked with suffering and evil. She has to collect these separate fragments in order to reveal the circumstances of her imprisonment. To move on and stay alive, it is important to evaluate where the danger is threatened and to use the environment to hide from those who are looking for. Sometimes it is difficult to understand what is real and what it seems, since perception can deceive, making survival tasks more difficult and causing psychological discomfort.
