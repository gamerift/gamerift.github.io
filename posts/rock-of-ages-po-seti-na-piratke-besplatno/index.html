Game Description#

Rock Of Ages not only gives you the opportunity to play online with a friend on a pirate, but also allows you to feel the effect of combining elements of different genres. Gamers will be able to travel through different time periods, encountering different historical figures. The idea is simple - destroy the enemy castle before the enemy himself goes on the offensive. The main weapon is a huge boulder, which must be aimed at enemy buildings. But at the same time, you should be on alert, because the enemy has the same one, and he will definitely use it. If, after a short test with bots, the result is satisfactory, you can move on to online battles over the Internet.

As mentioned earlier, the Stone of Ages project touches on different time periods, ranging from the times of Ancient Greece to the Renaissance. When gamers move to the next time period, they are greeted by a cut-scene where a significant historical figure (for example, Leonardo Da Vinci) makes an introductory speech. After this, the network game transfers the battle participants to the map. The essence of the battle is to direct the stone to the target without losing all health points. Yes, the stone here has a scale responsible for durability, and if it is empty before it hits the enemy fortress, the round will be lost. Protective elements will be turrets, mini-temples, rock fragments and other elements located in the direction of travel. Depending on their size, collisions will cause a certain amount of damage, and the remaining units will be converted into damage dealt to the main building. Thus the battle takes place, and the one who can eliminate the general first will be the winner. The possibility of free online play allows you to independently build fortifications to complicate the game for your opponent, preventing you from easily reaching the goal.
