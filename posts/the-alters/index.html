Game Description#

The Alters is a science fiction adventure that immerses in the world of choice and consequences. Imagine yourself stuck on an unfriendly planet, where there is no one to rely on, except for your own ingenuity. This is the intriguing plan of this innovative game, which immerses in the world of parallel lives and alternative realities. Going on this journey, you take on the role of Jan Dolsky, a worker who made an emergency landing on a foreign world and is now facing the problem of survival and salvation.

To cope with this dangerous situation, Jan creates clones of himself, known as “alter”. They have individuality and history, which is influenced by decisions that he made or could make GG throughout their lives. It is necessary to manage relations between these doubles that have various skills, goals and emotions. Switching between alter allows you to use their unique abilities to solve puzzles, overcoming obstacles and fighting opponents. You can also interact with them, plunging into their individual stories and influencing decisions.

It is extremely important to approach each action with caution, since the choice will have far -reaching consequences for both you and the changed ones. The Alters goes beyond the traditional game experience, serving as a deep study of personality, choice and fate. She encourages players to think about what could happen, they make other decisions in their lives, and how they could affect their present. Self -analysis is also encouraged, offering players to identify similarities and differences among themselves and doubles, ultimately asking the question of what really determines their uniqueness.
