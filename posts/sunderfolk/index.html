Game Description#

Sunderfolk is a cooperative role -playing adventure designed for joint passage on one screen. This tactical adventure is designed for a team of up to 4 people traveling together around the Sunderlands world. Title uses personal gadgets as the main interface for gamer commands and information management, instead of familiar controllers. A special program provides this connection by collecting all functions on the display of each smartphone. The company is going to overcome trials in the general virtual world.

The interaction begins with scanning the QR code on the screen that launches the installation of the desired program available for iOS and Android platforms. After installation, smartphones or tablets become full -fledged control panels for Sunderfolk. Gadgets show the actions of the characters, help to monitor the inventory, demonstrate cards on their hands and contain a set of rules, which facilitates development for beginners. A similar approach seeks to recreate the atmosphere of board gaming sessions, where everything is at hand for everyone.

It is necessary to choose one of the six archetypes of heroes to form a detachment for adventure. There is an arcanist, bard, berserker, pyromancer, ranger and robber to choose from, each class offers its own battle style and performs special tasks in the team. The abilities of the heroes are revealed through the collected maps of skills that determine available actions on the go. Amulets and weapons make it possible to configure the assembly of characters between tasks, allowing you to strategically prepare for future challenges, taking into account the alleged opponents.
