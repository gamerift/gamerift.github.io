Game Description#

CraftMATION 101: Programming and Craft offers to gamers to be on an unknown planet, in the company of the right robot and many mechanisms that will help you make it viable. You will find endless opportunities for the extraction and combination of resources, programming robots, creating tools and terraforming the planet. You can create your own army of robots that will work for you, collect resources, perform tasks and even create new robots. Visual programming will allow you to configure all processes and automate many tasks.

But do not forget that you are not alone on this planet. Here you will encounter many dangers and obstacles that may interfere with your mission. Be careful and do not forget to feed your robots - although you can program them for auto -education. Craftomation 101: Programming & Craft is a game that gives you complete freedom for creativity and experiments. Here you can become a real genius of automation and transform the icy planet into a viable world.
