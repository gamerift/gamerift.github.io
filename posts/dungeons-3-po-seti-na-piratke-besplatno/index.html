Game Description#

On the pirateDungeons 3 you can arrange battles with a friend for free, playing online or massively compete with other players online online. It is necessary to take control of the Dungeon Lord, who has united the forces of darkness in order to destroy the entire human race. The possibility of free online play includes the creation of an evil empire, or confrontation between factions for the right to individually rule the kingdom.

The gameplay of Dungeons 3 can be described as a dark simulator. You need to hire minions, sow evil and expand your dark domain. The single-player campaign consists of 20 unique missions united by a common storyline. Ruler Talia will have to control the army of demons. She is ready to crush everyone who gets in her way! It is necessary to obtain resources, build a base, train troops and fight with unbearable people. Network play allows you to take part in cooperative play.

There is a random generation system that creates new locations, unique events, etc. You can create your own kingdom of evil by hiring orcs, succubi, zombies and many other monsters. The main enemies are sappy heroes in shining armor. This is the case when the story definitely does not end with a happy ending, where the fair prince saves the princess. This prince knows pain and is doomed to eternal torment. May hell come to earth!
