Game Description#

In Planetary Life, you will find yourself in the role of a planetary architect. In this Simulator, you get divine control over the barren world. Create mountains and cut the valleys, erupt volcanic rage or create gentle slopes. Create the perfect environment: adjust the heat of the nuclei, work with gases in the atmosphere and control precipitation.

Planetary life is not only the formation of land. You will also become a shepherd of life itself. Get unicellular organisms and observe their evolution into complex creatures. Will you push their development or allow them to fight for survival in the Darwin struggle? The choice is yours. But “planetary life” is not only sunlight and rainbow. You can also play the role of space tricster. Strengthen volcanic activity and observe how the continents crack. Arrange pouring rains and drown the nascent life forms. In the end, sometimes the most interesting stories are associated with metaphorical observation of how everything burns.

Increase the temperature of the nucleus and tectonic activity. Feel in the tropics? Set down precipitation, cloud cover and surface reflection to create a paradise beach. Planetary Life offers a unique mixture of creation and destruction, giving you the opportunity to build a flourishing ecosystem or arrange a planetary apocalypse. So will you become an architect of life or a harbinger of death? The decision is in your hands.
