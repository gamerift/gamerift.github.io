Game Description#

Everafter Falls combines a heating simulator with elements of role -playing adventure. Gamers grow crops, bred animals and catch fish in a serene rural area. However, there is a more gloomy background. Terrible creatures that require combat skill live in the dungeons. A unique development system replaces traditional experience with mysterious cards. Gamers feed these cards to a mysterious creature, contributing to the growth of the character. The project encourages cooperation.

A multiplayer adventure with a separate screen allows friends to share the experience of housekeeping and adventure. A faithful pet helps users perform various tasks. The world boils with life. Bizarre residents inhabit the city, each of them has its own story. Relations over time become deeper, affecting the gameplay. Everafter Falls offers a captivating mixture of calm and excitement. Gamers balance between peaceful agriculture and exciting battles. The card development system adds an intriguing layer of secrets. Social interaction and cooperative game enhance the overall impression.

The world seems alive. Dynamic weather conditions affect the gameplay. The seasons replace each other by changing the growth of the crop and accessibility of resources. The cycles of day and nights affect activity. Users effectively manage the time to achieve maximum productivity. In addition to conducting agriculture and battles, players explore the world. Hidden secrets and treasures are waiting in the wings. Fishing allows you to relax and get a reward. Craft allows users to create useful items. The project encourages experiments and creativity.
