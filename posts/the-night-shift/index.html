Game Description#

The Night Shift is a first-person psychological horror game that immerses you in a nightmarish work shift. Inside a department store locked down due to a storm, the only rule of survival is constant vigilance, as deadly mannequins change their position as soon as you look away from them. The hero, a newly hired employee, finds himself trapped on his first night. The storm outside activates the emergency shutters, cutting off his escape and leaving him alone with the animated dolls in the empty shopping malls.

The gameplay keeps you on the edge of your seat as you move through spacious trading floors, claustrophobic service tunnels and creepy staff rooms. To survive the night and get paid, you must complete a list of tasks given by the manager, using only a flashlight and your own wits. In The Night Shift, each shift is generated with random events, which forces you to find new paths and adapt to changing threats.

The surroundings are gradually transformed, and the once clean premises are covered with dirt and signs of decay, hinting at an ominous secret hidden within the walls of the store. The answer to what is happening lies in the details: to survive, you have to search the environment in search of keys and useful items that will help in the confrontation with an inexplicable evil. The narrative develops through notes and environmental elements, revealing the truth about the mannequins and the department store itself. The story has several possible endings, depending on the actions and decisions made during this terrible adventure, where the main goal is to survive until the morning when the store can open again.
