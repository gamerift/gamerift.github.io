Game Description#

Batman: Arkham City is an action-adventure game with a third-person view, based on one of the most popular heroes of comics, cartoons, films and toys, Batman. This project is an expanded edition of the original game. The media product was developed by Rocksteady Studios. The official release took place in early autumn 2012.

The game world is five times larger in area than in the previous Batman: Arkham Asylum. The huge locations of the darkest of DC Comics cities terrify ordinary citizens, as power in the city increasingly passes into the hands of various kinds of looters, murderers and psychopaths. A huge number of gangs are controlled by their leaders - Penguin, Mister Freeze, Joker and other monsters in human form. Batman sees his own symbol illuminated in a spotlight in the stormy night sky, and this means only one thing - the city needs him. He sets out to restore order on the streets of Gotham City. This time, he won’t go into battle alone: ​​the developers have added the opportunity to play as Catwoman, the sworn enemy of the “Bat,” however, this time, the irreconcilable enemies have formed an alliance.

The gameplay for Batman and Catwoman is quite different. Bruce Wayne, in the guise of his alter ego, is able to literally soar over the city like a kite, looking for the next victim who has crossed the line of what is permitted. He fights using both his powerful physical strength and the use of special gadgets, while the “Cat” relies mainly on his instincts and agility. Among the innovations, it is worth noting a smoke grenade to disorient enemies, and a new detective mode. Separately, the developers highlighted 400 new achievements! The stunning graphics have improved noticeably since the release of the previous part, the game world has become several times larger, the artificial intelligence has become smarter, and the non-trivial plot has prepared several non-obvious surprises for you!
