Game Description#

Through the Ages is a digital adaptation of the board game of the same name, which offers the opportunity to play online for free with a friend or fight in multiplayer battles with other players online. Like most strategies, you will need to start development from a small village. By collecting resources, expanding land and exploring nearby mines, the technological foundations for the further development of the settlement are laid.

Introduced the possibility of free online play and a single player campaign. The online game Through the Ages is aimed at confrontation between participants. It is required to build up military power and show your political skills to your opponent. The task is simple - to lead your civilization to greatness. About a hundred cards are presented that allow you to build a powerful civilization. The deck determines the abilities of both the squad and the entire state.

To achieve victory, you can take many paths. One of the most important mechanics is to competently manage and manage resources. By correctly distributing funds and increasing military power, you can defeat any enemy army. The rules completely repeat the original board game. In the single-player campaign, you will have to go through many challenges of varying degrees of difficulty. Collecting cards unlocks new abilities and tactical options, allowing you to build a more effective strategy against your opponent.
