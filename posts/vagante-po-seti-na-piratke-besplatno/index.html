Game Description#

Vagante is a dark platformer that you can play online with a friend, fighting a huge variety of enemies on the Internet online or locally for free. The gameplay is a hardcore sidescorer with permanent death mechanics. The levels are randomly generated and offer a set of unique challenges and fight powerful bosses.

Mechanics allow you to quickly move around arenas, jump, parry enemy attacks, etc. Vagante’s gameplay is inspired by classic roguelikes with procedurally generated elements. The online game offers you to go through difficult tests with your partners, descending into the depths of dark dungeons. The plot is based on the heroes seeking fame, fortune and power. To do this, they are looking for a legendary dungeon in which they can find countless treasures. However, a huge number of obstacles and challenges await them along the way.

The possibility of free online play offers each participant to choose a unique class and appearance parameters. You can also set a backstory, which has an impact on some of the hero’s basic characteristics. As you progress through levels, you can enchant objects and upgrade abilities. Each of the four areas has a different visual style and contains a variety of enemies or bosses. You can also unlock additional classes, levels, music and more in a special Book of Secrets.
