Game Description#

Type-NOISE: Shonen Shojo takes place in a familiar but altered version of Tokyo, where six teenagers who have lost their memories gather. Locked in the city, they are forced to wander through bizarre labyrinths in search of a way out. The only way forward lies through the restoration of lost memories, revealing the sealed pages of the past. The main goal is not just an escape from physical captivity, but a meeting with the real you, forgotten for a good reason.

Progress through the story of Type-NOISE: Shonen Shojo occurs through solving a variety of point-and-click puzzles. Players will face more than 40 different challenges, from searching for cause-and-effect relationships to complex logic problems. Some situations develop into a kind of judicial debate, requiring the building of arguments and the presentation of evidence. All these obstacles are organically woven into the narrative, forcing you to constantly analyze the situation and collect evidence.

The plot explores the emotional inhibitions hidden within each of the six characters, offering separate stories for all. These internal conflicts are reflected in surreal locations - labyrinths born of mental trauma, with upside-down puppets and distorted shadows. By making decisions and discovering the truth, the player guides the group towards one of several endings. The entire game is laced with themes of jealousy, pride, and anger as the characters try to heal from the wounds of their past.
