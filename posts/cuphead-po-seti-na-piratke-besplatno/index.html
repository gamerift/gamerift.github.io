Game Description#

Cuphead is a two-dimensional gun-n-run game that allows you to play for free on a pirate online with a friend. You can use a local network or go through the campaign online online. The visual part is made in a cartoon style. Everything from the visual style to the animations harks back to early 20th century cartoons. At the center of the story is a cup-headed character who must defeat numerous bosses along the way, each with unique patterns of movements and attacks. Apart from the leaders, there are no more opponents in the game. The story begins with Chashek losing a bet with the devil and now he must pay him back.

A branching sequence of levels is presented. You can fight enemies in a random order, but some will have to be accessed as you progress. The network game is represented by a cooperative mode, where Mugman (mug man) joins the hero in order to together overcome all the enemies on his way. Enemies stand out with incredible strength and have several phases during battle. The possibility of free online play includes an unlimited number of restarts. At the same time, characters do not lose their weapons when they die. It is only important to memorize the timings and study the features of the bosses. In Cuphead, leveling up your abilities is simple.

The protagonist is able to parry attacks from opponents. Cuphead can also hit various objects coded in pink. A successful parry fills a special meter, allowing you to perform an enhanced attack or use a special ability. Levels are selected through a global map called the Outer World. There are several paths along which the player moves, finding his way to this or that enemy. There are hidden paths that provide access to secrets.
