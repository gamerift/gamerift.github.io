Game Description#

Forever skies is an atypical survival in the world of post -apocalypse, where the player will become the captain of the airship. Unfortunately, the protagonist is the only crew member, but even this does not reduce the number of his problems. After all, he still needs food, which is practically no in the air with the exception of rare birds. And the technique tends to break, and it requires constantly replacing old details.

Humanity destroyed the planet with his own hands. Without listening to the words of scientists about a potential environmental disaster, non -excess consumption of natural resources and emissions of toxic substances into the air led to the formation of toxic red dust and storms. They covered almost all countries and cities, destroying most of the population. Everything around was painted in the color of rust, including clouds. Then rescue airships were built, which would raise people up, where you can still breathe. But the scraps between themselves and the struggle for power did not allow everyone to escape and most of the vehicles fell. The protagonist is one of the rare survivors who managed to raise the device into the sky and get a chance to live.

The lower world Forever Skyce is full of riddles and dangers. Without people, the whole territory captured Flora and Faun, mutating under the influence of toxins and turning into aggressive strong creatures that are dangerous to humans. But at any case, at times, you will have to find products, water or other details. Of course, there are buildings that specially erected above the clouds in the hope of escaping, but they are not endless and they can disassemble them all in a short period to improve your home.
