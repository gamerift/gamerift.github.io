Game Description#

Ribby: The Game - this is not a typical role -playing adventure. Forget about the extermination of dragons or collecting a million gold coins. Here is the hero - Ribbie, a kind girl, who has lost and seeking the house. Title combines elements of role -playing games and visual novels, creating a unique experience. Of course, the main goal is to return Ribbie home, but this is only the top of Lilia. This bizarre adventure plunges deeper, exploring the themes of self -knowledge and friendship. Along the way, Ribbi meets a galaxy of bizarre characters, each of which has its own baggage and hidden stories. Talking mushrooms and mysterious frogs - this world is full of eccentrics.

The journey will not be sunny and rainbow. Forests, swamps and strange landscapes become a backdrop to fight Ribbie with a sense of abandonment and anxiety. The game is not afraid to be real, prompting gamers to face their own fears and uncertainty. This is a chance to look inside yourself, using the story of Ribbie as a mirror for thinking about yourself. Sometimes the most difficult battles do not occur on swords, but inside. Forget about bizarre schedule and epic battles. Ribby: The Game is focused on significant connections and the choice you make. Conversations have weight, and users decide how Ribbie will react. Will it be playful, will it remain calm, or maybe even climb to kiss? The emphasis is on the adoption of vulnerability and truthfulness in relation to itself.
