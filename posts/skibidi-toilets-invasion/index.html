Game Description#

Skibidi Toilets: Invasion throws you into a whirlwind of cyborg toilet chaos. Play as a secret agent armed with an arsenal of weapons tasked with quelling a toilet rebellion. Unleash your fury in seven diverse environments, from desolate cityscapes to chilling graveyards, each presenting unique strategic challenges. Welcome to a world where porcelain turns into brawl. This exciting FPS will pit you against rebel toilets, each of which has more firepower than a regular toilet plunger. Unleash your inner action hero across seven diverse maps, from eerie cityscapes to eerie graveyards.

Navigate crumbling streets, make your way through eerie cemeteries, explore abandoned factories and dive into secret underground bunkers as you wage war against the porcelain menace. Choose from a varied arsenal of firearms and experimental gadgets, each with their own modifications and upgrade paths. Upgrade your character to become a fast and powerful agent, unlocking special abilities that let you dominate the battlefield. Build a strategy, hone your skills, and prepare for the final showdown with the Skibidi rebels. Will you prevail in this bizarre bathroom brawl, or will the world succumb to a porcelain uprising?
