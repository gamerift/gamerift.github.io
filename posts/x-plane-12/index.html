Game Description#

X -Plane 12 is a realistic air system that allows you to fly around the world on a variety of aircraft. Each type of transport is represented by unique characteristics and individual control panels. Professional pilots took part in the development of X-Plane 12, which allows gameplay to transfer real experience from air management. Airplanes, helicopters, gliders, etc. are available for flights.

The elaboration of not only aircraft is released, but also the environment. The game presents a high detail of water, a wide variety of trees, a change in weather conditions and seasons. You can sit for the helm of such aircraft as Cessna Citation X, Cirrus SR22, GRUMMAN F-14 TOMCAT and many others. Each type of transport allows you to experience absolutely different experience from piloting by transport.

In total, about 17,000 airports around the world are available, including Bella Kula, Portland, San Francisco, etc. The main difference from other aircraft systems is that the gameplay is completely based on the theory of the blade element. This allows you to correctly evaluate the behavior of the propeller. Thus, each individual suburent of the blade or wing is calculated individually, thereby influencing aerodynamic force. The gameplay is based on physical quantities, such as geometry, mass, engine location, its characteristics, etc. Earth relief is calculated using SRTM.
