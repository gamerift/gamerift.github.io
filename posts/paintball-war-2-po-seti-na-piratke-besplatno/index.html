Game Description#

On the piratePaintball War 2 you can play for free online with a friend, or compete online online with other paintball fans. The gameplay is a competitive simulator where you need to fight for the title of champions of the popular paintball discipline. Online play allows you to split into teams, where it is important to interact with your teammates to develop tactics against enemies.

Actions take place in both realistic and fictional grounds. The locations are interactive arenas. Levels can consist of several floors and even go underground. The possibility of free online play in Paintball War 2 allows you to take part in modes such as Best Player, Target Capture, Target Elimination, Point Capture, Arms Race and simply compete in a classic paintball match. All modes set different goals for participants to determine the winners.

A wide selection of weapons is provided. Each participant chooses his own tactics and strategy. You can use automatic weapons, pistols, knives and grenades. A clan system is available, allowing players to join associations and fight against enemy factions. By receiving experience points, you can buy skins, weapons and much more. There are rare items. The rating table is taken into account both for an individual player and for the entire clan.
