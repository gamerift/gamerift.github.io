Game Description#

Together with a friend on the pirateICBM you can start a real thermonuclear war. Allows you to play online, carrying out real nuclear bombings all over the planet. Having connected online for free, everyone must choose the country whose interests they are going to represent. Numerous technologies are available for research, allowing the development of advanced warheads capable of destroying half of life on Earth.

Players develop parallel to each other, trying to outstrip their opponents in terms of the number of weapons. Using the opportunity of a free online game, you need to capture as many territories as possible across continents, planting your ideology. Developing nuclear potential and establishing bases throughout the planet, you will either be the first to strike the enemy, or build a weapon capable of reflecting enemy missiles. The game lasts until one of the players is wiped off the face of the earth.

The ICBM gameplay allows you to develop one of two branches: nuclear potential or high technology. The online game is aimed at confronting ideologies. No matter which way the participants go, only one can win. Procedural event generation is in effect. A huge library of weapons is presented: intercontinental warheads, 50 megaton bombs, missile submarines and many other weapons. In total, 20 types of military equipment, 12 types of missiles and 44 advanced technologies are presented.

Allows you to adjust the speed of the game. Diplomacy mechanics are available to gain the support of allies. You can share technologies and useful information with them. Having united against a common enemy, you need to try to strike at the most unexpected moment for your opponent. The gameplay is based on micromanagement. It is necessary to develop a plan, build bases, develop weapons, select targets for a massive strike, and much more.
