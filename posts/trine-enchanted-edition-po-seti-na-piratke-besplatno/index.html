Game Description#

Trine Enchanted Edition is an adventure platformer with puzzle elements that allows you to play online with a friend on a pirate for free. Each player can take control of one of three heroes. The network game is designed for 3 participants. Each character has unique abilities. You need to combine skills and help your teammates to overcome obstacles and solve spatial puzzles.

Trine’s gameplay is entirely based on interaction with objects and physics. The heroes go through many colorful locations: castles with strange mechanisms, dense forests, majestic cities and dark dungeons. The characters are connected by a special device called Train. During the passage you will have to fight the undead and various phantasmagoric monsters.

Each character represents a specific class: Wizard, Knight and Thief. Game mechanics allow players to interact with each other, where each character is assigned a specific role in the group depending on his abilities. For example, the Knight is very strong, the Thief is dexterous, and the Wizard can create objects. The Enchanted Edition includes: improved graphics and physics; free online play; saving at any point in the adventure; additional content and levels.
