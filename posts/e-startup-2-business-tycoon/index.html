Game Description#

** E-Startup 2: Business Tycoon ** throws you headlong into the world of product management. Development, production, sale - all this is on your conscience. Fasten the belts because you will also have to juggle with resources, monitor the constantly changing market and make sure that your office does not turn into a productivity cemetery. Do you think you have the next big thing? Title will allow you to bring your concept from a sketch on a napkin to a store shelf.

But this is not just a dazzling idea. The balance between the design, cost and desires of customers is the key to attracting attention. In the end, a product that looks like a million bucks, but there is a leg and leg, it will not be open from the shelves. The market here is not a simple walk. This is a lively, breathing beast that throws crooked balls in your direction. Be on the alert, adapt your strategy and ahead of competitors until they have taken up your customers.

It is very important to keep your hand on the pulse of trends - miss the moment, and your product may be dust in the basket. E-Startup 2: Business Tycoon is not limited to the product alone. You are also an office sheriff responsible for creating a space that stimulates creative potential and makes your employees work with full return. The hiring of the right people and maintaining their motivation can be the difference between the boom and collapse.
