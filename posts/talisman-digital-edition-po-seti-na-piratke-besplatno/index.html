Game Description#

Talisman Digital Edition provides the opportunity to play online with a friend, competing with each other on a pirate game for free. You can participate in various multiplayer competitions on the Internet online or on a shared screen. The project is a licensed digital version of the board game of the same name. The setting is made in the fantasy genre. The mechanics contain elements of classic board games and strategy games.

An important aspect of the gameplay in Talisman Digital Edition is decision making. You need to roll the dice and move around the map. The possibility of free online play offers you to go on a journey with one of 14 heroes. Classes are divided into warrior, assassin, thief or wizard. Each character has its own strengths and weaknesses. To win, you need to find a way to overtake all your opponents in order to be the first to get to the center of the map. The one who reaches the center before the rest receives the Crown of the Overlord. Mechanics and adventure features: single player campaign and online play; rules based on the 4th edition of the board game; many expansions: Reaper, Frost March, Highlands, Sacred Reserve, Underworld, Blood Moon, Firelands, etc.; multiplayer up to 6 people; modification of competitions and adding rules; interactive training and full-fledged guidance; a journal of moves and a table of records; increasing the level thanks to special Runes.
