Game Description#

On the pirateShards of Feyra you can play online for free with a friend, fighting in tactical battles online online. Players take control of two heroes as they explore mystical dungeons to save Feyre’s kingdom. The possibility of free online play allows you to team up with a partner to jointly destroy all the enemies in your path.

The setting is represented by a fantasy world. Online play and single player mode available. Once in the ancient catacombs, you need to complete a variety of quests and overcome difficult challenges, increasing your ranking on the leaderboard. The difficulty is adjusted and adjusted to each player individually. It takes up to two hours of real time to complete the campaign. Shards of Feyra has a high degree of replayability due to the variety of classes and unique character customization.

Starting a new journey, you have to choose strategy and tactics. Each location in the game is unique in its own way and contains a huge number of secret rooms, hiding places, etc. The combat system is flexible and contains a number of individual settings. Battles take place in a step-by-step mode. The main emphasis of the gameplay is on exploring locations and tactical battles. There are two unique campaigns available. Each of them offers special adventures in which you will learn the secrets of Feyre’s fictional world.
