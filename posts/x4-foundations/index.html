Game Description#

In X4: Foundations you can create any spaceship, and then sit down for its helm and start to smash the enemies right and left. The first -person shooter with RPG and sandboxes combines several temporary eras enclosed in a couple of hours of game, but it is impossible to enjoy the gameplay of this project even in a week.

You can construct ships of different sizes and goals. It can be small scouts and large ships, wearing several hundred combat birds on their huge backs. Management is possible both from the first and third -person. Since you play the role of the commander of the space base, in your authority simple walks around the station, replacing the pilot on any flotilla, the return of orders and much more.

During the game, you will trade resources received from the colonies, or fight with opposite fractions. Both of them will bring you money that you can spend on the study of new ships. Also, your character may become one of the few who will affect the inter -server economy. You can build a new station with the money received and add updated residential complexes, docks, various modules there, and later combine residential modules and military, and get a full -fledged barracks. Buildings are improved on the basis of combination.

You can put new details in any ship, and they are also studied for the game currency. For example, you are building a new spy bomber with missiles, and now you are available to the supersonic engine of the new model, as well as a rocket launcher, shooting at a speed of 400 units per minute. And there are many more opportunities, from teleportation to the development of civilization to such a level as to completely retire, leaving his assistant, and to engage in production, trade, inventing laws or war.
