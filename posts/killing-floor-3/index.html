Game Description#

Killing Floor 3 throws you headlong into the gloomy 2091. The shadow megacorporation created a monstrous army of bioenginery creatures entitled “Zeda”. You join NightFall, a rebel group with a serious mentality to “kill with fire”. As a specialist NightFall, you will unite with four other gamers to destroy these Zedov in a tireless struggle for survival. This is not a single riot. In Killing Floor 3, the main thing is to work in a team and achieve success. Levels force you and your detachment to develop a strategy and work together.

Has a success and you will receive money (intra -game money) and experience points. They open new skills and a whole arsenal of weapons, which can be used against the threat of Zedov. Beauty lies in diversity. For each style of the game there is its own weapon - from shotgun, turning the zedes into a piece of salsa, to powerful rifles for distant strikes. Experimenting is a key point. Finding the perfect weapon for yourself and your team is half the pleasure, and the second half is to put it into practice in a magnificent explosion of evaporating chaos.

So if you are looking for a tense cooperative experience, in which special attention is paid to teamwork and strategic thinking, the Title may turn out to be just what you need. Equip, take your detachment and get ready to make your way through the stream of Zedov. Remember that teamwork helps to fulfill a dream, especially if this dream is associated with survival in the conditions of the monsters Apocalypse.
