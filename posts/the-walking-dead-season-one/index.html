Game Description#

The Walking Dead: Season One is an interactive story divided into 6 episodes with elements of a point-n-click adventure and with QTEs in action scenes, telling the story of Lee Everett, a prisoner who was being transported in a police station when the zombie apocalypse began. car to another place of serving his sentence, but due to an attack on the vehicle by the dead, he managed to escape. Once free, he meets a little eight-year-old girl named Clementine, who remains completely alone during this difficult time. The protagonist will take care of her throughout the entire passage.

In the story, in addition to the characters developed specifically for this project, there will also appear characters that appeared in the original source, the printed comic book. Among them will be Glenn, Sean and Hershel, as well as several others. With the help of high-quality voice acting and well-developed characters, similar in character to real people, a certain attachment is achieved, which makes it more difficult to bear their loss. And there will be quite a lot of deaths during the plot of The Walking Dead: The Game, because the world after the apocalypse is very harsh, and the crowds of zombies are becoming larger. Many events, relationships between group members and the general mood are directly influenced by the gamer with his choices of dialogues or options for further actions. A total of 5 episodes and an epilogue are presented, divided into 7-8 chapters: First – “New Life” Second – “Thirst for Help” Third – “On a Long Journey” Fourth – “Around Every Corner” Fifth – “Time is Running Out” “Epilogue - “400 days”

The epilogue follows 6 different characters under the player’s guidance, and the story itself follows another group of survivors present in the second season.
