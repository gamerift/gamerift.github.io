Game Description#

In the sci-fi adventureI Am Not a Monster First Contact, you can play online with a friend, going through a fascinating plot for free on a pirate. You can play locally or online online, exploring a tourist starship called the Albatross. The online game offers you to engage in battle with alien organisms, saving station passengers.

The main opponents are the lizard race. They live in the far reaches of the galaxy. Aliens, upon contact with people, are capable of turning the latter into their own kind. You will need to save as many people as possible, preventing the lizards from carrying out their plan to capture Albatross. The gameplay of I Am Not a Monster First Contact is a tactical turn-based strategy game. You have to make decisions during the battle, trying to choose the most effective tactics against your enemies. Free online play allows you to play through the story mode with a partner.

The campaign consists of 30 unique missions. There are 8 characters to choose from with their own individual abilities. You can control several heroes at the same time. It is necessary to rescue passengers, while trying to find out the secret of the appearance of alien organisms on board. The protagonist of the story is Captain Laser. He plays a vital role in events. The people on board consist of VIPs, tourists and crew members. By rescuing them, various bonuses are awarded that are necessary to unlock new skills and abilities.
