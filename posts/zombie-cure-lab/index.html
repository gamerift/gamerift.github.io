Game Description#

Zombie Cure Lab - this is an opportunity to plunge into a colorful sandbox-laboratory, in which gamers have to put a variety of experiments on zombies. Scientists find a way to begin to control monsters that will be able to turn into hybrid creatures, half-dead people. Now you have to find a full medicine to calm and cure all the injured humanity from the disease of zombie. Users will build walls and fences to protect complexes with scientists. It is necessary to actively study technologies and methods, which help to achieve the right recipe for antidote.

Gamers in the zombie Kur Lab have to collect a variety of resources, give instructions to their subordinates, as well as manage 4 types of available units. It will be possible to arrange priorities and various tasks in the laboratory in order to increase efficiency in finding means for the treatment of zombies, which constantly attack scientific fortifications. We have to take into account the possibility of repeated viral outbreaks, which are able to get even to researchers and doctors. Users need to unlock many technologies (more than 200), as well as pump and expand the layout of the research complex. It will turn out to strengthen the shelter using modern equipment and various defensive stations. It is necessary to monitor the mood and well-being of employees, as well as suppress outbreaks of a zombie virus.
