Game Description#

On the pirate gameKingdom Two Crowns you can play online with a friend, going through a joint campaign on the same screen or using an Internet connection online. Two-dimensional travel allows you to build your kingdom to protect yourself from enemies in the form of the horde of Greed. The gameplay contains elements of action, strategy and tower defense. Having jumped on the back of a faithful steed, you need to go on a journey in which you will have to find supporters in order to build the walls of the palace together. The Army of Greed is advancing, so you need to act as quickly as possible.

The online game allows you to take control of monarchs who have only one goal - to save their kingdom. The borders are under attack by enemies, so there is no time to hesitate. It is required to quickly recruit everyone who is ready to confront opponents. Free online play includes cooperative exploration of picturesque landscapes, discovering new species of animals, secrets, artifacts, units, etc. The lands of the kingdom keep many secrets. 2 Kingdom Two Crowns modes are presented: Dead Lands - the gameplay is built on the principle of metroidvania, in particular, Bloodstained, from where some characters migrated, including Miriam. You can open several horses: a giant beetle, an undead horse, a demonic Gamiijin, etc.; Shogunate - exploration of new lands reminiscent of feudal Japan. Allows you to become a warrior who can gather deadly ninjas around him, traveling around the world on a cylinium. The task is to write a new treatise by destroying Greed from the bamboo groves.

A co-op mode is available, allowing you to go through the story campaign with a friend. You can play online or locally in split screen. In this case, the gameplay is not interrupted if one of the participants cannot continue the adventure. The project is based on the traditions of the European Middle Ages, but introduces elements of phantasmagoria.
