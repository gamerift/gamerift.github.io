Game Description#

Resonant Dusk is the story of the cursed Baritone Mansion, where shadowy entities known as Resonants are trapped within its walls. These echoes of the people who once lived here suffer from unresolved mysteries that chain them to the mortal world. The fate of each spirit is in the hands of detective Lucas Asher, who arrived at the estate after receiving a mysterious message from an old friend. It is he who will have to decide whether the restless souls will find salvation or will be doomed to eternal torment.

Navigation through the gloomy halls and secret rooms of the mansion is carried out using fixed camera angles and classic controls, which enhances the oppressive atmosphere of the project. While exploring locations, gamers will have to interact with Resonants to reveal their secrets. To advance through the story of Resonant Dusk, you need to use found objects and your own wits to solve puzzles that keep the ghosts at bay. Every decision you make directly impacts the narrative, revealing one of several possible endings to this gothic tale of the dark side of human nature.

Uncover secrets, shape destinies and accept consequences:

Uncover the tragic stories of the Resonants: Interact with the shadowy entities of Baritone Mansion to discover what unfinished business keeps them in the world of the living. Navigate an oppressive atmosphere: Classic controls and fixed camera angles create a feeling of constant vulnerability and tension. Solve Puzzles to Free Spirits: Use found objects and logic to solve the riddles that keep ghosts trapped in their eternal prison. Control destinies and determine the ending: Your decisions directly affect the fate of each spirit and lead to one of several unique endings to the story.
