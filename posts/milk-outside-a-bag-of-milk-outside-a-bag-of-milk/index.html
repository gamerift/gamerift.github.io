Game Description#

The narrative adventureMilk outside a bag of milk outside a bag of mil continues the story of the previous part. The player finds himself in a crazy and mysterious world filled with different combinations of unusual colors and surreal events. On a new journey, you will again have to help the young girl become a little happier. Initially she will be terrified, but as she progresses she will meet new people and realize her own dreams.

The gameplay progresses in the style of a psychedelic narrative, filled with pyramidal word structures and fictional events. Pixel graphics are stylized to match the emotions and mood of the main character. The environment reflects the protagonist’s perception of the world, which is somewhat distorted and different from other people. The action takes place immediately after the end of the first part and continues the girl’s story.

In the passage there will be many dialogues and events that affect the psychological state of the heroine. When faced with different people, moments and surrounding views, the mood of the ward will change. Emotions and other events change the appearance of the environment, the color scheme and style of the environment. Each playthrough is unique and not only the gamer can influence it, since many scenes and actions are procedurally generated.
