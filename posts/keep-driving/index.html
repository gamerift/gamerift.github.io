Game Description#

Keep Driving immerses in the atmosphere of freedom of a long, slow trip to the procedurally generated world. The action takes place in the early 2000s, you are behind the wheel of your first car, a long road and a festival are ahead, which you need to get to the other end of the country. Passing along the highway and dirt roads, you are faced with difficulties, both from the environment and from the bizarre hitchhiker, looking for a passage. These passengers are not just a background: each of them brings its own story, adding new layers on a trip while you continue your way.

The game is focused on a unique combination of research and management of resources. You can configure your car, repair it when it breaks, and use items that you select along the way to solve problems. A step -by -step combat system helps to overcome obstacles, for example, getting stuck for a slowly moving tractor or cope with troubles on the road. It is interesting that none of the two trips are like another, since the world is generated procedurally, and each race seems new.

Thanks to several endings, Keep Driving offers you to replay and try different routes to see how the trip will change. Each time, returning to the beginning, you get a chance to explore a new set of opportunities. It doesn’t matter if you meet strange characters, find songs for your mixture, or just enjoy the open road-there is always something new. The charm of the game lies in its ability to turn the ordinary into an adventure, offering to relax.
