Game Description#

Strategic Command: American Civil War is a new part of a small series of step -by -step strategies. The engine was improved in it. As the name implies, the continuation is dedicated to the civil war in America. The game transfers users to the 19th century. The developers created the largest card in the history of the franchise. It consists of 66,000 hexes and covers the entire North America from the Caribbean to Canada (and New Mexico). There was an opportunity to swim along the rivers. For this, 3 varieties of river ships were added. In addition to them, there are coastal, armored and sea ships of different types. Developers finalized the battles in the open sea. Also, the creators remade the intelligence system. Now you can use rangers, armored trains and even balloons to scatter the “fog of war.”

The game has a strategic command: The US Civil War has 2 factions. You can fight on the side of the Confederation and the Union. 6 campaigns are available, covering the period from 1861 to 1864. In addition, there is an editor that allows gamers to create their own campaigns. Modov support is also available. In this Wargime, you need to recruit volunteers, train them and form brigades, divisions and corps from them. You can choose the generals who will command them. Among them there are really existing historical personalities like Robert Lee or Thomas Jackson. In addition to the battles in this Title there is diplomacy. The likelihood of whether European countries intervene in this conflict intervene or not. It is also necessary to study technology and competent distribution of resources here.
