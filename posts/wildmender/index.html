Game Description#

Wildmender allows you to play online with a friend. You can enjoy this online pirate game for free. This gives you a unique opportunity to spend time with a friend online online. Plus, you’ll be able to take advantage of free online play as you explore the devastated desert, collecting seeds and resources for your garden. As you move through the sands, you will find hidden treasures - collect seeds and resources to immediately feed yourself or grow your garden. However, be careful with your resources, because the desert is unforgiving: there are dried up seas, poisoned canyons, and distant cold mountains, each of which is more dangerous and useful than the previous one.

Embark on this solo odyssey or invite up to three companions to join you in your garden, creating connections as strong as your plants' roots. Together you will travel through the harsh desert, collecting valuable plants to grow in your reserve. Create the necessary tools to change the terrain and guide water to your prized plants. Immerse yourself in the secrets of a long-vanished civilization, unravel its ancient magic, make friends with disembodied spirits and seek the help of benevolent gods to fight off the evil ghosts that threaten to devour the entire world.

Surrender to the secrets that brought the world to its decline. Travel to the sacred temples of the gods, uncover their forgotten stories, revive sorcery, and discover powerful weapons, tools, and abilities to fight the advancing phantoms and breathe life into the desolate. But remember that Wildmender is not just about surviving, it’s about thriving. Invite friends into your garden for 4-player cooperative gardening where teamwork is the key to success. Customize your oasis with man-made structures and tools, creating a sanctuary that reflects your unique style. Bridges over canals, waterfalls and magical symbols - it is in your power to create this world.

With dozens of plant species to grow, over 50 buildings to build, and tons of perks and abilities to unlock, Wildmender offers a never-ending desert gardening adventure. Procedurally generated landscapes provide new challenges with each playthrough, and the day-night cycle adds dynamism, with plants blooming in the light of the sun and ghostly creatures wandering through the cool nights. Travel in style with a variety of outfits and accessories. Tread the desert sands using your unique talent, while choosing different difficulty levels and game modes to suit your tastes.
