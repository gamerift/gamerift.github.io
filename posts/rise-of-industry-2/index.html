Game Description#

Rise of Industry 2 is a setting in which production magnates form the economy, factories produce goods at a frantic speed, and transactions are concluded according to stationary phones with a rotating dial. Success depends on smart investments, effective production and happiness of the right people. Each solution has weight, whether it is the conclusion of profitable trading transactions or the choice of the industry in which it should be dominated.

A decade is a whirlwind of globalization, developing technologies and corporate rivalry. Each industry Rise of Industry 2 - from the wheat fields spread to the halls of meetings, has its own characteristics. One entrepreneur can take a place in the electronics market by flooding stores with computers and cassette users, and the other turns farmers into an agricultural empire that produces dairy, grain and commodity crops of the highest class.

Opportunities extend as far as the economy allows, provided that competitors will not bypass you. Scaling is not only the construction of more, but also efficiency. Supply chains require thin settings, factories need electricity, and transport logistics can increase or reduce profitability. Expansion too fast - and expenses will get out of control. Too slow development - and the possibilities slip away. Leaders have to juggle production costs, labor needs, normative obstacles and at the same time carefully monitor the market trends.
