Game Description#

Cat Quest 2 is an action role-playing game that you can play online with a friend using an online Internet connection. On the pirate platform you can play all co-op modes for free. Events are transferred to Felingard. You need to explore the open world, learning spells, upgrading weapons, fighting monsters and much more. The kingdom is inhabited by cats and dogs. The story tells about two kings who are at war with each other. The cats of Felingrad and the dogs of the Lupus Empire have been at war for a long time. However, fate brings two heroes from different factions together. They need to be returned to the throne to end this centuries-old confrontation.

You can explore the world alone or invite a friend to play together. The world of Cat Quest 2 is inhabited by various creatures and contains a huge number of secrets. The online game offers you to take control of one of the presented heroes. When playing alone, you can switch between protagonists at any time. The arsenal of weapons includes master swords, staves and many other types of weapons for close and ranged combat. Using the opportunity of free online play, you can level up to create your own purring warriors.

A wide selection of spells are available. They can be both supportive and destructive, capable of bringing death down on the heads of enemies. Many active and passive abilities are presented. They can be combined with each other, creating different shapes for the cat. There are a huge number of dungeons in the world containing traps, obstacles, loot and treasures! However, it is necessary to defeat strong opponents guarding ancient treasures. A wide variety of cat quests are presented, including main and secondary tasks.
