Game Description#

Death in CULTIC is the beginning of a mysterious path, so you shouldn’t be afraid of it. The player will have to muster up the courage to fight through hordes of perverted cultists in order to gain the upper hand in this battle. As the character progresses, he encounters hundreds of NPCs with completely frozen minds, who are guided by blind faith in their ideals. The cultists are further corrupted by an immortal and invisible entity that seeks sole domination of the world.

But here’s the thing: fighting here is not so easy. The usual laws of physics come to naught, and the fired bullets come back. Therefore, you need to not easily stand while shooting at monsters, but dodge, demonstrate mobility and non-linearity of the mind. There is freedom of choice here - you can fight enemies in different ways. The player can be in constant motion: shoot from a firearm, run, jump, slide and dodge, or you can slowly but surely break through the ranks of cultists.

Fortunately, there are a lot of weapons in CULTIC - there are ordinary firearms and tons of explosives for every taste. By properly harmonizing between a nimble robber and a tank, the hero will gain a significant advantage, because the enemies will not be able to predict the next step. The enemies won’t stand still either – they act according to their own tactics, partially copying the main character’s combat. The graphics have been developed and optimized - old hardware is enough to run it.
