Game Description#

The cooperative game First Class Trouble is represented by the opportunity to play online on a pirate for free with a friend or random players. Online, you need to create your own team to survive the disaster together. Online play includes co-op and versus mode. The main goal is to find a way to turn off artificial intelligence that has gotten out of control. First Class Trouble takes place on a spaceship. The possibility of a free online game offers you to split into 2 teams: ordinary people and killer robots in human form.

People act as guests of the ISS Aletheia passenger liner. The central computer of the CIIS stopped pumping oxygen. We will need to join forces. to reboot the system. It is necessary to interact with each other using gestures and voice chat. Androids pretend to be ordinary people, so you need to carefully study the situation and monitor the behavior of all characters. Robots must prevent passengers from achieving their goal.

It is necessary to deceive and confuse the survivors. Every effort must be made to move rivals away from the main hall and ensure that all participants throw their ally into outer space. Passengers search for clues, enter closed spaces and share observations. If you calculate the personoid, then he is eliminated from the game by throwing him out of the gateway. Multiplayer is designed for co-op for up to 6 people.
