Game Description#

In Blood Bar Tycoon, users are in the place of the manager of the Vampire Bar. The goal is to create and manage a series of blood bars so that people and vampire customers are satisfied. Along the way, gamers build blood factories, collect blood and encounter problems such as hunters and vampire-competing. Due to the possibility of micro -study or assignment automation, users can accurately configure the work to develop their dark empire.

In Blood Bar Tycoon, you have to turn dilapidated taverns into elegant, fashionable establishments. Users can change the design, furniture and layout of their bars to reflect their own vision. At the same time, people are caught and processed for blood, which is then served with demanding vampires. The key point is the balance between the satisfaction of customer needs, personnel management and disaster prevention. The tasks are not limited only to the blood to flow the river.

This is a constant threat of hunters who can burn your business to the ground, and unpredictable whims of vampire elders. Each elder has his own character, and gamers have to learn how to build relationships with them in order to expand their influence. Unsuccessful attempts to appease them can lead to unpleasant consequences. As the Crimson City moves, you will open new areas and opportunities for development.
