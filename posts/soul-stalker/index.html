Game Description#

Soul Stalker is a bullet heaven-style roguelike that invites gamers into a world filled with ghostly challenges and addictive gameplay mechanics. In this game, gamers take on the role of a ghost manager as they navigate levels filled with unforgiving ghosts. Armed simultaneously with four to six types of weapons, users fight off waves of enemies trying to hinder their progress. This requires not only quick reflexes, but also strategic thinking as gamers choose how to use their arsenal against various threats. The variety of character options is amazing: users can choose one of 21 characters, each of which has unique abilities.

In addition, there are 35 weapons, 84 items and 21 pets, allowing for extensive customization. This variety encourages experimentation, allowing users to mix and match elements to create a build that suits their style. While going through various tasks, users are faced with a number of difficulties that require both adaptation and skill. Each run in Soul Stalker lasts about 25 minutes, and the locations change with each session. This variety keeps the gameplay fresh and exciting, as gamers never know what to expect from each new journey. During the passage, survival in waves of enemies, each of which lasts from 30 to 60 seconds, becomes crucial.
