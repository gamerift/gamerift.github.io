Game Description#

Orcs Must Die! Deathtrap is a third -person action player and trap protection, which used the main elements of its predecessors and added several new opportunities designed to diversify the gameplay. The action takes place in the same universe as in the previous parts of Orcs Must Die!, And gamers will have to join chaotic battles with the waves of orcs and other monstrous creatures, using all kinds of weapons, traps and strategic solutions.

Whether it is a single or joint game, each match sets new tasks for users, so there are no two identical ones. Here you enter the role of a combat magician - the central figure, which is entrusted with defending himself from the advancing hordes of enemies. There are many traps at your disposal: spikes, fiery pits, springing devices - all of them are designed to destroy orcs with maximum efficiency. However, gamers are not limited only to the installation of traps - they are also available weapons that allows you to enter into a direct fight with enemies.

Such a combination of strategy and action provides interesting dynamics when the most effective players can juggle with both direct battle and strategic placement of traps. Title develops these mechanics using an expanded upgrade system, which allows even more customs of weapons and increase the level of complexity. One of the significant changes in Orcs Must Die! Deathtrap - the introduction of Roguelike elements that add an element of unpredictability to each session.
