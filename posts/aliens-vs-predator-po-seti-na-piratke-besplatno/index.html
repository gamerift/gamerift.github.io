Game Description#

Aliens vs Predator is a first-person shooter in which you can play online with a friend, competing for free on a pirate in different confrontation modes. Online co-op play is available. The possibility of free online play offers you to fight for one of three factions: Alien, Predator or Human. Each side has its own unique characteristics that affect the gameplay mechanics. The atmosphere changes depending on which side you choose. For example, the campaign for a person is more like a survival horror game than a traditional FPS. The Aliens vs Predator network game includes: Deathmatch - a standard deathmatch in which everyone acts for themselves; Species Deathmatch - a team battle where creatures of other species are hostile; Mixed Species Death Match - a team can contain representatives of different races; Domination - capturing control points; Survivor - survival against aliens; Predator Hunt - marines fight a predator; Infestation - people against aliens, where every killed soldier turns into a xenomorph.

The single player campaign for each faction is very different. Depending on the chosen heroes, different tasks are set. The campaign for Aliens starts as a facehugger, and later as a chestbuster, who needs to grow into a huge individual. Predators can disappear and stalk their targets. People are very weak in front of alien organisms, so they prefer not to enter into direct confrontation with a huge number of enemies. The game is partially based on movies about space monsters.

The human arsenal includes the VP-78 pistol, M41A/2 pulse rifle, ZX-76 shotgun, M260 flamethrower, M42C sniper and M59/B machine gun. Xenomorphs use Claws, Tail and Roar. The game is partially based on the film series of the same name. Predators fight with Wrist Blades, Plasma Caster, Mines, Homing Throwing Disc and Spear, and can also use special types of vision. They also have medical capsules, a decoy and a scanner at their disposal.
