Game Description#

DAVIGO: VR vs. PC provides a unique opportunity for free online gaming. This asymmetrical multiplayer game lets you play online with a friend, pitting a VR-controlled GIANT against a PC CELEBRITY. In this physics-based fighting game, you can enjoy multiplayer play over the internet online, all completely free of charge. Rising above the battlefield, gamers wield immense power as they play online and use VR motion to deliver devastating blows. No knight can resist your power!

Despite their size disadvantage, KNIGHTS is not without its tools. Armed with a beam for fast movement, a hammer for counter-attacks and powerful missiles, KNIGHTS can defeat the GIANT using agility and strategy. Their small size allows them to quickly maneuver and exploit the GIANT’s vulnerabilities. DAVIGO offers seven official maps, from the fiery depths of ALTAR to the icy summit of SUMMIT. Each map presents unique challenges and opportunities for both GIANT and KNIGHTS. Users can also explore the modding scene and download custom maps created by the community, guaranteeing an endless supply of new battlefields.

Choose from four modes to customize your experience. In BRAWL mode, gamers fight tooth and nail, while TOWER and CAPTURE modes allow for strategic challenges. CLASSIC mode allows you to experience nostalgic sensations while challenging players with a limited arsenal. DAVIGO makes it possible to shape your game using modding tools. The free DAVIGO map editor allows you to create your own maps using the Unity engine. These maps can be easily shared with the community via mod.io, opening up endless possibilities for creating unique battlefields and experiences.
