Game Description#

Coin Push RPG combines the mechanics of an arcade machine with elements of pumping characters. In the center of the process is a full-fledged simulator of a classic coin-pusher, where actions directly affect resources. Good luck drums, bonus towers and jackpotes form the rhythm of sessions and provide an influx of coins. It is these coins that launch the actions of fighters subordinates to the player.

In the Coin Push RPG project, characters do not enter the battle for free. They begin to act only after they receive a fee. Throw coins - you start the battle. Each fighter has his own branch of skills and equipment features that affect effectiveness. Gamer forms a detachment, selects equipment, passes the dungeons and defeats enemies, gradually strengthening the team. The gameplay is built around the parallel development of the village. There are built buildings that bring constant bonuses.

Infrastructure improves craft, opens up new functions and affects the effectiveness of the team in different modes. The pace of progress in all other aspects of Title depends on the pumping of the base. Fights are launched depending on the money earned. Automatically distributed coins activate heroes. In testing mode, opponent waves appear, which requires maximum returns from autoformat. In the adventure segment, the gamer faces a sequence of tasks, where the strategy and composition of the detachment is important. All project mechanics are closely connected: a successful session behind the automaton strengthens the team, and a well -assembled group allows you to go further.
