Game Description#

Crawl is a cooperative game that you can play online for free with a friend. Co-op on one screen and online multiplayer are available. The gameplay is an action roguelike that allows you to cooperatively explore dungeons filled with monsters. Having chosen your hero, you need to upgrade his abilities and skills. Single mode and online play available.

While exploring procedurally generated dungeons, you will have to overcome numerous traps, engage in battles with monsters and solve puzzles. The possibility of free online play allows you to engage in battles with other participants for dominance. If someone manages to kill the player’s protagonist, then he takes his position in the hierarchy. At its core, Crawl is a race for experience and loot. Here, any mistake can cost everything.

One of the main goals is not only to survive, but also to reach the final boss. Any trap or monster can be controlled by a living person. Multiplayer is represented by co-op for up to 4 people, allowing an entire team of adventurers to join forces to fight an ancient evil. Artificial intelligence challenges even the most experienced players. A huge number of weapons, items and more than 60 types of enemies are available, including demons, parasites, undead, etc.
