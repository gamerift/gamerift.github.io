Game Description#

Police Simulator: Patrol Officers is a policeman simulator, offering to plunge on weekdays of this difficult profession. Players put on the cap of the cop to go to patrol the streets of Brighton. It is necessary to start the path with the simplest tasks. For example, write a fine for parking, etc. Later, new missions will open, in which it will be necessary to take part in the chases of the monitoring of criminals. The city is available for research. You can get acquainted with its inhabitants and become a full -fledged part of the community. By guarding your site, it is necessary to save him from crime, becoming a real hero.

Everyone decides what to do to him in a particular situation: to act tough or find alternative ways to solve problems. The main thing is to remember that you need to respect the law. Successfully coping with duties, experimental glasses are accumulating that can improve certain abilities. The intuition system allows you to seek evidence and evidence. It can also be used during the interrogation of the suspects. The Police Simulator game process: Patrol Officers is conditionally divided into 2 modes: a simulator and a casual adventure.

The first transfers reliable sensations from gameplay, and the second is designed to relax at the game. The developers have introduced a comprehensive traffic system where traffic violations, accident, traffic jams, traffic lights, etc. are simulated. The tasks consist of receipt of fines for parking, violations of traffic rules, emergency calls, the search for missing people and organizing police barriers. Gradually, new modes, missions and cars open.
