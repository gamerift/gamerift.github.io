Game Description#

Assetto Corsa is a car simulator developed based on test drives of cars using a model of real behavior. Includes both single player and multiplayer modes.

The developers presented a variety of licensed cars and tracks, which were specially digitized specifically for the race to convey maximum authenticity to their prototypes. The machines can be customized to your individual taste, because… the creators have added an extensive set of settings and support for modifications. Features of the game: an improved engine to give a realistic feel to the car; advanced aerodynamic simulation; for the first time in the history of car sims, details such as tire stains, heat cycles, bubble formation and graininess have been worked out; many official prototypes of real cars.

Circuits such as Monza, Silverstone, Imola, Mugello, Spa, Brands Hatch and many others have been recreated as accurately as possible. Licensed car brands include: Abarth, Audi, BMW, Classic Team Lotus, Ferrari, KTM, Lamborghini, Lotus, McLaren, Mercedes, Scuderia Glickenhaus, Pagani, Porsche and Tatuus. Assetto Corsa is designed to convey the most accurate car behavior with a deep driving simulation. Openness to modifications allowed the simulator to win a huge community, daily increasing the potential and capabilities of the game.
