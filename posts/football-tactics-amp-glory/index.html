Game Description#

Football, Tactics & Glory is a football team management simulator with strategy elements. As a coach, the player will need to identify the weaknesses and strengths of his opponent in order to identify unique tactics. With the help of a well-chosen strategy, you can defeat even a stronger opponent. Each match is like a puzzle where you need to find the key that helps you win. Each athlete on the field is a unique character, and you can even develop a relationship with each of them. Control your players as if you were playing chess, coordinate their actions and watch your opponent’s moves. The only thing that will help you win is strategic planning.

Make big decisions that can change the fate of the team. There are no boring statistics, graphs, or character upgrades in this game. Live an entire season in a couple of hours of real time. Use the face editor to create a unique athlete. With the help of various modifiers, each gamer is able to create a league, invite other players or fight against AI. Play the multiplayer mode and explore the role-playing gameplay elements. Take control of the players yourself, and then no one will say that the match was lost by accident. Improve your management skills and find the perfect tactics that constantly influence the final result. This gameplay is suitable even for those who are not particularly knowledgeable or do not like football at all. Grow your own club by managing financial aspects and composition.
