Game Description#

A real-time strategy with an in-depth system for leveling up your settlement -Stronghold Crusader HD - was released with an addition that allows you to display the picture on a large screen with good quality. The basis of the game remains the same: we develop, build a large army, attack the enemy, whoever he is, win and move on. **The game has 4 historical campaigns, which are divided into stages in history. **To arms! This short campaign tells the story of the First Crusade, which took place in 1094-1099. During this campaign, the player undergoes actual training; Saladin! All missions in this campaign are made according to the siege type principle. The player will only have the Arab army at his disposal; Royal Crusade. The famous historical event during which Frederick Barbarossa, Richard the Lionheart and Philip II united. They were all on their way to recapture Jerusalem from Saladin; Standard crusader wars. Here, as some of the barons, we will be presented with players controlled by computer intelligence.

The game separately develops military affairs, agriculture and resource extraction. Food is obtained from farms where fruits will be grown or cows will be raised. There will be farms where your people will plant crops that can then be processed into bread.

There are two types of army in Stronghold Crusader: mercenary Arab soldiers and crusaders. The first are hired exclusively for money and have lower properties than the troops of Europe. To hire crusaders, knights, crossbowmen and archers, you will have to make bows, crossbows, swords, shields, spears and other things for them. Resources are mined in strictly designated areas. A quarry is located at a place where there is stone; iron in the place where there is iron; oil where there is oil. The game has many buildings, including religious ones, which will increase your reputation, as in previous parts of the franchise.
