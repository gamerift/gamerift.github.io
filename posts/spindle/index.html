Game Description#

The uniqueness of Spindle is captivating. The concept of the project assumes the complete absence of death as such - no one dies, while the world around us is surrounded by chaos. Death itself is trying to find out the reason for what is happening. This time she won’t be alone; her faithful companion, the pig, is going with her on a journey through the old-school world. The main character arms herself with a scythe, takes a companion with her, and moves on to living beings.

The locations are made in the form of corridor levels with detailed design. The graphics are childish and pleasing to the eye. There are no freezes or stutters - optimization has a positive effect. Works even on the oldest computer hardware without freezes or delays. Regarding Spindle itself, the pig plays an important role - it helps Death restore the usual order of things. Spindle Features:

hand drawing of the surrounding world; Death in an unusual role; exploration of dark dungeons; You can pet the pig.

Cheerful musical accompaniment will allow you to relax after a hard day. Death’s Companion - a cute pink pig stays with the protagonist in any difficult moment. Together they travel around the colorful world, exploring previously unknown locations, collecting information and just having a good time. Here the GG is depicted in an unusual appearance - she fishes like a person, mows the grass, solves puzzles and completes mini-games that are characterized by increased complexity.
