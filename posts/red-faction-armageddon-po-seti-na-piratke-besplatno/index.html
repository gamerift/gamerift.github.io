Game Description#

Red Faction: ARMAGEDDON is a direct continuation of the previous part of the series, and tells the story of a character named Darius Mason. It is worth noting that in addition to the story campaign, there is multiplayer, which allows you to play online with a friend on a pirate. Several new modes have been opened for passage in the company, in particular, fighting off crowds of enemies, as well as controlling objects. Due to the option of passing through the Internet online, missions can be taken on by a group of up to 4 people, acting in co-op.

The main locations of the project “Red Faction: Armageddon” will be catacombs, mines and other similar premises on the territory of Mars. It is here that the protagonist is based in the story, conducting raids on the surface of the planet and putting himself in danger over and over again. Now, gamers have the opportunity to go through unsafe places together. Characters will have a choice of weapons, including blasters, long-range rifles, rocket launchers and other powerful weapons. The locations are linear, but the game does not create a feeling of isolation, and in any situation you can use the space to avoid coming under direct fire or to take an advantageous position. Each time the current task is updated, a corresponding message appears on the screen. Therefore, gamers will know exactly what to do next. The possibility of free online play gives complete freedom of action in the area of ​​moving around the territory of the levels and using equipment. In addition, online play introduces the innovation mentioned above. This is a mode of repelling waves of enemies when they advance in an endless army. The task in this situation is to hold out as long as possible.
