Game Description#

Reskue sends an agent to a silent space station in deep space to investigate the reasons for the loss of communication and salvation of scientific personnel. The main mission is to awaken scientists from cryostasis, emerging from it in a disoriented state and in need of protection. The mysterious hostile force has taken control of the object, turning the station technology against the survivors. The operative must dispose of available equipment and ammunition, moving along dangerous locations divided into cells, and controlling the ship on radioactive fuel.

The approach to the passage of Reskue determines the survival strategy, offering options from a direct collision to secretive actions. The choice of objects and weapons allows you to create a unique profile of the operative, focusing on such characteristics as protection, speed or total damage. Setting up weapons and competent equipment management - key elements for overcoming the dangers of the station and protecting the scientific discovery that caused the conflict. Such a system allows players to select equipment for confrontation with an unknown enemy and failed technique.

In addition to standard battles, gameplay offers tactical tools to control the environment. Instead of relying only on shooting, you can build barricades for defense or arrange special traps with unique effects, slowing enemies. The game also includes the local Hotseat mode for two. In this scenario of the confrontation, one person takes on the duties of an agent, and the second player receives direct control over hostile forces, actively interfering with the implementation of the rescue operation.
