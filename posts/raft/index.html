Game Description#

Raft is a first-person survival sandbox in which the player will have to survive on a small raft and explore new lands. The main character will begin his journey by waking up hungry and dehydrated on a small wreck of a ship. Get food and drink by catching garbage floating past the hero on the waves. To catch the item, you can use the hook. Find useful items and materials to craft a mug, light a fire, and filter sea water.

One of the initial tasks of a gamer in Raft is to provide himself with protection from aquatic predators and obtain the largest amount of food. As resources are found, the raft can be expanded and additional upper floors can be built. A shark swimming in the vicinity of the protagonist’s territory is capable of killing the hero if he falls into the water or gets too close to the edge. She can also break a large piece of the raft, which will require you to repair it and waste extra materials.

Wood can be obtained by growing palms or other trees. Get food by catching fish or growing a variety of vegetables. Craft items such as axes or hammers to mine more materials. In the first chapter, the player has the opportunity to get to a desert island. There are bears and other dangerous animals on it. The main advantage of sushi is the huge amount of resources and the ability to make your survival easier.
