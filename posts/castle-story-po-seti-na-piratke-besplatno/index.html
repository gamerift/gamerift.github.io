Game Description#

Castle Story is a colony simulator designed for playing online with a friend or joining a coalition using an online Internet connection. You can get together for free on a pirate site to build your own unique world and build a fortress consisting of various blocks and tools. You need to control the little yellow men Bricktrons. They deftly wield construction tools, building castles brick by brick. Each individual Bricktron can carry one stone, so all buildings are built in real time.

You can play solo with bots, but free online play includes a PvP mode where players can equip bobbleheads and send them into battle in someone else’s castle. The online game Castle Story involves confrontation and joint survival, both with a partner and several players at the same time. By collecting resources, you need to expand your possessions and hire new units. Workers can explore, carve, dig, build, fight, and more. The gameplay has no boundaries and is limited only by the imagination of the player himself. It is possible to design a structure of any complexity, as well as create both an entire village and a mighty impregnable fortress.

It is very important to protect yourself from mystical creatures called Corruptrons. By creating catapults, you can launch boulders at enemies, killing several enemies at once with one shot. The environment is characterized by interactivity, which allows you to destroy every pebble in the game. By damaging the foundation, you can see a tower or a majestic structure collapsing into the sky. A world editor is available, allowing you to create unique biomes designed to further diversify the gameplay.
