Game Description#

For The King 2 is a continuation of an unforgettable adventure RPG with elements of the board game and Roguelike, where you can defeat the formidable queen alone or together with three comrades. You have to explore the world and increase your level in order to reveal the secrets of the past and overthrow the despotistical queen. Try to avoid the queen fanatical guards who control everything around, and combine their forces with their apostates to conquer this world.

Although King Bronner, the Queen’s husband, died many years ago, you will still fight for your village, country and king! Use network and local joint game to choose your path. Gather a team of courageous characters with your unique abilities and features and fight shoulder to shoulder with your friends. The enemies in Farul are inexorable and ruthless, but think through your strategy and outgrade them with the help of a new cell combat system and tactical constructions. Equip your team with everything you need to overthrow the usurpator. Do not forget to rummage all chests and fallen enemies, because it is there that powerful objects that will increase your characteristics and give tactical advantages.

In For The King II you will find many new biomes, procedurally generated cards, quests and events. Beautiful graphics and the original soundtrack will load you deeper into this stunning fictional world of adventure. Play more often to gain access to special items and events in the next passage. Enter this unforgettable battle to accomplish a feat and become a hero who will save the world!
