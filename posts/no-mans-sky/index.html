Game Description#

Travel on an intergalactic level has become too simple and ordinary. It’s becoming increasingly difficult to create a game that captivates with its graphics, but the developers ofNo Man’s Sky, an indie adventure game with an open world and a wonderful RPG base, were able to recreate the image of space. Distant, limitless, completely open and so human.

We play as a character, but then our gameplay switches to an entire space station or ship. The whole content of the new indie is a third-person multiplayer mode in which each player does exactly what he wants: destroys other ships, terrorizes planets, makes colonies, captures other players and forces them to pay a fee for being in outer space . No Man’s Sky is more of a life simulator in space than a story game.

Different temperature conditions, different terrain and creatures living on it - this is the process of automatic map generation in the game. To upgrade our ship, we will have to fly long distances from the initial point, but quests from “headhunters” will help us with this, pointing to the right planets and civilizations.

On each planet there are limited quantities of resources that can be mined, sold, distributed among other players, or crafted something of your own that will be more expensive in the future. Economic forecasts and building a sales funnel will also be needed in the game. Note that each planet initially has its own level of radiation and radiation. Its terrain is generated automatically based on game algorithms. You won’t be able to find something similar in one galaxy.
