Game Description#

Chefs Together is a multiplayer cooking game with RPG elements that can be played online. In this kitchen simulator, players team up with a friend to cook meals, level up their diner, and satisfy the needs of customers. The online game throws players into the deep end, forcing them to juggle numerous orders at various culinary stations. Time is of the essence—if you wait too long, hungry customers will leave without paying. The game is available for free, and you can play it on the Internet online, even pirated.

As you climb the culinary ladder, your restaurant expands. New counters are opening where you can cook according to new recipes. But be careful! As your reputation grows, so do your visitors’ expectations. You’ll have to be on your toes to keep up with the growing pace.

Chefs Together isn’t just about following recipes. It is a balance between inventory management, fast service and customer satisfaction. You must ensure that the pantry is stocked, plates are flying out of the kitchen, and smiles are shining on the faces of diners. There’s also multiplayer mayhem - you can play pranks on your fellow chefs by stealing their ingredients or turning them into animals while serving.

There are 19 different cooking stations in the game, each with its own set of 19 recipes. There’s no shortage of culinary tasks, from flipping burgers to making ice cream. As you progress, you’ll unlock new abilities that will help you cope with the heat in the kitchen.
