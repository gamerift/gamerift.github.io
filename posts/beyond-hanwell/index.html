Game Description#

Beyond Hanwell takes players to a post-apocalyptic London. Westminster, once a bustling hub, now serves as the backdrop to a complex journey of survival. Anomalies, a growing threat, inhabit the ruins of Hanwell. Gamers fight them using collected weapons or gadgets from the environment. The title offers more than just survival. She is a mystery. The director, a shadow figure, holds the clues. Side missions and hidden areas enrich the narrative. Steel Arts Software is committed to creating a single-player experience that will stand out in a market dominated by multiplayer gaming.

Users navigate a world in decline. The city, once a symbol of progress, now poses challenges at every turn. Anomalies, unforgiving and dangerous, force gamers to adapt. Combat becomes a matter of survival rather than skill. The plot unfolds gradually. Users piece together the puzzle, revealing the truth about the Anomalies and the Director. The journey takes place through iconic London locations, now twisted and abandoned. The atmosphere builds tension, turning every corner into a potential threat. Beyond Hanwell offers a different type of gaming experience. It focuses on exploration and story rather than competitive multiplayer. The world seems lived-in, creating a feeling of immersion. Steel Arts Software’s vision is clear: a single-player game with depth and atmosphere.
