Game Description#

Colin McRae Rally 2005 is a racing simulator in which you can play online with a friend or compete with other players for free on a pirate ship, participating in various rally races on the Internet online. The network game is represented by both cooperative races and various tournaments, where you need to take first place in the tournament tables.

The Colin McRae Rally 2005 fleet is represented by more than 30 types of cars: Ford Focus WRC ‘01, Peugeot 206 WRC2003, Mitsubishi Lancer EVO VIII, Audi Sport Quattro S1 and many others. Each machine has its own parameters and characteristics. The possibility of free cooperative play allows you to compete in 9 countries: Australia, Japan, Finland, Greece, Great Britain, Spain, USA, Germany and Sweden. Depending on the country, you will need to deal with different types of surfaces that affect the maneuverability and controllability of vehicles: gravel, asphalt, dirt, snow, ice, etc.

The championships include about 70 rally stages, offering their own unique tests and challenges. Machines are susceptible to damage. The body of the car is deformed during a collision, thereby affecting the maneuverability of the car. A full-fledged career mode is available, where you need to make your way to the top from the lower leagues. The final stage is a competition with Colin McRae himself at the 2004 Dakar Rally Nissan Pick-Up. In total, you need to overcome 6 rally tournaments in a four-wheel drive car.
