Game Description#

Titan Quest 2 is a world that goes into ancient myths, where fate hangs in the balance, and the gods and monsters enter the battle. In the center of the story is Nemesis, the goddess of retaliation, who spoils the threads of fate and punishes everyone who challenges her. Your task is to pick up a weapon, challenge the divine forces and change the balance of fate itself. This role -playing game promises to give you a trip according to the rethinking version of Ancient Greece, where the gods, legendary monsters and ancient secrets are waiting for you.

Against the background of Greek mythology, Titan Quest 2 offers users to explore the vast world. In Title, you will find many diverse places, from the sun -flooded beaches and majestic temples to dark, unknown kingdoms. On the way, you will meet creatures and tests inspired by classic myths. Whether it is the struggle with a formidable griffin or outwit the harpia - the game is full of opportunities in a new way and excitingly come into contact with these ancient legends.

The world is filled with secrets, at every angle you can find something new, and every discovery can lead to new adventures, both mundane and grandiose. As you delve into this huge world, the campaign encourages you not to lose your mind. It is not only about battles, but also about the disclosure of the hidden faces of the world. The developers created a rich tapest for history, where each conversation or object can lead to new events.
