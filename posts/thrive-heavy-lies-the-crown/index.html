Game Description#

Thrive: Heavy Lies The Crown is an exciting medieval fantasy urban planning strategy that will check your leadership qualities and make you make a difficult choice to ensure the survival and prosperity of your kingdom. In this game, the fate of your people largely depends on the decisions you have taken, and the path you have chosen - favor or tyranny - will determine your heritage. The will is your weapon, you must build and strengthen your city, form the land and develop your kingdom with the help of a deep technological tree. But be careful - even seemingly a trivial choice can have severe consequences that will determine your fate and decide whether you will be a soulless tyrant or a fair monarch.

The Thrive system, based on the narrative, offers nuances that often make the best choice unclear, challenging your decision -making skills in real time and leading to real -time consequences. Will you choose compassion and unity with nature or rapid growth and development at all costs? Set your presence on a large -scale 4x land with four friends, competing for resources and developing relations with numerous villages and kingdoms of AI. Develop advanposts and small villages to get more resources and expand the sphere of trade. But be careful - your every solution affects your support point in the active economy of the new territory, and your legacy - fear or friendship - will be determined by your actions. The combat system in real time reflects the costs of war: the death of soldiers affects the morale, ammunition requires replenishment, and the troops experience fatigue. Participate in the siege to show that you can withstand any opponent, but come to all conflicts with a clear strategy so that you do not expect a bitter reminder of a high price of defeat.
