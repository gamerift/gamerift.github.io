Game Description#

In Easy Delivery Co. The player takes on the role of the driver-who travels along the picturesque mountain town with severe weather conditions. The main task is to deliver parcels to local residents on a compact kei-kare. Remuneration of labor is significantly lower than the minimum, forming the economic background of the situation in which the hero found himself. The open world allows you to explore the surroundings outside the main routes, and the physics of transport affects the controllability of the machine on a different relief.

Gameplay in Easy Delivery Co. Built according to a simple scheme: pick up the order, get to the destination and hand it to the recipient. Transport management is based on arcade physics with an emphasis on simple controls for navigation. The key element of resource management is the need to buy fuel sold in the city at a high price. Interaction with the local population acts as a central mechanism for the development of history.

In addition to delivery tasks, this game is revealed as an interactive adventure with a rich history. Conversations with the townspeople shed light on details about the local community, its past and unusual secrets. The atmospheric environment creates a contrast between the monotony of work and secrets awaiting their disclosure. The narrative is supplemented by the personal goal of the hero: his long -standing dream to escape from this position and leave the city.
