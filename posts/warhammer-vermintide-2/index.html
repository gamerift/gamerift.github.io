Game Description#

Warhammer: Vermintide 2 is a first-person slasher with abilities, magic, character upgrades, a variety of weapons and meat, combining a unique combat system of 5 different factions. The modernized first part is made on a new graphics engine, allowing us to see every severed limb; look at dead enemies and collect dropped loot around them, not paying attention to graphical inaccuracies.

The plot of Warhammer: Vermintide 2 revolves around Helmgarth Castle, surrounded by a small settlement. 4 players set out on a journey to defeat the forces of Chaos that began in this small town. At the beginning, you create a character who will accompany you throughout the game. All characters now have 3 specializations, from which you can choose one of 15 professions that lead to the end of your career.

The game has a gradual reward system. If you kill enemies, they may drop health potions, sometimes rare scrolls for studying or upgrading spells, maybe even weapons or armor, but this is rare. The main reward comes to us from completed tasks. Armor, weapons, scrolls, recipes and resources for upgrading our profession - all this is distributed between 4 players. If there are only two or three of you in co-op mode, then the last place will be taken by artificial intelligence, which can reflect your skills and play on the same level.

Overall, the game turned out to be a worthy version of itself 3 years after the release of the first part. Redrawn graphics, improved crafting system and character upgrades. The plot tells us the story of a world of chaos in several missions, in which there is time for jokes, murders and a huge amount of meat!
