Game Description#

Quake 2 takes users back to the era of old-school shooters that are great for online play. Here, you can either spend time on the story campaign or play pirate games with a friend, testing your skills in this genre. If we talk about the single-player campaign, the focus is on the conflict between people and the Strogg, who decided to launch a full-scale attack on Earth. Humanity decided to counterattack by organizing an attack on the aliens’ home planet, and the main character had to end up there. But during the landing, his capsule crashes, and he ends up right at the enemy base. Now, he needs to reunite with his allies, destroying enemies along the way. And given the possibility of playing QUAKE 2 online together over the Internet, this can be done together.

In total, QUAKE II has 36 levels, which in turn take place in different locations. These can be bunkers, open spaces, the surface of the planet and other places. Depending on the location of buildings and third-party objects, you can use objects as shelters. Opponents move quickly and are also capable of using cover, so you should react to their attacks without exposing yourself to targeted fire. Monsters differ in appearance, weapons and level of damage. Therefore, when meeting particularly large creatures, you should not get into open battle, but instead fire from cover.

As for arena battles, online play involves holding such competitions on specially designated maps. Thanks to the specific terrain and the presence of interactive objects such as springboards and accelerators, you can quickly change your location, repeatedly forcing your opponent into disadvantageous positions. Weapons can also be changed on the fly by simply picking up barrels in the designated areas. The possibility of free online play allows you to track your progress in battles thanks to constantly updated ratings.
