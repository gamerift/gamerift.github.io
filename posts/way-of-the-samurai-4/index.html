Game Description#

Way of the Samurai 4 is a rare world game in the setting of Japan of the nineteenth century. The tie is trivial - the nameless Ronin arrives in the same nameless city and is immediately in the epicenter of local showdowns. The world is a sandbox consisting of city quarters under the control of various groups and a suburbs.

The situation in the city is really interesting. Western barbarians came, who were met by local bandits, and the police turned out to be between two lights. We are free to help any fraction and solve problems in any way. Helping one or the other, the player will come to one of the 10 endings. This allows the game to be amazingly reigree.

According to gameplay, this is a fairly original slasher with medium dynamics and elements of a real Cando simulator. You can not wait for special realism, our hero is free to throw enemies into the air with his techniques and kill them right there, although the dissects can not be expected. An interesting chip with cutting off some cat scenes using the exposure of the sword, which adds the effect of immersion.

The game is also full of strange Japanese humor and various ridiculous scenes, which is characteristic of games from the country of the rising sun. This is a rare representative of the Samurai life simulator, which makes this game very attractive for many categories of players.
