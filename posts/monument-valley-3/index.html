Game Description#

In the Monument Valley 3, players will go on a new journey, leading a student of Nur through a world built from optical illusions and mobile architecture. The main goal is to comprehend the meaning of sacred light, moving along locations with changeable gravity. For advance, it is necessary to directly manipulate buildings, rotate entire levels and change the perspective, paving routes through impossible geometry.

Features of the surrealist adventure Nur:

Changing prospects for creating new paths. Direct interaction with architecture for solving puzzles. The student’s emotional journey in search of sacred light. The study of mysterious temples and ruins among rising waters. Using a boat for navigation and opening secrets.

This journey through a series of puzzles unfolds against the background of rising tides, demanding from the player a constant adaptation to the environment changing from his actions. The basis of gameplay is to solve these spatial problems to discover the secrets of the universe. The research process in the Monument Valley 3 will conduct Nur according to many locations, from temple complexes to places in the decline.

Its companion and main transport is the boat necessary to cross the variable seas and detect hidden paths. The discovery of these lost routes is the key to understanding the entire narrative and history of the lands. Each area challenges logic and intuition, because the Earth itself shifts, ignoring the usual points of view. The game provides a full story immediately on launch, and additional chapters and puzzles will appear in the future, guaranteeing the continuation of the campaign after the first passage.
