Game Description#

InYmir you can play online as a pirate for free with a friend, doing city planning and developing the civilization of the Pigmen. We have to go through eras, starting development from the Stone Age. On the Internet online you can both join forces and perform in versus mode. The gameplay is divided into two main parts: the world map and regions. Territories are generated randomly. By collecting resources such as metals, plants and animals, you need to upgrade your fortress and train your army. Ymir content and mechanics: classic 4X strategy; the possibility of free online play; thoughtful socio-economic mechanics; 7 unique biomes; full character development: reproduction, work, old age, etc.; simulation elements that affect the gameplay .

Single player campaign, online play and training available. Battles take place in real time. You can strengthen the walls of the castle. The toolkit allows you to create walls, bridges, gates, stairs, towers, loopholes, etc. It is required to use the features of the landscape for tactical purposes. A squad can consist of an unlimited number of units.

Each soldier has his own unique features and characteristics. In battles, it is not the number of units that plays an important role, but the skill of the commander. The maps consist of hills, plains and water. By wisely using the landscape and making the right tactical decisions, you can defeat an army significantly superior in numbers.
