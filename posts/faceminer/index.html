Game Description#

FaceMiner offers a deep immersion in the world of collecting biometric data. Here gamers fall into the chaotic world before the beginning of the new millennium, where the main task is to collect and classifying data on faces on a mass scale. As a user, you start small, gradually building an empire from biometric data. On this path, you have to expand the scale: to update software, develop infrastructure and maximize profits, overcoming the sea of ​​corporate problems. You have to find a balance between expansion and growing expenses, such as energy consumption and maintenance.

The gradual nature of FACEMINER allows gamers to focus on development. With each update, your data collection operations become more and more complex, and the sphere of influence is expanding. As your empire grows, the difficulty of controlling it also increases. On this path, you will find many obstacles - from maintaining cooling systems to the prevention of bankruptcy. The bets are high, and an attentive attitude to the details is the key to survival.

Although the main gameplay rotates around the data collection, there is a strong narrative element. Using the in -game corporate message system, you can disclose secrets about the creators of the software. This huddling adds an intriguing layer to the game process focused on business. The characters participating in your journey are not just background figures; They give an idea of ​​the hidden motives of the company you work for.
