Game Description#

On the pirateFinders Reapers you can play online with a friend on the pirate, participating in online shooter competitions on the Internet. The gameplay is a low-poly third-person action game. Multiplayer includes 8 competitive modes. It is necessary to observe objects falling from the sky in order to collect them and sell them on the market. The proceeds can be used to unlock guns, equipment, resources and much more. Money is also used to buy ammunition.

There is a quest system that allows you to complete tasks of varying complexity. For successful completion of missions, experience and money are awarded. Stars are earned in single missions. You can open new characters on them. The possibility of free online play includes competition in 8 modes: resource collection, deathmatch, last man standing, etc. All modes are aimed at confronting participants. Only the strongest survive!

The visual part stands out with bright graphics and unique artistic performance. Battles take place on a dusty farm, within a pirate cove, in a war-torn village, etc. There are about 200 characters in total. Heroes are unlocked as you progress or when certain conditions are met. There are 50 such requirements and goals. The arsenal of weapons is quite extensive: rifles, shotguns, grenade launchers, etc. Many guns are hidden in locations. There is a tutorial in the main menu that introduces all the mechanics of the project.
