Game Description#

Mordheim: City of the Damned provides the opportunity to compete online on the Internet, participating in various multiplayer modes for free on pirates. You can play with a friend, fighting in co-op or against random people. The game mechanics are action with a touch of tactical RPG. The action takes place in the Warhammer Fantasy universe. The setting is reminiscent of Renaissance Europe.

It is necessary to go to the streets of the city of Mordheim, fighting among themselves for fragments of the stone of distortion. The possibility of free online play allows you to take part in turn-based battles. Each participant takes control of a squad consisting of several fighters. Warriors can increase abilities and improve skills. You can also choose different types of weapons. The City of the Damned contains a huge number of traps and obstacles. You need to collect fragments of Wyrdstone - a stone that bestows untold riches. Factions in Mordheim: City of the Damned: Sisters of Sigmar - fighting Amazons; Skaven - a stealth class operating from the shadows; Imperial mercenaries - ordinary ground warriors; Cult of the Taken - mutants, heretics and other evil spirits.

Each faction is represented by unique units and an individual fighting style. Depending on the choice of side in the conflict, the style of play completely changes. For example, Sisters fight with powerful hammers, while Skaverns prefer agility and stealth. All 4 campaigns can be completed in any order. As you progress, new levels, weapons, units, etc. open up. The combat is presented in a turn-based style, where each player takes turns taking actions. You need to collect gold to unlock equipment, level up, and even improve your luck factor.
