Game Description#

Old World in the past was also known as 10 Crowns. The game is a step -by -step global strategy in the antique setting. The gameplay is strikingly different from the games of this genre: it is allowed to give several orders to Unit in one move. Such a resource as “orders” is responsible for additional moves. The more recognition points, the more moves can be made. The resource “Order” can be used in different ways: in battle, in construction, in events or diplomacy.

The number of moves directly depends on the number of available “orders” points. “Legitimacy” is another important tool in the Gameplay Old World. As the game should be implemented by ambitions, among which there may be the construction of the Wonders of the World. As soon as the task of performing the ambition is completed, the leader will be fame, and with it - the points of legitimacy, which will strengthen authority among the population. Another feature is that the rulers die from old age. Therefore, during the party, you need to have time to prepare a worthy receiver to marry and give birth to a child. As soon as the new ruler ascends to the throne, you need to again increase your authority among the people, having declared new ambitions. There are 10 of them in the game.

The dynamic system of events generates closely 1000 unique situations that endow the characters with memories or personal features. The cities are represented by both the populated center and the village settlements. As the territory will improve, tombs, tombs, amphitheaters, etc. will become available on it. Many historical personalities and dynasties will also meet. A large accent of developers is on making difficult decisions.
