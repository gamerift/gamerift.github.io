Game Description#

In Aztecs The Last Sun, the player becomes the ruler of Tenochtitlan, a great state that can win the whole world. The user relies on many tasks, as the head of his own power. He has to develop a city, increase military power and improve the quality of life of citizens. Having taken the control over the Aztecs, you will need to go through the three great centuries, to dominate in the nearest territories and write new pages in world history.

The gameplay is presented in the form of a urban planning simulator and the management of the economic component. The ruler will need to provide subjects with food so that they are engaged in construction and military affairs. In history, this state was stood out thanks to the architectural miracle located in the middle of Lake Teskoko on the nameless island. The landscape will act as a problem, offering difficulties in technological and economic aspects.

For prosperity, Juzer should observe the three different features of the city. Military business helps residents live calmly and beat off rare attacks of small enemy detachments. The economic component is necessary to acquire the necessary resources, maintain a high standard of living and construction. Religious growth helps to control subjects and increase their moral spirit. The user is able to freely install buildings and develop original infrastructure.
