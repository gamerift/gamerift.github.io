Game Description#

Vanquish The Eternal provides an arsenal of unique abilities, extensive talent trees, and a variety of empowering buffs to counter the forces of evil. Players will choose from eight fully voiced characters, including the mysterious Alchemist, to fight back against the nightmarish creatures. Advancement is built on a meta-progression system that allows you to gradually strengthen your heroes for subsequent battles. Deep customization of your fighting style is a key element in preparing for the battle with the main antagonist, whose power increases with each new challenge.

The opponents are vile and vile creatures generated from the bowels of the earth on the eve of All Hallows' Day. These fiends of darkness and horror are able to change their appearance, masquerading as people, but their true essence remains invariably evil. The main goal of these creatures is to search for the strongest among the living in order to absorb their souls. The culmination of the confrontation is the inevitable meeting with the King of the Damned himself, who rises from the underworld. Vanquish The Eternal offers 21 New Game+ difficulty levels, testing the strength of even the most seasoned gamers in the battle for their own soul.

Immersion in the dark atmosphere of the title is complemented by the presence of a comprehensive compendium. Inside this collection contains detailed information about the world, as well as detailed images and descriptions of all characters, monsters, bosses and summoned creatures. In addition, the system tracks statistics, allowing you to analyze your successes and failures. Successfully overcoming challenges and completing special conditions is rewarded by unlocking achievements, of which there are 62 in this adventure. Each of them is a testament to mastery in confronting the eternal darkness.
