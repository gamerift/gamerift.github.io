Game Description#

Forza Horizon 4 can be played online online, or you can compete with smart artificial intelligence, competing in various world championships and tournaments. Playing online with a friend, or going through a single player campaign is everyone’s choice. All game content and add-ons are available for free on the pirate site. An important feature of the gameplay is the seasons, which significantly transform the visual style of the project.

The gameplay is a complex and sophisticated car simulator that gives you the opportunity to explore historical monuments and attractions of Great Britain. The fleet consists of 450 cars. All vehicles have unique characteristics and control features. Cars can be customized and modified.

By participating in races, winning and performing stunts, you can become a true legend in the world of Forza. The possibility of free online play allows you to challenge even the most experienced players. Leaderboards are provided that track statistics and achievements. Multiplayer Forza Horizon 4 is a real test for the most daring racers who consider themselves real pros.

The huge world is inhabited by living players racing through the prairies and canyons of a detailed open world. The lands of Britain consist of lakes, valleys, castles and numerous picturesque landscapes that will take your breath away. The campaign invites you to go anywhere and do anything, completing races in random order. Over 100 licensed brands and manufacturers available. Every driver will find a car that suits his needs and preferences: Ford Hoonigan Mustang, McLaren P1, LaFerrari, Bugatti Veyron, Venom GT, Koenigsegg Agera and many other supercars.
