Game Description#

Kadomon: Hyper Auto Battlers calls the gamarines into a world filled with more than 180 creatures who need to collect, fight and develop. The Title combines the elements of autoboeviks, rogeliks and card battles, allowing users to develop unique strategies. The purpose of the game is to assemble the KDOMON team, each of which has various abilities and types in order to pass more and more difficult tests. As you pass, you will encounter powerful bosses and open rare monsters with special abilities.

Mechanics Kadomon: Hyper Auto Battlers based on synergia and evolution. KDOMON has different characteristics, from activated and passive skills up to 12 different types. Thanks to these features, a gamer can experiment with different combinations, looking for the most effective ways to defeat the enemy. As the Title passes, the task is becoming more and more complicated: stronger opponents appear, who experience the entire team for strength. Each victory makes it possible to open new heroes and develop its current composition to a higher level.

Dynamic video game setting includes random events that make the gameplay unpredictable. These events can change the course of the battle, forcing users to adapt their strategies on the fly. In addition, users have the opportunity to purchase special items that can lead them to KDOMON outside the usual capabilities. With full development, a platoon can reach the fourth form known as a hyperform that has increased characteristics and new powerful skills.
