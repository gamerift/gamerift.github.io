Game Description#

Murder Is Game Over Murder Otaku is a mysterious incident at the pop culture convention YokaiKon, where a detective duo must unravel a mysterious crime. The main characters, Detective Guy and his faithful dog Cleo, arrive at the historic 1920s hotel that serves as the venue for the event. Their mission begins after the tragic death of a famous J-pop singer, whose death shrouds the noisy event in an ominous mystery. The protagonists need to immerse themselves in the peculiar world of anime fans in order to find clues leading to the solution.

The investigation process forces you to methodically explore every corner of the gloomy hotel. Interacting with eccentric suspects, from obsessive stalkers to jealous competitors, the heroes piece together clues. The developers have created a tense atmosphere, where the matter is complicated by rumors about the curse of the Oni statue, adding a touch of mysticism to the detective story. The gameplay of Murder Is Game Over: Murder Otaku requires the gamer to pay attention to detail and use logical thinking to compare facts. By interviewing witnesses and uncovering hidden clues, the investigative duo gets closer to the truth.

The ultimate goal is to expose the criminal hiding among the participants and guests of YokaiKon. The evidence collected must be analyzed to form a compelling chain of events that points to the culprit. Before the sun rises, the mystery must be solved and the perpetrator brought to justice, bringing closure to this complicated and unusual case.
