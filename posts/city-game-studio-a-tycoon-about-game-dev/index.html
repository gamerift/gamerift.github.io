Game Description#

City Game Studio: A Tycoon ABOUT GAME DEV is a strategic simulator in which it is important not only to chase the demand of players, but also be able to reduce expenses and vice versa, be able to spend money where they are actually needed. Games developers presented a simulator in which you will have to develop a company that develops games. A small studio creating video games decided to expand, so you must make every effort to create a whole headquarters of developers who can satisfy the needs of modern gamers.

Together with the protagonist, we go into the past. In the yard - January 1975. Recently, together with your friends, you have developed a video game. Now you are looking for a building, new developers, game designers and everyone who can participate in the design and creation of flagships in the world of games. Your first game quickly gained popularity, so you, along with a huge check, go to the building better, where there is enough space for creativity and relaxation. Over time, your team is growing popularly, and the best artists, designers, testers, programmers and leading developers responsible for marketing and strategy want to get to you. However, problems end in the case when you become an independent developer. Yes, most of your games can be published by you, but in order to collect a large fan base, you will have to follow the crowd. This is not the first time you come to the publishers who create a hole in the budget, so for each next project you will need more and more money. How will you deal with this?

Map, studio, games are generated by accident. Each new round will delight or upset you with random events; Find the best among the best; The decoration of your studio largely affects the performance of employees; Create a bestseller, but remember that he will leave the rental.
