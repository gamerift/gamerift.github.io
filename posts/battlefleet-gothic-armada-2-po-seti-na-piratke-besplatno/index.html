Game Description#

Battlefleet Gothic Armada 2 is a strategy game set in the Warhammer 40,000 universe. Battlefleet Gothic Armada 2 can be played online for free as a pirate, fighting with a friend or players from all over the world. Over the Internet, you will have to take part in space battles, controlling one of 12 factions. Network play allows you to fight on the same team or take part in confrontations. Both infantry and space shuttles participate in the battles.

You can play for the Imperium, Space Marines, Adeptus Mechanicus cult, Necrons, Chaos, Eldar and other factions. All opposing sides are represented by an individual set of units, shuttles and heroes with their own unique abilities. Soldiers and equipment can be improved. The action covers such tragic events as the 13th Black Crusade and the Gathering Storm. There are 3 story campaigns available for the Imperium, Necrons and Tyranids. The Eye of Terror system is introduced. Belisarius Cole and Guilliman are available for control. You can customize fleets and ships by choosing different weapons and modules. Gameplay, modes and content: 3 campaigns for unique factions; Single playthrough and the possibility of free online play; 12 races with their own set of units, ships and heroes; Epic battles of galactic proportions; References and adherence to the canons of the Warhammer universe; Individual customization of shuttles ;Many additional modes.
