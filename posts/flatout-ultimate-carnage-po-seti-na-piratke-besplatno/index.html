Game Description#

FlatOut: Ultimate Carnage is an arcade race for survival that allows you to play for free on a pirate online with a friend, participating in crazy races with total destruction of both the car and the environment. Network play is available locally and online online. The project acts as an expanded port of the second part, adding several new modes, such as carnage and time trial. Free online play allows you to take control of four new pilots: Lewis Durand, Lei Bing, Jill Richards and Curtis Wolfe. You can take part in competitions such as Racing, Derby, Stunts, Death Derby and Head-on mode. Multiplayer allows up to 8 players to team up.

The FlatOut: Ultimate Carnage fleet now has two additional vehicles: Bonecracker and Grinde. A special feature of the gameplay is heavy physics and realism. The cars have 40 deformable parts. In total, there are about 8,000 destructible objects on the Flatout tracks. Single career includes 3 car classes. The campaign consists of circuit races, demolition derbies, time trials, etc.

You will have to face 11 professional racers. 48 cars available. Competitions take place on 39 tracks in 6 different locations. Pilots show their skills on city streets, in the desert, in sewers, etc. Included are 12 types of mini-games, which feature funny ragdoll characters. It is necessary to launch the driver through the windshield while participating in tournaments such as High Jump, Free Kick and Ring of Fire.
