Game Description#

Locuras en el Banaverso is a single-player platformer that takes gamers on a chaotic adventure through a bizarre multiverse. The game follows the eccentric YouTuber El Bananero, who not only plays the role of the main character, but also voices him. From the very beginning, El Bananero finds himself in an unexpected situation - he is kidnapped by none other than Santa Claus and his gang of henchmen. As fate would have it, the kidnapping turns into an escape through a portal that leads him into the depths of the multiverse.

What follows is a whirlwind of events, puzzles and humor as El Bananero tries to navigate his new reality. As El Bananero moves through the multiverse, he finds himself not alone. He teams up with Andres Di Bono, a companion who helps him navigate various strange and bizarre environments. The duo travel through many different levels, each offering their own challenges. Whether they’re diving into underwater kingdoms or trying to outwit a world filled with cakes, the gameplay is full of unpredictability.

Gamers face obstacles that require both quick reflexes and creative problem-solving. These levels are designed to not only challenge the player’s skill, but also to keep the player engaged with ever-changing scenarios. The enemies in Locuras en el Banaverso are as colorful as the worlds El Bananero encounters.
