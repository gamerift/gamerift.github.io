Game Description#

Resident Evil 5: Gold Edition introduces the opportunity to play pirate online for free with a friend, going through the story campaign together online. You can play alone. In this case, artificial intelligence takes on the role of a partner. Free online play allows you to play through the Lost In Nightmares and Desperate Escape add-ons included in the Gold Edition together.

Events move to Africa. The protagonists hunt for Albert Wesker and try to find out the truth about the deadly Ouroboros virus, which turns people into terrible monsters. The plot consists of 6 chapters, which are divided into several episodes. Before starting a chapter, you can choose weapons and equipment, as well as buy or sell unnecessary guns and items. The inventory consists of 9 cells. It is available in real time. Many QTE mechanics are presented. In many cutscenes and gameplay moments, the online game divides responsibilities between the heroes. A mistake by one of the participants leads to a gameover. Protagonists of Resident Evil 5: Chris Redfield - a former member of ST.RS, and now one of the founders of BSAA; Sheva Alomar - Chris’s partner throughout the entire passage; Jill Valentine - one of the heroines of the previous parts, considered dead; Josh Stone - Commander of Delta Squad and Sheva’s mentor.

The bosses are Ricardo Irwin, Excella Gionne, Albert Wesker and various monsters. The gameplay places a huge emphasis on action, relegating the quest part to the background. In most cases, puzzles come down to finding clues. In addition to the story campaign, the Mercenaries mode is available. It allows you to play 8 maps from the main adventure with 10 characters. Some of the heroes and their weapons are unlocked as you level up.
