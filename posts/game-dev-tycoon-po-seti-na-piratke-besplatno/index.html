Game Description#

Game Dev Tycoon gives gamers the opportunity to become video game developers. Moreover, on the pirate platform they are allowed to play an economic simulator online with a friend. Thanks to the efforts of fans, this can be done online online. The campaign starts in the 1980s.

Network play in Game Dev Tycoon offers users competition. In multiplayer, players compete with each other, deliberately pirate other people’s releases, spy and steal data. And all this is done in order to beat the opponent in terms of economic indicators in a competitive race. The possibility of free online play is limited to supporting only two gamers at a time.

The gameplay is as follows. Users establish studios that develop and design games. At first, all the business takes place in the garage. Over time, they allow you to move into an office and hire additional employees. When creating a product, you can choose a genre (action, adventure, strategy, etc.), engine, platform, setting (cyberpunk, fantasy, and so on). It is also necessary to decide on dialogues, the development of artificial intelligence, the design of the world or individual levels, sound, and graphics. All this requires time and budget, so compromises will have to be made. As you progress, technologies are unlocked that provide new opportunities. For example: non-linear gameplay, full 3D, leveling trees, orchestral soundtrack. The situation on the market is also changing: different consoles appear and disappear, age ratings are introduced, and the AAA industry is born. Finished products must be sent not only for gold, but also for reviews by the video game press. Reviewer ratings directly affect sales.
