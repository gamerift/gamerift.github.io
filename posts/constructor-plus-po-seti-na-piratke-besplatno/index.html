Game Description#

InConstructor Plus you can play online and with a friend on a pirate for free. You have the opportunity to play online for free with other players over the Internet. You have to become a novice developer and build the city of your dreams from scratch. This sequel to Constructor HD has nearly triple the content, providing a vast and varied sandbox for your entrepreneurial spirit.

Choose from over 147 unique buildings - from humble shacks to grand mansions and towering skyscrapers. Populate your city with quirky residents, bustling businesses, and even grandiose monuments to attract residents and their precious money. But beware, the road to real estate tycoon fame is not a bed of roses. Competitors are lurking, wanting to rob you of your hard-earned money and hinder your progress.

Moral problems at every turn. Play by the rules and manage your resources carefully, or enter into “dubious deals” to make a profit faster. Remember that wealth breeds envy, so be prepared to defend your empire with wits, cunning and perhaps ruthlessness.

Constructor Plus is designed for both traditionalists and innovators. Classic mode offers a familiar experience, while new features such as rural and lunar development push the boundaries of the genre. With an intuitive setup process and rich content, it’s time to unleash your inner tycoon and build the city of your dreams. Just remember that in the cruel world of development, only the strongest survive. Are you ready to take on the challenge?
