Game Description#

InPANICORE you can play online with a friend on a pirate for free. You’ll find yourself in the shoes of urban explorers - adrenaline junkies craving the thrill of discovering forgotten relics in abandoned buildings. Explore schools and buildings online, immersing yourself in the eerie atmosphere of forbidden places. This multiplayer adventure game features one location shrouded in warnings and rumors, turning exciting exploration into a nightmare.

Despite the chills caused by official warnings, explorers set out in search of the secrets that the abandoned place holds. Curiosity soon turns to panic when they realize they are trapped. Unknown forces block their escape, forcing them into a desperate struggle for survival. Fear increasingly grips them when they realize that they are not alone.

It’s not just looking for a way out. Time is running out, and explorers need every ounce of courage. Their journey becomes a desperate attempt to solve puzzles, avoid the horrors that lurk in the dark corridors, and outwit a relentless monster who is ready to lure intruders into a trap. Every step matters, every groan in the dark can mean a terrible death.

Will you go alone or team up with friends? PANICORE offers both options, but remember: the monster hunting you has a mind of its own. He hears your steps and even panicked screams. The environment becomes your ally - cabinets, tables, shadows - anything to avoid interfering with the invisible horror. Think quickly, find the necessary items scattered around the location, use them wisely - this is your only chance of salvation. Will you be able to escape before the darkness engulfs you?
