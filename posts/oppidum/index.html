Game Description#

Oppidum takes the player to the island of Insul, where they will have to master the mechanics of survival alone or in a team of up to four people. The characters find themselves in a land of widespread decline due to a deep-rooted corruption that has fractured the local society. Key gameplay elements include setting up a central camp, systematically gathering supplies scattered throughout Insul, and navigating its varied zones.

Mastering these systems directly helps to resist the destruction of the island and achieve the goal for which the protagonists were called to this game world. Gameplay in Oppidum involves crafting the necessary tools, weapons and protective equipment from materials found in the environment. Gamers will meet various inhabitants of the island, affected by the division of society and the spreading disease; helping these residents helps not only themselves, but also the restoration of broken ties within the community.

Direct encounters with creatures altered by Insula’s corruption occur, requiring the use of crafted items for self-preservation in hostile skirmishes. In addition, you will have to discover and learn to control Tamai - the energy fundamental to the existence of the island and the development of the character, which will prove vital to overcoming difficulties. The personal base acts as the main operations center, providing a place for rest and preparation between forays into the outside world. In this customizable shelter, you can engage in detailed crafting, build buildings from found blueprints, care for crops and animals, and prepare food to sustain you on your travels.
