Game Description#

Trash Goblin is an adventure about a store that invites gamers to plunge into the noisy life of a silver city, where every day it opens up new opportunities and sets new tasks. Having risen in place of orphaned goblin, players daily search for treasures hidden in bags of garbage. Each bag is a mixture of potential and disorder filled with trinkets that are waiting for them to be cleaned and repaired. The Title rotates around the craft and creativity, allowing users to breathe new life into thrown out objects. This process is more than just a task; This is the heart of creating a successful store.

In the silver city, the atmosphere is filled with capabilities. Gablin gamers interact with many colorful buyers, each of which has its own tastes and requirements. All this turns into a kind of dance, in which you have to balance between customer requests and limited resources. The user may face the fact that he needs to have time to complete the order of a bizarre client, or with the fact that he needs to find innovative ways to turn the old trinket into something desired. Such dynamics creates a fascinating rhythm, involving the players in the daily work of their stores. Cleaning and repair of trinkets is not just repairing items, but also a deeper understanding of customer preferences. Each interaction gives an idea of future decisions, pushing gamers to adapt their strategies.
