Game Description#

Puss! - A psychedelic game about a sweet cat in such a genre as Avoid-Em Up. Get ready for madness, absurdity and real hardcore. The gameplay is a real challenge, which is only the most daring and experienced players. You control a cat that got into another dimension. He has only two choices: to stay here forever or try to choose from this nightmare.

Management is sharpened under one hand, however, it is necessary to practice and get used to have sufficient dexterity for dashing cat control. Some sections of the game are a real hardcore bullet-gell. Each level contains tense fights with bosses. All the leaders are unique monsters, to which it is required to look for special tactics. Free your partners to help you in the future.

At the end of the episode, the leading table will show your result and compare it with players around the world. Levels go in an accidental sequence. Gameplay is much more complicated than it seems at first glance. Puss! Checks your reaction and speed. During the passage, open three additional skin for the hero. The visual style of the game is an acidic psychodel. And also, there is a meeting with Baphomet himself and hellish black cats.
