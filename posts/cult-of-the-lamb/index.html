Game Description#

InCult of the Lamb, finding himself in the lands belonging to the false prophets, the gamer is invited to found his own cult, creating a powerful community of admirers. In an adventure game with roguelike elements, the player needs to spread his word to the forest dwellers, gradually making various forays into countries and regions in order to try to expand the sphere of influence of his religion, making it cover all regions.

The user is transformed into a lamb that is possessed. One day the hero was lucky enough to be saved from death thanks to a mysterious stranger. Now the savior asks for tribute in the form of reliable followers, trying to lay the foundation for his special religion. Here the gamer is asked to collect the resources necessary to construct various types of buildings, gradually strengthen the faith of his subjects by carrying out sermons, as well as dark rituals in order to earn the blessing of the sinister gods. In the process, you will have to explore a randomly generated map of the Universe, engage in battles with cultist enemies, destroying leaders, which will allow you to establish yourself in these lands and seize power. To travel safely, the hero needs to teach his flock, which allows him to perform various mystical rituals and reveal the secrets of 5 secret areas.
