Game Description#

Mordhau is an immersive hack and slash game aimed at online battles over the Internet. For free, you can play pirate online with a friend or fight against random players from all over the world. The main emphasis of the gameplay is on the complex fencing system. You can use feints, change sides of attacks, carry out a series of attacks, use different grips on weapons, etc.

The online game offers a huge list of functions, including hand-to-hand combat, archery, covering the body with a shield, horse riding and many other features. Mordhau allows you to play alone or use the free online play option to compete in 6 main modes. 6 types of competitions are presented: Duel - a duel between two participants; Teamfight - a team battle between two factions; Invasion - a mass battle of two groups of 32 people; Frontline - capturing points and completing various goals; Brawl - classic competitions such as Deathmatch or Skirmish;Horde - jointly holding 21 waves of opponents.

Extensive character customization is introduced. You can change your physique, face, voice, select weapons, armor and skills. By receiving experience points, you need to develop the abilities of your hero. There are 3 types of armor available. Protection affects speed and agility. Weapons are also divided into heavy and light. The choice of armor and weapons have a direct impact on battle tactics and play style. The heavier the armor, the higher its price. Any element of equipment can be customized according to your own preferences.
