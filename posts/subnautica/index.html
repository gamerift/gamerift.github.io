Game Description#

In the world ofSubnautica, your ship has crashed in an oceanic area, and there is only one straight line - down to the bottom. The landscapes around you are illuminated by the sun, the coral reefs shimmer beautifully under the sun as you slowly sink towards the endless darkness of space. Exploring a huge open world allows you not only to plunge under the water and look at the inhabitants of this ocean, but also to build your own underwater base there and build a research center on it. The player is also given an understanding of the laws of the underwater world.

The classic adventure game with elements of survival tells the story of an explorer who sailed on a ship that crashed. We escaped in a special capsule, so our main goal now is survival in a new place. Immediately, such important items as water, food, oxygen are revealed to us, for which there is a race in the underwater world.

The player will have to collect materials to create improvised items in the first levels of Subnautica. Knives, auxiliary items and equipment for deep diving. At the later stages of the game, equipment drawings for complete immersion in the depths of the ocean will be available. The developers also provided for the creation of their own home - a base. There is a warehouse, technological devices, protection from the inhabitants of the aquatic world. During the entire journey, the player will have to find out: who actually designed the levels, what is beyond the seabed, and also get acquainted with the inhabitants of the water, who should be feared at night.

Subnautica allows you to immerse yourself in the exciting world of survival RPG, where the main charm is the depths of the sea. The central feature is that the events take place on a planet almost completely submerged under water. The hero’s spaceship falls into this wet environment, forcing him to put on his scuba gear and go in search of salvation. Plot elements are flexibly integrated into the gameplay, but the game is much more fun to tell about itself in the gameplay component.

Exploring the vast underwater world is fraught with incredible beauty and tangible danger. While looking at the fascinating riches of the underwater world, you need to take care of food and oxygen supply, and also avoid painful encounters with predators. It is no coincidence that the aesthetic appeal of the project is so loudly affirmed - with the support of a graphics engine and competent game design, the dive into the depths of the amazing world is filled with wonderful meetings and discoveries. A huge variety of flora and fauna is made with the greatest love and detail, everything sways and breathes just like in reality. Subnautica is not only a visually enchanting work, but also a captivating simulation of an aquatic kingdom explorer.
