Game Description#

Krampus is Home is an atmospheric horror game in which there is the possibility of online co-op on the Internet, as well as PvP confrontation between players. On the pirate Krampus is Home, you can play online for free with a friend, or compete in multiplayer matches for up to 7 people. At the center of the story is a boy named Sebastian. The horror film takes place on Christmas Eve.

The hero is left alone at home and has to go through a surreal test in which he must do everything possible to survive until the morning. Online play allows you to fight an ancient evil, or take the side of an otherworldly entity. When playing in solo mode, you need to survive by hiding from monsters and trying to be as inconspicuous as possible. Artificial intelligence reacts to any noise.

Also included in the free online game are planning and tactics mechanics. It is necessary to use various tricks and tricks to deceive your opponents. Confrontations take place on numerous interactive maps. There are screamers in the game. Each enemy has unique abilities and attack patterns. By joining forces or acting in confrontation mode, you need to defeat the evil from European folklore, whose name is Krampus.
