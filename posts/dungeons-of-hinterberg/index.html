Game Description#

Dungeons of Hinterberg is not your typical dungeon crawl. Sure, there are monsters and loot, but this game adds a plot twist - the idyllic Austrian Alps. Yes, you heard right. Just imagine: fresh mountain air, charming townspeople and… oh yes, dungeons teeming with monsters. Welcome to Hinterberg, a tourist hotspot with a hidden side hustle: monster slaying. So, you are Louise, a savvy lawyer, ready to ditch your corporate routine for a vacation with a twist.

Hinterberg beckons with its adventures - exterminating monsters in real dungeons? Write it down. But dungeon is not just hacking with a sword. Mysteries lurk around every corner, requiring your brains as well as your brawn. Consider magical explosions to clear a path, or using the environment itself to solve a puzzle. Hinterberg’s dungeons are more like escape rooms with fangs.

But the fun in Dungeons of Hinterberg doesn’t end once you conquer the dungeon. Hinterberg is a lively village and the locals are more than just friendly faces. Louise’s growth as a monster slayer depends on them. By building relationships with these people, Louise discovers new skills and gains valuable knowledge about the hidden world around her. And it’s not just about gaining experience points - sometimes the best improvements come during a friendly conversation over a glass of local beer.
