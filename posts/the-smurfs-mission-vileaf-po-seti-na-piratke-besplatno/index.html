On the pirateThe Smurfs Mission Vileaf you can play online for free, going through various challenges with a friend or other players online online. The Smurfs - Operation Evilleaf is a vibrant adventure with elements of role-playing, action and platforming. The possibility of free online play allows you to take control of different heroes, including Smurfette, Strong Man, Znayka and Cook.

The plot tells about the sorcerer Gargamel, who finds a way to grow the tree of evil. The main characters will have to visit 5 unique worlds to stop the villain. The online game The Smurfs Mission Vileaf is designed for cooperative interaction between participants. You need to solve various puzzles, overcome obstacles and fight enemies. Protagonists must avoid poisonous plants. Their goal is to find an antidote that can heal the forest.

At the same time, you have to save the village and other Smurfs.
