Game Description#

MOONY Black Lotus is a journey through the ruins of a once prosperous civilization, where the past is a collection of disparate fragments. In this dystopian world filled with government conspiracies and unforgiving mechanisms, the main character must face the truth about his life. Moving through the remains of the city, where shadows flicker in unison with the metallic echo in the mind, the character must restore the lost essence.

Advancing deeper into this waking nightmare requires not only dexterity, but also strategic thinking to circumvent numerous threats. The search for his father’s legacy becomes the main goal for the young protagonist, forcing him to piece together his own story. This side-scroller combines exploration of murky depths with puzzle solving and classic platforming elements. As your adventure progresses in MOONY:Black_Lotus, you encounter mysterious strangers whose abilities can be used to overcome obstacles.

For example, one gains the ability to pass through strong steel bars or see in complete darkness, which opens up new ways to explore the environment. Each new memory brings us closer to the solution, but at the same time plunges us deeper into the secrets of the hidden corruption that destroyed this world. The gameplay is built on constant vigilance, because danger lurks around every corner. To survive and continue your quest, you must actively use stealth while avoiding ruthless enemies known as the Lotus. The protagonist will have to uncover forgotten stories and face the truth, no matter how difficult it may be.
