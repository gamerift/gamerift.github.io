Game Description#

The first-person shooter -Far Cry 3 - will teach you how to survive in the wild conditions of the open world, more or less value relationships with good people, and show you what real madness is. The series rewarded us with enough action, showing that a huge arsenal of weapons is just part of what can be given to players in return for their desire to enjoy a completely open world and a good story.

We fly to the island to relax. Our company is American residents who know neither hassle nor trouble. But one day it happens that a whole crowd of thugs comes after us, who were well paid to get our bodies. Behind all this is Vaas Montenegro - a sadist, drug addict, psychopath and owner of the largest crime syndicate on the entire coast. The new island becomes your home.

You see how your friends are captured one by one, and the only task for the rest of the game is to understand what happened to your friends, how to get them out and whether it is possible to do this before they are killed. As we progress, we will upgrade our hero with tattoos, which will fill his entire body by the end of the game. They are needed for the character’s new passive and active abilities.

The gameplay of the game is divided into 3 possible ones: stealth, action and fast passage. The project includes main and secondary missions, and each of them requires you to kill enemies. Gradually, new connections will be established on the island, with whom you will eventually come to Vaas and kill him, but that’s another story.

In the game we will have to hack systems, climb towers, find and sell weapons, create our own economy and upgrade every piece of ammunition. Try to complete Far Cry 3 without killing a single person (except for animals that have valuable fur for crafting).
