Game Description#

Just Die Already is a 3D open world adventure that allows you to play online with a friend, competing in open interactive locations. Online internet connection available. The pirate site offers all network capabilities for free. The visual style is made in low-poly graphics. A huge open world is available for exploration, where anything can kill. The environment is just waiting for the player to hit the oak tree. The project features funny ragdoll and funny character physics.

The creators of the adventure are the famous developers of Goat Simulator, so you can understand what awaits players on this crazy journey. You can run, jump, fly on a jetpack, ride in vehicles and much more. It is only important to remember that every object and object can kill, smearing the character in a bloody puddle all over the asphalt. You can use trout, trampolines, pistols, wires, fireworks, bazookas, axes, trombones, horns, jetpacks, etc. Each location contains hidden locations and caches. Features of Just Die Already: playing for weak grandfathers who are trying to fall down the stairs or slip out of the blue; control of a grandmother or grandfather; the ability to break bones and dislocate a joint; you can load an old man into a catapult or tear off his head; receiving rewards for successfully completing tasks; competitions for up to 5 people; tearing off opponents’ limbs and total brutality.
