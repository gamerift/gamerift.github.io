Game Description#

Holoreversi is a tactical table puzzle that offers a fresh look at the classic rules of reversali with the participation of virtual talents from the Hololive group. The project introduces original mechanics into the familiar process, such as the ability to put chips on each other. This technique, called the accumulation, allows you to carry out powerful chain reactions during a coup of stops, radically changing the situation on the board. In addition, on the field you can place special totemicized chips that the opponent cannot turn over, which adds another layer of strategic depth to each batch.

Each Hololive presented talent has a unique skill that can turn the course of the battle. For example, characters from the Nepolabo group, including Momosuza Nene, Omara Regiment, Yukikhan Lami and Shishiro Botan, bring their special abilities to the duel. In the process of passing Holoreversi, new skins for boards and chips open, and some of them even change voice acting. You can check your strength in solitary mode against computer opponents and in online matches with other connoisseurs of tactical confrontations from around the world.

In addition to the competitive component, the project offers collecting. In a special gallery, you can view unlocked illustrations and listen to replicas of your favorite characters. The battles are held on bright, stylized boards, and the chips themselves are made in the form of Hololive participants, which creates a unique atmosphere. Such a combination of classical rules with innovative ideas and thematic design makes each batch unpredictable and fascinating until the very last move, demanding from duelists of constant concentration and the ability to adapt to a changing environment.
