Game Description#

Midori no Kaori is an adventure game based on the art of peaceful store management. In this relaxed environment, gamers will create and manage their own establishment, drawing on elements of Japanese culture to shape their vision. The title doesn’t focus on fast-paced action or intense competition, but rather encourages players to create a space that reflects their personal taste. There are no strict goals or deadlines, making it a refreshing alternative to many other games that prioritize speed or performance. The goal here is simple: relax, design and immerse yourself in a world built on creativity and tranquility.

One of the main features of Midori no Kaori is its emphasis on customization. Gamers can customize almost every aspect of their store, from the structure of the building itself to the smallest decorative details. The freedom of personalization goes beyond the traditional limitations found in other games, allowing for a completely customized experience. Whether it’s the choice of wall color or the perfect placement of furniture, every decision contributes to creating a unique store atmosphere. This makes the process feel organic, as if the store is a reflection of the user’s aesthetic choice rather than something dictated by a set formula. The adventure also draws heavily on nature and traditional Japanese design elements.
