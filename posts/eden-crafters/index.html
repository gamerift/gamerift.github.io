Game Description#

Eden Crafters throws glove in the hands of survival and automation lovers. In this cooperative adventure with the open world, players will turn to turn a hostile planet into a hospitable refuge for humanity. Think about this as a space project of reconstruction - harsh conditions, fluids of the house of dreams. Task? Overcome the unpleasant habits of the planet and build a new house for humanity. Imagine radioactive zones and insidious landscapes are not the best real estate. Gamers will have to dispose of resources as a professional, create the necessary tools and, in fact, become McGaver of wildlife.

This is where the most interesting begins: Eden Crafters is a resource management and a smart solution to problems. Users collect materials to build the foundation of a new civilization, simultaneously creating the necessary tools. The game allows your inner inventor to take a walk. Create intricate conveyor ribbons and mechanisms to automate boring affairs, such as collecting and processing resources. These are not just fashion gadgets - this is the key to terraforming the planet.

It’s time to turn on the thermostat, create a favorable atmosphere and change the poisonous view of the lake to something more refreshing. The action takes place in a huge open world, which is asked to be created. It doesn’t matter whether you prefer a solitary life or are going with friends in a network cooperative, the mission remains unchanged: to turn this world into a paradise suitable for breathing. Toxic lakes? Rather, in the pools of the future. This is the chance of mankind to create a new Eden among the stars, and who does not like good projects “do yourself”?
