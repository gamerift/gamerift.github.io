Game Description#

Lovers in a Dangerous Spacetime is a space shooter that can be played online online. A cooperative mode has been introduced, allowing you to play online with a friend for free on a pirate. By uniting as a team and coordinating actions, you will have to fight the forces of anti-love. The main goal is to save the space babies. The online game is designed for four people.

Not only is there a free online game available, but there is also a single-player campaign that includes a variety of levels with varying degrees of difficulty. Events take place in outer space. In Lovers in a Dangerous Spacetime you need to work together, controlling the space shuttle. The opportunity to play for free allows you to feel like real partners in combat. The ship consists of guns, engines and shields. Each participant has a special role in controlling the ship.

While exploring the expanses of the galaxy, you need to collect crystals to upgrade your ship. By equipping the vessel with new body kits, you will need to engage in battle with numerous enemies and strong bosses. The opponents are evil robots, cosmic dust, alien invaders, etc. With each subsequent level, the difficulty of the gameplay increases, and the enemies become much more aggressive.
