Game Description#

The cooperative horrorThe Escape: Together can be played online with a friend or on a pirate for free, gather a team of up to three people to escape together online from an evil spirit holed up in a huge house. To escape and survive, you need to interact with each other and collect all the useful items that can be found in the location.

In the story, brothers and sisters are left alone at home and a paranormal creature begins to hunt them. A network game is built on both mutual assistance and division of tasks. There is only one global goal - to escape from the mansion. To do this, you will need to perform a number of special actions. Free online play in The Escape: Together invites you to explore the house together to find all the key items. Quest objects must be used in special places, opening a path for yourself to escape from the cursed house.

The game contains a huge number of riddles and puzzles. They must be solved in order to open access to previously closed rooms or to obtain a necessary item. Well-coordinated teamwork is the key to success. You can play with friends, alone or with computer partners. The arrangement of items and the sequence of rooms can change, thereby providing each new launch with a completely unique experience.
