Game Description#

InEnshrouded you can play for free online as a pirate, alone or with a friend, exploring the vastness of fantasy lands. Across the internet, online players take on the role of the Flameborn as they attempt to save their race from extinction. The possibility of free online play allows up to 16 people to team up on one server to jointly fight enemies and explore the vibrant world of the kingdom of Ambervale. The gameplay is an action RPG with a huge emphasis on survival and crafting. The online game Enshrouded invites each traveler to choose his own unique hero’s path. However, everyone has a common goal - to ignite the ancient power of the Flame in order to unravel the secrets of the mysterious world.

You will need to fight with creatures that have been swallowed up by the Veil, forcing them to carry out the will of others. In the forests you must fight monsters, and in the caves, angry Wookiees wait. The misty regions are home to dangerous and secretive fallen. The combat system is based on dodging and parrying blows. It is necessary to look for an individual approach to each enemy, trying to find its weak points. Using an extensive skill tree, each Flameborn can create an individual build for their character. From the collected materials you can not only craft weapons or tools, but also build a shelter. By finding survivors in the depths of Ambervale, you can unlock epic armor or weapons. Weapons and armor are improved. Each player is able to master a shield, sword, staff or bow. The territories consist of Brushwood Wastes or the thicket of the Rampant Forest.
