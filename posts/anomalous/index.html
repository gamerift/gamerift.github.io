Game Description#

Anomalous takes users to the fictional and totalitarian city-state of Kosolov. Local authorities were able to brainwash the population and forced residents to work until exhaustion in order to get rich at the expense of others. One day, the leaders of an industrial and closed country began to experiment on people. They decided to increase the military potential of the power and began to conduct experiments on dead workers in order to revive them and force them to perform their duties again. Attempts to use regenerative biomass and install bioimplants have led to disaster. After this event, the city was filled with mutants. The main character’s task is to get beyond it. Maps represent closed and open spaces that are full of secrets. Among the scenery there are destructible objects. For example: barrels and boxes.

Anomalous is a retro first-person shooter. It offers fast and dynamic gameplay in the spirit of 90’s jokers. There is no need to reload the guns here - it is only important to monitor the ammunition. The cartridges themselves, like armor and first aid kits, are scattered throughout the area. You can also find bonuses at levels that provide temporary boosts such as increased damage. In close combat, the protagonist fights with an ax and a shovel. For attacks from afar, he has a pistol, shotgun, machine gun, rocket launcher, machine gun, double-barreled shotgun, plasma gun, crossbow, sniper and laser rifle. New trunks must also be looked for at locations. The fighter is able to carry the entire arsenal on himself.
