Game Description#

In Shikiyoku -teru Yumemi in the Nightmare- Paranormal detective Teru Yumami uses the ability of Dream Link to penetrate the worlds created by ghosts after death. His sister Luna helps him return to reality with the help of his strength, a curse of the red thread. Together they investigate paranormal incidents and help people who have learned too much, performing a mission inherited from the late mother.

The plot develops in the format of the detective series, and the entire gameplay with the study of locations and mini-stories is designed for 10 hours. The mechanics of investigations in Shikiyoku -teru Yumemi in the Nightmare - obliges to collect information in a variety of places, including a high school, the Hosta quarter to Shinjuku and Akihabaru. Teru himself works as a host, adding the plot an unexpected turn. The narrative of this project is built around the human drama on the topic of lust, or Shikiyoku, mixing comedy moments with an increasingly serious and gloomy mood.

Such a balance determines the gameplay, allowing the players to gradually reveal confusing secrets. In the course of the history, 9 episodes of individual cases are intertwined in a single large -scale storyline. Its nucleus becomes a strong connection between Teru and the Moon, as well as the mystery of the death of their mother, which occurred 4 years ago. The driving force of events is incidents generated by a phenomenon called the curse of Shikiyoku. This game begins as a horror adventure with lateral scrolling, but develops into a serious narrative leading his brother and sister to forbidden lands.
