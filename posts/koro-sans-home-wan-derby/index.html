Game Description#

Koro-San’s Home Wan! Derby puts the player in the role of repelling during a special baseball event. The character Koro-san organizes the game and invites other Hololive gamers, but the situation arises: all the audience want to be pitchens. Such a arrangement leaves Koro-san with the only figure on the home base. The appointed listeners take on the entire defense in the field, allowing you to concentrate exclusively on the blows. The main task is to continue to beat off the ball.

Gameplay in Koro-San’s Home Wan! Derby is built on reactions to flying balls and the performing of the Homeran. It is necessary to cope with magical feeds from opponents and their supporters. Progress is tied to the achievement of a certain number of home ranges necessary for each level, and the fulfillment of this requirement opens access to the next location. The end of the final stage leads to the appearance of a previously hidden character, serving as an incentive for complete passage.

The hero’s improvement occurs through a system of characteristics that uses a currency called bread obtained for passing levels. This resource is spent on strengthening Korosan, increasing its capacity of Slagger. For another test, this casual game contains the RTA mode, designed for competition competitions with a constant timer on the screen. The timer does not stop at the time of upgrades, so the time in the menu directly affects the final result, requiring quick and effective decisions.

Five steps to the champion of the Home Ranes:

Focus on the recapture of balls flying from the pitchers. Make a home ranges to go to the following levels. Antist from the special magical feeds of opponents. Use the earned bread to enhance the characteristics of the character. Check your speed in a special RTA mode.
