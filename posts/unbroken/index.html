Game Description#

Unbroken immerses gamers on a complex and unpredictable journey. The mission is clear: to track down the traitor Nielsen, who found a shelter in this troubled world. Title offers several ways to achieve the goal, but the trip is rarely simple. When time goes, users are faced with increasing chaos, moving through unpredictable events and changing landscapes. As research, the world around them changes, reflecting the choice made in the way.

Gamers face a variety of environments, each of which has its own social structure and customs. The kingdom itself is saturated with class division, with noticeable differences between the poorest and richest regions. These differences apply not only to architecture and technology, but also to the attitude and behavior of characters. As you climb these areas, the dynamic construction of the world in the game is becoming more and more obvious, showing the harsh evolution of the world through your actions.

The battles in UNBROKEN have a fast pace and inexorability, users have to think on the go. Smol, unique mechanics, gives special abilities, such as bullet time, adding depth of the combat system. Gamers can use the environment in their interests, sending objects into flight or using enemies as shells. Grindhaus -style violence is the main aspect that enhances its intense atmosphere.
