Game Description#

FEAR 3 is a first-person shooter with elements of survival horror, the storyline of which can be followed online on the Internet. You can play online with a friend or join forces with a random participant. The gameplay is distinguished by an improved cover system compared to previous parts. A free co-op mode has been added to the pirate game, allowing you to play as an Operative or Paxton Fetell with a friend. The network game is a cooperative confrontation mode. FEAR 3 directly continues the plot of the second part.

The story centers on F.E.A.R. 3 An operative and Paxton’s disembodied spirit investigate a prison in Brazil. The hero was captured after the crash of a UH-60 Black Hawk helicopter. The possibility of free online play in FIR 3 offers confrontation with special forces from Armacham Technology, cultists, zombies, etc. The plot features Alma and Michael Beckett, the protagonist of the second part of the adventure. There are 2 endings presented: the true one - Victory of the Operative and the alternative - Triumph of Fettel. The online game has a rating system represented by statistics. After each episode, personal achievements are shown and compared with the successes of other players. The higher the result, the faster you can upgrade the hero. The ending of the adventure also depends on the accumulated points.

The gameplay for the most part has not changed. The main gameplay is focused on intense shootouts with enemies. The protagonist is able to slow down time. Allows you to control Fettel using telekinesis. The incorporeal shell can cause daze in enemies, as well as gain telepathic control over their minds. Bullet time mode applies to two players. Procedural generation allows gameplay to be unpredictable. The location of players, the location of key items, etc. change.
