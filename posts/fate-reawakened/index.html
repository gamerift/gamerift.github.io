Game Description#

Fate: Reawakened returns the classic experience of passing dungeons with a modern bias. This collection included all four games of the cult series of FATE, offering to visit the old Growl city again and go through the constantly changing gates of the dungeons. Yuzers will have to fight as a procedurally generated dungeons, each of which offers new trials and awards. There are several games in one release, so you will have something to do, and not one of the two adventures will be the same.

Title implemented many improvements that transfer their favorite series to modernity. Thanks to the improvements such as a higher number of landfills and directed lighting in real time, Fate: Reawakended seeks to create a fresh but familiar experience. The gameplay has retained its main mechanics, such as in real time and a deep system of developmental development, with which gamers are already familiar. Now, thanks to the opportunity to play in several languages and enjoy the full localization of voice acting, the title opens the door for a wider audience.

One of the distinctive features is procedural generation. Gamers will explore the dungeons in which the layout, meetings and falling loot are randomized, which makes each passage new. Whether it is a near -battle weapon, distant attacks or magic, each battle requires quick thinking and adaptation to stay alive. This is a world where power and glory are waiting for you, but earning them is not so simple.
