Game Description#

Rus vs. Lizards gives you the opportunity to play online for free and as a pirate, with a friend or alone, in a network game on the Internet online. In this exciting game you are assigned the role of defender of your native land from the invasion of alien reptiles. In your arsenal you will have a choice between a trio of heroes: Radislav, a virtuoso of bladed weapons, Vseslav, a lightning-fast wielder of a two-handed sword, and Drocheslav, an invulnerable master of martial arts with a berdysh. Your task is to inflict a crushing defeat on the armies of evil lizards.

These are no ordinary scaly enemies. They are described as unholy lizards who have allied themselves with alien forces and threaten the very heart of the land of Rus. The game promises a deep dive into Slavic mythology with mentions of gods such as Perun (god of thunder) and Svarog (god of the sky). The description mentions “godly celebrations” and “the blessing of Perun,” which hints at a plot woven from Slavic beliefs.

The main focus seems to be on combat. Expect “active combos” and “speed attacks” while defending beautiful landscapes such as Baikal and Siberia. The developers claim that everything was done “strictly in accordance with scientific documents,” although how this will affect the gameplay is still unknown.

On a humorous note, the game also boasts that its 3D graphics are “done in 8 polygons MINIMUM”, suggesting a charming low-fi aesthetic. If you’re looking for an action-packed Slavic mythology action game with a heavy emphasis on killing lizards, Lizards Must Die might be the game for you.
