Game Description#

9 Kings throws users into the very heart of the medieval kingdom, where the strategy, tactics and resource management dictates the need for survival. This urban planning Roguelike combines a lot of a mechanic, offering unpredictable experience with each launch. The expansion of the kingdom requires careful planning, because the power of the empire depends on each decision. Using a deck of various cards, rulers form their possessions, adapting to unexpected challenges.

When the rival kings strive to affirm their dominance, conflicts arise that force rulers to protect their cities from enemy waves. Barts in 9 Kings are unfolding in a combination of the defense of the tower and tactical war. Units, fortifications and spells determine the outcome, pushing leaders to experiments with various strategies. Defensive structures should be placed effectively so that the invaders cannot break through the walls.

Each card gives an advantage, and combining the desired effects leads to an uncontrollable synergistic effect. The perks obtained during the campaign change the style of the game, creating new approaches with every attempt. Victory requires adaptation, since the enemyâ€™s tactics change over time. Management plays a central role, and automation elements simplify the production and deployment of troops. Resource balancing, expansion of infrastructure and the conclusion of unions contribute to long -term survival. Diplomacy with other rulers can turn the course of events, but betrayal remains a constant threat.
