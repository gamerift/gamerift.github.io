Game Description#

Urban Strife actions take place in the post-apocalyptic world, and the gameplay is presented in the style of the old-school TBS. The main character lives in a dangerous environment, where every resident does everything, for his own benefit. The resources are running out, there is no work, and the majority kills and robs to survive. The player can enter into alliances, choosing 1 of several fractions, automatically becoming an enemy for other groups.

As soon as the central character selects the side, he will have to fulfill instructions for remuneration and just like that, increasing the reputation and trust of people. He has to make sorties, get food and materials, and then be attributed to the base. The resources found will allow you to create weapons and useful consumables. Equipment items gradually wear out and require repair with prolonged use. People inside the faction need food and new opportunities that only the protagonist can get.

The combat system is made in a step -by -step style. There is a storyline that develops as the main quests are performed. The user has numerous elections that affect the outcome of the whole history. It all starts with how the ward awakens after amnesia. He was told that he was in half a dead state, but good people helped him knock out. Having risen to his feet, he will have to become the leader of the distant city shelter in the south of the United States, and then help people. You can negotiate and create alliances with other organizations.
