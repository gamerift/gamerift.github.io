Game Description#

Tony Hawk’s Pro Skater 3 + 4 combines elements from the two previous parts of the series, representing their content in the form of a single package. The project includes a wider composition of available skaters and represents completely different parks for research. The player performs sequences of tricks in these environment, striving for the performance of specific tasks. This collection also adds fresh opportunities for tricks to the arsenal of movements, expanding the methods of interacting with the level of level.

Online functionality is the central part of Tony Hawk’s Pro Skater 3 + 4, offering multi-user races up to 8 people with a cross-platform connection. These sessions take place both in new and returned regimes. The editors Create-a-Skater and Create-a-Park are expanded, providing more tools for customization. The new function allows gamers to create unique goals in their custom parks, which can then be shared with others online so that they can try to fulfill them.

This game recreates parks from the third and fourth parts of the franchise, presented with simplified tasks and the standard format of a 2-minute session. Users will find more complex trials and an improved mode of the new game that changes the main gameplay. The control scheme is modeled according to the system used in the release of the first two parts. This design provides a specific method for studying and performing combinations of tricks for both beginners and experienced skaters.
