Game Description#

Greedland invites players to play online, throwing them into an unforgiving vortex of alien mutants. This is a high-octane shooter that you can play with a friend on a pirate for free. Combining roguelike and survivalist elements, the online game requires you to destroy hordes of alien baddies faster than you can say “They’re coming!” Starting with a basic Peashooter, in multiplayer you’ll quickly gain enough firepower to make even the toughest alien think twice.

As you progress, Greedland dumps everything but the kitchen sink on you. We’re talking waves of mutants, each uglier and more evil than the last. But here’s the catch: the more you explode, the cooler you become. Before you know it, you’ll be wielding plasma disks, orbital cannons, and destroyers like a one-man army. It’s like going from a water pistol to a tactical nuclear bomb in the blink of an eye!

Now let’s talk about the arsenal. Greedland has more weapons than you can shake a stick at. And we’re not just talking about ordinary peashooters, friends. Each weapon has its own unique charge - from explosions that destroy the air to laser beams that will make you feel like a fantasy superhero. Mix and match them as you please - the battlefield is your oyster! And if you’re feeling down, why not call a friend? Local co-op mode lets you share the fun of alien explosions. After all, misery loves company, right?
