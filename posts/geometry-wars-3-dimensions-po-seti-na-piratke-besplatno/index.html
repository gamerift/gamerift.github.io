Game Description#

Geometry Wars 3: Dimensions is a dynamic twin-stick shooter that gives you the opportunity to play online with a friend, competing with each other on a pirate game for free. It is possible to unite online against a common enemy via the Internet. Game mechanics develop the ideas of the previous two parts, improving balance, effects, graphics, etc. An online game designed for two players is presented, and a full-fledged story solo mode is also available.

The Geometry Wars 3: Dimensions campaign consists of over 100 unique levels. Each subsequent stage presents a new challenge to players. There are also over 40 bonus locations available. The gameplay is a classic arcade shooter, where reaction speed and attentiveness decide. Battles take place on 15 three-dimensional grids. A total of 12 confrontation modes are available, including Pacifism, King, Claustrophobia and 4 additional types of battles.

Free online play allows each participant to receive 6 unique companion drones, including Attack, Collect, Ram, Snipe, Defend and Sweep. A more difficult Hardcore mode is available. The speed of the game has been increased several times and the enemies behave much more aggressively. On hardcore, drones and super attacks are disabled. A separate leaderboard is provided, which differs from the global lists of players on normal difficulty.
