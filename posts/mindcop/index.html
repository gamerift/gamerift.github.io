Game Description#

Mindcop is a detective story where gamers go on a journey through a tangled web of secrets and deception. The action takes place in the small, seemingly calm town of Merrilin Crater Camp, and at the center of events is a murder that disrupts the peace and quiet of the village. Players take on the role of a detective with an unusual ability: he can penetrate the minds of suspects. With only five days to solve the case, the game turns into a race against time, where every decision matters.

Time passes as users collect evidence, interrogate suspects and use their unique mental abilities to get to the bottom of the truth. At the heart of Mindcop is detective work that unfolds through a process of investigation and interaction. Gamers investigate crime scenes, engage in conversations and, most importantly, use a gameplay mechanic called mind surfing. It allows users to delve into the minds of suspects and navigate their subconscious to piece together hidden truths.

Mini-games in these journeys of consciousness serve to uncover crucial clues and motives that may lead the investigation further. Each suspect presents a different challenge, and no two minds are alike. However, time is of the essence. Every action, be it questioning a witness or mind surfing, takes up precious minutes. How users manage their time is becoming one of the most important issues.
