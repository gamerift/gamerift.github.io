Game Description#

BlazBlue: Continuum Shift Extend is a continuation of a dynamic fighting game made in anime style. You can play online with a friend, fighting for free on a pirate. Local mode and online confrontations are available. The sequel introduces several new modes, various story scenarios, additional heroes, online play and a lot of other content.

The story begins after the Second War ended. The Librarium branches were completely wiped out from the face of the earth. There are not many survivors left who are afraid of only one person like fire, pronouncing his name in a whisper. BlazBlue: Continuum Shift Extend introduces many new heroes, including Makoto, Valkenhin and Platinum Trinity. The possibility of free online play allows you to fight for Relius Clover. He uses the puppet Ignis to assist him in battle.

Some events are closely intertwined with the previous game Calamity Trigger. Four unique scenarios will tell different stories. Mars and Abyss are introduced as updated modes. Both old and new characters have their own unique fighting style, weapons, combinations and super attacks. A training mode is introduced that allows you to study the fighter in detail. Various multiplayer features have been added, including various team content.
