Game Description#

The firefighter simulatorInto The Flames can be played online for free on a pirate account, working with a friend to save people throughout the city. Using the Internet online, you can team up in a whole team of experienced rescuers whose goal is to respond to emergency calls and eliminate disasters. The gameplay allows you to control vehicles, be it a fire truck or an ambulance, knock down doors with an ax, activate hydrants and control the most extreme situations in every possible way.

The network game is designed for up to 12 people. Each member must perform a unique function within the squad. The ability to play free online games is aimed at the ability to cooperate. Into The Flames requires you to give orders and complete assigned goals. The gameplay is influenced by many factors. For example, a lot may depend on the weather or terrain. Fires occur in both commercial establishments and rural areas. It is important to think about equipment and tactics in advance.

Emergencies are caused by gas leaks, careless handling of fire, and much more. New situations and activities gradually open up. You can drive a volunteer, sitting behind the wheel of a car or SUV. About a dozen types of vehicles are presented. Each situation has several development options. Depending on the chosen equipment and strategy, further actions can develop in a completely unexpected scenario. For successful completion of missions, experience is awarded and the level of firefighters increases.
