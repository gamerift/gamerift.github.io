Game Description#

Grimshire represents a script with a spreading plague that threatens an isolated mountain settlement. The player arrives here after the absorption of his hometown with chaos, getting a chance for a new beginning on the local farm. Its role consists in managing the economy, collecting forest gifts and ensuring a permanent reserve in the cellar. The main task is to prevent bloating of food in preparation for the arrival of the disease, which requires competent supplies to maintain everyone’s life.

Local inhabitants must act together in order to increase collective chances of surviving. GrimShire fakes an emphasis on cooperation, manifested in the collection of materials for general construction projects and transferring items to research for the discovery of new rescue methods. The gamer has to interact with other characters and make friendship with them, building connections inside the settlement. The plot directly affects the topic of possible losses, clearly making it clear that not all members of the community will be able to survive future events.

Five pillars of survival in the face of the plague:

Tire farm management and supplies of supplies. Unification of efforts with settlers for the implementation of general projects. The study of the surroundings in search of valuable resources. Mastering conservation methods to prevent tilting of food. Building a relationship, realizing the risk of a possible loss of everyone.

Providing the community with everything necessary includes the production and conservation of food. The entire gameplay is built on the cultivation of fields, planting gardens and breeding animals to obtain eggs, milk and meat, as well as on the use of smoking and salting methods to preserve supplies. The study of the environment is another component of this title: you can catch fish in local reservoirs or go to mines by ore.
