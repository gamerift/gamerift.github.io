Game Description#

Shootouts in Moros Protocol take place in a sci-fi setting where users have to face a variety of mutated monsters. The game is stylized as old-school designs drawn in pixel graphics. The gameplay is an action first-person shooter with roguelike elements. The main character is forced to confront the monsters and bandits that appeared in the universe due to the activities of two megacorporations involved in neurobiology.

In the Darkverse universe, users have the opportunity to explore dark laboratory dungeons that will be formed randomly. In battle with enemies you have to use a wide arsenal of weapons, including firearms, shotguns and high-tech guns. Players will be able to try out the following characters: Alex (a systems expert who has an impulsive character), Abby (an AI who controls a spaceship and has an ironic sense of humor) and Odie (a 6nar droid who is a robotic assistant).

During the passage, players have to wander through various locations and shoot enemies with various weapons. Equipment items and other bonus elements will be scattered around the map, which strengthen the protagonist. Gamers will also be able to upgrade their characters, acquiring new combat abilities, ammunition, guns and consumables. The plot will involve dealing with a mysterious mystery that affects the fate of the heroes.
