Game Description#

Lunar Eclipse begins when the fair -haired main character wakes up in her house, where she has lived all her life, now characterized by deep silence and the absence of its inhabitants. This sudden emptiness and a change in the familiar situation encourage her to look for the reasons for the widespread disappearance. Its promotion includes movement by locations with weak lighting and deserted corridors, areas that hide unknown dangers.

It follows its path, moving the desire to understand the new state of the world and the fate of all the others. The mechanics of the game in Lunar Eclipse are focused on the examination of specific locations containing many objects, and the solution of interconnected puzzles. The gameplay requires the heroine to overcome physical obstacles: climb into structures, climb through close openings and move along vertical surfaces to find the paths. The successful solution of these logical tasks is presented as a way to comprehend the essence of the events.

The plot develops as the protagonist alone passes through these quiet, gloomy sections of the once familiar place. The battles are direct skirmishes at a short distance with aggressive opponents. The kitchen knife serves as the main means of self -defense, which determines the battle in the immediate vicinity of the threats. Each action committed by the player during such physical contacts has direct consequences, turning skirmishes even with ordinary hostile creatures in tense moments that require accurate execution. The key goal is to survive in such contractions, simultaneously finding out the causes of global changes and the disappearance of people.
