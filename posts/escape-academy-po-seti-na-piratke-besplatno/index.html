Game Description#

Escape Academy is a quest with the ability to play online with a friend on a pirate for free and offers to become an escape master. Over the Internet, online or locally, players can team up to get into the Escape Academy. Here you will have to solve various puzzles and puzzles together. The online game Escape Academy consists of confrontation modes and cooperative missions. You will need to hack servers, get to know the faculty, and much more. But the main thing is that the possibility of free online play allows you to just have a cup of tea together!

The action takes place in a specialized school, where they teach their students the skill of escaping from any trap. There are more than a dozen unique quest rooms created by hand. A special feature of the gameplay is that all the rooms were created by experts in the field of escape rooms. They were based on real puzzles and existing puzzles, which adds realism to what is happening.

A single player mode is available, and there are also several different multiplayer competitions, including co-op room completion. You will need to explore the campus, meet the students of the academy and much more. Each character is a colorful hero with his own character, secrets and characteristics. The difficulty of the gameplay increases with each subsequent stage. Some puzzles can be solved quickly alone, while others are designed to be completed through interaction.
