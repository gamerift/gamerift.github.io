Game Description#

MX VS ATV Legends shows what intensive and at the same time dangerous racing competitions can be. Here, users are invited to participate in mass races on the proposed routes. Each location is full of surprises, so you need to carefully monitor the behavior of the vehicle so as not to fly from the distance at the most inopportune moment. Moreover, each type of transport has its own unique characteristics, and even the most experienced riders will need time to adapt and get used to the manner of behavior.

And although MX VS ATV Legends is positioned as an arcade, management here requires skills. So, for example, the difference between a quadrocycket and a motorcycle is noticeable after the first race, and if you take into account the features of each track, there will be a lot of differences. Moreover, stock equipment of any unit is significantly inferior to the top assembly in almost all aspects. Therefore, if possible, new spare parts should be purchased, as well as supply the rider with new equipment. As for the game modes, in addition to the selective races, you can take part in the career, having previously selected the suitable category. Motorcycles, ATVs, bugges and other things will dissect on the beaches, in the mountains or in open areas. Moreover, the further the user goes, the more difficult the conditions on a specific route become.
