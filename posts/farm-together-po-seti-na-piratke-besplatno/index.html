Game Description#

Farm Together is an agricultural simulator that you can play online with a friend, using all the multiplayer capabilities of the project for free on a pirate site. You can cooperate on a common screen or compete online on the Internet. Single player mode is available in Farm Together. Players develop a farm by growing or harvesting crops, planting trees, taking care of thinness, etc.

When selling goods, you need to buy improvements, new buildings or tools. Network play allows you to both develop your farm together and compete for the title of the best agricultural worker. As you gain experience, you can unlock many additional items and objects that will help you improve your own possessions. Free online play includes co-op play through the story campaign.

You can get behind the wheel of a tractor to explore your territory. However, it is necessary to monitor the fuel. Allows you to play in free mode, relaxing and simply developing your vegetable base. Together with friends or alone, you need to cultivate the soil, or raid opponents' territories, engaging in vandalism. A full-fledged editor is available that allows you to customize your avatar, tractor or farm itself, choosing fences, roads, buildings, decorations and much more.
