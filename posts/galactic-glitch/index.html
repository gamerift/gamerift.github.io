Game Description#

Galactic Glitch is a Roguelike shutter based on physics, in which manipulations with objects play a decisive role in achieving success. The main attention is paid to the use of the environment and weaknesses of the enemy by creative use of the gravitational gun. Instead of relying only on traditional firepower, users need to think using stones, mines and even enemies as a weapon. Acute sensations when you throw an asteroid in the face of the enemy or launch rockets into it, are the main part of the gameplay.

Each passage of Galactic Glitch sets new tasks thanks to the unpredictable mechanics of the game physics. The arenas are filled with destroyed objects, and users are invited to use them in their interests. Here you can always find something new, because it encourages the study of hidden areas and collecting upgrades and objects. These discoveries can completely change the course of the race, allowing gamers with each new attempt to create the perfect configuration.

As they move, gamers open new abilities that offer new ways to solve game problems. The strengthening is diverse, and they can be combined differently to create unique strategies. Whether it is the modernization of a gravitational gun or the acquisition of new weapons - there is something to experiment with. The Roguelike element guarantees that no race will be the same, which makes the gameplay fresh and dynamic.
