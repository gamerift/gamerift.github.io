Game Description#

Wreckfest is a derby car simulator that allows you to take part in a confrontation with a friend or fight with players around the world online for free. You can play online on a pirate or use a local connection. Next Car Game is the spiritual successor to FlatOut. Several competition modes are presented: Single career, Own event and Network play. All cars have a detailed physical model of damage, behave individually on the roads and are presented with unique characteristics that can be changed.

For winning races you are awarded money and experience points. They allow you to buy new types of transport or update spare parts for your car. The gameplay can be described as a race for survival. Wreckfest’s free online play features include classic circuit races and demolition derby, in which you have to ram your opponent’s cars while trying to keep your vehicle running. Derbies take place in huge interactive arenas.

Each type of car is subject to tuning and styling. All cars are presented in a class that allows you to participate in certain events. The degree of damage affects the behavior of the car on the track, and can also completely slow down the vehicle. If the suspension or engine fails completely, the competitor is eliminated from the competition. The better the race result, the more victory points are awarded as a result.

Career mode contains 5 challenging championships. In the single-player campaign, you can set the difficulty level of your opponents. Test mode introduced. Some must be completed to unlock unique vehicles, while others are only additional, affecting the global ranking. Multiplayer races can include competitions of up to 16 participants.
