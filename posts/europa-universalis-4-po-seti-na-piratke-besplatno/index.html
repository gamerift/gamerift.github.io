Game Description#

InEuropa Universalis 4 you can play online with a friend, and also fight online with random people on the Internet, using all the multiplayer features on the pirate for free. The gameplay is a global strategy, the events of which start from the late Middle Ages and end with the Modern Age. The online game allows you to take part in the Battle of Varna and witness the death of Napoleon.

Mechanics include trade, diplomacy, warfare, colonization, and more. The global map is represented by the entire planet Earth. It is divided into regions and provinces controlled by nations. Each country has its own unique features, units and tactical advantages. Provinces provide various resources: money, recruits, sailors, etc. The free-to-play online game features both co-op and adversarial play. Each player is engaged in the development of the army, diplomacy and economy.

It is required to obtain resources such as prestige, stability and gold. Views within the country may consist of monarchy, republican traditions, the righteousness of the theocracy, and unity among the horde. The military aspects represent the creation of an army, vassal states, espionage and much more. A huge selection of religions is available in Europa Universalis 4: Christianity, Islam, paganism, etc.

The factor of chance plays a huge role. All events are generated randomly. Allows the development of advanced technologies. They are divided into three types: administrative, diplomatic and military. Each of them is aimed at improving one or another aspect of the life of their country. Depending on the development branch, a nation receives various bonuses and boosts that determine the style of play.
