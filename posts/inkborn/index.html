Game Description#

Inkborn represents a card combat system based on the consistent use of glyphs for a tactical effect. Gamers have cards according to special symbols, activating chain results during step -by -step clashes with otherworldly creatures. Such a gameplay is the basis for promotion. The scene is a world created from ink, folded paper and metal containing a hidden story for disclosure.

The narrative unfolds as the expeditions are completed and the tasks set. Before each expedition to Inkborn, players distribute points in the personal tree of skills to determine the strategy for the race. Progress includes the use of skills from past attempts for the development of new abilities, while each class has access to 30 different perks to choose from. Between travels, the central city operates as a hub, which can improve preparations for subsequent attempts.

This cycle of expeditions and preparation opens many approaches to the challenges of this game, as different builds become available. Moving along the procedured map opens new ways through the fog of the war. The choice of the player in the process of studying this location determines the route to the final collision of the act. More than 250 cards are available for the collection, and during the passage you can find more than 100 of their combinations. In addition, the world of this project offers 40 script events with unpredictable situations and more than 100 quotes that give advantages during the race.
