Game Description#

Colony Siege is a strategy game with the ability to play online with a friend or random players, fighting on a pirate ship for free in various cooperative modes or versus matches. You can join forces online against a common enemy or arrange epic battles with the participation of several people. In Colony Siege you need to build bases, create labyrinths, train units, etc. The online game invites each opposing side to defend its colonies, destroying enemies with a variety of deadly weapons.

You can burn your opponents, cut them, blow them up, and otherwise destroy them in the most brutal way. The possibility of free online play includes cooperative playthrough of the story campaign for two. There are elements of tower defense. By joining forces, it is necessary to contain the hordes of alien monsters. They attack in waves. With each subsequent stage there are more of them, and their strength increases.

There are 2 factions available: Xenos and Rifts. Each opposing side has its own unique type of weapons, type of units and special abilities. Playing as a rift, you need to build defensive towers and barriers against a swarm of Xenos. There are mobile units, traps, laser barriers, etc. Having taken the side of the alien invaders, you will have to attack your enemy’s base. For successful completion of tasks, money is awarded. They need to be spent on new units and upgrades. A variety of bonuses can make defense easier. You can travel between planets and stations.
