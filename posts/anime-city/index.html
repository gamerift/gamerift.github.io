Game Description#

Anime City transfers gamers to a bright 3D virtual Tokyo, inspired by anime culture. Title combines the mechanics of the visual novel and sandbox, creating an interactive environment, focused on everyday life, social interactions and school experience. Users stand in the place of a high school student, moving along a dynamic, fictitious city landscape, interacting with the characters, exploring key places and participating in various events.

Title offers many ways to plunge into this detailed city - from clubs after lessons to public events and part -time work. Each choice is able to influence the player’s path, making the study useful and dynamic. The heart of Anime City lies in its social mechanics. Gamers attend classes and participate in school events, establishing relations with peers and other NPCs. The formation of connections can lead to unique plot moments and the opportunity to enter into clubs and take part in group events.

Each interaction builds a relationship in different ways, and the result is affected by the choice made during dialogs or daily actions. As users advanced, their social status and interaction with society affect experience, giving depth to routine everyday decisions. Thanks to these relations, players receive a piece of school life, overcoming difficulties, friendly relations and events taking place in the anime.
