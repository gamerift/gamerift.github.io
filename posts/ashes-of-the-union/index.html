Game Description#

Ashes of the Union features a world torn apart by cataclysmic upheaval. The once-mighty Soviet Union lies in ruins, the victim of tectonic forces that have split the land and created a hostile, mutant-infested landscape. Human survivors cling to existence in underground bunkers - isolated remnants of humanity. Above the ground, as far as the eye can see, a deserted wasteland stretches. Ruined cities overgrown with vegetation serve as eerie monuments to a bygone era.

Users take on the role of a survivor, venturing into this dangerous world in a heavily armored vehicle. Scarcity of resources determines daily life. Fuel, scrap metal and medicine are valuable goods needed for survival. Combat is a constant threat. Mutants roam the earth - grotesque creatures adapted to the harsh environment. They vary in size, shape and aggressiveness. A well-equipped vehicle and sharp reflexes are essential for survival.

Research is key. In abandoned settlements you can find clues to the past and valuable loot. Users scour these ruins for supplies, information, and hidden dangers. The title provides a sense of discovery as players piece together the history of this fallen world. Vehicle customization plays an important role. Gamers improve their cars by installing stronger armor, more powerful weapons and specialized equipment. These modifications can make the difference between success and failure in the face of insurmountable odds.
