Game Description#

Murders on the Yangtze River takes you to early 20th century China. Think “Ace Attorney” and historical detective. Channel your inner Sherlock Holmes as you unravel a series of mysterious cases along the banks of the legendary Yangtze River. Step into the shoes of John Shen, a law student turned detective. Grief and a thirst for justice force him to return to China after his brother’s mysterious death is ruled a suicide. A chance encounter with a detective novel fuels his suspicions - the crime scene depicted in the picture too closely repeats the death of his brother.

John sets out on a quest for truth, traveling through a world filled with fascinating characters and complex mysteries. Murders on the Yangtze River isn’t just finger-pointing and finger-pointing. The game promises puzzles that will test your logic and deduction skills. Prepare to scrutinize crime scenes, uncover hidden clues, and ask suspects razor-sharp questions.

Will you be able to identify inconsistencies in the alibi and bring to light the true culprits? The developers did not forget about accessibility. An easy-to-follow tutorial welcomes newcomers to the genre while seasoned sleuths can enjoy the increasing difficulty. Murders on the Yangtze River is designed for players of all skill levels. But the game’s charm extends beyond the puzzles. Imagine traveling through a breathtaking landscape rendered in the captivating style of Chinese ink painting. Explore the scenic spots of Dongting Lake, Yangzhou and Shanghai. Immerse yourself in the rich cultural tapestry of a bygone era.
