Game Description#

Vagrus - The Riven Realms is a role -playing game with elements of strategy, which will need to take control of the leader of the caravan and survive in a dark surreal world. Lead a travel company, get in your ranks of powerful warriors and travel on the Great Earth, fighting enemies and earning useful materials. Explore a huge continent that contains unique locations, strange fractions and numerous dangers. You can interact and communicate with many secondary characters. The whole world is a wasteland, filled with strange anomalies and monsters.

Meet short and long stories telling about the former battles and events that changed the planet. Each gamer’s choice affects history. In particular, you can recruit unique companions fighting for you. Hire a merchant, mercenary or researcher. Work for the fraction, following the mysterious personalities. Key features of the game:

The whole surrounding world is a sandbox, fully created by hand and having a huge amount of content. Take part in step -by -step battles, where allied units with monsters fight. Kill monsters, humanoids and huge bosses. Open more skills and abilities in the protagonist, gradually increasing his level. Plan your trips so that they do not end with tragedy prematurely. Gather the necessary items of certain partners and go on the road.
