Game Description#

In Squigl Football, a gamer plunges into the world of management of a sports club, where success depends on the creation of effective ligaments from players, coaches and tactical schemes. The main goal is to conduct your team through the lower divisions to the treasured championship in the National League. The basis of everything is the deck collection system, and Australian football was the source of inspiration for the project.

The Squiggle Football game process is focused on strategic solutions, eliminating routine like contract negotiations or random injuries. You have to take the best beginners in the draft and ruthlessly part with veterans whose time has passed. Each action is evaluated by the board of directors, whose discontent can quickly lead to your dismissal and completion of the game.

Four pillars of your future dynasty:

Carefully select the talents in the draft, not afraid to say goodbye to veterans. Look for powerful combinations of players, trainers and tactical schemes. Keep the team through the matches of the lower divisions to the main championship. Manage the club effectively to earn the confidence of the leadership.

The project offers great variability due to the presence of more than 850 unique athletes. The key to creating an invincible team lies in the search for synergies between talents, mentors and a general strategy. The results of all matches are determined by an advanced simulator operating on the basis of the analytical data of Squiggle and the rules of Australian football.
