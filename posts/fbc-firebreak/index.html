Game Description#

FBC: Firebreak gives gamers the first-person cooperative shooter for 3 people, unfolding in the headquarters of the federal department, attacked by forces from other dimensions. Speaking as operatives of the Firebreak division, the group should intervene in the most acute situations that occur within the besieged structure. This detachment is obliged to penetrate into the crisis zones, restore order, restrain chaos and fight for restoring control over the object.

A long siege reached a critical point, demanding direct intervention in the most unstable sectors of the building. The successful completion of tasks in FBC: Firebreak largely depends on the coherence of actions and mutual assistance between the three fighters. The teams have to encounter paranormal phenomena and hostile entities arising throughout the bureau, which requires an operational assessment of the situation and joint response measures for survival.

The gameplay will force to move according to many unpredictable locations inside the main complex of the department, the oldest house, overcoming events that actively distort the usual reality. Effective command work is a key factor for overcoming such difficulties. Before sorting out, each operative selects weapons and forms a personal crisis kit. It includes specialized devices, grenades, objects of support and improvement of paranormal origin found inside the agency.
