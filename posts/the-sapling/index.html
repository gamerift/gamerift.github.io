Game Description#

The Sapling is a simulator in which you will be selected by growing new plant species or crossing animals. Build your own world inhabited by unusual animals and a phantasmagoric fauna. The Sapling allows you to choose a random mutation mode to be a third -party observer, providing nature to conduct evolution itself and create its own ecosystems. At any time, you can take the development of the environment into your own hands.

The sandbox mode allows you to play endlessly, switching between manual and true mutations. The ecosystem can develop both violently, and vice versa, slowly wither if organisms cannot interact with each other. The gameplay includes the work of a full -fledged instinct system. You can ask both personal parameters to organisms, and provide them with artificial intelligence so that he builds the needs of the animal, or indicate his ability to see, hear, make sounds, etc.

Available procedural animation system. The dynamic soundtrack works on the same principle, adapting to what is happening and smoothly reducing the paths, depending on the processes occurring on the screen. Many settings and additional parameters will help you create a unique ecosystem and observe your life in the world created.
