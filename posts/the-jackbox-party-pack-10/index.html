Game Description#

Jackbox Party Pack 10 is a great set of five exciting games that are guaranteed to bring laughter and fun to any party for free. Whether you’re planning on playing pirate online with a friend, a work party, or just hanging out with friends, this set has something for everyone: online play for a variety of tastes, including strategy, drawing, trivial, and musical. In addition, you will have the opportunity to play online for free online, which makes this set even more attractive.

Tee K.O. 2 (drawing, writing): Tee K.O. 2 takes you to the whimsical T-Shirt Island, where warriors engage in a fierce battle over designs the likes of which have never been seen before. But here the winner is determined not by a fist, but by a finger. Your artistic skill and creativity will be your greatest assets in this epic competition.

FixyText (Writing, Teamwork): FixyText takes you to the Wild West of text editing - a place where everyone is in a group chat and typing at the same time to create the most memorable utterance. But here’s the catch? There’s no delete key, and auto-correct won’t save you. Prepare for chaos and hilarious typos!

Hypnotorious (Hidden Identity, Roleplaying): Hypnotorious with intrigue and mystery, where secret roles and tentative alliances are the order of the day. As participants, you will have to solve absurd questions and give equally absurd answers. The question remains, who is on your side in this web of deception?

Timejinx (Trivia): In Timejinx, you take on a journey as a time traveler from the future, participating in an evening of high-stakes trivial games. Your task is to protect the current timeline by answering questions about the past. It’s a race against time that will put your knowledge of history to the test.
