Game Description#

Rift Wizard 2 is a roguelike wizard simulator that puts players in the shoes of an immortal amnesiac wizard. Main goal? Travel through space and fight an old enemy. Each playthrough of the game is a new adventure in which no two tasks are the same. Procedural generation ensures that users will always encounter different obstacles, monsters and environments, which makes the game fresh and unpredictable. The challenge lies in how gamers adapt their strategies as they progress through different levels, where every decision matters and every spell cast can be the difference between life and death.

In Rift Wizard 2, magic isn’t just about flashy or powerful spells, but about creating a unique arsenal of spells, passives, and magical artifacts that suit the gamer’s preferred style. Every choice matters. In one case, the user can rely on destructive magic, and in the other, on protective spells or buffs that increase survivability. The game’s flexibility allows for a variety of approaches, allowing users to experiment and try different combinations of abilities to discover new strategies. There is no one-size-fits-all approach here, you need to find what works for everyone, often through trial and error.
