Game Description#

Lumber and Plunder sends players to a remote island, according to rumors, hiding a long -forgotten treasure. The main lesson is the extraction of resources from the environment, for example, the cutting of trees in the mysterious forest. The materials obtained are sold for currency invested in improving tools. Such a progression system opens access to new areas and helps to climb the central mountain, the main goal in finding a legendary treasure.

In the process of study, Lumber and Plunder, the narrative is revealed through scattered tips and echoes of the legend. These fragments of the information form together the history of Captain Elias and the treasure, which is protected by him at the cost of life. The disclosure of this secret becomes the main motivation for the expedition. The world contains the relics of the past, shed light on forgotten events, allowing the player to recreate the story through the study of the environment at his own pace.

The gameplay is focused on climbing through a mountain pass and conquering the area thanks to efforts and improved equipment. Progress is noticeable, because new tools allow you to overcome previously insurmountable obstacles. This project offers a meditative trip with an accent to a calm study of the world buried under the wind and stone. You can go on an expedition alone or join others in a common environment. The ultimate goal is to achieve the top, solve the riddle and find the award.

What awaits the seeker on the island:

The extraction of valuable resources and their subsequent sale. Gradual improvement of equipment to advance forward. The study of the mysterious forest and climbing the mountain. Collection of fragments of the legend of the lost treasure. Passing the plot at its own pace without haste.
