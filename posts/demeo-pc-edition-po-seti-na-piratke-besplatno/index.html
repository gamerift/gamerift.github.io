Game Description#

Demeo PC Edition - a role-playing adventure with the ability to play online as a pirate with a friend for free, gathering your party of heroes online online. In the company of partners, you will need to go on an exploration of Demeo, where everyone will be able to demonstrate their strategic skills and demonstrate the ability to work in a team. Free online play is available for up to 4 people at the same time. You can play as a hunter, sorcerer, assassin, bard, warlock or guardian. Each hero has a number of unique features and significantly influences the style of play, as well as the role in the team.

Alone or with partners, you will need to explore the expanses of Gilmerra - a fantasy kingdom consisting of 8 provinces. The map includes mountains, caves, forests, oceans and other biomes with unique loot and enemies. The online game invites you to work together to defeat powerful bosses who have invaded the village of Thorp or live in the ruins of Castle Holloumrak. The gameplay of Demeo PC Edition recreates the spirit of party board games. It is very important to constantly interact with each other, exchanging items, loot, or taking turns performing special actions.

You have to fight the darkness itself. Its leaders are Rakarn and Alfar. You will need to assemble a deck, which can consist of 60 cards. With their help, you can deal damage, cast spells, or heal partners. Decks are individual and tied to the hero’s class. Events start from the Elven Necropolis. Gradually you will visit the Black Sarcophagus, the kingdom of the Rat King, go to the Ront desert and fight many demons from the other world.
