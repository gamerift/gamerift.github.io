Game Description#

Conceptively Zompiercer is a sandbox with an open detailed world, but the whole thing is played as a first-person shooter about survival during the zombie apocalypse. The planet is covered by a virus. People mutated in bloodthirsty walking corpses. Not many managed to survive, but you are lucky more than the rest. Or not? Survival is just beginning!

The game from an independent developer under the nickname DDDIMANN is not a comprehension not by the setting, but by the scale and the implementation of ideas. Physics here is fully present with most objects and objects can be interacted. Artificial intelligence is also implemented quite well - zombies are able to knock out the doors and climb over the broken windows.

Local bestiary is diverse. You will meet any zombares on your way, starting with ordinary barely weaving walks, which can be killed from one shot in the head, ending with armored creatures and terrible mutants with unique abilities.

In the game you will take up the exploration of the world, the search for useful objects, food, water, weapons and ammunition. Your task is to find a safe place in this world, covered by the consequences of the epidemic. You will travel both on foot and by train, in the cabin of which you can press the buttons and move the levers.

A lot is tied around the train, since it is not just an armored vehicle, but your house. You can equip yourself with a cozy corner for relaxation, create a warehouse for supplies and cartridges, equip a workshop and much more. You need food, water and weapons to survive, and the train needs fuel. Do not forget about it.
