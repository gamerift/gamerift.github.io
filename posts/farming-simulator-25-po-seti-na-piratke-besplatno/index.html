Game Description#

Farming Simulator 25 offers players the opportunity to play online, immersing themselves in farming in different parts of the world. Now you can start a farm for free in North America near historic grain elevators, along rivers, or create a farm with a friend in the picturesque landscapes of Central Europe, dotted with ponds. Even if you play on a pirate, the online network game will allow you to manage farming: from crop and livestock farming to forestry and business - open farm shops and processing plants. Each location with its unique terrain and cultures brings variety to the gameplay.

The dynamic weather in the game brings new challenges to farming life. Gusts of wind, hail, and other conditions affect crops, forcing players to plan and adapt. Deformation of the soil adds realism, as tractors leave marks that do not disappear. Mornings with changing light and mist create an atmospheric start to each day on the farm, making these moments come alive. The new features highlight the features of farming in Asia, including water-retentive rice fields and new crops such as spinach and peas. The addition of buffalo and goats expands the livestock options, and baby animals enhance the feeling of farm life. A wide range of machinery, now equipped with GPS for accuracy, allows players to choose from over 400 tools and vehicles, making Farming Simulator 25 a richly varied farming management experience.
