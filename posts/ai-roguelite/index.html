Game Description#

AI Roguelite is a text role -playing game where artificial intelligence generates all content and controls all game mechanics. The system determines each location, a non -game character, an enemy and an object that a player faces. In addition, recipes for creating things and fundamental rules of interaction also come from the neural network. As a result, the gamer has to explore the Universe, 100 percent consisting of elements created by a large language model, which provides a procedural adventure as a procedurally generated.

The world of AI Roguelite is formed from the answers of the neural network to contextual requests, inhabiting the area with appropriate entities. The model determines the specific weapons or enemies discovered in locations like an underwater city, which facilitates the study. The creation of objects works according to the similar principle of AI-interpretation. The algorithm decides which object will come from a combination of ingredients, such as a wooden stick and stone, or even a computer chip with a zombie brain.

Artificial intelligence bases the final result on the names of the components provided. The gameplay of battles departs from the traditional statistics of objects. When a gamer attacks the robot terminator with a raw salmon, the outcome depends on several factors. The success of the action takes into account the names and descriptions of the involved things, an assessment of credibility on the part of AI and a throw of a cube on the attributes of the character. After action, the neural network creates a narrative describing what happened. In conclusion, the system analyzes its own generated story to conclude whether the opponent was killed, wounded or remained unharmed.
