Game Description#

In addition to the option to play online with a friend on a pirate,Problem Here offers users the opportunity to immerse themselves in a space setting. Here, gamers are given the main task - to enter the atmosphere. It would seem that everything is quite simple. But as soon as the team sets out to achieve the goal, problems begin and everything becomes much more difficult. So, having established online communication via the Internet, users should act together to avoid the worst consequences. 6 people can take part in the session at the same time.

The premise of Problem Here is that a dangerous virus leaked on the ship, which was taken from Mars. It affects all objects it can reach, as well as people. And people can succumb to the virus at the most inopportune moment, and from that moment on they will try to destroy the ship and kill the uninfected. The task of the survivors in this situation is to identify enemies as quickly as possible and notify each other of the potential danger. Affected users, in turn, must do everything to make it more difficult for the remaining crew members to plant fungus on the ship’s territory, steal things (for example, room keys or flashlights) and perform other manipulations. Network play allows gamers belonging to the same “camp” to communicate with each other in order to coordinate actions in advance. In addition, the possibility of free online play allows you to track the status of the ship, as well as the time until it enters the atmosphere. If before the time limit expires, healthy characters emerge victorious in a particular round.
