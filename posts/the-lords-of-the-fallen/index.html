Game Description#

Lords of the Fallen invites gamers to the gloomy world of Fantasy, where you will become one of the legendary dark crusaders and will fight against the Demon of Adira. Lords of the Fallen is an epic adventure in a vast territory, which is five times more than the world of the original game. Adir tormented the country for many centuries, and now it needs to be overthrown again. Go around the world of the living and the dead, fighting with many bosses and mastering swift battles. Configure the appearance of your character using many visual parameters and select one of the nine grades to go the path that is right for you.

You will need to overcome the horrors of two parallel worlds in order to overthrow the Demon God Adir. Choose from hundreds of unique types of weapons or refuse the metal in favor of magic with its destructive secret spells. Go through a large -scale single -user campaign alone or invite the second player to join you in a continuous network multiple -user game. Your lantern allows you to cross the border between the worlds, use this dark art to get to forgotten places, unearth legendary treasures, or to influence your enemy directly. Only those who learn to fight for real ones will survive. Fall in the world of the living and rebuild in the world of the dead, use all your skills to resist the creatures of hell that will want to prevent you. Whether you become a legend of light or the legend of darkness - you decide.
