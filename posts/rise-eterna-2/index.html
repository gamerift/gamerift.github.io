Game Description#

Rise Eterna 2 offers you to take control of Yacht, a soldier of the Atrakian Empire, whose goal is to return the former power to his state. The story continues the narrative of the original several years later, plunging the gamer into the epicenter of a conflict for the throne between two influential houses. The situation is complicated by a mysterious third faction, hatching a plan for final retribution and weaving its own intrigues. The main task of the protagonist is to track down and eliminate a powerful entity called the Destroyer, on which the future of the entire region depends.

The gameplay of Rise Eterna 2 is based on turn-based tactical combat, presented in 24 missions with a variety of landscapes. The environment plays a critical role in the success of operations, as terrain features directly affect unit performance. Each type of surface a fighter is on changes his mobility or combat effectiveness. To achieve victory, it is necessary to wisely use the advantages of the terrain, take advantageous positions and put the enemy in obviously losing conditions, which requires a thoughtful approach to planning from the player.

This title offers a well-thought-out squad formation system that allows you to experiment with team composition and tactical schemes. Each hero develops through a unique skill tree, with the available skills determined by his biography. The mechanics of gems add additional depth to customization: more than 20 varieties of gems serve to enhance abilities and equipment. This approach allows you to fine-tune fighters for specific tasks on the battlefield, creating synergy within the squad and implementing complex strategies.
