Game Description#

F1 Race Stars is an arcade racing game with cartoon graphics. This pirate game allows you to play with a friend online. This can be done both online online and in split-screen mode, sitting together with two, three or four people at one PC. The title has an official license. That’s why there are recognizable car drivers from popular teams here.

F1 Race Stars parodies Formula 1. Racers and vehicles are depicted in a disproportionate and caricatured manner. In terms of gameplay, the project is reminiscent of battle racing on maps from consoles. Here, bonuses are also scattered along the tracks, from which items randomly drop out. These are different weapons, acceleration and other things. Arsenal here is outwardly harmless, since it is a family product, and it is primarily aimed at a children’s audience. Instead of machine guns and rocket launchers, bubbles and rain clouds are used.

The title has a damage system. A damaged car drives worse and is more difficult to control. It can be repaired if you go through a pit stop. There is a single career, which consists of 30 competitions. The tracks are partly based on real-life roads from the USA, Belgium, Monaco, Brazil. But here the routes do not correspond to reality. They are replete with jumps and loops. There are different weather conditions. In addition to ring competition with bots, time trials, capture the flag, and elimination trips are available at different difficulty levels. Network play supports up to 12 private players. The possibility of free online play allows you to participate in various events and compete for a place on the leaderboard.
