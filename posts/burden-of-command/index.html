Game Description#

Burden of Command gives players a completely different idea of what projects can be in the theme of World War II. The local scale is considered here, and the focus is a small group of fighters. The gamer is given the opportunity to become the commander of this group, making important decisions in key situations. The guys have to visit hot spots around the world, from Western Europe and ending with Morocco, where the remnants of enemy forces were still based at that moment.

The accent in the Burden of Command gameplay is made precisely on tactical solutions. The gamer should adapt to the opportunity to adapt to the situation. So, each card has its own relief and scale, and also models a completely unique situation when you need to work only with what is available at this moment. The space is indicated by hexagons, and in each of them there may be a certain number of fighters. This allows not only to calculate their own forces in battle, but also to understand what the enemy is capable of within the framework of each specific move. Making decisions is one of the most important aspects of the gameplay. Difficulties can begin at any time, and if there is a choice between holding the territory or the death of a fellow, you will have to solve on the spot. Moreover, if someone from the detachment dies, the team loses the participant until the end of the assignment. Therefore, each next step must be weighed, pondering the possible outcomes. At such moments, psychology is manifested when people, being in extreme conditions, go to everything to achieve the result.
