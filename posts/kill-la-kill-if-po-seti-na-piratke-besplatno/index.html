Game Description#

KILL la KILL -IF is a whirlwind fighting game based on the anime of the same name, which you can play for free online as a pirate with a friend or fight with other players online online. The online game offers you to fight as characters such as Satsuki Kiryuina, Ryuko Matoi and other heroes of the original show. The story is independent and directly continues the original source.

The action takes place at Honnouji Academy. There is a Goku Uniform that gives the wearer great powers. In a single-player adventure, you will control Satsuki, the central character of KILL la KILL -IF. Free online play allows you to engage in intense battles with enemies using attack, defense and special primary attacks. Mechanics contain a rock, paper, scissors element. The visual style of the fighting game is completely identical to the anime adventure.

All protagonists are capable of performing melee and ranged attacks. By combining them with each other, you can use spectacular combos. You can use counterattacks, causing enormous damage to the enemy. By activating the “Bloody Valor” special ability, each fighter can perform a unique super attack. During fights, you can choose three types of phrases to unbalance your opponent: Taunt, Provoke and Imitation. However, they require energy. When entering into a confrontation using this mechanic, the damage is taken by the one with the fewest energy points.
