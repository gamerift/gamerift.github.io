Game Description#

** Salvus: aries ** throws you headlong into the afterlife. Here death is not the end, but a new beginning, although gloomy. You wake up a nameless soul in purgatory, and Rubilovo is just a memory. It turns out that your past life was not entirely sunny and rainbow, and now you are serving a sentence as a reap. You must collect souls until your debt to the universe is paid. It sounds quite simple, right? But not. This is not your grandmother’s visual novel.

Salvus: Aries is a mature story in which everyday elements are combined with adventures and dark fantasy. While you will navigate the strange bureaucracy of the afterlife, you will solve the secrets associated with your own death. Who were you? What sins have brought you to this difficult situation? The answers to these questions are hidden, they are guarded by those who surround you tightly. But the biggest problem is not some kind of mythical monster, but the confrontation of your inner demons. Stiring - and one -way ticket to the fiery depths.

Let’s talk about pressure. The book “Salvus: Aries”, consisting of 450,000 words, is a marathon, not a sprint. The plot unfolds in a unique mixture of modern fantasy and historical events, which allows you to maintain freshness. Artistic design is a feast for the eyes: detailed spelling of characters, amazing CG and causing backgrounds. Although the soundtrack is not mentioned, they say in a whisper about the enchanting OST, which will even more immerse you into the world.
