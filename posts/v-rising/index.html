Game Description#

Waking up in V Rising, the hero as a vampire must hunt for blood, while simultaneously avoiding direct sunlight. The survival slasher film puts you in the shoes of a bloodsucker who wakes up in his own castle and must regain his possession and authority while operating in a vast open world full of secrets. Here you have to conquer the lands of the living, unite with allies to complete the task.

The protagonist is a vampire who wakes up one day and realizes that he is completely weakened after centuries of sleep. Now the hero needs to hunt in nearby settlements to get human blood for food, being careful of the sun, which will allow him to survive. The character’s castle was also virtually destroyed during the period of his sleep, so it must be gradually restored, as well as getting new servants among the people in order to raise the Empire. The user here is asked to act in the Gothic world, exploring its vast expanses where mythical creatures live, human settlements, impenetrable forests, dark dungeons in which valuable resources can be obtained. During the journey, the protagonist also meets different characters - allies and opponents with whom he has to fight, plunder villages together, and raid opponents.

The main rule of a vampire is to always be in the shadows so that the sun does not turn him into ashes, so it is worth hunting and acting at night. As he progresses, the vampire hero collects resources and interacts with various ancient techniques, which allows him to enlist the support of the Dark Forces, and also uses the acquired knowledge to rebuild his house, in which not only loot is stored, but also his army arrives. You can move alone or team up with vampire friends, which gives great advantages, for example, when fighting with a more powerful enemy. Here you will also have to learn how to use an arsenal of all kinds of weapons and different abilities, which ultimately allows you to successfully aim, dodge enemy bullets and combine melee and ranged weapons along with skills. As the hero progresses, he upgrades his own skills and receives the support of the forces of evil.
