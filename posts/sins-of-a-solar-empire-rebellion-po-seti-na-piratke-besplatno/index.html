Game Description#

In the space strategySins of a Solar Empire: Rebellion you can play online with a friend on a pirate, fighting for free in various multiplayer modes. You can compete online or locally. Events develop in three dimensions. The possibility of free online play provides a choice of maps with unique features. Participants are required to build infrastructure, extract resources, set up production, develop technologies and much more.

The gameplay of Sins of a Solar Empire is divided into two components: global control and local play. You can seamlessly switch between the two modes. In local mode, it is necessary to manage individual units, equipment and objects. In global mode, you need to send fleets between continents. Shuttles can jump into phase space and travel through wormholes, quickly covering vast distances in space. Ships are divided into fighters, bombers, frigates, cruisers and flagships.

The Rebellion expansion introduces 2 new types of ships: corvettes and titans. 4 types of planets have also been added: Barren, Ferrous, Oceanic and Greenhouse. Online play allows you to explore Moons, Asteroids, Stars, Pirate Bases, Magnetic Clouds and other planets. Resources are divided into 4 types: Credits, Metal, Crystals and Influence. A map editor is available that allows you to create your own unique continents. Game factions: Trade Military Coalition - people who use armored vehicles, Gauss cannons, rocket and pulse cannons, and also have nuclear weapons in their stock; The Marchers - descendants of colonists with psi abilities, controlling drones using telepathy and fighting energy weapons; Vasari - a technocratic race that uses nanotechnology and controls the Dark Fleet; Pirates - an unknown race with a huge number of bases throughout the galaxy, whose goal is robbery and robbery.
