Game Description#

GLASS is a surreal journey through the fragments of someone else’s memory, where the protagonist finds himself trapped in a dark and unstable world. The narrative leads the hero through a series of bizarre and frightening locations, including a city engulfed in flames, an estate with dark secrets, an ominous carnival and even a location with floating hot air balloons. Each of these places is filled with strange objects and puzzles, the solution of which gradually brings us closer to solving the tragic story of the mysterious author, known only by the initials T.N. His pleas for help, left in letters, serve as the only guide in this distorted space.

The protagonist begins his journey, not remembering anything about himself and how he got to this place, feeling only the strangeness of what is happening. Immersion in the psychological thriller GLASS forces you to piece together information about the writer, whose fate becomes the central mystery. Moving from one surreal picture to another, for example from the Crystal Spire to an ancient mansion, the hero is forced to confront the oppressive atmosphere and solve non-obvious mysteries. This process of investigation becomes not just a search for answers about another person, but a way to understand how this author’s past relates to the character’s own amnesia and what exactly brought him here.

The gameplay is based on first-person exploration of the world, where the focus is on exploring the environment and solving puzzles built into the narrative. Interaction with objects and reading the notes found form a holistic picture of the tragedy that happened to the writer T.N. The gradually revealed details of his past have an irreversible impact on the protagonist’s worldview. The ending of the story promises to forever change those who dare to follow this path to the end and find out the truth hidden behind a series of disturbing and bizarre images.
