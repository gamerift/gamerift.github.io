Game Description#

Locomoto instructs the player the role of a train conductor as part of the adventure-symptulator of life, focused on railway travels. Key classes include operation, management and setting up the composition itself. Gamers are direct responsibility for passengers sitting in their locomotive. The main task is to safely deliver these diverse personalities to the places they need through various locations under the Low Fai soundtrack during the path, while providing travelers during the trip is of paramount importance.

The train management in Locomoto implies practical expansion and external design. You can increase the length of your composition, building additional wagons, which directly increases the number of passengers, which the locomotive is able to simultaneously accommodate. Interior design involves the creation of furniture; For this, resources in the world are collected, and specific used materials determine the color of the created item. Preparation of transport for the next flights is also part of the management cycle.

Interaction with the inhabitants of this game gives information and awards. You will meet unique characters, receiving tasks from them, the performance of which brings objects such as wardrobe elements, materials or drawings to create things. Helping these personalities, you can find out the details about their characters and life situations within the world. It is also necessary to determine the appearance of your avatar, choosing from many features and shades inspired by animals, and then additionally adjusting it with a variety of clothes collected over time; Passenger service during the path affects their overall impression and is part of the gameplay.
