Game Description#

The rethought project MUD and Blood is a shootout between the two sides of the conflict, where the user has to observe the events. The setting transfers during the Second World War, where soldiers converge in battles. The updated engine and improved artificial intelligence allows you to try out the old classic flash game, filled with innovations in terms of functions and a graphic component that immerses gameplay into the process.

Going to Mud and Blood, the players await:

Simulation of hostilities, where fighters' detachments converge on a strategic map. The generation of random events and territories in which combat operations occur. Tactical possibilities that allow the use of a variety of maneuvers, combining the landscape advantage and the skill of soldiers among themselves. The choice between 12 classes that allow you to configure the specialization of each unit and make a universal and balanced garrison of shooters. A visit to the beaches of Normandy and the territory of Berlin, where fatal battles take place. A variety of weapons of those times and equipment elements that allow you to pump and improve the group. The opportunity to call support in the battle process in order to influence the outcome of the battle. The destroyed environment and obstacles representing shelters from enemy shells. The movement between obscuring objects and the application of deadly shots on enemies.
