Game Description#

JUMANJI: The Video Game is a cooperative adventure based on the film of the same name, offering free pirate play online with a friend, solving numerous puzzles and overcoming obstacles online online. Online play JUMANJI: The Video Game allows up to four people to play. Each hero has his own unique abilities and characteristics. Free online play allows you to fight your enemies locally.

All the characters from the original film have returned to the game. The characters are Dr. Bravestone, Ruby, Moss and Professor Oberon. Taking one of the four heroes under your leadership, you must work together to overcome numerous traps and survive in the wild. There are many dangers and enemies along the way. In each situation, it is necessary to find special tactics against adversaries or interact with the environment to solve the puzzle.

The main events take place in the virtual jungle. The heroes of Jumanji find themselves inside a game from which they can only escape if they complete all the tests. During the passage, the characters will visit mountains, an ancient city, desert and many other locations. Heroes' skills can be upgraded. The abilities you choose influence your battle tactics. Various costumes, items and skins are also unlocked. A single player campaign is available, allowing you to play with computer partners.
