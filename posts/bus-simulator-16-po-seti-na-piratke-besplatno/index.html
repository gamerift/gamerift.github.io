Game Description#

Bus Simulator 16 is a bus simulator that allows you to play online with a friend for free on a pirate, driving 13 types of transport, including the famous Mercedes-Benz and MAN bus models. Alone or together with friends online, you must follow traffic rules, transport passengers through five developed areas, adhere to a schedule, and much more.

Two-door, three-door and articulated transport models are presented. A network game is aimed at confrontation or cooperation between participants. The rating is influenced by clean driving, compliance with traffic rules, good treatment of passengers, etc. The trip may not always go smoothly. You have to reassure passengers, repair doors or a broken engine. As a driver, you have to check people’s tickets, take care of passengers in wheelchairs, etc. Everyone should try to earn an impeccable reputation. Mechanics and functions of Bus Simulator 16: the possibility of free online play; control of 13 types of buses; a huge open world with 5 well-developed areas; scheduling and route scheduling; numerous signs, detours, pedestrians, etc.; unforeseen situations: violent passengers, faulty doors, etc.; customization and modification of cars; managing your own company with friends.

If you cope well with your responsibilities, the company offers lucrative contracts, and with the proceeds you can buy new types of transport. It is allowed to decorate the bus with billboards and banners, which also brings additional income. There is complete customization of the vehicle, including painting and interior decor. Each driver sets his own style!
