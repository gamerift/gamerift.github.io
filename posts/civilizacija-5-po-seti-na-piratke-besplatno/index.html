Game Description#

On the pirateCivilization 5 all network modes are available for free, allowing you to compete with other players online online. You can play online with a friend using a local connection. Sid Meier’s Civilization V is a turn-based strategy game where you make moves across a hexagonal map. For the first time in the series, barbarian states appeared. Captured empires are now called satellites. The possibility of free online play allows for remote shooting. Each player has his own technology tree.

At the center of the game mechanics is the construction of your own city and the development of civilization. Borders are expanded through honeycombs and resource collection. When a city is captured, you can go two ways: annex it or create a “satellite” area. It takes several moves to completely destroy a city. When shooting directly from the city, you can fire using arrows, catapult shells, artillery and rockets. Units are divided into ground units, consisting of infantry and cavalry and ranged troops: archers, siege weapons and warships. Online play allows you to take control of one of 43 powers.

Sid Meier’s Civilization V asks each country to choose a leader. All heroes are represented by 29 unique flavors that determine tactics and strategy. City-states have allies. They consist of two types: cultural and coastal. You can extract three resources: bonus, strategic and rare. They are necessary for the development of troops or the construction of Miracles. Diplomacy allows you to form alliances with opposing factions and act against a common enemy.
