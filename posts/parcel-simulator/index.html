Game Description#

Parcel Simulator includes the inspection and processing of parcels in the warehouse. The player will have to receive and sort incoming delivery, checking on each sticker such parts as weight, destination and type of contents. The process consists of entering or scanning barcodes, inspection of departments for smuggling and making a decision on admission or refusal for each box based on constantly changing rules.

As Parcel Simulator passes, the main emphasis shifts the automation and expansion of the enterprise. The gameplay allows you to install equipment for processing and identifying, so that the markings are checked in automatic mode. To increase the throughput of the workshop, conveyor tapes and special storage systems are created. The task is to design complex mechanisms to manage the flow of goods, with their subsequent distribution by contents and the status of admission before sending to the warehouse.

Further development in this game includes the management of logistics and the growth of the enterprise. It is necessary to send export departments according to the type of cargo and ensure their shipment on the right day. This will require the creation of well -thought -out storage systems and extraction with loaders and unloaders for effective work. The modernization of the workshop will open access to new loading docks, which will increase the volume of goods received. The acquisition of licenses and improvement of technology will make it possible to earn more money from each parcel, as well as set up the appearance of the warehouse, inspection tables and conveyors.
