Game Description#

Millennia is an addictive strategy game that allows you to play online with a friend. You can enjoy this game for free on the pirate website online. You will have the opportunity to play a free online game, where you will be able to control your civilization for 10,000 years - from the emergence of the first cities to space travel. Your choices shape the identity of your nation and the world around it.

Each playthrough of the game offers a unique experience. Will you be a conquering warlord or a pioneering explorer? Will your people revere the Great Khan or the Master Builder? The decisions you make reveal different Peoples Spirits, shaping your culture and its legacy.

Millennia isn’t all about brute force. You will need a sharp economic mind to manage resources and build a prosperous nation. Start with basic necessities such as food and manufacturing, and then explore ways to process them into valuable goods. Transform raw materials into powerful weapons, create a vibrant trade network and ensure the growth of your empire.

Knowledge is power. Research unlocks new units, buildings and upgrades that strengthen your cities and armies. Whether it’s mastering iron production, training elite archers, or creating lucrative trade routes, your research choices determine your path to success.

The world of Millennia is full of surprises. The era variants offer unique historical twists, introducing futuristic technology along with fantastical capabilities. Will you be building cloud cities in the Age of Aether or exploring underwater metropolises in the Age of Utopia? The future of your civilization depends on your choice.

The culmination of millennia will be the Age of Victory. Lead your nation to dominance and determine the conditions of victory for the entire world. Will you score a quick victory in the Renaissance or carefully plan a triumph in the modern day? The fate of the world is in your hands.
