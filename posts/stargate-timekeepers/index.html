Game Description#

The events of the Stargate: Timekeepers project take place within a separate universe called SG-1. It all begins with the Battle of Antarctica, where Commander Eve McKine leads the battle against Anubis' fleet. After this, the user will be able to take control of a squad subordinate to this very Eve in order to penetrate behind enemy lines and complete a number of dangerous tasks. The outcome of the war depends on this, so no one has the right to make a mistake.

The story campaign of Stargate: Timekeepers consists of 14 consecutive missions, during which you will have to fight several unique types of hostile creatures at once. Some people focus on close combat, while others, on the contrary, prefer to stay at a distance from the target. Some locations (for example, the starting one) can be passed in stealth mode, eliminating enemies one by one. Each unit has a scripted route and viewing radius. Detection does not occur immediately, and before a critical situation occurs, you can manage to neutralize the enemy. Moving, like other active actions, occurs using the mouse. But you shouldn’t think that during each subsequent sortie you will be able to concentrate exclusively on fighting. Sometimes, you will need to switch to healing allies, neutralizing enemy electronic devices, as well as a number of other tasks. Moreover, any sortie requires a clear understanding of the team’s capabilities. Each character belongs to a specific class, and this must be taken into account for successful combat. For example, Derek Harper has controlled drones with him, which provide remote neutralization of devices, as well as conduct reconnaissance of hard-to-reach areas.
