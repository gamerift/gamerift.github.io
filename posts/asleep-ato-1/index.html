Game Description#

Asleep - Ato 1 is a psychological horror movie in which the boundaries between reality and nightmares are erased. The main character, Ana Lsia, is in a city covered by shadows, and tries to understand what is happening, focusing in a terrible environment. Struggling with a loosening mind, she encounters an unfriendly world, filled with creatures, which, it seems, become only sinister than darker. The user rises in its place, exploring the place where each corner is fraught with a potential danger or hint to unraveling mystery.

In this 2D adventure, gamers should use their ingenuity and a true flashlight to fight off hiding threats. A flashlight is not just a light source, but a tool that allows you to interact with the environment, open new paths and pass by enemies sensitive to light. The mechanic makes every step think through: how long you can stay in the dark, how every source of light affects the monsters around you and what will happen if you cannot keep your mind.

The darkness here is not just a lack of light, it is a creeping force that undermines the grip of the protagonist to reality. The study plays a huge role in Asleep - ATO 1, since the title combines the solution of puzzles with elements of Survival Horror. While Ana Lsisi fights with internal and external demons, gamers will move through various environments, avoiding the growing insanity caused by darkness.
