Game Description#

The Black Pool will take you on an exciting adventure - literally. Together with a friend on a pirate boat, you accidentally discover a mysterious pool and, lo and behold! A new world with its own secrets opens up. But don’t worry, this mysterious corner also grants supernatural powers that will help you survive. Explore alone or with friends online to return home in this exciting online action game.

The Black Pool is a roguelike, meaning every time you play it is a completely different experience. You’ll explore randomly generated worlds, battling strange enemies and collecting amazing abilities along the way. These abilities can range from fire explosions to ice manipulation. The key is to strategically choose abilities that work well together and perfect them to become unstoppable. With millions of possible combinations, you’ll have to constantly experiment to find the perfect one.

Black Pool becomes even more interesting with friends. The game is built around co-op play and the abilities work well together when used together. Imagine redirecting your friend’s lightning strike to melt a giant ice monster, or powering up his healing spell to get everyone in on the fight. Teamwork in this game truly makes the dream come true.

With a huge arsenal of abilities, varied worlds to explore, and plenty of replayability, this roguelike promises an unforgettable adventure. Remember, if you fail, don’t be discouraged. Each attempt teaches you valuable lessons and unlocks permanent improvements, making you stronger for the next attempt.
