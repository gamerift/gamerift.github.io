Game Description#

The Jackbox Party Pack 8 is a party game. On the pirate site, this project allows you to play online with a friend. You can do this via the Internet online or sitting together in front of the same computer. The indie title is a small collection of mini-games. There are 5 of them in this part. Each of them has its own graphic design and unique gameplay with its own rules.

Party Pack 8 doesn’t just include free online play. The title is designed for multiplayer. The minimum number of users for the fun described below may vary between 2-4 people. And the maximum number of participants is usually limited to 8-10 users.

In Weapons Drawn, everyone involved is engaged in social deduction. Each player is both a killer and a detective. They are all trying to expose their rivals and not get caught themselves. An entertainment called The Poll Mine invites you to participate in surveys. Different teams try to guess how their competitors answered. And the winners will be able to escape from the witch’s lair. Drawful Animate – here you need to draw pictures and add cyclic two-frame animation to them. Ideas for drawings are taken from random words that form random phrases. In Job, gamers compete to come up with funny answers to interview questions. And The Wheel of Enormous Proportions is a quiz game. In it you need to spin the wheel and complete challenges. Especially distinguished people get a chance to win big. All of these mini-games feature online play.
