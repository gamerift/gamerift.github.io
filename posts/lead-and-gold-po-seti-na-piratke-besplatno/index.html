Game Description#

Due to the fact thatLead and Gold is a team project, it can be played online with a friend on pirate. Battles take place here in a 4v4 format, where each user chooses a character of a certain class. The heroes' skills can be combined to speed up the process of achieving the goal. Depending on the initial conditions of the game mode (there are 6 in total), you have to act differently each time, adapting to the conditions of the map and the current tasks. In addition, it is important to understand that when playing online online, other users will try to interfere, and in order to outplay them, you will need to show maximum skills in playing shooters. List of game modes and their features in the game “Lead and Gold. The Quick and the Dead”: Demolition - you must first capture barrels of gunpowder to destroy enemy buildings. Capture - you need to establish control over strong points in the specified sequence. Robbery - one team defends a bag of gold, and the other tries to capture it, overcoming obstacles on the way. A powder keg is a kind of analogue of an explosion, only here there are only two buildings, and one team acts as defenders of these same buildings. Greed - after capturing a bag of gold, it must be delivered to the designated location. The one who does this faster than the opponent will win. Skirmish - a timed battle with instant resurrection. The team with the most points at the end will emerge victorious.

As each round progresses, players can earn progression points that increase the effectiveness of their characters. For example, a demolitionist can wield sticks of dynamite, while a trapper can effectively hit targets at long range. Each class has a main weapon, as well as auxiliary items such as explosives. Playing online will allow you to try out all the heroes in different combinations in order to create your own tactics and achieve the desired result. In addition, the possibility of free online play opens up access to all maps that differ in area, level of content, terrain and other indicators. Therefore, each time the skirmishes there will take place differently.
