Game Description#

Mechabellum is an auto battler with the ability to play online for free as a pirate with a friend or other players, trying to colonize distant planets. Online players must command an army of powerful mechs, fighting in massive PvP matches for a sphere of influence. Online play allows you to customize your units, increase or improve their characteristics, develop your own unique tactics and much more.

Having set the parameters and combat characteristics, you need to watch how your personal army enters into battle with the opposing faction. There is a variety of transport. For example, the fast Wasp plane and the massive Vulcan airliner are available. Each type of transport must be used for its own strategic purposes. Mechabellum’s free online play experience relies on anticipating your opponent’s actions in order to launch powerful counterattacks or avoid massive damage.

The outcome of the battle depends on the choice of combinations. For example, if the enemy is superior in power and the match is considered lost, you can order an airstrike or sow chaos on the battlefield. You can also use crawling robots, shoot shrapnel, incendiary bombs or a laser that cuts through the air and destroys everything in its path. You can fight both in one team and against artificial intelligence.
