Game Description#

**Pigromance ** tells about the efforts of one pig to avoid turning into a food product, this is a story about the escape contrary to the established expectations. The action starts on a farm, where the piglets were previously behind the fence, in the usual environment. Over time, this design of the content succumbed, initiating the mass departure of animals from the agricultural limits in search of an alternative to their prepared role.

The main character, a specific pig, takes this journey, passing through locations with a variety of threats to his life. The key task for the player is to ensure the survival of the protagonist in the face of debugged industrial processing systems. Further events of the game Pigromance are already taking place at a functioning sausage plant, in a space equipped with devices for systematic killing and processing of carcasses.

There, the pigs meet fire -breathing pipes, freezers for immobilization and many saws integrated into conveyor lines. New tests create mechanisms that recognize the dividing marks, as well as the pursuit of butcher when using lifts. Gamers have to conduct a protagonist through platform sites of locations with traps and solve the problems associated with the operation of the plant’s production facilities, while the hero seeks to evade captivity and the final stage of processing at this enterprise.
