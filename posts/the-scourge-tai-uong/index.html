Game Description#

The Scourge | Tai Uong is a puzzle RPG that takes gamers into the eerie setting of Saigon, Vietnam, capturing the essence of urban legends from the 1990s. The story centers on a protagonist who must navigate a tragic family history and is haunted by a dark, supernatural presence that is the result of past choices. Users will have to go through a series of complex puzzles, exploring various locations of the city, in an effort to uncover hidden truths and confront otherworldly threats.

The journey begins in a dilapidated apartment against the backdrop of an unnamed epidemic, where the main character experiences a mixture of hallucinations and nightmares. These ghostly experiences push the narrative forward, requiring gamers to use their intelligence to piece together all the clues. As the story progresses, the character must make important decisions that affect not only the immediate plot, but also the larger results of his journey. Each choice made has weight, forcing the player deeper into the labyrinth of secrets and horrors.

Gamers at The Scourge | Tai Uong will face a variety of puzzles that require critical thinking and resourcefulness. The environment is rich in detail, encouraging thorough exploration. Every corner is filled with potential secrets waiting to be discovered. Whether it’s deciphering an ancient riddle or piecing together scattered notes, the challenges often reflect the protagonist’s inner struggle.
