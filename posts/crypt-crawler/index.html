Game Description#

The plot of Crypt Crawler starts after the fall of the heroes of the kingdom under the onslaught of the hordes of undead, poured from the open gates of the underworld. The mission to seal the fault is assigned to a lonely mechanic goblin. The character will have a 16-level descent into the dungeon in a dungeon in a monsters to achieve and close the portal. Each stage offers a new tactical task, forcing the goblin to climb deeper into the enemy territory for the sake of fulfilling the mission.

The journey immerses in a gloomy fantasy atmosphere, requiring strategic actions for survival. The Crypt CRAWLER gameplay is combined with a view of the dungeons and tactical construction. Moving by locations, players collect resources and search for drawings to open new defensive towers. These automated buildings are necessary to restrain undead - they cut, beat the current and explode enemies.

Success depends on the competent placement of fortifications to create deadly zones and control the advancing waves of opponents, combining real -time battles with strategic planning. The main campaign can be held alone or in a local cooperative up to four players. The team gameplay motivates coordinating tactics and distribute responsibilities for the construction and fighting. For those who want to continue passing after the completion of the levels created by hand, the game offers the procedurally generated arenas-sinks.
