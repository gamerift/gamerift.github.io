Game Description#

Renaissance Kingdom Wars transports players back to the turbulent 16th century, offering a unique experience that can be shared with a friend while playing online as a pirate. As a mercenary captain, you will fight in battles that will determine the future of the continent, playing online online. This online game combines real-time strategy and grand strategy elements, providing a free immersion into the European politics of the era.

Starting as a hired sword, you will lead your troops into large-scale battles, sieges and naval battles. Your valor on the battlefield can earn you power over the city, where you’ll have to flex your diplomatic and economic muscles. But why stop there? The game will allow you to climb the ladder of power and become a king or even an emperor.

Having fought through more than 500 provinces, from Scandinavia to the Holy Land, you will witness the march of technology. The Renaissance brought rapid advances in warfare, transforming medieval tactics into the deadlier and more sophisticated methods of early modern warfare. Spears give way to pikes, trebuchets to cannons, and the widespread use of gunpowder completely changes the face of battle.

The grand campaign unfolds against a backdrop of religious strife, political intrigue and economic competition. You will face the Sultans, Kings and Emperors of the Holy Roman Empire to determine your place in history. Whether you remain loyal to your liege or decide to seize power yourself, the choices you make will reverberate across the continent.

In Renaissance Kingdom Wars, you not only have to command armies, but also master the art of government. As you expand your influence, you will have to balance military power, economic development and diplomatic subtlety. This is a game that asks the question: do you have what it takes to not just survive, but thrive during one of the most dynamic and dangerous periods in history?
