You can playBeat Hazard 3 online for free and online. This online game turns your music library into a space blender, letting you fight space battles with a friend. Even on pirate, each song becomes a unique soundscape with its own waves and enemy intensity. For example, guitar solos turn into laser barrages, and funky basslines turn into asteroid fields that you need to dodge.

Think of a classic two-verse shooter, but controlled by your playlist. Beat Hazard 3 isn’t shy about turning up the volume on the music and chaos. Do you want to blow up aliens to dubstep?
