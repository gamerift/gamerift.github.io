Game Description#

Pathless Woods offers free online and online multiplayer play where you can team up with a friend, even on a pirate. You will be immersed in a gigantic world where you need to survive while enjoying tranquility: extract resources, farm and create the house of your dreams. However, be on your guard: hostile bandits are not asleep, and you will need to protect your settlement.

The game will allow you to unleash your inner potential as an architect. Build everything from classic wooden houses to grand bridges and towering pagodas. Do you feel like a social person? Team up with three friends for a shared adventure. Explore together, build together, and survive together—each player brings their own skills to the table.

This is no walk in the park. You will have to confront the elements and fight off both wild animals and hostile people. Learn to adapt and use the earth’s resources to your advantage. Explore breathtaking landscapes, interact with friendly characters, and uncover secrets hidden in ancient ruins. Who knows what treasures you might find?

But beware - Jaer’s world-hating cultists are out to get you. They steal your supplies and disturb your peace. Stay alert and protect yourself and your friends from their relentless attacks. The path to a peaceful life will not be easy, but the reward is worth it.

Finally, try your hand at traditional Chinese farming methods. Master watering, fertilizing, and temperature control to grow delicious crops. Harvest your harvest and enjoy the delicious taste of Chinese cuisine - all while building a new life in an exciting world.
