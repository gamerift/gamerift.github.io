Game Description#

BeamNG.drive is a car simulator that simulates the behavior of cars during collisions. Excellent physics, realistic controls and spectacular accidents are the main features of the game. The simulator allows you to find out what the consequences will be if, for example, the steering wheel of a Ferrari jams and the car flies into the oncoming lane, where it collides with a 40-ton truck. Or assess the damage to an army jeep whose brakes jammed when it was going down a hill at a speed of 90 km/h, flew off the road and crashed into a tree.

The huge fleet of vehicles will not leave car enthusiasts indifferent; with the help of mods it is possible to add even domestic cars. You can choose several locations for your crash tests: a training ground filled with various jumps and loops, a snow-covered forest, or a small town. There is also water and air transport, and long trains travel along the railways, capable of knocking down an unlucky driver.

Thanks to the excellent skidding physics, you can drive through the tracks, boldly using the drift technique on turns, while trying not to crash the car, or vice versa, depending on how the player wishes. Free mode allows you to do the craziest experiments. In this sandbox, you come up with your own tasks - a beautiful passage of a track, or a giant collision - the game will definitely not disappoint!
