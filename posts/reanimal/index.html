Game Description#

REANIMAL is a journey through a distorted reality where monsters are the product of the traumatic memories of the main characters. In this world, a hellish version of their home island, the characters must face unimaginable threats. One of these nightmares is an invulnerable monster nicknamed the Sniffer, from which you can only escape.

The gameplay is based on stealth and puzzle solving in an oppressive atmosphere, forcing you to work together to survive among the mutated animals and other grotesque creatures that inhabit such ominous locations as the Mill and the Freight Station. Confronting these horrors is entrusted to a brother and sister known as Boy and Girl, who are forced to overcome the dangers of a dark world together. Moving between locations by boat, they are able to non-linearly explore the disparate corners of the island, each of which holds its own dark secret and is part of an overall fragmented history.

REANIMAL’s co-op mechanics are designed to constantly maintain tension, whether it’s when solving problems together on land or during water crossings. The interaction between the two characters becomes the only chance for redemption in desperate circumstances. The main goal of this creepy adventure is to rescue three missing friends, one of whom is a character named Hood. Escape from the island that was once home becomes not just a task of survival for the brother and sister, but a way to face their difficult past.
