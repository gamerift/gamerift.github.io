Game Description#

Forts is a two-dimensional strategy that allows you to team up with a friend over the Internet online in order to fight against a common enemy for free on a pirate, or play online with other people, entering into confrontation. The gameplay is completely based on the laws of physics. You need to build a base, buy weapons and destroy your opponent’s outpost, destroying it under the foundation.

To build fortifications, you need to collect resources and develop the technology tree. As you level up, you gain access to new weapons and tools. All enemies pose a serious threat. Alone or using the free multiplayer option, you need to find their weak points in order to deal critical damage. There are no restrictions in construction - everything depends only on the set of resources. Up to 8 players can take part in the competition at the same time.

The single player campaign consists of 28 missions. Multiplayer allows you to choose from 4 factions with 12 commanders. Each of the heroes has its own unique abilities and skills. A smart matchmaking system selects opponents based on level and personal skill. A standings table is provided that tracks achievements. There are a total of 16 powerful guns with individual features. There is an extensive editor that allows you to create your own levels and upload them to the network. Forts has a training mode available that allows you to master the mechanics of the project.
