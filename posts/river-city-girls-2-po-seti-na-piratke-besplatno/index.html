Game Description#

River City Girls 2 is a dynamic beat-em-up with the ability to play online or locally, fighting enemies with a friend or random players. You can play online for free on pirates in a company of up to four people or go through the story alone. Events are transferred to a metropolis called River City. Online play allows you to take control of familiar girls from the first parts, such as Misako, Keko, Kunio and Riki, as well as two new heroines: Marian and Provi.

Each girl has not only a unique appearance and character, but also special abilities and combos. There are completely new locations to explore. The list of enemies and bosses has been updated. The ability to play free online games allows you to interact with your teammates in every possible way. For example, performing powerful paired combos or finishing moves. Strikes can be delivered on the ground and while jumping. Special moves are elemental effects that cause devastating damage to enemies.

Having accumulated enough experience, you can level up and improve your skills. Additional techniques and super attacks are gradually unlocked. There are 40 shops selling items and accessories throughout the city. You can also recruit defeated enemies in shops, buying them as mercenaries. There are now more interactive objects in locations that you can interact with. There is a dynamic change of day. Characters, mechanics and features: 6 playable characters with a unique fighting style; Vibrant locations with various secrets and bonuses; Mercenaries who help in battle; Combo attacks, elemental special moves and air combat; Directed cutscenes in the engine and voice acting for all characters ;Anime style and dynamic eight-bit soundtrack.
