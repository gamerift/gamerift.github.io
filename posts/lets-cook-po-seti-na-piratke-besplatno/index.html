Game Description#

On the pirateLets Cook you can play online with a friend for free, compete online with other players online, or join a team to manage a restaurant. Gameplay Lets Cook is a fast-paced first-person chef simulator. Online play allows you to join forces locally and work together to raise your restaurant business from scratch. It is necessary to distribute responsibilities and try to serve guests not only as quickly as possible, but also efficiently. The higher the service rating, the more customers visit the restaurant. People leave reviews about the quality of service and come back with friends if they liked everything.

When receiving income from a restaurant, you need to buy new kitchen equipment, purchase appliances, dishes, furniture and food. The more expensive the ingredients, the higher their quality and, accordingly, the price of the final dish. The possibility of free online play is designed for 16 participants. You can compete in teams or create your own chef team for one crazy business idea. As it expands, the establishment has more seats. In this regard, the number of orders is increasing. One player must take orders, the second must make preparations, the third must cook, the fourth must deal with delivery, and all in that order. The better the level of organization in the team, the greater the chances of becoming the best restaurant in the city.
