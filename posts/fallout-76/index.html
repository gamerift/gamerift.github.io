Game Description#

According to the plot ofFallout 76, the first people came out of shelters after a nuclear war. They set off to explore the Wasteland, where mutants are already lying in wait. The player will be able to visit 6 regions, each of which has its own types of dangerous animals and landscape. Among the scorched desert there are also wooded areas with red trees. What is most surprising is that quite recently the desert territories were inhabited by those who were able to survive the apocalypse on the surface. You need to find notes to find out more stories about local residents.

In Fallout 76, in addition to the single-player campaign, full-fledged multiplayer was added. Upon reaching level 5, players can engage in PvP battles. However, the duel will not begin if the second user does not want it. If you don’t shoot back, enemy bullets don’t do much harm.

You can acquire allies who will help in building a base and clearing monsters. There is no particular need for construction, because at the beginning of the game the gamer receives C.A.M.P. – a portable camp with outbuildings and a house. The user’s peace will be guarded by security turrets. However, it is much easier to eliminate high-level enemies in a group. Competition still takes place - players fight for sources of titanium and aluminum, rare and valuable metals for crafting.

The character has basic characteristics, such as strength and health. Parameters are upgraded using perk cards received for leveling up. At any time, even during battle, skill points can be redistributed. If you need to open a lock, then the “gunsmith” ability changes to “hacking”. The appearance is chosen according to taste - new outfits are purchased in the store for local currency. In addition to regular clothes, you can wear pajamas or buy a vampire costume.
