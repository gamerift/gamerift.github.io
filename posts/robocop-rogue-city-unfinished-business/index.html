Game Description#

Robocop: Rogue City - Unfinished Business - this is a separate addition, the action of which takes place after stripping Detroit from criminal groups. Hope for a calm life appears with the construction of Omnitower, a new residential complex from OCP. However, this peaceful undertaking interrupts the capture of the building with a detachment of elite mercenaries, who turned it into an impregnable fortress.

Robokop has to storm the tower in order to neutralize the threat and restore the legality, using all his arsenal for this. Each floor of the tower in Robocop: Rogue City - Unfinished Business is a test for a player. The opponents are technologically advanced enemies: from flying drones and robots-Kamikadze to stationary turrets. It is ahead to fight with seriously armored machine gunners, maneuverable fighters on jet satchels, and even cyborgs with katans.

The hero’s arsenal has both the classic Auto-9 pistol and a new weapon like a cryo gun for freezing enemies. The environment can be used to commit cruel achievement in this project. The plot is deepened into the past of Alex Murphy, offering a mission where his life is revealed to the transformation in the cyborg, with the voice of Peter Weller. A separate episode makes it possible to control the cult step of the ED-209 in order to demonstrate the mercenaries all the power of this urban pacifier. As they pass, meetings also take place with other familiar characters, which closer to the new story with the original game.
