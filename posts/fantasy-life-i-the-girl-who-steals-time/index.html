Game Description#

In Fantasy Life I: The Girl Who Steals Time, immersion in the atmosphere of leisurely existence within the boundaries of the fantasy world is available. This title includes the possibility of changing roles among 14 different professions called lives. Participants can collect resources by fishing or wood harvesting, cooking, form an island space, or unite with other gamers to confront creatures in extensive areas.

The narrative begins with the adventurer’s accession to the team of the archaeologist Edward on a sea journey. Following the light from a large bone education in the form of a dragon leads this group to an unknown, uninhabited island. Upon arrival, the team discovers a significant collapse, the shape of the dragon from the bones and the recorded appeal of the girl with a request for help in the salvation of the world. The Title Fantasy Life I: The Girl Who Steals Time requires the movement between the island in its current state and its type of thousand years ago to identify the causes of its decline.

The key element here is 14 specific lives. Each has unique features and allows you to interact with the environment in various ways. Collective lives support the process of accumulating objects or materials, craft lives allow you to create equipment or provisions, and combat lives involve confrontation with monsters. Anyone has the possibility of the transition between any of these roles at any time, building their own journey. The materials collected through the selected lives serve to promote the restoration of the island.
