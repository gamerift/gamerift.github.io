Game Description#

Maiden and Spell immerses players in a whimsical world of magical girl battles where you can play online and enjoy the game with a friend. In this fast-paced and colorful air fighter, available even on a pirate, eight magical heroines face each other in exciting duels. Here you can fight online for free on the Internet, dodging enemy attacks and responding with powerful spells. Maiden and Spell Multiplayer offers a unique gaming experience for anyone who is ready to immerse themselves in a world of magic and battle.

The game has two main modes in which you can test your strength. Versus mode pits you against friends, while Story mode challenges you to increasingly challenging opponents. As you progress through the story mode, you will uncover the secrets of the Star Kingdom and fight hellish bosses that will not let you relax. The Great Circle, a treacherous dungeon at the edge of the map, lures adventurers with promises of powerful treasures and ancient secrets.

The charm of Maiden and Spell lies in its simple gameplay combined with depth. Your success depends on a tiny 3-pixel hitbox, a barrage of shiny projectiles and your reflexes. The game’s aesthetics are a feast for the eyes, with cute characters paired with fantastic environments. For those looking to challenge themselves, unlock additional content and complete additional challenges that will push your skills to the limit. When you’re ready to take your magic online, head to the lobby for cooldown-enabled multiplayer.
