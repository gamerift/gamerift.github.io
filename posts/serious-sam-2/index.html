Game Description#

The entire Serious Sam 2 - a first-person shooter with a lot of action - shows us the atmosphere of an absurd fight with many monsters, weapons and a huge number of bonuses scattered on each planet.

Cool Sam is full of hope to kill Mental. Everyone in the universe knows that the antagonist is immortal, and in order to destroy him, make him vulnerable, you will have to collect the Medallion of Power. Its parts were scattered all over the world, but the “Great Council”, which contacts Sam, says that all 5 parts were taken by Mental’s minions, and now it is necessary to find them and take the parts. Our rivals hold parts of the Medallion of Power in their clutches, and Mental knows that no one is able to put them together, however, using his full arsenal, Cool Sam can take them. We will have revolvers, pistols, cannons, rocket launchers, shotguns, assault rifles, grenade launchers, poison arrows and many other weapons in our hands. It lies freely in many places on the map, but you have to be curious to get it - the rooms are akin to Easter eggs.

The first opponent is the monkey Kwongo. The second one is Zoom-Zum. The third is Chan. The fourth is a Clear magician who uses this part to conduct his magical rituals - the hardest to get, because the ritual can kill Sam. The fifth part is located in the dragon Cecil, and this is the only villain that Sam cannot kill. In addition to weapons, various bonuses are scattered around the map. Even though this is a first-person shooter, we will have several abilities. Regeneration, fast running, jumping great distances. However, the shooter is defined in the FPS subgenre, since the action inside the game is an endless stream of monsters with stretched but pleasant graphics.
