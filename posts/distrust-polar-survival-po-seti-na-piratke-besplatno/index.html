Game Description#

Distrust: Polar Survival is an isometric survival game that you can play online for free as a pirate with a friend. The story can be shared online on the Internet. The project is inspired by the film The Thing. The gameplay allows you to take control of one of two heroes who will have to go through numerous procedurally generated locations. Events develop in wild cold conditions. Online play allows for interaction between teammates as they try to survive on the space station.

The polar station was completely deserted. The main characters are two rescuers who survived a plane crash. They should not sleep, because in this way they will introduce monsters from nightmares into reality. The possibility of free online play is aimed at joint exploration of the deserted premises of stations, trying to survive in extreme conditions. It is necessary to monitor the level of hunger, cold and fatigue. The protagonists gradually go crazy and suffer from hallucinations. In the later stages, it is difficult to distinguish truth from fiction. The gameplay of Distrust: Polar Survival is represented by: anomalies lurking in the night; hardcore survival conditions in the vastness of the north; procedural generation of premises; cooperative mode for two; more than 15 heroes with unique abilities; random events and unexpected plot twists; hallucinations and surreal images.
