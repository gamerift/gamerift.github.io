Game Description#

Call of Juarez: The Cartel immerses users in the atmosphere of a Western with a modern interpretation. In addition to the dynamic story campaign, the user has the opportunity to play online with a friend on a pirate. Dividing into teams, gamers destroy each other on the proposed maps, using a rich arsenal of weapons and opportunities provided by a particular map. The online game mode will allow you to test your shooting skills, as well as determine who is the best at this particular moment.

The plot campaign of the project “Call of Juarez: Cartel” takes place in the modern world and tells the story of a group consisting of a detective and two agents. They are tasked with taking down a drug cartel that has carried out a major terrorist attack, killing hundreds of innocent people. But everything turns out to be much more difficult than expected, because the enemies do not intend to give up so easily. Players can expect ambushes, heavy weapons fire, as well as protracted battles in narrow rooms, where at a high level of difficulty it is sometimes impossible to raise your head. Of course, this will not always be the case, and depending on the location in which the main action takes place, the dynamics and overall pace of the shootout will change. In addition, an extensive arsenal of weapons will allow you to choose your own gaming style, breaking through in battle or methodically shooting enemies from cover.

The online game offers team battles in places similar to the locations from the single-player campaign. These can be office buildings, warehouses and other premises. Statistics display the number of living participants, the number of kills committed by a particular user, as well as the time remaining until the end of the round. The team with the most points at the end will win. The possibility of free online play allows you to compete with other users in existing arenas, improving your skills and taking leading positions in the ratings based on the results of battles.
