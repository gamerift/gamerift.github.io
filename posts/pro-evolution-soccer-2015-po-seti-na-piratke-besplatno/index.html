Game Description#

Pro Evolution Soccer 2015 is a football simulator that offers you to take part in online tournaments over the Internet. You can compete with a friend or random players from all over the world. The pirate Pro Evolution Soccer 2015 features all multiplayer modes and championships for free. Online play, a single player campaign and a huge amount of varied football content are available.

The possibility of free online play allows you to compete both against other players and to play together with friends on the same team. For the first time in the series, second leagues were introduced for the championships of England, France, Italy and Spain. Also in PES 2015, the physics and animations of football players have been significantly improved. Now athletes behave more realistically on the field. Dribbling has also improved. Great attention is paid to inertia, which makes the gameplay even more believable.

The developers placed special emphasis on conveying the individual characteristics of each player. For example, Messi controls the ball better without letting it go too far from him, Ronaldo can run the entire field with the ball, and Robben makes powerful shots with his left foot. The style of play depends on the team. Passes and shots have been significantly reworked. Hitting the ball now feels more intuitive. The artificial intelligence of the players on the field has been updated. They adapt to the game and can also make mistakes and bad decisions. myClub mode allows you to go from a beginner footballer to a world-class professional player.
