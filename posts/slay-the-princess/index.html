Game Description#

Slay the Princess is a dark and daring adventure that will put your skills to the ultimate test, combining the genres of psychological horror and dating simulator. Your mission is simple: kill the princess and save the world from destruction. But don’t be fooled by her charms and promises. She will stop at nothing to stop you from completing your task. Make your way through the dense forest and find the hut at the end of the path. Head down to the basement where the princess awaits you, ready to challenge you at every turn. But don’t let her deceive you with her lies and cunning. Focus on your target and destroy it once and for all.

This game is not for the faint of heart. To overcome the obstacles in your path, you will need strategic thinking and cunning. And if you fail, you die. Many. So be careful and don’t lose your head. Choose your words carefully, because every decision you make will affect the outcome of the game. Will you be a hero or a villain? The choice is yours in this branching narrative adventure. Immerse yourself in the fully voice-acted world of Slay the Princess, brought to life by the incredible talents of Jonathan Sims and Nicole Goodnight. It’s an RPG like no other, created by the same team behind the critically acclaimed Scarlet Hollow.
