Game Description#

Rogue Masters is a six-player online game where you can play online with a friend for free. In this online game, you fight the ever-destructive force of Entropy on a pirate ship. Combine your efforts with five allies and use your power to fight waves of vile creatures. Over the Internet, your online game will test your skills, and your enemies will become stronger as you progress. Prepare for the ultimate test - Champion of Entropy - and prove your strength.

The combat system is inspired by Souls-style games, requiring precise timing and strategy. Choosing the right weapon depending on the situation is the key to victory. Loot and equip your character with various items to give him a personal touch. Don’t forget to customize your appearance using the extensive transmog system - saving the world requires looking good, right?

Rogue Masters offers flexibility. Organize your own game or join existing ones using the list of servers, equipped with connection quality indicators to ensure a smooth gaming experience (using a peer-to-peer connection). Craving a solo adventure? No problem, offline mode will allow you to tackle Entropy alone. And the best part? No microtransactions - everything is earned as you play. So grab your friends or go solo and unleash your inner Rogue Master!
