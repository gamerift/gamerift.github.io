Game Description#

Before Your Eye s will take users on an emotional journey from a first-person perspective. The player influences the plot with his own blinks, changing the story and actions. Innovative technology allows you to use a webcam to track the gamer’s blinks and interact with the virtual universe. Along the way you will encounter joyful and heartbreaking memories as your whole life flashes before your eyes.

It all begins with the soul setting off on a journey through the afterlife. It is there that you have to remember everything that happened before. The main character awakens after the death of a mythical ferryman on board the ship, and he will need to carry souls to the afterlife. In order for the central character to move forward, he will need to tell the story of his own life. Because of this, the carrier sends the protagonist to relive his moments.

The walkthrough includes dark repressed memories, a voice-over story, and interactive moments. The developers wanted to convey how people go through life’s impossible expectations that they place on themselves and the regrets behind them. About how much of this does not matter and only aggravates the internal state. This is transmitted through the sailor ward, and the mechanics of blinking will track the gamer’s emotions.
