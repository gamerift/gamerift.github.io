Game Description#

On the pirateHELLDIVERS you can play online with a friend, taking part in all multiplayer modes for free. By controlling the Hell Marines, you can take part in intense battles over the Internet, online or locally. The Marines are an elite force protecting Super-Earth from an alien threat. Alone or in a team, you need to become a participant in an intergalactic war. HELLDIVERS multiplayer includes a Proving Grounds that allows you to play campaign missions with various modifications.

When playing in solo mode or using the free online play option, you can take part in two additional competitions, represented by random maps and favorites mode. Includes previously released expansions: New Hell, Masters of the Galaxy, Making It Hot, and Democracy Strikes Back. The multiplayer mode introduces the Friendly Fire function, increasing the hardcore nature of co-op play.

Multiplayer allows four players to join forces. Each hero in Helldrivers has unique abilities and performs a specific function in the team. Partners need to develop an individual strategy in the fight against alien creatures. The plot part includes a set of different tasks connected by one common story. To complete all available content you will need to spend over 100 hours of real time.
