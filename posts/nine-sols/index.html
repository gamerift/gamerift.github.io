Game Description#

Adventures inNine Sols take players into an oriental fantasy setting where they will have to fight against 9 Sols. The game is a 2D platformer with soulslick elements, where you have to face unique bosses. Events take place on the territory of New Kunlun, which is a refuge for survivors. The ancient gods abandoned mortals, forcing them to confront dangerous monsters. A hero named Yi has arrived from the distant past to defeat mythological creatures.

Users in Nine Sols have to move through a variety of acacias, overcoming landscape obstacles and destroying enemies. During the battle, you will have to rely on edged weapons, carrying out slashing attacks, deflecting and parrying the opponent’s blows. Also in fights you need to use a Taoist talisman. Each battle combines different gameplay mechanics, where for each boss you need to choose the appropriate tactics to win.

During the passage, players will have to fight with ancient guardians, terrible monsters from Eastern mythology and mysterious aliens who arrived in the world of Kunlun. The protagonist will have to deal with spatial puzzles, avoid treacherous traps and eliminate opponents in locations. You will be able to explore a vast two-dimensional map, as well as upgrade your character by selecting the desired combat abilities and skills.
