Game Description#

Dread Hunger can be played online online by up to 8 players, joining forces for free on a pirate site. Single playthrough and the ability to play online with a friend are available. The possibility of free online play includes joint survival in the extreme conditions of the Arctic. The fact is that two members of the team are possessed by evil spirits and are eager to destroy the rest of the group. However, this is not so easy to find out.

The mechanics are built around the need to stay warm and eat. You can hunt and make fires to keep warm. You should be wary of the cold and packs of wild wolves. The online game Dread Hunger is aimed at interaction between participants, forcing them to come to the rescue, exchange resources and much more. Playing the role of the possessed requires you to feed your party members spoiled food, set beasts on fire, use magic, and stab your party members in the back when they least expect it.

Sometimes you have to make difficult decisions in order to survive. Defeated players can be cut, using limbs as weapons, or, much worse, food. As the captain of the group, efforts must be made to ensure that the ship can set sail again. To do this, you need to find coal to power the ship, or blow it up so that people will never be able to leave this place. Dynamic changes in weather conditions and time of day are presented.
