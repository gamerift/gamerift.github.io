Game Description#

Fran Bow is an indie horror quest with unusual art and animation. The backdrops are made very attractively; you will want to explore the world of the game thanks to the interesting plot.

A ten-year-old girl named Fran Bow-Dagenhart has been unhappy since childhood - her parents were killed. She was able to escape and later good people were able to find her in the forest. They really can’t help her. Fran is sent to a mental hospital, where she is diagnosed with a serious disorder due to childhood trauma. To begin with, the player is given the goal of leaving the hospital and finding a lost cat named Mister Midnight. Later, the girl will understand that she needs to go to her own Aunt Grace.

Find items throughout each area that Bow can pick up to solve puzzles and open doors to the next areas. Keep the heroine’s mental state relatively normal, because it’s already difficult for her. The fact that the protagonist is not herself is reflected in almost everything: movements, strange dialogues (conversations take place with your choice of the next phrase) and decisions during the passage.

At the hospital, your girl was prescribed a huge number of pills. They don’t help, they don’t even drown out little Fran’s pain, but taking them gives access to another reality. With the help of this “gift” you can penetrate into inaccessible places. All that remains is to figure out whether the second world is real or is it a hallucination due to the side effects of the drugs.

The atmosphere of horror is also created by the characters you meet in the world: a huge talking ant, living shadows, suspiciously carefree Siamese sisters, truly scary tribes of cones and skeletons. The unusual style of this game will definitely attract you.
