Game Description#

Psycho Fear offers an addictive online game where you can play online with a friend. This puzzle game is filled with deadly traps where survival requires both quick thinking and strong nerves. Together with a fellow Internet surfer, you will have to outwit the mysterious organizer and get out of his sinister traps. The game is available for free, even on pirate sites, allowing everyone to enjoy this intense adventure.

Death lurks around every corner in this unforgiving game. There is no room for mistakes - one wrong move and it’s game over. But don’t give up! The karma system is a lifeline, allowing you to get a second chance for your hard-earned points. And if you have a buddy watching your back in co-op mode, they can play the hero, using a syringe of Serum Y to bring you back from the brink.

Whether you’re flying solo or teaming up, you’ll step into the shoes of a poor soul caught in a labyrinth of puzzles and dangers. It’s time to put on your thinking cap and flex your problem-solving muscles if you want to make it out alive. This isn’t your grandma’s crossword - Psycho Fear takes survival horror to the next level by forcing your brain to work on all cylinders. To crack the code and cheat death, you will have to monitor your surroundings, look for useful items and interact with everything that is not nailed down.

The main thing here is traps, traps and more traps. Each one is a work of diabolical art designed to push your gray matter to the limit. To survive, you’ll have to think outside the box - very outside the box - from basic brain puzzles to mind-blowing murders. The project exudes atmosphere, enveloping you in a cocoon of tension that will have you jumping through shadows. Every corner is designed to keep you on the edge of your seat, completely immersing you in this living nightmare.

At the heart of Psycho Fear is a karma system that is more than just decoration. It is the puppeteer who pulls the strings of your destiny, and every choice you make causes a ripple in the world. It’s not just about scoring points - your karma score can make or break the entire gameplay. It will turn things up when you least expect it, throw twists into the plot, and even decide whether you make it to the end of the credits or become another nameless victim. So be on your guard - in Psycho Fear, karma is always on alert, and she has a wicked sense of humor.
