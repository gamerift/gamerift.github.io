Game Description#

A musical game designed for the good taste of the player Beat Saber. The levels are a small interactive location where blocks of 2 colors fly towards the player. The player must cut these blocks. The difficulty of the game is that the number of blocks and the method of destruction are always different. For example, in some levels you will have to chop them with a sword of a certain color, in others you will have to hit them from the right side. Some levels require a strike from a certain side, with the appropriate sword, at high speed.

You download the tracks yourself, which is why many players liked Beat Saber. The game also supports VR glasses play mode. Difficulty levels are also divided into minefields, levels with walls, defensive levels and others. If you find yourself in a “mine” field, then the song has deep rhythmic motifs, and they will be performed in the form of bombs that cannot be touched, just as a person cannot hear them with a standard sound card. There will also be levels that the player will have to dodge.

All levels are hand-drawn by the developers, so the animation definitely deserves respect and your download. The game invites you to become a musical super-hero who copes with an alien invasion operation in which the main enemies are simple cubes that represent sound.
