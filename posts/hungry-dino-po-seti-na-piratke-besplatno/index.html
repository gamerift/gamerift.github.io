Game Description#

Hungry Dino is a bright and dynamic arcade game, the gameplay of which is aimed at playing online with a friend, competing for free on a pirate in various competitions with players around the world. You can play both over a local network and online online. A solo mode is available, where you have to overcome many different challenges. While controlling the dinosaur, you need to absorb various objects or flee from predators.

The online game Hungry Dino invites up to 8 people to join forces. You can divide into teams and compete with each other. The possibility of free online play provides participants with a huge number of interactive maps and monsters with their own unique abilities. At levels there may be powerful bosses from whom you need to escape. Some creatures are not so easy to defeat, so you will need to join forces with other participants.

Depending on the round, the sides switch roles. Players can be both prey and predators. The task is simple - eat as many tasty treats as possible. By absorbing loot or various bonuses, various power-ups and victory points are awarded. By competing on various interactive maps, you can get a temporary boost, an extra life, stop all monsters of the same color, and much more. By choosing the right tactics in matches, you can easily get to the top of the standings.
