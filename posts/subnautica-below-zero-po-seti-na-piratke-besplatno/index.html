Game Description#

Subnautica: Below Zero is an ocean survival game with the ability to play online with a friend, together on a pirate ship, escaping numerous underwater creatures for free. Alone or online, you will have to face the horrors of the deep sea. Health, thirst and hunger indicators are available. It is necessary to monitor oxygen levels and temperature.

The online game takes you on an adventure to an alien oceanic continent. You will also need to return to planet 4546B, exploring the cold Arctic region. The plot of Subnautica: Below Zero reveals in more detail the events on Alterra and invites you to find your sister together with the heroes of the story. The possibility of a free online game invites you to interact with each other, going to explore abandoned stations. It is required to collect diaries, journals, various data and much more.

There are many different biomes represented, including the seabed, land, caves, snowy mountains, etc. All biomes have their own unique properties, so it is important to be able to adapt to the environment. There is transport and a huge amount of different equipment. The polar fox allows you to travel long distances. You will need to swim past the gigantic hole-tailed titan, find the ghostly leviathan, see penguin wings and many other amazing inhabitants of the unknown continent. Many creatures can attack, so you need to run away or fight off enemies.
