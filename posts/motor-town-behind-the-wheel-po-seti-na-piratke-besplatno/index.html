Game Description#

Motor Town Behind The Wheel is a driving simulator that allows you to get behind the wheel of trucks both alone and online, connecting with a friend for free on a pirate. You can play online, transporting a huge amount of various cargo with your partner. An open world is available, in which different settlements are represented. Between them it is necessary to transport various goods or people. By successfully completing instructions, funds are credited. They are necessary to purchase new vehicles or spare parts.

Network play allows you to deliver in a cooperative setting or compete with each other. A special racing mode is available where you can improve your driving skills. Trips take place both during the day and at night. If you break the rules, the police will pay attention to it. You will need to monitor fuel and repair vehicles.

The gameplay is represented by realistic physics. Taxis, buses, trucks and other types of transport are available to drive. An assistance system is available that allows you to better feel a particular type of machine. In racing mode, you can participate in two types of races: Onroad and Offroad. Presents online tables tracking the best performance of drivers around the world. Gameplay features: single-player mode and the possibility of free online play; realistic physics and simulated driving; interactive open world with a daily cycle; passenger and cargo transportation; buying and renting cars; racing and autocross.
