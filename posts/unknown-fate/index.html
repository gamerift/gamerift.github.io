Game Description#

The game consists of constantly searching for objects and solving puzzles. We become a certain entity that helps the main character, Richard, cope with amnesia. He wakes up in a world completely unfamiliar to him, tries to remember at least something, but his attempts end in failure. The main task of the player is to find the appropriate items needed in the task, as well as solve a lot of puzzles. The developers of Unknown Fate have created a fascinating world, the history of which is revealed gradually. It combines colorful graphics, interesting, charismatic characters, third-person role-playing and a dialogue system that shows us the true story of Richard, who is gradually returning his memory to its place.

The character will be able to shoot at special points to move forward in the location; will be able to jump and get objects, as well as kill monsters encountered along the way. At one point, he even drops his hands and closes his eyes after one character’s phrase: “this world will die.” But he continues to move because he is… A man.

The gradually engrossing plot is revealed gradually, as stated above. From the very beginning we will solve puzzles in order to at least somehow get acquainted with the gameplay, then the game will become a little more difficult due to the artifacts found in the locations. When we learn to shoot, the game will throw in several monsters to test our abilities. This is where the increase in game difficulty ends.

Overall, Unknown Fate is a great story that doesn’t end too quickly. Only after such games do you want to think about the present, about the world, about humanity, about memory.
