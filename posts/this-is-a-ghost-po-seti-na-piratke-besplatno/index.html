Game Description#

You can play the horror gameThis is a Ghost online with a friend or join a team with other players for free on a pirate to investigate a mysterious case in the same mansion? where supernatural creatures settled. On the Internet, online players must interact with each other and solve various puzzles, while simultaneously trying to catch the ghost. The online game is represented by the choice of your hunter, who serves in a secret organization for the investigation of paranormal activity. Finding yourself locked in a gloomy room, you need to look for evidence to help determine the type of entity. Once the ghost has been identified, it will need to be sent back to the afterlife.

The possibility of free online play in This is a Ghost allows you to cooperate with friends of up to 4 people. Each hunter performs his own function and explores the location using unique equipment. A total of 14 tools are available that allow you to interact with the outside world. A total of 45 abilities are available for all characters, which must be combined for maximum effectiveness.

At the locations you can find 15 special objects with their own unique characteristics. There are 4 interactive maps available with an individual set of rooms and tasks. Having signed a contract to destroy monsters, you need to find out as much as possible about the entity. There is a real detective investigation to be done. After successfully completing a task, your reputation increases, which opens access to new contracts and equipment.
