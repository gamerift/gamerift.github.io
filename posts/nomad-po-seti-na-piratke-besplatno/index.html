Game Description#

Nomad provides the opportunity to play online with a friend, teaming up for free on a pirate to survive together in the vast open world. A co-op and versus mode is introduced, allowing you to fight against other survivors online online. While exploring various biomes, you need to collect resources to build a shelter, look for provisions for food, supplies for weapons, etc. You can engage in crafting, creating new types of weapons or useful tools. The possibility of free online play allows you, together with your partners, to create an entire residential city, defending yourself from monsters and other players.

The gameplay of Nomad is presented in the form of a cubic survival game, the actions of which take place in the world of the post-apocalypse. Game mechanics include hunting, crafting, killing and more. Online play offers unlimited possibilities, allowing participants to build their own world in an interactive sandbox. Everyone chooses their own path: to be a bloodthirsty bandit or a real hero, saving and helping others. You can drive vehicles and park cars in your own garage.

The island has 4x4 square kilometers of territory. Scattered throughout the area are various resources that are needed to build your own buildings. They consist of a foundation, walls, windows, doorways, ceilings, etc. Tools and weapons are created at the workbench. You can engage in farming, growing various vegetables and fruits. Harvest can be sold or traded for other useful items, offering lucrative deals to survivors.
