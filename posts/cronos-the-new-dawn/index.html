Game Description#

Cronos: The New Dawn represents to gamers a unique mixture of time travel, horror to survival and tactical battle. The action takes place in a gloomy post -apocalyptic world and immerses players in a dark and unpredictable narrative. History begins with a mysterious assignment from the team, a mysterious organization that sends you to Poland in the 1980s. As a traveler, you are instructed to find people who died during the Apocalypse, and return their souls to the future. The goal is not only to survive, but also to understand the subtle balance between the past and future, where each mission entails the consequences that spread in time.

The structure of the game is largely based on a cyclic cycle. You start in the abandoned future, comb the remnants of civilization in search of temporary faults, and then go into the past to find important figures that can help restore or change the course of events. These temporary faults serve as a portal between the two worlds, creating a rhythm back and forth that makes you be alert. The arising puzzles and tasks require not only gross power, but also acute mind and perspicacity. You cannot rush, every step requires careful planning, whether it is the management of limited resources or a decision on how to survive after a meeting with a grotesque being that appeared on the ruins of mankind. Survival in Cronos: The New Dawn is not just avoiding danger, it is resource management and strategic thinking.
