Game Description#

Dig or Die is a two-dimensional adventure designed to be played online with a friend, going through the story for free on a pirate. The connection is available via the Internet online. The plot starts with the fact that a spaceship with the main characters on board crashes. Once on a wild planet, the protagonists need to build a reliable defense to protect themselves from the bloodthirsty monsters living on the continent. The planet is extremely unfriendly to strangers. Network play is aimed at constant interaction between partners. You must do everything possible to prevent enemies from reaching the protagonists.

Using the opportunity of a free online game, you need to build structures directly in real time. You will also need to put logic into the structures, since they can be flooded or destroyed. The gameplay of Dig or Die is based on three mechanics: strategy, physics and the water cycle. Rain and high tides can destroy structures and make heroes very vulnerable to attacks from monsters. The characters act as representatives of CRAFT & Co.

The heroes are engaged in the sale of automated tool systems. While exploring space, they found themselves on a hostile planet, from where it is not so easy to get out. Using various tools and finding useful resources, you need to do everything possible to escape this hostile continent. A solo mode is introduced, as well as a special game At the Bottom of the Sea. Base Defense and Celestial Peace are also available. There are no weapons. The world is surrounded by electrical traps and other dangers. Procedural generation is in effect. It is important to craft useful items to effectively defend against threats.
