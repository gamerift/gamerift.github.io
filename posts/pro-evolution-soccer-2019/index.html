Game Description#

PES 2019 - one of the games that won the recognition of fans through the territory of the entire planet - Pro Evolution Soccer 2019 is a new football league hidden in the old filling. Gradually improving their brainchild, the developers came to a perfect schedule, plausible sound and real physics of movement. This part includes several additions in which you will hire professional football players for several matches and prove that the team management is your craft from birth!

In PES 2019, standard football control mechanics. Gather a dream team and play on the football league. In career mode, you choose several professional players with whom you have to pick up all possible cups. The characteristics of the character are distributed to:

The name of the state for which it plays; A general account of the player’s position showing his professionalism among the rest of the OPZ, CPZ, OZ, OP; Age; Height; Team.

The game has several game modes:

Career. You create a team. Set it. You get several full -fledged contracts that begin to lead you through the career of a football coach from the first minutes of the game; Fast game. Take control of the team, go to any field and conquer it, removing the enemy from the road; Network game against a real person; Network cups.

The new system in PES 2019 “Magic Moments” will demonstrate the blows and behavior of players from several angles. Fatigue now affects the appearance. You can hire the best players in Brazil for 10 matches, as well as rare “characters” include Beckham, Coutinho and premium agent.
