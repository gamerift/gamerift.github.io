Game Description#

Get Packed Fully Loaded is completely tailored to playing online with a friend on a pirate, because this project is cooperative with the possibility of simultaneous participation of up to 4 people. Here the scenario is played out in which a salt mining company takes over a settlement called Ditchlington. At the same time, there are those who are ready to help and speed up this process, and they are beginning to act. In addition to the fact that the gameplay is designed for online playthroughs, the system provides several game modes with their own rules and victory conditions.

In the Get Packed Fully Loaded campaign mode, users need to remove all items ordered by the owner from houses and buildings as quickly as possible. A limited amount of time is given for this, so you should decide in advance who will “clean up” a specific area. Tables, sofas, beds and even basketball hoops all need to be removed from the property and placed in the back of a truck parked down the street. Everything happens with a top-down view, so each user can easily navigate and choose the room closest to them. When the next segment is completed, an employer report appears on the screen, which displays the performance of all group members. Some segments (such as pulling a car out of a garage) will require the interaction of several people at the same time. Moreover, if it is broken, the level of damage will jump to several thousand dollars. The online game involves completing 18 story levels in 6 different stages, where the complexity of the tasks will gradually increase. In addition, the possibility of free online play opens up access to 10 levels in versus mode, and also gives the opportunity to customize heroes. This allows you to move away from the usual appearance, creating a completely new image for the hero.
