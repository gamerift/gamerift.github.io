Game Description#

With Nuclear Option you’ll experience free online play, immersing yourself in the heart of the battlefields of the future. Pilot your fighter with a friend or play online with air combat masters. Immerse yourself in the storm of events on the pirated version, because here you will find exciting battles on the Internet online. Your piloting skill is a strong shield, and your arsenal offers a powerful cocktail of destruction in this addictive online game. And all this is absolutely free!

Choose a squadron of five aces, each of whom is a master of his craft. The nimble CI-22 “Cricket” sweeps through the ravines, drowning out the rebels with precise strikes. The versatile T/A-30 Compass seamlessly transitions from light attack to training pilots, serving as a jack-of-all-trades in the heat of combat. For stealth infiltration, the SAH-46 “Chicane” stalks the shadows, a predator bringing silent death from the night. Do you crave air supremacy? The FS-12 “Revoker” is a steely and furious blur, tearing through enemy formations with unrivaled speed and firepower. And for the destruction of entire landscapes, the SFB-81 “Darkroach”, a harbinger of annihilation with its ammunition of tactical and strategic nuclear weapons, is suitable.

But Nuclear Option is not all about brute force. Each aircraft boasts a meticulously detailed cockpit, a symphony of gauges and displays to keep you one step ahead of the storm. Situational awareness is your lifeline, the holographic HUD will tell you the secrets of nearby targets and lurking threats. Master the art of controlling a virtual joystick or feel the satisfying weight of a physical controller in your hands.

The sky above is not a playground. Realistic physics simulates every stall, spin and g force, turning your plane into a living, breathing machine. Every bullet bite, every missile scar leaves its mark on your plane, subtly changing its characteristics and constantly reminding you of the fragility of your metal horse.

Push the boundaries of combat in a sandbox of possibilities. The Mission Editor gives you the power to forge your own crucible, creating customized scenarios that will test your mettle. Fly solo, hone your skills against AI opponents, or team up with friends in co-op or competitive multiplayer. The choice is yours, pilot. As the sun dips below the horizon, casting long shadows across the battlefield, remember: in Nuclear Option, the sky is not the limit, but the launching pad.
