Game Description#

The futuristic third-person action gameEARTH DEFENSE FORCE: IRON RAIN can be played for free with a friend on a pirate, connecting via the Internet online or fighting shoulder to shoulder on a shared screen. You can go through the campaign alone or play online in co-op mode, together controlling fast mechs armed with various melee and ranged weapons. Players take on the role of members of the EDF, trying to stop aliens from the far reaches of the galaxy.

Multiplayer allows you to go through 50 action-packed levels together. There are 4 types of different mobile weapons available that can cause critical damage to the enemy. You have to fight huge mechanical monsters that have arrived on our planet with the goal of enslaving people. The possibility of free online play offers the choice of one of 5 difficulty levels that affect the aggressiveness of enemies and the survivability of the protagonists.

An extensive customization system is presented that allows you to individually customize your hero by choosing gender, face, hairstyle, physique, voice, armor color, abilities and weapons. The campaign is available for single playthrough, but all the features of the gameplay are revealed in paired playthrough or multiplayer co-op with several players at once. EDF fighters are the last bastion of defense for humanity.
