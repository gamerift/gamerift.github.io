Game Description#

Angola ‘86 is a strategy game that immerses gamers in a tense and multifaceted border war in South Africa. Users take on the role of a South African Defense Force (SADF) commander who is faced with the challenging task of conducting counter-insurgency operations in a hostile environment. The story, an unstable border region between South Africa and Angola, provides a unique and historically accurate backdrop for strategic decision-making.

The main gameplay involves tracking and neutralizing SWAPO rebels, a guerrilla force supported by the Angolan army FAPLA. These rebels are infiltrating from Angola, seeking to destabilize the region and challenge South African control. Users must carefully coordinate the actions of various military units, including ground troops, special forces, artillery and air support, to effectively counter the insurgent threat. Besides the military aspect, Angola ‘86 also brings a political dimension to the gameplay.

Users must manage support for the war effort at home, ensuring the government has the support it needs to continue operations. The game highlights the importance of maintaining international support, especially from the United Nations, as losing it could have serious consequences. The realistic depiction of the South African Border War allows gamers to gain a thorough understanding of the challenges faced by military commanders during this period.
