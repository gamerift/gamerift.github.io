Game Description#

The strategic simulator - Mashinky - about how to create your own network of trains and cars that will bring you money, and in return you can modernize or send them to rest when buying new equipment. Around you are beautiful landscapes of the Czech Republic, Poland, Germany and England. The sun sits down, and floods the evening light of the forest and fields, granting nightlife to residents. In the game, a system of day, night and time in general is thought out. The player must create an extensive transport empire, guided by a small number of resources from the very beginning. He will have a little material, transport and a map accidentally created by artificial intelligence.

You start at the head of an incredibly small transport company that can lay the paths to neighboring cities, and transport residents and goods in small quantities. You are also provided with a map that needs to be explored by paving new ways to create an empire! In order to competently and clearly follow the laws of business, you will have to develop all directions at once: laying out ways, construction of stations, buying equipment, advertising campaign. The laid paths will be constructed in a simple or difficult way. For example, you can pave the route from one station to another, and you can add intersections and traffic lights, pedestrian crossings and intersecting routes. Your transport will improve depending on use, but sooner or later its professional acceptance will end, and you will have to pretty much to spend money on new funds. Do not forget to update! The game invites you to switch between three modes: management, strategy in isometric space and first -person view from the car carrier. The latter is complemented by various graphic effects.

Mashinky is a business manager of passenger and freight transportation, created by Czech developers. Among its features, an improved graphics and its high realism can be distinguished, procedurally generated locations with various, as well as transitions from one era to another - from steam engines to electric trains. Each launch of the game offers a new location, with its own set of objects, slopes and mountains. The player develops his transport system - from short routes, to a whole network of messages, with stations, stops and routes. The more diverse, the greater the benefit: the money earned can be spent on improving transport and infrastructure. The project received a modern schedule, and the opportunity to observe the process from any angle, including from the train car. All units, buildings and landscapes are drawn in very detailed and high quality, so that the image looks very realistic. It is also worth noting an excellent sound accompaniment, which harmoniously fits into the format of the railway simulator.
