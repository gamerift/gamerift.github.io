Game Description#

Dawn of the Tiberium Age can be attributed to atypical mods for the game Command & Conquer: Tiberian Sun. The purpose of this project is to combine two universes together. In one of them, Albert Enstein changes the course of time, which affects history and leads to an open confrontation between Russians and West. In another, the organization created by the UN and the secret ancient society fight, while an alien substance that destroys the biosphere spreads on the Earth. Fans tried to cross the very first parts of the main franchise and branches of Red Alert. And they did all this on the engine of the sequel, which is called Tiberian Sun. Due to this schedule, it turned out to be more detailed than that of the originals.

Dawn of the Tiberium Age has 4 factions: Soviet Union, Alliance, Brotherhood NOD and GDI. You can arrange fights between these groups in random order. An unofficial product allows, for example, pushing the foreheads of the USSR and Kane’s wards or organize a battle where the Global Security Council will attack the unification of Western countries. This is a fully independent modification that does not require installation of any additional components. You contains 2 modes. The classic offers the usual gameplay from original real-instrument strategies (without mixing two different settings-scientific, fantastic and alternative historical). Fans tried to convey the old sensations from the process as accurately as possible. And the expanded makes edits in balance and adds new functions to the warring parties. In total, there are 22 single missions, 10 cooperative tasks, 93 cards for multiplayer. Enthusiasts on their own revived the online service and screwed the support of modified versions of the games.
