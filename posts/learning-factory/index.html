Game Description#

learning factory - a simulator that is designed to create an automated process of processes and, most importantly, understand one simple truth: what do cats want? The gameplay allows you to head the Kotovod factory. It was once closed, but now it has been possible to renew the process and create your own cat store. Learning factory gameplay largely gives an idea of ​​how machine learning works.

Your factory is analyzing a huge database, trying to study the behavior of pets. The main quest of the game is to understand what the cat needs. Their desires and behavior are not amenable to a simple study by a person, therefore it is necessary to build a neural network, which will study these animals 24 by 7.

To collect information about cats, it is necessary to build an entire entertainment center for purring creatures. When studying their behavior, it is necessary to analyze the collected data and create better objects and materials for pets. Gradually moving in this direction, your plant will grow and replenish with the latest equipment. Having automated all the processes, a ghostly chance will appear to make a cat happy. But the question is, is it possible at all?
