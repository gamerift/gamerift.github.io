Game Description#

VTOL VR is a military flight simulator with a first-person view and in the surroundings of the near future. The game requires a VR headset and a motion controller. The title allows you to freely rotate your head while sitting in the virtual cockpit. Modes include training, campaign and free flight. There are a lot of tasks here. In addition to other aircraft, ships, tanks, anti-aircraft and artillery installations act as opponents and allies.

There are 3 types of military equipment available in the game: the F-45A stealth fighter, the A/V-42C transport aircraft and the F/A-26B attack aircraft. There are a lot of switches you can touch on airplanes. In VTOL VR you can set or retract the landing gear and flaps, interact with the throttle, etc. The ejection handle also works here. During movement, weight, traction, lift, and resistance are taken into account.

Using the head, you can aim and control hanging guns and thermal imagers. There is no familiar interface: all information about the state of the vehicle, as well as radars, displays, and guidance are located on the panels. It is possible to refuel not only on the ground, but also in the air. Landings (including vertical ones) are carried out at airfields and aircraft carriers. There is an editor where you can create custom maps, missions and entire story campaigns. You can also upload custom creations.
