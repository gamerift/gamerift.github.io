Game Description#

InLEGO Brawls you can play online online or compete for free on a pirate game with a friend or random players. You can play online with up to 8 people. Participants are divided into teams of 4 players. The LEGO Brawls online game features several modes, the main one being battle royale. the possibility of free online play allows you to create your own Lego man, be it a ninja cleaner, a construction worker, a clown with a hammer, etc.

Many colorful levels are available for multiplayer battles, such as Barracuda Bay, the flooded caves of Ninjago, a saloon in the Wild West, Monkey Kid’s jungle, etc. By fighting in various competitions you need to earn bonus points. They allow you to unlock many cosmetic items: minifigures, skins, various emotions and other additional content. Leaderboards are presented, displaying the results of the most active and powerful players.

The goal of the battle royale is to control the center of the map. Gradually the location is filled with the color of the leading team. Additional online modes include training and party. In the training mode, you can hone your gaming skills and learn the controls, and the party mode allows participants to have fun without any goal. All Lego minifigures are divided into categories such as Classic Space, Pirates, Alien Conquest, Hidden Side, Jurassic World and many others.
