Game Description#

Spirit City: Lofi Sessions is not an ordinary adventure game. It combines game elements and practical tools to improve focus and reduce stress. You will find yourself in a virtual world filled with a variety of activities, the essence of which comes down to collecting and interacting with spirits. These aren’t just pretty additions to your virtual homes - they play an important role in achieving the goal of making you more productive.

In addition to fancy perfumes, Spirit City: Lofi Sessions offers a set of tools for creating good habits. A to-do list, timer, and even a diary are integrated into the gameplay, encouraging you to stay organized and manage your time effectively. The more you use these features, the more experience points you gain, which unlocks new content.

So what makes an adventure game special? This is how it combines relaxation and benefit. You can relax and unwind while moving towards your personal goals. A focus on productivity intertwined with interactive elements creates a unique space in the world. It’s more than just a game, it’s a place where gaming and productivity can coexist.
