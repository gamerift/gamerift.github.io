Game Description#

Frogun Encore is a standalone expansion to the popular indie game Frogun that can be played online. In the new 3D platformer, players go on an adventure around the world with a friend, exploring unique worlds. Renata and Jake, now older and more experienced, once again encounter Beelzebub’s minions. This exciting action game is available for free for those who prefer to play the pirate game, and you can enjoy it online online. Network play allows you to go through levels together, giving new emotions from joint victories!

Players will jump, skip and gallop through colorful levels. The game has more moves than a chess master: double jumps, catapults and toss jumps add spice to the gameplay. And let’s not forget about the most interesting thing - a real sofa cooperative! Now you can take your friend along for the ride, whether you want to work together or engage in a little friendly competition.

The game has more layers than an onion, with secret areas and shortcuts waiting to be discovered by those quick enough to master the advanced mechanics. Collectibles are scattered like confetti at a parade, including fly emblems and journal entries that you can piece together the stories of other adventurers. And for fashionistas, discover a variety of hats to look stylish.
