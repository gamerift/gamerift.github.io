Game Description#

Winter Memories invites players to once again join Yui, Rio and Miyuki, the trio who created unforgettable summer moments just six months ago. As the seasons change and the snow melts, a new chapter opens in your story. Rediscover a serene coastal village and reconnect with loved ones during this cold season. The past gives rise to the present, but what new experiences await you this winter?

Winter Memories is the sequel to the 2020 game Summer Memories, developed by Dojin Otome. It immerses players in the lives of three young women - Yui, Rio and Miyuki - who renew the friendship forged during their previous summer escapade. The game is a travel simulator in which players actively interact with the characters, unraveling their relationships in various scenarios.

The snowy town serves as the backdrop of the game and offers players a variety of snowy activities to take part in. From skiing and snowboarding to ice skating and snowball fights, the player’s choices determine how events unfold in a dynamic day-night cycle. Adventurers can devote time to a single character or interact with multiple characters at once, shaping the narrative trajectory with multiple possible outcomes. Winter Memories embodies the aesthetic of winter charm, with a bright and whimsical art style that seamlessly complements the aura of the season. Additional voice acting gives the characters additional depth, enhancing their emotional resonance.
