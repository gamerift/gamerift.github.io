Game Description#

KYNSEED is a role -playing game in an adventure genre and a life simulator in an open world. The game saw the light in 2018 and has already managed to find its own fan base. The essence of the passage is to completely immerse into the invented world, filled with various inhabitants, who have their own affairs and habits. The player is invited to interact with these characters, simultaneously equipping his own life. The developers have added the possibilities of collecting various materials and artifacts, building buildings and their own crafts.

The player is opened by an unknown magical world where he is invited to create his own generic dynasty. All the inhabitants of this world are gradually dying, so it is important to convey their skills to the younger generations. As such, there is no linear plot, so the player himself has the right to determine the fate of his heroes.

The developers went to the risk and performed the game in retro-stylistic, but this not only did not harm the project, but also benefited it, since the projectâ€™s atmosphere turned out to be very comfortable, and the characters and the world around the last century are reminiscent of the games and the world around him. The passage is also complemented by the soundtrack, which is made in a single style with the game. KYNSEED is a wonderful project that causes strong nostalgia for the uncomplicated games of the last century from the first minutes. However, it offers modern game mechanics and verified gameplay.
