Game Description#

Lorn’s Lure is a dynamic action platformer with a first-person view, which was developed using retro graphics, reminiscent of the project of the 90s of the last century. The user is invited to try himself in the role of a lost android, who is forced to challenge himself with a difficult, almost impossible challenge, exploring a giant cave that has not been explored by his people before. It is quite difficult to move around the available locations, because the player is constantly surrounded by abysses, falling into which will mean returning to the control point, of which there are very few. Due to the hardcore jumps, which require a perfect sense of your own virtual protégé and his abilities, the Lorna Bait project will defy any gamer, and each new level requires more and more dexterity and speed of reaction.

The robotic creature will be helped along the way by two one-handed pickaxes, which are used by climbers to climb steep slopes. With their help, the android can climb walls, run sideways along them, pull themselves up and cling over the abyss after making a long or not entirely successful jump. The gameplay of the game is not based only on dynamic scenes; the user is given a choice - to go through the single-player campaign thoughtfully, or at maximum speed. While exploring the underground depths, the protagonist stumbles upon secrets that need to be unraveled to complete additional activities.
