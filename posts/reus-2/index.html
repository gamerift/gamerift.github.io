Game Description#

Reus 2 stands out with its uniqueness and fascination. In this game, you have the power of the gods, controlling powerful giants that form the world for the prosperity or death of human civilizations. From the very beginning, everything is extremely clear: in Reus 2 you have to decide the fate of mankind, making your choice and committing actions.

You will get the opportunity to choose giants for yourself, each of which has its own abilities and characteristics. These colossal creatures are architects of your world, and their solutions will be reflected on the fate of civilizations that will appear in the future. Your role is akin to the role of a cosmic puppeteer, manipulating natural elements and directing the development of mankind.

One of the main features of the project is the intricacies of the capabilities that occur during the combination of various elements and giants. The ecosystem created is a fragile balance, and experiments with various combinations of giants and resources give amazing results. Whether it is the education of the society of ambitious explorers of space or the observation of the growth of self -destructive inciters of war, Reus 2 allows you to play a key role in the formation of these diverse human tribes.

With each passage, you leave an indelible heritage in the space, and the game inspires you to search for new paths and strategies. In your hands, power over the development of mankind, and the consequences of your choice are very great. Regardless of whether you take the role of a friendly conductor or harbinger of problems, Reus 2 offers to think about traveling through the annals of human civilization, where your giants are driving force.
