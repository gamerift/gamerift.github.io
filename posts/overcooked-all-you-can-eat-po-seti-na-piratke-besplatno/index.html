Game Description#

Chef SimulatorOvercooked! All You Can Eat can be played online online, or you can join forces in splitscreen mode. Together with a friend or players from all over the world, you can feed visitors delicious dishes, preparing them in the most extreme conditions. Single-player play is available, but playing online is much more fun as it maximizes the full potential of the gameplay. All in-game content is provided for free on the pirate site.

Events take place in the Onion Kingdom. Online game Overcooked! All You Can Eat offers you to go through 200 unique levels, choosing one of 80 chefs. Chefs from all over the world will have to create a chaos, trying to prepare the order as quickly as possible. The faster and better the work of the chef in the kitchen, the more reputation points the establishment will receive, which will allow it to reach a new level, and the chefs will improve their skills.

At the same time, the cooking conditions are very unusual and sometimes too extreme. You will have to cook in different exotic places, on moving platforms, floating in the clouds, etc. Free online play is available to all users regardless of platform. To process orders faster, it is necessary to distribute responsibilities. For example, one player does the slicing, the second fry, the third servers, the fourth serves, etc. Only a well-coordinated game will allow the restaurant to become number one in the whole world!Gameplay features: a huge amount of humor and exciting situations; different modes, including campaign, survival and training; cross-platform support; many recipes and culinary delights; individual and inclusive customization of colors, interface and filters.
