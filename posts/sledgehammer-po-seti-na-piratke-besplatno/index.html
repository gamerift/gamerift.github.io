Game Description#

Sledgehammer is literally a festival of destruction and speed that you can experience with a friend on a pirate. With the ability to play online, the user does not have to limit himself to single-player mode, because now you can destroy real opponents, demonstrating your superiority. Rounds of online battles take place on different routes, which have their own terrain features, scales and ways to take the enemy by surprise.

Sledgehammer’s single-player campaign consists of sequential segments, each taking place in a different setting. This could be a mountainous area, a highway crowded with ordinary drivers, city streets, forests and other places. As you progress, the user will gain access to more advanced and massive units, whose weapons will fully correspond to their dimensions. There are 6 chapters in total, and each subsequent chapter will demonstrate a completely new scale of destruction. As for multiplayer, the possibility of free online play allows you to organize battles with 8 participants. This can be either a race on a track with a start and finish, or a death match in a limited area. Depending on which car the gamer chooses for the fight, he can install various guns on it, including a rocket launcher. Multiplayer allows you to select tracks and arenas before the start of the battle. Therefore, you can test different assemblies and, based on past experience, choose the most suitable combinations.
