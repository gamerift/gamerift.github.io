Game Description#

Sally Face has a rather depressing but intriguing atmosphere with a predominant detective style of storytelling. The main character, Sully, lost his mother and is now moving to another city with his father. Soon after the move, a murder occurs. Now we need to understand a whole series of mystical and occult events. Game Features: An adventure game with an intriguing atmosphere of mysticism, occultism and slight absurdity. Lots of puzzles and riddles. For those who consider themselves Sherlock Holmes, there are hidden hints and details that simply cannot be found. Nostalgic drawing style, inspired by cartoons of the nineties of the last century. A deep and superbly developed plot, full of secrets, mysteries, mystical and inexplicable events and drama. Memorable characters and personal stories. Everything done by one single person.

Graphically, the game is made in the style of cartoons from the nineties of the last century and can cause a slight nagging nostalgia. The art is not angular, but beautiful enough for the player to develop warm feelings for all the main characters. The musical component is especially worth noting. It fits perfectly into the plot and atmosphere of the game, allows you to feel like every event and action, and sets you up for the right emotions. The theme of the main menu alone will stick in players’ memories for a long time.
