Game Description#

InSuperliminal you can play online online, alone or with a friend, exploring locations and interacting with objects using optical illusions. On pirate free, each player takes on a unique role in the gameplay. The gameplay of Superliminal is a first-person adventure. You need to take objects and change their size by changing the perspective, thereby moving further through the plot.

A single player campaign and multiplayer focused on co-op are available. The main character of the story is a participant in an experimental dream therapy program. He finds himself locked in the dream world. To break the repeating cycle, you need to solve all the puzzles along the way. The free online play feature invites participants to exchange items and constantly interact with the perspective of the environment.

Depending on the viewing angle, the environment or objects may change size. For example, when moving a cube from one point to another, it is necessary to change the viewing angle so that the object in your hands changes its size. This mechanic must be used when solving puzzles. With each subsequent level, the puzzles become more complex and require maximum attention from the players. New mechanics are also being introduced and there are more illusions related to space. Almost all objects on the location are available for interaction. They can be grabbed, carried, thrown, etc.
