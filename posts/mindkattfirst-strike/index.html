Game Description#

mindkatt: first strike represents a setting in which cats with developed intelligence use mechanical structures to interact in human society. This project puts players in a futuristic environment, where despotistical orders reign. These cats, having passed serious mental evolution, are in contact with the world through robotic devices. Their life in this difficult era, subject to people, forms the core of history.

In the general world, Mindkatt: First Strike, human groups are kept under the control of Mindkatt - a developed type of cats facing slavery. These evolving creatures become guns in a large -scale struggle for influence. Possessing unique natural talents, these creatures stubbornly resist the fate imposed by him, achieving freedom. This main confrontation sets the tone for the entire gaming process. The gameplay includes dynamic moments and tense combat clashes.

Gamers meet colorful characters while a deep storyline gradually unfolds due to their direct involvement. This starting episode introduces a wider universe. Further issues are designed to expand this story, offering an increasingly confusing adventure. This adventure launches a chain of events that is destined to surpass the initial performances. The developing plot seeks to challenge expectations. The denouement of this narrative will determine the final fate of the species of Mindsatt and all of humanity. This game promises an exciting development of events.
