Game Description#

Nitro Gen Omega represents the world where cars dominate the landscape after the lost war of mankind. Survivor people are huddled in cities erected on giant supports, so the mercenaries teams are forced to travel to dangerous wastelands. The player originates under his beginning to one of these detachments, based on a flying ship to complete tasks for the supply of resources and technologies. The task is to study this world, the leadership of a detachment and control of combat fur.

The battles in Nitro Gen Omega are held step by step and divide into two stages. In the planning phase, the gamer distributes orders to the crew of four pilots, where everyone is responsible for a separate node of fur: movement, weapons or maintenance. Then, the results of the actions of both parties are demonstrated in the phase of execution. This title offers high rates, because the pilots can be injured or die, and their fighting spirit affects behavior, which sometimes leads to unauthorized deeds in the heat of battle.

The fur is the main tool for survival, and it can be improved using the found parts to achieve a balance between protection, heating and ammunition. The project offers two different models of robots, which serve as the basis for various assemblies. A flying ship serves as a base where the crew lives, trains and builds a relationship that affects the gameplay. In this role -playing game, you can explore the continent, visit settlements to take tasks, and hire new fighters, each of whom has unique characteristics and goals.
