Game Description#

Contract Rush DX represents a 2D-world, built on the battles in the style of run and shoot and platform navigation. The player will have to choose from the list of characters to perform missions in manually drawn environment. The action mixes the shooting with the study, and cartoon aesthetics are combined with frank blood images during battles. The central gaming cycle requires to accept and execute contracts, which promotes deep into the numerous conflicts of the world. This structure draws a gamer through a series of focused action scenes.

The gameplay in the Contract Rush DX takes place on spacious locations designed for research and containing secrets and non -game characters. At these levels are amplifiers that expand capabilities, increasing the speed of movement, changing the shooting schemes or increasing rate of fire. Such improvements are crucial in large -scale collisions with bosses completing each main contract. The game also offers subcontracts-separate tests with a 3-star assessment system, tied to awards for execution under certain conditions.

This title offers a set of available heroes with various combat approaches. Some characters specialize in Shoot Um Up mechanics, using long -range weapons to eliminate the opposition at a distance. In contrast, others are concentrated on close -up attacks, demanding a different tactical interaction with opponents. The presence of these different styles allows you to strategically experiment when passing complex stages, since the abilities of a new hero can help overcome specific obstacles.
