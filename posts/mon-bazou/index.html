Game Description#

While repairing a car inMon Bazou, players have to deal with a variety of everyday problems that affect the life of the main character. The protagonist is a guy who dreams of turning his old car into a racing car that can compete with professional drivers and reach great speed. The game is made in the form of a simulator, where users have to take on the role of a novice racer who will live the everyday routine and do simple things, trying to earn a living.

The young man’s desire to build a career as a driver in Mont Bazou leads to many events, as well as tasks that need to be realized. Gamers will complete tasks aimed at increasing finances. You’ll have to deliver pizza, manage a sugar shack, chop wood and take part in competitive races. When you receive your first capital, you can invest it in a garage and the equipment necessary to repair your car. You have to purchase the necessary parts and components to create your dream car.

The events taking place are transferred to Canada, where you will have to drink maple syrup or extract it for sale. Players have complete freedom of action while exploring the open world. You have to develop the character’s skills, keeping him in good physical shape and giving him the opportunity to eat on time. You will be able to drive your own car, boat or ATV and perform various pranks to survive and improve the life of the protagonist.
