Game Description#

DayZ StandAlone is a multiplayer zombie survival game that offers the opportunity to play online with a friend, fighting for free on a pirate ship against a huge number of the living dead. Over the Internet, online players must collect resources and supplies in order to survive in the vast open world. The network game is designed for both co-op and confrontation. Events are developing in the post-Soviet country of Chernarussia.

All residents turned into bloodthirsty monsters. All participants start empty-handed. You need to find clothes, equipment and supplies to fight off not only zombies, but also other players who pose the maximum threat. The territory consists of 230 square kilometers of land, where many fields, forests, villages and towns are represented. The possibility of free online play is aimed at confronting participants for loot. Important resources are water, food and medicine. Clothing consists of several types that affect the hero’s speed and defense.

The arsenal of weapons consists of kitchen knives, machine guns and large-caliber guns. Modifications for guns consist of elements such as forends, stocks, bipods, sights, etc. The trunks in DayZ StandAlone can be painted in different colors. Secondary activities include hunting, fishing, growing crops, etc. Up to 60 players can fight on one map at the same time.

The main thing is to survive at any cost. You can make friends, unite in clans and start bloody feuds. You are allowed to rob, subjugate, threaten and do whatever comes into your head, surviving in a harsh post-apocalyptic world. A dynamic system for changing weather conditions and random generation of the location of enemies and various game elements are introduced.
