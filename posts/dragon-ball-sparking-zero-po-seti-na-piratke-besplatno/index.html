Game Description#

DRAGON BALL: Sparking! ZERO breathes new life into the Budokai Tenkaichi series, offering incredible battles with over 180 fighters from the DRAGON BALL universe. Now you can play online with a friend and immerse yourself in battles not only alone, but also online online, testing your skills in battles with real opponents. The game is available for free on the pirate market, which makes it attractive to those who want to enjoy all the features of the game without additional costs. This multiplayer game allows you to experience the unique abilities of characters ranging from Z to Super, GT and even movie characters.

The main feature of the game is the exciting 3D battles that will keep you on the edge. You’ll unleash beam attacks, rush attacks, and planet-shattering finishing moves faster than you can say Kamehameha. But it’s not just about pressing buttons. The combat system is thought out to the smallest detail and allows you to perform such fast techniques that even Goku will feel dizzy.

For fans of competitions, there are online battles in which you can test your strength. But if you’re more of a couch warrior, grab a buddy and take them on in the Hyperbolic Time Chamber. The game has a variety of tournament modes, each with its own victory conditions to spice up the game. And for history buffs, you can relive iconic storylines or create your own dream matches.

Speaking of stories, Sparking! ZERO lets you see scenes from the perspective of eight different characters. It’s like you’re sitting in the front row and seeing the world of DRAGON BALL with new eyes. And if that’s not enough, the game has a creation mode where you can create your own battles and share them with the world. This is a playground for DRAGON BALL fans, limited only by your imagination.
