Game Description#

GTA 5 has received dozens of awards for its open world, and almost the most expensive multi-platform project in history isGrand Theft Auto V. The story opens before us in the same fictional state - San Andreas, made 1 to 1 as Southern California. We play as three robber friends who have spent their entire adult lives stealing cars, robbing small shops and people on the street. One day someone decides to get involved in organized crime, which they have been fighting for the last 5 years. The plot picture is revealed in three characters at once. The story comes from them. We don’t see a special scenario before the meeting of all three repeat offenders, but after the meeting, real chaos opens up. Separately, I would like to talk about the capabilities of the game and its assets. Firstly, GTA 5 did a great job of implementing the underwater world. Characters can dive, snorkel, swim and attract girls with their bodies.

Secondly, the game has received many awards for its open world, and it really is. True, one cannot do without the characteristics of the characters. One of them slows down the driving time, but only after the action bar is filled. It fills up while he is driving at maximum speed in the oncoming lane and avoids crashing. The second one slows down time when shooting, but the scale only fills up after head hits and driving fast. The third gets “frenzy”, in which he takes less damage and deals more. Depends on the headshot. In the new world of the game you can swim, wait for the bus, have parties, pump up your car, play sports, trade shares on the stock exchange, buy and sell weapons and drugs, rob people, kill them and hide from the police. The plot side will also not leave you indifferent, you just need to choose it correctly.
