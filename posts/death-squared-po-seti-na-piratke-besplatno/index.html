Game Description#

InDeath Squared you can play online with a friend on a pirated game for free, jointly solving numerous puzzles online online. Players can either compete or try to solve inventive puzzles together. The online game is all about communication and interaction, and is full of exploding robots.

The gameplay of Death Squared is designed for both single play and offers the opportunity to take advantage of free online play for four players to compete both in teams and in free-for-all mode. In single-player mode, you will have to go through a set of different locations, the complexity of which increases with each new stage. The main goal is to guide your robot protagonist through the location, moving towards the final point.

As you might guess, this is not so easy to do. Various obstacles and dangers await you along the way. If you fall into a trap, instant death follows, so you should be as careful and prudent as possible. It is important to think one step ahead and be discerning. If you play in a team, then mutual understanding between the participants is necessary in order to come to the rescue on time. The gameplay encourages bold experiments and unconventional moves, generously rewarding risks.
