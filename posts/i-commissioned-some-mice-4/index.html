Game Description#

In the I Commissioned Some Mice 4, gamers have to find 750 hidden mice and 750 pieces of cheese scattered into 15 unique canvases. Each fantasy illustration is a search task, and all 15 levels are available from the very beginning of the game. Progress is monitored automatically: each found object after click is removed from the scene. The main goal is to systematically clear all the images from their hidden contents.

To help in passing I Commissioned Some Mice 4 offers an unlimited tint system for those who are stuck on a specific object. Navigation within each canvas is carried out using a mouse for moving and scaling, alternative keys on the keyboard are also available for these actions. The timer fixes the duration of the completion of each level, offering a personal performance indicator. In addition, the presence of 3 separate conservation cells allows you to pass the game repeatedly without rewriting existing achievements.

Each of 15 custom -made paintings has its own embossed sound, helping to plunge into the process of finding objects. To complicate the task, a unique function is provided that allows you to return a small number of detected objects to the image, creating a new challenge with fewer goals for the search. This mechanics increases the difficulty of finding the latter, accidentally selected elements. As an alternative, a complete reset of progress is available for everyone who wants to start the entire collection of tasks from the very beginning.
