Game Description#

Only Climb Better Together is a competitive platformer that you can play online with a friend on a pirate for free or compete online online with players from all over the world. The main goal is to ascend to the sky by jumping on platforms and overcoming various obstacles. Free online play includes co-op. You can split into teams or play for yourself. Accuracy is very important. Any wrong action can cost you victory. The athlete must be controlled from a third party. The basic mechanics involve running at high speed and jumping both long and high.

The challenge also lies in the fact that there are no saves and checkpoints. There is no second chance. If you jump over or fail to jump to the platform, the character falls into the abyss and is eliminated from the competition. The online game Only Climb Better Together includes several different modes and a huge number of challenges for the most experienced players. Multiplayer allows 8 players to compete for the title of champion at the same time. There are trampolines that allow you to quickly climb onto high platforms, trash cans that block paths, and much more. There are elevators, there is a dynamic change of day and night, which significantly affects the gameplay. Single player option available.
