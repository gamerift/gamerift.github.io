Game Description#

FreakOut: Extreme Freeride is a sports arcade game with simulation elements that you can play online with a friend for free on a pirate or random players from all over the world. The gameplay is inspired by the iconic Tony Hawk’s Pro Skater series. Single mode or online play invites you to conquer the ski slopes while skiing. FreakOut: Extreme Freeride requires you to perform a variety of stunts and compete in various multiplayer modes.

Finding yourself far from civilization and at an altitude of more than 2000 meters, you need to race along crazy tracks, conquering slopes and obstacles. The ability to play free online offers competition in different modes, including competitions for the maximum number of points or team championships. You can ride off-piste with friends, exploring terrain and overcoming obstacles. In freeride mode you will need to ride for fun and perform the craziest stunts.

There are 4 mountain landscapes available with their own individual characteristics. Each mountain has Special Events that allow you to get extra points and special prizes. The moveset is represented by 16 types of jumps. You can improve your skiing skills by choosing from over 30 pieces of equipment. There are 8 main routes and 6 additional routes. Each track has different challenges, such as collecting 5 dogs, slipping between a certain number of trees, etc. The campaign can be completed with 6 characters. Each hero is represented by unique chips and features.
