Game Description#

InAge of Empires 2 Definitive Edition you can play online online, fighting in large-scale battles with a friend or random players. Free on the pirate Age of Empires 2 Definitive Edition, you will have to choose one of the available factions to develop your city, form alliances and participate in wars. You can play online and go through the single player campaign.

The re-release of Age of Empires 2 includes improved textures, all released addons: The Forgotten, The African Kingdoms and Rise of the Rajas, the Last Khans expansion, 3 new campaigns: Tamerlane, Ivaylo, Kotyan Sutoevich) 4 additional factions: Bulgarians, Cumans, Lithuanians and Tatars. The possibility of free online play allows you to take control of one of 35 civilizations. Depending on your choice, you will receive unique types of buildings, individual types of units, vehicles and abilities.

A huge number of single-player campaigns, online play, quick matches, a scenario editor, a Royal Battle mode and a lot of other content are presented. Some mechanics have been improved or reworked. For example, the campaign for Eldorado gave way to the scenario for Pachacuti. You can choose several different AI for battles, use a new search algorithm, etc. It is now possible to place orders in a queue, farmers are replenished automatically, etc. You can go through many scenarios alone or together: Rulers of the West, Edward Longlegs, Gothville Dynasty, Dawn of the Princes, Olgerd and Keistut, etc. Depending on the campaign, new units are added: Burgundians, Sicilians, Bohemians, Poles and others.
