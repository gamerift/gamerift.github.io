Game Description#

Super Destronaut puts a player at the head of one warship, opposing built alien invaders built. Gamers control their horizontal axis vessel in the lower part of the screen, which is a design borrowed from the Arcadian shooters of the 1980s. The main task is to methodically disassemble the waves of opponents, descending along the specified trajectories, where each eliminated enemy replenishes the general account.

During clashes, defeated aliens can leave temporary amplifiers that directly increase the rate of fire or scatter of shells, providing a tactical advantage for cleaning the battlefield. The gameplay in Super Destronaut is structured by several formats for solitary passage. The classic regime offers arcade game experience, focused on the accumulation of points over an indefinite number of stages. The attack temporarily shifts the task of eliminating the maximum number of invaders in strict time frames.

The Hardcore option changes the rules for a more complex confrontation, including adjustments to the speed of opponents or the frequency of their attacks, checking the endurance and gamer reaction. This format represents an increased level of complexity for those who are looking for a serious test of their skills. In addition to single tests, the game includes a local multiplayer for 2 people in a competitive format. This mode allows direct competitions in the general arena. The task is shifted from survival against computer opponents to replay a living opponent.
