Game Description#

SuperFighter Deluxe is pure 2D chaos. On the pirated SuperFighter Deluxe, you can play online for free with a friend, fighting with opponents online online. Game mechanics combine action, fighting, shooter and platforming. There is a wide selection of weapons and a wide variety of gaming systems, allowing you to create real absurdity and chaos on the screen.

The network game is represented by several modes. Levels are generated randomly. Objects in locations can be moved, exploded, destroyed, set on fire, thrown, hidden behind them, etc. The weapon is knocked out of the enemy’s hands. The arsenal of weapons consists of bottles, knives, chains, pistols, flamethrowers, rocket launchers, chainsaws, etc. Free online play includes competition in modes such as Versus, Survival and Campaign.

Multiplayer competitions allow up to 8 players to participate simultaneously. The campaign can be played with friends or alone. Events take place in a retro-futuristic world of alternative reality. The campaign consists of three acts: Prime Time in Sunny City, Operation Sunrise and Escape From Precinct 9. At the beginning of the game, each participant customizes his fighter, choosing appearance and clothing options. The initial characteristics are also set. An extensive editor is available to create unique levels. C# is used, which allows you to write a Script API for your scripts.
