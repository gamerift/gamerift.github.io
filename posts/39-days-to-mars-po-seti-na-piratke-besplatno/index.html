Game Description#

39 Days to Mars is an arcade puzzle game aimed at playing together online. Local co-op available. On the pirate 39 Days to Mars, you can play online for free with a friend, immersing yourself in an alternative world of the 19th century. 39 Days to Mars is about two talented engineers who need to build a space shuttle. The setting is made in steampunk style.

The story centers on Sir Albert Weeks and the Honorable Clarence Baxter. Multiplayer allows you to combine the talents of two explorers to find a way to launch Her Majesty’s Terrifying Ship into the open air. It is necessary to build a steam engine by throwing coal. You will also have to study the navigation map and drink tea, while figuring out how best to make interstellar travel. Free online play in 39 Days to Mars allows each participant to demonstrate their engineering skills and demonstrate the highest level of deduction.

Co-op is available for two players. The puzzle can only be solved through interaction and mutual assistance. You can complete the story mode alone. The control of the second character will be taken over by artificial intelligence. The gameplay contains action and visual novel elements. It will take several hours of real time to complete the main campaign. The main task is to become the first in the world to see the Earth’s orbit!
