Game Description#

Elden Ring is an open world role-playing game from the creators of Dark Souls and Bloodborne, in collaboration with writer George R.R. Martin. The gameplay is a third-person action RPG where you explore the expanses of a fantasy world, equip yourself with a variety of weapons and fight powerful enemies. In the game, even one enemy poses a serious threat. Battles are based on timing and parries. Carefully study the monsters' patterns to effectively counterattack their attacks. The game features intense combat and hardcore difficulty. Several types of attacks are available, block and roll. Particular attention is paid to the character’s endurance.

Elden Ring places great emphasis on RPG mechanics. At the beginning of the game you can create your character. Go explore the open world either on your own or with the help of your trusty horse. The world is divided into areas, each with a unique layout, appearance, and enemies. Each area is represented by a powerful boss. By defeating the monster, you gain new abilities and access to previously closed locations. Unlike many RPGs, there are no peaceful NPCs or hub settlements to catch your breath or gear up. The world of Elden Ring is a place of pain and sadness. Here you will find only the ruins of ancient castles, many aggressive monsters and difficult trials that only the bravest traveler can pass. The visual side is represented by a gothic style and a dark fantasy setting.
