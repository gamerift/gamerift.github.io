Game Description#

Refind Self: The Personality Test Game is a unique interactive science fiction in which the gameplay allows you to explore the depths of the user personality. In this 2D-visual short story, you fall in the place of the Android and find yourself on the grave of the doctor that created you. From this moment on, your journey unfolds as you are exploring different locations associated with the previous doctor, meeting along the way with various robots. Each decision by you affects the course of the narrative, shedding light on your individual features and preferences.

Refind Self: The Personality Test Game is based on the fact that everyone plays in his own way, making a choice that reflects his personality. Whether it is the route that you choose, the characters you interact with, or how you react to key points, the project adapts to these elections to offer more personalized experience. This is a short but fascinating game that lasts about an hour, which makes it a great option for a quick breakdown from the routine.

There are several ways in Title to explore, and many characters that can be found, so with each passage the game constantly offers new ideas. As you are testing and making various decisions, you begin to reveal a more complete picture of the one you are, according to the analysis of the video game. A unfolding story is not only an action, but also thinking, since it encourages the user to think about his decisions and what they say about him.
