Game Description#

In Escape First 3 you need to team up with a friend, together trying to get out of the trap using an online Internet connection. On the pirate platform you can play online for free, solving puzzles cooperatively, or compete in the speed of solving puzzles. The project was developed for virtual reality helmets. Allows the use of devices such as Valve Index, HTC and Vive Oculus Rift.

Multiplayer includes 3 different campaigns.

You can team up to 6 people. Using the free online game, you need to solve various puzzles while trying to find a way out of the room. To do this, you need to interact with all interactive objects: move, study, disassemble, etc. In Versus mode, all participants are in single rooms, so the one who first finds a way out of the trap wins. Escape First 3 allows you to use the desktop version without using VR devices.

The teams consist of Abandoned School, Guardian of Lost Souls and Secret Place. The first location is an educational institution that has not been functioning for a long time, in the second case you need to explore the old fortress, looking for an ancient artifact, and in the third you need to visit the estate of a deceased relative, where he left a huge fortune. However, it is not easy to obtain a hidden inheritance.
