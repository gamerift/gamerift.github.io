Game Description#

New Arc Line is a game that invites gamers to venture into a city where the line between magic and technology is not just thin, it’s practically non-existent. Set in the sprawling steam-powered metropolis of Newark, it immerses players in a world where secrets lurk in every corner and power is as fickle as the magic and the machines that power it. The city is a place of contradictions: elegant but corrupt, vibrant but mired in inequality. This is a city that promises both hope and destruction in equal measure, depending on where you are.

From the moment gamers step into their character’s shoes in New Arc Line, they don’t just enter a new world; they enter into a narrative that will change with each choice they make. The story begins with a simple but desperate premise: you are an immigrant arriving in Newark, desperate to find a cure for the disease that is slowly killing your family. The city, with all its promise and technology, seems like the perfect place to find a solution.

But as they say, nothing is as it seems, and Newark is no exception. Beneath the city’s glittering exterior lies a labyrinth of corruption, greed and betrayal. Adventure allows you to forge your own path, and your choices matter. Every decision you make can change the course of your journey - who you ally with, what factions you support, and how you interact with the people you meet along the way. But the stakes are high.
