Game Description#

In Bee Island, you will become the head of a prosperous hive, taking on the role of a defender from impending threats. The main attention in the game is paid to defense and strategic construction. Enemies make sporadic attacks, and your task is to protect the hive. They are armed with bombs, poisonous aerosols and missiles, their strength is constantly growing. To reflect their attack, you have to build a whole complex of protective structures - ballists, machine guns, anti -aircraft missile systems and much more.

To ahead of the enemy’s offensive is the main thing. To maintain your advantage, take up the development of advanced technologies and innovative structures. At the same time, the most important choice arises - what technologies to give preference to achieve victory. The game puts a dichotomy before us: will your path lead to peaceful coexistence or to the complete destruction of the enemy? The prosperity of the hive depends on its inhabitants, so the queen needs a constant influx of workers and warriors. Create kindergartens, schools and military bases for growing new generations of bees. Their contribution will play an important role in the development of your hive. Resource management is the basis of game mechanics. In the presence of seven different types of resources, their effective distribution acquires paramount importance. To access certain resources, it is necessary to build factories. Collect honeycombs and strategically properly place the buildings on them to optimize the performance and effectiveness of the hive.
