Game Description#

Below The Stone is an exciting indie game that immerses in the depths of the mysterious kingdom. Taking the role of a brave gnome, the user will go to insidious searches for rare resources, reveal ancient secrets and create formidable equipment. However, it is worthwhile to beware of dangers that are in wait for as an adventurer descends further into the abyss. Covering more than 25 different biomes that differ in visual and design, the project offers a variety of enemies, treasures and dangers in each area.

Deadly beings, cunning traps, puzzles and formidable bosses in Below The Stone will check ingenuity and cunning. In addition, you need to stumble on precious stones that give magical improvements and can be connected together to create even more powerful versions. It is recommended to use diamonds to improve weapons and armor or cook potions that will provide invaluable help in travel. However, it is very important not to attach too much to your equipment, because the application is made in the sling genre, which means that death is a constant state. If the daredevil perishes, then his property will be irrevocably lost, and you will have to start a new expedition with a new gnome. Fortunately, there is an opportunity to maintain part of the equipment in the kingdom.
