Game Description#

The Universe in Lord Ambermaze succumbed to a terrible curse that stopped the normal course of time. The main character is a guy named Zin, who owns an ancient magic relic under the name “sword of movement.” Thanks to the enchanted weapon, the protagonist gets the opportunity to move freely around the world and resist the dangers that the Lord Ambermase prepares. Users will have to save the kingdom and the princess, which is in the imprisonment of the criminal.

A long journey to the Lord Ambermeze allows gamers:

Fight against ordinary rivals and bosses of locations on special tactical arenas. To participate in step -by -step battles, where, when moving the protagonist, enemies will move. Apply tricks and unusual skills to eliminate monsters living in dungeons. To study different rooms filled with deadly traps and valuable treasures.

During the passage of levels in the plot driving, players will move through the field divided into cells with a view of the top. Users will have to rely on Zina’s skills, building a strategy to eliminate rivals. It is necessary to take into account the area, drive monsters on traps or apply blows to them with a sword. It will also be possible to select scattered bonus coins and apples that increase health to the hero. For users, the main task is to clean the card from enemy creatures to move to the next level.
