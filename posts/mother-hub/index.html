Game Description#

Mother Hub - this is not a typical shooter. This game throws you headlong into a hopeless situation - humanity on the edge of the abyss, and only you keep the reins of government. Each of your solution, each path you have chosen can become the difference between survival and oblivion. Fasten, because here the choice has consequences. Here it is not just that you need to press the trigger. Of course, there is an action here, but it is deftly woven into a strategic tapestry. You will have to think, assess the situation and interact with the outside world. Behind every turn you can fire a hint, a hidden path or an unpleasant surprise.

The conversations here are not weak - the system of dialogs is the key to the unraveling of the secrets buried in the depths of this world. The words you have chosen can persuade the scales of the scales, so speak carefully, because your language may be stronger than the sword. Forget about the straight line. Mother Hub encourages research. Curiosity is your friend. Go off the beaten path, look into dusty corners, and perhaps you will come across something fateful. The thing is to collect a puzzle, one mysterious message, one dusty terminal at a time.

The more you dig, the deeper the story unfolds, revealing the truth about this abandoned world and the fate that humanity is waiting for. So are you ready to accept the call? Adventure is not for the faint of heart. She will make you think, sweat and, perhaps, will not even let you sleep at night, wondering what had to be done differently. But this is the whole high of the game, right?
