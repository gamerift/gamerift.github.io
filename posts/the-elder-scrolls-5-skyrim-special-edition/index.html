Game Description#

Skyrim has become one of the favorite games on two continents of our planet. The first/third person slasher captivated players not only with its open-world RPG content, but also with its graphics, sounds, characters, features, and, of course, dragons.

Our character inThe Elder Scrolls V: Skyrim Special Edition begins the same story, but you immediately notice that everything around him is completely different. If in the original version of the game we move along a snowy road, now the colors of the autumn forest prevail, maintaining the same atmosphere. The mods that we will encounter in the add-on are graphics, a few new weapons, a few new quests that do not affect the official plot in any way, as well as the picture that makes everyone buy this add-on on a well-known site - a new race. You will find out about it yourself.

The rest remained in place. You create the hero in the editor, which is slightly improved with the addition of face modification elements. The first missions immerse you in the atmosphere of a new mod that changes the glare of the water and makes the leaves spin before your eyes. Stones, grass, sword - everything looks completely different. Crafting and upgrading items remains the same, which is a shame. But there were more items or loot began to drop out more often, it is unknown. Definitely, the undead, skeletons and monsters on your way will pay more attention to you. The most important reason to play Skyrim with the add-on is Russian professional voice acting, and not auto-translation.
