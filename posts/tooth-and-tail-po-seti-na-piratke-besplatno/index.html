Game Description#

On the pirate gameTooth And Tail you have the opportunity to compete with a partner online online, or play the pirate game online for free with a friend in co-op. A local mode has been introduced, allowing you to play on one device. The network game is represented by short multiplayer sessions of 10-15 minutes. Maps are generated randomly. Full faction customization is available, allowing you to customize your heroes individually.

In the world of Tooth And Tail, there is a civil war between four factions. The possibility of free online play allows you to fight for any of them: Longcloaks, Common Folk, CSR and Civilized. Each side is represented by unique tactics and different units, including flamethrower boars, toxic skunks, landing owls, etc. A solo mode with dynamically increasing difficulty is introduced. Tasks are divided into main and additional. Secondary goals are called heroic. The confrontation can accommodate up to four players at the same time. You can fight in deathmatch mode or split into two teams of 2 participants.

Each player selects a faction and six units of warriors from the presented 20. 15 units can attack, and the remaining 5 act as defenders. Heroes are divided by rank. Depending on the elitism of the fighter, his cost increases. The mechanics are based on building a base, searching for resources, etc. It is necessary to install farms, mills, camp fires and other buildings. Food acts as an important resource. To win the battle, you need to destroy all the mills and fires of your opponent.
