Game Description#

Necro Story provides users with a unique opportunity to step into the shoes of a slightly evil necromancer. The title combines elements of humor and the conventions of traditional role-playing games, inviting gamers into a world where the undead and dark magic are part of everyday life. The narrative unfolds with the help of a ghostly white magician who brings playfulness to the often dark theme of necromancy. Together they embark on a journey through the underworld to save humanity from an impending threat.

This setting sets the stage for the varied adventures and interactions that characterize the gameplay. Users are immersed in a system that includes more than 20 different necromancy spells. Each spell belongs to a specific school of dark magic, which provides a rich selection of possibilities for study. The depth of the skill tree encourages gamers to develop their character in unique ways. As you progress, choice becomes paramount, shaping your experience and allowing for a variety of styles.

Customization in Necro Story is a key feature, allowing users to create their own versions of powerful demons using Dark Pacts. This flexibility ensures that no two players will approach the adventure game in the same way. Team composition adds another layer of strategy. Gamers can create a party of up to six characters, including the ability to summon additional skeletons and zombies. Thus, during battle you can command a small army. Up to nine characters can participate here.
