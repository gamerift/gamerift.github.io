Game Description#

AdventureTimemelters invites you to go with a friend or alone on an action-packed adventure where you can slow down or rewind time using the Time Shift ability. You can play online for free, fighting on a pirate ship with a friend against a huge number of enemies and completing many joint quests. Online Timemelters gives you the opportunity to go through a story campaign with a companion. The gameplay mixes action and strategy mechanics with role-playing elements. There are levels to protect the settlement, turning the gameplay into a real tower defense. At the center of the story is the witch Tegan, who embarks on the warpath against universal evil.

A story mode, online play and various challenges are available to test your skill. A skill tree with different leveling branches is presented. Each participant can customize their character depending on their playing style. For example, you can enhance your magic to deliver devastating attacks from a long distance. The possibility of free online play allows you to go through 20 unique maps together to protect your own base. In addition there are a variety of puzzles, boss battles, survival missions and much more. The story campaign and tests always offer you to complete the most varied quests. All tasks introduce new mechanics to the gameplay. You will need to solve more than 40 exciting puzzles related to interaction both with the environment and with your partner.
