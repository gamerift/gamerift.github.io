Game Description#

Secrets of Grindea is a 2D adventure that allows you to team up with a friend or up to four people to experience the epic storyline of a fantasy tale as a pirate for free. You can play online using an online Internet connection. Each participant takes on the role of one of the party’s protagonists. All heroes have unique abilities and fulfill their role in the journey.

Not only is online play available, but it is also possible to go through the campaign alone with bots. The difficulty of battles increases gradually. The stronger the heroes become, the more powerful types of enemies they encounter along the way. All enemies have unique patterns of movements and attacks, but using the opportunity of free online play, you can, together with your comrades, develop a unique strategy of action, crushing everyone who dares to challenge the protagonists.

At the beginning, you are allowed to create your own personal hero. Secrets of Grindea features an extensive class system that allows you to fully control and develop your character’s abilities. Who will it be? An ice mage or a powerful warrior with an axe? Haircuts, hats, clothes, accessories and any other element of the protagonist are subject to customization. The world of Grindea is filled with dangers and secrets, as well as both main and secondary quests.

The environment is generated randomly. A pet system has been introduced to ensure no one is left alone. But first you need to catch and tame the animal. It will accompany you throughout the entire journey, and will also be upgraded along with its owner. By collecting resources, you need to help build the city of Arcadia, returning it to its former greatness.
