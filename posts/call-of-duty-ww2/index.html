Game Description#

The events of the new part of Call of Duty: Wwii unfold during the Second World War. The developers again returned to the origins, adding to the old plot campaigns the schedule of our time, the gameplay of the last parts and the voice acting from the professional studio within the company. The basis of the plot is a landing in Normandy. The narrative of the European Theater of Acts of the Second World War begins with it. All Call of Duty: Wwii will be deployed inside Europe: not a step towards the USSR or East. We will play for the French, English, Americans and rebels among the Poles. The game campaign is saturated not only with large -scale shooting, the sounds of real battle and a lot of blood, but by betrayal, killing friends, and the change of command.

The game also offers you to go through the whole plot in one player mode, a multi -user and joint game. If the “one player” must completely plunge into the events of the Second World War, then the cooperative part of the first -person shooter demonstrates separate missions, where artificial intelligence projects a task for two people. The weapon of the game is fully consistent with that time. It is a pity, but the developers have not yet added effects such as “weapons froze” or “jammed”. Take it, shoot, kill, hold a position and you will be happy. The joint regime and multi -user missions open before us open battlefields for the battles of 30x30 (and more), and the scenarios of some tasks go beyond the scope of the shooter, turning the game into the horror.

Call of Duty Wwii - Definitely, the best shooter on the World War II. The protagonist of a single campaign is Ronald Red Daniels, a soldier of the American army. Together with him, the gamer will follow several historical episodes, starting with landing in Normandy. The gameplay is presented in the form of a first-person shooter, with a historically reliable presentation of the history of 1944-1945, when Hitler’s troops began to retreat under the onslaught of allies and the Cash army, authentic military equipment and weapons. A single story is designed for 6-8 hours, several levels of complexity are presented. You can also play in multiplayer mode. The latter offers several options at once. For example, the Zombie is a cooperative fictional scenario, according to which the Nazis invented a way to revive the dead that users will have to fight. Classic multiplayer battles are also available.
