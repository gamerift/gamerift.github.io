Game Description#

Trine 4 The Nightmare Prince is a platformer that allows you to play online with a friend, joining forces on a pirate and using all online functions for free. You can gather in a group of up to three people. An online Internet connection is presented, as well as local co-op on a shared screen. The perspective is made in 2.5D style. The gameplay is a dynamic arcade game with puzzle elements, where you have to go through fairy-tale locations, saving the world from the nightmares of the Prince.

At the center of the story are heroes familiar from previous parts. The free online play option allows participants to take on the role of the wizard Amadeus, the knight Pontius and the thief Zoe. The protagonists of Train 4 need to find Prince Selius, who has become a victim of dark dreams that are wreaking havoc in the kingdom. His dreams send dangerous creatures to people and paint surreal pictures, mixing reality with the fictional world. A network game is based on the interaction of players, as well as with the surrounding interactive environment.

You need to cover your partners, solve puzzles together by activating mechanisms, helping a friend, pushing blocks and much more. Locations Trine 4 The Nightmare Prince will take you through gloomy ruins and haunted tombs, allowing you to visit a tranquil birch grove and blueberry forests. Puzzles are tied to elementals. For example, there are riddles related to fire, air, light, magnet, electricity, etc. Each hero has his own unique skills and is represented by an individual ability tree. Together we must fight back against dark creatures and powerful bosses.
