Game Description#

Tiny Bunny is a black and white visual novel containing a considerable amount of horror and sappress. Get lost in the forest, in no case can you hear his voice, otherwise death will follow. The events of the game occur in winter. Something follows you and constantly walks between the trees. There are rumors about constant screams from the forest, and the board with the losses of children is replenished each time. If you hear his voice, then no one can help.

The plot Tiny Bunny will make a moral choice: to survive or remain a person? The mysterious creature was nicknamed the “bunny.” The game was based on the novel of the same name by Dmitry Mordas. The Quiet-Mark village begins to suffer from the fact that children began to constantly disappear. In the center of history is a sixth grader who recently moved with his parents.

By chance, he witnessed terrifying events. The short story is divided into five full -fledged episodes, tells the history of the soul. Stylized the game under the old black and white ribbons. The soundtrack of the game emphasizes the horror and despair of what is happening. On your way, there are many puzzles and puzzles. If you are ready to plunge into an anxious and oppressive atmosphere of adventure, then incredible plot turns await you that will not leave anyone indifferent.
