Game Description#

In addition to the ability to play online with a friend on a pirate, Mars Colony: Challenger immerses gamers in the atmosphere of deep space. Events take place on a space station, which in turn is based on the surface of Mars. Here, players will have to provide themselves with conditions for a comfortable existence by monitoring the condition of equipment, monitoring and growing new samples of food, and also coping with difficulties that arise along the way. In online co-op mode, the chances of survival increase significantly, so the process will definitely be exciting.

The base itself in Mars Colony: Challenger consists of several closed rooms located at a short distance from each other. Each of these rooms has its own designated items and gadgets, so if necessary, gamers will need to repeatedly run from one room to another. Some devices, such as generators, can be installed in open areas, but they must first be connected to power sources in order to operate. During the entire process, the vital signs of the characters are displayed in a separate column, due to which you can monitor your health status. As for the challenges, you have to face them when completing missions. Each mission, in turn, has 7 stages, involving the execution of various orders. The possibility of free online play provides the option of co-op, so even if the next quest turns out to be too difficult, you can figure it out by working together. In addition, online play allows you to set up communication properly.
