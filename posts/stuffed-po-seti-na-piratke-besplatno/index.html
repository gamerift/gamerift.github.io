Game Description#

You can play the pirateStuffed online for free, fighting with a friend or random players online online. Gameplay Stuffed is a first-person shooter game. Events take place in the dreams of a little girl named Ellie. The online game allows you to take control of a teddy bear, protecting the girl’s world from her nightmares. The enemies are the heroine’s toys. Among them are toy gnomes, animals, soldiers, dolls, etc. Shadows are especially dangerous.

The free online gaming feature focuses on co-op and competition. Up to 4 people can take part. You can fight among yourself, divide into teams, or perform in co-op. You need to fight off waves of opponents, earning victory points. With each new wave the enemies become stronger. Levels are generated randomly. Accumulated points can be spent on improvements and pumping, as well as on opening new cards.

You will need to fight powerful bosses. Each leader has an increased health reserve. Fights with them take place at the end of the night. The arsenal of weapons consists of popcorn grenades, cola cannons and other unique guns created from everyday items. Victory points allow you to unlock new outfits in the form of fur coats for bears. Points are awarded after each successfully cleared wave. Cosmetic items provide the opportunity to individually customize your hero.
