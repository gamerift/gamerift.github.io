Game Description#

In Coal LLC, the player is engaged in the extraction of constantly growing coal volumes to perform a daily quota, and the failure leads to the end of the passage. The activity begins with one Old Kirki, forcing to descend deep underground to achieve production goals before dark. The underground world is updated daily, offering a new location of resources requiring quick development. As the company’s industrial activity expands, the environment begins to demonstrate signs of decline.

The staff of employees in Coal LLC can be increased from a small team to millions and, ultimately, billions of workers. These people are hired and raised to specialized posts in the structure of the organization, each with its own functions. A specific profession opens up special game bonuses and gives access to a variety of equipment that changes the strategy of action. Such a system helps to turn the enterprise into a giant industrial complex, entirely focused on the extraction of coal for factories on the surface.

A variety of maps, many professions and a large selection of tools provide numerous ways for the development of the company. The bowels of the Earth are generated every day again, so another attempt requires adaptation to the new layout and its features. The peace regime in this game removes the pressure of the daily norm and the threat of termination of activity. Such an alternative allows the process of development and growth of the enterprise to continue without the constant risk of failure.
