Game Description#

Space Hulk Deathwing Enhanced Edition is a first-person shooter that you can play online with a friend on a pirate, competing online for free. The plot and setting are based on the board game of the same name, Space Hulk: Deathwing. A single-player campaign and online play are presented, allowing you to confront enemy factions together and go through the storyline together. 8 multiplayer maps available.

At the center of the story are space marines from the first company of the Dark Angels Chapter, called the Deathwing. The main task is to get on board Olefurs’s spaceship, where the shuttle Will of Caliban is located. It contains the secrets of the Horus Heresy Order. The campaign consists of 9 unique levels. Co-op mode allows up to 4 people to team up.

There are 5 classes to choose from. Each Space Hulk Deathwing Enhanced Edition hero has his own unique abilities. The possibility of free online play allows you to take control of the Apothecary Nahum, the Librarian Epistolary and the Terminator Barachiel. All heroes have three development branches that determine the style of play. If you play with bots, you are allowed to give orders: go, stop, follow me and heal.

Characters are able to hack doors, locks, install turrets, etc. Allows you to teleport, restore health and replenish supplies. Space Marines use Terminator power armor and powerful weapons. You will have to fight against the Tyranids: gene-stealer hybrids, evolved hybrids, etc. Each level contains up to 5 collectibles.
