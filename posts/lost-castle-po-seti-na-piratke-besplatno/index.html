Game Description#

Lost Castle is an action roguelike for playing with a friend or for completing a campaign online online with up to four people. You can play for free online or on one computer. Dungeons, events, enemies and many other game features are randomly generated. All characters, weapons, spells and armor are unlocked as you progress. The gameplay is distinguished by its hardcore complexity, so the online game provides a real challenge not only to one player, but also challenges the entire company at once.

Events take place in the fantasy lands of Harwood. Once upon a time this place was a kingdom of prosperity and peace, but disaster overtook the inhabitants - the king’s castle was under the influence of evil spells. Now this place is infested with demons, undead and evil spirits. If the corruption is not destroyed, it will begin to spread throughout Harwood, so the possibility of free online play allows the heroes to join forces to fight back evil and return the kingdom to its former greatness. Key features of Lost Castle: an exciting adventure for four players; co-op adventure and PvP mode; unique challenges for each fighter; randomization and random generation of enemies, bosses and loot; hardcore difficulty; 100 items, 60 potions and a lot of auxiliary equipment ;190 types of weapons and 60 types of armor;development of skills and abilities;collection of souls and the ability to exchange them.

Souls must be collected in order to exchange them for new skills after death. They help you become stronger, or develop your character along a certain branch, putting together your own hero build. The action is built on the principle of old-school arcade games from the kill-em-all genre. Each class has individual abilities and attack patterns. All team members perform their own special function, allowing a group of fighters to create a unique combination of attacks.
