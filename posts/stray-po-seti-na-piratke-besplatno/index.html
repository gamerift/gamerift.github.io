Game Description#

InStray you can play online for free with a friend on a pirate ship, controlling red cats and overcoming numerous obstacles along the way. Over the Internet, online players can go through the story campaign together. Co-op mode is activated using one button in the main menu. The second participant controls another cat in split-screen mode, but only the host owner can see the HUD. The online game allows you to climb together on the roofs of a futuristic metropolis, solve puzzles together and interact with the androids of the city.

The gameplay is a third-person adventure that requires you to explore the post-apocalyptic locations of a cyberpunk city. The red-haired hero is able to jump onto beams, ledges and balconies. The cat can also “meow”. There is a separate button for this ability. Some items are allowed to scratch, such as carpets or sofas. Each location has unique spatial puzzles aimed at interacting with the environment. The B-12 drone travels around the world with the redhead. He helps scout the area and translates the androids' speech into cat speech. Certain segments contain enemies. They can be defeated using the environment or simply escaped using the verticality of the levels. Content, mechanics and features of Stray: Possibility of free online play; A unique main character, who is a cute fluffy; The atmosphere of a real cyberpunk adventure; Simple and casual controls; Charismatic robot characters living in a post-apocalyptic city; Inventive puzzles and puzzles , found at levels.
