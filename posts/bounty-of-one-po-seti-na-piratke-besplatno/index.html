Game Description#

Bounty Of One is a fast-paced top-down shooter that allows you to play as a pirate for free online with a friend or cooperate with random players both online and locally. The action takes place in the Wild West. There is a big reward for the hero, so absolutely everyone is trying to get the protagonist’s head. The online game offers how to join forces to shoot endless hordes of opponents. Hunters from across the region seek to kill every member of the outlawed party. The possibility of free online play in Bounty Of One unites up to 4 participants who will need to confront absolutely everything alive in the Wild West.

Experience points are awarded for destroying enemies. At their expense, you can improve skills that allow you to more effectively destroy enemies and increase the protagonist’s survival skill. There are ordinary opponents, and you also have to fight powerful bosses. Each new launch of the game is different from the previous one due to random generation. It is necessary to open a wide arsenal of weapons, consisting of a wide variety of guns, including exotic barrels. Bosses drop rare loot, allowing you to significantly improve your hero’s skills. There are 30 unique items available for collection. At the start, you are given 2 unique characters to choose from: Serra - an experienced shooter and Nigel - an old sheriff. As you progress, you can unlock 3 more characters.
