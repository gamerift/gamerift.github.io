Game Description#

Delta Force: Land Warrior is a tactical shooter that you can play online for free on a pirate, fighting with a friend or teammates online against terrorists. The online game features cooperative missions and modes such as Mortal Kombat, King of the Hill and Capture the Flag. The ability to play Delta Force 3 for free online allows up to 4 people to join forces. Protagonists of Delta Force: Land Warrior: Cole Gas Tank Harris is a talented demolitionist; Erica Mako Swift is a specialist in underwater explosions; Rydel Pitbull Wilson is a strong machine gunner; Jenn Snakebite Tanaka is a master of close-quarters confrontations; Daniel Crossbow Lonetree is an experienced sniper.

Each fighter in Delta Force: Operation Special Forces is a pro in his field and fights with the help of unique weapons. There are six weapon slots available in Delta Force 3. The arsenal includes automatic weapons (M4, G11, Steyr AUG, AK-47, APS), machine guns (M249 SAW, FN MAG), sniper rifles (M82A1, M40, PSG-1) and other powerful and huge weapons, such as Jackhammer, MM- 1 or OICW.

Battles take place in spacious locations, thereby providing a wide choice of tactical actions. Before starting a level, a detailed briefing is provided where you can familiarize yourself with the goals and objectives. Missions consist of sabotage, sabotage, capture, liberation, destruction, etc. The campaign consists of 19 unique missions. The protagonists must destroy the terrorist organization New Dawn and its accomplices in the person of the Armed Popular Front.
