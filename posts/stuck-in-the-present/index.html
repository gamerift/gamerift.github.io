Game Description#

Stuck in the Present sends two friends, Fei and Rich, a trap of surreal corridors of the dilapidated metro system. This underground labyrinth under a large unfamiliar city becomes the physical embodiment of their common painful past. Forgotten memories come to the surface, distorting the environment and creating a terrible reflection of the internal confusion of the heroes. To search for a way out, the couple will have to face a depressed story, because the escape depends on the clue of past events.

The Stuck in the Present gameplay is focused on the study of life -based memories materialized directly at the station. Gamer controls Fei, helping her discover significant objects and people who keep the keys to her history. The main gaming mechanics consists in sketching finds that allows you to document and comprehend key points to restore events. Interaction with the environment and things reveals their connection with the experience of the heroine and surrealistic changes in the location.

The plot develops through the relationship of Fei and Rich and Rich, faced with hidden injuries and recent disagreements. The player’s decisions directly form the dynamics between the characters, determining their communication and how the game will end. Fay anxiety becomes a tangible force: the growing emotional distance with Rich enhances the danger around. The choice made directs the heroes to one of several finals reflecting their path through the past.
