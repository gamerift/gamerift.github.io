Game Description#

Lords of the Fallen, a dark fantasy action RPG, lures players into a vast, interconnected world, offering an adventure on a larger scale than its predecessor and the ability to play online with a friend on a pirate ship for free. After a long era of ruthless tyranny, the demonic entity known as Adyr, once defeated, has awakened once again. Eons have passed, and the ghost of Adyr’s resurrection still haunts him. As one of the famous Dark Crusaders, you will journey through the realms of the living and the dead in this large-scale online role-playing odyssey with the possibility of free online play. Get ready for colossal boss battles, intense combat scenarios, exciting character interactions, and a gripping narrative. The choice is yours: will your legend be light or dark?

Your path will run through two vast, parallel worlds in which you will strive to overthrow Adyr. The living kingdom is replete with cruel trials, but it is in the nightmarish kingdom of the dead that unspeakable horrors await you. Create a character with a unique appearance from a variety of visual options and choose from nine character classes. Your path may vary, but you can tailor your character’s development to suit your playstyle by upgrading your stats, weapons, armor, and spells. Survival depends on your skill in deep and tactical combat. Choose from a variety of brutal and effective weapons, or delve into the arcane arts to create devastating attacks. You can play through the extensive single-player campaign alone or team up with a fellow player for seamless online interaction. Be prepared for the fact that heroes from other worlds may come knocking at the door of the game. Your lantern can travel between worlds, opening gates to forgotten places, legendary treasures, and the very essence of your opponents. To fall in the kingdom of the living to rise again in the kingdom of the dead. This is your last chance to regain your previous state, even if infernal creatures have attacked you.
