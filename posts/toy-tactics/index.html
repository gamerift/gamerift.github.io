Game Description#

Toy Tactics is a colorful action strategy game that will take you into the world of exciting strategies and physics experiments. Here you will become the main general, tasked with controlling little toy soldiers and defeating the enemy. You will have to place units on the battlefield and create various formations to guide your toy army towards victory. But don’t forget that physics plays an important role here! When the toy soldiers collide with each other, their helmets will fly in all directions, just like in a cartoon. So be careful and remember to take this factor into account in your tactical decisions.

Fight unusual enemies such as skeletal dinosaurs, evil necromancers and cunning samurai. In this game you will find yourself in different territories and will be able to use your imagination to win battles. You can freeze the game and draw a formation to surround the enemy, attack them, or even chase them. You are given complete freedom of action on the battlefield, and you can choose any path to victory. The game has many opportunities to improve your army, for example, you can use artifacts and various spells to destroy enemies. Defeat your opponents to gain extra power and call for reinforcements, build medical tents to heal wounded soldiers, or call down meteor showers on your enemies' heads.
