Game Description#

InWild Bastards you’ll find yourself in the captain’s chair of a ragtag spaceship filled with the galaxy’s most notorious criminals. Think space pirates with a penchant for trouble. This isn’t your grandpa’s space opera, but a roguelike shooter where every decision can mean the difference between exploding into stardom or becoming cosmic dust. The missions are spread across a series of tactical campaigns, each of which is an adventure. You will need to manage a team of criminals - a ragtag squad with 13 unique personalities and skills.

Each team member brings something different to the table, so don your strategy hat. It’s all about choosing the right rogue and causing chaos with his special abilities. Wild Bastards brings a twist to the game in the form of mod management. Think about how you can decorate your criminals with cool gadgets and upgrades. This adds another layer of challenge, forcing you to think on your feet and adapt your tactics on the fly.

Every meeting feels fresh; the world itself is procedurally generated, which means no two playthroughs are the same. Just when you think you have the situation figured out, the title throws you a curve ball, leaving you guessing and your finger itching. So if you’re looking for a thrilling ride that combines strategy and thrilling action, this is it. Just remember that these bandits are savage for a reason - they may cause you more trouble than you expected. Buckle up, spaceship captain, it’s going to be a wild ride.
