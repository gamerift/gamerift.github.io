Game Description#

Builders of Egypt is a urban planning simulator in which the player acts as a manager of a great country located in the Nile River Valley. The huge rate is made on the economic development of the state, for this a large number of mechanics have been added, complicating the process of state development. The gamer will see the history of the origin of the Egyptian state in the proto -the -stage period, the golden age, ending with the death of Cleopatra Seventh. This project uses events that took place in ancient times and has historical reliability.

Opportunities of the game “Builders of Egypt”:

Religion Management - developing temples and erecting new places of worship, people will spiritually relax and restore the mental balance. With a small number of investments in this development branch, people will arrange riots that will lead to financial losses. Trade - with its help, a budget is drawn up, which will need to exist and constantly improve the territory. Product prices are dynamically changing due to the geopolitical situation in the world. Military operations - you must not forget about the army, because sometimes enemies will take place, or Pharaoh will want to collect the army from all areas for campaigns in distant lands for the purpose of capture. Diplomatic relations between different cultures connecting in a huge city, which builds a gamer, with the help of representatives of different countries, must be regulated and not allowed to develop conflicts on racial grounds.
