Game Description#

On the pirate version ofMedal of Honor: Warfighter you can play online for free with a friend, participating in various cooperative competitions or in online confrontations over the Internet. Medal of Valor: The Fighter reboots the franchise. The plot tells about a Tier 1 special forces squad. They must stop the spread of PETN explosives. A huge emphasis is placed on the personal stories of the fighters. The story focuses on their relationships within the team, with their wives and children.

The online game offers you to fight for several units: SAS from England, SASR from Australia, KSK from Germany, Alpha squad from Russia and GROM from Poland. Each faction has its own unique abilities and uses individual battle tactics. The possibility of free online play allows up to 12 operatives to fight simultaneously. Players compete as two teams in various multiplayer modes. Medal of Honor: Warfighter is represented by several classes of fighters, including sniper, machine gunner and attack aircraft.

By teaming up in special fireteams, participants can see the locations of partners through walls, which increases the dynamics of gameplay and allows for better interaction with partners. A single-player campaign is available, in which heroes from past games have returned, such as Mom, Monk, Voodoo, Stump and other characters. Interaction with the environment has been improved, and the maps themselves are distinguished by their interactivity: stacks of paper fly apart, plaster crumbles when shot, chandeliers repent, beams collapse, etc.
