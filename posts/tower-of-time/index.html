Game Description#

At sunset, artara gradually open up new horizons of another world. Thousands of pages of books, manuscripts and video stations tell us the plot of Tower of Time through the prism of distant views on the plot from the game character. The new system in the RPG game is revealed from the first minutes of gameplay. We create a unique character who does not know what is happening in his world. More than a thousand objects around it determine the fate of the development of abilities and feather, and our combat detachment increases its skills with us, relying only on the strength and firmness of its leader.

You can collect unique heroes and break through with them after 60 hours of gameplay on closed locations. The features on the map will be protrusions, perfectly drawn levels that the developers made by hand. Also, the key factor will be the effect of slowing the time at the moment of which you can highlight the area of ​​the detachment attack, plan your actions and apply abilities.

The plot develops in linear and in a simple way. A series of terrible cataclysms occurred in the world, due to which the entire territory of Artara was immersed in a fire that controls dangerous monsters. The main character remembered the tower, which always seemed to him the main place in the world. He goes there, and his journey lasts 50+ hours of gameplay. The development of the characters in Tower of Time does not occur by gaining experience, but in accordance with the dropped or purchased magic scrolls. At the beginning of the game, you choose one of the 7 classes, and then study its spell tree. Around you, the city and locations that can be improved from improvised means. Roughly speaking, your hero brings only good and light into the world, so good memories and pleased NPCs remain behind him.
