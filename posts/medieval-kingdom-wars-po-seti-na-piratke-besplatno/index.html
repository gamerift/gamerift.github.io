Game Description#

Medieval Kingdom Wars can be classified as a global strategy game. In addition to the single, this project also has multiplayer, which is available online online. You can play this pirate game online with a friend. The project uses a medieval setting, and the action takes place in Western Europe. The title takes users to 1336 - the era of the Hundred Years' War.

Single-player content in Medieval Kingdom Wars consists of 9 scenarios and a great campaign. And the possibility of free online play includes co-op and battles between users. In the single, the user begins his journey in the role of a nobleman who is in the service of the king. There is high replayability due to 14 states and 50 lords to choose from. Initially, the gamer owns several villages. As a vassal, he helps the ruler expand his domain. You can make or break relationships with the sovereign and representatives of the nobility. If desired, they are allowed to weave palace intrigues in order to turn the nobility against a specific person or organize a coup and try to sit on the throne. At the same time, bots can also rebel and overthrow the new ruler.

In addition to diplomacy and politics, there are battles and construction. Both on the global map and on the battlefields, everything happens in real time. The indie title has 30 units, including siege equipment. You need to manage your students in real time. Network play allows you to chat with other users. The social function is available even while completing the campaign. There is also a world editor and mod support. There are also 4 advisers in the game: Chancellor - deals with diplomacy and helps conclude truces, declare wars, sign trade agreements; Seneschal - is responsible for research that can improve the economy, army, and the lives of ordinary people; Marshal - shows global goals, and also gives reports on all battles and the state of the troops; Clerk - describes the events that recently occurred in the virtual world.
