Game Description#

God Mode is a cooperative shooter and action game that allows you to play with a friend online (including on a pirate). Gamers can jointly destroy monsters online online. The project uses a third-person view. In this case, the camera hangs over the character’s shoulder.

God Mode emulates old school jokesters. Here you need to shoot crowds of enemies in arena locations. An individual or a group of users will not be allowed to proceed further until all opponents are destroyed. The network game, by the way, is designed for a maximum of 4 participants. As a reward for eliminating monsters (enemies are represented by skeletons, minotaurs and other mythical creatures), they give experience and gold. Currency is needed to open access to new opportunities and buy weapons. You are allowed to carry only two guns at a time. Trunks allow you to improve, and heroes - to customize. Skills provide temporary buffs. For example: restoring HP or summoning a giant hammer. But these skills consume energy. It is replenished by killing enemies. You can take vows before going out. These are special challenges that reward players with additional experience and coins.

The title does not have a full plot. To pass, you need to overcome a series of arenas, and then fight the final boss. After this, users will receive a reward and will appear in the main menu. They can then start the race again. There are a total of 5 purgatories available, but gamers will only see one map in one session. Therefore, the possibility of free online play increases replay value. In it, team members have 3 extra lives, and this supply is shared by everyone. And the fighters here have to compete with each other for results and loot. To prevent the levels from quickly becoming boring, the developers added modifiers that are chosen randomly each time. They can enable health reduction, apply fog, and apply other effects.
