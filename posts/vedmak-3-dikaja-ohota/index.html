Game Description#

The legendary third-person slasher series -The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt, which tells us the story of Geralt of Rivia, who becomes one of the few characters in the central series of action-packed books. We start the game remembering the events of the second part. But let’s not talk about them. Our hero is a born killer, capable of killing and ending the lives of thieves, criminals, assassins, kings, leaders, generals and monsters living throughout the map. The gameplay in The Witcher 3 Wild Hunt has changed a lot since the second part, and we will also play a little as Ciri, the heroine of the new part. At this moment, the main aspects of control are closed for us, but we can teleport.

We begin our journey by simply killing a monster. Since our hero is the only witcher who doesn’t care about the task, but doesn’t care about the profit, we agree to find the child of Destiny, who is located at the other end of the map, based on tips, at least. While we are moving there, shores, pirate islands, trading cities, government representatives and other heroes open up in front of us. Despite the vast and huge locations, the developers added a system for crafting items, a lot of indirect and side quests, and also diversified the game combat by diversifying spells, dodges, super attacks, the number of swords and all the items that Geralt of Rivia likes to use. Go through the entire journey from start to finish, explore new territories, capture enemies, make spells and potions from many opponents, and then enjoy the gameplay, where a third-person slasher is the path to a unique plot that has captivated not only readers, but also players .
