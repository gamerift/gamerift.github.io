Game Description#

Wizordum is a first -person retro -player in which gamers assign the role of a magician going to search for a chaos source. The Title combines the elements of the classic shooters of the 90s and modern gameplay mechanics. Users move around the world of terrabrum using various spells and magic objects to combat enemies and overcoming obstacles. The gameplay focuses on research and battles. Users should strategically use their arsenal of spells to defeat a number of enemies, each of which has unique habits and methods of attack.

The project also presents a variety of magical objects that can be used to strengthen the player’s abilities or gain tactical advantages in battle. In addition to the fighting, Wizordum includes elements of solid solutions, which require gamers of critical thinking and a creative approach to the use of magical abilities. The world encourages research, it has hidden areas and secrets that reward players for their curiosity.

The combination of action and solid solutions creates a dynamic and fascinating experience for users. In general, the Title offers a unique combination of retro and modern gameplay elements, providing complex and exciting experience for lovers of first-person jokes and fantasy. Strategic battle, study and solid solutions - all this guarantees that players will not remain indifferent to the world of terrabrum.
