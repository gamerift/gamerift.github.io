Game Description#

Crusader Kings 3 is a global strategy that allows you to team up with a friend for free to play together online or fight a pirate game against other players by connecting online via the Internet. Crusader Kings 3 includes the Northern Lords expansion, allowing you to explore the countries of Scandinavia. Events cover the period from 867 to 1453. You can turn off the counter in the settings so that the story develops until the moment when the hero’s dynasty becomes extinct. The map covers the lands of Spain, Mongolia, Central Africa, etc.

The territory of Crusader Kings III has increased 4 times compared to the previous part. The lands include counties and 3 baronial possessions for each of them. The free multiplayer option includes playable classes such as Muslims, Pagans, and other non-Christian units, but theocracies, nomads, and merchant republics have been removed. The main characters of Crusader Kings 3 are represented by 3D models. Multiplayer allows you to set 5 lifestyles, including 3 skill trees for each. New stress mechanics have been introduced. It is activated at the moment when the protagonist makes decisions contrary to his character.

A dynamic system of religions is presented: heresy, cannibalism, ordination of women, etc. You can create side branches within dynasties. For example, the Yorks and Lancasters as an offshoot of the Plantagenets. Every dynasty has its own head. The army includes militias that can be recruited from the vassals' house. It is also possible to hire professional warriors: crossbowmen, infantry, cavalry, etc. Heroes with high scores can be knighted. Their strength is 10 times greater than ordinary soldiers. A love line is available, allowing you to give birth to descendants and connect noble houses.
