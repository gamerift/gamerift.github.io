Game Description#

Crab Champions is one of those projects where the gamer is given control of an extraordinary hero. As you can guess from the name, the characters of the game are crabs. The setting is tropical islands - the player will be surrounded by palm trees, sand, ocean, stones and exotic animals. And also other crabs, armed to the teeth. Crab Champions can be played in three different modes: Co-op. Here is a typical PvE - several (up to four inclusive) gamers cooperate and act harmoniously to cope with a hostile environment. There are elements of leveling and mining - chests containing useful devices and improvements for the characteristics of crabs are scattered throughout the islands. There is also an economic component in the form of local currency, for which the same chests and weapons are purchased. The goal of the mode is to hold out as long as possible against advancing enemies. There is also a single-player variation for offline games. Racing. There are no weapons or goals to destroy - we run, jump, slide along ledges and other uneven surfaces in order to overtake our opponents and get to a certain part of the location as early as possible. You can also run the routes alone to set your own records.Duel. Up to 6 people (or bots for offline mode) can gather on one map to battle against each other. The crabs form teams, and the first one to score five wins in the rounds wins. There are custom matches where you can customize your own rules.

New content is constantly being added to the game, such as cosmetic skins, emotes, or songs when you win a match. There is no need to pay for them - everything is unlocked by the player for certain achievements or completed tasks.
