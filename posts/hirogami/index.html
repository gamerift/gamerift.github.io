Game Description#

Hirogami will allow you to take under the control of HIRO - a protagonist armed with a paper fan to combat digital invaders. This character from the village of Sisiki goes on a trip to save the world created from folded paper. The central conflict is tied with a force called bad and corrupting local inhabitants. For navigation through the environment and overcoming the barriers, the hero uses the ability to turn into various animals.

This gaming mechanic is key to solving puzzles and landscape research. Hirogami transformation abilities open access to various locations and offer solutions to problems. The protagonist can become an armadillo for breaking through the barriers, a monkey for moving along the crowns of trees or frog to climb on high ledges. In addition to animal appearance, the Glaverorea is able to turn into a sheet of paper for the flight on the rising air streams or sliding under traps.

Another reincarnation, into a paper aircraft, allows you to overcome deep crevices. The gameplay is focused on scattering filth and purifying inhabitants with an altered consciousness. Hiro uses a fan, creating wind gusts against digital creatures. Additional skills allow you to apply physical impacts or use other paper forces to win opponents. The main task of Title is to stop the spread of damage and restore the balance in the world. This game encourages a study of locations, opening access to secrets and hidden paths.
