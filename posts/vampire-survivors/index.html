Game Description#

Survival in the Vampire Survivors universe is based on minimalist gameplay, where you have to move around the map and knock out as much gold as possible. The main task for the protagonist is to advance through the levels generated in roguelight style. You need to try to survive the night, fighting off monsters and higher undead. Vampires will hunt for the protagonist, not giving him the opportunity to rest at a rest or come up with a future plan of action.

Vampire Survivors players have to test their own reaction speed and shooting accuracy as they move around the battlefield. The map is made in an isometric form, where you can observe and control the character from a top view, examining the immediate surroundings. The main character is a girl who has some light magic, which will allow her to cope longer against the insidious and aggressive bloodsuckers. It is necessary to carefully monitor the situation on the map in order to react to enemies in time. The gloomy setting provides players with: 7 characters to resist dangerous vampires. 24 types of weapons and 14 bonus skills that allow you to increase the hero’s survivability. 2 stages in the levels through which the user will go. The ability to select the desired set of armor and weapons. Collection valuable items and stones that enhance the character. Leveling up and creating your own build.
