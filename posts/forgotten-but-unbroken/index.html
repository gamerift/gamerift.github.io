Game Description#

Forgotten but Unbroken is an exciting tactical turn-based strategy game that takes inspiration from XCOM but offers its own unique and unforgettable twist. You will have the opportunity to fight with or against real heroes of World War II, unjustly forgotten by history. It’s time to take your place as the leader of a small but powerful resistance group and fight the might of the Axis forces to help liberate Europe! As you progress, you will have the opportunity to follow in the footsteps of our protagonist, Commander Martin, and build, upgrade and manage your own base. Recruit new teammates, train six different classes of specialists, and strategically place your units to achieve victory over the enemy. But be careful, because the decisions you make will have far-reaching consequences and affect the outcome of the war.

In Forgotten but Unbroken, you will have the opportunity to impress allies from the west and east with your tactical prowess. They can even send you money, supplies, and elite units to fight alongside your heroes. With gameplay mechanics unique to the genre, this game promises to be an unforgettable experience that will keep you coming back for more. Rise up, heroes, and join the resistance. It’s time to take back your freedom and do your part in the fight against the Axis forces. Get ready for an epic journey that is sure to leave a lasting impression!
