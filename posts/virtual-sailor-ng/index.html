Game Description#

Virtual Sailor NG is a simulator with the open world, concentrated on the management of water vessels. The physical system of the project reproduces marine dynamics and behavior of various boats on the water. This allows gamers to direct vessels anywhere within the environment, which opens up scope for research. Players master all forms of sailing, using the first -person species and 6 degrees of freedom. Both single and multiplayer regime are available.

This simulator inherits and unites the elements of its predecessors, Vehicle Simulator and Virtual Sailor, into a single product. One of the key features of Virtual Sailor NG is the possibility of modification that Title provides to its users. The game represents much more detailed landscapes than in the previous parts, along with an advanced physical model. This flexibility is most clearly manifested in the construction of boats, which is supported by a large library of existing mods and additions from the community.

To support the creativity of gamers, the gameplay is supplemented by an integrated editor of landscapes and vehicles. These integrated tools simplify the creation and exchange of their own content. The design system itself is a component that can serve as a training manual on the basics of shipbuilding. She also acts as a utility for those who want to create preliminary projects of water vessels.
