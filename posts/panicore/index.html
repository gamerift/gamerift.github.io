Game Description#

PANICORE throws you into a nightmare as an urban explorer. You and your friends, thrill-seekers who are attracted to creepy abandoned places, ignore the warnings and go to a creepy place. But the exploration takes a turn for the worse when you find yourself trapped. An unseen force is blocking the exit, and terrible creatures lurk in the shadows. Panic begins. You realize that you are not alone.

This co-op horror game will test your mettle. With death looming, you and your teammates, of which there are up to four in the demo, must solve puzzles and navigate treacherous corridors. The main thing is to maintain silence: monsters are attracted to sounds, including your voice. Outsmart them by hiding in closets, under tables, or in the shadows. Each choice can mean survival or a terrible end.

Fear sets in. You are not alone. Unknown forces block your escape, and an inexorable monster watches your every move, attracted not only by your steps, but also by your very voice. To survive, you will have to use your wits. Can you solve puzzles, navigate treacherous corridors and outsmart the lurking horror before it’s too late?

Time is ticking. There are key items scattered throughout the location that need to be found and used to escape. Can you work together to outwit the relentless AI and find a way out before it’s too late? PANICORE offers multiple escape routes, adding replay value to this chilling adventure. Will you face the horrors alone or with friends? The choice is yours.
