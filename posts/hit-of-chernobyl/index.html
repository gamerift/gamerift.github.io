Game Description#

Hit of Chernobyl immerses users in a post-apocalyptic environment where survival is of paramount importance. Finding themselves in an area contaminated by an unspecified disaster, gamers take on the role of an adventurer. He must navigate a landscape full of dangers, not only from the environment, but also from other characters. The world is populated by marauders, journalists and thrill seekers, each with their own motives. Finding unique artifacts becomes the main task, requiring users to overcome a landscape replete with difficulties.

Survival mechanics play an important role in shaping the gameplay. Gamers have to cope with basic needs such as hunger and thirst, which creates an additional burden during exploration. Resources are limited, so garbage collection becomes critical. This is not just a random walk through a fictional world - it is a strategic task. Every decision about when to move or seek shelter affects the player’s chances of survival. Having a day/night cycle adds another layer of complexity. During daylight hours, users may feel safer, but night brings new threats. Exploration in Hit of Chernobyl isn’t just about wandering around, it requires careful planning and execution. The area is full of hidden dangers and users must always be on alert.
