Game Description#

Broforce is a powerful paramilitary organization that is called upon when the world is in danger due to an excessive number of evil forces. Take control of dozens of different brothers to counter terrorist attacks and save civilians. Destroy your opponents with napalm, shooting, lightning and other various character skills.

Broforce is a 2D shooter platformer with pixel graphics. You will move around locations, destroying opponents in every possible way. Eliminate enemies in the most brutal ways, spreading shock and fear throughout the world so that terrorists fear you everywhere.

The gameplay contains several multiplayer and single player modes. In Deathmatch you can fight against other users and use cool abilities against each other. In Explosive Run, you’ll need to team up with your brothers to fight enemies in a land racked with massive explosions. Or go through levels against artificial intelligence.

Fight on custom maps using grenade launchers, machine guns, blow up locations and defeat enemies together with your friends. Fully exploding maps, many abilities, a huge number of weapons and the ability to create your own levels are a small list of the advantages of the game.
