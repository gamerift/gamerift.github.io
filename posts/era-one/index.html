Game Description#

Era One puts the player into the future with the destroyed political system of mankind. After a catastrophic gravitational event that paralyzed mass migration, societies on board the giant space cities enter the war for stinging resources. The mutated bioshahters pose a constant threat to the entire solar system. In these conditions, a woman named Alya de Chars is trying to get a source of energy for a secret ship, striving for a new beginning. The gameplay combines real -time strategy with the construction of bases and warships.

Gamers control the construction of their own objects using the assembly system system. This process makes it possible to create stations, defensive outposts and entire cities in space without size or form restrictions. To support these structures in the ERA One, it is necessary to collect raw materials and establish the distribution of energy. The fortifications become mandatory for protecting assets from hostile actions, which requires the placement of turrets and other protective complexes to reflect attacks of other factions. This game gives freedom in construction.

The construction of starships is based on a modular principle, providing the gamer with control over the final form and the appointment of a vessel. A variety of hull projects and sizes of ships laid the foundation for the fleet. In this project, you can install special components for movement, weapons, cargo compartments and scanners, adapting each ship to specific tasks. Such a gameplay allows you to form specialized ships intended for combat clashes, hidden operations or research missions into unknown space sectors.
