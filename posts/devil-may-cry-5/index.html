Game Description#

Devil May Cry 5 is a new third-person slasher for fans of the series. Dante and Nero return to the game universe to defeat all the demons, but will they have a different task? Let’s take a closer look. The developers did not touch the game mechanics; they only significantly changed the speed of the battle. If shooters have a subgenre - FPS - then this slasher is an analogue. The player has control over three characters with unique abilities, behavior and style of combat.

An additional foundation of the game is interactive inserts with charismatic characters.

The storyline develops before us in the last part. When Dante and Nero fight “The Savior”. Since we play as Dante throughout the second half of the fourth part, the narrative is now told from his perspective, although the game involves the use of three characters at once. In fact, DMC 5 is a slasher with lots of kills, fast battles and stylish finishing.

The developers focused on two components: they paid attention to the plot and special graphic effects on their engine - RE Engine. Now the entire visual component depends not on the monsters, but on the techniques used. That is, the more combinations and abilities you use in the fight against demons and monsters, the more colorful the battle will be. On the other hand, there is the plot. Since Dante is about Nero, who has made his own mobile demon-slaying agency, he is also involved in the new invasion that we reflect throughout the game. Players will also appreciate the mysterious appearance of V - a character who at one time stands on the side of our heroes, at other times he is a denouncer of darkness.
