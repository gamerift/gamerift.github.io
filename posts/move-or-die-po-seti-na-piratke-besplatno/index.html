Game Description#

Move or Die is a casual adventure designed for online competitions. You can play online with a friend, or use a local connection for free on a pirate. A special feature of the gameplay is that the rules change every 20 seconds, so the possibility of a free online game invites each participant to express themselves to the maximum in order to adapt to fleeting game processes. The online game Move or Die tests the strength of the participants' relationships! Allows you to go through a single-player campaign with bots.

There are many different modes to choose from. They boast a unique set of rules and individual situations that make each new race truly unique. The locations are distinguished by their artistic design and contain numerous traps, obstacles, interactive objects, etc. By increasing your skills, you can unlock characters, abilities, and modes. The leaderboard tracks achievements and shows players' positions in the global rankings. It is important to move all the time, because if you stop, the protagonist will simply explode. There are only 20 seconds to win. Time has passed!Key features: multiplayer for four; many modes: jump shooting, Chainsaw in the back, Rocket Run, etc.; stillness equals explosion; charismatic characters and unique game situations; tests, challenges and other unique content; huge amount of sarcastic humor.
