Game Description#

Gang Beasts is a fun low-poly fighting game containing platformer and survival elements. The game is aimed at playing online pirates online, teaming up with a friend or random participants online online. The emphasis is on funny physics and ragdoll. The action takes place in the vastness of Beef City. You need to choose your fighter to engage in battle with other players, trying to push them out of the arena or do everything possible to get your opponent into a trap. Online play generates many funny and crazy moments.

Before the start of the battle, you can not only select a character, but also completely customize your fighter. A wide selection of clothing, hats, accessories and other external attributes is available. Battle arenas are characterized by interactivity. Using the opportunity of a free online game, participants will have to push opponents from a height, throw them under cars or trains, clamp them in a vice, throw them into meat grinders, under saws, etc. Despite its cute appearance, Gang Beasts' gameplay is incredibly brutal.

You can fight both in classic deathmatch mode and perform in teams, competing in gangs. The developers place the main emphasis on ragdoll, which generates curious and funny situations. Units push, fight, fall, roll, etc. The last one to survive on the map wins. Each element of the location poses a serious threat, so you need to be as concentrated as possible.
