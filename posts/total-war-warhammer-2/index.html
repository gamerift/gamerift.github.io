Game Description#

Total War: WARHAMMER 2 is a turn-based strategy that invites you to immerse yourself in the gloomy campaign of the New World. The gameplay is highlighted by large-scale battles between six races. Enter the battlefield to save or destroy the world. Total War Warhammer 2 consists of two parts: a turn-based campaign in the open world and tactical battles in real time.

Play both in single mode and take part in multiplayer battles. Delve into the intricacies of the global political life of the fantasy world, assembling a personal squad of various units. All army soldiers have unique characteristics and abilities. Learn the tactical intricacies of Total War: WARHAMMER II while immersing yourself in hundreds of hours of intense strategic gameplay. Playable races and heroes: High elves - Alariel the Radiant, Eltharion the Gloomy Face, etc.; Lizardmen - Nakai the Wanderer, Mazdamundi, Gor-Rok, etc.; Skaven - Kwik the Goblin, Skrolk, Tretch Dastardly Tail and others; Dark Elves - Karond Kara Lokhir the Hard-Hearted, Morathi, Malekith; Tomb Kings - Settra the Imperishable, Khatep, Khalida, etc. Vampires - Luthor Harkon, Aranessa Salty Fury, Silostra the Fin, Count Noctilus.

Races are represented by unique campaigns such as Avelron, Yvresse, Hexoatl, Pestilens, Exiles of Nehek, Pirates of Sartosa and many others. Take command of an army of thousands of dragons, monsters, vampires and other mythical creatures. Fight in melee and ranged combat, use siege weapons, and deal devastating damage with powerful spells. Depending on the playstyle, you can see four endings tied to the Whirlwind, Horned Rat or Malekith.
