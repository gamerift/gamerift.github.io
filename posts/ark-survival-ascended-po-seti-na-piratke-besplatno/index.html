Game Description#

ARK: Survival Ascended is an online game revived in the latest video game technology Unreal Engine 5. It allows you to play online with a friend over the Internet online, even if you have a pirate. This gives you the opportunity to play online for free in this amazing world, surrounded by bright colors and the soothing sound of azure waters. The roar of the jungle awakens curiosity, not fear. Here you have to create a tribe, tame dinosaurs and primitive creatures, and climb the food chain through exploration, crafting, building and fighting.

This updated version is powered by Unreal Engine 5, which provides high-quality graphics capabilities. These include global illumination and mesh streaming, which provide incredible detail. Water has dynamic physics that create realistic waves and splashes as creatures move through the fluid. Foliage reacts interactively to different influences. If you cut down a tree, it will have a realistic impact on the environment.

In ARK: Survival Ascended you can travel through all available worlds such as Scorched Earth, Aberration, Extinction, Genesis Part 1 and Genesis Part 2. It is a project that offers you a unique survival experience where you can create your Survivor, join tribes and interact with the living ecosystem. You must consider important factors such as food, water, temperature and weather, and expand your knowledge and capabilities through research.

Quality of life improvements: redesigned interface, intelligent pathfinding for creatures, wild cubs, photo mode and much more. The game encourages gamer creativity through cross-platform modding, allowing you to create your own content and create community creations. You can also create cross-platform tribes to expand your survival options.

ARK: Survival Ascended supports a variety of multiplayer options: up to 70 players in open online multiplayer, up to 8 players in private sessions, and up to 2 gamers in local split-screen mode. Immerse yourself in this updated world and immerse yourself in an incredible survival adventure.
