Game Description#

Magicka 2 is an isometric role-playing game that allows you to play as a pirate online with a friend, using all available multiplayer modes for free. You can play through the campaign online or play locally. The adventure is set in a fantasy setting and contains a huge amount of humor. You have to explore the ruins of Aldrheim and study the lands of Midgard, destroyed by magicians after the protracted Wizard War. Many creatures react very hostilely, so you always need to be on alert in order to be able to repel the monsters that inhabit the kingdom. Multiplayer features 4 powerful wizards to choose from, including the guide Vlad.

The heroes act as powerful magicians. You can use spells on both your friends and enemies. Some spells have enormous power, allowing you to instantly destroy all enemies in your path. You can master up to a thousand spells by experimenting and combining the abilities of the heroes. You can combine up to five elements at a time to create unique types of spells. Magica 2 supports co-op for up to four people. The opportunity to play free online games includes completing a story campaign. Friendly fire is enabled by default, which results in many funny and hilarious scenes to watch.

There is a wide selection of clothes, staves, weapons, etc. You can wreak havoc or play the role of a good wizard - everyone chooses their own path. The dynamic spell pronunciation system in Magicka 2 makes it possible to create crazy chains of spells, where you never know what effect you can get from this or that action. The Artifacts auxiliary mode allows you to make changes to the gameplay and customize the passage using various modifications.
