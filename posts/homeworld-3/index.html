Game Description#

Homeworld 3 is a new part of the famous space battle franchise, which is a sequel and continues the storyline. Nevertheless, the game is also suitable for those who are not familiar with the universe, if you only dive into the mode of multiplayer confrontations on ships. Now abandoned galactic stations are scattered across the universe, which have turned into arenas where you can feel the battle with a random user.

The galaxy began a period of true abundance, when travel over vast distances became available to everyone thanks to the opening of hyperspace gates. There is no longer a need for powerful equipment and spare fuel to get to the desired solar system; this can be done in seconds, even on a weak ship. But this did not solve the problem of military clashes, which only intensified, simply cyclical each other. But suddenly catastrophic failures began in the operation of the gate, the nature of which is unclear to scientists. The Karan, which has already acquired the status of a myth and has religious significance for everyone, may be the key to unraveling the mystery. After all, if the disruptions continue to intensify, the entire galaxy could be in danger.

For fans of multiplayer combat, Homeworld 3 has a mode that combines real-time strategy and roguelike. Two players will need to guide their ships through a series of tests, working as a team or, on the contrary, interfering. Quests, enemies and dangers along the way will be generated randomly. For each victory, you can receive unique items or unlock powerful abilities that are saved in subsequent matches.
