Game Description#

Paradise Beach Simulator is a first-person management simulator centered on an abandoned island. The player’s goal is to clear the area of ​​garbage and dilapidated buildings to create a functioning business. Initial activities include ordering equipment via the express boat and preparing the beach area for the first visitors. Guests arrive by ferry and their satisfaction levels increase as their requests are fulfilled, and all equipment requires regular maintenance to remain operational.

In the game Paradise Beach Simulator, the gamer will have to prepare food and drinks for all the customers in the diner. The process involves cutting, grilling and assembling dishes using ingredients sourced from the immediate surroundings. To provide resources, it is necessary to engage in spearfishing and collecting local flora, as well as cultivate and harvest from personal fruit trees. Various entertainment options are also offered, such as sunbathing areas, hot tub access and jet ski tours, based on specific customer preferences as communicated to the property manager.

In addition to commercial activities, the island holds history and secrets that are revealed during exploration. Traveling on a jet ski allows you to move around the perimeter of the location in search of treasures and other interesting places. As the business develops, it becomes possible to establish a store to sell created items. The growing demands of an enterprise ultimately lead to the need to hire personnel for their specific skills to handle the increased operational workload.
