Game Description#

Chinese Frontiers is an atmospheric immersion in the time of ancient China, where users will be able to feel the life of a simple resident of the village. The game is a simulator of life where you have to take under the management of the employee, which is working on the construction of a great Chinese wall. Gamers will be able to test various types of crafts of that time, and will also monitor the character indicators, giving him food, water and rest in time.

Gameplay mechanics in Tianis Frontirs are allowed:

Engage in various difficulty on behalf of the hero to secure survival. Cooperate and interact with the residents of the village by buying or selling resources. Improve your own housing and pump the character, developing his skills and skills. Craft useful things, such as labor tools or weapons that helps in hunting. Freely dispose of the time where the main project is the construction of the wall.

Events transfer to a small village, which is located near the construction site of one of the sections of a large protective wall. Players will have to serve the emperor and conscientiously fulfill his obligations in order to finish construction in time. In the process of passing, users will control the character in the first person and freely study the surroundings, doing various routine. It will work to go fishing, grow rice or engage in animal hunting to get food.
