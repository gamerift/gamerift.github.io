Game Description#

Empyrion - Galactic Survival shows the world of the future. In the 24th century, in the ice of Antarctica, they find a spacecraft of aliens. Scientists investigated the object and discovered the technology of the hyperdrod. Earthlings send an expedition to the planet Empirion, which is in the Andromeda nebula. The crew gets to the destination, but tolerates the crash and cannot return home.

In survival mode, the player leaves the rescue capsule and is alone against the hostile world. There is no atmosphere, so oxygen is worth its weight in gold. Food, resources and ammunition is also few. It is worthwhile to beware of alien soldiers and unmanned drones. Aggressive life forms and changing weather conditions can kill an unprepared astronaut.

Hunting and farming will help to quench hunger. Plants are extremely useful in everyday life - medicines and potions are made of them, which are used in the provision of first aid. Separate crops are edible and will save when it was not possible to catch game. In addition to the main regime in the Empire Galaxy, there is a creative - a huge sandbox that allows you to realize any ideas without worrying about natural needs. You can plow space both alone and in a multiplayer. Players are united in alliances and fight for resources.

To repulse the enemies, you need to build long -range weapons (turrets) and other gadgets. New technologies are opened by the experience of experience, which are accrued for almost any actions. For craft, various minerals are needed. Gamers can open deposits on the planet, as well as excavations on astrocks rich in ore. If weapons allows, you can attack alien bases and steal valuable materials from there.
