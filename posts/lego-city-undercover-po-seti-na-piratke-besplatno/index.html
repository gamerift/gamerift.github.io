Game Description#

LEGO City Undercover is a Lego adventure with the ability to play online for free as a pirate, exploring bright locations with a friend and overcoming many obstacles. Using the Internet online, you need to work together to go to the streets of Lego City to clear more than 20 different neighborhoods from bandits. The online game consists of missions such as investigations, hijackings, chases and much more. There are over 100 different items available to collect.

The gameplay is a third-person arcade game with puzzle and platforming elements. There are many heroes with their own unique abilities and characters. The adventure contains a huge amount of signature Lego humor. The opportunity for free online play offers you to take control of one of the available heroes, including both a good character and an antagonist. Depending on the faction to which the protagonist belongs, he has his own set of weapons and equipment. Minor characters in the main storyline include Chief Marion Danby, Natalia Kowalski, Vinny Pappalardo and many others. LEGO City Undercover Heroes: Chase McCain - the main character and an experienced police officer; Frank Medok - a partner who always attracts bad luck; Ellie Phillips - the weapons manager and Chase’s love; Mad Dog - a dangerous criminal who escaped from prison; Chan Chuan - the owner Limousine store; Forrest Blackwell is a billionaire and the main antagonist of the adventure.
