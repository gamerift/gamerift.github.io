Game Description#

Sons of The Forest is a first-person multiplayer horror game with survival elements. The game is a sequel to the acclaimed hit The Forest. As in the previous part, you need to explore a mysterious forest inhabited by bloodthirsty monsters. Survive by hiding from enemies and carefully study the surrounding areas. Arm yourself with powerful weapons to face numerous opponents.

The story centers on a special forces soldier with a Fight Demons tattoo. The new protagonist replaces TV presenter Eric LeBlanc from the previous game. Unlike Eric, Timmy is a well-trained warrior, deftly handling melee and ranged weapons. The Sons of the Forest bring a new type of weapon to the game - modern firearms. The protagonist got into a helicopter crash. He was attacked by an unknown mutant. The hero of Sons of The Forest wakes up on a mysterious island inhabited by strange monsters always thirsting for blood. You can play alone or team up with a friend in co-op mode. The developers also allow you to test an updated model of the deadly ax on your enemies. Grab your weapon and blow off the heads of all the evil spirits in the area!
