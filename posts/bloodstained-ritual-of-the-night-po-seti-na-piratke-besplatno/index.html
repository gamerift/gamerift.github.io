Game Description#

You can playBloodstained: Ritual of the Night online with a friend on a pirate account for free. In this**online online multiplayer game, you will find yourself in the role of Miriam, an orphan struck by a crystallizing curse. Explore a sprawling side-scrolling castle infested with demonic enemies to break the curse and prevent a similar fate for humanity. Bloodstained is not your average side-scroller, but a Metroidvania with the genre’s hallmarks of exploration, ability progression, and hidden secrets.

The base game is already packed with content, but there are 13 free DLCs planned for the future, including boss rush and speedrun modes. The combat has a smooth, satisfying crunch, and the story takes place within a dark fantasy narrative written by Koji Igarashi, the godfather of the Igavania games (think Castlevania). The voice cast features a stellar cast, including industry veterans David Hayter and Ray Chase.

But Bloodstained isn’t just about plot, it’s also a looting fest. Players can look forward to a huge arsenal of weapons and spells that can be upgraded and visually customized. But it’s not just about aesthetics: the game uses beautiful 2.5D graphics, where 3D models seamlessly transition into 2D environments, creating a visually unique experience. With a variety of playable characters (two of them are still shrouded in mystery!), Bloodstained: Ritual of the Night promises a deep and action-packed adventure for Metroidvania fans.
