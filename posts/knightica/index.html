Game Description#

Knightica instructs commanders to gather a detachment and strategically arrange its fighters to use combat synergies. Units have unique abilities activated by accurate arrangement and turning on the battlefield. The player forms these forces from various fractions, such as the legions of undead or adherents of the guild of magicians, before their entry into automated battles. The assembled army then goes on a hike through a series of trials in order to free the kingdom.

Each campaign involves the adoption of important decisions, starting even before the first battle. In this game, Knightica Gamer defines its commander, starting warriors and objects that affect the entire subsequent race. The further choice consists in moving the map, evaluating the benefits of visiting a mysterious shop and a risk of collision with an elite demon. Such a structure guarantees new opportunities for experiments with powerful combinations of units, making each adventure unique.

Further advancement requires attention to several strategic aspects in addition to arranging fighters. The commander decides whether to improve the troops, collect a larger army to suppress opponents with a number or focus on the economy. Success depends on the competent use of collected spells and demonic souls to reflect growing threats. The entire game detachment should adapt to overcoming various enemies and frightening bosses, using unique demonic tactics to ensure the future of the entire kingdom.
