Game Description#

Game of Grass begins with a call from a neighbor who offended the player’s lawn. The main task is to demonstrate superiority through the cultivation of the remarkable courtyard. The process includes methodical mowing of vegetation using a variety of tools. Gamers acquire upgrades to increase the efficiency of their equipment, which allows you to quickly clear the territory.

The goal is to achieve the status of the legend of haircuts of grass by methodically improving the site, and advance in this simulator is linearly and focused on tangible results. The gaming process in Game of Grass goes beyond the maintenance of the site, delving into the agrarian simulation and economic strategy. The gamer plants and grows crops that generate constant passive income for investment. A special test within the framework of this mechanism is the collection of rare gold yields detected during harvest.

As the lawns improves, she begins to attract thousands of visitors and various animals, adding a new aspect to the simulation. These elements introduce strategic decisions on the distribution of resources throughout the land allotment. To facilitate the process of clearing the Earth, the choice of devices and even weapons is available. Promotion goes on a straight path through the purchase of modernizations affecting the quantity and quality of vegetation. This cycle of haircuts, earnings and upgrades forms the main mechanics of the incremental project. Promotion is marked by the goal of getting every affordable subject and improvement within this title.
