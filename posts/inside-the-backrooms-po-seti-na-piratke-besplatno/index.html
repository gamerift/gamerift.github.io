Game Description#

Inside the Backrooms is a horror game with the ability to play online for free as a pirate with a friend and random players, trying to survive a terrible night. The cooperative mode allows you to jointly confront a certain mysterious entity over the Internet. It is very important to interact with partners and survive in every possible way in gloomy closed locations. The network game Inside the Backrooms is designed for three people. There are 4 different levels available to explore. At each location you need to collect quest or useful items that will help you escape from the trap. You also need to solve puzzles.

The characters are hunted by several entities with their own unique abilities and weaknesses. You must first understand how to properly attack the monster, and then begin to act. Sometimes it is better to avoid direct confrontation with the monster. Each level has its own individual goals and mechanics. Some puzzles are quite intuitive and simple, while others will require you to rack your brains and force your teammates to interact with each other. It is necessary to treat with special almond water. Heroes can keep in touch using radio. A flashlight allows you to illuminate dark areas, and protective suits do not allow radiation to pass through. Mechanics and content: Possibility of free online play;Stamina indicator, limiting the actions of the protagonists;Inventory for storing things;Character alarm, limiting visibility;Unique attacks and abilities of monsters;Three-time revival on one map.
