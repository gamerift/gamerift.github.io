Game Description#

Beyond Senses is a cooperative action game with horror elements that you can play online with a friend to survive a nightmare together online. You will have to face ghosts, demons, the devil and other bloodthirsty monsters. There are entire units dedicated to tracking down anything paranormal. The network game Beyond Senses offers free pirates to take part in asymmetrical fights, where 4 players control hunters, and one participant takes on the guise of a powerful demon.

There are 3 game modes: Hunt, Selfish and Exile. Each one comes with unique rules and objectives to win. There are 3 locations available: a cemetery, a forest and a house of horrors. The characters are John Hughes, Lauren Williams, Elijah Knight and Amy King. There are only two demons for the game: Doctor Walker and Valak. You can increase your character’s level to level 30. The possibility of free online play opens up access to clothing, abilities, weapons, etc. Skills are upgraded by how often you use them. Weapons and Items: 4 supporting items: Holy Water, Sound Emitter, Incense Bomb and Devil Trap;

6 light sources available: HIS, telephone, lamp, flashlight, portable spotlight and night vision devices;

3 types of weapons: Repentance, Peacemaker and Destroyer;

5 abilities: Invisibility, Energy Spell, Resurrection, Banishment and Teleportation;

5 nicknames: Ghost Hunter, Monster Banisher, Coward, Puppet Hater and Living Legend.
