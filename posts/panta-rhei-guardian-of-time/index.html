Game Description#

Panta Rhei - Guardian of Time tells about the journey of Fi, a guard expelled to the garden of the past. The chaotic force threatens to destroy the course of being, forcing the Glaverroy to leave his refuge. The reluctant protagonist will have to go to the source of time to confront this threat. This path will require him to face the shadows of the past and take a forgotten debt for the sake of saving the house. The main tool and weapons in this game is time itself.

Key mechanics in Panta Rhei - Guardian of Time are focused on direct time management. Fi has abilities that change the environment to solve clockwork puzzles or opening new routes. In battle, these forces allow us to cancel the latest actions, correcting errors or doubleting the effectiveness of the attack. The gameplay also includes battles with an emphasis on close battle, depending on certain combinations and competent positioning against hostile creatures. The study takes place in the kingdom of time - a procedurally generated world with many secrets requiring disclosure. Each location offers special tests, and the performance of side tasks changes the area for subsequent attempts.

Features of travel through a temporary stream:

Direct time management to solve puzzles and surroundings. The possibility of canceling the latest actions in the battle to correct errors or tactical maneuvers. Dynamic battles in close combat with emphasis on a combination of shock and positioning. The procedurally generated world, changing after completing tasks. The system of creating objects for the development of unique strategies in every race.
