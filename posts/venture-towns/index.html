Game Description#

Venture Towns allows players to build a city landscape. This project transfers to the gamers the corporation forming a settlement. From the initial structures to vast skyscrapers, the player leads the development, striving for economic superiority. The game emphasizes the deliberate arrangement of various architectural elements, contributing to a systematic approach to urbanistic growth and current economic activity.

In Venture Towns, the processes of the construction of objects open the ways for development. The grouping of suitable structures near each other forms specialized zones, generating increased income and increasing the value of the surrounding territories. This approach stimulates the design, based on specific considerations, in which each development solution promotes the total monetary income of the settlement. Players observe the direct consequences of their plans, since the inhabitants are equipped in these forming quarters, carrying out daily labor activities for earnings.

This population directs its earnings to buy widespread goods, including personal transport, housing and pets, exerting a direct impact on the local economic system. Some inhabitants participate in the drawing system, getting the opportunity to win a large amount. The system monitors these individual financial flows, demonstrating the direct influence of the created environment on the life of citizens. This simulator represents urban planning as a direct business event, offering gamers to realize the vision of the prosperity of the metropolis and the current financial structure.
