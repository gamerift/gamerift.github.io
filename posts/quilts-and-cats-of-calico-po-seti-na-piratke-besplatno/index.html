Game Description#

Quilts and Cats of Calico is a challenging puzzle game with a heartfelt theme that can be played online. Players make quilts, scoring points for patterns and designs, while meeting adorable cats. You can enjoy the game for free with a pirate friend. Fans of the board game Calico will find familiar territory here with a few surprises. Online network play allows you to explore the basic gameplay with unchanged rules, and in the campaign mode there are variations of the rules and new scenarios. For those who prefer single-player experiences, Quilts and Cats of Calico features a single-player mode with customizable difficulty levels to hone your skills and experiment with strategies.

It features a campaign that transports players into a magical world with an aesthetic reminiscent of the creations of Studio Ghibli. In this world, cats play a key role, and players take on the role of a wandering quilt maker who strives to gain respect in a city inhabited by cats. As players progress through their careers, they will face a villain whose actions threaten to destroy the peaceful coexistence of humans and cats. The players’ task is to create exquisite blankets, improve their skills and help those who meet on their way. Warm relationships and charming cats await them, ready to lend a helping hand.

Don’t be surprised if your feline companions decide to interact with your blanket while they play. These furry friends can snuggle up next to you, playfully bounce against the boards, or simply curl up for a nap. Players can pet these virtual cats or gently push them aside if they become too clingy.

Quilts and Cats of Calico isn’t just about cats, it allows players to create their own! A comprehensive customization system allows players to name the virtual cat, choose its fur color and dress it in a variety of outfits. This personal feline companion can join the player on their quilting adventures. Don’t forget that players can also customize their own in-game portrait and background for a truly personalized experience.
