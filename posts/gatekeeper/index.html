Game Description#

Gatekeeper invites you to play an exciting quest online. With a friend or even three friends you will go to save the Universe. The heart of the universe has been stolen, and now it’s up to you to save it. Travel across galaxies, fighting hordes of robot enemies who guard the interplanetary gates. Play online for free on the Internet and solve the mysteries of the Universe with your friends in this online pirate game.

Uncover the secrets of the stolen Heart. It is locked in a quantum cage, and the key to it is held by the ancient giants - the Sirens. Each playthrough of the game brings surprises to you, keeping the action fresh. But you are not just a space truck driver. Choose your hero - each of them has their own special skills to suit your play style. Level up and customize their abilities, then add the power of mysterious artifacts you discover along the way.

Do you think you can handle it alone? Certainly! But there is strength in numbers. Team up with friends and become an unstoppable squad of space defenders. Overcome difficulties, protect Totems and discover new areas to explore. Every race is unique. There are more than a hundred collectible items scattered throughout the game, each of which brings its own adjustments to the gameplay and offers a new experience.
