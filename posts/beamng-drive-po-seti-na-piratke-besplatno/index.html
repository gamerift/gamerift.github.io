Game Description#

InBeamNG Drive you can play online online or take part in dynamic chases with artificial intelligence. The pirate game features a variety of online challenges and competitions for playing with a friend, or a set of single missions for playing alone. All machines, spare parts, components, etc. available for free or for in-game currency, which is awarded for winning races. The gameplay is based on realistic physics. This is especially true for soft objects, in particular rubber.

All components and components of the machine are simulated in real time. Free online play allows participants to build their own car, offering over 10 different bodies, including compact cars, massive trucks, and more. Each type of vehicle can be customized to your liking, choosing from numerous types of suspensions, engines, etc. There are 12 interactive and highly detailed maps available with complete freedom of movement. The landscapes of each location are varied: prairies, plains, mountains, tropical jungles, metropolis, etc. Tasks give you the opportunity to work as a delivery driver or prove yourself as an experienced racer. The BeamNG.drive network game includes modes: scenarios - various orders and police chases; free drive - the ability to explore extensive game maps; time trials - by choosing a car and setting a route, you need to show a result superior to your opponent; modifications and community - fan works that allow you to post both your cars and maps; cooperation with Automation allows you to export cars.
