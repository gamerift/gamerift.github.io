Game Description#

In The Front, your task, as a resistance fighter, boils down to go into the past in order to prevent the formation of a tyrannical empire that threatens the very existence of mankind. The game is a craft shooter with an open world, immersing a player in a post -apocalyptic sandbox, teeming with mutants and hostile groups.

In this terrain destroyed by the war, you have to fight with tyrannical imperial forces, exiles, bandits and rebels in an epic battle, which will determine the future of mankind. Unit with comrades, explore the ruins of the destroyed world, create an arsenal of weapons and equipment, build fortified bases equipped with Tower Defense style traps, and modernize your equipment. Do not forget to manage tanks and helicopters, confronts not only to enemies, but also the harsh elements reigning in this desert area.

One of the features of the product from Samar Studio is the system of construction in the style of “sandbox”, which allows players to show their creative potential and build a base using a wide set of building components. Opportunities are limited only by your imagination. Strategically correctly placed traps - your allies in this tireless struggle for survival. Carry monsters and other formidable adversaries through a chain of cruel traps - from explosive devices to invisible killers - so that they are killed. The game presents more than 20 types of functional vehicles, including combat helicopters and self -propelled anti -aircraft guns, so the creation and piloting of vehicles is of great importance. In addition, players can expect the emergence of custom -made basic machines, which promises an even greater strategic depth.

Use your tactical abilities in working with logical systems and schemes using control devices such as pressure plates and infrared sensors to create automated defense systems. Raids on enemy camps and bases turn into an exciting lesson: armed with a near -fight weapon, firearms, explosives, tanks and helicopters, you can protect your territory or strike in the very heart of the ill -wisher. Get supporters using muffles for the destruction of NPC. These NPC not only strengthen your combat potential, but also help their workbench skills, increasing the effectiveness of the operation and resource production.
