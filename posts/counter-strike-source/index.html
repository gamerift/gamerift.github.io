Game Description#

Emerging as a worthy competitor to its previous installment,Counter-Strike: Source is an updated version 1.6 in which players found both disadvantages and advantages. For example, in the new part, many weapons have a completely different bullet spread, which is why players had to get used to it for a long time. The ballistics and shooting technique have not changed, but the graphics and the “atmosphere of an open location” added additional charm to the game for fans of the first-person shooter.

The main game mode is victory on the map in team resistance between terrorists and special forces. The terrorists' goal: to plant a bomb, prevent it from being demined, or kill all opponents in a round, which, by the standards of competitive games, lasts a minute and 45 seconds. Special forces must guard mining points or prevent terrorists from planting a bomb. There are modes such as hostage guarding or deathmatch, but they are considered unofficial. In the first round, the player is given a base amount of $800. Each round he receives money regardless of winning or losing. With the money you receive you can buy:

New pistol. The choice falls from Glock to Five Seven. The most expensive pistol for terrorists will be the Tec; Assault rifle or semi-automatic rifle; Shotgun or machine gun; Grenades, armor, sapper kit; Light machine guns, submachine guns.

With all available weapons, players will fight 30 rounds (or less) to win. Victory occurs when one of the teams reaches 16 rounds won. A huge selection of maps and weapons, unique ballistics and millions of fans around the world - Counter-Strike: Source is a continuation of the legendary franchise.
