Game Description#

Dale & Dawson Stationery Supplies invites players to plunge into the complex world of office intrigue, where paper clips are currency and the cooler is a place of lively discussions. You can play this game online with a friend, and you decide who to be: an influential manager or an employee honing the skills of creating the appearance of being busy. For those who are looking for a free opportunity to have fun, the pirate game is available online on the Internet. Here you have to either obey or lead, simultaneously improving the art of fast work.

But here’s the catch - not everyone is right in the head. Slackers play pranks, pretend to be cool, and do everything they can to blend in with the worker bees. It’s a game of cat and mouse in which a manager tries to catch bad employees before they destroy the company. The specialists, bless their hearts, are just trying to keep a low profile and get through another day without loss.

It’s all powered by contactless voice chat, so you can hear the panic in Bob’s voice when he realizes he’s sent that rude email to the entire company. Forge alliances, throw your officemate under the bus, or engage in good old-fashioned water cooler gossip. In the cruel world of Dale and Dawson, everything is fair. Just remember that in this office, even the walls have ears - and they can report to the boss.
