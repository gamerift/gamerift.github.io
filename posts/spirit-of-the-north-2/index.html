Game Description#

Spirit of the North 2 allows the gamer to manage a fox as part of a third -person adventure. The scene is the ancient world full of ruins, where the red -haired heroine is in isolation, far from the native edges. Accompanied by a wise companion-warrior, the protagonist goes in search of missing guards. The main task is to free these creatures from the influence of a shaman named Grimnir, as well as in the return of the fox home.

The key element of gameplay in Spirit of the North 2 is the study. It is necessary to cross an extensive open world that stores traces of a forgotten story. The route runs through the biomes that differ from each other, including thick forests, snowy peaks and ancient underground crypts. Each location offers unique trials and secrets related to the main goal. Moving forward, the fox finds runes that enhance its abilities and help to overcome obstacles to access new zones.

It is possible to change the foxes of the character through settings of appearance and skills, which allows you to adjust it to different styles of movements or solutions to problems. Collisions with desecrated guards are important stages of promotion according to the plot of this game. These episodes are implemented in the form of puzzles requiring certain actions to heal creatures, without stopping on a direct collision. A successful completion of such episodes is necessary to fulfill the task of saving the keepers, which is the basis of the gameplay.
