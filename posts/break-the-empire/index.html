Game Description#

Break The Empire is an action rogal, where the goal is to get to the capital and eliminate the emperor. Gamers have to choose one of the 6 heroes, each has six unique spells and six passive castomization skills. Additionally, 12 common abilities are available for any build, which allows you to try different approaches to the main task - the destruction of the imperial system.

The battles include the irreversible death of the hero, which increases the rates of each solution and forces to start again after failure, and the environment is interactive: the fire spreads along the grass, the water surfaces can be frozen, and it is easy to fall into the abyss. The passage of Break The Empire is built on promotion on the randomly created routes for the final clash. The routes run through different biomes, such as magic forests, captured cities, fortresses or gnomes, each location challenges its call.

Gamers will encounter dozens of types of opponents with different parameters and features, as well as with 9 unique bosses blocking the path. Trophies have random properties, which significantly expands the number of available combinations of weapons and tactics at every attempt to pass this title. You can use various tactics, for example, to command the undead when storming the fortifications or to beat imperial fractions. A direct confrontation using the character’s skills and the found equipment found also remains a key opportunity. Combinations of abilities create many effects in battle.
