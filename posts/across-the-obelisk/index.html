Game Description#

Across the Obelisk is a cooperative game with role -playing elements and collecting its own deck. The gameplay presents a single and multi -user regime. Participants choose a variety of heroes, open access to new items and cards, creating a set of characters. After that, you have to fight monsters and bosses in deep tactical contractions.

Features of Across the Obelisk:

Dynamic assembly of decks in the Roguelite style, where each card improves by different items, increased level or other methods. Cooperative regime up to 3 participants at the same time. 16 unlocked characters with individual abilities, appearance and role in battles. 500+ cards and things to choose from, as well as the possibility of craft and improvement. Minor and main quests with a choice system, which will affect the further development of the plot.

The procedural generation creates a map, danger and a chance of falling out of all elements, which provides endless reiglable. The equipment gives additional bonuses and effects, such as bloodshed, poisons, protective or attacking skills. Many combinations will help increase the chance of critical damage and other aspects. In the course of passage there will be many randomly created ways and quests that change the world. Gamers are able to control fiery wizards, shadow magicians, healers, riders and other warriors.
