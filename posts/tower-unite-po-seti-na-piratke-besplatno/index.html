Game Description#

Tower Unite - a virtual party for playing together on the Internet online, or for single play. Together with a friend or other players, you can play pirate online, participating in different types of competitions. Once in a huge shopping complex with entertainment, you are offered to try out every attraction or challenge other players completely free of charge. A total of 22 types of arcade competitions are available. Each slot machine offers a choice of two modes: online play with friends or single play.

The following mini-games are presented: Minigolf, Accelerate, Ball Race, Virus, Zombie Massacre and many others. All Tower Unite events take place on the territory of a large center called the Plaza. A special feature is the creative mode, which allows you to create your own tests and post them publicly. Using the opportunity of a free online game, you need to fight with opponents in intense sports disciplines. In essence, the project is a collection of competitions with a varied set of rules and mechanics.

By selecting the Workshop item, you can fully customize your character by setting the parameters of appearance, clothing and accessories. About 2,000 unique objects located on the territory of the entertainment complex are available for interaction. Winning competitions earns in-game achievements and increases the player’s rank, giving him the opportunity to take a leading position in the world’s online leaderboards. You have to run, jump, squat, use jetpacks, get drunk, dance, vomit, mess around, drive and much more.
