Game Description#

RWBY Grimm Eclipse is an anime action game with the ability to play online with a friend or compete in a pirate game online. All multiplayer modes are included and all released add-ons are included for free, including Horde mode, Merlo’s Laboratory, etc. The online game is designed for four participants, where each hero plays his role in the team. The main enemy of the protagonists is the mighty Grimm, who guards the Remnant locations.

The opportunity for free online play offers you to take control of Ruby, Weiss, Blake and Yang. Each character has its own character and a huge background behind it. Only by joining forces are the heroes able to repel the powerful monster. The main storyline contains many main and secondary tasks, various tests, mini-games and a huge number of different activities. The gameplay is inspired by games such as Dynasty Warriors and Left 4 Dead, combining the mechanics and key elements of these two games. Features of RWBY Grimm Eclipse: co-op for four; the ability to play solo with bots; characters with their own skills and abilities; leveling up heroes and developing skills; a variety of enemies and difficult bosses; ranked tests and challenges; endless mode on 5 unique maps.
