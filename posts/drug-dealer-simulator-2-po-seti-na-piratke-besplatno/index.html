Game Description#

InDrug Dealer Simulator 2 you can play online for free. Step into the shoes of Eddie, a petty crook on the run, and enjoy online pirate gaming. Explore the fictional Caribbean island of Isla Sombra in first-person co-op with a friend. Enjoy online gaming where power is above the law and opportunity beckons to those brave enough to seize it.

Isla Sombra may be a haven for criminals, but that doesn’t mean it’s a relaxing place. Eddie has to prove he’s right. The game offers many ways to climb the criminal ladder: from buying and selling drugs to making your own product. Players can work alone or team up with a friend as they navigate the island’s varied landscapes - villages, harbors and bustling cities.

The world of DDS2 is full of life, filled with colorful characters with their own agendas. But keep in mind that the island has more than just sun and beaches. An improved artificial intelligence system means danger lurks around every corner. Expect militia raids and trouble from rival gangs. The economy is no less brutal, with two currencies: local notes and the coveted US dollar. Will Eddie rise to the top of the food chain or become the next victim of Isla Sombra’s ruthless game? The choice is yours.
