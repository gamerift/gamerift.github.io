Game Description#

The Room 4: Old Sins is an adventure puzzle game inspired by the works of Lovecraft. The story begins with the disappearance of the chief engineer and his high-ranking wife. After this, the hunt for ancient artifacts begins. A chain of events leads to the attic of their mansion, where there is an old dollhouse…

You have to explore a mystical toy mansion that is full of various rooms. Puzzles and puzzles await you in every room. Just like in previous games in the series, you have to go through many tests and learn the secrets of a tragic story. The Room 4: Old Sins is a re-release that includes numerous improvements, including character models and backgrounds. The developers have significantly redesigned the lighting and increased the resolution of shadows. The project began to sparkle with new colors and became even more exciting and deeper. Features of The Room 4: Old Sins: Dark Lovecraftian visual style; Challenging and inventive puzzles; Atmosphere of exploration and suspense; Improved textures, effects and gameplay; Puzzles based on interaction with the environment.

Go through creepy locations and follow not the most obvious clues, which are designed to make the passage easier. However, finding them is not so easy and they are only hints on the correct solution to the puzzle. Control mechanisms and interact with the environment. Uncover the mystery of Guildford Manor, located in the UK. An unforgettable mystical adventure awaits you, full of mysticism and mysteries.
