Game Description#

Doloc Town - this is a farm simulator in the conditions of post -apocalypse. The player accepts the role of a beginner whose task is to build an existing economy in the desert territory. The environment pushes to the study of its predominantly unexplored areas. Key activity includes the landing of cultures, the construction of the necessary structures and the consistent search for information about the past of this changed world. Therefore, the main goal is to cultivate the Earth and the movement among the ruins of the former civilization.

The collection of resources is the basis of the game cycle for the development of the farm in Doloc Town. The extracted materials are used for the manufacture of tools, cooking food and the construction of a variety of buildings in order to improve the venue. Contacts with various inhabitants of the settlement provide a chance to find out about the difficult past location. Directly the central settlement, peculiarly assembled from the surviving fragments of trains and sections of the collapsed bridges, operates as a significant node.

Dialogues with local residents, possibly with a joint meal, are able to open individual plot details and personal stories, closely intertwined with this area. Behind the line of the main settlement, a variable landscape extends for active movement extends. This area includes branched valleys, impassable swamps and many abandoned structures, the legacy of former inhabitants. Research activities are focused on the search for the necessary things, finding sheltered relics and other important discoveries in these areas.
