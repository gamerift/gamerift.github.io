Game Description#

The space simulatorMars First Logistics can be played online as a pirate for free, while exploring the surface of Mars with a friend. Using the Internet online, you can join forces with a partner to create space rovers capable of carrying huge loads, explore the area to collect resources, and much more. The possibility of free online play allows you to earn money together with a partner to open new components for constructing improved buildings or for creating updated equipment.

The online game includes a powerful 3D editor containing a huge number of different blocks, buildings and parts. Each player is able to select any object from the list for further construction. You can design servos, hydraulic cylinders, rocket engines, and many other objects. The goal is to allow Mars colonists to settle into their new planet. To do this, you need to deliver them valuable and heavy cargo. By improving technology and developing new technologies, it is possible to create more bizarre and powerful technologies that allow you to get into previously unexplored areas or deliver gigantic loads in a single trip. A huge open world with various biomes and unique resources in each region of the Red Planet is available for exploration.
