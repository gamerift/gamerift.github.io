Game Description#

Green Hell is a survival game that allows you to team up with a friend to play on a pirate for free online. It is possible to gather in a group online via the Internet to explore wild lands together, fighting difficulties and enemies. Alone or using the possibility of free online play, you need to survive in the vastness of the Amazon jungle. It is very important to maintain a stable psyche so that the hero does not go crazy, otherwise exhaustion and other consequences will follow.

You need to collect resources, engage in crafting and master survival methods in every possible way. It is required to create tools for self-defense and various weapons that help in survival. However, the main danger is not the enemies, but oneâ€™s own psyche, which becomes a real trap for the character. Online play will allow you to support your partner and help your friend stay sane.

Green Hell is a nightmare that is very difficult to even imagine. Wild animals, tropical diseases, obstacles, traps and much more await you at every turn. Only the familiar voice of a loved one makes you move forward and overcome all the difficulties along the way. The story is a real psychological thriller with unexpected plot twists. Green Hell gameplay features: hardcore survival in the wild; mechanics of making fires, setting up a camp, setting traps, etc.; searching for resources and various tools for survival; obtaining food, gathering, etc.; treatment illnesses, wounds, injuries and much more.
