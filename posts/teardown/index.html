Game Description#

Teardown is a robbery simulator in a variety of conditions prepared by indie developers from Tuxedo Labs. Stylistically, the project was made using a voxel graphic component. This solution made it possible to implement complete destructibility of all available objects in the location. This freedom opens up the possibility of performing operations in a variety of ways, improvising on the fly. The gamer will have at his disposal explosives and a sledgehammer for destroying all kinds of surfaces to penetrate the territory, as well as several types of vehicles of different sizes for loading valuables and other things that need to be stolen according to the terms of a particular task. Three game modes are available: Campaign – the plot will guide the user through the story of a young man who fell into serious debt. The protagonist is forced to accept any job offers, even if they are related to crime. The player will have to open safes, steal jewelry, cars, and paintings throughout all levels. Sandbox - the gamer personally chooses the map and tasks, receiving freedom of action, and the entire arsenal of a burglar available in the game. Creative - designing your own locations to implement interesting ideas. After completion of the work, it is possible to upload the finished map to a special catalog so that other people can try it. There you can download user creations to increase the available content.
