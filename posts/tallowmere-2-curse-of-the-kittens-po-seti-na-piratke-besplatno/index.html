Game Description#

Tallowmere 2: Curse of the Kittens is a platformer with the ability to play online with a friend, exploring dark catacombs and various intricate rooms. On the pirate Tallowmere 2: Curse of the Kittens, players can compete for free both online and locally. The story centers on Lady Tallumire’s kittens, who began to behave in very strange ways. The network game offers to take heroes under your leadership in order to clear the premises despite unusual circumstances.

The gameplay allows you to destroy and break everything around. You will need to survive, attack enemies, defend yourself, find keys and much more. Characters levels increase and new abilities are unlocked. The possibility of free online play allows you to both compete with each other and go through an adventure in co-op. By receiving new weapons, you can fight stronger enemies. To be treated you will need to use a variety of potions.

Levels, locations of objects and enemies are generated randomly. Difficulty and various modifiers are also randomly generated. At the beginning of the adventure, you can select a character by giving him starting parameters. The gameplay places a huge emphasis on looting. The lower you go in the dungeons, the more enemies await the heroes, but they drop improved loot. This way you can create custom unit builds. Up to four people can play. Local mode available on one screen.
