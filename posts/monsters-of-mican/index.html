Game Description#

Monsters of Mican is a step -by -step RPG with a first -person view based on the principles of party interaction, and often called Blobber. Drawing inspiration in classic role -playing games for PCs such as Might and Magic and Wizardry, the project along with traditional elements of gameplay gives modern capabilities of the quality of life. Users participate in strategic step -by -step battles, exploring the dungeons created by hand, filled with various monsters and secrets.

The story of Monsters of Mican is focused on the disclosure of the ancient origin of creatures that inhabit the setting, and each dungeon offers new tests and plot elements. More than 250 monsters are represented in Title, each of which has its own behavior, depending on almost 100 semi -duty signs. This creates a dynamic combat experience in which the gamer has to adapt his strategy.

The system of creating a character is very extensive and gives 14 classes, each of which has unique abilities and several passive skills that can be improved. Such a depth allows you to largely customize heroes, adjusting them to various styles. There are many NPCs, each of which contributes to the history of the world and offers additional tasks or difficulties. The user can also make friends with monsters: the system of “monsters friends” allows you to call these creatures into battle as allies. In addition to the main gameplay, various post-game tests are presented in Title.
