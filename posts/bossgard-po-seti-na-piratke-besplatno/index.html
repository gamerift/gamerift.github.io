Game Description#

Bossgard is a cooperative competition aimed at playing online with a friend, allowing you to enjoy all multiplayer modes for free on a pirate. Local play is available on a shared screen and an online connection via the Internet is available. Players need to take control of an army of Vikings to fight a huge boss. The network game is represented by asymmetric multiplayer, where 5 participants fight with 1 powerful monster. Entire teams are allowed to compete. A single playthrough is also available with the ability to set the difficulty level for bots.

The main question the adventure asks is: who is the boss? Having gathered as a company, everyone chooses a unique fighter with his own individual characteristics. There are several scary monsters to choose from, several times larger than the protagonists. 5 people challenge a powerful titan. You need to look for the enemy’s weaknesses. When playing as the boss, you should be wary of the Vikings, especially if they attack in a crowd. The gameplay is aimed at mutual assistance. It is necessary to cover your partners, heal, strengthen, protect, etc. The characters are represented by a variety of Vikings, be it Canadian, Jamaican, English and even Japanese!Features of Bossgard: single battle with bots; choice of difficulty during solo playthrough; the possibility of free online play; cooperation of up to 6 people with asymmetrical gameplay; a lot of content: bosses, Vikings, maps, traps and modes: local competition aimed at dueling confrontations.
