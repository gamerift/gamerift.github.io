Game Description#

GRIP Combat Racing is a racing simulator that provides the opportunity to play online with a friend on a pirate for free, driving a high-octane racing car. Races are available over the Internet online or locally, where each player takes control of a flying combat vehicle. You can attach a heavy arsenal of weapons to it to destroy your opponents during races. Online play allows you to compete in different modes, where the racers are given a variety of goals.

At the beginning of the race, each participant chooses his personal futuristic transport. Races take place at speeds of up to 1,225 kilometers per hour, requiring incredible reaction speed to avoid obstacles on floors, walls and ceilings. In total, GRIP Combat Racing features more than 29 interactive tracks in 4 unique worlds. The tracks represent unearthly cities, ice planets, etc. The free online play option includes competition for up to 10 players at a time.

You can install 9 different types of weapons and shields on the cars. The arsenal includes mines, missiles, explosive arrows, turbochargers and much more. Local battles allow you to compete in splitscreen mode. An arena is available where you need to demonstrate your own skills. The single-player campaign includes 19 races, the difficulty of which gradually increases. The fleet consists of 30 types of transport. All cars have unique characteristics and properties.
