Game Description#

Granblue Fantasy Versus is an anime fighting game that you can play online with a friend, compete for free on a pirate, or challenge other people online online. The gameplay contains JRPG elements. The setting is made in a fantasy style, where magic is combined with technology. By participating in battles, you can improve your fighter’s skills by unlocking new types of weapons, accessories, etc. Each fighter in Granblue Fantasy Versus is represented by unique moves and character. The gameplay is based on the classic mechanics of games such as GUILTY GEAR and BlazBlue.

Each character has several types of attacks, the ability to grab, block an attack from the enemy, and also deliver a crushing superblow. It is noteworthy that supers can be activated with one button. At the same time, the gameplay is quite hardcore. It requires careful study of each fighter. The online game is aimed at dynamic confrontations between living people. Before going into multiplayer, it is recommended to complete the single player campaign or training mode. Gameplay features: possibility of free online play; bright hand-drawn graphics in anime style; unique characters with individual move sheets; spectacular super attacks; complex and powerful combo attacks; rich lore and various locations for fights.
