Game Description#

The Last Plague: Blight is a survival simulator in the open world, which is generated randomly. The user controls one character and watches what is happening from above. The camera is tied to the hero. It is allowed to rotate. Indi-project uses medieval setting with a light fantasy raid.

The events of the Blight game occur in a world where there is a filth. This is the name of a mysterious disease. She absorbed the village of a protagonist and left only ruins from the settlement. The main character was able to escape, but he only won time for himself. This rot gradually grows, capturing a new territory every day. The former villain was alone in the desert, and hungry and tired. He has to find a way to end this ailment. He heard about the druids living in a distant country, which can have valuable information.

The developers rely on realism. The hero suffering from hunger and thirst can transfer only a few things at the same time. He is forced to boil water and cook food before use. And this process does not occur quickly. At the same time, the liquid must be stored in bowls and other containers. If it is inaccurately transported, then she can result in dishes. Trees cabin and other activity spend a supply of energy. It must be restored with the help of rest and sleep. It is also necessary to fight wild animals here and defend yourself from adverse weather conditions. There is an opportunity to crash objects, as well as engage in construction. There are many types of raw materials. These materials also need primary processing.
