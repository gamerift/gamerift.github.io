Game Description#

Plants vs. Zombies became the game of the year, winning in many categories. The developers invite us to once again plunge into battles in a small location in order to free ourselves from the shackles of various zombies, hold back all the ardor of the monsters and save the city by using the latest technologies in the agricultural industry. Among the primary weapons in strategy, we have a pea shooter, a cherry bomb and sunflowers, which we use to earn money to purchase new strategic weapons. In Plants vs. Zombies 50 levels, the setting of which varies from a night garden to the roof of a house with two-level protection from zombies. As you play, plants will be upgraded, upgraded and acquire new properties, and you can give them unique characteristics for increased damage or greater vitality.

Plants vs. Zombies is a product of the Tower Defense genre, the idea of ​​which is very simple - the player’s task is to correctly position towers that will serve as protection against successive waves of attacking opponents. This is how it looks here, the role of defensive turrets is played by plants, and the attackers are hordes of zombies. In practice, upholstery is much more difficult, mainly due to the limited resources that can be applied. Each stage begins by placing several sunflowers, which from time to time create new suns, which in turn can be used to purchase more plants. The developers have added 5 game modes: Story campaign. 50 levels in different locations with gradually improving enemies and plants that reflect them; Zen Garden. In this mode, we test and improve our weapons in the form of plants; Mini-games that give us bonuses and points for upgrading; Puzzles in which we have to actively work with our heads; Survival. The most difficult mode, because for the entire round we are given a limited number of defenders, and the number of zombies will decrease too slowly.

The gameplay of the game in the story campaign is built on the following principle. We put up sunflowers, from which we get some money. With the coins we earn, we buy plants, install them on an available cell (preferably closer to home) and shoot back at new waves. The game’s Almanac lists all the plants and zombies, so tactics and strategy are your initial responsibility to win. Of course, there are dozens of tower types in Plants vs Zombies, each at different prices and with unique abilities. In addition, you can also use several types that have special features. For example, potatoes force the enemy to spend some time to eat them, thus being attacked, and garlic holds the enemy, who moves him to the next line. The project implements a game store in which we spend non-game currency, which is easy to earn in any level.
