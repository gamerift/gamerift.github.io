Game Description#

SupRartold sends a student of the guild of soliders on a journey through the man -made toy world. The local lands suffer from storms from puzzles that create obstacles to the inhabitants. To pass the final exam, the protagonist must be saved by the princess from the castle. The main task can be ignored by giving preference to other activities in a huge, interconnected environment. The study of the world occurs with a miniature perspective, and each object pursues a certain gameplay goal, being deprived of unnecessary jewelry.

The study of locations in the SupRareld is built on the principles of metroide in the first person, and the promotion by the plot is tied to the search for abilities that open previously inaccessible regions. The project does not use invisible walls; The only barrier is physical geometry, which opens access to numerous secret areas. The game also represents detective affairs, offering to investigate 5 murders. The deduction system allows you to condemn any suspect, considered guilty of the results of the investigation.

Most of the time is devoted to the study of the environment, the solution of puzzles and detective work. Battles are less common and often include riddles. Title gives a minimum of tips, forcing the gamer to overcome difficulties on its own. This approach is focused on experienced connoisseurs of the genre, tired of standard adventures and seeking new challenges. The local world and its mechanics require a quick wit to navigate, and not followed by clear instructions.
