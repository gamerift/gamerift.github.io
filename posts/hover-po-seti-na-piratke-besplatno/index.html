Game Description#

Hover will appeal to parkour lovers who also like to play pirate games online with a friend. Here, the events take place in the high-tech city of Hover City, located on a distant planet. The Great Admin cut off access to the Galactic Union, introducing a harsh dictatorship. Fun is prohibited, and any kind of fun is prohibited. Users are encouraged to take on the role of rebels who fight for freedom and justice, destroying propaganda and helping residents. Another goal is to reach the Orbital Station, which is carefully guarded. You can do this either alone or with friends by organizing a session online.

Before starting gameplay in Hover, the player has the opportunity to customize the character by changing the color of his clothes. Next comes the starting segment, where you need to master the mechanics of movement - there are several sequential locations with obstacles and objects of different shapes. After this, you can move on to completing story missions. Each level takes gamers to different parts of the city, offering them to undergo different tests and perform elements of varying difficulty. Slums, highways, rooftops and other areas constantly throw up something new, forcing you to quickly make a decision. For the most part, online play has one goal - to score points by running through barely noticeable boundaries. You can move in either a first or third person view, depending on the player’s preferences. This does not change the meaning, and the dynamics of movement will remain the same. As for the characters, there are a total of 10 different personalities available with their own unique designs. Of course, they don’t open right away, but as you progress, you’ll be able to try them all out. And if the suggested concepts aren’t fun, you can create your own levels. The possibility of free online play allows you to distribute them to other gamers so that everyone can evaluate their strength and skill in passing.
