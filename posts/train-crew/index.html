Game Description#

Train Crew offers to take on the role of the driver on the fictional railway Tatehama Electric Railway. The gamer will have to drive the compositions on the route between the stations of Akaamacho and Tatehama, carefully observing the schedule and speed restrictions. The length of the available length of the track is 28 kilometers in both directions recreated within the framework of this project. The key task is to control the movement of the locomotive using special controls that imitate the real workplace.

It is necessary to monitor the signals of traffic lights and the condition of the paths to ensure safe movement. The Train Crew gameplay is built around the use of a special lever - the main controller, which serves as the main tool for interacting with the composition. For a set of speed, the handle is pulled on itself, and for braking - from itself; The development of this mechanism requires attention and accuracy. This method of interaction is the basis for navigation along the simulator railway tracks.

Additionally, the drivers of the compositions can use other functions: give a sound signal for warning, contact the conductor through a zummer to coordinate actions, as well as switch the types of camera for a better review of the environment and instruments in the cab. At the end of the flight, the system issues a detailed assessment of the driver’s actions, analyzing the accuracy of compliance with the schedule and smoothness. In addition to direct control of the locomotive, this title includes the tasks of the conductor, adding another layer to interaction.
