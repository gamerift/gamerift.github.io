Game Description#

Legends of Astravia Transes users to a world filled with magic - a world where Oliver, mystic with amnesia occupies the central place. This is a tribute to retro-role-playing games, invites to explore the mystical kingdom of Astravia. The solution to ancient secrets and battles with formidable enemies are only part of the adventure. Traveling around the vast world, reminiscent of the classic Zelda dungeons, gamers will find hidden corners and nooks, which are asked to be opened.

Do you think you have already seen everything? Think again - the charming landscape of Astravia is fraught with more surprises than a chest with facial expressions. But Oliver’s journey is not a single inspection of attractions. In the shade, a vile trinity is hidden, planning to untie the cataclysm and again immerse astral in ruins. Horror! Users will be knee -deep in intrigues, helping Oliver reveal his forgotten past, assemble a team of allies and prevent the world from falling apart at the seams.

Legends of Astravia is not just a revival of the golden age of role -playing games. The developers cleverly wove fresh mechanics, as a result of which a dynamic combat system was obtained, which would not allow gamers to break away from reality. This is a delightful mixture of the charm of an old school and innovative gameplay, an obligatory adventure for lovers of role -playing games, eager for new adventures. So take a sword, stock up on potions and go to explore the fascinating world of Astravia - just do not forget to check the secret corners for the presence of a loot!
