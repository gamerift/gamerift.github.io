Game Description#

Dinosaurs Dominion is a single -user adventure focused on territorial conquest. The player has to control the dinosaur for battles, evolution and gradual seizure of control over the environment. Progress in each passage is earned through the study of the world and victory in battle. The defeat returns the creature by the beginning of his journey, which is a key feature of Roguelite design. The choice of 14 accessible species, from agile scouts to massive predators, provides different approaches to this survival cycle.

Dinosaurs Dominion offers fights one on one, checking the player’s skill. Such battles require accurate calculation of time and distance, rewarding patience and punishing for reckless actions in risky duels. The world itself consists of 3 open cards, offering to study bioom unlike each other. The regions vary from forests to ash wasteland and snowy cliffs. The study of locations opens up ancient attractions, revealing the history of each place through the details of the environment and specific finds.

Although each attempt in this game is self -sufficient, long -term effects are preserved between several passages, affecting future races. A system of 13 available mutations allows you to specialize, developing a dinosaur to a specific approach to battle and research. These adaptations are opened to honing abilities before subsequent attempts of conquest. In addition, moving through bioms can lead to the detection of relics and echo. The presence of these items, along with unlocked totems and trophies, provides additional information about the world of Title.
