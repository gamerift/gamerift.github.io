Game Description#

In Dice Kingdoms, you can play with a friend online for free on a pirate, taking part in turn-based battles based on dice rolls. It is necessary to build a base, develop a kingdom, train an army and defend against enemy attacks. On the Internet online you can fight against other players or play in co-op. Having accumulated enough resources, you can build a wonder of the world. A huge island consisting of different biomes and resources is available for exploration. The online game Dice Kingdoms is built on the principle of dice rolls. When a certain number is rolled, you are given a chance to make a winning move or receive a certain amount of resources.

An important mechanic is disasters. They can either help in battles or vice versa - destroy a city or destroy crops. Natural disasters are determined by rolling the dice. Some types of disasters attack only the enemy, but there are also those that cause harm to both sides of the conflict. To get as many resources as possible, you need to attack enemy cities. Free online play allows you to join forces with a partner to fight against a common enemy. A victory is counted if the opponent’s king is defeated. Buildings must be built in such a way as to provide maximum protection to the city. It is necessary to do everything possible to anticipate the opponent’s strategy and prevent him from breaking through the defense. By rerolling the dice, you can gain additional resources, but you can also expose yourself to one of many natural disasters.
