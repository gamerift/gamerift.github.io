Game Description#

GreedFall II: The Dying World takes the beloved original game to new heights, offering an even more exciting and challenging adventure. The RPG takes place three years before its predecessor and takes players to the old continent of Gakan, where they take on the role of a native of Tir Fradi, a land filled with danger and corruption. Here survival and the fight for freedom and homeland become paramount. A ruthless man seeks to conquer everything with his formidable army, and you, as the protagonist, must rely on your skills, allies and cunning to navigate this treacherous world.

A vast and diverse universe beckons to be explored, with new locations awaiting you, such as the bustling city of Olima and the picturesque coastline of Uxantis. Along your journey, you will encounter various factions, each with their own unique agenda and culture. Diplomacy, deception, infiltration, and combat are just a few of the ways you can interact with these factions, and the choices you make will have significant consequences both on the world and on your relationships with your companions.

The main highlight of GreedFall 2 is the character creation system, allowing players to fully customize their character’s appearance, skills and equipment. In addition, you can recruit up to four companions, each with their own personalities, tasks and potential romances. You’ll have the opportunity to help your companions overcome personal problems, delve into their stories, and even take control of them during battles.
