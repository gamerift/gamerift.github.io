Game Description#

On the pirateClash of Chefs VR you can play online for free with a friend, participating in cooking competitions. Multiplayer is available on the Internet online and offers both to join forces and take part in various confrontations for the title of best chef. Online play and single-player mode consisting of 80 stages are available. The possibility of free online play allows up to 4 people to compete simultaneously.

It is necessary to deal with cutting ingredients, frying, boiling, assembling and serving dishes. In Clash of Chefs VR you can throw leftovers at your competitors and play various tricks on them. It takes every effort to achieve the title of the best chef in the world. To receive bonus points and unlock new abilities, you need to place orders as quickly as possible. Satisfied customers increase their reputation and generate demand for the establishment. Subsequently, complex recipes are discovered that make even professional chefs sweat.

The action takes place in a classic American burger joint. It is necessary to fry cutlets, throw potatoes into deep fat, chop cucumbers and tomatoes, grease buns with sauce, etc. High speed, accuracy and concentration will be required. Any mistake can cost you time, which will lead to disastrous consequences. Also available is a Japanese shack, a pizzeria, a Mexican restaurant and many other levels with unique dishes. If the client was rude or the other cook was able to infuriate, then you can throw food or a plate right in his face. The main feature of the gameplay is letting off steam. It is possible to cause complete destruction in the establishment, breaking and throwing surrounding objects at opponents.
