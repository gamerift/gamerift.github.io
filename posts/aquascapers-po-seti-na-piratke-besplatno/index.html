Game Description#

Aquascapers offers the opportunity to play online and enjoy online gaming for free. You and a friend can create underwater masterpieces in sandbox mode, where all the tools and techniques for terraforming and modifying your aquascape are available online. On Pirate, you can also explore the depths together, striving to create the perfect rock formation through cooperative interaction.

Challenge yourself with the similarity rating feature. Here you will be presented with a real aquascape and tasked with replicating it down to the smallest detail. Every decision, from choosing plants to placing stones, matters to achieve the perfect result.

Ready to turn your hobby into a business? Aquascapers offers two different paths. Complete projects for clients to earn a stable income and increase your reputation. Higher-end clients demand more complex and visually striking aquascapes, forcing you to flex your design skills. Or you can create your own dream aquascape and put it on the open market. Bidders with different preferences will evaluate your work, and you can choose the most advantageous offer.

The Aquascapers story is one of growth. Start small - explore the world in search of natural resources and create aquascapes from scratch. As you progress, you’ll be able to unlock features like breeding and growing, which will allow you to increase your fish and plant stock and work on larger projects faster. The ultimate goal? The coveted S-level license and the ability to create paludariums - breathtaking aquascapes that combine elements of land and water. So grab your tools, dive in and let your creativity run wild!
