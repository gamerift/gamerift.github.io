Game Description#

Battlefield Hardline gives you the opportunity to compete online online, participating in various multiplayer modes. For free, you can play pirate online with a friend or enter into confrontations with random people from all over the world. The Battlefield Hardline network game is represented by such disciplines as Hijacking, Blood Money, Robbery, Capture and Team Fight. The additions are Crime, Robbery, Darkness, Escape and Betrayal.

The gameplay is a tactical first-person shooter. Battlefield Hardline is the 13th game in the series of the same name. The free play feature includes a tense showdown between two teams. Participants need to commit a bank robbery. One side takes on the role of the cops, while the other plays as the robbers. During the raid, criminals will have to jump into a car and rush through the city streets. The police players must chase them. The developers have included several classic multiplayer modes for veterans of the franchise. Up to 64 people can take part in multiplayer competitions at the same time.

The game includes several additional sets, such as Versatile, Precision and Suppression. You can use 12 unique golden battle sets, which will greatly help in confrontation and personalization of the hero. The main plot revolves around a Miami officer named Nick Mendoza. He and Hai Ming Dao are investigating a complicated case related to drug trafficking. The heroes will have to face corrupt officials and corrupt police officers from their department. The story consists of 10 chapters. You will need to collect evidence and fight crime using a huge variety of guns and auxiliary equipment.
