Game Description#

The House of the Dead 2: Remake is a rail shooter where gamers in the role of secret agents of James or Gary cleans the city. This game offers a single passage or local cooperative regime for the destruction of hordes of hostile creatures that block the path. The first -person gameplay immerses the operatives in cruel clashes with the hostile inhabitants of the metropolis. The main goal is to survive and find out the source of the flash, moving through the captured streets and buildings.

The House of the Dead 2: Remake recreates a classic gameplay with modern improvements. The campaign retains the structure with branched levels, and the choice of the route depends on specific actions, such as the salvation of civilians, which directly affects the development of the plot and leads to several endings. Particular attention is paid to joint passage, where agents need to cover each other for survival during attacks from all sides. In addition, sound accompaniment was completely processed, but for the original connoisseurs the ability to turn on the classic soundtrack is preserved.

In addition to the storyline, the project includes new formats to expand experience for each player. A special mode suggests fighting with all the bosses for a while, turning into a competition for the best result in the leadership table and pushing to find optimal tactics. A training section with several scenarios is also added, created to honing accuracy, reaction speed and resource management in controlled conditions. This mode allows you to prepare for the challenges of the main mission.
