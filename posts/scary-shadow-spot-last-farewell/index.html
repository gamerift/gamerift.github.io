Game Description#

Scary Shadow Spot: Last Farewell is a first-person psychological horror game that explores themes of grief, guilt, and the paranormal. Players take on the role of Mark, a man coping with the recent loss of his wife Jane. The game begins with Mark returning to his and Jane’s old country house to prepare for her funeral. However, what is meant to be a grim homecoming quickly turns into a terrifying ordeal.

As Mark goes deeper into the house, he encounters a number of disturbing phenomena. Strange sounds echo through the corridors, doors creak open on their own, and shadowy figures lurk in the corners. The house, once filled with memories of happiness, now exudes an unsettling aura of horror. To combat the growing darkness, Mark must seek out rosaries and crucifixes, using them to pray and ward off the evil forces that haunt the place.

Besides the supernatural elements, Scary Shadow Spot: Last Farewell also presents players with a series of twisting puzzles. These puzzles, developed by a psychologist, serve as a therapeutic tool to help Mark deal with his grief and guilt. By solving these problems, Mark will be able to uncover hidden places in the house and discover clues about the tragic events that led to Jane’s death. The game maintains a constant tension, forcing players to stay on the edge of the possible.
