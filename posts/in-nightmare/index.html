Game Description#

In Nightmare allows gamers to plunge into a dark atmosphere where they have to take control of a boy who has lost his beloved. The protagonist will make his way through dangerous locations where monsters live. The project is a horror adventure game with elements of an action platformer, where you need to overcome various obstacles on the hero’s path, solve puzzles, deal with monsters and look for the right path out of the frightening kingdom of darkness.

In In Nightmare, the protagonist escapes reality by disappearing into an eerie dream. To get out of a dangerous place, the boy has to go through a large number of obstacles. It is necessary to overcome landscape obstacles, deal with difficult logical thinking tasks, interact with interactive interior objects, lift objects and combine them with each other, and much more. It is necessary to open passages to new levels of the single-player campaign, which will gradually become more difficult. During the passage, users need to:

Move through dark corridors and rooms in which you can control the spirit of sleep. Face nightmares that disturb the protagonist’s peaceful dream life. Destroy monsters that were generated by the inflamed consciousness of a boy who wants to find his true love. Overcome the character’s fear that has been tormenting him for a long time.
