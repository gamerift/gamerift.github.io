Game Description#

Ori and the Will of the Wisps is an adventure platformer with stunning hand-drawn graphics. You will find yourself in distorted lands that you must save from evil and overcome all the obstacles prepared by fate for the main character.

You will take control of the little spirit Ori, who knows a lot about the obstacles and pitfalls of this world. Your owl friend Ku is in trouble after another flight. After this, you have to find your friend and save her, as well as free the surrounding lands from evil.

You have a ton of different abilities at your disposal, thanks to which you will overcome obstacles and fight enemies. Ori can jump high and far to overcome chasms. Use all your magical skills and powerful weapons in boss battles.

All locations have a unique theme of a certain world. They also have an incredibly beautiful design and special effects in the background. You will fight against huge monsters and small squads of opponents, and also solve puzzles to get to the owl and gradually liberate the lands.

Courage alone is not enough to explore the vast expanses of the game world. Visit dungeons, on top of the planet, and even in lava locations. Show your dexterity, resourcefulness and make quick decisions in difficult situations. Fight against the most powerful enemies of this world and do everything to win the war.
