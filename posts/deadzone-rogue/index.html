Game Description#

Deadzone: rogue immerses the gamer into events on board the ISS-X vessel, located in deep space, where the protagonist recovers without memories. Faced with the upcoming legions of hostile vehicles, the character is forced to enter into battles from the first person in the compartments of the ship, from machine departments to the Kardzer. Survival depends on the movement of these locations and collisions with numerous mechanical opponents.

The basis of the gameplay includes repeated cycles on the captured spaceship. In Deadzone: Rogue, you have to move along the cycle determined by battle, death, revival and subsequent attempts, which is characteristic of the structure of the route. Each entry provides opportunities to obtain equipment and meetings with the swarms of drones, spiders robots, brainless entities and dozens of other types of machines. The confrontation of large biomechanical bosses requires special combat skills and reaction speeds to achieve success.

Progress means gradual strengthening after each failure, preparing for the next clashes inside the hostile vessel. The combat system of this title gives control over the arsenal, numbering more than 30 basic guns, each of which can be modified using spontaneous effects and various improvements. For example, a shotgun can be changed to control the crowd with the help of emptiness energy, or a submachine gun to reinforce for cleaning the premises with ice shells. Available weapons, including plasma guns and sniper rifles, allows you to adapt to various combat situations.
