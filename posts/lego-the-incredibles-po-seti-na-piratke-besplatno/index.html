Game Description#

LEGO The Incredibles is an action game based on the cartoon of the same name, The Incredibles, which you can play online with a friend. Allows you to go through a co-op campaign or play in other multiplayer modes for free on a pirate. You can connect online online or fight with your partner locally. The story centers on the Parr family. Each member of the super family has their own unique abilities.

The environment is a world made in Lego style. Single player campaign and online play available. The plot is based on how the main characters fight various bandits. The script and characters of LEGO The Incredibles are represented by two cartoons of the same name. A huge world filled with various quests, hiding places, boosts and bonuses is available for exploration. Free online play allows you to visit Municeburg and New Urbem alone or with a partner.

All protagonists have individual superpowers, be it super strength, the ability to stretch, become invisible, etc. You need to use your skills to catch the villains and put them in prison. Multiplayer mode is designed for two players. As you progress, you can unlock various characters from other Pixar films. Among them are heroes from such franchises as Toy Story, Coco Mysteries and many others.
