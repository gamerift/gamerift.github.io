Game Description#

Treasure ‘n Trio offers challenges that only a team of three adventurers can overcome. A warrior relies on brute force, while a nimble thief passes many dangers unharmed. The wizard in the squad has teleportation magic, which opens up new paths and allows you to change position. The gamer will have to alternately control three heroes, because none of them alone will be able to solve the riddles. Their coordinated actions are needed to interact with the environment and collect unusual treasures.

The gameplay of Treasure ‘n Trio is based on turn-based puzzles unfolding on a cellular field. All actions are sequential: the gamer moves objects step by step to the desired points and activates various mechanisms. Progress through the stage depends on the correct order of using the characters’ unique abilities. Interaction with the environment is the key to overcoming the logical barriers of each level, which requires the player to be able to plan ahead.

The journey takes place through many worlds, each with its own theme and new puzzle elements. Locations vary from forests to dangerous, lava-filled zones, introducing unique enemies and mechanics in each new area. In addition to the main objective, the game has optional side quests for additional skill testing. These difficult modes return you to already completed levels, but with special conditions, which require completely different approaches and a high level of tactics.
