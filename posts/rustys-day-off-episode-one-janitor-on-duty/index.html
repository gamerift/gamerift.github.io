Game Description#

Rusty’s Day Off: Episode One - Janitor on Duty offers an original take on the first-person shooter (FPS) genre. The gamer will have to step into the shoes of Rusty, a janitor who is tasked with sorting out a situation at a workplace where a malfunction has occurred due to rogue robots. It’s not just running and gunning, there’s more to it than that. You’ll have to solve puzzles, interact with strange gadgets, and figure out how to stay alive while coping with mechanical chaos. Rusty doesn’t just mop floors - he puts out fires, both literally and figuratively, as he moves around the environment he’s captured.

The core gameplay of Rusty’s Day Off: Episode One - Janitor on Duty combines familiar FPS mechanics with something a little more interesting. Rusty’s arsenal includes things like plasma weapons and boxing gloves that bounce around the room, as well as a hoverboard to navigate the levels. These are not standard FPS tools that make every encounter more creative. Add to this the non-linear level design, and it becomes clear that no two playthroughs of the game will ever be the same.

Whether you’re solving puzzles to open doors or outsmarting rogue robots, there’s always something to think about. This is the kind of game where you never know what will happen next, and this unpredictability forces you to be on your guard. As you progress, you’ll encounter a variety of quirky characters, some of whom can help you along the way, while others can throw a wrench into your plans.
