Game Description#

GRAV is a sandbox that you can play online with a friend while exploring the vast expanses of space. On the pirate route you can visit various planets for free, traveling across continents alone or with friends online. Each planet is highlighted by biomes and creatures. The gameplay allows you to collect items, build a base, hunt monsters and much more. Multiplayer allows you to both cooperate with other survivors and declare enmity.

An intense PvP mode is presented, in which battle tactics and strategy decide. You can survive alone or form groups to fight random players from all over the world. GRAV requires you to engage in close combat or use numerous long-range guns - the free multiplayer option does not limit the participants in any way and encourages an inventive approach to survival. By defeating your opponents, you need to collect loot and various jewelry from them. They can be sold profitably by buying new guns and upgrades.

Collected materials can be used for crafting. Different types of armor, auxiliary items, weapons and much more are created. Construction allows you to build an entire city and create your own colony. The toolkit contains a huge number of blocks and modules. Having built your outpost, you need to think over a defense system by installing walls, turrets, towers, etc. The environment and game situations are randomly generated. Planets contain a wide variety of dungeons, enemies, quests and rewards. Areas are divided into safe and hostile. You have to make your way through the entire galaxy to find your place in the universe.
