Game Description#

Merge Maestro suggests placing an emoji chip on a mesh field. These elements are combined with others of the same type to create stronger and more high -level units. The main task is to overcome the advancing waves of opponents using these improved tokens. After each round, Gamer chooses a new element for its set, expanding tactical opportunities for future clashes. Such a cycle of placement and fusion forms a key game process that requires step -by -step solutions to clean the field and survival.

The strategic depth of Merge Maestro is provided by a collection of more than 300 different tokens with unique effects. In the process of passing, you can get radically changing the gameplay of the ability from a set of 50 forces introducing mechanics for a fundamental change in the situation on the board. All components are grouped in 10 thematic sets with increasing complexity, which makes each session unique. The combination of tokens and strength opens access to thousands of combinations, encouraging tactical adaptation with each new game and making each passage unique.

To overcome the 6th modes of complexity and 20 tests that change the rules of this project, it is necessary to adapt your approach. Working tactics include the construction of a city that causes periodic damage to opponents. Another way is to combine food and drinks to turn chips into more powerful forms. Another opportunity is to create an army through the destruction of their own units returning to the field in the form of a ruthless horde of the dead and forming a constant attacking potential.
