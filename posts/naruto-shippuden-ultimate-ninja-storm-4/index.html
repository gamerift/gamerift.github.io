Game Description#

Familiar to many fans of the Naruto series,NARUTO SHIPPUDEN: Ultimate Ninja STORM 4 transcends the fighting game genre, taking the game to the next level. In the final part, we will find out who will win in the confrontation between Naruto and Sasuke. We will learn a lot of details about the 4th Shinobi War, and also go over some aspects of the anime that were missed in previous parts. New graphics have been implemented for players, allowing them to play in wide resolution and at frame rates of up to 60 FPS without purchasing an add-on.

Nevertheless, the developers have released a sufficient number of additions, but they reveal the costumes for the characters, and nothing more. Basically we will see the battles of the real bigwigs of history. Hashirama will lead the shinobi into war against Madara Uchiha. How will this war end? It’s up to you to decide. Fans of the series will appreciate the graphic design from the developers who went deep into creating a game based on the anime, so in addition to successfully building a fighting game between characters, bosses and heroes, we will see glimpses of large-scale battles between representatives of the strongest shinobi classes! We will be able to combine many new techniques, make unique combinations, and play for any shinobi that catches our eye. The developers have added multiplayer and cooperative modes to the game. It’s an honor for a fan to fight each other in an interesting fighting game in the Naruto universe!
