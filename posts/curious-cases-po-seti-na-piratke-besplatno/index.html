Game Description#

Curious Cases is a first-person horror game with puzzle elements that you can play online with a friend, together trying to solve the mystery of an ancient estate. A co-op is available for free on the pirate site, allowing you to play online online from different devices. The project is designed for virtual reality headsets and supports devices such as Valve Index, HTC Vive and Oculus Rift. The possibility of free online play includes the joint passage of 3 chapters of the story campaign.

The story tells about detective Thomas Moore. The hero is contacted when the investigation reaches a dead end. Only he can assemble a talented team capable of solving any puzzle. He receives a case about the disappearance of a famous scientist. This happened at a time when he was on the verge of an important discovery. Who is behind his kidnapping? And what kind of statement did he want to make since he became someone’s victim? Too many questions and so few answers. We have to solve one of the most difficult mysteries of the century. Network game Curious Cases: the ability to assemble a team of up to 6 people; joint solving of puzzles, finding evidence, reading notes and constant interaction between participants; there is a Versus mode that allows you to compete in a duel or in teams; quest room mode, where required escape from the room before your opponents.
