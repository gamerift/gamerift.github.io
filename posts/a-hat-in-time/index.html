Game Description#

In the 3D platformerA Hat in Time we will have to play as a girl who both looks and is called Hat Girl. She goes on an interplanetary journey to return home, but ends up in the city of the Mafia. At this location, a Mafioso gets into our ship and completely inspects the ship, but after checking, he scatters “time particles”, and then spreads them throughout the entire Mafia planet. Additionally, having collected a fee from the Hat Girl for crossing state space, we are led to the Mustache Girl, who becomes the antagonist of the game.

The whole process is based on collecting “time particles” that should help us return home, but remember that while traveling through the levels, we will encounter enemies that we will have to defeat with our own strength and skills. No one will help little Hat Kid.

The game has open levels that we can explore without a time limit. In the locations there are conveniently located objects with which we will kill enemies, solve puzzles and logic problems. One of the Hat Girl’s weapons will be an umbrella, which will deal the most damage to enemies.

The gameplay of the game is based on a standard platformer, only in a 3D shell. We’ll set off to conquer levels, but what makes A Hat in Time special is the fact that you can visit the same location several times before completing the story. This way, every action you take will appear as a problem or a nice bonus in the future.
