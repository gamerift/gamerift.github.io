Game Description#

Heroes of Hammerwatch is a top-down roguelike that allows you to play online with a friend, or on a pirate game you can cooperate with other participants for free using an online connection. You need to fight your way through a huge variety of enemies while exploring procedurally generated levels. The online game invites up to four people to team up to confront universal evil.

Setting out on an adventure, the heroes will have to face an endless crowd of a wide variety of enemies, avoid traps, solve puzzles, solve puzzles and much more. A huge emphasis is placed on loot and a variety of weapons. Random generation increases the replayability of the story campaign several times. Taking advantage of free online play, the heroes must do everything possible to reach the top of the Forsaken Spire. Features of Heroes of Hammerwatch: unique content, randomly generated; progress saved during the next race; the ability to improve the city with 10 buildings; 7 classes with unique abilities; different play styles for each character; 100 types of items to collect; new game+ and challenge mode.

The gameplay combines RPG, action and roguelike mechanics. Each subsequent race can guarantee that you can improve your skills and overall performance. When increasing the level, you need to give the heroes enhanced weapons and equipment. Allows you to purchase new abilities and magic spells.
