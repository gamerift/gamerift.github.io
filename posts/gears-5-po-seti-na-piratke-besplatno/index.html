Game Description#

Gears 5 is a third-person shooter designed for online co-op play and allowing you to play for free on a pirate account with a friend online, fighting together against an army of enemies in a story campaign. There are 4 modes in total: Horde, Escape and Confrontation and Story Adventure. The network game Gears 5 allows you to team up with up to 3 people. You will have to fight monsters using fictional weapons, including guns, grenades, equipment for close confrontation, etc.

You are allowed to take with you 2 types of firearms, one auxiliary pistol and 1 type of grenades. Locations allow you to pick up heavy weapons. Each gun uses a unique type of ammunition. Ammunition rises from the bodies of the dead. The possibility of free online play includes constant interaction between players: it is necessary to cover partners, share supplies, shoot together from behind cover, and much more.

Blind shooting mechanics are available and health regeneration is present. The story centers on Kate Diaz and her friends and partners, including Delmont Walker and the robot Jack. The plot of Gears 5 consists of several acts. Allows you to freely explore vast locations and complete secondary tasks. Horde is a match where you need to survive 50 waves. Escape is a timed mode where you need to escape from the advancing poisonous gas. For successful completion, points are awarded for upgrading passive and active skills.
