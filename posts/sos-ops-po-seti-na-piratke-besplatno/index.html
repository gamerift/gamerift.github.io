Game Description#

SOS OPS! provides free online play. In this multiplayer co-op game, you can play online with friends or with anyone online. Play for free and enjoy teamwork while solving funky physics-based puzzles. Defend the city, rescue cats and complete hilarious rescue missions, all in the world of emergency response. SOS OPS! promises to give you moments of laughter along with the opportunity to play online and pirated.

Inspired by popular physics-based sandboxes such as Gang Beasts and Human: Fall Flat, SOS OPS! brings a unique twist by putting gamers in the shoes of dedicated rescuers. The project offers a variety of scenarios - from disaster relief to responding to everyday emergencies, which forces you to be on alert. But it’s not all seriousness: the game cleverly uses parody humor, references to popular films, TV shows and even video games, adding an extra layer of entertainment for pop culture fans.

Teaming up with friends online or locally is the key to success! Users can customize their characters with a variety of gear and accessories, making them truly their own. A diverse selection of vehicles and tools further expands the options, allowing you to complete every mission with the right gear. To prolong the pleasure. Developed by Ukrainian indie studio TryMyGames and published by ArtDock, SOS OPS! is the debut game for both companies.
