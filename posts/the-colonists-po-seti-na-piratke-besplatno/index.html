Game Description#

The Colonists is an exciting online game where you can play online for free with a friend on a pirate. In the game you have to control a team of robots, created in the image of human civilization, who went in search of a new home in the galaxy. You’ll build settlements by guiding robots through three different eras. The game offers a variety of modes, including a campaign, randomly generated maps, and a sandbox where you can build without restrictions. The Colonists also allows you to enjoy the gameplay online over the Internet.

In Campaign mode, players can choose between peaceful expansion and military objectives. Peace mode focuses on building sprawling settlements, while War mode focuses on competitive elements against AI colonies. Players must manage resources, build infrastructure, and research new technologies to help their colony prosper. Roads, boats and trains play a vital role in transporting resources and connecting different parts of the colony.

The game also offers extensive customization options, including a map editor and support for Steam Workshop, where players can create and share their own maps. Monthly challenges and speedrun trophies add an extra layer of replay value. Whether it’s a recreational game or a competitive game, The Colonists offers a variety of gaming options for different playstyles.
