Game Description#

You can playRampage Knights online either with a friend in co-op or with random players, teaming up on a pirate ship or fighting online online in PvP mode. All online features are available for free, including numerous heroes, equipment, weapons and modes. The gameplay is a two-dimensional beat-em-up, made in a dark fantasy setting. The campaign can be played alone, fighting numerous monsters, but by joining forces with a partner, the free multiplayer option opens up many additional mechanics, allowing the heroes to interact and solve puzzles together.

The gameplay features roguelike elements, generating locations, situations, loot and enemies randomly. You have to explore the expanses of a gloomy forest, a ruined castle and dark dungeons. The protagonists fight goblins, skeletons, trolls and other fantasy creatures. The project contains a huge amount of absurdity and absurdities. The characters skillfully wield melee and ranged weapons, and also use magic. As you progress, the power increases and the equipment becomes more powerful. Powerful bosses are present. Key features of Rampage Knights: action inspired by Golden Ax and Binding of Isaac; single and online play; random generation of game elements; hero development and leveling; more than 10 types of spells and weapons; class system with the ability to unlock new ones; black and absurd humor.
