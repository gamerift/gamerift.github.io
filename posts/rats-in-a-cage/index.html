Game Description#

Rats in a Cage proposes to solve puzzles in the form of a corporate certification. The player will manipulate colleagues, strategically placing coffee or spreading gossip, changing their routes and assigning other people’s merits to himself. The whole process oversees artificial intelligence, actively interfering with passage and sometimes interacting with elements outside the game window.

Such a gameplay constantly experiences the willingness to sacrifice others for the sake of personal benefits and career growth. In Rats in A Cage, other mechanics reflect a immoral office policy. You can block the way to colleagues, shifting the deadlines, or ignore and prohibit uncomfortable letters. Such actions are the key to solving tasks. To achieve success, the project forces to resort to corporate tricks: from stacking and humiliation to smell before the authorities.

These techniques are required for promoting the career ladder in the framework of this game. The main content includes 42 tasks, 3 Interjro and the same bonus mini-level. A special relationship is built with the control of AI: he uses non -standard methods, repelling the desire to pass his introductory test. The main task is to get the position, showing the ability to mercilessly intrigue. The final triumph depends on the ability to use the system and its inhabitants for the sake of increasing and superiority over competitors.
