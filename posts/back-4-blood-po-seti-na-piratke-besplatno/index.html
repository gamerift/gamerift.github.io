Game Description#

MultiplayerBack 4 Blood allows you and a friend to plunge into post-apocalyptic battles against the Ridden, monsters born from a deadly parasite, for free. On the brink of human survival, only you and your friends can restore order to the world. Play online with up to three allies or go into battle as a pirate, commanding an AI squad in an ever-changing, dangerous world.

Back 4 Blood is not just about destroying hordes of the undead. Strategy is key. You’ll choose one of eight playable Purifiers, each with unique skills and a huge arsenal of weapons and equipment. The enemies are not soulless either: Riddles are constantly adapting, throwing new threats at you.

Want a change of pace? B4B offers competitive multiplayer. You can team up with friends in a squad of Cleaners or take control of Riddle yourself. Both sides have unique abilities and weapons, making each match unpredictable.

Think you’ve already mastered the game? Think again. The rogue-lite card system throws up new surprises every time you play. Create your own decks, experiment with different strategies and face ever-increasing challenges. No two playthroughs will be the same. The Director, an ingenious artificial intelligence system, constantly adapts to your actions. Expect exciting combat, varied gameplay, and terrifying boss battles against Riddens so large they make a two-story house seem quaint.
