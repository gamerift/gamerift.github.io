Game Description#

Monstrum 2 is a survival horror game that you can play online on a pirate with a friend or other players from around the world. Having connected online for free over the Internet, you need to fight a powerful monster in a cooperative of up to four people. Events take place at the remote sea station of Sparrowlock, which acts as a kind of prison: a huge number of labyrinths, tiers, obstacles, etc. It was once the huge research center of Hong-Sha Miller. Free online play also includes monster control. The fifth participant chooses one of several powerful creatures with unique abilities. Depending on the choice of monster, the tactics and dynamics of the gameplay change.

Online play involves an asymmetrical type of multiplayer gameplay. A huge monster has an advantage in strength, but people can work together, use the environment for personal gain and use cunning. Metal labyrinths are procedurally generated, so matches are never repeated twice: the layout of rooms changes, objects for interaction are moved, puzzles are randomized, etc.

You can move in the shadows, hiding from the monster, avoid traps, use cover so as not to attract attention, and much more. You cannot survive in open battle against a strong creature. The survivors must come up with a strategy to take the powerful creature by surprise. Playing as a giant killer, you can destroy objects, crawl along walls, teleport, create traps, etc. It is important to hunt down victims one by one in order to brutally tear them to pieces.
