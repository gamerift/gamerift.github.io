Game Description#

We Are Football 2024 is a football simulator in which you can create a team of your dreams in MyFaction, collecting cards with your favorite superstars and legends. Compete on the network with your friends or go up the rating staircase of a quick game, getting exclusive awards. Universe mode is a real sandbox. Create rivals, plan weekly shows and even become a witness to completely new scenes. Matches for double titles add another level of strategy to your reservation solutions. Take into your hands the reins of government by the largest brands in the field of sports entertainment in Mygm game.

Compete with general managers of rivals, control your composition and do everything to become a leading player. Explore more types of matches, brands and options for the general manager than ever before. For those who strive for international glory, separate regimes of the European Championship and the World Cup for men and women are provided. Lead the national team or receive offers to manage it during your career. Watch what is happening on the field using a 3D image of the match with various camera angles.

Watch how your tactics come to life and analyze the results using the new function “Tactical analysis”. Introduce the matches to make fateful decisions and influence the outcome. We Are Football 2024 will appeal to customization lovers. Edit leagues, teams and players around the world. Create logos and t -shirts and even integrate your own graphics. The game also provides for the possibility of automatic conversion of existing WAF databases. If you were an experienced football manager or beginner, the gameplay offers exciting and comprehensive experience, allowing you to pave your own path to football fame.
