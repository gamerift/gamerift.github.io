Game Description#

The city-planning simulator -Cities: Skylines - is in the best traditions of the real game, in which its own physical laws were created to transfer them into gaming reality. On the territory of your new city, it will be taken into account that the profile road is built in one way, and the road at the airport - in another. It will count if you correctly lay the pipeline for the heating system, and also if you can show people that they live in a real paradise, and not just a metropolis where they want to buy them at the expense of shopping centers. The realism of this city-planning simulator is off the charts, and the developers achieved this due to the following factors.

The city will not function if you only created an education system or were able to supply the city with gas, leaving it without water. Only the complete picture is considered complete, so without gas, water, roads, grocery stores and other aspects of the city (we do not take into account the quality of the houses built) - people will not live in your city.

Traffic flows consist of a clear plan, so any change in a city road without being included in the register of the road construction law can take the overall system out of proper structure. Plan everything from the first pedestrian crossing to turn times for trucks. Also in the game there is a time of day that changes like in real life: day and night. The time of day greatly affects the productivity of citizens, areas are empty, no one drives on the roads. Use this fact to quickly get closer to the desired position of mayor. The player will be able to restore old buildings, give gifts to residents, organize rallies and holidays. Do everything to become the new mayor of this city!
