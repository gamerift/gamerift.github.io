Game Description#

Shin Megami Tensei V: Vengeance throws you headlong into a deserted Tokyo. Remember that school life? Gone. It was replaced by Daath - a nightmare kingdom infested with demons. You, an ordinary teenager, in one minute turn into Nakhobino - non-human and non-demon. With a mysterious savior at your side, you navigate the treacherous landscape of Da’ath, caught between warring gods and demonic tyrants vying for dominance.

This expanded re-release offers a buffet of options. Do you want a classic war of angels against demons with the weight of the world on your shoulders? The “Canon of Creation” awaits you. Craving a fresh story? Dive into the “Canon of Vengeance.” Here, revenge comes to the fore, and new characters and the mysterious demons of Kadishtu make their contribution. Whatever path you choose, be prepared to confront your beliefs. Every decision determines the fate of humanity in this post-apocalyptic chaos.

Exploration in Shin Megami Tensei V: Vengeance becomes a necessity as you journey through the ruins of Tokyo. Open spaces will stretch before you, teeming with familiar and nightmarish demons. Keep an eye out for life-saving items - you’ll need them. What about demons? They can be allies too. You just need to conclude the right agreement. Revenge or creation - the choice is yours. But remember that in the demon-infested wasteland of Daath, every decision has consequences.
