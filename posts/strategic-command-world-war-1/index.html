Game Description#

Strategic Command: World War I is a step -by -step strategy about the First World War. Events unfold since 1914. The right to choose a party is given, the game offers various ways to develop a combat script. You can start a war, enter into an alliance, put pressure. A very important role in the game is played by the player’s strategy. A long list of weapons is also presented to the choice and in order to win, you must safely use it.

Overwhelm the enemy by using a competent diplomatic position, or with the help of political intrigues, destroy the Union of countries. The game allows you to feel the commander in chief, on which the fate of the world depends. The war is required to win at all costs. Mobilize troops, develop tactics, use strategies in battle, use new types of weapons, including tanks, aircraft, aircraft carriers, submarines.

Try to go to desperate steps, creating the largest sea blockade to weaken the enemy. Check whether to realize Paris by attacking from Belgium? What will happen to Russia because of its political instability and how can this be used in attack? The game uses an advanced engine, as in previous versions of the game, which makes the game possible for both sides. The outcome of this war will depend only on your commander’s skill.
