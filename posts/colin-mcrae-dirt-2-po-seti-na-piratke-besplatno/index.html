Game Description#

Colin McRae: DiRT 2 is a racing simulator that offers the opportunity to play online with a friend, competing in various online championships. The pirate site has all the multiplayer content for free, allowing you to participate in competitions around the world, including Asia, Europe, Africa and North America. Single player campaign, online play, co-op and World Tour mode are available.

Free online play includes up to 8 players. Ranked matches (Pro Tour) and unranked races (Jam Session) are available. The fleet consists of cars of different classes. Depending on the championship, it is necessary to use only a certain type of transport. Colin McRae: DiRT 2 features many licensed cars, SUVs and buggies. You can compete with real pros such as Ken Block, Travis Pastrana, Dave Mirra, Tanner Faust, Mohammed bin Sulayem and others. The developers added a promotional video and event dedicated to the memory of Colin McRae, who died in 2007 along with his son.

The gameplay is based on realism and individual control of each vehicle. During the races, a navigator is present to help you navigate turns and evaluate the difficulty of the route. Up to 8 riders take part in the races. There are classic circuit races and hill climb sprints. Raid races are presented - competitions on trucks and buggies. Bonus championships are Gatecrasher - breaking yellow barriers, Domination - time trials and Last Man Standing - elimination matches.
