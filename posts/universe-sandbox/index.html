Game Description#

Universe Sandbox is a continuation of the space simulator, which showed the real behavior of various objects in space. The game can be used as a virtual allowance for astronomy to understand how black holes behave or asteroids move. At the same time, the game will be interesting not only to beginners, but also to people who know definitions such as Betelheze and quantum voids.

The gameplay of space sandbox will create objects, interact with them and destroy using Newton’s mechanics. Moreover, all elements exist in real time, with real gravity and interaction of matter. The full -fledged sandbox sandbox includes the creation of planets of hydrogen, gland, water and rocks, look behind the solar flashes and tails of flying gases, pulsars, black holes and, of course, dark matter! And who after that will say that science is boring? Universe Sandbox allows:

Create your solar system: place a star, add planets, enrich space with satellites, comets, rings and even black holes; to encounter planets and stars in order to observe how planetary bodies interact epic in physics and leave behind molten huge craters; To model the climate of the Earth: to observe from the cosmos how the climate on the planet changes depending on the seasons, or how life on Earth proceeds if it moves to various distances from the sun; Recall the theory of a large explosion: add age and mass to the star, and then see how the supernova flash will occur; With the help of spacecraft of Juno and new horizons, you can see an eclipse and other events; Play VR mode.
