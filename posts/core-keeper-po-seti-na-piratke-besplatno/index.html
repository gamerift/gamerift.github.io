Game Description#

The role-playing adventureCore Keeper allows you to play for free on a pirate online with a friend, exploring together online or locally dark dungeons generated randomly over the Internet. The possibility of free online play allows partners to fight mythical creatures, mine resources, craft items and much more. The online game is designed for 8 participants who will have to uncover the secret of the ancient Core.

It is necessary to collect resources to build a base or make new equipment. You will also need to fuel the Core using the collected materials. By fighting monsters in the depths of dungeons, experience points are awarded, allowing you to upgrade your hero. Each location in Core Keeper is represented by a variety of biomes and is populated by individual types of monsters. All creatures have unique attack patterns.

In some rooms you will have to fight powerful bosses. At the beginning of the adventure, you can create your own hero, but as you progress, you can change your appearance on the fly. Some walls can be destroyed. To disperse the darkness, you will need to use torches. You can go fishing and grow various plants. By mixing ingredients, you can create various recipes that will help you progress. Together with other participants you will need to not only survive, but also conquer wild lands.
