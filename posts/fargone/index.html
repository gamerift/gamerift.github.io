Game Description#

Fargone is a role shooter with survival elements in which gamers fall into the world after a mysterious nuclear disaster, known as the Wasteland. Here you will have to survive by any means, whether it is alone or entering into alliances with various fractions. Explore the gloomy and atmospheric lands of the wasteland, where life continues, despite the terrible catastrophe. Here you will find not only other surviving, but also stray corpses affected by infection caused by disaster.

You can choose your own survival path and decide with whom you will enter into alliances, and with whom to fight. The system of dynamic fractions will allow you to find new friends or enemies, and the RPG system of skills will give you the opportunity to develop your character at your discretion. Faced with diverse undead, including bosses, and create your own safe area using a camping system or strengthen existing locations. Collect resources, crafty objects and equipment to become a real survivor. A unique weather and biom system will allow you to plunge into the unique atmospheres of each zone, from toxic rain in the Khazzard zone to soothing snow in the mountains. But be careful, your character can get sick, become infected and even break bones. You will need to take care of your character and make decisions that will affect his survival. Are you ready to encounter the challenges that are waiting for you in Fargone?
