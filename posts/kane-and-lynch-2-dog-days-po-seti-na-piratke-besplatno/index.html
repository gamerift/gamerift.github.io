Game Description#

Kane and Lynch 2: Dog Days is a sequel to the cooperative action game, which introduces the opportunity to play online with a friend for free on a pirate site. Online playthrough and single-player campaign are available. The events of Kane and Lynch 2: Dog Days directly continue the previous part. Free co-op play allows teammates to take on the role of one of two heroes. Each character has its own unique character traits.

Multiplayer allows you to cover your friend, fight together against crowds of enemies and complete various missions. An important mechanic is saving your partner. When the health of one of the players reaches zero, he does not die immediately. The friend has time to revive the fighter. You can hide behind cover and shoot blindly. Kane and Lynch 2: Dog Days modes: Fragile Alliance - Kaneâ€™s gang needs to steal money from a bank; Arcade Mode - single-player mode in which the bandits are controlled by AI; Undercover Cop - one player goes undercover and must kill the rest; Cops and Robbers - two teams are divided into cops and robbers, trying to destroy the enemy group.

The plot begins 7 years after the end of the first part. The first scene starts with the torture of the main characters. Afterwards the story moves forward to two days earlier. Lynch moves to China. He has settled down and lives with his girlfriend Xiu. Kane continued to work as a mercenary, but a lucrative order awaits. When Glaser offers to make a deal, the former partners reunite.
