Game Description#

The gameHot Dogs, Horseshoes and Hand Grenades can be called a full-fledged weapons simulator. You need to go to the training ground, which contains a huge selection of different firearms: pistols, machine guns, shotguns, rocket launchers, grenades, etc. You can use any available ammunition, training on targets and taking the highest positions on the leaderboard. Afterwards you can go and set off fireworks.

Targets can be not only static within the walls of a gray pavilion, but also moving in open space. You can play in co-op with friends who will throw various objects into the air, and your task is to accurately hit the object. Among them are both ordinary horseshoes and bombs that explode in the air. There are up to 250 weapons available in Hot Dogs, Horseshoes & Hand Grenades, which are accurately modeled after real-life guns. They are represented by both futuristic and fictional guns, as well as historical and modern types.

Mini-games and various challenges test your accuracy and confidence in using guns. It is possible to play in virtual reality mode, which allows you to get maximum immersion in the gameplay. With the help of VR helmets, a full sensation of guns is created, which can be viewed from all sides and experienced in the field, feeling the full force of the barrel and its recoil.
