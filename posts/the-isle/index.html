Game Description#

The island on whichThe Isle takes place has a rich and varied environment that is randomly generated. All artifacts, items and events will be located in different places with each new launch. Players must team up to effectively explore the area, which contains many secrets, including various quest items that further advance the plot. The map contains an area of ​​16x16 square kilometers. Multiplayer survival game The Isle allows you to play with three different factions in three different modes:

Progression - allows you to play as dinosaurs, level them up and discover new types of reptiles; Survival - is represented by all the dinosaurs available in the game at once, but requires you to look for food, water and all the means of subsistence in the wild; Sandbox - a mode where everything that is open is open is in the game and does not set the player any goals or boundaries.

All inhabiting fauna and nature will develop and change as you progress, analyzing the player’s actions. The island consists of plains, deserts, jungles, ancient ruins, etc. There are two playable classes of people to choose from: modern and indigenous, as well as the ability to control dinosaurs. Each class has its own needs, including thirst and hunger. The difference is that each species obtains them in its own way, avoiding dangers or vice versa, hunting and tracking prey.

The gameplay allows you to go through the full cycle of growing up from adolescence to adulthood. A total of 30 species of reptiles are available: Triceratops, Carnotaurus, Tyrannosaurus, Gallimimus, Utahraptor, Acrocanthosaurus, Allosaurus and many others. Both ground and air creatures are present. Up to 100 people can participate in multiplayer.
