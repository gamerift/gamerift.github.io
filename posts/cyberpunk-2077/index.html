Game Description#

Cyberpunk 2077 is an action role-playing game, the events of which unfold in the futuristic city of Night City, located on the West Coast of the United States. You will play as a protagonist named V (or V). The sex, gender and appearance of the hero must be set independently. Depending on the choice of class, the character’s background differs. There are three of them: Nomad, Child of the Street and Corporate. The introductory part for one of the classes will provide individual quests, introduce you to unique characters, and the story itself starts from different areas of the city. However, after the introduction, the plot for any specialization will converge at one point and unite into one narrative line. During the passage, NPCs react differently to a representative of one or another specialization.

The first important goal in Cyberpunk 2077 is to steal a secret development from the Arasaka megacorporation. Johnny Silverhand, the leader of the rock band Samurai, will help in this matter. The role of the character was played by the famous actor Keanu Reeves. From this point on, Johnny becomes V’s constant companion until the final credits. The plot will feature such characters as Judy Alvarez, Victor Vector, Delamaine AI, Dexter “Dak” DeShawn, Jackie Wells, Jackie Wells, Lizzie Wizzy and others. The characters are voiced by Samuel Barnett, Michael-Leon Wooley, Jason Hightower, Lowenski Jean-Baptiste and popular singer Grimes. The gameplay of Cyberpunk 2077 belongs to the immersive sim genre, inviting you to decide for yourself which method to complete the level. You can achieve your goal in a variety of ways, depending on your skill and play style. Hack terminals and computers, obtaining the necessary passwords, without catching the eye of the guards, or arm yourself with the heaviest weapons and cause a real massacre.

Night City is an open world with a huge number of main and secondary quests available, not counting the many additional activities. A complete passage will require over 100 hours of real time. Side quests contain subplots and tell individual mini-stories. Each area offers dozens of missions. You will need to fight bosses, cyberpsychopaths, arrange showdowns with the mafia, carry out orders for fixers, steal valuables, clean out a cache, eliminate a target, etc. The experience gained is divided into 5 main parameters: strength, reaction, technique, intelligence and composure. Other skills are upgraded the more often you use them. Ability tree available. By receiving experience points, upgrade the required skill line. There is a limit of 50 levels, so it is impossible to make V a god, but it gives you the opportunity to create an individual character build.
