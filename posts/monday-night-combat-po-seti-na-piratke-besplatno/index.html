Game Description#

On the pirateMonday Night Combat you can play online with a friend, competing for free in various multiplayer modes online online. The gameplay is represented by third-person action with the ability to collect loot, pass various tests, etc. Monday Night Combat is a classic competitive shooter set in a future world called Fanland.

A new type of competition is emerging called Crossfire. It is quickly gaining momentum and more and more fighters want to compete for the main prize. The main target is Chicken Cantor. As soon as he lands, you must immediately ride him. You need to ride for at least 8 minutes to earn money. The online game includes confrontations between heroes such as Jackbot, Black Jack, Slim, Gapshot, Bouncer and others.

Free online play features include Blitz confrontation. The essence of the mode is that it is necessary to protect the money ball from the invasion of robots. Four players in a cooperative must fight off a wave of opponents in every possible way. The funds you earn can be spent on deploying turrets and defensive robots. Characters are divided into 6 unique classes.

A wide variety of tests are presented. For example, the ten-round Exhibition Blitz or the endless Super Sudden Death Blitz. When a team makes money, it goes into the Lifetime Earnings coffers. Funds can be spent on 370 tags. Fighters are customized individually. You can customize your appearance, skills, choose weapons and auxiliary items. Each class has unique equipment.
