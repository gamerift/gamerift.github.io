Game Description#

The sandbox 171 stands out against the background of other GTA clones with its entourage. The action takes place in Brazil. The creators offer to ride on the suburbs of San Paulo. Poor areas with narrow streets, graffiti on the walls, dirt and garbage add a special flavor of the local atmosphere.

The player controls his character with a third -person view. During shooting on the street, the camera is transferred by the hero’s shoulder. You can open fire, sitting in the car. At the same time, the protagonist is able to shoot in any direction without leaning out of the cabin.

In 171, it is traditionally possible to steal any car. There are workshops where you can pump and repaint the cars. Upgrades are visually displayed by transport. With an accident, cars are realized and in some situations explode. Law enforcement officers, bandits and ordinary passers -by are walking around the city. All of them react differently to the chaos created by the user.

The interface of the game is minimalistic. The user is shown a mini-card, the level of search by the police (from 1 to 5 stars), a strip of health and the type of weapons. During the chasing of the patrols of the cops are marked on the map, including on a large one. A gun, rifle, fists are available from the trunks. The hero does not hide the weapon into an invisible inventory, but hangs on his back. There is also a father -in -law of clothing stores, but they are simply decorated in the form of an editor of the hero skin, included on the outside of buildings.
