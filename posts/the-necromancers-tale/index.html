Game Description#

The Necromincer’s Tale is a role -playing game in the setting of dark fantasy. The adventure is presented by a top-distance. It is a nobleman to manage a nobleman living in the world of an alternative of the 18th century. The tale of the necromancer is a mystical story full of political conspiracies, dark knowledge and thirst for revenge. Exploring surrounding, everything may not be at all the same as it seems initially. The stronger the power, the more new faces of this world open to the hero’s eyes.

The protagonist found a book of spells, melting in himself the power of powerful rituals. As you know, necromancy makes it possible to resurrect the dead. Will the hero get all the secrets of the ancient scriptures and master such mystical rituals? It is important not only to learn necromancy, but to maintain humanity and reason. People do not strongly complain of dark magicians, so show cunning and secrecy, so as not to be burned enraged by peasants.

The Necromincer’s Tale gameplay is aimed at studying the territories, and a lot of attention is paid to conversations with the residents of the city and, of course, dark rituals. The battles are reserved for the background, but you have to fight repeatedly. It is always important to choose the right replica in the dialogue and not give yourself out. Citizens are suspicious of strangers, so be careful!

Immerse yourself in the world of madness, discovering the dark sides of this world. The power of forbidden magic attracts so much to itself, especially if the hero moves the thirst for revenge. Magic and spells have nothing with a society of close combat weapons, which can be thoughtlessly used, jamming of enemies. A strategic approach is required here. Observe the change in the hero, distorting morality and witness how he loses a sense of time and reality.
