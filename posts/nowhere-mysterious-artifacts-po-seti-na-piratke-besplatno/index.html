Game Description#

You can play the survival gameNowhere: Mysterious Artifacts for free online as a pirate online with a friend, exploring the vast expanses of a long-dead world. In the first chapter, entitled The Mystery of the Root Mountains, you will need to use the Internet online, together with your partners, to discover the secrets of mysterious places, look for supplies, build fortifications and engage in battle with enemies. Free online play allows four adventurers to join forces to unravel the mystery of mystical artifacts hidden in the depths of mysterious mountains.

The action takes place in September 1993. You will need to survive in harsh conditions, trying to make your life easier with the help of collected items and supplies. To protect yourself from a hostile environment, you need to build and improve a shelter. Enemies are especially dangerous at night. Network play Nowhere: Mysterious Artifacts is built on interaction and sharing of responsibilities. You need to monitor both your health and sanity indicators, and those of your partners.

Blueprints and recipes allow you to create new or improved items, as well as cook food with different boosts. Artifacts affect the style of play. Each item has its own unique property and gives specific abilities to the character. There is a dynamic day/night system. The properties of the surrounding world depend on the time of day, affecting the anomalies of the Nowhere territory or the aggressiveness of opponents.
