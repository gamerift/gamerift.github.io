Game Description#

Victory Heat Rally is a retro racing game inspired by arcade games of the last century, such as Hang-On and OutRun. You have to take control of small cars to rush along various difficult tracks at high speed. The gameplay is hardcore and pays homage to the classic games of yesteryear.

To fully master the mechanics of Victory Heat Rally, you will need to learn how to glide around the track, take corners correctly, and feel the road. The tracks are of increased difficulty, offering to overcome steep slopes, sharp turns, numerous obstacles and much more.

Two modes are available: arcade races and time trials. In the arcade you will take part in championships of three races on unique maps. There are two cars to choose from, each of which has its own characteristics, and is driven by a unique driver.

Despite the cartoonish visual style, the game’s mechanics are not that easy to master. The gameplay is made in the spirit of old-school racing in the Mario Kart series. Try to keep up with your rivals! The developers managed to bring back the spirit of classic arcade games, introducing many modern elements into the gameplay and unique environment design, emphasizing the atmosphere of the 90s with an appropriate driving soundtrack.
