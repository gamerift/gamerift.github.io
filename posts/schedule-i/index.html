Game Description#

Schedule 1 puts a gamer in the place of a small drug dealer trying to build a criminal empire from scratch. The action takes place in the gloomy city of Highland Point, and users get the opportunity to expand the scale of their activities, controlling everyone - from production to sales. You will begin with an empty wallet and the necessary connections, gradually rising to control over the criminal world. On this path, you will have to solve a variety of problems, including the purchase of real estate, hiring personnel and the fight against competitors.

The unique highlight of Schedule 1 lies in the mechanics of drug production. Different drugs require their production methods, and gamers can open new recipes to create options with special properties. This adds an element of strategy and experiments, since users are trying to maximize their profits, while managing the risk of persecution by law enforcement agencies and competing cartels. You can walk, roller skills or travel around the city to make sure that your product has reached its destination, which adds the flexibility of your distribution network.

Expanding your empire, the title invites you to automate processes, creating an effective system of release and distribution. However, this does not mean that you can relax calmly. Cartelies are always on the alert and are looking for ways to undermine your success, and law enforcement agencies continue to increase efforts to suppress illegal activities. You will have to think quickly and act even faster, whether it is negotiations on transactions, personnel management or the fight against competitors.
