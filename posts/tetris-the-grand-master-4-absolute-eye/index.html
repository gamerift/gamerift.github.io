Game Description#

Tetris The Grand Master 4 -Absolute Eye - marks the continuation of the TGM series, originating in the arcade halls. This fourth part goes after a 20-year break between the main parts of the series, representing new elements in the established formula. The project contains content designed both for beginners unfamiliar with the specific style of the series, and for gamers with extensive experience.

Tetris The Grand Master 4 -Absolute Eye offers several passage options, including recently introduced ASUKA and Shiranui options. It provides variations of gameplay aimed at testing the time of the gamer’s reaction and planning the installation of figures. These formats are suitable for those who are looking for various types of involvement, from the accumulation of glasses to high -speed races. In addition, Title supports a local multiplayer, allowing you to compete face to face on one system.

Certain formats significantly form impressions of the process. Marathon works with Standard control, concentrating the goal of achieving the highest possible account through lines cleansing. In turn, the Normal option uses the TGM control type, setting the gamers the task of cleaning strips in the shortest time. The ASUKA option represents a different test where tetrimino falls at maximum speed from the very beginning; Gamers strive to increase the level as part of temporary restriction by quick lines cleaning.
