Game Description#

Speed ​​Crew offers free and pirated online play where you and a friend or up to three friends (for a total of four people) immerse yourself in the vibrant atmosphere of online gaming. Compete together or online against each other across four decades of auto racing as you strive to defeat the elusive antagonist Torrento Dominion.

The fun comes at a frantic pace. Diagnose vehicle problems, grab the right tools and fix everything before your driver gets back into the car. Navigate dynamic pit lanes with changing platforms and hazards, all under the pressure of time. Don’t forget to dodge attempts at sabotage by the Torrento Dominion - it loves to turn friends into “disgruntled losers.”

Speed ​​Crew offers both local and online co-op, making it perfect for parties. There’s even an arcade mode for real challengers. The game has 48 levels, consisting of 4 chapters, and there is always a new challenge to conquer. Unlock new characters and quirky outfits as you race to victory.

The game is designed for any age and skill level. Want to add more chaos? Hit your teammates (in-game, of course) to disrupt their repairs. Feeling particularly devious? Take control of the Dominion Torrento and become a real troll in the pit lane. So fasten your seatbelts, grab your tools, and get ready for some exciting, fast-paced racing action!
