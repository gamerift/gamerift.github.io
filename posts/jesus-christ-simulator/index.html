Game Description#

In Jesus Christ Simulator, gamers find themselves in the role of Jesus, who has to solve the problems facing humanity. The action takes place in a huge American city where users use their divine abilities to create miracles and bring positive changes to people’s lives. These forces include flights, exorcism and various supernatural abilities that allow users to interact with the environment and its inhabitants in unique ways.

In Jesus Christ Simulator, gamers can deal with matters such as paying debts, providing shelter and even traveling to hell to save or condemn souls. Title focuses on the use of miracles to solve problems and improve the life of citizens. Gamers have to navigate in a world filled with both human and supernatural problems, using their abilities to combat demons and other threats.

The game combines the action and a strategy, demanding decision -making users that affect the storyline and the life of the characters they face. Title encourages gamers to explore various approaches to solving problems, making each passage with unique experience. The goal is to create an atmosphere of immersion in which users will be able to explore the moral and ethical consequences of their actions, trying to create the best world.
