Game Description#

The Crust is a survival with elements of the economy, where the player will build a whole small town with highly limited resources almost without the possibility of replenishing them. In order to grow at least something on such a surface and provide your people with the proper amount of food, you will have to try hard. However, with proper management, this is still quite possible.

The main character is the head of the space company, which was decided by the first to colonize the moon. The satellite is quite suitable for life if you create normal conditions there and build a buza of sizes. Cosmos does not like weaklings, so for the success of the project, quite difficult decisions often have to make. At first, there will be few rooms and astronauts are the minimum amount, but then, investors will begin to demand results from what they invested in and send more and more people to a unique colony. In addition, such a base will become a real business that will allow the protagonist to become a billionaire. After all, many are ready to pay huge amounts for such a unique experience.

In Cruist, the player will not encounter problems by the type of sudden invasion of aliens, since mainly the game is aimed precisely at a realistic image of a potential future and the severity of survival. Follow the alien scenario that the world society requires or go on your own development path? The consequences will depend only on the decisions made by the user.
