Game Description#

Libritopia: Librarian Simulator gives you the management of its own library institution. The main tasks include acceptance and sorting of literary works from all over the world. It is necessary to form collects of the works of Shakespeare and Jules Verne, placing them on racks for free access. This game makes it possible to create a personal classification system that allows you to catalog literature on the genre, author, country or any other method. The main goal is the effective management of the institution.

In Libritopia: Librarian Simulator Title, gamers are customizing the environment and personal avatar. The interior decoration of the premises can be changed, including the decoration of walls, floors, ceilings and windows. You can arrange objects of the situation and racks, forming space and passages. In addition to the institution itself, the player constructs a unique librarian, choosing a physique, hairstyle, clothes and accessories. The project allows you to maintain several exterior options for further use.

Everyday responsibilities require interaction with computer visitors entering the reading room. It is necessary to help customers in the search for specific volumes and arrange an extradition with a return for a service stand. The key task is to maintain silence, ensuring compliance with the rules, for example, a ban on mobile phones. Users can act together in a cooperative gaming mode, sharing tasks for fulfilling all requests and competing for the title of librarian of the month.
