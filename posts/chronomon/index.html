Game Description#

Chronomon combines elements of monsters care and the simulation of the farm. Players find themselves in the world after a disaster, where they have to catch unusual creatures, improve their skills and help in restoration. Communication with the settlers and step -by -step battles complement the process of arranging their own allotment of land, and the main task is to form a detachment of pets and control of resources in the proposed conditions.

The basis of the Chronomon gameplay is to search and keep a collection of more than 100 diverse creatures found in ordinary and rare variations with special characteristics. These companions perform different roles: they assist in battles, study locations and work on a farm. The development of wards occurs through a mechanic covering 39 talent branches and over 300 skills that are enhanced by combat techniques, special marks and passive bonuses, and more creatures use their capabilities to overcome puzzles on the ground, opening access to finds.

The farmer part of the project involves the cultivation of over 50 species of plants. The resulting fruits are processed in the food necessary to maintain a game character and his creatures in the form. Excess yields exchange with neighboring settlements for the required items, while the extraction of materials is not limited to agriculture: you can collect raw materials or fish - more than 20 of its types are available. Tinked animals increase the productivity of the economy using their skills to increase production.
