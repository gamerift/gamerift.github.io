Game Description#

Rising Lords is a two-dimensional strategy game that you can play online for free with a friend, using all the multiplayer features of the project on a pirate. Players can compete or fight in co-op over a LAN network or online online. The gameplay contains elements of turn-based games, board games and CCGs. The online game invites you to join forces with friends or start a bloody feud. When hiring slaves, you must send them to fight for your king.

Units are able to collect resources. The ruler can set the price for taxes. Weapons, armor and equipment for the army are created from the collected materials. Free online play includes co-op building of a multi-level city. Ordinary slaves can be turned into real knights. It is necessary to take care of your peasants so that a rebellion does not start. Participants enter into alliances, opposing a common enemy, or fight for dominance, becoming the sole ruler of the kingdom. Mechanics of Rising Lords: production of goods, collection of resources, diplomacy, taxation and many other management elements; training the army, increasing their combat abilities and morale; exploring a huge interactive map; sieging cities, choosing tactics and starving your enemies; multiplayer for 4 players; the ability to spread misinformation throughout the kingdom; flexible customization of the characteristics and parameters of the heroâ€™s appearance; 50 unique events with the possibility of moral choice.
