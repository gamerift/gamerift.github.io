Game Description#

In the universe ofUndertale, a role-playing game with pixel graphics, a war broke out between humans and monsters. The people won and locked all the creatures from the other world in an underground wasteland. The underground world of the game tells the story of one boy who finds himself in a closed space with monsters. 7 magicians guarded the dungeon area using barrier spells, but somehow a baby got there, for whom we have to play.

The setting means that the player will fight opponents on rare occasions. Much more often you will have to use the conversational battle technique to get out alive. Weapons and armor in Undertale are extremely rare. The player will have to go through many levels to upgrade his character, but an important part of the game is the plot side, controlled by the player himself with the help of dialogues, script turns that you choose.

Completing the story will not take more than 4 hours, and during this time you will decide whether to attack opponents or not, whether to resolve conflicts peacefully. The game allows saving at special points where the hero restores his health. Different locations imply a unique world. The hero will solve logical problems and puzzles, move through the dungeon world with respectable monsters or those that will tell the true story of the world. It will be that you decide for yourself: to return to the world of people or to stay with the monsters who have become your family.
