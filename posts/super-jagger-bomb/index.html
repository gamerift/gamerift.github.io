Game Description#

Super Jagger Bomb sets the gamer with the task of neutralizing incendiary devices on multi -level arenas. The main character in the cloak should quickly collect all the bombs in order to prevent the explosion. To move in locations, he uses running, jumping and jerking in the air. A special technique allows you to quickly fall down, which adds an element of tactical planning of the route to the process and turns it into a kind of puzzle.

Passing in Super Jagger Bomb complicates mechanical opponents and flying saucers, constantly patrolling levels. These enemies are interfering while the timer counts the time before the mission failure. The action of this project unfolds in a variety of decorations, from lost ruins to modern megacities. Sometimes auxiliary bonuses appear in the arena for temporary stripping the screen from all threats.

This arcade title is a test of reaction speed and accuracy of movements in solitary mode. Each location is a unique spatial mystery that requires finding the fastest and safe way to the goal. The complexity is growing sequentially, offering new platform configurations and types of obstacles. A successful game depends on the ability to quickly evaluate the situation and accurately perform acrobatic maneuvers.

Arsenal and tests of the valiant rescuer:

Collection and neutralization of unstable bombs for a while. Dynamic displacement of platforms using jumps and jerks. Confrontation of hostile robots and flying saucers. Traveling along a variety of locations from ancient ruins to megacities. Using powerful bonuses to clean up the arena from opponents.
