Game Description#

Suspects Mystery Mansion is a co-op adventure with the ability to play online with a friend or random players. On the pirate site, you can team up for free in order to perform various tasks to find evidence and identify the real killer. The online game Suspects Mystery Mansion is based on social deduction of the participants. There are many unique modes that present players with different conditions and goals for victory.

When one of the team finds evidence, it is necessary to study it in detail and draw conclusions from the find. Communication and interaction is the key factor in winning. Events take place in a huge Mansion. The opportunity for free online play offers you to take part in eight modes, where there are both Survivors and Killers. A special feature of the gameplay is that if the Avenger makes a mistake, he dies himself. Survivors must hide, and maniacs must carefully hide the evidence. 8 unique modes available: Whisper - you can only communicate with players who are nearby; Avengers - finding the killer with no room for error; Hide and kill - there is no way to vote and everyone acts only intuitively; Poisoner - a long and painful death for the survivors , if the maniac manages to poison the guests; Master of Disguise - a spy can take on the appearance of other group members; Everyone can kill - a classic death match, where everyone fights among themselves; Buka - one person becomes a super-killer; Team wars - real chaos, where groups will need to fight.
