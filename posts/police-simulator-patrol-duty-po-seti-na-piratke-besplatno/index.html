Game Description#

Police Simulator: Patrol Duty invites you to immerse yourself in the everyday life of a US police officer, collaborating with friends online. The cop simulator allows you to play online for free as a pirate with a friend or random people around the world, protecting the city from crime. As an inspector, you will need to record traffic accidents, search for stolen cars and detain criminals as a detective. Online play gives you the opportunity to team up and create the best police station in the area.

The players' goal is to ensure the safety of their area. You can stop suspicious cars, check documents of civilians, create checkpoints, etc. You should carefully observe the suspects, trying to identify strange behavior in them, etc. You will have to participate in chases, interrogate witnesses, look for evidence and much more. Police Simulator: Patrol Dut completely immerses you in the difficult everyday life of law enforcement officers. Key features of the project: single-player campaign and the possibility of free online play; cooperative mode for passing; realistic physics and behavior of vehicles; a huge open world with its inhabitants and infrastructure; hardcore gameplay that describes the complex everyday life of US cops.

There are 3 types of transport available. Each machine has its own unique features and characteristics. Initially, only an ordinary passenger sedan is available, but later a high-speed muscle car and an armored SUV are opened. The cars have a well-developed damage system. Together with your partner, you need to investigate crimes, trying to use evidence to find the criminal. There will be three areas to patrol: financial, industrial and residential. As auxiliary equipment, items such as handcuffs, walkie-talkies, radars and much more are presented.
