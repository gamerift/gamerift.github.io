Game Description#

Battlefield 2 is a multiplayer shooter that allows you to play for free as a pirate online with a friend or fight up to 64 people online online. The gameplay contains strategy and RPG elements. Both online play and single-player mode are presented, in which you can compete with 15 bots. There are 3 sides to the conflict to choose from: USMC (US Marine Corps), MEC (Middle East Coalition) and PLA/China (People’s Liberation Army of China).

The possibility of free online play allows you to unite up to 32 players on one team. One of the participants takes on the role of captain. His job is to be the ears and eyes of the other fighters on the field. The captain in Battlefield 2 can survey the entire map, observing what is happening from above. It also has an area scanner that highlights soldiers and equipment on the battlefield. Commander functions: Vehicle Drop - landing a vehicle at a specified point; Supplies Drop - dropping a box of ammunition; Artillery Strike - strike from an artillery weapon; UAV Overflight - calling an unmanned vehicle.

Soldiers are divided into 7 classes. Each fighter has his own unique abilities and characteristics. For example, a Sniper is effective at long distances, a Saboteur operates in the rear, and a Medic requires constant cover. In addition, you can play for such warriors as Stormtrooper, Support, Technician and Grenade Launcher. If you board a transport and there is a support unit among the participants, the characters’ health is restored.

Equipment is divided into 6 classes: Fighter-bombers, Attack helicopters, Fighters, Armored personnel carriers, Tanks and Transport helicopters. The models are real-life prototypes, for example the Chinese Su-30MKK, the American AH-1Z, the Russian BTR-90 BTR and others. There are a total of 30 maps available for battles. Depending on the size, they can accommodate from 16 to 64 players.
