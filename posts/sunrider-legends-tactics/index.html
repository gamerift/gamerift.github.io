Game Description#

Sunrider Legends Tactics will put a player at the head of the Navy leading his ships along the seas of the fantasy world in the era of sails. You have to command the courts in step -by -step battles, making tactical decisions on maneuvers, positioning and the use of weapons against enemy armades. The adventure will require solving strategic problems in the open sea and participation in special plot events.

The outcome of the enterprise depends on the captain’s ability to competently manage their vessels and replay opponents in tactical skirmishes. Sunrider Legends Tactics offers clashes with many tactical capabilities. Ships can be equipped with special weapons, such as chain nuclei, and manipulations with sails allow the use of wind for profitable positioning. For a fracture of the course of complex confrontations, the Avangard gun is available. In addition, you can collect boarding teams to capture enemy vessels, and any ship is able to become the purpose of boarding, which will bring new weapons or culinary ingredients as trophies.

The actions of the protagonist directly affect the narrative, opening access to the secret plot lines and additional characters for hiring. In this game, an attachment system is implemented: conversations with 14 companions allow you to accumulate glasses that open unique skills. Strengthening these relationships can lead to 14 various personal endings - one by one for each girl. After passing, the new game will offer alternative narrative branches and the opportunity to recruit other heroines.
