Game Description#

Wild West Dynasty takes place in 1800s America. The player’s task is to build his own ranch, providing himself with wealth and food. Over time, other settlers, workers and friends will join the main character. Each minor character has their own qualities, characteristics and mannerisms. Many of them will turn out to be bandits, and only the gamer can decide who is sent to the gallows.

Having become determined, the central character set out to build a Wild West dynasty, moving further across the Pacific region of America. He wants to find a better life for himself, his business and have new adventures. During the passage, the protagonist will establish farms, raise livestock, dig for gold, and build settlements and cities. If the user manages to find small deposits of gold, he will attract hundreds of adventurers. An ordinary mine will soon turn into a thriving town with gambling, cowboys, adventurers, merchants and saloons.

A lonely cowboy was once lucky enough to end up on an abandoned ranch. From this moment a new stage in his life begins. He repairs buildings and expands territories. Hunting animals and obtaining food, he sells it to nearby merchants and residents. Other townspeople and ordinary people may ask for a roof over their heads for various services or monthly payments. After a while, the gamer will be able to build shops, hotels, a sheriff’s office, salons, taverns and other buildings. The hero will face bandits, and for protection he will need to learn how to use firearms.
