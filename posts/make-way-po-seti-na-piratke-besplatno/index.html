Game Description#

Make Way provides free online play, allowing you to play online with your friends. You can create your own chaotic racing tracks on pirate and free online. It’s like a smorgasbord of different track components, where you choose and connect loops, corkscrews, railroad crossings and more to create unique tracks. Your goal is to dodge dangers, shoot enemies with strange weapons and reach the finish line. Each new round adds new elements, making your creation even more exciting and fun in this online game.

But Make Way is not only about speed. The option of cooperation or glorious betrayal is on the menu. Team up to build outrageous tracks, then cause friendly sabotage with well-placed barriers or naughty boosters. Up to four racers can battle for glory using outlandish weapons like slime cannons and Tesla coils. Remember that checkpoints can be fought back, so revenge with a giant hammer or a rooftop shotgun awaits!

But the checkered flag is not the end. Each round adds new fragments of the track, stitching it into a single whole. Achieve high scores, score points and gain bragging rights. As you progress, you’ll be able to unlock even more track components and vehicles, expanding your chaotic design potential and garage of winning cars. Want to add an extra layer of strategic chaos? Switch traps and safety barriers on and off to create tricky turns or lend a helping hand. With fully customizable rules, the potential for devious fun is endless.
