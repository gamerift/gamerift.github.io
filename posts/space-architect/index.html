Game Description#

Space Architect is a simulator where gamers have to become in place of the manager of the space station. The main goal is to build, maintain and expand the space station, simultaneously coping with the difficulties of resource management and interstellar trade. In the beginning, users are faced with a serious financial burden - a huge debt to a powerful megarporace, which forces them to make complex decisions on how to get income and stay afloat in endless cosmic voids.

With the help of asteroid production, the production of resources and trade, the station manager should turn a modest enterprise into a self -sufficient empire. In Title, much attention is paid to the ability to manage resources and automate their production. Asteroid production is one of the main classes in the early stages of the game. The space station needs various materials for the construction and modernization of its components, and asteroids are a ready -made source of valuable resources.

The process of production in Space Architect is simple, but the difficulty is how to effectively use the extracted. Users can do everything manually or entrust the completion of the tasks of an automated system or hired command, and each option has its own advantages and difficulties. The balance between manual labor and automation is becoming more and more difficult as the station grows. To stay won, gamers also need to engage in trade.
