Game Description#

Incursion Red River immerses you in the ruthless world of modern Vietnam, devastated by civil war. In the role of a mercenary of private military corporations (PMCs), profitable on chaos, you will participate in tense tactical shootings, reveal hidden truths and fight for survival in history, which unfolds in the course of the game. Combat with two friends for a cooperative game or go alone, like a lone wolf. Choose contracts from competing PMCs to create a reputation for yourself and earn awards. Investigated money in a limited arsenal from the merchant, choosing weapons and equipment corresponding to your style.

In Incursion Red River, special attention is paid to strategic planning and exciting gameplay. The weight and ease of use of equipment directly affect its combat effectiveness. The ballistics is modeled, which means that each weapon has unique characteristics that can be additionally changed. Correctly selected equipment, a thought -out plan and the priority implementation of tactical tasks are key survival factors.

The game offers extensive customization of weapons. Modify your faithful AK, turning it from the beast of the near battle into a long -range laser, changing trunks, guard, handles, lodges, sights and shops. Your shelter serves you an operational base. Here you will accept new contracts, collect a team, experience weapons at the training ground, manage the inventory, buy and sell equipment. This is your shelter after each dangerous sortie into the combat zone.
