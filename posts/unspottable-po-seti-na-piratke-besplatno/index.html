Game Description#

Unspottable is a competitive game that allows you to play pirate online with a friend for free, or compete with players from all over the world. Competitions are available online online. The gameplay allows you to get lost in a crowd of robots. You need to find your opponent among them. The cards are as varied and vibrant as possible. The gameplay relies on fun and dynamics. Network play allows you to join teams. There is no single-player campaign, so only the possibility of free online play is presented.

When the victim is in the crowd, it is necessary to strike. The controls are very simple and intuitive. There is a tutorial level available for beginners that shows the basics of the game. By defeating enemies, new abilities and development branches open up. Unspottable’s gameplay shows true competitive spirit. Since the developers did not introduce a single-player campaign, they directed the spirit of the project towards confrontation and competition.

You need to bluff, deceive and completely control the gameplay. Lies are the main tool in the fight against enemies. Each card is made in a unique way. There are many different missions, tasks and challenges available. There is also an expert level for real pros. Here it is necessary to challenge the same inveterate liars. Only the most experienced players gather at the difficult level. All missions and tasks become more complicated several times.
