Game Description#

The adventure inWarhammer 40,000: Rogue Trader begins aboard your own personal giant voidship. You’ll play online with a friend, traveling between multiple systems within the Koronus Expanse, a barely mapped and incredibly dangerous region of pirate space for free. Although considered a backwater of the Imperium, this region spans a vast expanse of void full of dangerous creatures and vast opportunities for profit and exploration. While completing online quests over the Internet, you can show mercy or contempt for your subjects, remain loyal to the god-emperor, or ally with the enemies of humanity. Every decision and every action, even when creating a character, changes the in-game open world and those who inhabit it.

Rogue Traders never travel alone. Assemble your retinue of righteous heroes, twisted psykers and treacherous xenos. They are all ready to follow you into the darkness between the stars. They will give you advice, help you in battle and allow you to accumulate even more strength. In return, you will be able to guide them through their own journeys, changing their destinies forever. Destroy the enemies of humanity in full-fledged isometric turn-based tactical combat. Use cover, the environment, and careful positioning to defeat your enemies. If that’s not enough, use your companion’s powerful abilities to turn the tide of battle and achieve victory even in the most difficult situations. Adapting the classic Rogue Trader rules opens up enormous possibilities for you.
