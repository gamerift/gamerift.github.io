Game Description#

Insurgency: Sandstorm is a sequel to a multiplayer shooter built on deathmatches in confined spaces, with the ability to use light vehicles. The game has been significantly improved and expanded. The key to victory lies only through well-coordinated teamwork.

The action takes place in the Middle East, on the ruins of settlements and military bases. You need to carefully choose your weapon and explore the territory, because without caution and tactics you can quickly catch a bullet in the forehead. Insurgency: Sandstorm takes conflict to a new level of realism. It becomes much more difficult to survive, saving every cartridge and clearly calculating your actions.

There are individual settings and complete character customization, using a variety of clothes, accessories, individual voice, etc. The gameplay allows you to direct fire support for your group, install guns on vehicles, use air support, or face opponents directly head-on. Players can look around corners and squeeze through openings. Use Smoke for Cover New features, guns, and rewards unlock as you play to help you gain a tactical advantage.

Multiplayer battles support battles for up to 32 players, and cooperative play against artificial intelligence includes up to 16 fighters. The “Duel” mode is represented by a full set of all game features. Players take on the role of Commander or Observer. The first takes a fierce part in the battlefield, while the second coordinates the process and acts as a powerful support in suppressing enemy fire.
