Game Description#

Slayer: The Demon Haunted World represents the world emerging from the period, which historians called the Aera of ash and dust. The belief in the end of this era was erroneous, because the shadows awaken again. A girl born under the moon and possessing white hair is the only one who can fight back the threat of fallen stars. She enters the tower for a battle with this evil, while the Universe balances on the verge of darkness.

The combat system in Slayer: The Demon Haunted World is based on tactics, where each action leads to consequences. The player’s movement is limited by one cell for a course - a mechanic that directly affects the attacking potential. Attacks are possible only in the direction of the heroine’s gaze, which forces to adhere to a thoughtful approach to fights. Each skirmish with the enemy is a tactical puzzle that requires correct positioning and timely maneuvers to survive among enemies and traps.

A single gameplay is built on the passage of dungeons filled with positional confrontations. Progress allows the protagonist to increase the level, opening opportunities to improve characteristics before future battles. Players collect equipment and master various skills throughout the entire adventure. These components give a chance to create an individual assembly of the character, making it possible to hone a special strategy for the Action RPG and Roguelite PVE scenarios of the game.
