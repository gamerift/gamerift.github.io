Game Description#

Squish is a dynamic arcade game that offers free pirate play online with a friend or compete online with random people on the Internet. The gameplay is a vibrant platformer with puzzle elements. The online game Squish is designed for parties and fun. You need to quickly jump on platforms while they are collapsing. You can push blocks, dodge falling objects, jump over them and trip up your opponents.

The gameplay is based on a competitive element. The last one standing takes it all. The possibility of free online play allows you to play in a mode where everyone plays for himself or fight in teams. The controls are quite simple, but the gameplay itself is very hardcore, requiring attentiveness, reaction and accuracy. The action takes place on interactive maps, where everything collapses, fails and explodes. You will need to truly survive. Participants rush around the map, trying to avoid falling blocks or push their opponent into a trap.

Multiplayer competitions are presented in 5 unique modes. Each of them sets participants various goals, rules and conditions for victory. For first place, as well as for participation, bonus points are given, which are awarded after the match. There is a wide selection of hats, skins and other wardrobe items available to make your character unique. In a special menu you can individually customize your heroes.
