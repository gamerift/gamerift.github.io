Game Description#

On the pirateKnights of Honor 2 Sovereign you can play online with a friend for free, participating in a confrontation or online online trying to conquer Europe together. The gameplay is a classic global strategy. You will need to develop provinces, train troops, spy on neighboring countries and much more. Network play allows you to declare war or engage in mutually beneficial trade. Combat in Knights of Honor 2 Sovereign is presented in real time. Multiplayer includes the ability for four participants to battle simultaneously.

More than 200 countries in Europe, Africa and Asia are available for development. Each power has its own unique set of units and special abilities. The possibility of free online play allows several participants to unite in alliances against a common enemy. Each faction has a Royal Court, in which it is necessary to hire marshals, clerics, merchants, diplomats and spies.

Marshals lead soldiers to victory, and diplomats can turn the most unfriendly countries into true true friends. Each unit performs its own special function. Due to this, you can develop your own unique strategy and tactics. The gameplay includes a huge number of mechanics: riots, wars, disasters and much more. You will need to develop the economy in more than 300 provinces and train 100 different types: archers, swordsmen, powerful Vikings or develop siege weapons.
