Game Description#

Conflict: Denied Ops is a tactical first-person shooter that offers you to take part in multiplayer battles available online, as well as go through a single-player campaign consisting of a huge number of missions that make it possible to uncover a global conspiracy. You can play pirate for free online with a friend, going through the storyline in co-op. Multiplayer allows you to switch between characters.

The story of Conflict: Denied Ops begins with two CIA agents tasked with hunting down terrorists who threaten the world order. The heroes will have to travel around the globe to find nuclear weapons. The Venezuelan government was able to buy weapons from the African diamond king. The characters' names are Graves and Lang. Their task will take them through numerous adventures and allow them to get involved in various military conflicts.

The core gameplay of Conflict: Covert Ops focuses on gun battles in an interactive environment. You can destroy buildings, shoot through walls and much more. In single player mode, you can give commands to your partner. By undermining obstacles and creating holes in walls, you can penetrate into inaccessible places and rooms.

Fire is conducted from behind cover, avoiding critical hits from the enemy. Destruction is simulated by Puncture-Tech technology. Free online play offers fighters the opportunity to use cutting-edge technology, including optics, micro cameras, satellite navigation and much more.
