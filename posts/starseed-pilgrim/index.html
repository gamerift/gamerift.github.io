Game Description#

Starsed Pilgrim puts a player in the role of a gardener in the void. This figure takes care of the silent space, planting seeds that create structures from color and sound. Each new sprout contributes to the composition that fills the abyss and transforming it with a direct action. The character moves through these new platforms, continuing the process of nurturing. The constantly spreading darkness serves as a motivation for this continuous creation, pushing the gardener to the construction of a new world away from its reach.

In addition to cultivation, the experience is focused on the study and adoption of the future. In the game Starsed Pilgrim, the character acts simultaneously as a refugee and adventurer, paving the course through the unknown. This journey is a process of cognition, revealing new places and governing them special rules. Each sortie demonstrates various interactions and phenomena in Title systems.

The hero studies through a direct part, deciphering the mechanics of the universe step by step and moving further outside. The results of this process are directly related to the concept of fate. Each world, grown from seed, is time, and its existence depends on the actions and discoveries within it. This game cycle encourages experimenting with various approaches to construction and wanderings. The universe contains more locations and systems than at first glance, inviting repeated travel to comprehend its full scale.
