Game Description#

Human: Fall Flat is an adventure puzzle game with amazing physics and engine. Take control of Bob, who every time has a dream from which he must find a way out. Usually such dreams are accompanied by colorful locations in which he needs to get to a certain point and solve many difficult problems.

Explore the open world in your own way. Climb any building, rock, tree or other object, going through the same place differently each time. Any decision you make will be correct if you were able to advance at least 1 percent. The gameplay has 9 levels where you can do whatever you want. Unlock 3D surreal levels.

In his dreams, Bob can very rarely walk straightly without staggering and falling on different sides. Climb, grab, push, lift and move to overcome the next obstacle and find yourself somewhere you haven’t been before. Master the main character’s movements to better solve puzzles.

Play local or multiplayer co-op with friends and random users. Throw each other, throw other Beans from catapults, fight, push and do everything that the game allows you. Find other solutions and paths that can only be completed with other players. Get your hands on a hiker costume, a dog costume, a construction worker costume, and other customization features to make your character unique. Enjoy good physics and innovative controls.
