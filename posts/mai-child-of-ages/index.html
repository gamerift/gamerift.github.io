Game Description#

MAI: Child of Ages tells about the adventures of the main character in the split world looking for himself after the last Great War. Together with her grandfather, she wanders through the era, studying the connection between antiquity and the gloomy future. The purpose of the trip is to learn about the events that entailed the disappearance of mankind, and to find out the origin of mysterious creatures that threaten the Universe. May collects evidence that helps restore the picture of the past and determine the fate of the world. A mysterious mentor helps her master a powerful artifact that can control time.

The gameplay in MAI: Child of Ags changes dynamically, alternating accurate platforming and battles in the spirit of Hack and Slash. Gamers have to control May at different stages of her life, using unique abilities from childhood and youth to overcome obstacles. Passing, built according to the principles of metroids, opens up new skills that expand the possibilities for research and battle. The heroine owns the stone of the Uroboro - a relic, which allows manipulating time to freeze objects and solve complex riddles. With the help of a diary, she creates weapons and tools from collected materials.

A third -party dynamic combat system requires a player of technical skills, including evasion, protection and attacks in near and distant battle. In this game, it is necessary to encounter creatures-reprints that captured the world, the victories over which are opened by new transformations for the stone of the Uroboro. The environment is filled with ancient relics and collection objects that reveal the secrets of a long -lost civilization. Additional puzzles and tests are a test of strategic thinking. In cooperative mode, the second gamer can provide support by controlling enemies in battle.
