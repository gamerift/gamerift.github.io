Game Description#

Voidsayer takes users to the world, captured by the abyss - all -consuming power, which leaves nothing behind. As one of the few survivors, you must accept this gloomy challenge, armed with a team of monsters. However, unlike others, you can repulse not with simple weapons, but taming and developing creatures. These are not just pets - they will become an integral part of your survival. However, there is one catch. The resources are scarce, and the battles you face will not be easy. Each step can be the last if you do not carefully choose a strategy.

The gameplay itself is a mixture of classic step -by -step battle with an intricate highlight. A hundred monsters that you can collect have its own character, statistics and genetic features that can be improved. Do you think you can rely only on rough power? Think again. Each monster requires attention, careful planning and proper equipment to show itself from the best side. Evolution does not occur automatically - each creature needs to be cultivated, so each choice is crucial for your survival.

There is no single formula of success, the whole thing is about adaptation and experiments. Now let’s talk about the levels. Forget about straightforward dungeons. Voidsayer offers procedural levels that constantly change the situation. At one point, you go through it, and in another, an accidental event may fall on your team, which will lead everything out of balance. Perhaps you will encounter an abyss distortion, or maybe an unexpected test will make you revise your entire approach.
