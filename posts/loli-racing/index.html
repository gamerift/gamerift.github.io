Game Description#

LOLI RACING is a dynamic arcade racing game in which anime characters compete for championship on a variety of tracks. The project invites gamers to plunge into the world of high-speed racing, where not only reflexes play a key role, but also the ability to enter a controlled skid. The competition takes place on 38 unique tracks, each with its own style, from Japanese temples and historical sites to futuristic metropolises and urban landmarks in Taiwan.

There are 15 different karts and characters available to participate in the races, each of which has unique characteristics. Vehicles differ in speed, acceleration, handling and drift efficiency, and their appearance matches the style of the chosen hero. The gameplay in LOLI RACING is easy to learn, requiring the driver to only control the direction of movement and use the skid at the right time to navigate turns. There are both single races and multiplayer competitions that allow you to challenge your friends.

Competitions for every taste:

Single career. Test your skills in a series of races of increasing difficulty. Network battles. Compete with other racers online to become the best. Variety of trails. Discover a variety of themed tracks inspired by real and fictional locations. Personalization. Find the perfect combination of character and kart to suit your driving style.
