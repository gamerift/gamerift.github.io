Game Description#

Mr. Prepper is a construction simulator where you have to build a bunker under a residential building. Mr. The survivor of those who believe in the theory of conspiracies and understands that a serious threat of nuclear war hung over the world. He does not care about his neighbors and those who do not believe him, so he alone begins to build his own bomb shelter. The nuclear war is close and you must be ready!

Like any other survival, Mr. Prepper offers to collect many materials and resources to engage in the construction of premises and create a bunker that will help it live for many years underground. But the paranoid government, forever calling the door and clearly suspecting that something is wrong with the survivor, is a special problem. Features of the gameplay:

building underground shelter in the style of Fallout Shelter; Create farms, greenhouses, tools, transport and much more underground; Join the trading with your neighbors: sell unnecessary things and buy useful materials; Be sure to draw up a global action plan; Be on the alert, because the secret police are watching you: they do not want you to know a lot of important information.
