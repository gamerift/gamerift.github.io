Game Description#

Winter Falling: Battle Tactics invites gamers to lead the troops and lead them to victory in the most stressful and tactical battles. And only your mind can save the dying world from devastation. You control a detachment of mercenaries, collect supplies and troops, perform tasks and even you can burn villages if you decide that you need it. But do not forget that all your actions have consequences.

The battles take place in real time, but you can choose your speed or stop the game in order to carefully consider your further actions. Plan a strategy on the world map, control the army and make sure that your troops have enough food. The game also offers the opportunity to create your own scenarios using the in -game cards editor and share them with other players. You can create your own units, weapons and races, thanks to the support of modding. Winter Falling: Battle Tactics is inspired by classics, such as Warhammer: Shadow of the Horned Rat, FTL and TOTAL WAR. The gameplay is unusual and is a synthesis of tactical RTS battles with an active pause, global card and your army management to complete current tasks. This is a fairly hardcore game, and each time you will have to start again.
