Game Description#

Void Crew invites you to go on an exciting journey through space with your crew members. In this high-stakes interstellar odyssey, teamwork is paramount as you and your friends face the challenges of deep space together. You will find yourself at the helm of a modern spaceship, which will rush through outer space, performing the most ordinary tasks. Asteroid storms, solar flares and random mistakes by crew members - there is never a dull moment in the game. Enemy aliens and desperate scavengers of humanity will test the crew’s strength as they try to reclaim lost territories from humanity.

Each crew member plays an important role. You can control the ship, man the turrets, perform emergency repairs, charge power cells, use the space station’s weapons, and even go on extravehicular missions to repair the ship’s hull or collect the wreckage of sunken ships. Working as a team is very important, but you can also work alone, trying to cope with all the tasks at once. The game offers various strategies to combat enemies - from brute force to accurate shooting. Victory can earn you valuable upgrade materials to ensure your crew is well-equipped even in the toughest conditions. Plus, you can customize your character’s suit to give it a unique style and boldly go where no one has gone before. Void Crew supports co-op play for 2-4 players, making it a great choice for wild space adventures with friends. Flexible player roles and ship customization options allow you to adapt your strategy on the fly, keeping the gameplay fresh and exciting.
