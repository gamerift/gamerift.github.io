Game Description#

No Rest for the Wicked immerses players in a world ravaged by death and despair. Developed by Moon Studios, the creators of Ori and the Blind Forest, this action RPG promises challenging combat and deep human storytelling. The kingdom mourns the death of King Harol, leaving his untested son Magnus on the throne. Meanwhile, an ancient plague known as the Blight returns, corrupting the land and its inhabitants. Amid the chaos, Madrigal Celine, hungry for power in the church, seeks to use the crisis for his own selfish purposes.

Players take on the role of Serim, a mystical warrior tasked with destroying the Blight. However, the fight against evil will not be easy. Serim becomes embroiled in the plight of suffering people and a complex political struggle for power. Every decision carries weight, determining the fate of the kingdom and the fate of Serim himself. The game features brutal strategic battles. Players battle monstrous creatures, soldiers, and challenging bosses while mastering a variety of weapons and skills. Crafting and customization allow you to create individual buildings suitable for each player.

The story takes place on the banks of the Isola Sacra and touches on themes of maturity and darkness. Cunning rulers and rebels vie for power as the plague transforms lives into twisted parodies of their former selves. The exploration takes place in a carefully crafted world - a living picture, replete with details. From sun-drenched meadows to the shady Nameless Pass, each location holds its own secrets, people and treasures. Looking for solace? The city of Sacramento offers you refuge. Players can buy and decorate properties, fish, farm, and interact with townspeople, gradually restoring the city to its former glory.

And for those who are looking for communication, up to three friends can join the campaign in online co-op, sharing with them the quests, challenges and open spaces of Isola Sacra. No Rest for the Wicked promises a journey through a world on the brink of destruction. Will the Serim cleanse the land of evil and pave a new path for the kingdom, or will darkness consume everyone?
