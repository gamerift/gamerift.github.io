Game Description#

Blightbound is a fantasy adventure that you can play online with a friend for free, or assemble a full-fledged team of three heroes to go through the Internet online or on a shared screen in a rich dark fantasy campaign about how the Plague overtook the once peaceful lands . The pirated multiplayer dungeon crawler Blightbound provides all online features and allows you to unlock all additional content, including heroes, modes, weapons, skins, accessories, etc. Alone or in the company of friends, you must enter the abyss of the mysterious fog to fight the monsters hiding in the darkness.

Despite the fact that the Dark Colossus was defeated 10 years ago, people did not have time to enjoy the world, because… the remains of the ear gave birth to the Plague. Now, using the opportunity of online play, you need to assemble an army of daredevils who can resist the threat. The team consists of three characters: a warrior, an assassin and a mage. A network game is built on constant interaction between participants. Each protagonist performs a specific function in the party and has individual attack patterns, a unique battle strategy, etc. You will have to fight both ordinary undead and powerful bosses. The levels feature a huge number of puzzles and various secrets. Gameplay Features: procedural generation of dungeons, secrets and enemies; over 20 characters to unlock; over 100 items to collect; many artifacts that enhance abilities; rescuing master craftsmen and improving the base; 3 regions to explore: Cemetery, Underfort and Bloody Ridge; the use of special abilities of heroes and constant interaction.
