Game Description#

Cavalry Girls is an exciting top-down shooter that immerses you in a futuristic world where brave and fearless girl soldiers fight unforgiving enemies. This addicting game seamlessly combines action, strategy and customization to deliver an immersive gaming experience. In a world where the versatile mineral “jade” fuels a bionic revolution and sparks conflict, players take on the role of commander of a team that directs mechanized armor-piercing girls to defend a besieged city from enemy forces.

Cavalry Girls offers a multi-faceted experience that includes combat and casual gameplay. In combat scenarios, players will have to carefully select equipment, form teams, collect powerful mechs, and use all kinds of weapons and skills to confront the enemy. The roguelike element introduces unpredictability as enemies are randomly generated and resources are limited, requiring strategic decisions and resource management to successfully fight the dangerous battlefield. On the other hand, the casual aspect allows players to interact with five different characters, each with a unique personality and backstory. Players can customize their dorms, strengthen bonds, give gifts to their comrades, and strengthen affection and loyalty. The game introduces new systems including Ideology, Synthesis, and Disassembly, allowing players to influence the story, craft equipment, and use enemy loot to their advantage.
