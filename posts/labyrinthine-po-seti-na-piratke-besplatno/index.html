Game Description#

Labyrinthine can be played online with a friend, or you can survive alone, trying to uncover the secrets of the dark labyrinth. Up to four people, united online via the Internet, the team will experience the horrors that this mystical place contains. Servers on the pirate site provide all the functions of the original game, and in-game content is available completely free of charge. Once inside a nightmare, you need to solve numerous puzzles, find quest items, hide or run away from bloodthirsty monsters. The labyrinth contains many secrets, and playing online together with your friends will help you get to the bottom of the truth.

Events take place in a place called Heppisburgh. The possibility of free online play includes searching not only for key items, but also for friends. Having found a compass, you can track your movement, and a signal flare will help your friends find their way around where to look for your hero. Audio diaries and notes are available everywhere, lifting the veil of secrecy about this strange structure. It is very important to try to stick together as a team. However, this is not so simple, since different game situations, be it traps or monsters, force players to split up.

You can play alone, but Labyrinthine reveals its full potential when played together. The protagonists find themselves at the fair, where the Fir Hotel used to be located. There are rumors that at one time people disappeared in this territory and a huge number of murders were committed. It all started in 1800 when the building was built. Not long ago, a boy named Charles disappeared. The cops who went looking for him also never returned. Is this a coincidence?..
