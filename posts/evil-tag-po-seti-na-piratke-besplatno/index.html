Game Description#

Survival horror gameEvil Tag allows you to play online for free as a pirate, teaming up to four people online online to undergo a rite of passage. Their task is to read a spell from an ancient book. Events take place in the basement of an abandoned estate. After completing the ritual, the heroes will need to spend the whole night in the mansion.

The students thought that this ritual was just childishness and they would have fun, but no one even guessed what a disaster their idea would turn out to be. The free online play feature of Evil Tag includes constant interaction between the heroes. Once the students have freed the ancient evil, they will need to free themselves from the shackles of the curse, all the while passing it on to someone else. The online game invites you to join forces to drive evil back into purgatory. You need to hold out until dawn to leave the cursed building.

You can hide in lockers, ride a bike to cover vast distances, move through dark areas of the location, hiding from a mystical entity, etc. However, the evil spirit does not intend to give up and will do everything possible to ensure that at least one of the teenagers remains in the mansion forever. When moving into people, your closest partner can become an enemy, so you need to be extremely careful. Will all participants be able to escape or will one of them fail?
