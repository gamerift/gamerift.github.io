Game Description#

Space Engineers 2 returns to the world of space development with updated experience. In this continuation, you will have a new adventure in the Almagest system, where engineering tasks and spaces of space are combined in the most unexpected way. This is a sandbox with an open world in which you can build from scratch - or, rather, from stars - from starships to entire space stations. The goal is simple: to raise your creative potential to a new height, while managing the difficulties of survival in the cold, inexorable spaces of space.

What distinguishes Space Engineers 2 is the level of freedom that it offers. The game provides a high level of customization and control, creating the feeling that your engineering projects are limited only by your imagination. Thanks to the updated Vrage3 engine, construction, destruction and even navigation in space have never been so smooth. The new 25-centimeter unified reshet system guarantees that everything will be easily combined with each other, eliminating the old restrictions that complicated the construction.

Whether it is an accurate starship or a massive base on an asteroid, this game offers accuracy as never before. Updated controls and functions also change the approach of users to construction. More intuitive controls, such as the abolition of the grid, accurate rotation and faster movement, take each step more natural. It is easy to plunge into the project, correct it on the fly and observe its development.
