Game Description#

Mini Motor Racing EVO is a racing arcade game that offers the opportunity to play online with a friend, competing for free on a pirate in multiplayer mode. Online Internet connection and local races are available on one screen. Multiplayer game Mini Motor Racing EVO is a battle royale game, combining elements of classic arcade racing with survival mechanics. Several multiplayer modes are included, setting different conditions for victory.

By defeating opponents, you earn money. The proceeds are used to upgrade cars and buy upgrades. By participating in multiplayer races, you can fight with opponents in several versus modes. A single player campaign is available, represented by a set of different missions. With each subsequent race the difficulty of the gameplay increases. Smart artificial intelligence is introduced that analyzes the player’s actions. Rival cars also make mistakes that give them a chance to get ahead. Each race is completely unpredictable. Key features of the project: possibility of free online play; full-fledged single-player campaign; bonus cars from Team Fortress and Portal 2; various improvements and upgrades; team competitions; leaderboards and records; custom track editor.
