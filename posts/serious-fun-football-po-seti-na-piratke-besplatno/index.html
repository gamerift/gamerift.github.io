Game Description#

On the pirateSerious Fun Football you can play online with random players or with a friend, competing for free on the pirate both over a local network and online online. The gameplay is real serious fun football. The main emphasis is on realistic physics and puppet ragdoll. Online play allows you to split into teams of three to show off your football skills to your opponents.

It is noteworthy that there are no corners, no penalties and all the basic football rules. All you need to do is aggressively take the ball from your opponents and score goals. A career mode is available, where you will need to climb the career ladder, starting from the very bottom and moving to the top of the sports Olympus. You can create your own football player by giving him appearance parameters. Wide customization of the athlete with a set of hairstyles and various attributes is presented. There are two difficulty modes: serious and fun. Gameplay and features of Serious Fun Football: Single mode, the possibility of free online play in co-op and much more; Full-fledged career; Management of a team or an individual character; Tournament tables and ratings; Wide range of hero customization; In-game currency and market; Several options cameras on the field; Two completely different game modes.
