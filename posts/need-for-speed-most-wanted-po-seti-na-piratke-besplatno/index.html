Game Description#

On the pirateNeed For Speed ​​Most Wanted you can play online for free with a friend. There is the possibility of cooperative play and a competitive mode is presented online on the Internet. Both online play and single player campaign are available. NFSMW takes place in the city of Rockport. You are allowed to freely explore areas of the city, moving along the Black List. According to the plot, the main character must win back his BMW M3 GTR from Razor.

Free online play features include various street races. You can compete in modes such as Ring, Sprint, Elimination and Fighter. Each race offers unique conditions for victory. For example, a sprint is a race from point A to point B; in a knockout, the race is completed by the player who was the last to reach the finish line, etc. The police have returned to NFS Most Wanted. During racing, a chase may begin.

To escape the patrols, you must either find a way to break away from them, or hide in specially designated areas. You can use your surroundings to stop your pursuers. Need For Speed ​​Most Wanted’s multiplayer mode is designed for four players. All participants save money to buy new cars or can spend money on tuning existing cars.

Allows you to pump up the technical parameters of the cars, be it the engine, transmission, suspension, tires, turbocharging, brakes or nitrous oxide. The blacklist consists of 15 riders. The main characters are Mia Townsend, Clarence Callahan and Cross. The game world consists of three centers: Rosewood, Camden Beach and Downtown. Each district is represented by unique streets and other features.
