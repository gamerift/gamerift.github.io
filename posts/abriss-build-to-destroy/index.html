Game Description#

ABRISS - build to destroy is a simulator based on physical laws. The gameplay is focused on building an atmospheric layer that destroys physics. The user has access to many designs that can crash into specified targets and destroy them. The story campaign gradually opens up access to new details, which will allow you to destroy large creations and observe entropy in digital and futuristic landscapes.

Opportunities in construction and destruction:

Selection of parts and components. Each object and object has a different weight, shape and function for a seamless design. Changing the direction of the device, their connectors and installing bombs to build rockets. Designing spinning laser missiles using three main components: thruster, spinner and laser. To win a level, you need to hit the main targets. Sometimes you first need to destroy an armored wall, and then attack vulnerable areas.

The gameplay features a complex destruction system. Being in different parts of the atmosphere, gravity changes, as do the laws of physics. There is an imitation of static, thousands of flying debris particles of small and large sizes. The campaign includes 7 worlds to overcome. Each episode features a destructible cityscape.
