Game Description#

High Sea Saga DX immerses players in the marine environment, focused on the accumulation of wealth and global research. In this Title, gamers control the pirate team crossing the oceans in search of hidden treasures scattered through a variety of geographical zones. Each such journey entails a clash with various wild creatures and battles with warring pirate fractions that pursue similar goals. Success requires the collection of an effective detachment and the construction of a swimming agent, ready for long -term crossings by open waters.

In High Sea Saga DX, users begin operations with a set of crew members in the tavern before starting the study tasks. Companions on the ship have a variety of skills, allowing you to configure the team to perform specific tasks. The completion of such instructions expands the affordable selection of quests and opens access to larger ships models. These aquatic vessels contain many objects, making it possible to change the internal layout to achieve surgical superiority.

Having a equipped ship and a close -knit group, travelers go on sea expeditions, applying unknown lands to the map. These marine campaigns involve the opening of previously unknown islands and the discovery of continents unexpected on maps. The construction of the base provides tactical support for the entire composition, which is extremely important for long sorties. Such a consistent development, including research, construction of the base, as well as a constant improvement of personnel and vessel, helps to continue adventure and the entire gaming process.
