Game Description#

Bio Inc. Redemption is a biomedical simulator where a player has to choose life or death. These are 2 main regimes where you need to perform a variety of tasks. You are able to create deadly diseases, mocking a person presented on the screen. Or put on the medic’s mask and find the virus, look for the vaccine and save the infected ones.

The gameplay provides more than 600 diseases existing in the real world, accurate symptoms, diagnostic tests and various ways of treatment for each virus. The game is incredibly realistic and completely immerse you in the gameplay, charming your microscopic world.

Participate in free mode or pass the missions for one of the parties in 2 different campaigns. The game was recreated from scratch after it became very popular among mobile applications. The computer version is more deepened and realistic, and new opportunities have also appeared.

Play the plot mode for a real hero - a medic, curing people from diseases. First you need to diagnose the disease, and then find the medicine. Each subsequent task will be complicated. Or examine the dark side and try to make a truly ominous disease. Overcome obstacles, for example, immunity, vaccines and develop improved deadly diseases.
