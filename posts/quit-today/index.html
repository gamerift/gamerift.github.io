Game Description#

QUIT TODAY is a 2D sid-screen in the action genre with a setting of a corporate workplace. The plot starts on ordinary morning, when the central character enters the office and finds out that all colleagues are confident in his imminent dismissal. Employees perceive this as a betrayal and communicate with him. First, the hero fights off his portfolio, trying to figure out what is happening. Upon learning of the dark affairs in the company, he decides to break through all departments with a battle in order to submit an application for care.

Key aspects of the last working day:

Collisions with staff: Allow conflicts with aggressive colleagues using your case. Random improvements: choose skills from the proposed sets to adapt to gameplay. Allies support: call for help from partners during stressful battles.

The further game process QUIT Today consists of clashes with employees in various departments, each of which throws new tests. If you can not resolve the conflict with words that are opponents, fists are used. In Title, the player applies his case to resolve such situations and promote the goal.

During this adventure, the protagonist receives random resources and chooses from different sets of skills, mastering the science of survival in the office. Even in such a hostile atmosphere, the hero finds allies who are ready to support. Partners can be called to help during the fights. In addition to direct participation in fights, they also open up opportunities to strengthen combat skills or improve working conditions in their divisions of the company. This game makes it possible to influence the environment.
