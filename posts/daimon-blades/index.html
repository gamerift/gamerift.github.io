Game Description#

DAIMON BLADES sends players on expeditions across a chaotic dimension, where players act as warrior monks from the cult of the Secret. Available for solo gamers or cooperative squads of up to 4 people, these raids are designed to contain daimonic forces. The central mission is to hunt down the Hermit, a mentor who betrayed the order. The player must descend into the depths of this world to find the arch-traitor and interrupt his ritual of devouring souls before completion.

The game includes character customization, resource gathering, and item crafting. Gameplay in DAIMON BLADES centers on daimon-possessed weapons inhabited by an entity with a unique set of attributes and abilities. These living weapons absorb the blood of slain enemies to strengthen and unlock new skills during a raid. Between campaigns, its power can be permanently increased at the forge, providing deeper development and increasing the chances of survival.

Blades are not just instruments of destruction, since they carry a harmful influence, which their owners should not underestimate. The narrative is a prelude to the events of E.Y.E: Divine Cybermancy, taking place two millennia before and detailing the origins of the Order of the Secret through sacrifice and bloodshed. Treks take place across 9 biomes with procedural generation, offering unique levels on each playthrough.

What awaits you in the depths of the daimonic world:

Dangerous forays alone or in the company of up to three friends. A living weapon that absorbs the blood of enemies to strengthen itself. Procedurally generated biomes full of unique threats and challenges. Dozens of enemy types and powerful bosses with different tactics. A deep system of character development through the creation of objects and damage.
