Game Description#

GAS Station Simulator - simulator of owning its own refueling, combined with the raising station for road transport, which is located in deserted areas, near the number 66, which is in the United States of America. There is an opportunity to control the gameplay using a gamepad. The player will have to refuel with all the affairs with the help of a virtual protege with a first -person view. Clients will not wait long, they want to get a quality service at the shortest period of time. Its further development depends on positive reviews from visitors to the car repair shop. By increasing its profit, the gamer opens access to new improvements for the building from the inside and outside, as well as to more modern tools and gas stations, which help to serve drivers much faster. There are 100+ options for improving business.

An important aspect is the timely replenishment of goods, which are sold in the shop, for example, motor oil or tire sets. The fullness of the services offered will depend on their availability, which will affect the overall impression of the client and his desire to return more than once. There are more than 50 characters with several dozens of possible problems and wishes. Each requires an individual approach and has a different amount of time to expect a service from a player.

Indie developers implemented a system of random events, which complicates the gameplay. With a huge expansion of the client base, the player will need to hire workers that add a headache, they may not go to work, be late, steal the details. All these situations need to be decided as soon as possible so that the business can continue without difficulties in the form of unscrupulous workers.
