Game Description#

Digger: Galactic Treasures puts the player in the role of a worker in a distant future where humanity has spread throughout space. This expansion was driven by the search for minerals, but habitable planets remain a rarity. The entire galaxy is controlled by the megacorporation GRAVEX Inc., which exploits the labor of miners and treasure hunters. The story begins with the inheritance of a decommissioned, half-disassembled ship and a notebook from his grandfather, who once worked as a researcher in a corporation.

The journal contains the coordinates of treasures and fragments of the mysterious artifact Heart of the Void. The main task in Digger: Galactic Treasures is to find and assemble an ancient device. According to legend, the Heart of the Void is capable of generating matter, allowing the creation of new worlds for people. This potential has not escaped the attention of GRAVEX Inc., which has also directed its efforts to search for the relic, which creates a direct conflict for control over it. To get ahead of the company, the gamer must navigate the galaxy, exploring asteroids and abandoned planets.

The gameplay involves the extraction of rare materials and minerals for the subsequent modernization of equipment, personal tools and an inherited ship. Exploration yields not only raw materials, but also rare objects from long-lost civilizations that form pieces of a larger puzzle. Finding every fragment of the Heart of the Void is the main goal, because assembling it will reveal the secret. The revelation associated with the device can radically change the fate of the entire civilization and its place in the universe.
