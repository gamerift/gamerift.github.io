Game Description#

Diesel Brothers Truck Building Simulator is a mechanic simulator that allows you to play as a pirate online with a friend, build cars alone, or cooperate with other players online online. Having opened your workshop World of Diesels, you need to start earning a reputation by collecting spare parts for free from landfills or flea markets. Having started repairing cars, you need to create a name for yourself as a skilled craftsman capable of constructing a powerful pickup truck to participate in racing competitions.

By repairing used cars or creating them from scratch, you can both sell your masterpieces and give them to other players using the opportunity of free online play. The tuning simulator allows you to search for faulty cars and create real off-road monsters out of them. You can start designing a car right from creating the frame. A wide range of spare parts are available in Diesel Brothers Truck Building Simulator, from engines and gearboxes to bolts and screws. Painting is carried out manually or automatically. Network play allows you to team up with up to three friends. Everyone creates their own unique avatar, selecting its appearance parameters and skills.

While exploring junkyards, you need to find different parts to create works of art from them! The body of a pickup truck can be decorated with non-standard stickers, applied unusual colors and added zest that makes the car truly unique. You can test your tuning skills in action by going to online or single-player competitions on numerous tracks, including tracks for standard racing and rallies. Each participant enters their car into competitions, showing a master class in extreme races, where they are allowed to perform a huge number of tricks, going through a variety of crazy tests.
