Game Description#

On pirateCossacks 3 you can play online for free with a friend. You can play the game in co-op or compete online. Each player builds his own base, collects resources, trains troops, etc. Military units are divided into infantry, cavalry, artillery and navy. Cossacks 3 sets players one global task - to destroy the enemy base. However, tasks in different missions may vary. Multiplayer allows you to place up to 32,000 units on one screen.

The missions in Cossacks 3 are very different. You can fight on the side of one of 12 nations. Each power has its own unique characteristics and battle tactics. In total, there are 70 types of units, more than 100 scientific research and about 140 different tasks. Cossacs 3 has an extensive editor available that allows you to create your own locations and tasks. Free online play includes co-op play through 5 historical campaigns.

Each scenario offers a series of exciting missions that include random events, unique enemies and much more. The campaigns are represented by countries such as Austria (How to become a Generalissimo), England (Roundheads against the Cavaliers), Ukraine (How the Cossacks gained glory), Russia (Cold, yes, need, and royal service) and France (The Rise of the French Navy). Also available to play through are 6 additional countries, including Poland, Prussia, Sweden, etc., as well as a number of single-player missions in scenarios such as Days of Splendor, Rise to Glory and the Golden Age.
