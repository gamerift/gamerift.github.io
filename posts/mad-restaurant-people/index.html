Game Description#

Mad Restaurant People makes it possible to feel all the difficulty of working as a waiter. The game is presented with a top distance, which makes it possible to observe the entire hall at once. Take orders, serve customers, serve dishes in time, count the guests and much more. Mad Restaurant People is a full -fledged waiter’s simulator who will not let you relax for a second.

The gameplay requires attentiveness, reaction speed, good memory and many other important qualities. Your task is simple - to satisfy customer needs. But to angry them will be easier. Clock the order or carry a dish for a long time - it is likely that the guest will be dissatisfied. Do not force guests to wait long! The story campaign is divided into levels. One or another stage can be replayed an unlimited number of times. Calculate the time, study the hall and try to move as quickly as possible. With every minute, there are more and more guests, so try to pay equal amount of attention to everyone. Features of the gameplay:

many different drinks and dishes that need to be remembered - everything is as in life; Each level sets different goals complicated in the process of passage; thematic episodes, various events and holidays; Two additional modes: blood pressure and new game+; A unique soundtrack for each level.
