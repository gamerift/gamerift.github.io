Game Description#

Sword of the Necromancer is a co-op adventure that can be played online with a friend, free on a pirate ship, helping Tama save Coco. Both local co-op and online playthroughs with a partner are available. The main character uses the power of the forbidden Necromancer Sword, turning enemies into allies. Multiplayer allows you to join forces with a friend to reach the depths of a dark dungeon together.

The plot of Sword of the Necromancer tells about the lengths to which a person can go to win back his love. To do this, you will need to fight powerful guards to get enough soul power. With their help, it will be possible to resurrect your beloved from the dead. The possibility of free online play allows you to overcome a difficult path together, increasing the chances of Coco’s resurrection.

Mechanics include elements of role-playing games, dungeon crawlers and roguelikes. A special feature of the gameplay is that defeated enemies can be recruited to your side so that they fight alongside the players. Gathering an army of monsters, armed with ancient relics and legendary weapons, you need to break through dark dungeons, moving into the very depths of hell.

By destroying enemies, the hero’s level increases, making it possible to upgrade abilities and various attributes. You can use a huge number of auxiliary items to make battles with crowds of enemies easier. With every step the protagonist becomes closer to the Necromancer - the main guardian of souls. Homunculus infusion is a special artifact that allows you to create a copy of yourself. It is the double that must be controlled by the partner.
