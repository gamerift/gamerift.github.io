Game Description#

Star Command Galaxies is a space simulator in which you play as a ship captain. You need to build your own shuttle, recruit staff and go explore the randomly generated expanses of outer space. Manage your staff, collect resources, fight the alien threat and upgrade your ship. Clear the galaxy from enemies and various parasites. Fight pirates, zombies and many other opponents.

You need to monitor the status of your team. The mood of the state is the basis of a successful raid and battle. Feed your subordinates, give them rest, provide entertainment, etc., because if their mood begins to decline, they will begin to discover new “quirks” in themselves that can have a negative effect on the gameplay of Star Command Galaxies.

When a team successfully completes tasks, fights enemies, etc., they earn so-called “benefits” - personal bonuses, with the help of which new abilities are unlocked, the level increases, etc., which will allow the unit to be experienced in that or other area. With each new stage, the enemies become stronger. Upgrade your ship to stay on equal footing with space threats. Ahead of you are a variety of races, numerous missions, unique planets, a huge variety of equipment, ships and much more.
