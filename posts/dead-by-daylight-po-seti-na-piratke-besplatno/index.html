Game Description#

Dead by Daylight is a survival horror game that you can play online on a pirate, competing for free with a friend or a group of people in asymmetrical multiplayer over the Internet online. The opportunity for free online play invites you to gather in a small group of 5 people, where four participants take on the role of survivors, and the remaining player controls a killer maniac. Several monsters are presented, including the Hunter, the Witch, Jason, Pyramid Head, Nemesis and other monsters from famous franchises.

Dead by Daylight’s multiplayer gameplay is built around both co-op and adversarial play. The task of the survivors is to find ways to leave the area alive. To do this, you need to look for quest items and keys that will help open the main gate. The killer must do everything possible to kill every member of the squad. Depending on the choice of monster, the tactics of the game change. For example, you can set phantom traps, traps, make quick jumps and much more. The survivors must start a generator that will force the gate to open.

The monster can hang people on special hooks without immediately killing its enemies. You can also make your victim bleed, allowing her to suffer a fair amount before dying. People need to make as little noise as possible without attracting the attention of a maniac. Allows you to hide in closets, use the shadow to move unnoticed, etc. Any sudden movement causes noise and instantly reveals the location of the victims. At the beginning of the game, you can choose one of the abilities: Deer Hunter, Knockout, Crusher, etc. Improvements are made on a special bloody web. There are 5 auxiliary items available, divided by rarity. Heroes are pumped up to a maximum level of 30.
