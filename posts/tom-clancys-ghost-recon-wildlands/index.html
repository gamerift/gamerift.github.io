Game Description#

The open world of the new part -Tom Clancy’s Ghost Recon Wildlands - truly inspires what the developers tried to convict all players of. It must be said, but there is no plot in a third-person shooter. You independently write the story of your team, which is initially aimed at destroying the cartel that trades and produces drugs. But more on that later.

First, it’s worth discussing the inner world of Tom Clancy’s Ghost Recon Wildlands. The cartel is not just a bunch of stupid bots controlled by artificial intelligence, but professional killers, under which are scientists, the government, and several thousand stupid and smart thugs. They have their own history, commanders, bosses who manage individual branches within the whole network. Our heroes slowly introduce us to the situation, and at the same time call their squad “ghosts”. Because no one could declassify us like that. The action takes place on the territory of an entire country, in which the bosses of this non-underground organization operate in 4 corners throughout the open location. There is actually a huge variety of terrain.

Multiplayer is designed for 4 players. Creating your own squad of friends and going to conquer the death and torment of the cartel bosses is interesting, especially when the plot does not put pressure on you. At the beginning we create our “ghost”. He will have a lot of weapons to choose from from the very beginning, but we recommend getting a sniper rifle, because the opponents will have simple attack aircraft, scouts, snipers, and radio operators. The shooter received the “tactical” tag. Only friends or a skilled commander with a trained team can distribute the team’s tasks and act harmoniously and quickly. Exploration of the area, interaction with NPCs and people of Bolivia, destruction of Santa Blanca. The new part is truly made from the heart. Thanks to the developers.
