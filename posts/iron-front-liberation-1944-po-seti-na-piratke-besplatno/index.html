Game Description#

Iron Front: Liberation 1944 is a tactical shooter that allows you to play online with a friend and compete with random people from all over the world. Iron Front: Liberation 1944 covers the events of World War II, taking combatants to the Eastern Front. The online game is dedicated to the confrontation between German and Soviet troops. It takes about 10 hours of real time to complete the storyline.

The single player game is represented by two campaigns. Each faction in Iron Front: Liberation 194 has a unique type of troops and equipment. Events are developing in the South-East of Poland. The Soviet army breaks through the enemy’s defenses, and German soldiers try to slow down the Reds' advance deeper into Europe. The possibility of free online play allows you to control both infantrymen and aircraft with tanks, including Panzerkampfwagen IV, Konigstiger, IS-2, Focke Wulf 190, Stuka, Pe-2 and P-39. The gameplay is based on realism and heavy physics of both characters and weapons.

Multiplayer modes are represented by Team Battles, Attack and Defense, Blitzkrieg and Capture the Flag. A co-op mode is available, allowing you to go through missions from the story campaign together. An editor is introduced that includes a wide selection of assets for creating unique maps. You can create locations for both single battles and multiplayer battles. The arsenal of weapons consists of numerous guns that correspond to their real prototypes.
