Game Description#

The prison atmosphere in Back to the Dawn shows the life of an anthropomorphic animal that is forced to fight for the right to exist in a zone. Gamers will explore prison laws, rules and locations in which a protagonist finds himself. The main character will pump and make new acquaintances, relying on his own social skills. It will be possible to use cunning and intelligence to solve many problems, where the main task is an attempt to escape from imprisonment.

Users in Back to the Dawn get the opportunity:

To immerse yourself in difficult trials, presented in the form of harsh cellmates and evil guards protecting prisoners. To survive and search for a way to freedom, using a variety of social ties and tricks for this. Explore the surrounding area and find weak gaps through which you will be able to get out of freedom and escape from the observation of the guard. Study local life and adjust to it correctly to get an advantage and avoid troubles in the form of aggressive convicts. To deal with a variety of riddles and spatial puzzles, using the found objects for this. Interact with an interactive environment, interior objects and secondary NPS, talking with them and finding out useful information. To assemble a team of prisoners-union, with whom it will be possible to develop a plan for escape.
