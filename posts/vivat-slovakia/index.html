Game Description#

Vivat Slovakia offers players to plunge into the open world of the action that unfolds in the Slovakia of the 1990s. This country, which has recently gained independence and located at the junction of the East and West, is influenced by the remnants of the communist era and at the same time emerging capitalism. This situation weakens state institutions, creating the conditions for the spread of corruption, where power is in resources. The gamer will have to get used to the role of a secret agent of the police acting in these conditions, with a third -person view.

The main scene in the Vivat Slovakia Title is the recreated Bratislava with an area of ​​about 10 square kilometers, where city streets and iconic places of that time are represented. The taxi driver’s legend is used to study the environment and promotion on the storyline. The gameplay provides for the management of more than 50 models of vehicles - from machines of the socialist era to rare imported cars, which reflects the social separation and fleet of those years. Driving and shootings with a third -person view form the basis of the gameplay in this urban setting.

The plot line of the project is based on the real events of Slovakia of the 90s, affecting the issues of devotion, systemic corruption and the struggle for influence. Tasks include interaction within changeable unions, where the criminal world is closely connected with political circles. This setting depicts a city in which gang activities are not hidden, law enforcement forces are inactive, and violence occurs openly. To survive in this game, it is necessary to interact with similar realities using firearms created according to the samples of that era.
