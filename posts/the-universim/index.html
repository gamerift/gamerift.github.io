Game Description#

A simulator of city construction, the development of an entire planet and a ward civilization -The Universim is a short sand strategy in which you have to try on the role of God. The game begins with the fact that you settle several people on one of the planets, give them food, indicate what needs to be done - set priorities. And then watch how people develop on their own, without your help. But who are you if not God? You still have control over everything they do. You can even change thoughts and intentions, punish pranksters and bandits, give people something or take it away, depending on their behavior.

The people you place on the planet are called Nuggets. These are unique creatures with their own characteristics, which are also personalized - each personality has its own characteristics, views, health and appearance. Quite beautiful and quite interesting graphics of the game show you the full control of the planet: punish, give, encourage, observe and influence, because you are God. You have time, natural disasters and disasters, changes in thoughts, punishment and more than 20 different abilities with which you can influence your Nuggets.

At the beginning of the game you choose a planet. So that real Nuggets, who over time become like real people - in behavior, way of thinking and movements - can exist peacefully, you need to give them the right direction in construction and thought. For example, food and water must be on the planet. They will get them themselves, but how will they come to this idea? To keep creatures' Happiness levels from decreasing, they need entertainment. Give them one!

The development of civilization also depends on resources. For example, if you tell them to kill one animal, it will become scarce and then completely disappear from the earth; if the Nuggets build factories and pollute the environment, then the elements will overtake them even without your intervention. If they work at night and sleep during the day, then a disruption of the biological rhythm will lead to the collapse of civilization!
