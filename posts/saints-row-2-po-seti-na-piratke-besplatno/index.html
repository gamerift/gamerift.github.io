Game Description#

Saints Row 2 is an open world action game that players can play together online on a pirate for free. On the Internet online you can not only compete, but also go through the story campaign with a friend. The main difference from the GTA series is that at the beginning everyone can customize their hero themselves, choosing gender, race, appearance and physique. During the game you can change the appearance of the character.

The gameplay allows you to engage in hooliganism, earn extra money, perform tricks and complete tasks. Network play includes not only story missions, but also many cooperative quests. A huge emphasis is placed on humor, so the adventure constantly throws up funny and funny tasks. The ability to play for free online allows you to explore the city of Stillwater, steal vehicles, fight opposing factions, blow up everything in your path, and much more. Story missions vary in difficulty. There are both easy and difficult quests. A wide arsenal of weapons is available, ranging from melee weapons to large-caliber guns.

The story centers on the 3rd Street Saints, led by Johnny Gat. The main character is Gat’s friend and partner. The main goal of the group is to confront other gangs by establishing control over the city. Supporting characters include Pierce Washington, Shaundi, Carlos Mendoza, Troy Bradshaw and many others. Gangs in Saints Row 2: Children of Samedi - reggae fans who worship the voodoo deity - the God of the dead; Ronin - samurai without a goal and a master, reminiscent of typical yakuza; Brotherhood - lovers of heavy music who grew up in poor neighborhoods; Ultor - company , which captured most of the shops and shopping centers.
