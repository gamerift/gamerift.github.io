Game Description#

On the pirateAge of Empires 3 Definitive Edition you can play online for free with a friend, fighting in epic battles locally or online online. Age of Empires 3 allows you to take control of a general to lead your people to wealth and prosperity. There are many factions to choose from. Each power has its own unique abilities and characteristics. Multiplayer and single player campaign available.

Emphasis is placed on historical facts. The events of the storyline begin in Malta in 1565. The campaign consists of 3 acts: Blood, Ice and Steel. The player needs to take control of a hero named Morgan Black. The possibility of free online play allows you to control one of 8 powers: Great Britain, Prussia, Holland, Spain, Portugal, France, the Russian Empire and the Ottoman Empire. All factions contain unique units and equipment.

Age of Empires III Definitive Edition includes two extensive expansions, The WarChiefs and The Asian Dynasties, adding Japan, China and India to the game. Global map available. To assemble a powerful army, you will need to mine various resources. Expanding your possessions and recruiting soldiers into the army, you must fulfill a number of conditions in order to move into a new era. Upon achieving the goal, new opportunities, units, equipment, etc. open up.

Battles take place on interactive maps with the participation of a huge number of units. An army may consist of infantry, cavalry, siege weapons, ships, etc. On the battlefield, it is not the total number of units that decides, but the tactical abilities of the player as a commander. In multiplayer matches, several participants can fight on the same map. The winner is the one who defeats the entire enemy army.
