Game Description#

Chippy and Noppo is a cooperative platformer with puzzle elements, the main characters of which are Chappie and Nippo. You can play the pirate game online for free with a friend, controlling the heroes. One player controls Chappie and solves puzzles on the lower platforms, while the other plays Nippo and jumps over various obstacles. In Chippy and Noppo you can play locally and online online on one screen. The possibility of free online play is built on cooperative interaction. Each character has their own limitations, requiring them to perform completely different actions to progress through the story.

The main action takes place in a toy factory. The protagonists need to collect parts from broken toys, change the color and shape of found objects in order to create the necessary items according to the received drawings. An online game constantly separates the roles of the participants, setting individual goals for them. The locations include not only the industrial premises of the plant, but also dense forests, deep seas and other memorable levels. At the end of each stage, participants are awarded a variety of bonuses and receive a rating ranging from one to three stars. For successfully and quickly completing set goals, players are awarded the highest rating and receive additional points to purchase cosmetics and other auxiliary items.
