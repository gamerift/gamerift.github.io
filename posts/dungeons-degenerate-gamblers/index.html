Game Description#

Dungeons & Degenerate Gamblers throws a crooked ball into traditional card games. Forget about the ink, peaks and trefches. Here the king is a blackjack, but with a highlight - a Roguelike -inventor, that is. Imagine yourself in a dull lighted tavern, smoke falls from your tube while you evaluate your opponent at a shabby table. But instead of swords and witchcraft, your weapons are cards. Unique cards that can improve your hand, ruin the enemy’s deck or even affect the course of history. Fasten the belts because in Dungeons & Degenerate Gamblers the only guarantee is a clash of good luck and strategy in the epic battle for chips.

The heart is to create your own deck. It’s like the choice of a detachment of adventurers - will you choose a heavy attack, protective shield or a mixture of cunning useful cards? Each choice forms your style, and most importantly - there is no only right answer here. Experiments are the key to success, and with a huge number of cards at your disposal, the opportunity to create a winning deck is simply huge. Think about it as the creation of your own school of magic, only instead of fire balls you throw aces and eights. Each meeting in the tavern is a test. Your opponents are a motley team, each with its own quirks and strategies. Some can be masters of defense, and you will have to slowly destroy them.
