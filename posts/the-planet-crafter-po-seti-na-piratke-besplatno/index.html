Game Description#

The space sandboxThe Planet Crafter allows you to play for free as a pirate online with a friend, surviving on the surface of a distant planet. Over the Internet, online players can engage in terraforming, explore a large open world, confront a hostile ecosystem, and much more. The online game The Planet Crafter offers both joining forces and fighting in confrontation. Alone or using the opportunity of a free online game, you will need to collect resources, build a base, create all the conditions for people to live: oxygen, temperature, pressure, etc.

The adventure begins with researchers being sent to the far corner of the galaxy to explore a hostile planet suitable for human life. Before colonizing an unknown continent, it is necessary to create all the conditions for the human body. While collecting resources and building a base, you will also need to fight back against severe natural disasters and alien organisms living on the planet. The gameplay is based on survival and management mechanics. You need to manage resources, explore crashed space shuttles, build machines to study the soil, and much more.

Gradually, the planet comes to life and takes on the shape of the Earth, which makes it suitable for habitation. It is necessary to unlock various blueprints that help you master new technologies. They help you travel to the far corners of the map to collect important resources from shipwrecks. You always need to monitor your oxygen levels and quickly return to base.
