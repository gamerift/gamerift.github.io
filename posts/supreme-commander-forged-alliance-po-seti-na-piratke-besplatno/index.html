Game Description#

Supreme Commander Forged Alliance is a stand-alone add-on to the strategy of the same name, in which you can play online with a friend. For free, players can compete online on the pirate Supreme Commander Forged Alliance. The expansion introduced a new Seraphim faction, including 110 new units. Network play introduces additional modes for cooperative play and multiplayer confrontations.

The plot begins two years after the creation of the Black Sun weapon. New heroes appear who take the place of the fallen UEF President, General Clark and others. Aliens have descended to Earth and now former enemies need to join forces against a common threat. The campaign consists of 6 missions that are common to all factions.

6 additional missions are presented, including Black Day, Red Flag, Meltdown and other full-fledged tasks with their own mechanics and history. The possibility of free online play allows you to take control of one of four opposing factions: Seraphmi, OFZ, Eon and Cybran. Each side has its own unique characteristics and fights using unique tactics.

The main combat units are T1, T2, T3 and various experimental units. Seraphim fight with the help of Siege Tank: Othuum, Sniper Bot: Usha-Ah, Bomber: Ahwassa and others. The OFZ army consists of Gatling Bot: Mongoose, Heavy Air Transport: Continental, Satellite System: Novax Center, etc. Eon Soldiers include Assault Tank: Blaze, AA Gunship: Restorer, Resource Generator: Paragon, etc. Cybran’s composition is represented by Engineering Station: The Hive, Rocket Bot: Hoplite, Killer: Barracuda, Megabot: Megalith and many other units.
