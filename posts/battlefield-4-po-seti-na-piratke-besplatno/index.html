Game Description#

On the pirateBattlefield 4 you can play for free with a friend online, taking part in cooperative modes, or compete online with players from all over the world over the Internet. The gameplay of Battlefield 4 is a tactical first-person shooter. The network game of the fourth part features many innovations. For example, VoIP solutions have been improved, and a browser mode has been added, allowing you to monitor the game from above or from a third person. Free camera is also available.

BattleLog has been significantly updated, aimed at eSports. The ability to play for free online provides access to intelligence gathering and allows for information warfare. You can call in artillery support by launching cruise missile attacks. The Levolution function has appeared, allowing you to interact with the landscape in real time. The multiplayer mode is represented by 33 interactive maps. Depending on size, they accommodate from 16 to 64 participants. You can choose one of four classes of fighters. Each soldier has his own unique skills and characteristics.

At the center of the conflict is the confrontation between three powers: the United States, Russia and China. The events of the storyline take place in 2020 and develop 6 years after the previous part. Starring Sergeant Daniel Rekers from the band Tombstone. The action takes place in Baku, where the main character and his squad went. Depending on the decisions made, players will have 3 endings. 5 additions are presented: Rise of China, Second Attack, Sea Strike, Dragon’s Teeth and The Last Stand.
