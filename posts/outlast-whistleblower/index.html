Game Description#

Outlast (+Whistleblower) - DLC for the game of the same name, which added more content and a new storyline. The addition contains a backstory of the events that happened in the original, where Miles Upshur played the game. The journalist went to the Mount Massive mental hospital, where, for information received, there were many violations. Here he encountered a crowd of terrible psychos - monsters who had taken over the hospital.

The backstory of the horror comes from the name of the informant, for whom you will play. Waylon Park was an employee of the Murkoff Company. It was this corporation that secretly carried out terrible experiments on the human brain in a mental hospital. By telling Miles about what was happening, he set himself up, so they also experimented on him. Now the hero, in order to get out of the terrible place, must himself fight monsters - victims of non-human doctors.

At the beginning of the playthrough, the player will have to run away from a psycho cannibal who dismembers people. The authors perfectly conveyed madness, fear and the desire to hide from this madness, which will steadily haunt the player. In the process, the user needs to run away from enemies, hide, jump over various obstacles and climb ventilation shafts to escape. The location has many corridors and offices with fallen furniture, boxes, beds, under which you can hide and wait. Next, Waylon accidentally meets Frank Maner, a sexual maniac, and ends up in his lair, where real hell is going on.

In Outlast, screams are heard everywhere, body parts are scattered, and patients create complete chaos. Murder, dismemberment, rape, eating - this is just part of what is happening here. In this version, various deviations and perversions are brought to the fore. Park himself will also have a hard time - he will often receive fractures, get roasted in the oven and have a lot of cuts, wounds and bruises from blows. But this is not so scary compared to what brutal psychos are capable of.
