Game Description#

Robonauts can be played online with a friend, allowing you to enjoy battles on 50 planets together. This arcade space adventure is available for free on pirates and features 12 levels, each offering new challenges. Players will be able to engage in online play, battling intergalactic opponents and using unique gravity manipulation mechanics to move between planets. Play online online to experience all the benefits of team play in exciting space battles.

The main character, Robo, goes on a space journey after unexpectedly leaving his home base. The campaign takes place on a variety of planets where players face squadrons of hostile creatures and fearsome bosses. The control scheme supports both solo adventures and local co-op play, allowing friends to team up to fight cosmic threats. Players equip their robotic avatar with an arsenal of weapons to combat various threats that arise throughout the mission.

The gameplay centers on the mechanics of moving around planets combined with shoot-em-up elements. Each planetary system offers new combat scenarios and obstacles, forcing players to be on their guard as they progress. The game has two difficulty modes - casual and hard - for different skill levels. The local “Against Everyone” mode adds another layer to the game, allowing players to test their skills against each other in head-to-head matches.
