Game Description#

Cyclopean: The Great Abyss shows the labyrinth of the underworld, full of oddities and difficulties. Locked in a place where there is little what your character should be guided in this dark kingdom with the help of available instruments, unions and decisions. The world is formed by a choice, from battle to dialogue, and each action can open or close the path. Users should manage limited resources such as gold, health and even sanity, to pave their way through a huge, mysterious environment. Target? Return either to the upper lands of dreams, or back to the world of wakefulness.

Cyclopean: The Great Abyss offers many promotion strategies. The battle occupies an important place, but this is not the only way to promote. Secretity, dialogs and the use of the right alliances at the right time - this is the key to success. Each solution can change the balance between life and death or sanity and insanity. As you pass, gamers may encounter various alliances, each of which has a unique set of advantages.

Some allies appear with a gamer, while others have to open in the process of research and interaction with the world. Cyclopean remains the faithful mechanics of the old CRPG, resembling a classic like Ultima and Questron. Title switches between a two -dimensional view from top to bottom and a three -dimensional perspective from the first person to pass the dungeons. Users begin with an accidentally chosen dreamer, a character with given abilities and equipment, but it can be transferred to get a potentially the best option.
