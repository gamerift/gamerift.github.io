Game Description#

Fights in Tight Spaces - a mixture of a card game with a step -by -step strategy and animated battles made in classical setting. Here, a gamer will learn to build his own tactics in any conditions, knocking out the right cards and their location in order to win in battles. To create a deck in the game, there are 150 cards compiled taking into account the abilities of the enemy. The user can make combinations based on his own game style. By the method of accidental choice of combinations of events, the gamer receives damage or improvement. Before each battle, the user creates amplification for his ward. Actings develop in a period when espionage began to be used simplified. Having collected the data, spies send it to analysts, who are already beginning to decipher the information received. There was only one team that is engaged in real espionage - this is the “Group11”. The company independently, with the help of its employees, is fighting criminals, which operate outside the digital world. The player will try on the role of an agent of the elite “group 11”, which is engaged in the destruction of all kinds of threats. The team also protects the VIP Person, giving them bodyguards. Particularly important people have to be protected and protected from enemies. To achieve the goal, you can use improvised objects found surrounded. Sama is a cardinal deck is subject to improvements. Here you can control the territories, develop additional maps using sets of skills and abilities. The player will learn how to select ideal combinations, which will allow you to perform tasks to destroy enemies throughout the globe. Each session brings new threats that it is necessary to cope using tactics open in the process of possibilities and deliberate strategy.
