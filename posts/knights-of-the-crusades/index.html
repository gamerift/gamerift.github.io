Game Description#

Knights of the Crusades sends a player during the first crusades, where a lone knight will come to begin. It is ahead to go to the foundation and management of the holy order, choosing the Templars or creating their own fraction. The process combines real -time elements with the construction of settlements and management within the sandbox. You have to solve problems related to war, religious influence, diplomacy and strategic planning.

The gameplay in Knights of the Crusades involves a trip from Western Europe to the Holy Land to participate in the starting crusades. You will take responsibility for the creation of the chosen holy order and will lead his expansion. This will require the seizure of lands called villages, and their development through the construction of many necessary buildings. A comprehensive economic system with survival elements functions both on a tactical map and on global, demanding close attention.

The extraction of resources, especially silver and pieces of piety, directly affects the expansion of the control zone and ensuring the army. Conflict modeling is a key aspect of the gameplay, putting you against computer troops in ground and sea battles using real -time tactics. The defense of the founded settlements and offensive maneuvers, such as large -scale siege of cities, form the basis of the military component. At the end of the plot scenarios, recreating historical events like a reconquist or defense of Edessa, the game goes into a free research regime.
