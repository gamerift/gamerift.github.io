Game Description#

In Achilles: Legends Untold, users are included in the image of the famous mythological character, which goes to the next fateful adventure. This time, Achilles is in the center of the conflict between Aid and Ares. Its main goal is to defeat phobos - the child of the god of war. But this does not mean that he himself will come and offer to converge in the battle. To find it, you will have to go a long way, faced with the most dangerous and vile creatures that have ever existed on Earth.

Features of the project “Achilles: Legends Untold”:

High complexity - every exact strike of the enemy is able to cause critical damage, so you need to monitor the behavior of the opponents in the battle, blocking the attacks or simply dodging them. Own style - using a craft of weapons and the introduction of rare objects, you can move away from the classic combination of the shield and sword, using a new approach in battles. The plot background - since history is based on the mythology of ancient Greece, over and over again, references to popular myths and stories of that time will meet in the way.

This manifests itself in different situations - from dialogues with third -party characters ending with modeled situations. So, Achilles will have to fight with a cyclop, an army of skeletons or ordinary soldiers, who nevertheless are not inferior to him in the speed of strike or power. Moreover, in some situations, you will have to deal with both swordsmen and archers who are fired from high points. In such a situation, you must first deal with the nearest goals, and then move to the fact that they are outside the reach.
