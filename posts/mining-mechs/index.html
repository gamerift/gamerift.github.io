Game Description#

Mining Mechs invites players to the intriguing world, where they take control of the latest mining fur and reveal their inner world of Shakhtar. The game allows you to plunge into the depths of the underground subsoil, extract valuable resources and modernize your fur for more significant extraction. Regardless of whether you prefer single adventures or joint actions, up to 7 friends can participate in the network multiplayer cooperative game.

There is a variety: a lot of furs, each of which has unique advantages and abilities. The capabilities of customization make it possible to configure the fur in accordance with their style of the game and preferences. Connection of mines to the surface to obtain passive income gives the gaming process a strategic character and encourages the adoption of thoughtful decisions. As you penetrate deep into the mine, the tasks are becoming more and more difficult, there are dangers, enemies and secrets that make your skills and courage. The attractiveness of the game is not limited only to the prey of minerals: it has many achievements that can be obtained by passing various tests. The presence of leaders' tables contributes to a friendly competition, allowing you to evaluate their successes and results in comparison with other players.
