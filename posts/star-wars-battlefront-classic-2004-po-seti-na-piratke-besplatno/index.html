Game Description#

InSTAR WARS Battlefront (Classic, 2004) players can participate in competitions online online, fighting on numerous planets of the Star Wars universe, or they can play for free on a pirate online with a friend, fighting in both versus and cooperative. The online game is represented by capturing and holding strategic points. The opposing sides have from 200 to 250 warriors and only one point to capture. The task of all participants is to destroy all enemy soldiers.

The opportunity to play Star Wars Battlefront for free online offers you to take control of one of four factions: Rebels, Empire, Republic and Separatists. Each of them has 5 unique fighters. 4 of them are similar for all factions: infantryman, heavy soldier, pilot and sniper, and 5th character is exclusively individual. Jedi and Sith, one on each side: Luke Skywalker, Darth Vader, Mace Windu and Count Dooku. You can’t control them, but they fight on the battlefield. The game has Ewoks, Endora, Wookies and other characters.

Faction Unique Troops: Wookiee Smugglers, Republic Jet Troopers, Imperial Dark Troopers and Separatist Droidekas. Flying vehicles: Bespin Cloud Machine, Vulture Droid and others. Ground transport is an Armored Assault Tank, Tactical Support All-Terrain Vehicle or Skiff. Battles in STAR WARS Battlefront (Classic, 2004) take place on Bespin, Hoth, Tatooine, Naboo and other locations.
