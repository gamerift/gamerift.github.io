Game Description#

On the pirate version ofSilverfall: Earth Awakening you can play online with a friend for free, going through an intense fantasy campaign in co-op mode. Allows you to fight together online over the Internet, repelling various monsters, and also take part in confrontations with other players. The events take place in the fictional world of Nelve. In this universe, magic and technology intersect. The peculiarity is that in the process of going through history you will have to make difficult choices.

Moral dilemmas lead to different consequences. The online game allows you to take control of one of 6 races: Elves, Goblins, Trolls, People, Half-Lizards and Dwarves. Each faction in Silverfall: Earth Awakening has its own unique characteristics and traits. You can customize characters. You will have to confront both ordinary monsters of different races and powerful bosses, to whom you need to find an individual approach. The possibility of free online play allows you to make a difficult journey in the company of friends.

There are over 156 skills and magical abilities available to research. Includes 8 Ultimate skills and 12 High Level spells. You can engage in crafting, making thousands of variations of weapons, armor, enchantments, etc. You can upgrade your hero up to level 45. Multiplayer mode is available for 8 players simultaneously. A huge amount of cooperative and PvP content is presented, in which other participants will have to demonstrate their power.
