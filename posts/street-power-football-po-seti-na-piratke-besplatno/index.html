Game Description#

On the piratedStreet Power Football you can play online for free with a friend or players from all over the world, showing off your football skills online or locally. The gameplay of Street Power Football combines a special street style and incredible dynamics of the matches. Multiplayer and single-player modes are available, featuring various tasks and challenges.

You have to fight with the legends of the street T-shirt. Each character has its own unique skills and abilities. During matches you need to use a variety of techniques and even use super strength. The possibility of free online play allows you to both team up in co-op and play in confrontation modes. The main task is to become the king of street football.

Several unique platforms and modes are available. The single-player campaign takes place under the auspices of the legendary Sean Garner, Liv Cook and Melody Donket. They act as mentors and help the protagonist rise to the top of street football. Each of the six modes offers unique gameplay. You can play in the Freestyle dance competition, take part in competitions of three players per team, and there are also competitions available such as Trick Shot, Panna cage battles and much more. You can decorate your football player and team with outfits and tattoos. Various emotions and gestures are selected. Competitions are held to the dynamic music of Black Eyed Peas, DJ Snake, Snap, etc.
