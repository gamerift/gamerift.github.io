Game Description#

On the pirateJames Bond 007: Nightfire you can play online for free with a friend. Multiplayer mode is available over a local connection and online online. The possibility of free online play allows participants to compete in locations from the single-player campaign and on unique multiplayer maps. Locations such as Fort Knox from Goldfinger are available, there is Atlantis from The Spy Who Never Loved Me and others. Network play allows you to fight with bots. The level of artificial intelligence is configured individually.

In the single-player campaign you will need to fight enemies using a variety of guns and gadgets. You can use stealth mechanics to eliminate targets quietly. The arsenal includes six pistols: Wolfram PP7, Type 40, Raptor .50 and others. There are two sniper rifles, one Frinesi Auto 12 Shotgun, four automatic cannons SG5 Commando, Storm M32, Deutsche M9K and AIMS-20, two Sentinel Guided Missile Launcher and Scorpion missile systems. Bond uses several types of grenades.

Spy devices include master keys, a mobile phone hook, a keychain stun gun, a camera lighter, a decoder, various tranquilizer darts, night vision devices, etc. James Bond can hack computers using a special hacking device called Q-Worm, which infects the OS with a virus. It is a regular credit card. The campaign consists of 9 episodes, including No Choice, Second Try, Meeting Point, Rise of the Phoenix and others.
