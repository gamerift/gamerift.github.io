Game Description#

Endgame of Devil will put a player in the role of a devil who is entrusted with protecting the castle. The task is to stop the adventurers' units trying to take possession of treasures in your possessions. To do this, it is necessary to call on and command the servants to reflect the onslaught of the invaders. Combat clashes are automatic, moving the gameplay towards the strategic formation of the detachment before the battle. The outcome of each fight depends on the planning and elements of the randomness inherent in the structure of this slide.

Key aspects of power over the underworld:

The call of faithful servants from hundreds of accessible options. Creation of synergistic detachments for repulsive adventurers. Observation of automatic battles, the outcome of which depends on the preparation. Collection of powerful artifacts to strengthen their troops.

The formation of defensive forces in Endgame of Devil includes the choice of creatures from a list with 240 items. Before the start of the battle, the game offers a random set of these fighters for a call. The task is to select a suitable personnel for the current situation and create a composition with units that support each other’s functions. The assembled army is responsible for the reflection of an enemy attack. Gamers need to defeat adventurers for a certain number of rounds in order to save their jewelry.

Victory over the opponent group opens access to values, including more than 200 items. This collection contains 19 legendary artifacts and 13 special adventurers used against them. After successful defense, one artifact will have to be selected to expand their capabilities in subsequent clashes. These little things have properties that can change the conditions of the conflict and open new tactical ways.
