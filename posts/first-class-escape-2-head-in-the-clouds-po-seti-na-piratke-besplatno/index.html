Game Description#

The corporate quest First Class Escape 2 Head in the Clouds invites you to team up with a friend or company to play online for free on a pirate, competing in solving inventive puzzles and puzzles. Alone or online, you need to visit an airship soaring high in the sky. Participants are required to uncover the secrets hidden within the walls of a luxury aircraft.

You will need to face many difficulties along the way. Each riddle is a real challenge for players. The possibility of free online play in First Class Escape 2 Head in the Clouds allows you to cooperate or compete with each other, trying to solve all the assigned tasks faster than others. Story campaigns can be completed in random order.
