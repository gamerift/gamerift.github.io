Game Description#

BROKE PROTOCOL: Online City RPG is a cubic sandbox aimed at playing online with a friend on a pirate for free. Players can join forces or organize mass slaughter online over the Internet. Events take place in a huge city. You can go to work, communicate with NPCs, buy apartments, garages, join bans and much more. The open world of BROKE PROTOCOL: Online City RPG is represented by a huge territory and wide possibilities. Network play allows you to explore openword with your partners.

By receiving experience points, you can upgrade your abilities and skills. The mechanics are represented by elements of survival: hunger, thirst, endurance, addiction, etc. The possibility of free online play includes confrontation between participants. Everyone starts the journey with a minimum amount of money. While earning money, you need to buy exotic cars, upgrades, drugs, etc. You can join gangs, causing trouble in areas or stealing cars, or saving residents as a medic.

Any careless action may result in imprisonment. Everyone chooses who to be: a paramedic, a fireman, a gangster, a policeman, etc. Up to 100 people can play on the server at the same time. More than 100 types of different nits are presented as non-player characters. You can fight with dozens of types of weapons: grenades, pistols, shotguns, rifles, etc. Transport is represented by armored vehicles, airplanes, boats, trucks and cars. Custom scripts and modifications are supported.
