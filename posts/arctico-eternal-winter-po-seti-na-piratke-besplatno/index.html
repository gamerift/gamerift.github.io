Game Description#

Arctico (Eternal Winter) can be played online as you travel the world with a friend. Participants in the adventure navigate the Arctic using a dog sled. Players for free on pirates unite in co-op or confrontation. An online Internet connection is available, allowing partners to build and customize a camp. Game mechanics include collecting resources and exploring the open world. Various experiments can be conducted within the confines of a peaceful island, and online play encourages teamwork and creative thinking.

Traveling through the open world, a dog sled, a kayak and a parachute are used. Using pesels you can quickly get to the desired point on the map. However, it is necessary to satisfy the basic needs of animals: feed, care, give them rest, etc. Whether playing solo or using the free online play option, you will need to start building your base by placing drills and piles. The foundation is the basis of buildings in Arctico (Eternal Winter). By exploring the territories, plant seeds are collected, which are sold at the local market or planted to beautify their lands.

The scientific outpost needs to be constantly expanded by purchasing new equipment or studying advanced technologies. Decorative items are found in the vastness of the world or bought on the market. By unlocking the glider, players are able to quickly descend from the mountains to the plains. The gameplay is meditative. Exploring vast horizons and experiencing serene moments, alone or in co-op, players create a life far from civilization.
