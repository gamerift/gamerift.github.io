Game Description#

On the pirateTinkertown you can go on a fantasy adventure alone or play online for free with a friend to jointly overcome the obstacles and traps prepared for brave heroes. The gameplay is a top-dun sandbox. You can take part in online confrontations over the Internet or go through a story campaign in co-op. You have to travel to a magical land, where possibilities are limited only by the player’s imagination. Tinkertown multiplayer allows teammates to pick up a pick, shovel, hammer and other tools to explore every corner of Tinkertown.

By collecting resources, you need to build your settlement. You need to cut down trees, go down ore into mines, etc. A small village can grow into a whole city with towering spiers and castles. Almost all lands live peacefully, but when you descend into the dark depths of the dungeons, you will face a battle with the creatures of darkness and total horror. In the dungeons, monsters guard treasures. They will kill anyone who dares to descend into their domain. The possibility of free online play allows friends to join forces to confront their enemies together. By collecting jewelry and gold, you need to upgrade your heroes and expand your settlement.

You will have to travel through forests, deserts, mountains, lava, etc. Protagonists are able to craft weapons and equipment. You can choose from one of the classes, be it a robber, warrior or mage. Each class has its own set of characteristics and determines the play style. With the help of accumulated resources and raw materials, it is necessary to build houses and various buildings. To craft a weapon, you will need to use a workbench. You can take three more friends with you on an adventure to fight bosses together or build a majestic city that will become an impregnable fortress for villains and monsters.
