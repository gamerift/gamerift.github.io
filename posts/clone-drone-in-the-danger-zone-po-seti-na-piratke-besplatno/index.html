Game Description#

Clone Drone in the Danger Zone is a cubic fighting game designed to play online with a friend or compete online with different players from all over the world. On the pirate Clone Drone in the Danger Zone, you can take part in dynamic robot gladiator fights for free. Battles take place in vast arenas. Full dismemberment is available, allowing you to chop the limbs of your opponent. Free online play allows you to take on numerous cooperative challenges in interactive arenas.

The robots are equipped with a human mind, which is embedded in the android body. Robots use laser swords as weapons. Online play consists of intense battles between participants. Winners are awarded improvements and updates for their character. Introduced multiplayer and a story mode that focuses on an epic story about the defiance of the human spirit. The game has an endless mode.

There are 86 levels to choose from and 7 difficulty levels, including a level for beginners and for real pros. You can reach the rank of Titan. To do this, you will need to destroy all enemies in your path and receive special coins for improvements. The Until the last bot mode is an analogue of the royal battle. Up to 15 players fight each other in arenas. Matches last from 5 to 10 minutes. The last one to survive wins. Closed duels allow you to fight in pairs. The fights are commentated by professional commentators who record every action of the player. Robots with swords, bows, hammers, spider-like androids and many other enemies are presented.
