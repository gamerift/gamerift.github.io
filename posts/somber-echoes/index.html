Game Description#

Somber Echoes is a single -user gloomy scientific and fantastic Metroidvania, immersing gamers into the terrible and mysterious depths of the abandoned spaceship atromytos. From the very beginning, users take on the role of addressing, which should withstand their twin sister, harmony before it can untie the space catastrophe. Title weaves elements of Greek mythology into a futuristic setting, mixing two different worlds to create an atmosphere that seems at the same time ancient and alien.

According to the plot of the address, the path passes a full path, fighting with otherworldly horrors and solving a difficult task - to stop the cataclysmic ambitions of his sister. The ship atritos serves not only the decoration, but also the key to unraveling the deep secrets of the universe, which the game hints from the very beginning. As you pass the Somber Echoes, you have to navigate the seven unique areas of the ship, each of which has its own visual identity and its own difficulties.

Thanks to this diversity, each area seems fresh, forcing gamers to be on the alert, discovering a new environment for themselves and learning more about the disaster that befell atromytos. The atmosphere changes as various sections pass, and this dynamic construction of the world gives the game depth. The feeling of isolation and research is constantly present, every new corner of the spaceship makes it possible to open something new, whether it is strength, part of history or a deadly opponent.
