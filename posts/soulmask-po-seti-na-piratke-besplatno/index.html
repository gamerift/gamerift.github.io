Game Description#

Soulmask offers you and your friend a free immersion in the game where you can play online and online even on a pirate. This survival adventure will take you into a wild world where, after dying and meeting a mysterious mask, you will begin your journey. From the very beginning, you have to rely only on the dexterity of your mind and the magical power of the mask. You will explore the vastness of the world, filled with diversity from tropical jungles to snow-capped peaks, each of which hides its own challenges. Take on the challenge of a brutal struggle for survival: hunting, gathering resources and building will be your companions in your quest to strengthen your tribe and uncover the secrets of antiquity.

The mask is the key to the development of your tribe. By synchronizing your consciousness with NPCs, you can master them and gain their skills. Recruit warriors, hunters and even the occasional lazy drunk - a well-developed tribe is very important for success. Soulmask boasts a sophisticated artificial intelligence system to automate tasks for tribe members. Farming, construction, repairs - everything can be configured to optimize the workflow. These virtual people even have their own personalities and activities in their free time, adding life to your settlement.

Combat in Soulmask is physics-based and varied. Choose from eight different weapon styles, each with their own strengths and weaknesses. Do you consider yourself a fighter? Fight with a sledgehammer, mano-a-mano style. Do you prefer ranged combat? Shoot down flying creatures with well-aimed arrows. The world is filled with the remains of ancient civilizations, hinting at a forgotten past. Explore these ruins and temples to uncover the secrets of the mask and the world itself. Soulmask’s hundreds of hours of content offer a varied experience. Build a farming empire, explore the mysteries of the world, or take part in a brutal tribal war - the choice is yours.
