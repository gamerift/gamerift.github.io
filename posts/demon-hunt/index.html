Game Description#

Demon Hunt is an action in which players pilot furs through landscapes flooded demons. The process is built on a combination of firing with the view of the Bullet Hell under the Rogue-Lite. Gamers go hunting, faced with numerous opponents and strong bosses in every sortie. During the battles, it is necessary to survive, collect resources and defeat the main enemy. This structure guarantees that each race in the game offers different trials and opportunities for development.

In Demon Hunt, 3 unique furs that have a special basic ability and personal tree talents for upgrades are available to a choice. Armament systems bring a strategic depth, because each gun can evolve 3 different paths during the raid. This choice allows you to adapt combat tactics to specific tests or personal preferences. In addition, the collected artifacts give a direct increase in characteristics and are combined to open synergies between the elements of equipment.

Development continues between expeditions at the central base intended to manage constant improvements. Here, the things found are processed in materials to create and improve equipment to future raids. A system of spheres also operates, which allows them to install them in special nodes to change the activation method. Participants also distribute talent glasses in a tree of their car skills, setting up a gameplay under themselves. The study of locations opens access to interactive objects like sanctuaries and chests with additional awards.
