Game Description#

Ships at Sea goes to the world of sea simulators, offering players to navigate the huge and inexorable Norwegian sea. Forget about following the given path - this is an open world, captain. You yourself will choose your course - from the transportation of goods through the insidious waters to the provision of critically important assistance to the service vessel. Do you think you have fishing abilities? Throw the nets and see if you can catch large fish.

Each career in Ships at Sea sets you unique tasks. Cargo holds are not bottomless, so you will need a sharp mind for the distribution of weight and plan the most effective routes. Service missions will check you for strength. Become a hero that other captains need, delivering fuel to them, repairing their cars or even towing them from the jam. The excitement of hunting awaits you in a career of commercial fisherman. Start small, and then set up various techniques to become an acce of fishing.

But the open sea is not always smooth. Here real weather conditions await you. A foggy morning, a howling wind, raging storm - to survive, you will have to adapt your strategy on the go. The economic climate is no less dynamic. Sell ​​your cargo or catch at the right time to get maximum profit - the market fluctuates, as in the real world. In addition, follow the marine life - thanks to the system of artificial intelligence, the sea seems truly alive.
