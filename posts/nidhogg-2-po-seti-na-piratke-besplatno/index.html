Game Description#

Nidhogg 2 is a 2D adventure that can be played online with a friend through a story campaign. Online features for shared adventures are available for free on Pirate. You can go through the story online, or in local co-op. The rules and mechanics are extremely simple: you need to kill everyone who gets in the way. You need to tear apart your opponents, tearing out their throats and piercing their guts with piercing weapons or long-range weapons. The possibility of free online play allows you to take control of a hero named Vroom.

When playing in co-op, the second participant takes on the role of his partner. The gameplay is distinguished by the dynamics of combat and incredible cruelty, allowing you to paint the walls red. The brutality of the action is emphasized by the fast-paced soundtrack of Doseone, Osborne and Daedelus. You can use settings and modifications, adjusting the game to suit you. You are allowed to create complete madness, jumping over chasms and obstacles, overcoming traps and fighting all the monsters blocking the path. Features of Nidhogg 2: single-player campaign and online play; battles with AI or duels; 4 unique types of weapons; full character customization; 10 stages in the world of Nidhogg; about 10 in-game modifications for the adventure.
