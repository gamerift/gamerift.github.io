Game Description#

Trolls vs Vikings: Reborn unfolds the conflict in the fantasy world, where the player has to push the forces of trolls with viking opponents and Scandinavian gods. The main action is to manage troll detachments to protect the territory from advancing opponents on a field divided into cells. This strategy in real time requires the tactical placement of units and the use of magical abilities. Survival under the onslaught of constant attacks depends on adaptation to enemy constructions and using the unique skills of each troll.

Tactical tests in the world Trolls vs Vikings: Reborn:

34 levels of the main path, checking strategic exposure; 35 special tasks with comprehensive goals and battles against the gods; Whested spell mechanics to control the battlefield; Unpredictable events caused by Loki tricster gods.

The campaign is a linear sequence of defensive missions. The structure of the Title is built around many difficult scenarios requiring a deep understanding of game mechanics. Progress in the gameplay is associated with the system of fragments that reward for success. Viking units are endowed with individual animation that determines their behavior during skirmishes. Gamers can also receive Steam achievements for the implementation of special tasks and overcoming difficult tests. This game offers tactical experience, where each solution affects the outcome of the battle.
