Game Description#

Jetrunner combines first -person parkour with dynamic shooting in the framework of futuristic sports. Opponents move along the arenas created by hand, using a combination of running, jumping, sliding and firing for promotion. The fully voiced story campaign is dedicated to the history of the newcomer-sensation of Nina White, rising along the competitive ladder. Her path describes battles and challenges in an attempt to achieve legendary status in this discipline. The player’s main task is to perfectly master the movement and battle in order to enter his name in the history of jetranning.

Elements of success on Jetrunner arenas:

Exciting plot campaign about the ascent of a new sport star. Sixty unique tracks in detailed locations of the future. The advanced Pauru system, including running through the walls and acrobatics. Arsenal of six gadgets for a tactical advantage.

In this world, 60 unique arenas are represented, covering the locations from the city landscapes of New York to Seoul streets. Each environment is a new route for conquering, offering its own set of navigation riddles. Gamers use such abilities as running along the walls, overcoming obstacles and using a hook hook to effectively intersect these complex levels.

To get an advantage, rivals can master six gadgets created to increase mobility and efficiency. These tools are critical of detecting short routes and execution of maneuvers that reduce the time of the race. In addition to the storyline, this game offers a special test mode that focuses on accuracy and strategic planning. It requires optimization of routes and impeccable execution.
