Game Description#

JoJo’s Bizarre Adventure All-Star Battle R is an anime fighting game with the ability to play online with random players or fight on a pirate ship for free with a friend, both in local matches and online online. The fighting game is based on the anime JoJo of the same name. The online game offers you to take control of one of 40 unique characters, including Jonathan Joestar, Dio Brando, Jotaro Kujo and many other heroes from the cult animated show. The fighters are collected from all 8 volumes of the manga.

The possibility of free online play is represented by such modes as All Star, Arcade, Online, Versus, Practice and Gallery. In Allstar matches you can take part in more than 100 competitions, while customizing various gameplay mechanics and match conditions. There are many costumes, illustrations and other bonus content available, some of which will need to be unlocked as you progress. During battles, each fighter has an energy bar. When dealing damage to an enemy, you need to lower it to zero.

The moveset consists of light, medium and heavy strikes. You can dodge and perform a special attack. Techniques in JoJo’s Bizarre Adventure All-Star Battle R are divided into 6 categories: Ripple, Vampirism, Mod, Stand, Rider, and Bao. For example, with their help you can restore strength, use the environment, summon war horses, etc. Technology influences battle tactics.

The special Heart Heat Gauge gradually fills up. If you fill 50 percent of the energy, you can inflict an enhanced attack, and if you do not fill it to the end, then the unit carries out the most powerful Great Heat Attacks. There are taunts and Strange Cancel combo attacks. The locations contain Stage Gimmicks in the form of passers-by or minor objects. Campaign Mode includes a full storyline.
