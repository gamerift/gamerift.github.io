Game Description#

God of War 2022 is an action-adventure game with a third-person view. This project is both a sequel to the previous part and a restart of the entire universe of the famous, cult slasher about the adventures of Kratos – a Spartan warrior, the protagonist of all 8 games in the “God of War” series, fighting against various gods and mythical creatures in a fit of rage and revenge. This game is called a restart because of the transfer of the location of the action and the change of the main mythology, which is used as the primary source. If in previous series of the franchise Greek myths and legends were taken as a basis, now - Scandinavian ones. Some characters from previous parts appear in cameo roles as you progress. The story often mentions Ragnarok and features various deities in which the Vikings believed.

The gameplay starts with the beginning of the journey of Kratos and his son named Atreus. The purpose of their adventure is to scatter the ashes of the protagonist’s wife, who died recently. She wanted this to be done from the highest point of all worlds and dimensions. The antagonist of the single-player story campaign will be Balder, the god of spring and light. The slasher mechanics inherent in the series were changed and the entire gameplay began to resemble role-playing adventures. As his main weapon, Kratos uses the magical ax Leviathan, instead of the twin blades that were lost at the end of God of War 3. Atreus also takes part in battles with his own bow. Thanks to the leveling branch of both main characters, you can create various builds and create combinations of the abilities of the son and father.
