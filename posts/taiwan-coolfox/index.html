Game Description#

Taiwan Coolfox is a 3D action-adventure, where anthropomorphic animals are the main characters. This project unfolds in the formation of horizontal scrolling, organically combining the elements of the platformer and intense contractions. A solitary regime is concentrated on instant immersion in the combat system with a side of the side, requiring an accurate positioning and skillful execution of maneuvers against opponents.

The main gameplay cycle includes the Taiwan Coolfox movement in various areas, each of which offers its tests, checking the reaction and command actions of the player. The clashes provide for the sequence of blows, evasions and special techniques requiring complete control. The structure gives priority to combat clashes and straightforward progress, not focusing on a complex storyline, which provides targeted game experience.

Mastering a mechanic of confrontation:

Type of peace: three -dimensional space, horizontal shift. Management subjects: Humanoid animals in clashes. Bow System: side mode, high accuracy of presses. The course of passage: individual stages, the final fight with the main enemy. Available actions: movement, jumping, attacks, evasion, special abilities.

This title is divided into many stages, each of which ends with a key fight with the boss. To further advance, users need to defeat these powerful opponents, thus opening access to the next location. Management in the battle mode with side scrolling allows you to move left and right, jumps, strike with your hands and feet, dodge, interact with the walls and use special movements, offering a complex of combat solutions.
