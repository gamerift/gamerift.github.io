Game Description#

Shadow Labyrinth is a 2D action-platform that draws inspiration in the classic Pac-man, offering a fresh look at the familiar gameplay. Gamers enter the place of swordsman No. 8, which is revived by a floating yellow ball named Puck. Together they move around the alien world filled with relics and the remnants of past wars. The new situation opens up many difficulties, enemies and secrets, turning, it would seem, a simple labyrinth into a world of secrets and dangers. As the plot develops, users should adapt and develop, turning into hunters.

Shadow Labyrinth has a complex and at the same time useful combat mechanics. The basis of gameplay is battles with about 30 different bosses. Each of them requires a gamer to master various abilities and strategies. The victory over the bosses gives new skills, which are the key to the opening of previously inaccessible areas of the game. This feeling of progress is directly related to the aspect of the study of the game, since each victory gives new opportunities for further studying the world similar to the maze. The design is such that users are invited to return to the previous stages, stronger and better equipped if they are faced with a particularly complicated enemy. The basis is the relationship between the swordsman No. 8 and Puck. As they delve into the alien world, their goal is to reveal the truth.
