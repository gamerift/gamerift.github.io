Game Description#

Munchkin Digital is a role-playing adventure with the ability to play online with a friend, fighting for free on a pirate ship with monsters in the hope of stealing ancient treasures from their tenacious clutches. Co-op mode is available online and locally. Alone or together, you will have to descend into the dark dungeons, where evil of epic proportions lurks. Munchkin Digital’s online game includes a versus mode, allowing you to stab your friends in the back.

You can equip yourself with the craziest and funniest gear ever seen in an RPG: Horny Helmet combined with Ass-Kicking Boots? Easily! How about picking up the Napalm Staff or the Bloody Dismemberment Chainsaw? Munchkins can afford the craziest weapons! To get the treasure, you will need to destroy all the monsters and not receive dozens of ancient curses locked in the walls of dark tombs. The opportunity for free online play invites each participant to create their own character, combining races, classes and many different parameters to create a unique hero build.

The level increases after killing monsters. When a champion reaches level 10, he wins the treasure race. However, achieving the goal is not so easy. This will require learning the techniques and mastering the various crafts available in the Dungeon Manual. They contain a lot of information on overcoming traps and describe tactics for fighting mystical creatures. It’s very easy to lose. Death can strike at any moment: the hero can be fried or eaten. In the world of Munchkin, the demise of a hero is inevitable.
