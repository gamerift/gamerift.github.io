Game Description#

Worlds of Aria offers a unique opportunity to play online with a friend and immerse yourself in an exciting adventure. In this free-to-play online pirate game, you must guide a group of misfits through a kingdom in chaos. The fate of Queen Julia’s throne depends on your decisions, and dangerous enemies and unexpected allies await you online. Your actions and successful dice rolls will determine the development of events in this magical world.

The game takes inspiration from tabletop role-playing games, bringing to life a collaborative storytelling experience. You can play the game alone or recruit up to three friends to play online or locally. With Friends Pass, your buddies can join the party without shelling out a dime. As each chapter turns, you’ll encounter moments of cooperation and betrayal. Will you stay the course or will you unleash your inner villain?

Laura Bailey’s narration will bring the world to life as you explore the nooks and crannies of Aria. From bustling taverns to treacherous mines, there’s no shortage of places to discover. One minute you can be the captain of a ship on the high seas, and the next you can be the manager of a mead factory. The game features 12 unique characters, each with their own skills and quirks. Assemble your dream team and see how they collide and mesh as you tackle the challenges ahead.

Reproducibility is the name of the game here. Don’t like how things turned out? Don’t worry! You can always try again, discovering hidden secrets and alternate endings along the way. Who knows? Maybe this time you will be able to make a critical roll and turn the tide in your favor. In Worlds of Aria, the story never ends until you say it’s over.
