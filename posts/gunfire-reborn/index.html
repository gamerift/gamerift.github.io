Game Description#

The shooterGunfire Reborn contains elements of a roguelike and role-playing game. The player can choose to control several heroes with individual abilities. The game has a huge amount of randomness, from procedurally generated locations to dropped guns and loot. Gunfire Reborn is designed for both one and a company of up to 4 people.

The game features 100 different types of items. Various mechanics will encourage you to study each fighter presented, who are more or less suitable for a specific situation and location. The heroes of the game are anthropomorphic creatures with charisma and individual appearance. The protagonists control 34 types of guns, represented by 7 classes: pistol, machine gun, machine gun, grenade launcher, shotgun, emitter and sniper rifle. Elemental effects cling to the trunks, including corrosion, fire or electricity.

Leveling up occurs due to the found scrolls. Moreover, the scroll can either improve the skill indicator or worsen it. There is another element of random distribution here. At the end of the level, the heroes will receive a chest with a cup, which allows them to improve one of the two main abilities. Enemies are classified by difficulty. Some mobs only need a few shots, while others need a whole clip. Weapons also differ in charge parameters, so you should carefully choose a gun that helps kill a particular adversary. Boss battles are quite intense, as there are attacks in the arsenal that are impossible to dodge. You should think through your tactics in advance and be as careful as possible.
