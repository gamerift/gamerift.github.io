Game Description#

An open-world action-adventure game,NieR: Automata tells the story of androids 2B, 9S and A2 as they battle a dystopia created by powerful machines. All of humanity was evicted from their planet by mechanical creatures that arrived from another world.

In an attempt to retake the Earth from aliens, people send combat robots to somehow resist the invaders. This must be the final attempt, the last hope to get your home back. This is a war fought between machines and androids. Uncover long-forgotten truths about the world and fight numerous enemies to return the people to their lands.

Fight your opponents using powerful melee and ranged attacks. Kill bosses and take part in battles against hordes of enemies on a wide variety of maps. The combat system is very simple and even a beginner can easily destroy opponents one by one.

Combine light and heavy attacks, and switch between weapons. Dodge enemy strikes with style, and then counterattack your opponent with beautiful attacks. As players progress, they will earn new types of weapons and level up. Learn combat skills and change equipment to create a unique warrior. Find huge swords, axes or firearms with unique characteristics to resist the invaders.
