Game Description#

The Wolf Among Us is an episodic graphic novel based on the Fable comics series created by Bill Willingham. The game’s plot acts as a prequel to the main story. The game is divided into 5 episodes, where every decision made by the player has a consequence and affects the overall one. history.

The gameplay is point-and-click, but some sections contain action and fights, which are QTE scenes. All the characters presented in the game are heroes of famous fairy tales, who, with the help of spells, can exist in the world of people or “Simps”, as they are called here. The magical heroes of The Wolf Among Us call each other Tales. They look like ordinary people and try not to give themselves away in order to peacefully coexist with humanity in the city of Fabletown. Those who break the law and reveal their identity go to the Farm, where they take on their usual form from stories and fairy tales.

The main character of “The Wolf Among Us” is detective Big B, aka Gray Wolf. He represents the West Side Police Department and investigates the mysterious murder of a girl named Vera, whose head was delivered to the door of the police station. Shortly before this, he helped her solve the problem with the Woodcutter, since she owed him money. Snow White will be your partner in this difficult task. Over the course of 5 chapters, the heroes will find themselves in a series of intrigues and conspiracies that will reveal a terrible secret. Who to trust? Who is friend and who is enemy? During the course of the game, the Wolf will meet such characters as Ichabod Crane, Beauty and the Beast, Tweedledum and Tweedledee, Bloody Mary and many others.
