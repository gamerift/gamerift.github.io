Game Description#

On the pirate Pogostuck Rage With Your Friends you can play online for free with a friend, participating in various competitions, as well as taking part in online confrontations over the Internet. Competitions are held on pogo sticks or popularly called grasshoppers. You must overcome all obstacles and traps to get to the finish line faster than your opponent. Online play allows you to play cooperatively or fight in duel matches. Features and modes: possibility of free online play; single-player campaign; 4 competitive disciplines; vivid graphics; hardcore gameplay; convenient and intuitive interface.

Pogostuck Rage With Your Friends modes include competitions such as The Monolith Of Perseverance, Tethered To Your Friends, Loot and Skate. Allowed to perform in multiplayer matches from 2 to 16 people. Co-op supports up to four players. The gameplay is tailored for precision. It is necessary to calculate the trajectory, force, inclination and height of the jump. Allows you to make 360 ​​degree turns.

The competition is aimed not only at accuracy, but also at speed. You need to be the first to cross the finish line, ahead of the other participants. You can customize your characters by unlocking new clothes, equipment, pogo sticks, etc. There is a leaderboard that tracks results and also shows position in the live rankings, comparing achievements with players around the world.
