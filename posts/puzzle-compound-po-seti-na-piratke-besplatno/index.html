Game Description#

On the piratePuzzle Compound you can play online with a friend for free, solving various puzzles together and online online exploring crime scenes, trying to find clues, loopholes and much more to try to escape from the trap. The online game Puzzle Compound is designed for up to 4 people. Free online play features many different modes, including timed puzzle solving. The complexity of the puzzles gradually increases, presenting participants with new challenges.

It is necessary to carefully explore the premises, trying to find all active objects. Each location has many unique interactive objects that you can interact with. Together with your partners you will have to uncover the secret of the Jefferson mansion. Gradually, players discover new rooms, hidden passages, various secret loopholes and much more. It is important to find all the unique artifacts in the rooms. Some have to be returned to their original place.

Jefferson Castle consists of a huge number of different locations with their own unique external style. Additional modes include Bank Heist, where you need to score the maximum number of points, Wild West, which requires you to hunt for treasure, and Escape to Atley, where a group of criminals need to escape from the city.
