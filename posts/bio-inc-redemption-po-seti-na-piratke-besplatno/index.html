Game Description#

Bio Inc Redemption is a medical simulator that you can play with a friend online on a pirate, competing for free in different biomedical modes by connecting online via the Internet. There are only two choices here: Death or Life. There are a variety of illnesses to deal with, including severe cases that require teamwork. The possibility of free online play includes creating your own team of professionals who can compete for someone’s life.

It is necessary to identify the sources of the disease, conduct examinations, tests, prescribe medications and prescribe a course of treatment. The online game Bio Inc Redemption makes you feel like a team of specialists ready to solve even the most difficult problem. The Disease Library contains over 600 different cases that include symptoms, diagnostic tests, and more. The gameplay is very realistic and somewhat frightening. Players can join forces in two unique campaigns.

In the first, you have to mercilessly torture your victim, testing a combination of the most insane diseases on him. In the second, you will need to fight for the life of every person. Each scenario has 9 unique cases. There are 4 difficulty levels to choose from. There are a huge variety of tools, reference books, etc. available. Each successful completion of tasks is accompanied by rewards. There is a sex roulette that gives random diseases.
