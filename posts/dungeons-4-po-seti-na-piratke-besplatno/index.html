Game Description#

Dungeons 4 welcomes the Ultimate Evil and his loyal servant Talia to another victory against the forces of good. Your task is to build a cozy dungeon, send your creatures to the Overworld and remind unsuspecting people that Absolute Evil is in charge. Dynamic Evil is your tool and the lush green forests and plains are your canvas. And if you want to play online with other players, then you have the option of free online play. You can play online with a friend or with strangers, as well as on a pirate. To do this, you only need to connect to the Internet online and select the game mode. You can cooperate or fight with each other, and create your own maps and scenarios.

Make sure your dungeon is a fortress, because the pesky Overworlders won’t want to be distracted while you turn their lands into a haven for the Ultimate Evil. “Gold, gold, gold and precious stones, gold, precious stones and gold!” An ancient song echoes as the Dwarves come, competing with the ever-expanding Evil for space and resources. Their raiding parties, joined by elves and humans, seek to find the heart of the Dungeon. Dungeons 4 retains the spirit of the game, offering larger dungeons, more creatures and an expanded Overworld. The Dwarves added depth by building underground fortresses, and the Hand of Evil became customizable. Talia gains new abilities thanks to a system of perks, and the Horde, undead and demons willingly carry out the orders of the Ultimate Evil. Snots play a prominent role in the game. Featuring a campaign narrated by your favorite English announcer and skirmish maps, you’ll experience a variety of challenges. Spread your evil throughout the world, transforming it into stunning biomes. A two-player multiplayer co-op game, both players control the same dungeon, working together to destroy a world ripe for the influence of the Ultimate Evil.
