Game Description#

Spirit of the Island allows you to play pirate online for free with a friend, turning a tropical archipelago into a popular resort. Having connected with a partner online, friends need to build farms, care for animals, collect provisions and resources, and prepare various delicacies. The online game Spirit of the Island is aimed at cooperative interaction between participants.

The plot tells that in the home country of the protagonists there is a certain ritual of growing up, which implies that it is necessary to go on a journey in which you will need to find yourself. The heroes find themselves on a distant tropical island. It was once a popular resort, but now the continent is going through hard times. It is necessary to make him famous again, while simultaneously revealing the secrets of his past. Free co-op allows you to split up tasks to complete your goals in half the time.

It is necessary to make tools from available materials. The main thing is to build a house for the night and find a source of food. By collecting resources you need to create farms and open various shops. By completing tasks and taking actions, you gain experience, which allows you to upgrade your skills and discover new recipes. There are 10 unique abilities available, including mining, gathering, fishing, farming, etc. There are many mini-games, for completing which bonus points are awarded.

The economy plays a special role in forcing tourists to return to the tropical paradise. You can purchase materials or sell them. It is also possible to hire assistants to distribute tasks. The archipelago consists of 14 small islands. Each of them contains at least one unique character. All heroes have their own habits and character. Residents of the continent celebrate various holidays, including Ocean Day. Therefore, you should always keep an eye on the calendar.
