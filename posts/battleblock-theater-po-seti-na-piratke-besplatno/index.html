Game Description#

On the pirateBattleBlock Theater, you can join forces with a friend to play online for free, or compete online online to play a fun 2D arcade game set in a theater. It is inhabited by strange creatures, which are high-tech cats trying to cause as much trouble as possible for both the player and those around him. There is an editor consisting of 20 blocks that allows you to create your own amazing adventure.

The bottom line is that it is necessary to free about 300 comrades who are in trouble. The opportunity for free online play invites you to go on an amazing journey with your friends, where drama, betrayal, betrayal and the test of true friendship await the heroes. A huge arsenal of weapons is available, allowing you to overcome about 100 levels by combining guns and changing barrels. You are allowed to take two types of weapons with you. BattleBlock Theater gameplay features: single-player campaign and online play; arena battles where you have to show your superiority over enemies; crazy mode, characterized by hardcore difficulty; completely manual character animations; more than 450 levels in single and multiplayer modes; the ability to freeze , blow up, send, etc. 300 friends yearning for freedom.
