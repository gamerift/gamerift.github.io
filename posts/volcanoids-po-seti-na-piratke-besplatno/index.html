Game Description#

Volcanoids is a first-person shooter aimed at online confrontation over the Internet. On Pirate you can play online with a friend, competing for free in all multiplayer modes. The online game allows participants to find themselves in a steampunk world, where they will need to build a huge steam complex right in the center of a volcanic island. You can take control of a giant drillship and drill through the landscape, terraforming or destroying enemies along the way.

It takes challenging mechanoid technology to make the volcano bubble. Due to the fact that lava turns everything into a lifeless wasteland, it is necessary to truly survive. Volcanoids players get a rig of enemies. It must become a death machine for its own creators. With its help, you need to reclaim your lands from strangers and invaders. The rich lands of the distant continent are available for exploration. The drilling machine acts as both a mobile base and a powerful weapon.

The volcano periodically erupts, burning out all the surrounding lands. At this point, all you have to worry about is survival. The time between eruptions plays the biggest role. It is necessary to use a limited period of time in order to have time to do all the necessary actions to the maximum: collect items, develop technologies, find additional materials, etc. You have to fight with an enemy robotic race.

The drilling station acts as a base. It must be equipped with new devices, installations, modifications, etc. It is important to automate production in order to perform various actions faster. You also need to create an impregnable fortress that opponents cannot get to. However, this will require a lot of effort. It just won’t happen!
