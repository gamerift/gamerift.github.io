Game Description#

Starship Troopers - Terran Command is a real -time strategy developed based on the Feature Film Star landing. Engage in the construction of the base, get resources and create an army to fight with giant insects living on a foreign planet. You need to colonize the hostile planet, which was suitable for the human race, but a huge number of hostile alien creatures live on its surface.

Take the role of the leader of the Terran army. Destroying the hordes of insects, you have the opportunity to become the hero of the federation. Winning in battles, boil with the technology tree and update the leading characters, appointing them to command the unit. Use the mechanics of True Line of Sight and True Line of Fire to help you in battle. Bagai study your tactics and occupy strategically important positions, preventing your army with advance. The infantry is armed with Morita machine guns and MX-90 fragmentation grenades. In addition, rackets and engineers are available. Use the help of Power Armor infantry, TAC airstrikes and fighters, helping to get an advantage in battle. Features of Starship Troopers - Terran Command:

dynamically generated locations; 10 types of units with unique characteristics and abilities; a deep tactical process tied to interaction with locations; An extensive tree of skills that allows developing in several directions; the ability to appoint leaders to units; Explosions, baked blood, beetles and total cruelty.
