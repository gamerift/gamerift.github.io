Game Description#

Celestial Empire offers to go to the Han dynasty when the Chinese empire was one of the most powerful in the world. Here, the user is given the role of the Governor of the province, who was personally chosen by the emperor. Now, everything must be done in order to make this place better and provide a high standard of living for people. And given that there are a lot of ways for the development of the structure, you can not limit yourself to a single strategy, but try different options.

Opportunities available in the Celestial Empire project:

The study of the surrounding world - the system allows you not to sit within the border of its own province, but go out into the “wild nature”, building up the valleys and organizing fields for the extraction of useful resources. The formation of society - the success of passage largely depends on the mood of the inhabitants. The higher the level of relationship and trust, the faster people will fulfill their tasks and improve their own home. Random events - everything will not be as rosy as we would like, mainly because of random events. Spontaneous disasters, outside attacks, lean years - all this needs to be prepared in advance.

In addition to the study of adjacent territories, the formation of partnerships with other communities and settlements will be an important factor in development. Trade and exchange of valuable resources will not only improve the financial situation, but also get at your disposal previously inaccessible materials to speed up the construction process or strengthen the army. The higher the user status among neighbors, the greater the chance of forming relationships with larger partners.
