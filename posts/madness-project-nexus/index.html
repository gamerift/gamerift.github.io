Game Description#

MADNESS: Project Nexus is a three-dimensional dynamic shooter with a side view, as well as a huge number of opponents and violence that happens on the monitor screen. The arcade controls are easy to remember, allowing you to quickly start enjoying the gameplay. This project from indie developers is a driving mixture of beat’em’up and slasher using not only melee weapons, but also firearms with different damage.

The main feature of the gameplay is the ability to play cooperatively on one personal computer. The second player can control using a gamepad from any manufacturer. Going through all the difficulties together will clearly make the task easier, plus it will add fun while playing the game, in which each stage starts in a new world that makes changes to the environment. Available modes: History - the user is given the role of leader of the entire army, which will lead the fighters to destroy all the offspring that did not submit to the existing authorities. The enemies will be: cultists living deep underground, cannibal bandits, well-armored warriors “Mag Agents”, which is three times more than the protagonist. Arena – before starting, you are invited to create your own character and customize it to your taste. After this process, the player finds himself in a small area of ​​the map, where he encounters waves of enemies, giving a few minutes of rest between attacks. As you move towards more serious units by destroying weak initial waves, the gamer has access to upgrading guns.
