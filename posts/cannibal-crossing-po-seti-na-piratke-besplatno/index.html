Game Description#

Cannibal Crossing is a survival game that allows you to play online for free as a pirate with a friend to explore the post-apocalyptic world alone or team up with up to four people. The city of Earlsboro is randomly generated. You need to fight an army of infected, collect useful items or garbage, build a base, look for ammunition, etc. By destroying enemies, you can upgrade your abilities and level up. Using the opportunity of a free online game, you will meet interesting characters and find yourself in unusual situations.

You have to fight not only the infected, but also bandits. Cannibals and wild animals pose a particular danger in Cannibal Crossing. Other survivors will also cause a lot of trouble. With every step there are more enemies, and they begin to act more aggressively. You can find various guns in the locations. They are subject to improvement and modernization. Due to random generation, it is necessary to adapt to unique situations. The online game includes a huge number of tasks, loot, weapons, etc.

Other survivors need help. Some characters provide access to abilities or offer to complete a unique task. The character list consists of 10 heroes. Each has its own abilities and characteristics. A wide selection of classes is presented, influencing the style of passage. By collecting useful items, you can install turrets, build walls and other defensive structures. It is advisable to hide at the base at night. A wide arsenal of weapons is available: firearms, explosives, bladed weapons, various equipment, etc. You have to fight with ferocious boss monsters.
