Game Description#

Don’t Shout Together is an online multiplayer game where you can play online with a friend. In this horror game, available on the pirate market for free, you and your friend must escape from an ominous forest while playing online on the Internet. You are faced with a choice: restore a functioning vehicle or build a telephone booth to call for help. Remember that every move you make brings the fatal outcome closer - time flows inexorably only when you are active. Inaction in this situation is not just an unaffordable luxury, but a direct path to death.

Imagine making your way through the forest with your heart pounding and a bomb strapped around your neck that triggered a scream. One scream and the game is over, not only for you, but for your entire squad. Let’s talk about pressure, right? And as if that’s not enough to make your palms sweat, the forest is infested with mutants and monsters eager to turn you into their next meal. This is a recipe for disaster, or at least severely frayed nerves.

Don’t think you can get through by muting your microphone. It’s a one-way ticket to Boringville, population: you. The real thrill is walking along the razor’s edge, where every creaking floorboard or snapping branch could be your death. Don’t forget about teamwork - in this nightmare you will have to sink or swim together. You’ll have to master the art of whispering sweet strategies into your walkie-talkie if you want to make it out alive. So gather your bravest (and quietest) friends, calibrate your mics, and get ready for a game of deadly hide-and-seek with your own vocal cords as the seeker. Just remember that in Don’t Shout Together, loose lips sink ships and set off explosives.
