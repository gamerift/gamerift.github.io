Game Description#

Resident Evil 7: Biohazard is a survival horror game that continues the famous series of games, one of the founders of the genre. The release is timed to coincide with the twentieth anniversary of the universe. An option is available to play using a virtual reality helmet and control of special complete controllers from VR glasses.

The seventh part was a return to the roots of the first games in the series, because, unlike parts 5 and 6, which were focused on action, shooting and the plot taking place in different parts of the world, this project has the main location in the form of a small estate of the Baker family, which gives the atmosphere is claustrophobic and increases the degree of fear. Also, there will no longer be enough ammunition, they will need to be treasured and used wisely and with utmost precision.

The plot will tell about the search by the main character named Ethan, his own wife Mia, who disappeared a couple of years ago. The investigation into the loss takes the protagonist to an ill-fated estate where terrible things happen. The Baker family lives there, distraught by the influence of the antagonist of the part - the little girl Evelyn, who has supernatural powers.

Resident Evil 7: Biohazard has multiple endings, achieved through a single choice near the end of the game. This will not affect the gameplay in any way. The home is filled with enemies and puzzles that Ethan must overcome on the way to saving his own life, Mia and Zoe, the Bakers' daughter, who did not fall under the influence of Evelyn. The bosses who complicate the protagonist’s life are Jack and Margaret Baker, as well as their son, Lucas. The final battle will take place with Evelyn - a living biological weapon.
