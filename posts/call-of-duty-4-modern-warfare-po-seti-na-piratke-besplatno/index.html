Game Description#

Call of Duty 4: Modern Warfare allows a pirate to take part in various online multiplayer competitions for free. You can play online with a friend or fight against random players around the world. The single player campaign of Call of Duty 4: Modern Warfare consists of three acts, a prologue and an epilogue. After completing it, a special arcade mode opens. Free online play allows you to take part in a wide variety of competitive matches alone or in a team. Network play includes 6 modes: Free play - classic deathmatch, including the Last Shot and Martyr perks; Team battle - group deathmatch, where the faction with the last survivor wins; Championship - capturing flags and then holding them; Headquarters - attack or defense control points;Sabotage - planting a bomb or defusing it;Search and destroy - the meaning is the same as in the previous mode, but there is a timer.

There is a system of hero classes. Weapons in Call of Duty 4: Modern Warfare are divided into primary and secondary. The first includes 5 types of heavy guns, and the second includes pistols. There are 5 classes in total with the ability to edit them and assign perks. The development system allows you to level up to level 55. Competitions may include various professional modifications, such as PAM4 or ProMod.

The entire arsenal of weapons is available in multiplayer matches. When you win battles, rewards are given and a reward system is in place. They are in turn divided into categories. Some are awarded for participating in the game, others for winning, etc. For effective use of the gun, you can get a rare modification or perk that improves the barrel. The number of kills rewards you with weapon modules, and headshots give you access to weapon skins. Only one gun in a specific class can be designated as a golden weapon. Three kills in a row will reward you with a drone.
