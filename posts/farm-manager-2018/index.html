Game Description#

Farm Manager 2018 is a simulator for building your own farm, where the territory of your competitors will slowly go into your possessions. A change of day and nights was added to the game. Also, such cultures as cucumbers, apples, strawberries and other varieties were added to the new part. If you are tired of growing simple wheat or oats, then you can definitely change the direction, but do not forget to monitor the economy.

All 20 crops have an individual ripening period, and after the collection they have a separate shelf life, after which no one will accept the goods, and the only thing you can do is to process it. Before planting a new culture, do not forget to prepare the ground for growing. The game is not only a farm simulator, but also a small simulator of a mechanic, because you will follow your own technique; You also have to see for the economy and constantly buy, prove new varieties, details, equipment and other materials and resources.

When you get tired of a simple planting of plants, and you will be tired of monitoring a slowly growing sprout, you can buy a whole processing factory or several thousand chickens, cows, sheep and other animals from which you can get meat or other nutrient resources. The simulator - Farm Manager 2018 is not only fields, but also factories, factories, processing buildings, own personnel, training costs, salary, plot campaign and a change of seasons, which constantly catches you when you have not yet moved away from summer heat, but have already mastered in autumn melancholy.
