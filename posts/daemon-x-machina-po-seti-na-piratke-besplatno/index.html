Game Description#

On the pirate siteDAEMON X MACHINA you can compete online for free. You can team up with a friend in co-op to play the campaign online. Free multiplayer features include story mode co-op while controlling a massive flying Arsenal-class mech. Before the start of the adventure, each player sets up and customizes both the hero and the robot to his liking. The protagonists' task is to protect humanity from rebellious machines.

The defenders are called Externals. The battalion was created to prevent the Moonfall disaster. As a mercenary of the Orbital Organization, you need to enter into battle with the Immortals - powerful robots at the mercy of a crazed AI. There are numerous tasks to complete, both primary and secondary. The goal is to destroy all bots once and for all. It is important to remember that in one moment anyone can turn from friend to enemy if artificial intelligence manages to take over the central system of the combat mech. Features of DAEMON X MACHINA: single-player campaign and online play; the ability to fly and move quickly; a variety of parts for customization; collecting spare parts from defeated enemies to use modules for leveling up your character; joint destruction of huge and ferocious bosses; confrontation mode: duel or teams of 2 participants; world rankings and tournament tables.
