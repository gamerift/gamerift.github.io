Game Description#

Looterkings is a 3D arcade game that allows you to travel through procedurally generated dungeons with three friends online. For free on the pirate Looterkings, you can play online against other participants or take part in co-op competitions with a friend. You need to control funny minions while exploring various caves and other locations.

The story tells of an underground world in which there is almost no mining left. In order to restore the underground to its former greatness, you need to make your way up to steal various wardrobe items, accessories, furniture and much more from the Elven Queen. Online play allows you to unite into a small horde of goblins to jointly clean out the queen’s chambers. The rooms are generated randomly, so each subsequent looting is never repeated twice.

Free online play allows you to take on the role of different goblins. Each of them has its own unique ability. Individual buffs allow you to create a variety of strategies and tactics for stealing jewelry. The mechanics of permanent death are in effect. If all four goblins die, then you have to start the passage again. After each level, you can upgrade your weapons and armor to make the game easier. Auxiliary items are purchased from merchants. Experience allows you to increase the strength of your squad and opens up access to new opportunities.
