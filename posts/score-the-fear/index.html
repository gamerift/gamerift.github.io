Game Description#

Score The Fear is a 3D adventure where a player interacts with a ghost intending to become a frightening entity. Central activity lies in the study of closed rooms in order to find a spirit named Yu-kun, hiding in different places. Being discovered, the ghost initiates the scene designed to scare the gamer. The plot develops for three game days, and its progress directly depends on the feedback.

Each meeting is another attempt to manifest his terrible nature. The gameplay in Score The Fear requires a yu-kun 5 times to complete one day. After the fifth detection, a screen appears with a proposal to assign from 1 to 3 stars for the efforts of ghosts. This assessment summarizes the daily events and serves as the main mechanism for influencing the narrative. What is happening on the second day is formed on the basis of previously set points.

The main tasks for the mentor of young ghosts:

Explore the rooms in search of a hidden spirit. Become a witness to Yu-kun’s attempts to scare you. Evaluate his efforts on a three -star scale. Watch how your verdicts affect the development of the plot. Open all 6 unique endings of this story.

The total glasses in the first two days determine the final result for the third. Such a system creates an extensive plot leading to one of the 6 possible finals that reflect the development of the spirit based on a verdict. One session of this adventure takes from 5 to 15 minutes, and the total time to get acquainted with all the outcome is about 60 minutes.
