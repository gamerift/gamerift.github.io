Game Description#

Burger Bots Inc is an intense fight for survival, where the only means of defense for the entire team is a single shotgun. Horrors lurk in the basement of the establishment, and the staff provides the resources to prepare cutlets to combat them. The survival of the team directly depends on coordinated actions and the ability to withstand random, nightmarish events that bring chaos to the work process. Each shift turns into a test, because the main task is not only to serve visitors, but also to stay alive, repelling attacks from hostile entities.

The action takes place in an intergalactic diner, where a team of up to eight members jointly manages the kitchen and dining room. Using distance-based voice chat, actions to fulfill orders and distribute roles are coordinated. The main goal of the Burger Bots Inc. project. is to make enough profit to pay the rent each week, otherwise you’ll have to start all over again. This restaurant management simulator forces you to constantly balance between culinary tasks and the need to fight off monsters.

The success of an enterprise is determined not only by the speed of cooking, but also by the ability to work with unique clients who have their own rules of behavior. Dissatisfaction with any of them can lead to the fact that an ordinary visitor becomes a new enemy for the entire team. In addition to serving strange and sometimes frightening guests, gamers will have to deal with the timely ordering of fresh ingredients and garbage disposal, maintaining order in this unusual establishment.
