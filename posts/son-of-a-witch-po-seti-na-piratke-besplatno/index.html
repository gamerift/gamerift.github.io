Game Description#

The action roguelikeSon of a Witch can be played online with a friend or with up to 4 people. All multiplayer content is available on the pirate site, including dozens of weapons, magic items, spells, 8 enemy factions, 28 bosses and much more. You can go on a joint adventure online online or join a cooperative battle in local mode. The goal is to find a magical artifact that can save the entire kingdom. Gameplay of Son of a Witch includes: possibility of free online play; exploration of procedurally generated dungeons; numerous shops, treasures, dungeons with challenges, etc.; quests from various NPCs and other heroes; 42 unique companion pets; three classes of heroes: warrior, archer and mage; mechanics of permanent death.

There are 10 heroes to choose from. Each one has their own unique weapon and new special items. Multiplayer allows you to adventure as a Knight, Sorceress, Druid, Archer, Pyromancer, Paladin, Barbarian, Skeleton Ranger, Alchemist or Lich. Some characters are unlocked as you progress.

The world contains about 130 unique types of weapons and magical items. Some weapons require a mana cost. While exploring the world, you need to collect gold to buy new equipment. You can also find special artifacts, such as a voodoo doll, etc. The weapons used are the Meteor Staff, Bomb, Celestial Smasher Hammer and other unique types of weapons.

There are 8 unique areas to visit: forest, battlements, citadel, dungeon, catacombs, desert, hell and ice mountain. Each location contains up to three powerful bosses. Enemies are divided into factions: Goblins, Royal Guard, Thieves, Undead (skeletons, zombies, vampires), Ice Mountain Clan, Desert Mercenaries, Demons and Animals (boars and dragons, beetles, bats, hornets, etc.)
