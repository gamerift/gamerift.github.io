Game Description#

STAY tells of the maid motel Kes, plunging into the abyss of obsession and horror. The unknown is imbued with a dangerous sympathy for her and begins the affair campaign with letters. Correspondence becomes a threatening character, forcing the kes to enter into a dangerous game of survival against an invisible, but relentless threat. The narrative unfolds in the walls of its secluded workplace, creating a claustrophobic atmosphere for subsequent events, and the decisions made determine the finale.

The facets of madness in the struggle for life in Stay:

Immersion in the psychological thriller about the persecution. Disclosure of the motives of an obsessed antagonist through letters. Making decisions that directly affect the development of events. The struggle for survival in a closed and oppressive space.

The letters received during the passage contain messages based on real stories of harassment collected through numerous surveys. Such a design solution is designed to show a perverted perception and growing despair of the pursuer without embellishment. The contents include materials with direct references to violence, so people who are sensitive to such topics should be careful. Promotion in the plot is associated with this correspondence, gradually revealing the way of thinking and the intention of a stalker.

The gameplay simulates horror for the survival of the 1990s, using stationary cameras from a third person and tank control as the main method of interaction. The player overcomes the dangers, choosing a strategy: you can use secrecy mechanics and hide to avoid collisions or collect weapons for an open confrontation. The study of the world and key decisions made throughout the passage affect the outcome of history, which ultimately leads to one of 4 possible endings for protagonists.
