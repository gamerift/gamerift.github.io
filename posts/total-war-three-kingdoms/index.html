Game Description#

Total War: THREE KINGDOMS is the first game in the series that recreates epic conflicts in Ancient China. The game combines a turn-based campaign of empire building and conquest with epic real-time battles. 190 AD. A beautiful but fragmented country awaits a new emperor and a new life. The player will have to unify China, create the next great dynasty and leave a legacy that will endure for millennia.

There are 12 legendary commanders to choose from, and help will come in the form of heroic characters. With their help, it will be necessary to destroy all enemies on the economic, technological, political and economic fronts. What kind of commander will you become - one who values ​​the bonds of lasting friendship, forms fraternal alliances and commands respect even from his enemies? Or those who are known for their treachery, terrible betrayal and masterful political intrigue? It’s up to you to decide.

The game recreates the Three Kingdoms of Ancient China in all their natural beauty. Battles will take place in lush subtropics, parched deserts and snow-capped mountains. The player will have plenty of time to admire legendary sights like the Great Wall of China and the Yangtze River. During the restoration of harmony in China, you will have the opportunity to visit all its nooks and crannies.

A new empire will be forged with the help of one of the 12 legendary commanders who came from the famous historical epic of China - the Romance of the Three Kingdoms. Fearless commanders, mighty warriors and renowned businessmen - they all have a unique gaming style and their own tasks. They will be supported by a variety of heroes who take command of your armies, govern provinces and strengthen your empire in every possible way. Heroes are the heart of the game itself and China will be defined by these champions.

The game uses the Guanxi model, a Chinese concept of dynamic relationships. All relationships between the characters determine the course of the game. Each of the characters is endowed with his own character, motivation and what he loves and, on the contrary, does not accept. They also form their own relationships, both positive and negative. Gorgeous graphics and spectacular wushu style turn the game into the art of war. The colorful interface, breathtaking views and Chinese-inspired artwork make traveling through this world a feast for the eyes.

The turn-based campaign and real-time battles are even more interconnected than before. Each action in battle now has much more significant consequences, influencing the attitude of the heroes towards you as a commander, as well as whether friendship or enmity arises between them. In a world where strong allies are one of the key components of success, the way to achieve victory is given new meaning. Game Features: An exciting journey through a detailed reconstruction of Ancient China. 12 different potential emperors and legendary heroes. A system of friendship and enmity between heroes. A dynamic system of relationships between the parties to the conflict. Almost complete freedom of choice in how exactly everyone will bow to you Ancient China.
