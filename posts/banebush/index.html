Game Description#

Banebush is a title whose action takes place in the mysterious Hukendary Mud, a place where few looked and where even fewer who returned. Users stand in place of the nameless protagonist, who finds himself in a distant and deserted area. The world is filled with puzzles, secrets and many trials that make users be on the alert. This is a game that requires research and strategy, because each choice has its own weight and affects the further path. As the players make their way through thick forests, forgotten ruins and winding caves, every corner hides something new, offering experience rich in discoveries.

Banebush gameplay is based on a solution to problems. From the very beginning, gamers are faced with obstacles that can only be overcome with smart thinking and resourcefulness. The environment is full of interactive elements offering gamers to manipulate their environment to move forward. It does not matter how to cross the river, open a secret door in ancient ruins or use the materials found along the way for the manufacture of the necessary tools - the game makes each action to be important. As the journey unfolds, the need for thoughtful planning increases, prompting users to adapt their approach depending on the resources available at hand. Resource management plays a crucial role. Surviving at Hinterland Mood is not only to solve puzzles, but also to maintain health, tools and supplies.
