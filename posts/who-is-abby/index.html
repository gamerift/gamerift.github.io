Game Description#

Who is Abby begins with Tom, a man determined to find his missing sister. The title immerses users in an intense psychological thriller where mystery and exploration go hand in hand. With every step, Tom plunges deeper into the mystery of Abby’s disappearance, revealing a story shrouded in mystery and darkness. As you take on the role of Tom, the journey becomes personal, with each discovery adding another piece to the puzzle of Abby’s life.

Tom’s investigation is far from simple. Trust in the police is weakening, and he is left to rely only on his own instincts and tenacity. The world is huge and detailed, each location tells its own story. As you move through these places, you search not only for Abby, but also for the truth hidden in the corners and shadows. Each chapter poses new challenges for you, forcing you to dive deeper into the plot, which twists every now and then, not allowing you to break away from reality.

One of the main elements is research. Who is Abby invites you to thoroughly explore your surroundings. Every item you come across could be important. A seemingly innocuous item may be the key to progress in your search or understanding of an important part of the plot. It’s not just a game where you follow a given path; this is a game where curiosity and attention to detail are rewarded. The environment becomes a character itself, offering clues and hints needed to solve the mystery.
