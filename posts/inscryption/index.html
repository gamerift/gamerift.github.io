Game Description#

Inscryption is a card adventure with roguelike elements that focuses on the fact that the darkness still hides many unknown secrets. The strategy is made in an escape the room format, has multiple puzzles and a terrifying atmosphere. The user is transported to the goblin’s hut, where he needs to embark on dangerous adventures to find out what secrets are hidden in this building. The events take place in a forest house, where the protagonist is trapped by some sinister creature who wants the guy to play board games with him.

In the process, the gamer acts on a three-dimensional map, where he makes moves, choosing certain cards, simultaneously exploring the house and solving puzzles. The hero’s task is to survive, despite the horrors that occur in the hut itself. The player gradually assembles a card deck from forest inhabitants - mutated animals that look scary due to the surgery that was once performed on them, as well as frightening experiments. The user needs to solve logical problems in order to get out of the locked building in the process, but as they progress, reality ceases to border on fantasy, and an ordinary game turns out to be not at all what was expected at the beginning.
