Game Description#

InWarCraft 3: The Frozen Throne 1.26a (Irina Bot) you can play online for free through Irina Host Bot, competing on a pirate with a friend without Garena. You can fight both with bots and with any players from all over the world. The strategy is the official add-on for Reign of Chaos. The gameplay has undergone changes compared to the previous part of Warcraft 3.

From now on, you can build a store at your base, buying the necessary items right on the spot. Each faction has its own unique shops. A tavern is available where neutral heroes are hired. New units and one hero per race have been added. The balance has been reworked and prices for buildings, improvements, items, etc. have been changed. Characters have acquired additional attacks and defensive abilities. Multiplayer WarCraft 3: The Frozen Throne allows you to compete locally. The project includes: the possibility of free online play; competitions without Garena and battles through Irina Host Bot; simple game creation and selection of various modifiers; all multiplayer functions and modes; creation of maps and your own rooms.

The story part is represented by 4 campaigns: Terror from the Depths, Curse of the Avengers, Lord of Darkness and the Foundation of Darotar. You must play as blood elves, undead, horde and night elves. Each campaign has a unique hero with individual skills. During the passage you will play for such characters as Arthas, Sylvanas, Illidan, Thrall and others. The scenario for the orcs is divided into three acts and is in no way connected with the previous chapters.

The story of the add-on directly continues the previous game. Events start after the end of the Battle of Mount Hyjal. All races of the fantasy world of Azeroth began to return to everyday life. However, the consequences of the Third War do not allow a full return to a peaceful existence, as a result of which new conflicts arise between the inhabitants of the adjacent lands.
