Game Description#

ODD Dorable opens a world in front of the player, created based on hand-drawn illustrations of a 4-year-old child. The main goal is to help the princess find magic sweets scattered around the district to return lost magic. The open world of the project consists of several funny, dissimilar locations, complete secrets and hiding places. Formation advancement requires the study of these areas, interaction with the environment and solving local puzzles organically inscribed in children’s drawing.

The gamer has to carefully study every corner in order to find everything you need to complete the mission. The ODD Dorable gameplay is built on a solution of puzzles built directly surrounded. These tasks are created accessible and require not so much complex actions as an attentive study of the area and logical thinking to solve them. During the adventure, you can meet several bizarre characters living in different regions. They can give a hint or simply add the atmosphere to this unusual place.

The passage of the entire Title is designed for a short period of time and will take about 30 to 60 minutes, offering a concentrated experience ideal for one evening. The plot basis is the collection of 6 special magic sweets hidden around the world. The search for all sweets is the key to the completion of the main task and the Princess returning to the Princess of Her Magical abilities, which is the end point of the trip. The game in every possible way encourages curiosity when navigation by cartoon setting, causing a sense of childhood delight and discovery. This single game experience combines the study of the world with a solution to simple puzzles, offering a whole and finished journey.
