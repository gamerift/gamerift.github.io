Game Description#

On the pirate War Mongrels you can team up with a friend to fight intense battles online with numerous opponents from the Second World War. Multiplayer is available for free and allows you to play online with players from all over the world. There is not only the possibility of free online play, but also a single-player campaign that allows you to complete story quests in solo mode.

The plot is based on real events. Alone or with a partner, you must go through a series of dark events, fighting in bloody battles with enemy forces. The gameplay is an isometric action game with tactical elements. The online game War Mongrels is built on constant communication and interaction between players. In each mission you need to choose new tactics against enemies.

The environment is completely interactive. You can interact with objects, luring opponents into traps. Allowed to undermine and collapse the building. All characters have unique abilities and characteristics. The heroes have their own biography and character. There are stealth mechanics. The arsenal of weapons consists of numerous guns of different calibers. As you progress, the heroes' skills are upgraded and new abilities are unlocked.
