Game Description#

Airships: Conquer the Skies (2018) is a multiplayer sandbox indie action and strategy game developed by David Stark under his own publishing house for various platforms, including PC. Airships has extremely positive reviews on various resources. The game is truly loved by gamers from all over the world.

So what will you have to do? Build pixelated skyships in steampunk style! The buildings are shown in cross-section and from the side, and their modules are controlled by crew members. During battles, players give commands to their own fleet. It should be noted that all the ships, the landscape - all this can be destroyed by establishing your own “order”. Fight with friends over a local network or the Internet.

The insides of ships are somewhat reminiscent of an anthill. There are many sailors inside the ship, each of them is responsible for a specific task. They all work tirelessly for the benefit of your own fleet. When building a ship, every choice matters. So build wisely. Better yet, choose your own tactics and always stick to it.

There are also some pretty intelligent NPCs in the game, so be careful with them. Before you know it, you’ll be boarded. There is also an economic system and the capture of cities using your own flying ship. Interesting, isn’t it? Well, are you ready to build your own fleet and take control of the sky?
