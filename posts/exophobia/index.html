Game Description#

Exophobia is a first -person pixel shooter in which you will go to explore a spaceship. The shuttle fell to the surface of an unknown planet. Here you will encounter the hordes of alien opponents. The game is made in retro-stylistic, drawing inspiration in arcade shooters of the 80s.

Explore the station and find out the background of the inhabitants of the planet, as well as try to find out what caused the shipwreck. EXOPHOBIA gameplay is based on the dynamic action, where you quickly move around the location, jump, dodge enemy attacks, throw grenades, stunned opponents and much more.

As in classic shooters, the gameplay is based on positioning. Study the behavior of enemies, use the features of the environment and act as soon as possible to the enemy. Aliens represented by several types of enemies. The levels contain various secrets that are not so easy to find. However, the most attentive players will receive rewards and bonuses if they find all the secrets on locations.

Having discovered a special room, you can improve the character, equipment and abilities. They will make the character much stronger and more agile. Each level is represented by a complex boss, which will need to look for an individual approach. Having died, the hero is reborn from the control point.
