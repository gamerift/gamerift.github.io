Game Description#

RECHARGE is a simulation-arcade racing experience in which the player controls radio-controlled machines. The gameplay was built on a physical model of behavior and allows the customization of vehicles. Gamers compete on tracks created based on professional RC tracks and other locations, including beaches and entertainment parks. The project is intended for use with several input devices, supporting both standard gamepads and specialized controllers.

These features create the basis for individual and competitive races. RECHARGE offers modes for a single and group gameplay. In solitary mode, the racer competes with computer opponents of various complexity. You can also compete with the recording of your best circle displayed on the highway in the form of a ghost machine. The network mode maintains online sediment up to 12 people in each race. These events are held in private lobby for familiar companies or in public, open to everyone.

This game provides a system for assembling a personal RC machine using mechanical and visual modifications. Productivity improves through the upgrade of the components, allowing you to change the engine, electronic speed control and chassis. These improvements affect the behavior of the machine on the track. Various colors, stickers and several body types are available for visual settings, which creates a unique appearance of the technique.
