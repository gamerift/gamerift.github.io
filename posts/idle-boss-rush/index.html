Game Description#

In IDLE BOSS Rush, the starting point for creating a powerful kingdom is a lifeless wasteland. The main task is to turn a modest camp into a prosperous city, gathering an army to defeat giant bosses. Each battle with the enemy is a serious test that requires either time or competent tactics. Further development depends on the management of the population and resources. The economic foundation is important for the maintenance of an army that can reflect the onslaught of huge monsters and expand the boundaries of the settlement.

The gameplay in Idle Boss Rush offers a balance between active and passive styles of the game. The detachments continue to fight on their own, allowing you to pick up the accumulated prey after the absence. For more effective management, gamers can configure their builds for specific opponents, accelerating progress. This implies the strategic selection of buildings and complementary improvements to increase combat power. The project supports any selected approach - from direct management to automatic development, ensuring continuous movement forward.

A detailed setting of a character and city is available through a few interconnected mechanics. You can improve objects to unlock unique effects and appoint advisers specializing in the military, economic or magical spheres. The supply of advisers with powerful equipment in combination with citywide upgrades opens access to powerful combinations that more effectively destroy enemies. Additional progress is opened through special Boss Rush tests, giving constant bonuses. In addition, this strategy has a voluntary prestige mechanics for those who are looking for new opportunities for long -term development.
