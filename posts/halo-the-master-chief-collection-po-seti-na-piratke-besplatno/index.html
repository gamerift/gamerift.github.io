Game Description#

Halo The Master Chief Collection is a collection of games about the Master Chief, which you can play online with a friend, using all the multiplayer features for free on the pirate. By fighting online online, you can organize PvP competitions or play in co-op. The pack included all four parts, as well as a branch part of Halo with the subtitle Reach. Free online play includes battles on every map ever released for multiplayer competition.

The gameplay is a first-person shooter set in a science fiction setting. There are car sections made from a third person. The re-release of Halo The Master Chief Collection has not undergone any changes in terms of plot, lore and gameplay. The developers have updated the visual effects and sound. Each episode includes a single player campaign with co-op options and online play. All enemies introduced in the adventure have unique objectives and tactics. During the battle, the protagonist can take control of a space shuttle, turret or car.

In total, the Master Chief Collection includes 67 missions in 6 unique campaigns. The plot begins with the heroic deed of the Noble Six squad and ends with the appearance of an intergalactic threat in the fourth part. The multi-user mode includes 120 network cards with their own features and modes. Dostna Kuznica is an extensive map editor that allows you to create your own levels and scenarios. New objects were added to it, giving more creative freedom to the authors.
