Game Description#

Riven evokes thoughts of forgotten puzzles and worlds shrouded in a secret. Here users are on the era of Riven, the accumulation of islands, whispering secrets in the wind. Lush jungles meet with the reverence of caves, and each environment is carefully worked out and suggests itself for research. But this is not an excursion tour. This is Adventure, which throws a glove, demanding from gamers to become masters of puzzles. Task? To unravel secrets, guessing one riddle at a time. Each solid puzzle opens another screw in the story of the narrative, bringing users closer to the truth.

And what is it true! The story of the authorities, knowledge and the fatal consequences of ambitions unfolds around the mysterious gene and his power over Riven. The wife of Atrus, Catherine, becomes a girl who has fallen into trouble, and you are a valiant knight who goes to her release. But this is not about girls and not about knights in shining armor. We are talking about observation, deduction and volume of the exciting moment “Yeah!”, When the decision rises in its place. The puzzles themselves are similar to intricate gears, smoothly lingering with the environment. There are no far -fetched tasks.

Each resolved puzzle is like the discovery of a lost fragment of Riven history, a satisfactory click that makes you move forward. The genius lies in the construction of the world. This is evidence of a force that can captivate with its internal logic, its secrets, which are waiting for them to be disclosed. This is a game that asks you to think, mess around, really live in her world. No training lessons, no catchy graphics that would distract. Only pure, without impurities, the bliss of the solution of puzzles.
