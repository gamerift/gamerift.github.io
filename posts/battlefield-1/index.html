Game Description#

Battlefield 1 is a first-person shooter in the setting of the First World War. The game is the 15th in the series of the same name. The plot is divided into five chapters and begins with a prologue. The story takes place in 1918 and is told from the perspective of tankman Daniel Edwards, who controls the Black Bass tank. Before the war, he was an ordinary driver, but now he has to break through the German line.

The player will use guns from the First World War, including pistols, shotguns, semi-automatic shotguns, pump-action shotguns and mustard gas. The developers have significantly reworked the melee combat system. Among the vehicles available will be cars, motorcycles, tanks, armored vehicles, airplanes, airships and horses. Battlefield 1 includes 6 modes: Capture - holding a position and earning points; Supremacy - the same as the previous point, but without military equipment; Operations - one team captures points while the other defends them; Assault - destruction of telegraphs; Team fight - a standard deathmatch; War pigeons - you need to catch a bird and hold it for a certain time; to send it with the news to headquarters.

The main classes of fighters are represented by Assault, Medic, Support and Scout. In addition, elite ranks are available, including Sentry, Flamethrower, Tank Hunter, Trench Raider and Infiltrator. Up to 64 fighters can participate in multiplayer simultaneously. One of the features of the gameplay is that the losing side is provided with support in the form of an airship, armored train, ship or heavy tank.
