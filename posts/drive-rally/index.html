Game Description#

Drive Rally is an arcade game about rally races that transfers gamers during the glory of the 1990s. Having focused on speeds, skill and exciting sensations of an open road, Advencura offers nostalgic experience for fans of classic rally races. Users take on the role of a rally racer, competing in various iconic places around the world. From the dusty deserts of Africa to the snowy peaks of Scandinavia - each area offers unique trials and opportunities.

The game presents a diverse range of legendary rally cars, each of which has its own distinctive characteristics and control style. Gamers can configure their cars in accordance with their preferences, whether it is powerful, gross power or more mobile, technical driving style. One of the distinctive features of Drive Rally is the presence of a second driver. The second driver provides important navigation and feedback, adding strategy and realism to the gameplay.

Users should work closely with their second driver to overcome complex routes and avoid expensive mistakes. The help of the second driver is very important for success, because even the most experienced driver can benefit from a competent navigator. In addition to the main race mechanics, the title offers a number of additional opportunities. The photo mode allows you to capture the most memorable moments and share it with friends, and the capabilities of settings allow you to personalize the car and create a unique driving experience.
