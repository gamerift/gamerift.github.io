Game Description#

Deltarune - spin -off puzzle Undertale, which is talking about the name of the game, acting as an anagram of the word. The difference from the understail is that now there is the opportunity to fight immediately by the team of heroes. They can protect themselves, use magic and many other capabilities. The design and graphic performance has changed significantly.

The protagonist of Delta Runa is a boy named Chris. The monster from school drags him into the dark world with Susie’s girl. There they are met by a ralsey who calls the heroes bright and reports that they are elected, who will destroy the forces of darkness. The fighting is represented by step -by -step battles, where protagonists choose between such mechanics as “attack” or “mercy”. The conservation system has undergone significant changes. From now on, Deltarune can be stored simultaneously on three slots. From the innovations, a run that can be set as an automatic to the setting. Suzi, Ralse and Lancer will walk simultaneously with Chris. They follow the protagonist and some puzzles will be tied to this, where you will need to go through the area along a certain trajectory.

During the passage of Deltarun, it is necessary to collect used and quest objects. The consumable includes, for example, first -aid kits. Keysticks such as a mysterious key or a mobile phone. They cannot be thrown out of the inventory. All characters can increase characteristics using various equipment, including protective objects and weapons. There was an opportunity to use magic. Also, heroes have the parameters of courage, rudeness, insolence, kindness and fluffy.
