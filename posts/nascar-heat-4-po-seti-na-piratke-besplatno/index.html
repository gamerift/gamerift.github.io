Game Description#

On the piratedNASCAR Heat 4 you can play online with a friend, taking part in online multiplayer competitions for free. Online play and single-player career mode are available. Players can take control of all licensed cars and teams participating in NASCAR races. There are 38 official tracks, a huge number of sponsors and a lot of other content.

NASCAR Heat 4 introduces new features and competitions such as Track Map, Unlockable Paint Schemes, Dynamic User Interface, More Driving Control Options, Deeper Career Mode, Sonoma Raceway & Charlotte Roval Track Updates. In addition to visual improvements, transport controls have been significantly updated. Each car behaves individually on the track and requires a thorough test drive before racing. The possibility of free online play allows you to get behind the wheel of any car under the auspices of the Nascar championship.

A special mode is pair drifting. Together with another player, you can organize competitions in drawing shapes and patterns with tires. In single-player mode, the artificial intelligence of opponents has improved, so opponents analyze the situation more carefully and adapt to any events. A special training mode will help you adjust the range of your skills, develop stability in driving, get used to the individual control of a particular car, and much more.
