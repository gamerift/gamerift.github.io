Game Description#

In Out of Ore, gamers have to take many heavy equipment under their control, with the help of which it will be possible to extract ore, move the ground, dig pits, build or repair buildings and much more. It is necessary to test yourself as a driver of construction equipment, which is intended for various purposes. The main character goes on the plot for the very north of the country where there is a small base from Company Mining Corp. During the passage, the users will have to build roads, bridges, tunnels, as well as connect different routes among themselves.

The main task for players in Out of Ore is the fulfillment of its obligations using a wide arsenal of tools and a large fleet with equipment. Users will go behind the wheel of various excavators, bulldozers, tractors and other machines, which are designed for hard work on crossed areas. The gamers will be able to clear bridgeheads for filling the foundation or roads, equalize the earth, dig or loosen the earth to change the landscape, etc. The main goal for users is terraforming locations to fulfill the plan issued by the management. Gradually, the protagonist will develop and pump his professional skills, which will allow you to try out more steep and technological modes of transport, which will help in solving complex problems.
