Game Description#

The Jackbox Party Pack is the first in a series of competitive party games that you can play online for free. Together with a friend or in a company of up to 100 people, participants take part in various types of competitions on the Internet online. You can play from various devices, be it a personal computer, phone, tablet, etc.

Online play and single-player competitions are available. The Jackbox Party Pack includes 5 unique types of competitions. The possibility of free online play allows each participant to show ingenuity and creativity. Meets the players of YOU DON’T KNOW JACK 2015 - a comedy tournament in which you need to answer questions as funny and original as possible.

The second Fibbage XL competition is similar to the first, but the emphasis is on bluffing. Participants will be faced with tricky questions, so you should be as careful as possible! Drawful Word Spud is a drawing competition. In fact, what is needed here is not so much artistic skills as creativity. The final Lie Swatter competition allows you to team up with up to 100 people to experience a competition filled with wacky facts. Which ones are true and which ones are lies? The smartest one will win!
