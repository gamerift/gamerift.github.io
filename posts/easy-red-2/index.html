Game Description#

Easy Red 2 is a first -person shooter with elements of a strategy that immerses in an authentic atmosphere of World War II. The gameplay can plunge into tense and cruel battles, where the main thing is planning and tactics. The gameplay relies on realism. Take a break through the tanks and the enemy’s huge army. Do not fall into the explosion zone and look for ways to survive on the battlefield with your partners.

Spacious cards are available that allow you to make maneuvers. Many aspects of gameplay are created with historical accuracy. Each battle in Easy Red 2 is represented by a realistic approach to battle. Many units, military equipment, a huge amount of explosives, mines and much more. Each action in the game is of great importance and can turn the outcome of the battle.

Events unfold in Italy, allowing you to fight off the coast of Antio and capture the peaks of Montecassino. Visit Japan to take part in the Battle of Quajalein. The developers try to convey emotions and a sense of anxiety during fights as much as possible. The player is rewarded not for the number of murders, but for the ability to strategically think and apply survival skills.

Go through a series of historical events: landing off the coast of the Italian city of Kassino in 1944, the war in the Pacific Ocean, the conquest of Quajalein in the Marshall Islands and much more. Each main mission is represented by a large number of secondary tasks. Fight both with hand -to -hand combat and firearms, and jump into the cabin of tanks, including Panzer 4, Sherman, M3 Lee and others. The battles take place on interactive maps, where bags of sand, walls, fences, cars and defensive structures can be destroyed, which will greatly affect the further outcome of military operations.
