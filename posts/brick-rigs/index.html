Game Description#

Brick Rigs is a sandbox that allows you to create a variety of vehicles, and then enjoy realistic driving and destruction physics. You need to build cars using various blocks. The Brick Rings toolkit allows you to create a dragster, a fire truck, a forklift, a helicopter, an airplane or even a tank - it all depends on your imagination and skill.

Gameplay Brick Rigs is a full-fledged construction set with which you can create any type of transport. No one forbids building a train and erecting rails, and placing many objects at the end of the railway track in order to ram them at full speed, watching how individual parts of a complex puzzle fly apart according to physics.

You can design a racing car and go at full speed to conquer local landscapes or conquer the air by creating an aircraft, observing all the expanses of the map from above, etc. There is a multiplayer mode that offers you to test the physics and strength of cars with your friends. The gameplay allows you to organize both races on ring tracks and demolition derbies, ramming each other and watching how parts of your vehicle fly apart.
