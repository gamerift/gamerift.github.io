Game Description#

An exciting auction of old things in Storage Hunter. Gamer will be able to take part in the auction, where abandoned warehouses are played, which are perfect for perestroika and old containers. Of all these things killed over time, you can make brand new things suitable for everyday use. The user’s task is extremely simple, just that you need to correctly and wisely assess the cost of each item that is in this room. The winner will be the one who will work the most at the auction. Each location or people participating in the auction can present a couple of interesting surprises, but not everyone will clearly like such antics.

The protagonist will be one of the applicants for abandoned warehouses and other unique items in Storage Hunter. Everything will begin with small garages on a cargo port. It is necessary to bet on those containers for which the owners did not pay rent. Thus, they can easily be got them. To accumulate money and more skills in order to be ready to invest a large amount in the future. In these territories, you can find ordinary households, spare parts for cars and even military causals. More than a thousand items can be redeemed for large or not very money.
