Game Description#

Ace of Spades: Battle Builder provides the opportunity to play online with a friend on a pirate in an updated version of an old project. Thanks to the changes made by the developers, users lost many problems and also got the opportunity to create game worlds on their own. But the most important thing is that the scale of what is happening has also increased. Now, up to 32 players can simultaneously create objects on maps online. They can either join existing clans or remain alone.

What often happens on the maps in Ace of Spades: Battle Builder can be called pure madness. Each user has complete freedom of action, so he can recreate architectural structures, destroy them and build them again, arrange his own base, or give up on construction altogether and immediately engage in a firefight. Depending on the selected mode, the circumstances of what is happening are constantly changing, and you also have to adapt to this. For example, the possibility of free online play in zombie mode will turn gamers into survivors. They will need to fend off the attacks of the dead for as long as possible, using any available means as weapons. Capture the flag, as the name suggests, involves capturing an enemy item and protecting your own.

In the “Territory Control” mod, the map is pre-divided into zones that need to be captured. The team that manages to capture the largest area wins. These are not all the modes that can be tried, and each time gamers will find themselves in more and more amazing conditions. Before each skirmish, online play allows you to select a character class. There are 4 of them in total - paratrooper, sniper, engineer and miner. Each has its own arsenal of weapons and equipment, and depending on this, its combat potential varies. And given the fact that such battles can be carried out in the arenas themselves, the level of the character’s capabilities can increase.
