Game Description#

InCastleStorm an amazing world was created in 2D format, where you can play online with a friend on a pirate, as well as play solo. The focus is on the confrontation between knights and Vikings, and users have the chance to directly participate in this event. So, gamers can choose their favorite side and enter the battle online online. At the same time, it supports the option of both multiplayer (on opposite sides of the conflict) and co-op, when session participants take one side. In case of a 1 on 1 confrontation, you can split the screen to watch what is happening from your perspective.

Before the start of a battle in CastleStorm, you are given the option of distributing forces and resources for the future battle. So, you can adjust the formation of the soldiers so that they march in a certain sequence. If you place a row of swordsmen, and behind them place priests with the healing skill, the effectiveness of the first line will greatly increase due to regeneration. Improvements also affect weapons, because depending on the current level of equipment (spears, blades, halberds, etc.) the damage to the enemy will differ. For example, if an archer uses pumped arrows, then when defending a castle there is a high probability of preventing opponents from approaching their target at all. The possibility of free online play allows gamers to act together, wisely spending resources and defending themselves even against highly-improved enemies.

As for the modes, the network game offers both a story campaign with 12 unique types of landscapes in locations, and survival. In addition, there is a “Last Man Standing” mode. In this case, users control warriors who fight off crowds of opponents until only 1 hero remains. As for the effects, the destructibility of buildings and third-party objects is carefully designed. If you destroy a wall support or an important part of the wall, the building will literally fall apart.
