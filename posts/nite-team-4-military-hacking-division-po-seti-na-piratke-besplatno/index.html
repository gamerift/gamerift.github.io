Game Description#

NITE Team 4 Military Hacking Division is a military simulator with the ability to play online with a friend for free on a pirate, completing various cooperative missions or competing online online in 70 missions. At the very beginning, the Academy introduces the basics of gameplay, teaching hacking. The online game offers training in various cybersecurity methods and modules, shows how to collect information, scan ports and even take fingerprints.

Alone or with a partner, you will need to develop attack strategies, study digital forensics, and much more. The possibility of free online play in NITE Team 4 Military Hacking Division allows you to take on assignments from one of the world’s intelligence agencies, such as the NSA, CIA, GCHQ, CSIS, MSS, GRU and many others. Each organization has its own unique missions and methods to combat cyberterrorism. The multiplayer mode allows participants to test their hacking, infiltration, hacking and resurfacing skills.

The campaign includes 4 highly developed special operations. For example, tracking down criminals on the darknet, coordinating strike drones, etc. For successful completion of tasks, rewards are given and your reputation increases. In total, you can earn 20 unique rewards. They are divided into rare and epic. In the Open World section, you can take on more complex missions: wiretapping phones, online research, faking websites with important information, etc.
