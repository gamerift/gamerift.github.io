Game Description#

Rus vs. Lizards is a game in which users go on a journey to Ancient Rus' - a world plagued by an invasion of lizards and unholy creatures. In this epic adventure, you take on the role of a blessed hero, charged with the sacred duty of protecting the holy places of the Ancestors from the green plague. In the game you will find intense battles with hordes of lizards and other evil spirits, in which you will be able to show your heroic strength in the name of the Ancestors. Traveling through the lands of Hyperborea, you will participate in the sacred massacre in honor of SVAROG, and the blessing of Perun will give you powerful strength.

Your mission spans various regions, including Baikalsk, the Republic of Tyva and Hyperborea, and is to protect these territories from the inexorable invasion of evil lizards. To strengthen your resolve, you can even drink the sacred waters of Lake Baikal before engaging in battle with heroes and evil spirits. Lizards Must Die features legendary warriors of Ancient Rus' such as Radislav Bagirov, a master of sword and shield, and Vseslav the Sorcerer, known for his mastery of two swords. In addition, Drocheslav, the son of Sergius, is a mighty warrior about whom there are legends that he has unprecedented strength and immortality. Moves like GOIDA, Perun’s Thunderclaps, Slavic Chest Slams, Pinwheel Kicks, and Slavic Brotherly Rushes in Lizards Must Die provide an immersive experience based on historical and mythological elements (no). Accept the call of duty and take part in the godly celebrations that will follow your victories, all for the glory of Yarila.
