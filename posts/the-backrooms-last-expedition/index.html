Game Description#

The BackRooms: Last Expedition is a cooperative in the horror genre, in which from 1 to 4 gamers are united in teams to survive in an alarming world. The main attention is paid to the passage of a series of dangerous, constantly changing levels, each of which is filled with mysterious creatures. Together, players should try to escape from the horror surrounding them, using their collective forces to resist the unknown. From terrible decorations to unexpected meetings - Title holds the users in tension as they delve into the unknown.

In The Backrooms: Last Expedition, teamwork plays a key role. Each level sets new tasks for gamers - from avoiding terrifying monsters to solving confusing puzzles, requiring acute mind and quick solutions. Here you need to not just survive, but rally and rely on each other’s ability to get out. Although some may seem that the tension here is too large, it is clear that cooperation is the only way forward. Gamers should remain vigilant and be attentive to threats that lurk in shadows and are ready to attack at any time.

Each card has been worked out with particular care, offering a unique environment that enhances the voltage. The old favorites were rethought with new details that add new layers of fear. The sign atmosphere of BackRooms was carefully preserved, allowing users to plunge into a world, at the same time familiar and frightening. Attention to details guarantees that none of the two sessions will be the same, maintaining experience unpredictable.
