Game Description#

TCG Card Shop Simulator puts players in the role of a trading card store (TCG) owner who is responsible for the day-to-day operations and long-term growth of their business. From the very beginning, users are immersed in the mechanics of arranging shelves, pricing goods, and interacting with customers. You’re not just haphazardly putting products on display; careful planning is required to ensure that the right products are available at the right time. One of the key parts of the job is deciding which booster packs will be featured in the store, balancing between popular options and more obscure ones that may appeal to collectors or a niche audience.

You need to keep an eye on trends because the market is constantly changing and what is popular one day may lose its appeal the next. The ability to set prices allows for some flexibility, encouraging gamers to test their business acumen by adjusting offerings based on demand, supply, and competition. Another element of TCG Card Shop Simulator is hiring and personnel management. Employees are an important part of keeping a store running smoothly, but they also have their own needs and quirks.

You will have to balance between personality, skills and expenses. It’s not enough to just hire a bunch of people and put them to work; players must ensure that employees work in places where they can play to their strengths. Someone who is great at organizing inventory may not be the best at recommending cards to shoppers, and vice versa. Find that middle ground that can be the deciding factor.
