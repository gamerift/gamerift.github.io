Game Description#

Shapehero Factory combines the elements of the simulator of the factory, rogelitis and Tower Defense. Players build huts for the mass production of heroes who automatically fight with the waves of enemies. The goal is to defeat the great disaster sealed in the book of magic. With the help of minions, collect a variety of characters from basic figures found on a magic scroll. Circles and triangles form soldiers, and circles and squares - sprites. Adding squares to soldiers gives them shields, and combination opens even stronger opportunities. The production of heroes includes the transportation of figures and assembly of combinations. Meanwhile, enemy troops are trying to break through to the gate. For defense, you need to choose a Persian suitable for each enemy attack method.

After winning the battles, players receive awards from a random pool. These awards allow you to open new factories or modernize existing ones, which leads to the constant development of gameplay and the creation of unique buildings. In addition, each playable character has the ability to choose and create a hero, which contributes to multiple playing the game. The constant development of the character is available through the book of arcan knowledge, which uses experience points gained after each game to strengthen the Persians. Shapehero Factory offers a mixture of genres: strategic construction, customization and wave protection. Players are experimenting with combinations, optimizing the layout of factories and moving through the book of magic in order to eventually encounter a great disaster.
