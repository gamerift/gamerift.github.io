Game Description#

On the pirateJitsu Squad you can play for free online with a friend or online online in a team of up to four people to fight a variety of enemies and overcome many difficulties. The network game is represented by co-op with crazy and chaotic battles. The gameplay is a cartoonish beat-em-up, in which participants are required to apply unique abilities, use a variety of weapons and much more.

Jitsu Squad’s gameplay is inspired by old-school titles such as Streets of Rage and Final Fight. The mechanics are based on intense combat, allowing you to defeat enemies with several types of attacks, use special abilities, grab opponents or use the environment. Accumulated points can be spent on improving characters and upgrading weapons. The opportunity for free online play invites each participant to take control of a unique hero with his own set of skills and characteristics. Traveling through the galaxy, you need to stop an insane villain who wants to take over the entire universe. Content, mechanics and features: More than 500 chain combo attacks; Transformations and enhancements of heroes; Helper characters; Many enemies with unique attacks; Various levels and biomes; Improvement system and increasing the levels of protagonists; Team Tag mode; Mode rage, causing crushing damage to enemies and bosses; Parrying enemy attacks.
