Game Description#

NBA 2K21 is a basketball simulator with the ability to play online with a friend for free on a pirate, competing with each other or playing on the same team both locally and online. NBA 2021 provides many new game modes to choose from. Particular emphasis is placed on realism and plausible physics. The developers have significantly improved the graphics, and also placed great emphasis on detailed and smooth animations of athletes.

A single mode is available, network play is presented and the opportunity to train on a special site is available. In NBA 2K21, you can compete in classic MyTEAM modes or play through the cinematic MyCAREER story. In it you will take control of one basketball player and go with him throughout his entire career path, from amateur to professional.

An improved Neighborhood mode has been introduced, offering you to fight in duel matches and compete in teams of three or five people. Skills are honed in the Park menu, and Pro-Am allows you to team up with teammates to earn shared rewards and bonuses. You can dress your athlete in stylish clothes or change his appearance by going to the MyPLAYER menu. You can become not only a basketball icon, but also a trendsetter. The teams are presented as classic squads of famous clubs, so you can assemble your own star squad. Special MyTEAM cards include both stars of the past and modern basketball icons. Every player is capable of creating his own dream team!
