Game Description#

Automobilista 2 is a racing simulator designed to play online with a friend or compete with people from all over the planet online. Once behind the wheel of a racing car, you can try out many legendary cars that have ever participated in auto racing completely free of charge. The possibility of free online play allows you to race alone or with other players along numerous tracks at different times of the day. For complete immersion, you can use virtual reality helmets. Players will see a whole history of auto racing, spanning the classic eras of motorsports.

World famous brands such as Brands Hatch, Mount Panorama and Imola are represented. Both real-life machines and known prototypes are available. You can get behind the wheel of Grand Prix cars, GT cars and racing trucks. You will have to race along the tracks of 5 continents. A 24-hour cycle with atmospheric pressure simulation is presented, as well as dynamic changes in weather conditions. During the races, wind speed and temperature are taken into account. As you progress, you will unlock historic cars and additional race tracks. Key features of Automobilista 2: single playthrough and online play; realistic controls that take into account the characteristics of each vehicle; a huge variety of cars of different classes and brands; gameplay depending on weather conditions and physical factors; tracks on five continents around the world; opportunity play in VR mode; create your own championships and competitions; rating system for multiplayer races.
