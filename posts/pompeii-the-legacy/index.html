Game Description#

Pompeii: The Legacy sets the gamers the task of leading the Roman family and restore the city 20 years after the eruption of Vesuvius. The project requires to take into account the different needs of the patricians, plebeians and slaves, since each group has its own goals. Players from scratch lay streets, erects forums and build aqueducts. Any infrastructure or social decision affects the position of the family and determines its legacy for centuries, generating the consequences for future generations.

Strengthening the influence of the family in Pompeii: The Legacy depends on the chosen political and economic strategy. The gameplay allows you to form laws, agree on trade rights within the empire and conclude unions through contracts or marriage. The conquest of the emperor’s location and the ability to rejoice of rivals become the key to survival and growth. The economy of the settlement is kept in the construction of housing, temples and arenas, and establishing trade routes guarantees a stable influx of resources for the needs of citizens.

The settlement is threatened by both natural disasters and the human factor. Earthquakes, fires, hunger and epidemics arise suddenly, checking the city for strength. Constant danger is sabotage by political opponents, and this strategy offers optional battles in real time for direct command of the legions. Long -term progress is achieved through research in two main branches: technological and philosophical. These mechanics, together with family bonuses and imperial privileges, give advantages that determine the development of decades for decades.
