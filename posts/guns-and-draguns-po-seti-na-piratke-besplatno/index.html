Game Description#

Guns And Draguns can be played online with a friend, even on a pirated platform, and absolutely free. This is a dynamic shooter in the roguelite style, where online play becomes the epicenter of battles with fantastic enemies. On the Internet online you will not only have to cooperate with your comrades to win, but also compete for the title of the best player. You and your friend will seek upgrades and improve skills to succeed in this exciting adventure.

Steam’s remote co-op feature allows you to invite your friends for local co-op battles, even if they’re on the other side of the world. They don’t need to pay money or download the game - just send them an invite and you’re done. As you delve deeper into the dungeon, keep an eye out for elusive bonus rooms. They are full of chests that will enhance your abilities and give you the edge you need to survive.

Don’t get me wrong - you can’t just beat Rambo. Communication is key if you want to make it out alive. But if you’re feeling particularly brave (or antisocial), you can always go solo. With seven unique characters to choose from, you’ll have plenty of options on how to deal with those pesky dragoons.
