Game Description#

ZERO PROTOCOL throws you headlong into a chilling Antarctic research center. Beneath the ice lies a crashed alien spaceship, and you have to unravel its secrets. In this sci-fi thriller, you’ll not only have to fight your way through hordes of enemies, but also think and solve puzzles that will test your logic.

Don’t expect a linear path. ZERO PROTOCOL allows you to forge your own path, and every decision can lead you down a new rabbit hole. One wrong turn can lead to unforeseen consequences, but that’s where the fun lies. The story unfolds organically, rewarding exploration and keeping you guessing what’s lurking around the next corner.

Think about it: the research facility is a giant puzzle box, and you are a determined researcher trying to crack its code. Each puzzle solved and enemy defeated reveals another piece of the mystery, bringing you closer to understanding the truth about the alien technology and the facility’s secrets. So if you’re looking for an exciting adventure that will challenge your mind and keep you guessing, an adventure game might be just the game you’re looking for. Just don’t forget to bring thermal underwear - it gets chilly down there.
