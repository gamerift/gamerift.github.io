Game Description#

Worldwide Rush is immersed in the management of the global passenger corporation. The start of the career begins with the construction of the main office and the selection of equipment from an extensive park in 120 models, including cars, trains, planes and ships. The main goal of this gaming project is to connect thousands of settlements with profitable routes, based on dynamic demand. To increase profits, you can invest in the hotel business and advertising campaigns, while the entire logistics system requires constant improvement.

Competitors controlled by artificial intelligence are actively fighting for the market share, forcing to choose between partnerships, rivalry or complete absorption of their business. Each session at Worldwide Rush is unique due to the procedurally generated areas, the geographical features of the regions and random events that constantly change demand. A gamer can focus on creating a largest airline, developing a network of high -speed railways or establishing marine domination, which opens various strategies for global expansion.

High -quality satisfaction of customer needs contributes to the prosperity of cities and unlocking new destination around the world. Each passenger is an independent virtual agent who is looking for an optimal way of following through an affordable transport infrastructure. Gradually, the gameplay is scaled until the coordination of trips of tens of thousands of people in real time. The action of Title unfolds on a carefully recreated planet, allowing the player to build his logistics empire both in familiar locations and on distant continents.
