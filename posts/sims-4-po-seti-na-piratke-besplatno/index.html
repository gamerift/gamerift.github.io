Game Description#

Sims 4 is a life simulator that you can play for free online with a friend or build your life alone. Using an online Internet connection, you can build your family, make friends with your neighbors, and much more. The events take place in a small town, where each house is inhabited by unique Sims leading their own individual lifestyles. Before you start the game, you can create your own character or choose a ready-made one, with your own characteristics and character.

By interacting with other Sims, they develop friendships, enmities, or romantic attachments. Online play in The Sims 4 allows you to build a house together, start a family, or simply cooperate to freely develop your character. The Sims 4 is represented by such towns as Newcrest, Willow Creek and Oasis Springs. The possibility of a free online game invites participants to settle in one of the city districts to help each other or, conversely, to build unfriendly relationships. The character editor is represented by a huge number of hairstyles, accessories, clothes, etc. Character traits and goals in life are selected that influence the protagonist’s behavior and social activity.

Construction mode allows you to build your own house from scratch. There is a wide selection of building materials, furniture, decorative items, appliances and much more. Once the mansion is built, you can move your Sim into it. From this moment on, you need to replenish 6 scales of the hero’s needs. Each character has 15 emotional states. You need to ensure that your Sim eats, rests, sleeps, goes to the toilet, goes to work, etc. on time. For your hero, you can set three wishes that will need to be fulfilled during the game. With the help of mini-games, the hero’s skills are developed: artistic talent, musical abilities, a penchant for sports, business skills and much more.
