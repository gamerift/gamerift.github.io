Game Description#

On the pirateWargame Red Dragon you can play with a friend online for free, fighting in co-op or against other players. On the Internet online you can unite into teams to confront enemy squads. Network play allows you to collect resources, develop new technologies and destroy your opponent’s bases. The gameplay is aimed at spectacular and dynamic military battles.

The possibility of free online play allows you to take control of one of several countries, be it Japan, China, North Korea, South Korea, Australia and New Zealand. Each faction has its own unique set of units and fights using individual equipment. It will be necessary to develop new weapons and technologies. In total, more than 17 countries are taking part in the military confrontation. All powers pursue their own personal goals.

You can team up with other leaders to repel enemies together. The gameplay allows you to create alliances, engage in trade, etc. Landscapes and interactive environments play a huge role in the gameplay. Features of locations must be used for your own purposes. Players can control not only infantry units, but also a wide variety of equipment: tanks, planes, helicopters, combat vessels, etc. The gameplay places a huge emphasis on realism and heavy physics. Multiplayer mode brings together 20 players who can compete on the same map.
