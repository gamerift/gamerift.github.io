Game Description#

From the Depths allows you to accept command of battleships, aircraft, submarines, space ships, balloons and many other modes of transport. The story campaign will visit more than a hundred lands and fight against 8 fractions. Misses consist in planning competent tactics and protecting your own fleet in a series of difficult tests. In addition to the main campaign, there is an adventure mode where it will be proposed to start the game alone on a small ship and grow to a whole fleet, furrows the expanses of the universe.

“From the depths” includes:

design of your own fleet and fortress; more than 1000 components for construction; over 38 elements for creating missiles, torpedoes, underwater charges and bombs; The ability to install guns, lasers, jet engines, control computers, repair robots, air pumps, automatic control units and much more.

Wide tools allows you to create anything, right up to fighting robots. It all depends on tools, imagination and the correct design. The fact is that from the Depths rests on the physical properties of materials. For example, when you build a ship, it is necessary to adjust the center of gravity and displacement: if the tree is installed under the bottom of the ship, then the dish is pushed out, while the metal settles. The developers introduced realistic physics, where each damaged block affects the effectiveness of the shuttle and its behavior. The multiplayer mode will allow you to play both in the cooperative and fight against each other. The card editor makes it possible to create its own unique and unique missions, campaigns and even planets.
