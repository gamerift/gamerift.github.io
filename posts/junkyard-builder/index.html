Game Description#

Junkyard Builder puts gamers in charge of an abandoned junkyard full of potential. The key is to transform unwanted items into valuable resources with your own hands. You will have to travel to different locations and inspect the area in search of materials suitable for restoration and sometimes unique finds. The process revolves around the restoration, systematic waste disposal and day-to-day management required to operate a waste sorting facility to eventually build a large scrap metal business.

In the Junkyard Builder project, discovered items are recycled for profit. The proceeds go to improve the character’s skills and purchase various equipment necessary for development. Over time, new tools become available, which helps improve workflow and increase productivity on site. Key tasks include processing collected scrap to increase its price on the market, as well as meticulously searching mountains of waste for special hidden things, such as money or gold bars, which directly affects progress in the game.

The gameplay involves direct control of special equipment needed to work with various materials found on the territory. You’ll have to learn how to handle machinery, from tower cranes for heavy loads to industrial presses capable of flattening entire machines, which is an important part of this simulator. Repairing things, such as old cars or broken furniture, bringing them back from disrepair is another accessible mechanic that allows you to turn junk into something valuable.
