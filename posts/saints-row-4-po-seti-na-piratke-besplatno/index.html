Game Description#

Saints Row 4 is a crazy third-person action game that allows you to play online with a friend for free as a pirate. Online multiplayer competitions are available. The action takes place in a simulation of Steelport. The saints reach a new level, becoming leaders of the state, and the protagonist sits in the chair of the president. However, the Earth is attacked by aliens led by the evil Zinyak. The antagonist puts the hero in cyberspace and now he needs to find a way to defeat the villain by breaking out into reality. The city is a retrofuturistic dystopia. The metropolis contains elements typical of Washington.

The main difference from previous games lies in the fact that superpowers are given out. The free online game allows you to use different abilities: run at great speed, jump high, parkour, rush vertically over buildings and even fly. Vehicles in Saints Row 4 are still available for theft, but they act as an auxiliary means of transportation after the discovery of levitation. Network play allows you to control cars, motorcycles, trucks, tanks, etc. You can also steal alien vehicles, including their warships and levitating scooters.

Many characters from previous games have returned: Kinzie Kensington, Shaundi, Oleg, Pierce, Benjamin King, Matt Miller and Johnny Gat. The arsenal of weapons has been replenished with additional guns, melee weapons and special barrels, which are comic types of devices. Among them are the gravity gun or the dubstep gun. Tasks are divided into main and secondary. They can be activated using your phone or by clicking on the corresponding icon on the map. You can romance all your partners if you follow their instructions.
