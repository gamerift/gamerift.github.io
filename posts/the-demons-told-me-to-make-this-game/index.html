Game Description#

The Demons Told Me to Make This Game begins in the city of Red Havyvs at 5:00 a.m. on October 26, 2016. This moment marks the beginning of the last day of the world: the bloody ritual ends and a spirit named a dark light appears in the void. Being this incorporeal essence, the player cannot directly interact with the physical world. The main way of influence is the instilling in the inhabitants of the city and whipping them in the ear of instructions for sending their actions.

In The Demons Told Me to Make This Game, the gamer will move along a repeating time loop to solve secrets. People that you obsessed act at their own discretion, and their decisions often lead to death. At the disposal of the user there is control over the course of time - a mechanism for the abolition of fatal errors and experiments with various outcomes in nonlinear plot lines. The narrative in this game requires a solution to a series of such cycles, divided into certain temporary segments, to send characters who are destined to save the world from ancient gods and other monsters.

Survival of wards is a complex process built on manipulations. The creation of a successful temporary line involves observing events from different angles and conducting numerous, sometimes cruel, experiments. You will have to force the obsessed to commit disgusting deeds, which are contrary to their nature, and observe their repeated death, extracting new knowledge from each case. The impact on these personalities includes the use of their deepest fears or the use of a mini-game to capture their mind. While you are sending an exorcist squad against urban darkness, your own motives remain an open part of the story.
