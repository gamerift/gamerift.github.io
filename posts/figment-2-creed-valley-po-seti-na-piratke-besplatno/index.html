Game Description#

Figment 2: Creed Valley is a cooperative arcade game that you can play online with a friend or on a pirate for free, cooperate with other players to explore the world of the subconscious of the human mind. Network play allows you to play through the story locally or online online. The action takes place in a universe of nightmares. The main character is a character named Dusty, who has to gain courage in the world of the Mind. Quests are presented in the form of musical puzzles or spatial puzzles. For example, you will need to fight a boss to the rhythm of a dance. Free online play in Figment 2: Creed Valley allows a partner to take control of Piper’s assistant and explore the Valley of Faith together. The moral compass has broken and now the world of the mind is engulfed in terrible nightmares.

There are many vibrant and surreal locations to explore. The gameplay is presented in classic isometry. On the path of the heroes, there are many charismatic characters from whom you can take on secondary quests. The protagonists fight in close combat with a sword. Abilities and weapons are improved. You can roll and parry enemy attacks. You will need to fight with a variety of enemies. Boss fights take place in arenas. Each leader will need to find individual battle tactics, using the interactive environment for their own purposes.
