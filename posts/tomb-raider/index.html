Game Description#

Tomb Raider is a restart of the series telling about the adventures of Lara Croft. The ship on which the girl’s crew was sailing was wrecked. On the island she is captured and left tied in a cave. The adventurer frees herself from her bonds and goes to look for the crew members.

The player can choose the difficulty, which affects the course of battles. A vast world opens up before the gamer, divided into several locations. Artifacts, notes and other useful things are hidden in each place. Special achievements are awarded for exploring tombs and discovering them. Parkour will help you get to hard-to-reach places - the heroine climbs walls, climbs ropes, and glides with a parachute. Such tricks are not always successful. Danger lurks at every step - the heroine can fall into the abyss, fall into a bear trap, or run into a pack of wolves.

In Tomb Raider there are 2 ways to eliminate an enemy - direct combat or stealth assassination. As the game progresses, Lara’s arsenal includes a bow, a pistol, a machine gun, a shotgun, and a grenade launcher. Even an ice ax is suitable for a fight. Weapons are upgraded using spare parts from boxes scattered throughout the island. After upgrading, the firing range improves and reloading speeds up.

Along the way there are camps that serve as save points. Here you can relax, watch story videos and distribute skill points. As you progress, the heroine increases her level, gaining experience for killings, found things and completing missions. For a new level, 1 skill point is given, which upgrades one of 3 skill branches: hunter, warrior and survival master.
