Game Description#

Railway Empire 2 is a transport strategy that offers to go into the past, at the beginning of the 19th century, and lead a small railway company, which will become the leader of the economy thanks to steam cars. Here you have to compete with competitors and build a network of railways, which will connect cities and companies throughout the continent. At your disposal 60 famous steam machines that pull freight and passenger cars, opening the historically significant era of the time of the industrial revolution.

Game cards in this sequel have become larger, more detailed and brighter than ever. You can travel throughout the United States and continental Europe on two huge maps, and pay attention to more detailed cards to specific regions. An improved laying of ways and new functions will help you make important decisions and promote the development of the economy and the growth of cities. Semafors are automatically installed, bridges withstand up to 4 paths, and expanded railway stations are accommodated up to 8 paths. Choose a manager for your railway company from 6 characters, each of which has its own strengths and weaknesses. Pass 5 campaigns or any of the 14 different scenarios. You can set your level of complexity in a fully customizable free mode or build an ideal railway network in construction mode. Fight with the competitors of your railway empire with friends in a cooperative network game. And you can also sit in one of your trains, get comfortable and enjoy the beautiful types of landscapes while the iron horse rushes along the rails.
