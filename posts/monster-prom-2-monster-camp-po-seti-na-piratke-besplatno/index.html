Game Description#

On the pirateMonster Prom 2 Monster Camp you can play online with a friend, trying to pick up charming heroines or charismatic handsome men for free. By joining forces online, you need to win the favor of 6 girls. Each has its own unique character traits and preferences. The online game is designed for both interaction and confrontation between participants. Who will win the heart of the sultry zombie beauty?

The game features sarcastic humor and immerses you in a real romantic atmosphere. The events of Monster Prom 2 Monster Camp take place in a summer camp. Free online play allows characters such as Amira, Oz, Vicky and Brian to play. Some heroines from the last part have returned, including Damien, Calculester, Milo, Dahlia, Joy and Aravi. It is noteworthy that all the characters are monsters and other mystical creatures.

Joy, for example, is a witch, Damien is a red demon, etc. However, all the characters are very funny, charismatic and memorable. It is necessary to seek a special approach to each participant in the monster camp. You need to study their character traits and try to find the key to the hearts of monsters, because even mystical creatures have feelings. The sequel to the adventure expands the capabilities of the original game and offers a lot of new content to enjoy.
