Game Description#

Delta Force: Xtreme 2 is a tactical first-person shooter that you can play online with a friend for free on a pirate site. Online confrontation missions and cooperative tasks aimed at playing together are available. At the center of the story are the soldiers of the Delta special forces. The online game allows you to go through many military operations in Laos and Uzbekistan.

2 campaigns presented. Each has 10 unique missions. The Uzbek scenario proposes to destroy terrorists who threaten to use chemical weapons. In Laos, you will need to fight a smuggler cartel. The possibility of free online play allows you to play both scenarios with a partner. You can explore the open world and move freely through cities. All missions in Delta Force: Xtreme 2 present the player with a number of different tasks. Complete freedom of action is provided. Everyone chooses for themselves how they want to achieve their goal.

Before starting the mission, you can listen to the briefing, study the map, and choose equipment and weapons. It is also allowed to use easel weapons and vehicles. The gameplay is aimed at realism and survival. Enemies, as well as the main characters, die from several bullets. It is necessary to develop tactical skills and approach each battle wisely, because any wound can be fatal. The extent of the injury depends on where the bullet hit. There are 3 game modes available: single, cooperative and multiplayer.
