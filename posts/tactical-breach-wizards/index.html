Game Description#

Tactical Breach Wizards is a tactical role-playing adventure with an isometric camera that allows you to better assess the situation in the location to make the most successful decisions. The user will be transported to an alternative universe, where a distant futuristic future has arrived. The gamer takes control of a group of soldiers who have magical abilities that allow them to be combined with shots from firearms.

The single-player campaign is a set of tasks to clear laboratory rooms where illegal experiments take place. During the course of the story, the player will become acquainted with a large number of diverse personalities who are present to advance the storyline, which will take about 8 hours of game time to fully complete all available tasks and other content.

Defeated enemies, such as guards, scientists and their subjects, can drop valuable loot in the form of rare equipment that you can equip on your own virtual protégés to improve their performance. There is also an ability upgrade. The gamer independently chooses which skills to use on this or that character, gradually selecting the most effective combinations to destroy the enemy in turn-based combat, which gives time for reflection.
