Game Description#

endless alice - this is an adventure from a third person with an intriguing combination of elements of the Rowlaik and visual design, inspired by anime. Title invites gamers to explore a huge, constantly changing world, where each solution can determine their path. Six characters to choose from, each of which offers its own set of abilities and styles of battle, allow users to combine them in accordance with their style. The world is filled with monsters and large -scale bosses, which turns every meeting into a test of skill and strategy.

As you pass, gamers will face new trials that make them be alert with every step. For those who want to get an additional portion of thrills, the project also offers the opportunity to play alone or unite with friends in cooperative mode. Up to four users can combine efforts to cope with the most difficult opponents, sharing the test and joy of victory with them.

The cooperative regime allows you to realize more dynamic strategies and create an atmosphere of partnership, since gamers combine their unique abilities to overcome complex obstacles. Playing alone or with friends, Endless Alice offers a flexible experience taking into account various preferences and styles. The world is a constant adventure in which there are no two identical travels. Thanks to the procedurally created environment, each race seems fresh, providing users with endless opportunities and surprises.
