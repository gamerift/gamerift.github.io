Game Description#

Shank 2 allows you to fight against computer opponents together with a friend online. You can play this pirate game online with a friend. There is also content for fans of single play. There is a story campaign of 7 missions.

Like its predecessor, Shank 2 belongs to the Beat ‘em up and Action genres. The possibility of free online play in this project is designed for two people. The sequel also uses hand-drawn graphics and a side view. The protagonist can cut enemies with a chainsaw and machete, throw Molotov cocktails and grenades at them, fire with a shotgun and pistols. In the arsenal there was still a place for a sledgehammer, throwing knives, mines and sharpening. Hand-to-hand combat is also present. You can also pick up objects from the ground and hit your opponents with them. But such things break quickly. But the portable turret from the original was not included in the sequel. There are boss battles. It is necessary to look for vulnerable areas among the leaders. After a fight, you can restore your health if you find beer in the level.

The hero learned to intercept the attacks of bandits and finish off his enemies. You can take away weapons from still living adversaries and immediately use them. They also allow you to use elements of the environment as methods of murder. For example: pushing containers onto people, throwing them into traps, exploding nearby barrels. The online game now has a survival mode. In this arcade game, users are asked to deal with the advancing waves of opponents. In the second part, the achievement system was slightly changed. Now achievements are called medals.
