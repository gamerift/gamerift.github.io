Game Description#

Warhammer 40,000: Space Marine - Master Crafted Edition puts the player in the center of large -scale conflict, where battles and constant pressure do not leave time for respite. Captain Titus from the Order of Ultramarinov heads a detachment of hardened fighters sent to restrain the invasion of the orcs on an industrial planet. These worlds produce equipment and weapons for the endless war of mankind, and the loss of one of them can lead to catastrophic consequences.

In Warhammer 40,000: Space Marine - Master Crafted Edition battles are being built on a continuous change of distance battle and near massacre. The arsenal of the protagonist includes both firearms and a chain sword, which allows you to instantly change tactics depending on the situation. The orcs come in number, break through the fortifications, turning the defense into a massacre. Moving deep into enemy positions, the title faces signs of the presence of another, even more sinister power hiding in the shadow.

In addition to the plot campaign, the project offers a network mode, where space marines fight against the ages of chaos. Eight -way matches allow you to choose classes with a different role on the battlefield. In the process of matches, gamers gain experience and open access to the new equipment and the appearance of the fighters. The function of the cross -platform game has been implemented, which expands the circle of participants in matches. This publication includes the entire previously released additional content, adding more opportunities for configuration and additional missions. The action is focused on an attack without stopping: the pace does not decrease, and each episode requires immediate action. This gameplay leaves a minimum of retreat or expectation.
