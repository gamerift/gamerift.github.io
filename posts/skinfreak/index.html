Game Description#

Skinfreak is the story of a creepy night during which a serial killer known as Skinfreak goes on the hunt for his next victim. His terrifying goal is to add human skin to his collection, and this time his gaze falls on Belle, an employee of one of the city’s companies. A girl finds herself in a deadly trap when a mundane work task turns into a desperate fight for survival. She will have to escape the rotting embrace of the maniac, so as not to become another exhibit in his terrible collection.

All the horror unfolds within the walls of a lonely office building located on the outskirts of the city. Late in the evening, Belle’s boss calls her to work to install new software, which at first does not foretell any danger. The silence of the night and routine activities such as making coffee or checking colleagues' emails create a deceptive sense of calm that is soon destroyed. Suddenly, for the heroine, this place becomes an arena for a deadly game of hide and seek, where the stake is her own life.

The Skinfreak project invites users to immerse themselves in an atmosphere inspired by classic slasher films of the 1980s with elements of Italian giallo films. This interactive B-movie experience transports gamers back to the era of horror with a distinctive aesthetic and intense suspense. The visual design mimics the graphics from the PS1 era, adding to the sense of nostalgic dread. Belle faces only one task: survive the bloody night of terror and find a way to escape from a ruthless pursuer who sees in her only material for his next monstrous creation.
