Game Description#

Contraactville beckoning lovers of simulators with their history about how to turn from rags into rich. Forget about gorgeous houses, you start with a penny in a port town. But do not be afraid, starting a tycoon! In Contractville you can build your own fate yourself by demolition, construction and interior design. And what is interesting: you can sell your architectural masterpieces at the auction, turning your sweat and ingenuity into cold, firm money. But this is not a single game.

You can create a company along with three friends by forming a formidable team that will engage in contracts, manage resources and overcome economic turnings of the game. Consider that this is a virtual business incubator is an ideal place for bearing entrepreneurial dreams with friends. Of course, the road to wealth is not paved with sparkles. The project sets you tasks on strategic planning and resource management, forcing you to be on the alert.

The world imitates real scenarios, so get ready to use your inner architect and business gurus in order to overcome obstacles. Beauty lies in freedom - Adventure gives you the opportunity to pave your own course within the framework of an economic ecosystem. So, if you are not tolerant of revealing your creative potential, building an empire with friends and checking your business grip, the Title can become your new virtual platform.
