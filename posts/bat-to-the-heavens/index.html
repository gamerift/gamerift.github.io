Game Description#

Bat to the Heavens offers gamers to accept a unique set of calls. At first glance, it may seem that this is just another platformer, but his mechanic quickly emits him among other games of the genre. Instead of relying on running or jumping, gamers must use the bat as the main tool to move in confusing levels full of obstacles. This is not a typical game where you simply move from point A to point B. Complex and time are important here. Each site requires users to master the movement system using the impulse and thorough control to perform complex maneuvers.

Bat to the heavens is based on the use of a bit that serves both the tool and the means of movement. Players waving a bat to gain height, slip through narrow places or jump over large abysses. Bit, despite its simple concept, opens up a whole world of possibilities. Each level has been carefully worked out to push gamers to find the best way to get from one side to the other.

It’s not always a matter of speed, sometimes you need to spend time to understand the surrounding environment and find the best way to use the Bita movement. At first, the system may seem confusing, but as soon as you begin to understand how the bat interacts with the environment, the tasks begin to seem more likely puzzles that need to be solved than the tasks that need to be completed. One of the determining features is the pace at which it challenges gamers.
