Game Description#

Phantom Squad is a cooperative shooter with a view of 1-4 players, combining tactics in real time and battles with high rates. Before the collision, groups must formulate the assault plan, using gaming tools for marking implementation routes, tasks and entry points during the operation. Constant voice communication serves as the main element of the coordination of maneuvers and the reaction to the change of operational situation.

Gamers create a unit for the execution of these cruel tasks in which each solution directly affects survival. Opponents act assertively, forcing detachments to move meaningfully and adapt their strategies to neutralize enemy actions in order to avoid imminent failure. In combat conditions, Phantom Squad puts gamers in the face of deadly shootings, in which accuracy becomes the only criterion for success. The only crazy bullet is capable of eliminating an ally due to constantly active fire on its own, requiring each fighter of iron discipline.

The gameplay is built on the principle, according to which one accurate shot determines the result of the entire task, but there will be no second chance. In addition to firearms, survival depends on the use of more than 20 special devices created for tactical superiority over the enemy. The arsenal includes reconnaissance devices, neutralization tools, palpitations for tracking targets and a breakthrough charge. Operatives use this equipment to take control of various locations, including urban buildings, jungle complexes and Arctic laboratories.
