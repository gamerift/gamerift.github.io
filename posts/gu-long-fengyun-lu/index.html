Game Description#

GU LONG FENGYUN LU is a massive martial arts RPG based on the classic works of Gu Long, the famous Chinese writer. The plot tells about the turbulent times of the end of the Ming Dynasty, when various heroes and factions fought for power and glory. The main character is Feng Yun, a young martial artist traveling across the country, encountering friends, enemies, love, hate, loyalty and betrayal. Along the way, he learns the secrets of his mysterious past and his destiny to change the destiny of the nation.

The game recreates the style and atmosphere of the writer’s novels, which are known for complex and intriguing plots, distinctive characters and elegant handwriting. The gameplay is presented with an isometric camera. Gameplay elements take inspiration from classic party-based RPG adventures. The main character and his companions have unique abilities and skills that can be combined to create special chains of attacks. There are main and side quests, and the world can be freely explored.

The project also includes some original elements and innovations that enrich the gameplay and plot. Players will explore a diverse and exciting world of martial arts, experience over a million lines of dialogue, meet various heroes, choose a companion and upgrade them through intense battles, and finally unravel the mystery of their past. The game contains elements of adventure, role-playing and strategy gameplay, and also has many plot twists.
