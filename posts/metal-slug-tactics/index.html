Game Description#

TheMetal Slug Tactics player chooses his warriors to create a suitable powerful team capable of stopping the hostilities. Run n' Gun has roguelike elements as well as intense combat. The continuation of the series of the same name allows you to look at the war from a new angle with a greater share of adrenaline, gaining experience after each fight, which helps to unlock additional skills or weapons. The game was the first tactical RPG, unlike its predecessors, and features replayability, pixel graphics and complex replay mechanics.

Donald Morden returns after a long absence, because the guy staged a coup and was hunted by the world government. Now the protagonist has assembled a powerful army, which he has been building for many years, and has the opportunity to take revenge on his offenders. Now the battle group of Falcons-Peregrines, acting together, breaks through the enemy’s defense lines to destroy the leader of Morden before he declares a worldwide war.

The user needs to create the perfect team of fighters capable of fighting with maximum efficiency. To this end, during the battle, soldiers must carry out thoughtful attacks on the enemy, which can cause enormous damage. The ability to call for reinforcements, for example, heavy artillery and air support, is also implemented here. To complete the mission, the player will have to overcome a lot of obstacles, during which his charges will often die, but with the restoration of the skills acquired in the process, which allows them to become stronger each time.
