Game Description#

Undisputed is a sports simulator about boxing, where a special emphasis is placed on realism. Having created his own fighter, the player will have to go from amateur matches to the professional stage. The main goal will be to obtain the eWBC world title. Achieving this title will require constant honing of your skills, learning new techniques and choosing the right strategy. You can carefully choose your approach to each enemy and not take too many risks, or you can go for the trouble and show your full potential in every battle. It is worth noting that a certain part of success will depend not only on behavior in the ring, but also on decisions made during breaks.

Over time, combat in Undisputed will require a more serious approach, which means you will need to hire a trainer, boat, nutritionist and manager. There are several gyms available for training, each of which allows you to study unique abilities. The fighter’s condition will largely depend on the intensity of the fights and training. After a difficult encounter, the hero can be left to rest and heal his wounds, or he can go into confrontation again, thereby showing the fortitude of a true warrior. The feint system is extremely diverse, which is why each battle is unique. All movements were clearly copied from real boxing, which, in combination with modern graphics, gives complete immersion in what is happening.
