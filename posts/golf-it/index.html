Game Description#

The simulator of a real golf - Golf it! - It is a sports, but ridiculous brainchild of the studio of developers who want to turn American sport into a kind of fantastic game. The player’s career begins with simple levels, where to calculate the trajectory and the impact of the impact is easier, but gradually we will approach the complex levels at which even pushing the ball will be difficult. On the way we will meet teleportation, huge towers, as well as interesting locations made based on several countries.

The player will have to visit 5 cards, where 18 levels are logically thought out. In the mine, we will drive the ball along the slopes and railway tracks, trying to collect the maximum number of bonuses. Everything is easier on the pasture, but remember about the weather features. The pirate bay will delight the players with a large number of different units, because of which the movement of the ball will be not only beautiful, but also long. There are simple levels in winter. Japan will seem difficult for you, but this is at first glance.

In solitary mode, you have to fight only with defeat, and in the multiplayer game mode, you will resist the professionalism of your rivals, who skillfully operate with the ball, club and location features. There is also a card editor in the game. You can create your own location, place the hole so that only you can get into it. Create your card, learn to control the club and drive all the balls into the hole. Do not forget that victory is always closer than you think.
