Game Description#

InANIMAL WELL players must explore a vast labyrinth created in pixel art. Players will interact with the interactive environment, pick up objects and combine them with each other, solve spatial puzzles, overcome landscape obstacles and much more. The character has to move around two-dimensional locations with a side view, periodically encountering dangerous monsters. It is necessary to illuminate dark areas on the map by lighting special candles.

In the process of passing ANIMAL WELL, users have to explore the world, which consists of a large number of intricate riddles and labyrinths. You have to make your way through various corridors and rooms, lighting candles and illuminating the area in order to open passages to new areas of the map. You will have to make your way through aggressive biomes, in which there are wild animals and other opponents that will attack the main character.

Come out to enjoy the non-linear exploration of the universe and levels, which represent zones divided into sectors on a large map. It is necessary to survive and confront dangerous enemies, using any available means. It will be possible to escape from some creatures using the environment or objects that act as traps and can detain the animal. Users need to carefully examine the locations to find all the quest items to solve the riddles.
