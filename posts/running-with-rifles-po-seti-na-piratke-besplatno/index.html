Game Description#

Running With Rifles is a tactical shooter that allows you to compete online for free on a pirate or play online with a friend, fighting in co-op mode against a common enemy. Network play supports cross-platform. The gameplay is an isometric action game with RPG elements. Each participant plays the role of an ordinary soldier, carrying out orders to destroy enemy troops or defending their lands from terrorists.

Free online play allows you to take on a variety of roles and tactics. For example, advance the front line or go deep into the rear, committing sabotage or stealing important resources. A huge amount of different equipment and weapons are available. As you gain experience as a commander, you can have more soldiers at your disposal. Features of Running With Rifles: hundreds of interactive maps for exploration and battles, represented by snow-covered valleys and cities; smart AI that analyzes actions; a cover system and the ability to interact with the environment; a rich arsenal of weapons, equipment and equipment; various tasks: destruction of radio towers , vehicle theft, hostage rescue, etc.; more than 40 participants on the server; different modes: cooperative, PvP, PvPvE, etc.

Units are represented by artillery, paratroopers, ground troops, etc. When leading a personal squad, you need to use warriors to control ships, tanks and a wide variety of armored vehicles. However, no one forbids you from becoming a lone warrior - an expert in sabotage and a master of secret penetration.
