Game Description#

Touch Type Tale - Strategic Typing immerses users in battles between armies that take place during the Dark Middle Ages. The game is made in the genre of real-time strategy, where you have to deal with printing and solving multi-task missions. The protagonist is a guy named Paul who wants to prevail over the evil forces. Gameplay mechanics allow you to test the typing speed of users, where the ability to type the necessary keys blindly is useful in order to keep control over the events that are happening.

The Touch Type Tale project creates unique challenges for hardcore RTS fans, where they will have to rely only on keyboard interactions, changing the usual gameplay for strategy games. Users will be able to go through a single-player campaign, completing progressively more difficult levels, or go to competitions in multiplayer mode, competing against other players. Gamers will have to fight for the right to the throne while watching the movement of troops on an isometric map. When participating in tactical battles, players need to: Learn unusual mechanics, where you have to control the situation solely using the keyboard. Develop maneuvers and create unique tactics during missions. Defend the lands of your own kingdom, where various buildings and troops will be located. Manage soldiers, creating detachments of infantry or riflemen.
