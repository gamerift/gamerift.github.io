Game Description#

Dusk-12 takes users to an epidemic-ridden town with a post-Soviet flavor, located somewhere in the Russian outback. This first-person shooter can be played either alone or online with a friend (including on a pirate). Multiplayer is only available online online. It offers old school gameplay.

The project has not only the possibility of free online play, but also two single-player campaigns. They let you play as two characters. Each has its own storyline. In the Russian translation, the title is called “Territory of Darkness”. There are no auto-seeds here, so you have to save manually. There are no hints either. Here you can run around locations on foot and sometimes use military equipment. There are several types of difficulty. Boss battles are also available.

The choice of the protagonist affects not only the development of the story, but also the gameplay. If Artem is chosen, the product will resemble a classic linear shooter. And Gorin brings variety to what is happening. He has 4 abilities: vision in the dark, acceleration and deceleration of time, striking using telekinesis. In addition, there is online play, which adds a little replay value. The plot tells about the tragedy in Chernogorsk-12. 4 years ago, a disaster occurred in this locality. After this, the settlement was quarantined, and it lost contact with the world. Since then, only mutants can be found here on the empty streets. These monsters themselves are quite tenacious. They can withstand many bullet wounds if you don’t aim for the head.
