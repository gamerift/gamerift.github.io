Game Description#

Terra Invicta is a cosmic 4x strategy, the events of which develop in the distant future. Once, alien forces invaded our galaxy, dividing humanity into 7 factions. Each union saw its development of the future. You have to lead one of the seven ideologies to impose it to the whole world. Explore the solar system, expand the territories and fight enemies in tactical fights.

The aliens invaded the far corner of the Kuiper ice belt. The peoples of the Earth not only could not unite, but also broke up for ideological reasons. People without knowing the true motives of the invaders form separate fractions, where everyone pursues their goals: to sow fear, to instill hope, to be guided by profit, etc. Associations at Terra Invicta:

The Resistance - defensive detachments that protect the planet; Humanity First - an offensive union, eager to kill all enemies; The Servants - worship alien forces; The Protectrate - agreed on surrender; The Academy - make a proposal to create an interstellar alliance; The Initiative - seeking benefits in the current chaos; Project Exodus - builders of the shuttle, with the help of which they want to leave the system.

For research, a galaxy with more than 300 asteroids and planets is available. Your main task is to do geopolitics. Cut the war with both the representatives of your civilization and the guests from the neighboring system. Each side has politicians, scientists and warriors. Improve the abilities of all heroes, gaining access to new technologies.

Develop space launchers, as they have a very important strategic purpose. All ships are based on realistic drawings of popular science fiction writers. You can also create your own shuttles by installing modules, weapons, drives, radiators, etc. The gameplay is fully built on the simulation of Newton’s physics, where concepts such as the power of attraction, impulse and maneuver, etc. are observed.
