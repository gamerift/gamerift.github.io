Game Description#

Pixelorama is a universal tool designed for pixel art lovers, regardless of whether you create simple sprites, intricate animations or experiment with more complex designs. Possessing an understandable interface, the program offers many functions that simplify the creative process. Users can easily configure the tools in accordance with their preferences by adjusting the mouse buttons for quick access. Regardless of whether you are a newcomer in pixel art or an experienced creator, the program supports many work processes, from basic design to creating complex animation.

The platform supports animation, allowing the authors to work with personnel, layers and onion skinning. The possibility of reproducing animation during drawing helps to optimize the process, providing more smooth transitions between personnel. Pixelorama also has a pixel perfection mode that allows you to finalize the design, providing the exact location of each pixel. For those who work on large projects, the program includes such useful tools as marks and various effects of image manipulation, allowing you to easily create detailed and complex images.

Palette management is another distinguishing feature that offers ready -made color schemes, as well as the possibility of importing user palette. It doesn’t matter if you prefer to adhere to classic colors or experiment with new ones - flexibility in this issue is provided. In the process, the program automatically retains progress, which can become a salvation in case of unforeseen circumstances.
