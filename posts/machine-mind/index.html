Game Description#

Machine Mind places the player in the role of consciousness transferred to a mechanical shell after the crash of a spaceship. The existence of a gamer is limited by the world, a changed planetary virus and subsequent nuclear conflict - the cataclysm, which forced the remnants of civilization to hide at the orbital station. Managing a series of rovers, it is necessary to provide its survival on the surface of the planet, now inhabited by aggressive raiders.

The main task is to restore communication with the survivors in the orbit and disclosure of all the circumstances standing behind the global event. The gameplay on the Machine Mind is built around the construction and strengthening of the operating base. It will be necessary to collect materials from a hostile environment for craft weapons, equipment and various modules. These components are then used to design and changes all -terrain vehicles, setting them up for various functions, from the extraction of valuable fossils to direct combat.

In addition, you can assign transport machines for automation of processes, such as the construction of facilities, the collection of resources and defensive patrols, which allows you to focus on strategic planning. This action proposes to explore vast wastelands and abandoned industrial complexes. The project contains clashes with enemy fractions and powerful bosses requiring the use of created weapons and combat modifications for equipment. Successful passage also depends on overcoming dangerous natural phenomena, including radiation zones and sand storms affecting operations.
