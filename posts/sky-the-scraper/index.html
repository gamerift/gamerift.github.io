Game Description#

Sky The Scraper tells about the history of a person named Sky, working as a rower of windows and pursuing personal ambitions. This game combines the action with movement on cables and the responsibilities of managing everyday life. It is necessary to go through a two -month period of hard work and care for yourself in order to influence the future. Its path forward is determined by overcoming the difficulties presented by his occupation and circumstances. This period of his life will determine the further direction.

The main gameplay is to use cables and skills for accurate cleaning of skyscrapers. During these tasks, a careless step can lead to a deadly fall, and the dangers of the environment, such as birds and lightning blows, create constant interference. In addition to physical risks in Sky The Scraper, the player also has to manage a limited budget. The income covers the needs such as taxes, rent and medical accounts.

Overwork directly affects the character of the character, requiring periods of rest and entertainment to maintain physical condition. The character improves his cleaning tools and acquires new abilities through the Skill Synapse skills tree. This system helps to create a set of skills aimed at improving the efficiency of work. The plot develops in 5 different scenarios representing unique characters and the situation. Interaction with people and overcoming trials in these situations directs a certain outcome. Gamer elections directly form the future of the protagonist.
