Game Description#

InDark Souls Prepare to Die Edition you can play online for free as a pirate, team up with a friend, or invade other people’s worlds using an online connection. The role-playing adventure allows you to go into a world where you have to collect Dark Souls, fighting various monsters and incredibly strong enemies. Each enemy poses a serious threat and requires an individual approach. Events take place in the kingdom of Lordan. The hero needs to light the sacred fire to restore order in the world.

Using the opportunity of free online play, you can call on another player for help so that together you can destroy a powerful boss. All leaders are very dangerous and have individual attack patterns. Multiplayer in Dark Souls Prepare to Die Edition is quite unusual. Anyone can invade someone else’s world and kill the player. It is also possible to leave messages and messages that can warn of danger or give a hint about the hiding place.

The combat system consists of melee and ranged attacks. You can choose from straight or curved swords, daggers, battle axes, spears and halberds. Ranged attacks are reserved for magic, bow and crossbow. The main elements in fights are rolls, blocks and parries. Humanity is an important component of mechanics. After death, the hero turns into a hollow and begins to lose strength with each new death. To regain humanity again, you need to use a special artifact. During the passage you will meet such characters as Gwyn, the witch of Izalith, Nito and others.
