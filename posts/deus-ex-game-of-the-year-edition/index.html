Game Description#

Deus Ex: Game of the Year Edition is a stealth action game that tells about the world of 2052, when corporations have completely seized power over society, and augmentations have become an integral part of human life. The world is experiencing an economic crisis, various terrorist organizations have become active, and people are suffering from a pandemic - the Gray Death. The main character of the game is special agent JC Denton, who must deal with all this chaos, coming face to face with the Illuminati, conspiracy theories and the secrets of Area 51.

The gameplay is an immersion sim. A large location is provided where there are many different methods to achieve the goal. The game does not punish the player for the path he has chosen. It all depends on observation and incremental capabilities. For completed actions, the player receives experience points, distributed among 11 different hero skills: edged weapons, firearms, heavy and explosive weapons, hacking locks, terminals and computers; medicine, swimming and wearing equipment.

Nanoaugmentations are unlocked as you progress through the game’s story. They provide many capabilities, including increased lung capacity, increased strength, speed, health regeneration, invisibility, improved vision, protection, spy drone, disorientation of robots and cameras, and much more. At the very beginning of the Deus Ex: Game of the Year Edition campaign, three upgrades will be available by default: Light, Friend/Alien, and Datalink. Depending on the style of play, Denton will use various tools: lockpicks, multitools, weapon modifiers, first aid kits, energy batteries, etc. The storyline can be completed entirely in stealth mode, without killing anyone, including bosses.
