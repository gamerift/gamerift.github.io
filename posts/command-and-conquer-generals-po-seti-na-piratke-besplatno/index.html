Game Description#

Command and Conquer Generals allows you to fight with other users over the Internet online or via LAN. You can also play Pirate with a friend online. This is a real time strategy game. In it you need to extract resources, build buildings in the camp, create troops, destroy enemy units and the enemy base. This branch is different from other titles in the series. There is a different interface and a completely different setting. The engine has also changed. This is the first part of the franchise with fully 3D graphics. Some units have additional abilities. They need to be activated manually. Mission introductions have also been redone. The action now takes place in an alternate 2010, where West and East are fighting militants. Command & Conquer: Generals has 3 unique factions: Global Liberation Army (aka GLA) - does not use electricity and likes to disguise its fighters, launch anthrax missiles, and engage in looting; China - prefers to use nuclear technology (including warheads), mine fields and buildings, turn off other people’s buildings, and its army is the largest here; United States of America - completely dependent on electricity, but has complete superiority in the air and espionage, can build molecular cannon, and their soldiers often have alternative equipment.

The possibility of free online play expands the content. A network game, like a single game, allows you to freely choose all three sides of the conflict. There are 9 additional generals in multiplayer. They are not available in single player mode. Each group received three military leaders online. Each of these commanders has their own equipment, leveling tree and abilities.
