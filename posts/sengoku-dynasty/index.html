Game Description#

Sengoku Dynasty tells the story of difficult life in feudal Japan. To convey everything with maximum accuracy, the developers consulted with a historical group, which provided valuable advice and confirmed records with information about the life of that period. The player will have to become the leader of one of the villages and lead it to prosperity and expansion in one of the ways.

The player will have one, almost abandoned settlement at his disposal, which he will have to restore and provide the inhabitants with everything they need. The lands of Japan are a bit sparse in terms of fertility, so resources are often limited and one must rely on hunting and rice to survive. But at the same time, it is important to monitor the condition of the forest and the animals in it in order to prevent species from becoming extinct. At first, being a wise and strong leader will be very difficult, but you should always listen to your heart and do what is most optimal and reasonable. The paths of the ruler of the Sengoku Dynasty: Leader - creating alliances, strengthening relations with neighboring cities, trade, creating families and touching stories, such a head makes the settlement one big family and always listens to the wishes of the people Master - the leader of the technical process, makes life easier for the population with with the help of unique buildings and tools, mainly focuses on collecting various resources and directing them in the right direction. Warrior - achieves everything with the help of his weapons, masterfully masters a variety of techniques, easily repels the attacks of opponents Monk - is in complete harmony with the environment, builds various temples, worships the gods and may be heard by them

Each direction has its own characteristics and advantages in some industries, allowing you to keep everything in complete balance. The player will be able to choose the style that is most comfortable for him and move in his direction, so as not to disrupt the way of life and attract interested settlers to the village. In any of the ways, you can make enemies and start a war, but it is better to avoid this.
