Game Description#

In Dawnfolk, the action of which takes place in a world covered by darkness. As a ruler of the kingdom lost to the shadows, you must restore and protect your people. With the help of a mystical creature, Luer, you must navigate in the darkness and work on the restoration of light on Earth. In the center - research, resource management and decision -making when you restore your kingdom from scratch. Each of your action has weight, forming not only your settlement, but also your legacy.

Title invites gamers to plunge into an unfamiliar medieval fantasy world, where the danger lurks around every angle. You will collect resources, build buildings and manage the survival of your people. The key aspect of the project is the inclusion of mini-games that allow you to more effectively collect resources. These mini-games, despite their simplicity, have a direct impact on the success of your community, adding an additional level of complexity and involvement to the game.

One of the remarkable features of Dawnfolk is its availability. Unlike other strategic games that require significant time costs, this one allows gamers to immediately begin actions. If you were a random gamer or experienced strategist, you can start building your settlement in less than an hour. Available design allows you to experiment and study along the way, which makes it easy and interesting without a steep curve of training.
