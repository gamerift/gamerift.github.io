Game Description#

Palworld brings you free online play in the vast wilderness. Here, in a world full of dangers and opportunities, you can play online with friends, joining forces to survive. You are given the opportunity to play free online games using the unique skills of the mysterious “Falls” and their dubious morals.

In this world, hunger and merciless poachers lurk around every corner, making caution and tough decision-making essential elements. Will you be able to form an alliance with these creatures or will you prefer to use them for your own survival? The ability to play online with friends online in this exciting online pirate game is your key to an exciting experience.

These versatile companions are more than just beasts of burden. They become mounts, soaring into the skies, crossing treacherous seas and conquering varied landscapes as you explore the vast open world. Need to build a pyramid? Gather a workforce from the pals, because in Palworld, labor laws are subject to your will. The Pals have a wide variety of talents. Some of them awaken life in barren soil, while others skillfully manage the delicate dance of watering and harvesting. With their help, your idyllic estate will flourish, demonstrating the harmonious partnership between man and beast.

Automation is at the heart of Palworld. Build factories, staff them with guns, and watch the wheels turn while there’s fuel and… well, life. Remember that fires are a consumable item in the progression mechanism. Danger lurks around every corner, and your friends will be your shield from the unknown. When the situation changes, without thinking, will you sacrifice one of them to save yourself? Their loyalty is unshakable, even in the face of their own death. Breeding reveals the secrets of the Pal bloodline. Combine rare breeds and create a lineage with unparalleled strength. But be careful, because the blood of the rarest fells often flows in the reserves. A daring robbery could be your ticket to riches if you manage to evade the watchful eyes of the law.
