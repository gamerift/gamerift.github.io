Gorogoa

Release Date: 2017/12/14

Game Description An entertaining puzzle quest, made in a non-standard genre for a computer game. While playingGorogoa, the player must match images on the screen in a 2x2 grid, where each image has some kind of animated meaning or message. Thus, you create a whole picture that moves you towards the development of the plot, but does not reveal the main message until the very end. The plot reveals to us a certain boy who goes through his whole life in these pictures, so Gorogoa can be called a philosophical parable in which the protagonist will grow up, look at the world differently, and after that he will grow old and begin to reflect on the past....
