Game Description#

Depersonalization may interest amateurs of anime, Howard Lovecraft and Tactical Japanese Role Games. This is a mixture of TRPG, horror and visual novel. When creating their project, Chinese developers were inspired by the world of space horrors created by an eminent writer. Their creation boasts a detailed and designed pixel 2D graphic in anime style. The project is divided into several modules. Each of them has various finals and branches. The gameplay consists of studying locations in real time, conversations with non -pains, strategic step -by -step battles with cards. Flexible settings are provided for fights.

Title has a high degree of reiglabicity due to nonlinearity, a variety of gaming process and various modes to choose from. Before the start of passage, you can configure the world and create your investigator. All heroes and heroines have 4 distinctive features. As you pass, these protagonists are changing. Their personalities are influenced by the decisions made by players. The attributes of these characters, in turn, affect the development of the narrative. All these parameters are submitted along with narrative. The game has a mechanic of throwing cubes. So thanks to the random, even owners of low characteristics with proper luck can provoke incredible events.
