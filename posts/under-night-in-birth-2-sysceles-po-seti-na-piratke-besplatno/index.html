Game Description#

Under Night In-Birth II Sys:Celes is the final chapter in the long-running saga of 2D fighting games. This sequel features free-to-play online play and an updated visual style. Play online with a friend or fight online with 24 unique characters. Expect a free and exciting showdown, where each character presents a new challenge with their own fighting style and motivations. Try out the possibility of online play even on a pirate and plunge into the exciting world of Hollow Night.

Three new characters will appear on the battlefield: the graceful Kaguya, the persistent Tsurugi and the mysterious Kuon. Their appearance, along with updated visual effects for existing characters, promises new fights and strategic opportunities. The game’s signature “dramatic, stylish 2D action” remains at the heart of the game, offering a combination of accessibility and depth that suits casual gamers and seasoned veterans alike.

Simple controls will allow beginners to quickly master the basics, and veterans to delve into the intricacies of the combat system. New moves for each character, such as Hyde’s Blaring Outrage and Lynn’s Soaring Dragon, bring new tactical options to familiar moves. Mechanics like Creeping Edge and Celestial Vorpal further deepen the gameplay by rewarding skill and strategic thinking.

Under Night In-Birth II Sys:Celes isn’t all about flashy combos and pixelated punches. The plot that concludes the Hollow Night saga comes to the fore. Whether you’re a seasoned fighter looking for a new challenge, or a newbie drawn to the world of stylish 2D brawls, Sys:Celes invites you to step into the arena and witness the final curtain fall on this epic saga.
