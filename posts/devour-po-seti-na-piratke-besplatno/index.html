Game Description#

You can play online inDEVOUR on Pirate both with AI partners and with a friend. You can team up with your partners by connecting online to the Internet to go through the storyline for free, where players will find frightening monsters, an abandoned mansion and a huge number of puzzles. The online game allows you to get together as a team of up to 4 people to stop the activities of a mystical cult that is taking people with it to hell.

The protagonists do not know how to fight, so all that the mechanics allow is to run, scream and hide. The main task is to prevent the creatures from catching you. The possibility of a free online game offers you to gather your brave group of 2 to 4 people who find themselves in a difficult situation. The heroes need to destroy Anna Puerto, an obsessed cult leader who was captured by the demon Azazel in one of the country estates.

The game mechanics of free online play are based on interaction between participants, where it is necessary to cover your partners at all times, distract monsters and solve puzzles together. A single player game is available for those who want to go through the chilling story solo, completely immersing themselves in the mystical history of the estate. Each gaming session lasts no longer than an hour. The rooms are generated randomly, changing the location of rooms, monsters, quest items, etc. Key features: you need to stop Azazel with the help of gasoline, fire and ritual goats; an ultraviolet flashlight as the only means of defense; gameplay difficulty increasing every minute; nightmare mode for the most experienced players; in-game voice chat.
