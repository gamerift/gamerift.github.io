Game Description#

Chef RPG gives a completely new idea of ​​what a culinary culinary can be. So, users get the role of a wandering chef, who has an important mission - to revive the legendary Le Sex restaurant. Once this place was a real magnet for travelers and everyone who wanted to eat and take a breath, and now this is nothing more than an abandoned building. It is time to return to him former glory, developing his own business and going to a new level in terms of cooking.

Before starting the journey to the Chef RPG, gamers have the opportunity to choose your own development option. You can concentrate exclusively on cooking, developing new recipes and improving old developments. You can emphasize the business, opening points around the in -game world. For example, in addition to the very establishment, you can open a bar in the city, and then surprise everyone and show the restaurant’s project on board the ferry. But it must be borne in mind that in this case, players will literally take all responsibilities. This applies to the purchase of products, the choice of the style and design of the premises, the arrangement of furniture and other actions. Of course, a significant part of the process is devoted to cooking each individual dish. If we are talking about the multi -component position of the menu, you must clearly follow the instructions so as not to spoil the order of an important client. In any case, the overall success depends solely on the actions of the protagonist, and if you do everything right, you can succeed without unnecessary problems.
