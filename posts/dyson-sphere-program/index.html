Game Description#

Dyson Sphere Program is a space simulator that allows you to develop your base and explore the galaxy. Go into the vastness of outer space to colonize distant planets. Build an intergalactic factory using the power of the stars. Design and plan production chains. Collect resources and develop your base. Start your journey as a small workshop and progress to the Dyson Sphere - a megastructure that orbits the stars.

In the distant future, humanity was faced with a shortage of energy, so they had to explore the far reaches of space. You play as an engineer of the COSMO space alliance. You are faced with an important task: to build Dyson Spheres to generate the energy needed by humanity. The game’s universe is represented by neutron stars, white dwarfs, red giants, gaseous and rocky planets. The galaxy is generated randomly, randomly distributing resources on the surface of the earth. Transform materials and transport them between bases, creating new transport routes.

Create conveyor belts and fit buildings into special grids. Use all the different tools provided by COSMO. Build the largest automated production line in the entire galaxy! Complete tasks as quickly as possible, receiving numerous rewards and bonuses. Features of the Dyson Sphere Program: collect resources on planets, which are oceans, lavas, deserts, icy lands, gaseous craters, etc.; discover secrets and reveal the mysteries of the universe; fly through the vastness of open space and make quantum leaps; use thousands of transport ships, transferring raw materials; install power plants of different types, building wind turbines, artificial stars and much more.
