Game Description#

Wuchang: Fallen feathers is not an ordinary history lesson. Of course, you will pass along the insidious path of China from the late dynasty of mines, a period abundant by warring groups and public riots. But this is not just a revived textbook. Get ready for a wild trip, in which historical personalities mix with fantastic creatures, erasing the boundaries between reality and supernatural. Stand in the place of the protagonist - a character shrouded in a secret who fights with a forgotten past. The disclosure of truth will not be a simple walk in the park. This world is as unpredictable as the political landscape of that era.

You need to be on the alert, navigate in muddy water and adapt to a constantly changing narrative. Wuchang: Fallen feathers throws you headlong into complex battles. Think about the complexity similar to Souls, where every wave of weapons and each evasion movement should be accurate. Enemies are of different shapes and sizes, each of them is a puzzle that requires a unique strategy to come out the winner. However, do not get hung too much on the breakdown of the skulls, the environment is your silent partner.

Learn to use the world in your interests, use the area and turn the course of the battle. But this is more than just battles. History unfolds organically, forming under the influence of your choice. Each decision you have made is reflected in the development of events, changing their course. This is not just a pre -written narrative that you passively absorb. This is your story, your journey through a rarely studied head of history, an invitation to leave your mark in the world standing on the edge of chaos.
