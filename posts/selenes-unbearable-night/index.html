Game Description#

Selene’s Unbearable Night is a third-person survival horror game that immerses gamers in a tense and atmospheric atmosphere. The title tells the story of Selena, a woman who finds herself in a difficult situation and is forced to overcome various dangers in order to survive. Users will have to search for key items, solve complex puzzles and confront numerous threats in order to complete the game. Selene’s Unbearable Night’s narrative unfolds through dialogue and cinematography, offering a deep dive into the characters' motivations and backstories.

There are two playable characters, each offering their own unique perspective on the events that unfold. This approach to the two characters allows users to explore different aspects of the plot and understand the complexity of the situation in which Selena finds herself. The combat in the title is varied and allows gamers to choose between stealth, melee and firearms to defeat enemies. Resource management is critical as users must carefully collect and use items to survive.

The environment plays an important role in both combat and puzzle solving, requiring users to pay close attention to their surroundings. The combination of narrative depth, strategic combat, and challenging puzzles makes for a fun survival horror adventure. The atmosphere is enhanced by the soundtrack and eerie sound design. Gamers will be on the edge of their seats as they navigate dark, claustrophobic environments, constantly awaiting the next threat.
