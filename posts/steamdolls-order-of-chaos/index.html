Game Description#

SteamDolls - Order of Chaos - Metro -Aviation in steampunk stylistics, characterized by a rich gameplay and incredible cruelty. There is a huge amount of blood and dismemberment. There is a time to play for a thief and anarchist named a whisper. Its task is to penetrate the well -protected territory.

You need to act as quieter as possible and try not to enter into a direct confrontation with protection. Hold on the shadows and commit hidden brutal killings. Slash from the back and apply deadly blows to the enemy. You have to fight not only with love guards, but also engage in battle with the pursuing ghosts that the witch sends. She appears naked in front of the player, so in the first seconds she can cause disorientation.

The protagonist is trying to reveal the secret of a conspiracy that can shake the whole world and change the course of history. Despite the two -dimensional levels, they can go in various ways. The developers of Steamdolls - Order of Chaos were inspired by Dishonored, so the gameplay contains some immersive mechanics. Throughout the campaign, the difficulty will increase and put you in more difficult situations. Carefully study the patterns of enemies and try not to make mistakes, since any of them can cost life.
