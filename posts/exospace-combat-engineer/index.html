Game Description#

eXoSpace Combat Engineer is a journey into the depths of a procedurally generated galaxy, where survival depends solely on engineering savvy and piloting skill. In the role of a bounty hunter, you will have to chart a course through unexplored sectors of space, each hyperjump in which promises unpredictable events. Fate can lead you to a research station in distress, throw you into the thick of a battle with pirates, or force you to face a moral choice - to provide assistance or engage in robbery. Each such outing inevitably ends in a clash with a powerful boss, who is the ultimate goal of the contract.

The central element of the entire process is the design of the spacecraft. Ships are created from hundreds of different modules, including not only weapons and armor, but also moving elements such as pistons and rotators, allowing the most daring engineering solutions to be implemented. The combat system of the eXoSpace Combat Engineer project is completely subject to the laws of physics, making collisions feel as realistic as possible. Any ship can be dismantled into pieces with precise hits, and the tactics of ramming or even “biting into” the enemy’s hull are full-fledged combat strategies.

In addition to completing story missions, gamers have access to a sandbox mode, which provides unlimited freedom for creativity. In this mode, you can design unique starships without any restrictions, hone their design and organize large-scale battles involving huge fleets. For those who are looking for maximum tension, there is a special hardcore format of passage, where any mistake becomes fatal, and death becomes final.
