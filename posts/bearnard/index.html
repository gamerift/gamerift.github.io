Game Description#

Bearnard sends the player on a campaign to return onions and protect his native forest. A single journey runs through a variety of landscapes, offering to encounter numerous enemies and solve puzzles based on physics. The combat system is a step -by -step tactic using a deck of cards that requires a strategic approach to defeat hostile creatures.

Promotion in the plot includes the study of locations in search of secrets and artifacts, as well as improving onion shooting skills while managing cards in the hand. The central place in the Bearnard gameplay occupies a collection of decks with unique abilities. These skills open access to treatment, protection and control of opponents in battle. Gamer finds new specimens for the deck in chests after victory over opponents, divided into ordinary, rare, epic and legendary. In addition, the combination of spontaneous cards creates powerful effects.

Success in battles depends on the competent use of assets to suppress various enemies in this difficult but fun game. The journey passes through the forests, mines, snowy mountains and gloomy dungeons. The puzzles of the environment require the use of onions for shots on levers or activation of platforms. In addition, this project offers bonus levels for checking the skills of platforming and strategic thinking. For their passage, the user receives gold, silver or bronze medals, serving as a confirmation of personal achievements within the framework of the gameplay.
