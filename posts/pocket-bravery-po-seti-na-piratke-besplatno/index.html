Game Description#

Pocket Bravery provides free online play, allowing you to play online with a friend or other online players online. Enjoy a nostalgic brawler infused with modern sensibilities by resurrecting the Neo Geo Pocket aesthetic. In this game you can play online with friends, even if you have a pirated copy, and it’s absolutely free. A variety of characters with unique abilities and gameplay styles await you on the screen. No matter your experience - whether you’re a seasoned veteran or a curious newbie - Pocket Bravery offers intuitive controls for a seamless online gaming experience.

Immerse yourself in a diverse cast of 12 fighters, each wielding their own unique “ichor” - powerful elemental energy that fuels their attacks and unlocks a strategic web of offensive and defensive options. Master the elements, unleash breathtaking final attacks, and defeat your opponents in local or online duels. For the purists, Pocket Bravery boasts ultra-low rollback latency netcode in online multiplayer, ensuring smooth and responsive matches regardless of geographic distance. So grab your controller, channel your inner warrior, and get ready to enter the fiery ring of Pocket Bravery.
