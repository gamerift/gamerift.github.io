Game Description#

Shattehed Pixel Dungeon is an adventure in gloomy dungeons where users will take on the role of an adventurer. The setting is made in the style of dark fantasy, where you have to face various monsters. The game was made in the genre of Rolewal Rowlian Adventure, showing the journey of 4 characters. Levels in a single campaign are made in a randomized form, filling each time randomly locations with opponents and rooms in which the protagonist will be able to get.

Gamers in the shuttered pixel Danzhen have to try out the classic gameplay Danzhen Crowler, where you will have to move along an isometric map with a view of the top. The hero will have special skills and abilities that come from his specialization. The game contains 4 classes: a warrior, a magician, a robber and a hunter. It will also be possible to configure a subclass where there are 8 options for pumping and 13 combat skills. In the process of passing, users will be able to master more than 100 talents, which will gradually strengthen the character. The testing in the dark dungeons allow gamers:

Fight with 60 opponents, deal with 30 traps and defeat 5 unique bosses. Pass 5 individual regions in the dungeons, moving through 26 floors with enemies. Collect the desired build, using 100 equipment items for this (wands, rings, weapons, armor, etc.). Craft more than 150 things, which will come in handy on a trip. Pass the plot missions in cross -platform form from a phone or computer.
