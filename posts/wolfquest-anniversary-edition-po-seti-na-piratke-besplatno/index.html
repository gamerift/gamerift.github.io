Game Description#

WolfQuest: Anniversary Edition is a life simulator in which you play as a wolf, living in the vastness of Yellowstone National Park. For free, you can play the pirate game with a friend online, teaming up in a wolf pack. You can interact with each other online via the Internet by going to explore the reserve or go hunting. The online game WolfQuest: Anniversary Edition allows you to find your mate to start a family. Game mechanics contain elements of survival. A large open world is presented, available for exploration.

The area extends from Slough Creek to north of the Lamar Valley. It includes more than 50 square kilometers of territory. The possibility of free online play also invites you to team up in order to protect both your flock and your kids from various threats. You need to feed your children, arrange your home to survive the cold nights, etc. Puppies need to be played with and cared for in every possible way. A pack of unfamiliar wolves can pose a threat.

Particular danger comes from other predators such as grizzly bears, cougars or coyotes. There is a struggle with them for territory and provisions. The innovative Age Perks system simulates the life cycle of animals. The Iron Wolf mode offers true survival in the vastness of the wild, where every day could be your last. There is a Slough Creek co-op mode for capturing and holding territory.
