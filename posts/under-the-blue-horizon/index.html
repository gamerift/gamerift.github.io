Game Description#

Under The Blue Horizon is a stand-up game-designer, designed for functioning as a constant aquarium on a computer screen. This virtual aquarium can be placed anywhere on the display running simultaneously with other programs or during operation. The main interaction of the gamer is to create and breeding fish, requiring shrimp for their development as a source of food.

With an increase in a population of different types, the ecosystem produces by -products, launching the key system of resource management of this project. This mechanics forms a central gameplay consisting of production and expansion. In Under The Blue Horizon, by -products left by water inhabitants are converted into Aqua currency. This resource serves as the basis for progress, allowing the player to expand the sea zone and erect new buildings in the environment. To help in the collection of resources, you can hire companions like crayfish and jellyfish for automated support.

These creatures contribute to the receipt of materials, and their strategic location surrounded is an element for increasing the speed of acquisition. The well -thought -out placement of assistants becomes an important factor in the management of underwater space. In addition to standard assistants, the gameplay includes specialized companions that introduce special functions. The hire of these comrades opens access to the optimization of the system and allows you to build additional types of buildings. This simulator also provides a set of options to control the window itself.
