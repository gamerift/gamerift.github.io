Game Description#

Demonic Mahjong races Majong bones with a horn structure. Players have to use chips from their hands to collect combinations that meet the established rules for counting glasses. However, the main task is beyond the scope of simple accumulation of points. The final goal is to build a hand and strategies that allow you to defeat the main boss, which turns every entry into preparation for the final battle.

The Demonic Mahjong strategic layer includes systems that change each game session. You can enlist the support of more than 100 different alloy spirits capable of changing key mechanics or strengthening certain layouts of chips. In this project, you can collect a collection of 100 relics that give accumulated improvements during one passage. In addition, 35 offers give special skills that include methods for hand manipulation and exposure to the priority of tiles.

The bones themselves for Majong can be improved with 17 spirits and 12 of their options, opening access to more powerful effects. The characters have their own active and passive skills, which forms the basis for constructing chips -based tactics. This game offers the path through the underworld, formed by a system of 10 ordinary and 90 special scenarios. The decisions made during these events, for example, interaction with the grandmother of Maine or the help of a ghost, directly determine the conditions of further battles. Each batch ends with a fight with one of the 24 bosses, among which there is a bold and horse -haired, who use their skills in a duel.
