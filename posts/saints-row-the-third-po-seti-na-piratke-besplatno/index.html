Game Description#

Saints Row: The Third is a third-person action game in an open world, the plot of which can be completed with a friend using an online Internet connection. All network capabilities of the project are available for free on the pirate site. If you play online with a partner, you will need to explore the city of Stillwater. The Saints became a real brand, and Johnny Gat gained the status of a cupmaster. However, the criminal organization Syndicate is trying in every possible way to resist the gang of heroes. With the help of three groups, including the Deckers, Luchadores and Morgenstern, the leader of the Syndicate, Philip Laurens, is trying to get rid of the Saints.

Online play allows you to create your own character by choosing gender, clothing, character traits, etc. Shaundi, Pierce, Oleg Kirillov, Kinzie and other heroes will help in the fight against criminals. Some will turn to the “light side” during the process. The gameplay of Saints Row: The Third invites you to explore a city consisting of several districts. Each area is controlled by one of the factions. You are allowed to fight in close combat, use melee weapons, explosives, shotguns, rifles, machine guns and various futuristic guns.

There are many main and secondary quests to choose from. You can get into any vehicle by stealing it right on the street. Or you can buy your own and install it in the garage. There are a huge number of stores available for weapons, clothing, etc. Additional tasks may include gathering, crazy Genki show, destruction of everything around, etc. Free online play offers a chance to share the madness and chaos of popular culture and famous world events. Several endings are presented: good and bad. They depend on the final choice involving Shaundi or Killbane.
