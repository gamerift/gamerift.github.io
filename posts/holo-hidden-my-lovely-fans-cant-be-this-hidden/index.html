Game Description#

The gameplay in Holo Hidden - My Lovely Fans Can ‘CANT THIS HIDden takes place at 11 stages drawn by hand and 1 bonus level. Each scene is a large -scale illustration, which needs to find hundreds of certain objects and supporters. Search items vary from obvious to skillfully disguised in the scenery. These locations also contain numerous references to the presented personalities of Hololive, embedding thematic elements directly into the search riddles for fans.

The searches begin at Holo Hidden - My Lovely Fans Can ‘Hidden, where players have to help virtual characters. The main goal is to detect their fans and various missing objects hidden in a detailed environment. Such activity in the style of hide and seek serves as a central element of the process on numerous locations. Places vary from uncleaned rooms to characteristic institutions like Pondachikova, representing visual puzzles, solved by attentive observation.

This project is focused on puzzles and does not have a complex, continuous plot. Instead, each stage functions as a separate scenario, representing a new environment associated with specific heroes. The general atmosphere is created due to carefree scenery and comic situations shown in a single game. The key lesson is invariably concentrated on observation and collecting, forming a direct test based on visual acuity and recognition of images in the stylized fantasy world.
