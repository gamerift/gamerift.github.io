Game Description#

Dino Path Trail shows the world where dinosaurs survived to the era, similar to the wild West, setting a person to survive in this altered land. The main goal includes the search and salvation of a stolen sister through the intersection of various lands full of dangers. The chances of surviving are strongly dependent on the solutions and actions of the player as part of the structure of this action-rowlaine, where it is necessary to repulse predators, hostile people and elements. The environment requires constant alertness in the face of a variety of challenges.

The study is a key part of Dino Path Trail, since the player has to move to different zones, each of which contains unique tests and resources. These areas extend from arid deserts to swamps, fraught with special threats, and frosty regions. The landscape is created procedurally, including additional zones and secret paths to make each passage unlike the previous one, along with the random appearance of enemies, which retains a tense atmosphere.

To succeed, you need to adapt to these constantly changing conditions and understand the features of the area. The character builds and develops a mobile camp, an important help for survival, which follows him. This portable shelter serves as a place for creating items, storing production and improving workbenches, offering some stability. The increase and arrangement of the base allows you to better prepare for campaigns in complex regions. In a world full of constant dangers, the camp is the main stronghold for preparation and rest.
