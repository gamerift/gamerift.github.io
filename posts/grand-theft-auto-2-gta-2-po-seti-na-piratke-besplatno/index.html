Game Description#

On the pirated version ofGrand Theft Auto 2 (GTA 2) you can play online for free, exploring a metropolis called Anywhere City with a friend. Alone or online, you can go through all three levels of the story campaign and work together to clear areas of the city, including The Downtown Area, The Residential District and The Industrial District. Depending on the area, a meat processing plant, a Krishna temple, a trailer park, etc. are available for exploration.

The story centers on Claude Speed. Online play allows you to take control of his partner. It is possible to switch between day and night mode. Each district in Grand Theft Auto 2 is controlled by a specific gang. All factions have an individual appearance, fight with unique weapons and drive special vehicles. Gangs in GTA 2: Zaibatsu Corporation - drug dealers appearing in all areas; Scumbags are completely insane maniacs who prefer to drive Dementia; Yakuza - the eastern mafia led by the dangerous Johnny Zoo; SRS scientists (Sex and Reproductive Systems) - scientists practicing genetic engineering and robotics; Hillbillies - rednecks, whose leader is Billy Bob Ben; Russian mafia - specialists in murder and arms trafficking; Hare Krishnas - fanatics, led by the Sunbeam.

To take the task, you need to drive up to one of the phones. They vary in color. The possibility of free online play in GTA 2 allows you to jointly complete all types of tasks: green, yellow and red. Blue machines provide information about factions. Authority directly affects the availability of missions. For completing quests you are awarded experience points and cash rewards.

There are 22 missions in total. In the second part, agents, army forces and special forces appeared, joining the regular police. They hunt for the hero if he has broken the law. The maximum wanted level is 6. In addition to gangs, 2 types of criminals are available: Auto Thieves and Street Robbers. As before, you can steal any vehicle from civilians or law enforcement officials. A wide selection of weapons is presented, ranging from ordinary pistols to grenade launchers.
