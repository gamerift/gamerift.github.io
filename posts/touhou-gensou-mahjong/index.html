Game Description#

TOUHOU GENSOU MAHJONG is not an ordinary mahjong game. Of course, the core mechanics remain the same - matching tiles, building chalks, challenging Riichi - but here’s the kicker: Touhou Project throws its huge cast of characters into the game. Over 120 familiar faces spanning two decades of history bring a new layer of strategy to the classic four-player format. Do you think you have everything figured out with Riichi? Perhaps Remilia Scarlet will throw a wrench into your plans with her ability to manipulate scoring. Or maybe Reimu Hakurei will disrupt your carefully crafted hand by swapping the tiles.

These unique character abilities bring delightful chaos and strategic depth to the game, making every match unpredictable. But TOUHOU GENSOU MAHJONG is not afraid to shake things up even more. Forget about standard rules - the title introduces unconventional twists to keep you on your toes. One moment you’re playing a familiar hand, and the next you find yourself in a situation that requires creativity. This variety means no two matches are the same, keeping the game fresh for both mahjong veterans and curious newbies.

This game is a love letter to the Touhou Project universe. Fans will enjoy the opportunity to play as their favorite characters, while newcomers will get to experience the rich history and quirky personalities that define the series. As you navigate the game’s digital landscape, strategy and skill will be intertwined with a touch of magic, creating a captivating and nostalgic experience.
