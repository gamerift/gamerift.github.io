Game Description#

The magic universe in Near-Mage shows the adventures of the young girl Illy Vraj, who was invited to live in Transylvania for the summer. The girl will meet with her cousin Dominica, who wants to tell her about the secret witch’s kind. The heroine will receive the opportunity to enter the Transylvanian Institute of Magic, where he will master the art of witchcraft. The girl will go to the preparatory summer school to become a full -fledged caster.

The protagonist in the NIAR-Mag has to attend various classes in order to master new types of magic. It is necessary to gradually develop the talent of the sorceress, which will influence other creatures. The game is a classic Point-And-Click Adventure, where the development of the storyline and the adventure of the heroine will depend on the user’s elections. Players will be able to visit three main locations, such as the city of Sigishoara, mythical Rusus, which is behind the curtain and invisible institute for ordinary inhabitants.

Players have to create their own spells, applying different studies studied from books. Magic tricks will affect the fate of some characters, which will completely change the story of the narrative. We will have to thinkfully perform actions to receive the desired result. The protagonist will communicate with other students, which will make faithful friends or envious enemies among classmates.

Users will be able to meet creatures, such as vampires, faces, frosty, harigs, hums or stims, which can be modified using special spells. Illy Vraja will be able to collect his own inventory, consisting of unique spells. The game is drawn in 4K resolution, where a short -range animation has been worked out. Users will be able to engage in customization of the character, choosing among a dozen detailed outfits and hairstyles.
