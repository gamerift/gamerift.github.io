Game Description#

You can play online inTaora: Survival on a pirated version and completely free of charge. An online network game will allow you and a friend to plunge into the zombie apocalypse on a huge infected island. You have to not just survive the night, but build a new life. Collect loot, explore the hidden corners of Taora, and craft the gear you need to take your place in this devastated world.

Forget single player. You have a team of four survivors, each with their own strengths. One of them can be an excellent shooter, another can be a virtuoso in repairs, and the third can be a healer who keeps everyone on their toes. To survive, you will have to work together. Level up their skills to specialize them to perform different tasks. Need someone to open your safe? There is a character for this. Need someone to patch up a bullet wound? This is another slot on your team.

Taora is infested with more than just the undead. It is littered with abandoned cars just waiting to be occupied. Repair them, modify them and turn them into your personal zombie-killing chariots. Step on the gas and explore the island. There are hidden locations just waiting to be discovered, and each one contains the loot you need to survive another day.

Building a base camp is very important. This is your refuge, your fortress against the relentless hordes. Here you can store loot, craft better weapons and armor, and the tools you need to keep your team alive. It’s more than just a place to store things, it’s a symbol of hope in a world that’s gone crazy. So buckle up, gather your team and get ready to fight for survival in Taora.
