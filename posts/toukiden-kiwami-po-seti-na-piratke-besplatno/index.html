Game Description#

Toukiden: Kiwami is an action game with RPG elements, which provides the opportunity to play online with a friend for free as a pirate. Participants take on the role of Slayers, fighting online with each other or with a variety of monsters over the Internet. The enemies are the powerful They. Each enemy comes with a unique design and a set of deadly attacks. Any opponent requires an individual approach.

Online play allows up to four players to team up. Toukiden: Kiwami takes place in Japan. All fighters have different weapons and are represented by various characteristics. There are many passive and active abilities available, with the help of which unique hero builds are created. The possibility of free online play is aimed at the interaction and confrontation of participants. When entering into battle with enemies, you need to tear off their limbs. It is necessary to find weak points in monsters in order to deliver deadly attacks.

When the monsters are defeated, they release the absorbed spirits of Mitama. By collecting souls, you can upgrade and buy new types of weapons. The setting is a fantasy world that combines the real and the fantastic. Each player begins his journey as a beginner, exploring the expanses of the village of Utakata and its surroundings. As you become stronger, you gain access to new territories and opponents. By leveling up, you can become an Oak Warrior - a veteran fighter with enormous strength.
