Game Description#

Alien Breed 3: Descent can be played online for free, working with a friend through the story campaign and destroying crowds of bloodthirsty alien monsters. You can play online online or go through the story on one device. The online game Alien Breed 3: Descent invites you to learn the terrible truth about the existence of an extraterrestrial civilization. Aliens are attacking from all sides, so you need to quickly react to situations and make decisions at lightning speed.

There is a huge variety of weapons available. Each gun has its own unique characteristics. The story centers on an engineer named Conrad. He encounters a horde of aliens. The Leopold spaceship is doomed and he must find a way to escape the trap. The opportunity for free online play invites you to travel with your partner, fighting back against strangers in the narrow and dark corridors of a huge space station. The project serves as the final chapter of the trilogy. The gameplay combines the mechanics of an arcade shooter and a survival game.

Many improvements and settings for weapons are presented. You can switch the camera mode, choosing between top-down perspective and third person. A new weapon, Project X, is available - an analogue of the famous BFG gun. The arsenal was also replenished with a stun gun. The campaign is represented by a set of missions that are connected by one storyline. Also available are the Survivor and Challenge modes, which test the players' skills. Co-op allows you to go through three special arenas filled with crowds of monsters. The upgrade store allows you to upgrade your heroes' abilities. If you find fallen comrades and special lockers, you can pick up experience points or upgrades for guns. High score tables are included showing each player’s global position.
