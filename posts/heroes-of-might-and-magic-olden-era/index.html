Game Description#

Heroes of Might and Magic: Olden Era returns to the world of Enrot, inviting both veterans and new gamers to the strategic campaign on the continent of Jadam. This prequel returns to the origins of the determining genre and the recognition of critics of a series of step -by -step strategic games. Here, the rival factions should unite against the hive - the swarm of the insectoids, led by the master of the demons of Inferno. Users can choose one of six unique fractions: temple, necropolis, Sylvanas, dungeon, hive and the mysterious sixth fraction that has not yet been disclosed.

Each fraction offers its gameplay and abilities. The study is a key point in this strategic adventure. Gamers collect resources, strengthen their kingdoms and modernize locks. The laws adopted in each fraction add a depth of gameplay, allowing you to create unique styles. Fights occur on the hexagonal battlefields with new mechanics, including active abilities.

In a single campaign or in multiplayer mode, users can engage in classical management of heroes and castles, focus on surviving heroes or taking part in direct battles in the arena. The game has a rich storyline filled with intriguing characters and quests. Players will meet familiar faces and discover new heroes, moving around the political landscape of Enrot. The world of Heroes of Might and Magic: Olden Era comes to life thanks to detailed cards and the exciting environment.
