Game Description#

Voids Vigil is an arena with an Autoboy in the Rogue -Lite style, where the action never stops. Gamers will have to clean the one who flooded the arena of the hordes of robots armed only with automatic weapons, drones and many special abilities. Title maintains interest, offering a variety of modes and complexity settings, which allows you to always find new tests. As you advanced, users should competently dispose of their koga - the key resource, which is used in stores that appear throughout the game.

These stores present a wide selection of items that can change the user approach to passing, offering countless opportunities for unique constructions and strategies. One of the key features is 15 classes of gamers, each of which can be unlocked as the user moves. Each class has its own abilities, strong and weaknesses, which means that even experienced gamers will be able to find new approaches to fighting.

Classes help to diversify the gameplay and give users the opportunity to adjust the play style to their preferences. Along with classes of gamers, more than 22 types of weapons are waiting for, which can be found and used - from quick and light to heavy artillery. In addition, more than 175 items are available here, each of which can affect how Gamer builds his character during each session. The key to survival in Voids Vigil lies in strategic thinking and quick reflexes.
