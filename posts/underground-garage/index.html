Game Description#

Underground Garage is a car symbulator with elements of a mechanic that allows not only to take part in the races, but also to completely create his own car from scratch. It is allowed to both buy ready -made models, exploring the secondary market, and create cars from scratch. Restoring transport or pumping cars, it is necessary to take part in illegal races, showing your superiority as a mechanic and demonstrating the skills of a first -class driver.

Game process underground Garage:

non -linear plot, where it is allowed to participate in chases with cops, engage in espionage, etc.; You can disassemble the car into small spare parts; night competitions on city streets and in villages; study of the landfill for interesting details; An abandoned hangar, airport, highways and other locations.

In total, 30 types of cars are available. Each transport has its own characteristics and features. Having renovated the car, you can put it up for sale. It is allowed to buy advertising, make connections, get in contact with various retail networks, etc. An extensive list of parts that can be used during work is presented. It can be both small bolts, screws, tubes, as well as motors, wheels, housing, etc. In total, about 6,000 parts are allowed.

After the car is completely ready, you can arrange a test drive in free mode. Street racing mode is available. If you need to buy accessories, for example, neon lights, vinyls, etc., then you can visit a special garage - it can be found in it a huge assortment of various details that emphasize the style of transport.
