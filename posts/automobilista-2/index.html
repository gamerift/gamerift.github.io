Game Description#

Automobilista 2 is a continuation of the video game of the same name, which displays a series of car races to a new level. An important innovation compared to the first part is the transition to the eminent Project Cars engine called Madness. This contributed to the addition of advanced graphic technologies, including a realistic weather change, precisely recreated carriers and tracks, and many other functions.

In the project, you will face a variety of single modes - Custom Race and Test Day. As well as a large number of tracks, including real historical places, like Silverstone. The visual style of the game exactly recreates all the subtleties of the competition on the roads. And all atmospheric locations, or cars of cars, will appear in new sensations if you connect VR shit, as the developers added support for virtual reality.

But the most important thing is cars. You have access to many cars of real brands. Each of them has its own characteristic management physics, and its own special mechanical solutions. Feel high speeds of racing cars, or drive on heavy trucks, feeling all their dimensions. Reach the peak of your career, and prove to your rivals that you do not exist equal gamers.
