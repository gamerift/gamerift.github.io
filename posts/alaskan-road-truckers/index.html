Game Description#

** ALASKAN ROAD TRUCKERS ** - A car simulator in which you will become a driver of huge trucks, being a trucker in snowy Alaska. Call a challenge of one of the most severe and dangerous environments that could only be in such simulators. Test your strength and try to survive in a harsh environment.

At the same time, you will have the opportunity to see the stunning beauty of this northern region. Alaska will meet with endless wastelands, high peaks, harsh winters and sunny spring meadows. The gameplay is closely tied to physics due to the fact that the player can feel as much as possible by staff and weather conditions. Find out how the climate and area affects the condition of the truck, what difficulties that drivers have to be overcome in the service sector and much more.

From the very beginning of the game, only the best and powerful trucks can be available that can cope with the harsh conditions of the northern territories. But at the same time, it should be understood that the complexity of the missions will correspond to the power of transport. You can easily lose or damage the load, which contributes to the fact that you will climb into debt and work out this money for the company. ** Some features of the Alaskan Truck Simulator: ** Dynamic change in weather and time of day; Interactive environment for study; Promotives and general indicator of your success and reputation; craft, modernization and repair; a huge number of routes and tracks; a full -fledged survival system.
