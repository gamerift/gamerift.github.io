Game Description#

Kenshi forces you to survive from the very first minutes of the game. An impressive RPG with an open world and the ability to create your own squad, the remaining player privileges are gradually revealed to us. For example, you can build your own buildings in any location, conquer them from other factions, create your own business, sell goods in peaceful settlements, craft whatever you want, explore the environment and do whatever you want. The main thing is not to be captured and remain on the trunk of the savages.

Abilities are upgraded in accordance with their use. To steal skillfully, we will have to practice constantly; in order to kill masterfully, using various combinations and super-techniques, we will not have to negotiate peace with the units we meet, but immediately cut from the shoulder; To skillfully cook food, you will have to constantly stand near the vat.

Your squad will include warriors that you hire. They will not move with you in the open world near you. The power of the squad is revealed directly in battle with a strategic focus. Over time, you will be able to accumulate enough resources by defending or attacking caravans, and build your own city, into which trade routes will gradually open, and random wanderers will become your settlers. The city, accordingly, will always have to be protected.

The game has no plot or specific ending. You will end the game if you die or when you get bored. The entire game has more than 870 kilometers of open terrain, which includes about sixty biomes - locations with changed terrain, weather and inhabitants. The most valuable items are hidden in more distant locations. This will force you to wander constantly. Increase your abilities, buy weapons, hire soldiers for your squad and don’t forget that crafting in the game comes first. There is even a separate ability - “screw a limb.” Develops every time your leg or arm is cut off.
