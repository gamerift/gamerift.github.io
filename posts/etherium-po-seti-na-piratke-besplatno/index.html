Game Description#

Etherium is a real-time strategy game that offers you to fight online over the Internet for one of three empires fighting for the valuable resource Etherium. For free, you can play pirate online with a friend, joining forces against common enemies or fighting in intense confrontations. Etherium is a rare resource that has been discovered on several planets. It gives unlimited possibilities to its owners.

The possibility of free online play includes base development, control of infantry, tanks and air forces. With the proper level of pumping, you can construct powerful colossi. Weather conditions pose a particular danger. Aliens from distant galaxies may also get involved in the conflict. The Etherium network game allows you to choose one of three factions. Each side of the conflict has its own strengths and weaknesses, as well as unique abilities. Multiplayer mode is designed for four people.

The gameplay mechanics are similar to Empire at War. Several planets with individual biomes are available. It is necessary to capture continents and unlock science points. They allow you to develop new types of equipment, infantry and supers. When collecting a fleet, you need to attack your rivals. The production of units takes almost no time, so combat operations develop very quickly. By collecting etherium, new opportunities for construction and crafting open up, and the player also gains a huge advantage over his opponent, winning due to advanced technologies. Leaderboards are available that track the personal achievements of players around the world.
