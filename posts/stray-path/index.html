Game Description#

Stray Path combines Freecell mechanics and strategic adventure gameplay. Gamers begin with the choice of one of several characters and plunge into the world, which is different each time. The Title offers a random set of enemies, tests and awards, all this is regulated by the cards that you pull. Each card is a new decision, whether it is a battle, avoiding a meeting or choosing equipment. The choice you make affects the outcome of your trip.

Design encourages you to think in advance, because you drag the cards, as in the Freecell game, and decide what to leave and what to discard. You will manage your inventory, carefully weighing the value of each item or a map of skills. Maps dictate what will happen next, offering moments of both excitement and stress. In some cases, you can find objects that will help you survive, and in others - provoke a heavy battle for which you were not ready. Moving around the world, you will find many enemies, each of which will represent its own problem.

Do you choose to fight or avoid them, the risk system and remuneration makes you be on the alert. Thanks to the accidental generation of events and enemies in the game, there are no two identical tasks. You will always be forced to adapt your strategies on the go, which each time leads to unique experience. In Stray Path, you can collect more than 100 different equipment, which gives you the opportunity to experiment with various styles.
