Game Description#

Woodpunk is a top-down shooter that allows you to play online with a friend. You can team up on one device or play pirates for free on the Internet online. which takes place in the world of the technological Middle Ages. The world has reached a new level of progress. The tree acts as the main source of energy, allowing humanity to make a huge leap in evolution. You will play as a mad inventor, confronting the powerful Marie de Medici. It is necessary to use available means to assemble robots or weapons. Woodpunk game elements: retro style with elements of a scrolling shooter; procedural generation of maps, weapons, etc.; a huge arsenal of hand-to-hand and small arms; more than a hundred enemies on the screen; interactive and destructible environments; possibility of free online play; technology tree for improving the hero and weapons.

Allows you to unlock new features, styles, 15 costumes and much more. By receiving experience points, the main character’s skills are upgraded. Events take place on a continent reminiscent of Italy, and the main character is similar to Da Vinci. The action takes place in two cities: G-Nova and Rhomega. Each area is represented by unique enemies, biomes, buildings, etc. At the end of each episode there is a boss battle.

In addition to the story campaign, a survival mode is presented. It is an endless game where you have to fight off an endless horde of opponents. The online game is represented by tournament tables showing the best results of players around the world. The gameplay is inspired by old school projects such as Metal Slug, Golden Ax, etc.
