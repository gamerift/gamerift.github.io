Game Description#

InDescenders you can participate in various competitions by playing online online. Together with a friend or random people, you can play online for free. Descenders on pirated version retains all multiplayer features, including many competitive modes, bikes, locations, accessories, etc. The gameplay is an extreme freeride downhill game, where the tracks are randomly generated. The possibility of free online play gives you the right to join teams to become the new downhill champions.

Before starting, you need to choose one of three teams to represent: Enemy, Arboreal and Kinetic. Each faction has its own uniform color and unique accessories to decorate their bike, helmet or equipment. The gameplay is based on physics, making you feel the speed and heaviness of the bike. The mechanics are aimed at freestyle, allowing you to spin, skid, jump, etc. When performing tricks in the air or on the ground, it is very important not to fall. By making chains of combinations, the counter increases, but if you fall, all accumulated points are burned. By winning competitions, the player increases his position in the leaderboard. Multiplayer play allows you to: accumulate reputation points for yourself and your team; wear the colors of your faction, receiving an exclusive uniform; unusual and valuable prizes for teams with a high reputation; a separate room for conversations between members of the same faction; the ability to take on the responsibilities of a captain .
