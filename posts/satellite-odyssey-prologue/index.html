Game Description#

Satellite Odyssey: Prologue is a first-person adventure game with horror elements. You play as Laska, a Soviet biologist who awakens from hibernation to discover that the crew of the spaceship Icarus is missing and its life support systems are malfunctioning. The narrative unfolds as you explore the ship, find clues, and interact with the artificial intelligence Zarya, who observes and influences your actions.

The action takes place in a retrofuturistic world inspired by classic Soviet science fiction. You move through the eerie abandoned corridors of Icarus, piece by piece piecing together the mystery of the crew’s disappearance. The atmosphere is tense as every decision you make can lead to different consequences, highlighting the importance of choice and consequences in gameplay. Satellite Odyssey: Prologue forces you to solve puzzles and make important decisions that affect the storyline.

The game’s design encourages exploration and critical thinking as you must use your wits to survive and uncover the truth behind the disturbing events on the ship. Interacting with Zarya adds complexity as the AI’s reactions and actions depend on your choices, creating a dynamic and immersive experience. Overall, the title offers a compelling mix of adventure, mystery and horror with a retro-futurism backdrop.
