Game Description#

Blackwind is a dynamic top-down shooter that allows you to play online for free as a pirate, fighting in co-op or in versus modes with a friend or random players. Multiplayer is available online online. Participants must take on intense battles against aliens while wearing advanced exoskeletons. Online play allows players to work together to stop an interplanetary invasion.

The main character is an experienced fighter named James Hawkins. His allies help him. Using the opportunity of free online play, partners are controlled by other participants. The events of Blackwind take place on the distant colony planet Medusa-42. Members of the squad find themselves on the planet after the crash of the Pandora shuttle. From this moment on, the fighters need to survive. The difficulty increases dynamically, presenting the heroes with more difficult tasks. Enemies become more aggressive, and their number increases significantly.

Each soldier has a unique role on the team. You can fight in hand-to-hand combat, shoot in bursts, deal huge damage with rockets, and much more. The exoskeleton allows you to carry out a powerful chain of attacks. By defeating enemies, a special skill tree is upgraded. A separate branch is presented for power armor. Using the suit, you can control a combat drone. While going through the story campaign alone, the drone helps you explore the world and is able to fight with opponents. In co-op, drones can be controlled by other players.
