Game Description#

Rogue Star Rescue combines Bullet Hell and Tower Defense. In addition to the single, there is the opportunity to have fun on the Internet online. You can also play Pirate with a friend online. This roguelike shooter features a top-mounted camera.

The possibility of free online play allows users to play Rogue Star Rescue in co-op. The indie title uses a sci-fi setting. The heroes travel between 10 planets and try to stop the star. It was formed from cosmic dust, and is now considered a threat to all living things. The plot has many endings. Characters can fire various weapons and throw grenades. They are also equipped with jetpacks. With the help of jetpacks you can jump and dodge. However, they must be refueled periodically.

The gameplay is as follows. Players run around the rooms and shoot opponents. Often they have to dodge a barrage of enemy bullets and hold the line. To move to the next zone, you need to destroy all enemies in the current room. Branched locations are generated randomly each time. They feature boss battles, side quests, and timed missions. Sometimes it is necessary to save other fighters. As a reward, they will join the group or be given a quest. As you progress, you earn experience and currency. Exp is needed for leveling up (each of the 6 heroes needs to be improved separately), and money is spent between outings to buy equipment. The range of items is expanding gradually. Network play supports up to 4 users. And with the help of local multiplayer, you can sit together at one computer.
