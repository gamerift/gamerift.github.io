Game Description#

FlatOut 2 is a racing arcade game, the main feature of which is the total destruction of vehicles. You can play online with a friend, fighting for free in various multiplayer competitions. Online Internet connection and LAN modes with split screen are available. The gameplay can be described as a race for survival. The car is damaged by collisions with surrounding objects, so it is important not only to be the first to the finish line, but also to try to hold out until the final lap. The network game is represented by standard races and derbies, where the essence comes down to the fact that all participants ram cars. FlatOut 2 modes: career - a set of various tests where you need to drive several laps, receiving cups and achievements; single race - random competitions in which you can compete with a friend; races - single or double races on tracks consisting of 6 laps; tricks - you can release the driver through the windshield, driving him into a goal, knocking down pins or using him as a dart in darts; derby - you need to ram with the participants, trying to survive in a mass carnage.

Damage is divided into categories ranging from one star to five: Push, Impact, Ram, Eject and Shatter. Depending on the damage, the car loses speed. In this case, it may happen that the transport completely fails. Flatout 2 puts an emphasis on the arcade feel, and the free online play option aims to increase the dynamics, conveying the spirit of competition.

With a greater emphasis on entertainment, Derby and Stunt events have become more extensive and varied. Racing is divided into four subtypes, including sports competitions and street races. Characters include Jack Benton, Ray Carter, Katie Jackson, Sally Taylor, Sofia Martinez, Frank Malkov and Jason Walker.
