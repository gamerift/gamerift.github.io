Game Description#

30XX is a two-dimensional adventure that you can play online as a pirate, teaming up with a friend. Servers are provided for free for connecting via the Internet online, but you can play locally on a shared screen. The online game offers you, in the company of a partner, to go through action-packed levels that are generated randomly, since the project contains roguelike elements. Taking control of one of the heroes, you need to explore constantly changing worlds, combining shots, jumps and many other possibilities.

The gameplay is inspired by the Mega Man series and also takes some elements from Enter the Gungeon and Dead Cells. Both a single-player campaign and a cooperative playthrough are presented, which doubles the dynamics of the gameplay.

Events take place in the distant future, when the familiar world has ceased to exist. The heroes will find themselves in the vastness of a green prison, where they need to destroy artificial intelligence that wants to take over the entire human race. Features of the 30XX gameplay: possibility of free online play; colorful characters with unique characteristics; hardcore gameplay and complex Power Fusion system; constantly changing environment; customization of all game elements; level editor with an extensive set of tools.
