Game Description#

Mercenary Kings has the optimal concept for playing online with a friend on a pirate. Here, users become part of the Mercenary Kings squad - the best team to recruit on Earth. But when the partners were defeated and are now being held in a super secret base, the gamer must do everything to stop the enemies. Here, he will be confronted by members of the Claw organization. In addition to the ability to do everything alone, the user is given the option of sharing the game online.

Each segment in Mercenary Kings begins at an allied base. Here, gamers can change their arsenal of weapons, customize their characters, and upgrade their skills. The process of receiving tasks from third-party characters also takes place here. In most cases, the concept of missions comes down to the usual actions - freeing a hostage and destroying all enemies at the facility. In this case, the execution sequence may differ. It is not at all necessary to adhere to any one scheme - there is a choice here. As for the locations, although they differ in visual design, they have a similar structure. There are several options for moving up and down, as well as a number of items that can be used as cover. Enemies begin to attack as soon as the character comes into view. Therefore, having the opportunity to play free online games, you can catch your opponents by striking from several sides. In addition, online play allows you to collect points not only to open more difficult stages, but also to form a rating. Thus, users can decide which of them will be more successful at one time or another.
