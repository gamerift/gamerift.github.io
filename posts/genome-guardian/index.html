Game Description#

Genome Guardian transfers gamers to the environment, where a microscopic becomes a scene for a high -octane action. This is a game where the usual meets the unusual, offering the world filled with microscopic organisms, some of which are harmless, while others are far from the case. A distinctive feature is that it is based on the mixing and selection of DNA nucleotides to create bio -weapons, which serves you the main protection from the hostile environment. This is not just a Title of survival, it is a game of adaptation, evolution and overcoming the obstacles that change with each passage.

Each session requires careful planning and quick reflexes, and this is what makes it interesting for those who love the challenge and strategy. One of the most intriguing aspects of Genome Guardian is the central role of DNA in gameplay. The users will have to unite four different nucleotides: adenin, cytosine, guanine and thyme. Each nucleotide has its own effect: adenine provides rate of fire, cytosine increases accuracy, guanine distributes damage to enemies, and Timin gives explosive force.

These combinations allow gamers to create a hybrid bio -weapon, which is specially adjusted to the obstacles facing them. The variety of opportunities for each combination opens up many strategies, so there are no two identical tasks. To survive here means to get acquainted with six modes, each of which has unique difficulties and depth.
