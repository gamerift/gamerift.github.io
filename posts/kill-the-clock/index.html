Game Description#

Kill the Clock immerses players in the role of the vengeful protagonist, moving along the gloomy European streets of the mid-1900s and finding that his sworn enemy is already killed by an unknown attacker. Accused of this atrocity, the player must reveal the riddle of the death of his enemy. The investigation occurs inside the repeating temporary cycle, forcing the protagonist to live the same seven -day to live again, while each round offers another option for a deprivation of life, which must be unraveled before the time is overturned, in the intricacies of insidious deception.

In Kill The Clock, gamers create the characteristics of their hero, and then use throws of playing bones to influence actions and promotion of history. Inspection of various places in the city helps to collect evidence in the case, as well as interact by conversations and tracking the alleged culprits. The process of interrogation implies the identification of dubious moments in conversations and presenting facts to open the hidden truth; Supernatural reading of thoughts helps to understand the true mood of the enemy, giving superiority.

The structure of this game guarantees that with every reversing of time, although the victim always meets his end, the personality of the criminal and the method of reprisals change, offering a constantly changing detective puzzle. Such a repeated investigative process makes it possible to recognize previously hidden circumstances. Any episode contains several differing finals depending on the elections of the actor, his actions and the prevailing relations with the characters, which leads to the opening of large -scale intrigues or to become another victim in a cunning action.
