Game Description#

Creativerse is a sandbox that you can play online with players from all over the world or join forces with a friend for free on a pirate to jointly explore the vast cubic expanses of the vibrant open world. By cooperating online online, you can fight different monsters together, collect resources, build cities, brew potions and much more. Network play allows you to join a team of several people. You can both participate in joint adventures and fight in tense confrontations.

A huge number of picturesque biomes are presented. Actions take place on land, in dungeons, in the air and on water - absolutely any area of ​​the map is available for exploration. The free-to-play option in Creativerse invites participants to engage in cooperative building of their own settlement by constructing buildings from blocks. Each piece of land contains unique types of resources. Treasures are hidden in dungeons guarded by various mythical creatures.

Together we can build an absolutely unique world. The game has 400 items that can be used as individual resources or used in construction. Multiplayer allows you not only to save and share your worlds, but also to travel to other people’s universes. After the character dies, all collected items remain in the backpack. Instead of standard axes, pickaxes or hoes, heroes use a special glove. Important places can be marked with beacons and a teleport can be used to quickly move between locations.
