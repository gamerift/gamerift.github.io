Game Description#

Reknum Cheri Dreamland starts an adventure in which Cheri’s heroine awakens from fantasies and falls into a real conflict. Her wanderings in the world of dreams will force to encounter materialized nightmares that threaten the kingdom. Progress depends on the passage of the thematic stages and the deciphering of poetic instructions of the ancestors. The study of the environment prepares for a deeper immersion in the main tests and plot lines of this game, conducting the characters through a series of difficult locations and battles.

In the Reknum Cheri Dreamland project, the player’s promotion is tied to the action and study of 8 unique areas. The key role in overcoming difficulties is played by the discovery of new weapons and pumping skills through an automatic system of passive skills. This mechanism helps to prepare for fights with strong bosses. Each location makes it possible to find prey that increases the overall power of the character and his readiness for subsequent tense battles that determine the main conflicts and move the narrative.

The main gameplay consists of overcoming difficult platform levels requiring honed actions. The function of auto -security records progress in these tests, automatically documenting a journey. The narrative is submitted through the voice acting and interpretation of the mysterious messages leading the heroine through the world of the dreams. The successful development of these zones and the victory over powerful enemies lead to the genuine denouement of this game adventure, revealing the final of the Cheri mission to save the kingdom.
