Game Description#

Worbital is a space arcade game designed for online play on the Internet, allowing a pirate to join forces with a friend. You will need to move around orbit, controlling the planet. To play online, you need to choose your continent, which revolves around the sun. The planet is available for free, and all guns that are installed on the surface are purchased for victory points. The higher the level and the more points collected, the more powerful weapons you can purchase to defend the land.

The gameplay is represented by planetary battles in real time. The possibility of free online play allows you to establish dominance over the galaxy. To do this you need to destroy all enemies in the solar system. The online game Worbital is built on mechanics such as gravity, launching projectiles across the galaxy, etc. You can release asteroids, create black holes, and blind your opponents with solar flares. When an enemy planet explodes, it can spawn several small continents. But it’s not so scary if the Sun explodes.

The developers guarantee pseudoscientific accuracy. In other words, there is nothing here that could connect the game with realism. The set of weapons is very extensive and large-scale: Railguns, Lasers, Planetary Rams, Magnets, Orbital Boosters, Shield Defenses, Warships, Colonizers and many other ridiculous weapons. The gameplay is pure chaos and chaos. It is necessary to protect your planet from enemies in every possible way and try to destroy all opponents, becoming the most highly developed civilization in the universe!
