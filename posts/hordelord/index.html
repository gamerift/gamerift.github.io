Game Description#

Hordelord throws a player on the closed arenas against the consistent waves of hostile creatures. Gamers use destructive weapons and high mobility to move the location and reprisals against opponents. The task is to score the maximum number of points for getting into the world tables of leaders, relying on accurate shooting and a system for fixing murders. The gameplay is distinguished by constant violence, concentrating on the suppression of opponents with speed and fire power.

Each skirmish requires accuracy and understanding of the situation for successful advance forward. The structure of the game process Hordelord determines two main modes. The Horde mode proposes to pass the final number of enemy attacks with increasing complexity, while the endless regime is a test of endurance before an unlimited onslaught. The arsenal includes six slaughter types of firearms, each with its own tactical features and improved alternative attacks for even greater bloodshed.

In order to configure the combat style deeper, you can use tarot cards, giving a variety of amplifiers and powerful combinations that complement various tactics and discover new capabilities in battle. Navigation by the battlefield is carried out using a multiplieg boots, a ghostly shovel and springboard locations, allowing to maintain an impulse and dodge the attacks. The game encourages certain styles of fighting, rewarding with spectacular reprisals and complex tricks by tangible bonuses. The arenas inhabit many types of opponents with unique strengths and weaknesses, as well as a special model of behavior.
