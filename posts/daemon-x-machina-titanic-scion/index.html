Game Description#

Daemon X Machina: Titanic Scion offers to pilot a fully customized fur arsenal around the open world of an alien planet, participating in battles on Earth and in the air. Gamers examine a variety of locations, including plains and mountains, moving on foot in armor, in flight or riding a horse. The gameplay is built on the confrontation with mechanical and organic enemies in order to extract their weapons and equipment. The resulting components are used to improve fur capabilities, which expands tactical options in future battles.

Daemon X Machina: Titanic Scion can go through the gloomy scientific and fiction story of Daemon X Machina or in the company of up to two partners in an online cooperative. The multiplayer regime applies to the main storyline and to fights with huge bosses. Teams of 3 players can be combined to jointly defeat these formidable opponents. This action offers battles with a third -person view, focusing on dynamic clashes with Slasher elements and Beat ‘Em Up.

The key feature of the project is the deep customization of the arsenal - an agile costume. A change in its appearance and weapons is available by creating new parts or embedding elements obtained from enemies. Such a system allows you to form a high -tech warrior that is ideal for the individual style of play. The gameplay combines the collection of trophies and the improvement of equipment, as in Dungeon Crawler, with the study of the open world as part of the continuous PVE-Hall.

Key aspects of your car:

Deep customization of a combat costume using craft and trophies. Free study of the world on foot, in flight or riding. Dynamic battles with mechanical and organic opponents. The joint passage of the plot and the battle with the bosses online.
