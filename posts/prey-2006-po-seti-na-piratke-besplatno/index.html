Game Description#

Prey features a multiplayer mode that allows you to play with a friend online, competing for free on a pirate in multiplayer battles over the Internet online or locally. The online game includes several modes with their own unique mechanics and tasks. The possibility of free online play offers you to fight with a variety of small arms, as well as control gravity.

Prey’s single-player campaign tells the story of Domasi Tavodi, aka Cherokee or simply Tommy. Being a former soldier in the past and an ordinary mechanic in the present, the hero lives an ordinary life in Oklahoma. However, a strange event occurs, as a result of which the hero Prey finds himself on a spaceship. The action takes place in a surreal world called the Land of the Ancients, as well as on the Sphere shuttle. In addition to guns and the ability to change gravity, Tommy is helped by a tame hawk named Talon. Each planet has its own unique gravity, which greatly affects the gameplay.

You can control vehicles, including a variety of aircraft. They have a gravity beam and energy cannons. There are sections with spatial puzzles. To restore health, you will need to use spores and reservoirs. In addition to the main difficulty, there is an additional one called Cherokee. This is a slightly modified and more difficult mode, where if the player dies, he will need to start the passage from the very beginning.
