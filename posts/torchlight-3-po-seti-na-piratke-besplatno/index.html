Game Description#

Torchlight 3 is an action game with role-playing elements that you can play online with a friend, fighting together for the Emberite Empire for free on a pirate site. By uniting over the Internet, online or locally, you need to save Novastraye, protecting it from the Netherim. Online play focuses on player interaction, allowing participants to choose from four classes with their own individual skills.

The possibility of free online play offers you to play as a Sniper, Sunset Mage, Mechanoid and Relmaster. Each character has its own unique place in the team. Before you start playing, Torchlight 3 allows you to choose special relics that will help you unlock more powerful skills for your heroes. An important mechanic in Torchlight 3 is the creation of forts, which are analogues of hubs into which the protagonists find themselves after each completed stage.

Here you can improve your equipment, get pets, decorate the interior of the room and much more. By going through the story campaign in the forts, the dungeons of Fazir become available. This is a kind of endgame, built on the principle of a bagel. Pet mechanics have been carried over from previous installments. Animals can travel with the heroes and help both in battle and in exploring locations. Pets come in a variety of styles and abilities. Their skills can also be enhanced with the help of found items. You can collect wards and change them during the passage.
