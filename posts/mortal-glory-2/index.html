Game Description#

Mortal Glory 2 is a fantasy RPG in the Roguelike genre, which combines a step -by -step tactical battle and team management. It is proposed to be in the role of the leader of the team of wandering gladiators, and your ultimate goal is to take part in the tournament, the victory in which guarantees glory and wealth. One of the main features of the game is the variety of available races, each of which has its own unique advantages and disadvantages. You have the opportunity to recruit members of the detachment from different places, equip them with powerful booty and train them to become formidable fighters. The decisions that you make regarding the travels of your team are crucial, since each place offers its own challenges and opportunities.

When it comes to a fight, Mortal Glbuild.13694042ry 2 offers rapid and strategic experience. You can deftly use the environment to get an advantage over opponents, whether it is rolling barrels, using blood magic or launching chain reactions. Each arena has its own layout, objects and special rules that can significantly affect the outcome of your battles. Adaptation and creative approach are key factors of victory over enemies. Due to the fact that the game is generated randomly, each passage offers unique experience. Experimentation with various tactics, races and equipment combinations will help you find the optimal strategy.
