Game Description#

On the pirateCompany of Heroes you have the opportunity to fight online online, commanding a personal platoon of soldiers. You can play online for free with a friend, facing off in duel or multiplayer matches. Network play allows you to join forces with partners, fighting in teams. Each participant’s army is divided into three directions: infantry, paratroopers and a tank unit. The Americans use defensive equipment, while the Germans fight with offensive units.

Players can distribute weapons to their units. After death, units leave their weapons. Other soldiers in the COH are capable of picking up weapons on the battlefield, including special weapons. The fighters' skills are upgraded to the third level. Parameters such as rate of fire, damage, speed, health, armor and viewing range are presented. Units are replenished near barracks, field camps, or support trucks.

All platoons have several important parameters: health, morale and cover. The last parameter affects how carefully troops move across the field. Introduced the possibility of free online play for up to 8 people and a single player campaign. The Company of Heroes storyline includes events such as D-Day, the Battle of Carentan, the Battle of Cherbourg, Operation Cobra, the Sack of Falaise and other important historical battles.

The main characters are three heroes: Captain John McKay - the commander-in-chief of the Able company, Sergeant Joe Conti - the narrator of the story and Hauptmann Joseph Gunther Schultz - the German commander of the Leer division and the operator of the Tiger-ace tank. Before going into battle, it is necessary to obtain three types of resources: weapons, fuel and personnel. The latter is used for the construction of buildings, training of troops, design of armored vehicles, etc. Weapons are needed to develop grenade launchers, call in artillery troops and conduct airstrikes. Resources are limited in quantity.
