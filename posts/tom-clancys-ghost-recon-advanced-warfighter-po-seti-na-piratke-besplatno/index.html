Game Description#

Tom Clancy’s Ghost Recon: Advanced Warfighter allows you to play online with a friend or team up with up to 4 people to fight in teams or against artificial intelligence. By participating in online matches over the Internet, you can customize your fighter by choosing weapons and equipment. The network game is also represented by a story-based co-op. The action takes place in 2013. At the center of the story is the Ghost squad, whose task is to capture several particularly dangerous war criminals. The free multiplayer feature allows you to play all the story campaign missions, such as Coup d’etat, Ready for bear, Quarterback and others.

A wide arsenal of weapons is available. There are a number of assault rifles (MR-C, M8 Carbine, G36), snipers (M99 Sniper and HK MSG90), machine guns (MK48 LMG and HK MG21), submachine guns (M8 Compact and MP5SSD) and pistols (M9 and G18). Depending on the faction, terrorists or special forces, the types of guns may change. Tom Clancy’s Ghost Recon: Advanced Warfighter is a tactical first-person shooter. During combat operations, you can give orders to your partners, use cover and the environment. Each team member performs his own function and is equipped with unique weapons. The enemies are both ordinary military personnel and heavy equipment. For example, a combat helicopter or infantry fighting vehicle. To shoot them down you will need heavy weapons. Mexico City is recreated based on a real map, and the locations have grown in scale compared to previous games in the Tom Clancy series.
