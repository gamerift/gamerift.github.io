Game Description#

Play online inArmed and Gelatinous: Couch Edition, you can engage in a crazy space battle with a friend for free on a pirate. This multiplayer party game features fast-paced battles between blobs across the Internet. You’ll get a variety of weapons as you transform your cute little ball into a chubby strongman. Compromise? You become slower and hit your target easier. Dodge bullets and plan your attacks - size isn’t everything!

Need a break from shooting? Team up and compete in fun sports mini-games like Blob Soccer and Dodgeball. This is the perfect option for your next get-together and is guaranteed to spark laughter and some friendly competition.

The game offers a lot of variety. Choose from 9 unique game modes, each with its own twist. There are 16 weapons at your disposal to wreak havoc with, as well as special item boxes to spice up the game. Complete 16 levels and unlock many bonuses, from new weapons to entire game modes.

Feeling brave? Challenge your friends or fight against artificial intelligence on different difficulty levels. A stunning soundtrack featuring 8 incredible artists will keep you energized.

Here are just a few points: Deathmatch allows you to fight to reach a certain number of points. In the Time Match game, the most points are awarded at the end of the round. In Stock Match you have to fight for the last globe. Want to score combos? Play Combo King! Blob Dojo is a sandbox for practicing techniques.

The fun doesn’t end there. Play Blob Soccer, where hilarious Dash attacks are an integral part of the game. In Blob Bombardment, dodgeball takes on a sci-fi feel. In Blob Blitz, football takes on a high-speed form. And in the unique Food Fight game you can fight for supremacy over tacos.

With plenty of customization options, you can customize the game to your liking. Set the score for victory, the round timer or the number of lives. You can even create custom weapon sets for weird matches like Missiles Only. So grab your friends, choose a mode and get ready for exciting space blob showdowns!
