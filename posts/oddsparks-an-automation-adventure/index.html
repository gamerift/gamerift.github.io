Game Description#

ODDSPARKS: An Automation Adventure combines the elements of automation and real -time strategy, focusing on both veterans and beginners in the automation genre. Players explore the strange fantasy world, solve ancient secrets, build automated workshops and go to adventure with their charming sparks. The main gameplay rotates around the design and automation of workshops.

Forget about conveyor ribbons - you have to control the movement of sparks in order to optimize the work of your creations. A wide set of tools will allow you to solve logistics puzzles at various distances and heights. The procedurally generated world attracts players to explore him along with their sparkles. Passing through a variety of biomes, you will encounter unique enemies, such as spanny and bilephents.

Value resources are stored in forgotten ruins that need to be found and included in the workshop network. The knowledge gained contributes to further research and change the world around him. ODDSPARKS offers both solitary and cooperative online groups for four players. A simple system allows you to easily join friends or organize a game with them. Build large -scale automated networks for effective transportation of goods and sparks. Powerful add -ons of the track will allow you to give your spark accurate logistics instructions. Conquer huge distances and towering mountains to achieve your goals.

Perform the tasks of the villagers to open new workshop buildings and see how your village flourishes. Explore the world filled with unique bioms, unusual enemies and forgotten ruins. Open the capabilities of customization of the characters to express your personal style. The game offers management using a gamepad and mouse and keyboard, which allows you to satisfy various preferences of the players. The affordable nucleus is supplemented by functions such as customary management, which provides a smooth course of the game for everyone. And yes, you can stroke sparks!
