Game Description#

Enigma of Fear is a title that puts gamers in the shoes of Mia, a paranormal investigator on a personal mission: to find her missing father. Finding themselves in the Perimeter, a place that exists outside of reality, Mia and her dog Loopy are plunged into the unknown, where nothing is as it seems. The Perimeter is shrouded in mystery, filled with strange happenings, sinister creatures and a web of secrets that Mia must unravel. As she delves deeper into the mystery, players must guide her through a series of clues, investigations, and encounters that will challenge both her intelligence and courage.

The structure revolves around the core mechanics of investigations. Mia’s detective skills are put to the test as she collects documents, examines evidence, and takes detailed notes on the clues she uncovers. The investigation system is dynamic and requires users to sift through a lot of information. Mia’s mental map serves as a tool to organize these clues, helping her connect the dots and uncover hidden truths.

But it’s not just about finding the clues, it’s about understanding their significance in a larger, more mysterious narrative. What really sets Enigma of Fear apart is its non-linear approach to gameplay. Users are not locked into a specific path, but are free to explore the open world as they see fit. Some will decide to come face to face with terrible paranormal creatures, relying on their fighting skills and quick thinking. Others may choose a more cautious path.
