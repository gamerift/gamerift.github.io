Game Description#

Terrordrome - Reign of the Legends is a dynamic and bloody fighting game, the action of which takes place in an alternative version of our reality, where characters from books, urban legends, cryptozoology, mythology and folklore exist. All heroes of Terrordrome are monsters and villains. The game shows a hypothetical world where all the scary stories heard turned out to be true. There is a story campaign for Alex Van Helsing. You have to defeat Dracula and send him to another world once and for all.

The roster of fighters includes such famous personalities as Frankenstein’s Monster, Bigfoot, Bloody Mary, It, Mister Hyde, White Lady Dracula, Wendigo, etc. Several original monsters designed specifically for Terrordrome - Reign of the Legends are available. Each hero has its own unique characteristics and techniques. All characters have several types of attacks, a special attack, a block, a dodge and a grab. In addition to the story campaign and version mode, the developers have added training where you can study the fighter’s abilities and master all the techniques. The game includes: many unique characters with their own characteristics; 5 stages in arcade mode; battles against other players or artificial intelligence; online and local play; training mode.
