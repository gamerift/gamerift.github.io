Game Description#

InSmalland: Survive the Wilds the player takes on the role of a hero who has been diminished under unexplained circumstances. The protagonist woke up in the forest, not knowing whether other people had the same fate. With a head full of questions, he begins a struggle whose goal is not only to find out the truth, but, above all, to survive in a new inhospitable world.

The player watches what is happening from the first person. Survival aspects will be unusual due to the hero’s small size. During the adventure, you will have to travel through a huge forest, which is a living ecosystem - the creatures living in it pose a threat to the main character, not only because they can treat him as food, but also because they can steal the supplies that he has accumulated for later life. However, we are not talking about wolves, bears or other predators, but about animals that are not usually associated with danger, such as squirrels and rabbits (much larger than the hero) and ants (whose colonies can destroy the fruits of his hard work).

You need to take care of both food for the hero and raw materials for the production of tools and weapons, as well as the resources needed to build a shelter. But even if you manage to provide him with shelter, you should still be careful - ordinary rains, drops of which harm the character, will also become problematic, not to mention downpours that cause floods. Another danger is the wind, which blows away a shelter built in a bad place. Although you can play alone, the authors have implemented an extended co-op mode in which up to seven other players can fight simultaneously.
