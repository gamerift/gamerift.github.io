Game Description#

The co-op adventureTick Tock A Tale for Two allows you to play online with a friend for free on pirate. Together with your partner you will find yourself in a strange world. A network game allows you to interact online over the Internet using voice messages. The main goal is to solve puzzles against time. Only by interacting can you find a way out of this world and unravel the secret of the mystical universe.

The locations are filled with mysteries and obstacles. You can play through the campaign alone, or use the free online play option to fully experience the gameplay mechanics of Tick Tock A Tale for Two. It will take from 2.5 to 3 hours of real time to complete the story campaign. Players will need to witness a deep and atmospheric story inspired by Norse myths.

The controls are intuitive and simple. The project has a low entry barrier. Alone or with a partner, you need to explore the watchmaker’s shop, go down to the basement, look for secrets in dusty rooms, etc. The universe created by watchmaker Amalia Ravn. The girl filled this world with a huge number of puzzles and riddles that were generated by her imagination. During the passage, the heroes will need to learn the story of the creator and collect many interesting facts about this unusual universe. A huge emphasis is placed on creation and narrative.
