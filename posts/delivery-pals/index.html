Game Description#

Delivery Pals appoints players the role of cat couriers in the last operating company on Earth. The planet is ruins abandoned by former inhabitants, with a corrosive atmosphere, anomalies and monsters. Alien creatures have become the only visitors who arrive at this world for certain dishes. Using thermal drums, your group must fulfill instructions and receive income so that the business does not crash in hostile conditions.

The main mission in Delivery Pals is survival, since the former people of people are actively striving to stop the activities of the enterprise. They systematically increase quotas and try to exterminate the rest of their lives on the planet, including the deliveryers. For navigation in this environment, players use a modified train that serves a mobile base for a crew up to 7 people. This headquarters contains a computer for processing orders, an ATM for financial management and camera monitors for observing partners in the field.

Each trip begins with the processing of the order and subsequent search for the necessary food. The ingredients can be detected in garbage bags, containers or mined from the environment, for example, staring an egg from a nest for making an omelet. The crew can use the kitchen table on the train for cooking if the desired item is not found. Accuracy is a key element, because the supply of a wrong treat to the client provokes an immediate attack on the courier.

Key elements of your dangerous trip:

Delivery of orders demanding alien customers. The study of post -apocalyptic land, complete anomalies and monsters. Using a modified train as a mobile headquarters. Preparation of food from found or obtained ingredients.
