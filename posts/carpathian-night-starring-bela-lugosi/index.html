Game Description#

In Transylvania, Carpathian Night Starring Bela Lugosi immerses in the world of 1451, captured by the army of Prince Dracula. The horde of zombies, werewolves and demonic creatures threatens to establish eternal night on Earth. To confront this darkness, two champions appear, ready to perform justice. Abbot Dorin, a monk-warrior, unites with Irina Dracula-a vampire-pyroman and a descendant of the very lord to whom they hunt. Their task is to drop the army of the dead and challenge Dracula in his own castle.

Players have to cross a two -dimensional world with lateral scrolling, passing through insidious locations and fighting with the legions of monsters. The game process Carpathian Night Starring Bela Lugosi is focused on the destruction of opponents and evading deadly traps surrounded. Promotion in the plot is marked by fights with powerful Dracula commanders. The path of the protagonists lies through the lands teeming with monsters and the dangerous pass of Borgo, ultimately leading to the gates of the castle. In its walls of heroes, final and most serious trials await.

This game offers a linear passage in the Gothic atmosphere. As they move, the details of the world revealing the secret history of the encountered creatures and adding the context of the environment. The narrative elements are woven into the action, while the heroes delve into enemy territory. The final goal is to get to the legendary vampire in his generic nest and enter into direct confrontation with him in order to put an end to his dark rule and save the land.
