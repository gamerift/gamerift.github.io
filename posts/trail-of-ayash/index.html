Game Description#

The world of Trail of Ayash is based on the myths and legends of the inhabitants. An open game location allows you to freely explore the area and learn more about the life of Native Americans. The player will encounter various tribes competing with each other, participating in diplomacy, organizing wars and expansion. The main character is Ayash, a respected man in his own faction. One day, a close person frames Ayash, and after that he is expelled from the tribe.

By controlling an exile, the user builds his own story and decides his own further actions. The world is open, and procedurally generated events take place in it. Tribes fight among themselves, and many locations will need to be explored, secrets unraveled, or secondary missions completed. In addition to the fact that the central character survives with his fellow tribesmen from the horrors of ordinary life, they will face nightmares that awaken under the cover of darkness.

The continent is home to many creatures born from the crossing of human minds and spirits. Monsters scare souls, muffle voices and attack, sucking the life out of ordinary people. Local monsters have their own methods of fighting, way of life and abilities. You can enter into dialogue with each NPC, take on a task, or find out information. Many factions are friendly, while others prefer to fight strangers and start wars. The protagonist will often fight and face deadly difficulties.
