Game Description#

PULSAR: Lost Colony is a cooperative space simulator in which you will control a star shuttle and explore outer space. Players take on a specific role on the ship: captain, pilot, scientist, weapons specialist, or engineer. Only coordinated work will lead to victory over the enemy.

While traveling through the universe, you need to scan the planets for life or useful resources. There will always be dangers along the way, so it is possible to launch a warp jump to avoid a collision and escape from the upcoming battle. The decisions will be up to the captain. The PULSAR: “Lost Colony” challenge is an attempt to bring to the simulator the full experience of managing an entire space station. There are many possibilities in the game:

command or control a space shuttle; choose your professional activity on the ship; participate in intense battles; visiting other people’s ships to greet or conquer the shuttle; collect loot on planets and throughout the galaxy.

Gameplay PULSAR: Lost Colony contains three dialogue system options: diplomacy, trade and intimidation. Contains flexible customization of the artificial intelligence of the crew for players exploring the vastness of space alone. There is an “Academy” containing a glossary and a textbook telling about the structure of the world and those who inhabit it. The game supports Oculus Rift and HTC Vive virtual reality headsets.
