Game Description#

The online gameTram Simulator Urban Transit invites you and your friend to play online for free and enjoy operating a tram online online. Take a trip to Angel Shores, a city pulsating with North American life. Take on the role of driver and operator of one of six unique trams, from sleek moderns to timeless classics, and cruise past busy streets and famous landmarks without spending a dime on a pirate ride.

Transport passengers to various stops while being aware of traffic and following traffic rules. Each tram boasts its own distinct driving experience, making every ride a new experience. Players face dynamic weather, day and night cycles, and various road obstacles. Mastering the art of driving a tram leads to in-game profits that allow you to run a tram company.

Tram Simulator Urban Transit offers strategic depth. Plan routes, manage schedules and strategically expand your tram network. Take control, add new stops and upgrade your fleet to optimize passenger flow.

Play the way you want. Immerse yourself in the game or learn all the intricacies in the Driving School tutorial. Choose between Story Mode for missions, Career Mode for immediate company expansion, or Sandbox Mode for pure driving and planning freedom. Multiplayer is enticing, allowing you to collaborate or compete with other tram enthusiasts across multiple platforms.
