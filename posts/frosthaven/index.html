Game Description#

Frosthaven is a digital adaptation of a board game that puts players at the head of a detachment of mercenaries in the northern settlement. This tactical role strategy uses step -by -step battles on a hexagonal map. You can go through the campaign missions alone or in an online cooperative with three other gamers. The action is unfolding on a border known by harsh conditions and dangerous inhabitants, constantly challenging the survival of the group.

The basis of the gameplay is the management of the batch of adventurers, where every hero performs his own combat function. At the start, a choice is available from 6 grades, including Banner Spear, Boneshaper and Drifter, with the opening of new ones as they move. In the Frosthaven Title, combat operations depend on the system of maps of abilities dictating the effects of characters in each move. Success in contractions with numerous enemies and bosses requires a balance between risk and a potential reward through thorough resource management.

In addition to individual combat clashes, responsibility for the development of the central outpost lies on the shoulders. The process includes the construction and modernization of various buildings that open up new game capabilities and methods of supporting the party. The events of the project unfold in a world with a variety of biomas subject to a change in seasons. A narrative of more than 130 tasks directs a journey through these lands, and decisions made directly affect the progress and development of the settlement.
