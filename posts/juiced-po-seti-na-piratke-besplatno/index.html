Game Description#

The pirate gameJuiced can be played online for free, taking part in street races with players from all over the world. Championships are available online online. Local multiplayer allows you to compete with a friend on the same screen. Both a single-player campaign and an online game are presented, including many different modes.

The story-driven adventure has one goal: defeat 8 street racing leaders in each of the eight districts. You need to get a car and assemble your own gang of racers to participate in team races, which are also included in the free online game. Cars are represented by classes. Some races require you to increase your reputation level. Events take place in Los Angeles. It consists of areas that are controlled by various street racing groups. Juiced areas: Angel North Central - an area with a stadium and an oil rig; Campbell Hills - a rural area located far from the city; DownTown - the center of the metropolis with the San Gabriel River; East Andel Island - a port consisting of trade and warehouse territories; San Ricardo - massive residential complex; Southside Beach - business district with the City of Angels beach; West Anderson - poor neighborhoods; Angel Westside - industrial area.

The Angel Superspeedway location, represented by a second stadium, is also available. At the beginning of the game, you can organize your own competitions here. There is a track called Eight. Races include Ring, Sprint, Stunt and Drag events. The latter mode uses only a manual transmission. Only 4 riders can take part. Stunts mode takes place without opponents. You need to engage in a controlled drift and earn style points. The fleet consists of 52 licensed vehicles from manufacturers such as Honda, Toyota, Chevrolet, etc. They are divided into 8 classes and control type: rear-wheel drive, front-wheel drive and all-wheel drive. The possibility of styling and tuning is available.
