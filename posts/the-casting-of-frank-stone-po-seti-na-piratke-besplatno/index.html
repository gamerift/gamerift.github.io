Game Description#

The Casting of Frank Stone immerses players in the eerie atmosphere of Cedar Hills, Oregon, with online play and the ability to play online with a friend. This horror game, which can be played for free on pirates, takes place in the Dead by Daylight universe and expands its lore. Players follow a group of friends as they explore online the dark past of Frank Stone, a brutal killer whose crimes left an indelible mark on the city. The story is set in a steel mill where nightmares and reality merge.

Supermassive Games has brought its storytelling talents to the table, creating a story that will keep you up at night. You will make difficult decisions left and right, and each choice will affect the fate of the inhabitants of Cedar Hills. The game is not for the faint of heart - along with fears, there are also emotional blows. One minute you’re contemplating a simple solution, and the next you’re staring down the barrel of a heartbreaking dilemma. Let’s talk about finding yourself between a rock and a hard place!

The game never stops trying to immerse itself in the atmosphere. You’ll find yourself shoulder-deep in a world that’s equal parts familiar and fantastically confusing. It’s like walking into a mirror version of a small American town, where every shadow could be hiding something unpleasant. And for fans of Dead by Daylight, this is a chance to look behind the curtain and get acquainted with the wider world of this universe. Just remember that curiosity killed the cat, and in Cedar Hills it can do much worse to you.
