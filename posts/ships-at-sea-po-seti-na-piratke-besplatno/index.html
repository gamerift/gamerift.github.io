Game Description#

Playing online in Ships At Sea, the next generation of ship simulators, with a friend on a pirate and completely free, you can take the helm of a variety of ships, from service boats to cargo ships, and navigate the stunning open world of the Norwegian islands. Play online online and engage in commercial fishing, transport cargo by sea or complete official tasks - the online game offers you all the possibilities of the ocean.

Calling all captains! Ships At Sea answers your needs by introducing service and cargo ships for the first time. Team up with friends online or go solo to build your career in the pristine Norwegian Sea. Buckle up for dynamic weather - rain, fog and storms will add another layer of challenge to your travels.

Ships At Sea is not limited to just waves. Explore the meticulously recreated harbors of Lofoten, Norway. Stock up on supplies, upgrade your ship at the shipyard, and keep it in working order between missions. The game is authentic - accurate maps, licensed ships and realistic equipment will put you in the captain’s chair.

Get ready to dive in! Ships At Sea uses the latest technology to create a truly immersive experience. State-of-the-art graphics and physically accurate buoyancy systems bring your ship to life. Support for various upscaling technologies ensures smooth gameplay, and color adjustment ensures everyone can enjoy the view. Are you ready to become the master of the seas? Set sail with Ships At Sea!
