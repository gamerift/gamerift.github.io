Game Description#

Rainbow Six: Siege is a tactical shooter that allows you to compete with a friend or random players online. Rainbow 6: Siege gives you the opportunity to play online for free on pirates in asymmetrical matches between attack aircraft and defenders. Network play takes place in a limited space. The main feature is that the buildings are completely susceptible to destruction. You can blow up walls, ceilings, furniture, etc.

Destruction is modeled based on impact force, weapon caliber, etc. The interactive environment must be used for tactical purposes. The free online play feature in Tom Clancy’s Rainbow Six Siege allows you to compete in several multiplayer competitions: regular, ranked and unranked. Ratings award awards and titles, while non-rating competitions allow you not to risk your reputation. Battles take place with the participation of two teams of five people. Three main online modes: Hostage - stormtroopers must save the hostage by breaking through the barricades and traps of the defensive team; Capture a position - you need to capture and hold a container with chemical weapons, trying to prevent enemies from breaking in for 10 seconds; Charge - you need to bring activate the deactivator or kill all members of the opposing faction.

10 single-user Operations available. They are intended to teach players basic mechanics and represent the abilities of Operators. Anti-Terror matches available. They require you to complete various cooperative missions for attack and defense. The opponents are fighters controlled by artificial intelligence. A special feature of the mode is the randomization of locations, time of day, tasks and other elements. Each unit is represented by recruits and experienced operatives. The former do not have any special equipment, while the latter use advanced tools for attack and defense.
