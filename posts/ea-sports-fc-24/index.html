Game Description#

EA SPORTS FC 24 is designed to revolutionize virtual football. With an impressive roster of over 19,000 licensed athletes, over 30 leagues, and recreations of over 100 iconic stadiums, it’s a fan’s dream. At the heart of FIFA 24 is HyperMotion V, a revolutionary technology that uses volumetric data collected from cameras strategically placed around stadiums during more than 180 top-tier matches. Thanks to this technology, an unreal level of realism is achieved. From natural and fluid movements of virtual athletes to realistic reactions and emotions, HyperMotion V takes gameplay to unprecedented heights. It also improves tactical skill, making them more intuitive in understanding the game and their teammates.

A distinctive feature of EA SPORTS FIFA 23 is the PlayStyles system, which uses Opta data to highlight players based on their unique skills. Individual PlayStyles reflect the strengths and weaknesses of the players on the field. Some athletes may have high speed and agility, while others may have accuracy, strength or passing. The simulator offers a variety of modes to suit different preferences. In Ultimate Team mode, for the first time, you can assemble your dream team, with a mix of both male and female soccer players. The Ultimate Team Evolutions add-on allows you to improve football players by completing tasks, increasing their rating and the number of appearances. In Career Mode, you can step into the shoes of a player or manager, forging your own unique path to glory with the help of a new agent and coaches. For fans of the online modes in Clubs and VOLTA Football, you can team up with friends in crossover matches², customize your Pro with different styles, and much more. EA SPORTS FC 24 usheres in a new era of football gaming, combining cutting-edge technology and rich content to bring fans closer to the real sport than ever before.
