Game Description#

In TerRatech Worlds, you will plunge with your head in a PVE survival with the open world on the mysterious planet of aliens. Put your way as a free prospector, exploring an extensive environment with an area of ​​300 km² with a variety of bioms, dynamic weather and a day/night cycle. To confront the hostile flora and fauna, rival fractions and insidious areas - and at the same time use the power of the planet in their interests.

Combat with five friends for a joint game on the network, going deep into the outlandish forests, hovering over insidious landscapes, overcoming huge rivers and oceans. The world is your canvas, examine it freely, without loading screens and restrictions. Create your fate using an intuitively understandable modular construction system. Create and set up vehicles, bases and huge factories, limited only to your imagination. Make resources or “purchase” them from others to nourish your creativity. Improve and create more and more advanced materials to expand the boundaries of your creations.

Get valuable resources, build effective transport networks and change the landscape using free -form terraforming tools. Automate production lines to achieve maximum efficiency and dominate other people’s boundaries. Wildlife is a harsh teacher filled with enemy “techniques”, which only wait for them to be plundered. Explore new blocks to strengthen the defense, and experiment with specialized combat structures. Unite with colleagues-researchers to raid enemy bases and reflect an impending danger. If you were a lone wolf or team player, Terrathech Worlds offers a unique combination of research, crafting, battle and basic construction. Determine your role, express your style of play and build an empire in a foreign world. The future is for you.
