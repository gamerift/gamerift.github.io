Game Description#

The Best Served Cold puts a gamer at the Knightsp Rack, an underground bar, working during the dry law in the alternative European city of Bukovia. You take on the role of a bartender responsible for the preparation of cocktails for visitors to the institution. Intections go beyond the maintenance, including direct clashes with persons identified as criminals and the need to navigate in dangerous situations.

The main actions include the investigation of the murders taking place in this environment while maintaining the work of the bar. The Best Served Cold gameplay is focused on the disclosure of secrets that customers share with their orders of drinks. You listen to personal stories and observe the behavior to understand the life of the NightKap regulars, collecting the evidence related to current criminal cases. The narrative represents the cut of the Bukov region, including wealthy drinks, corrupt representatives of the police and persons participating in illegal activities who may try to involve you.

The conversation serves as the main tool; You extract information through a cautious dialogue, potentially using different approaches depending on the situation and guest. Building relations with 22 different characters requires evaluating the one who offers help and who poses a threat to you or business in this game. The main storyline unfolds for five separate cases, each of which requires a thorough study of evidence and interrogation of the suspects to advance forward. The successful completion of these investigations is necessary to prevent further harm to people and ensure the survival of the Spikisi.
