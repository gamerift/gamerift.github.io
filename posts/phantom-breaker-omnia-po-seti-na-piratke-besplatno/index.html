Game Description#

Phantom Breaker: Omnia immerses players in an anime action game with the ability to play online with a friend, even as a pirate. This 2D fighting game offers you to fight for free to fulfill your cherished desires by choosing one of 20 unique fighters. Connect online online and immerse yourself in online play, where three fighting styles will allow everyone, whether veteran or newbie, to find their own approach. The story revolves around the mysterious Phantom, who causes chaos in Tokyo by using artifacts from the Fu Dimension to pit young people against each other.

The game features a variety of characters, including all the heroes from its predecessor Phantom Breaker: Extra, as well as several new faces created specifically for this game. Fans of popular anime will enjoy the appearance of Kurisu Makise from Steins;Gate and Rimi Sakihata from Chaos;Head. Players will be able to immerse themselves in the storylines of both the original Phantom Breaker and Extra, gaining complete insight into this dimension-shifting saga.

One of the features of Omnia is a trio of fighting styles - Quick, Hard and Omnia, each of which leaves its own mark on the speed, strength and mechanics of the character. The developers have also worked to reconfigure and rebalance the combat system, making it easy to pull off amazing combos and special moves. For the first time in the series, players can choose between Japanese and English voice acting, and the game’s text is available in multiple languages.
