Game Description#

Frostpunk 2 is a continuation of the survival game, which is based on management and construction. The events of the game take place 30 years after the catastrophe that covered the Earth. Now the planet has experienced severe cooling and people are suffering from constant frosts. In the urban planning simulator, you are asked to try on the role of a hero who, in the process, must lead an entire metropolis and take care of the well-being of local residents.

To this end, the character needs to collect resources and search for all kinds of energy sources. Coal has long been exhausted and people need to focus on extracting oil, the deposits of which are located in the Frostlands. Humanity is constantly looking for different development options, trying to adapt to a changing world. The virtual universe of a city-planning simulator is inhabited by different segments of the population and factions, each of which has different goals. Someone wishes prosperity to the suffering people who managed to survive, and who are ruthlessly trying to seize all remaining resources by force. They have to act in conditions of eternal cold, which gradually brings new external threats to the survivors. The user’s task is to unite the remnants of civilization under his own leadership and develop the city, trying to achieve prosperity in unrealistic and dangerous conditions.
