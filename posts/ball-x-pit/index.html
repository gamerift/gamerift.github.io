Game Description#

BALL x PIT is a dynamic roguelike, the events of which unfold in a world that has experienced the death of the great city of Ballbilon. After a mysterious cataclysm, a bottomless Pit was formed on the site of the metropolis, which attracted adventurers and treasure seekers from all over the world. The heroes will have to descend into its dark depths to fight hordes of enemies with the help of ricocheting balls and find the riches left over from the fallen civilization. Each mission becomes a unique challenge, requiring quick reactions and tactical thinking to survive in constantly changing conditions.

The central element of the mechanics is the Ball Fusion system, which allows you to experiment with more than sixty types of projectiles. By combining them, you can unlock powerful synergies and create hundreds of devastating effects to destroy your opponents. The resources extracted from the Pit are used to develop their own settlement called New Ballbilon. In this title, you can recruit other heroes with unique skills to help you on dangerous expeditions. Successful completion of BALL x PIT is directly related to competent resource management and constant improvement of your base.

What awaits the brave adventurer in the depths of the Pit:

Fight with opponents using the physics of ricocheting projectiles. Create hundreds of unique combinations thanks to the Ball Fusion system. Construction and development of our own base called New Ballbilon. Recruit and upgrade allies with special abilities for expeditions.
