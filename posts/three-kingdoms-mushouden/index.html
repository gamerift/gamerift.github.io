Game Description#

Three Kingdoms Mushouden transfers gamers in 182 of our era, on the threshold of the uprising of yellow dressings. The imperial court is covered by a deep internal confusion and fractional straightforwardness, which interferes with the work of officials, condemning the people into difficult trials. Players choose either a unique hero or a commander from the annals; They have to form troops and conclude alliances. Such preparations are carried out against the background of growing chaos, giving the opportunity to change the course of history.

In Three Kingdoms Mushouden, the characters decide whether to act as single wanderers or apply knowledge of that era to attract many famous military leaders to their side. The plot basis of this title relies on historical notes about the three kingdoms, narratives from the novel about three -dominance and interpretation from modern literary works. The project presents more than 160 historical figures, so it is necessary to maneuver between possible alliances and enmity to complete the tasks.

The game world includes such areas as they saw, Jijiu, Yuzhou, Binzhou, Yanzhou and Qingzhou, offering a total of 60 cities, mainly in the area of the yellow river. The volume of the text exceeds 450,000 words, and such a system provides flexible ways of development of the hero. Misses include the elimination of robbers, receiving remuneration, arrangement of ambush for merchant caravans, confrontation between foreign aggressors and efforts to restore order at court. The battles are aimed at the reconstruction of the battles using historical weapons, starting with local skirmishes for initial strengthening and then moving on to the management of larger army formations.
