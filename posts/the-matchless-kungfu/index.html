Game Description#

The Matchless KungFu immerses users in a world where martial arts are at the forefront. The gamer can choose the path of any warrior he likes and go on a journey through these lands. Be a loner or join a group of like-minded people - you can choose any convenient way. Only the decisions made will shape the character’s story, throwing him into different circumstances, giving him complete freedom of action in certain aspects.

The gameplay in The Matchless KungFu is formally divided into two parts - travel and combat. In the first case, the hero travels through locations, moving from one biome to another. Mountain temples, snow-capped valleys, settlements near water bodies and other places have many differences both in scale and in design. As for the combat, when switching to this mode the camera changes its location. You can watch the action as in a regular fighting game, but in addition to this, the camera rotates around the opponents, showing different perspectives of the battle. Each battle is unique, because all characters are randomly generated. Their life history, habits and fighting style are different. Therefore, if at the first launch one of the enemies seemed like an easy target, then the next one can turn the hero into a laughing stock, leaving not a single chance. But don’t think that there are strict rules here. Any item that comes to hand can be used, from chains to metal pipes. Combined with basic weapons, this can cause a lot of problems for even the healthiest fighters.
