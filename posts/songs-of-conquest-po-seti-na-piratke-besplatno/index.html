Game Description#

Songs of Conquest is a turn-based strategy with the ability to play online with a friend on a pirate for free, controlling powerful sorcerers called Overlords. Alone or online, you will need to go on a journey, wandering through mysterious and gloomy locations. As you travel, you need to engage in battle with numerous enemies and collect powerful artifacts that allow you to upgrade your character’s abilities. The mechanics combine classic TBS, RPG and action-adventure elements.

You will need to collect loot and explore numerous biomes. Online play includes the ability to confront other heroes or travel across lands together. You can control Baria mercenaries, Bards, Warriors and other characters. Each campaign is represented by a unique song that tells about the events of the fantasy world. By collecting resources, you need to build your own empire, properly managing the loot you find and significantly expanding your own borders. The possibility of free online play allows you to team up with up to four people and work together to fight powerful monsters.

The combat is presented in a turn-based system. It is important to find the enemy’s weak points and select individual tactics for the enemy squad. You can participate in intense duels. There is an online editor with which you can create your own maps and scenarios. A wide selection of scripts is available, allowing you to fill locations with events, dialogues, etc. The plot tells about the ancient knights of Arleon opposing the Necromancers of Lot. Mages live in distant swamps and resurrect the dead.
