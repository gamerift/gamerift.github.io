Game Description#

CENTO works according to the system where players introduce commands synchronized with music to activate skills for step -by -step clashes. The basis of this combat mechanics is to follow a certain bit, which corresponds to the combination of cards on the arm and actions of opponents. Success depends on the ability of Gamer to accurately synchronize your actions with a melody in order to use abilities from your deck. This card of cards proposes to use tactical entry of commands, collect gifts and improve skills in the course of passage.

A variety of levels in Cento offers battles to different background music, which directly affects the gameplay. Locations are replaced by city landscapes to ancient ruins, and each zone has a unique soundtrack. The pace and tact of the composition for each level radically change the sequence and timings necessary during the fights. This project is arranged so that the conditions and tactical tasks change depending on the area and the melody accompanying it.

Gamers form a personal deck, freely combining skills and special gifts received from the existing characters of this game. This approach pushes to find unique combinations of abilities to find the most effective tactical schemes for battles. The endurance regimen involves survival in a series of collisions with the simultaneous increase in current characteristics and receiving additional gifts. In this mode, powerful attacks can be carried out by using newly acquired perks against the opponent string.
