Game Description#

He is coming is a world where forgotten evil awakens. The king of demons is rebelled, carrying the promise of death with him. The users have to find powerful artifacts and weapons to resist this formidable enemy. In the afternoon you are exploring the kingdom, collecting resources and honing your skills, and at night you fight off its minions and prepare for the next monstrous test. The clock is constantly ticking, and each solution can mean the difference between survival and destruction.

The Title rotates around a strategic choice, where gamers should adapt to the unique tasks that every new monster set before them. Every three days a new boss appears, each with their strengths and abilities. Understanding their tactics is a key moment. Do you concentrate on increasing speed to deliver the first blow, or make a bet on protection to withstand their powerful blows?

The choice of equipment is crucial: more than 350 items are available in the Title that will help you choose the strategy. Finding the right balance between the attack and the defense is a constant task, and each call gives a new opportunity to improve their approach. In addition to a single game, in He is Coming there is an asynchronous multiplayer mode called Kingmaker. In this mode, users are fighting with other gamers who have developed their own champions assemblies. The bets are high, because you are striving to get the crown of the fallen king of demons.
