Game Description#

GEARS of WAR: Reloaded provides access to the original Title, which passed the procedure for a comprehensive reprint, preserving the essence of the original. This version is distinguished by the introduction of technical improvements, among which it appears to support the image output in resolution 4K and the possibility of reaching the screen update up to 120 frames per second, which ensures the smoothness of the visual series.

The fundamental game process GEARS of War: Reloaded is focused on the use of a shooter mechanic with a third -person view, where the character controls in an environment that depicts in detail the intensity of armed conflicts. Gamers navigate the territories of the world that survived the disaster, which are characterized by oppressive visual decisions and components inherent in the genre of Horror, actively participating in direct clashes.

Gameplay requires participants in strategic thinking when using shelters to defend and attack, as well as direct involvement in dynamic and cruel shootings. This project offers several formats for immersion in its universe, combining a narrative campaign designed in one gamer, with a wide range of cooperative modes and competitive activities. The project opens up an opportunity for the joint passage of the storyline of the campaign; This interaction is available both through the Internet connection with others and in local mode on one console or computer, collecting comrades in the same room.
