Game Description#

On the pirate Kane and Lynch: Dead Men you can play online with a friend, completing the co-op campaign for free. Local connection or online access is available. Kane & Lynch: Suicide Squad can also be played solo. Then artificial intelligence takes on the role of a partner. The network game is aimed at co-op, allowing you to take control of two heroes with completely different personalities.

Kane is a cold and calculating mercenary, and Lynch is a nervous and unstable hero, ready to act aggressively. The single-player campaign and multiplayer are represented by a set of different missions through which one storyline runs. Health is replenished automatically if you sit behind cover. When one of the teammates is wounded, an injection is required to restore the health of the other fighter and save him from death. Although the possibility of a free online game allows you to help out your partner with the help of injections, they cannot be used too often, otherwise an overdose will occur. Characters of Kane and Lynch: Dead Men: Adam Kane Marcus - the main character, who in the past was an ordinary citizen; James Seth Lynch - an unbalanced character who killed his wife at one time; Jennifer Marcus - Kane’s daughter; Seven - a recruitment agency professional killers; Retomoto - the head of the Japanese mafia; Yoko Retomoto - the daughter of an Asian mafia; Team Kane - an organization in which Shelley, Thapa and Rifik enter.

Supplies are replenished from killed enemies. Participants in the game can exchange ammunition. During the passage, computer partners join the heroes. They can be given three commands: attack, follow and move forward. In each chapter you will face different challenges. Protagonists rappel down ropes, hide behind covers, fight enemies, shoot back during a chase, and much more.
