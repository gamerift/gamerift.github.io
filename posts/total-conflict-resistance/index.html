Game Description#

Total Conflict: Resistance is an incredible mixture of global strategy, tactics and an action in the first person that provides incredible battles, many types of armored vehicles, weapons and armor, as well as unique mechanics to immerse you in exciting gameplay. You have to take part in events, manage units, create your army and produce or purchase armored vehicles and weapons. You can also build fortifications, outposts and settlements, manage military operations in the first person, as well as in tactical mode. It is possible to drive various cars, armored personnel carriers, helicopters, tanks and self -propelled guns, and conduct large -scale battles up to 200 people at the same time on tactical cards with a large selection of weapons.

Create your own power or join one of the existing factions and use diplomatic skills to conclude unions or declare war. Grab the provinces to make Cambridge the great again! With a first -person view, you can control your detachments using a large number of command functions, give orders at different levels - one fighter, detachment or the entire unit. If you prefer a tactical regime, you can give orders from a bird’s height of your detachments on the battlefield. The game also contains a detailed map of the island, divided into provinces, as well as foreign and domestic policy, economics, industry and diplomacy.
