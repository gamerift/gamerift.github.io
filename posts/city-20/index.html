Game Description#

City 20 is not an ordinary survival game where you need to search for resources. Of course, there is the usual wasteland after a disaster, the constant struggle for survival, the excitement associated with finding decent scrap metal. But the title adds a twist to the story - your choices rewrite the rules. Every decision you make sends ripples through this broken society, pushing NPCs in new directions and changing the course of events. Think of the butterfly effect, but with more radiation and fewer butterflies. It’s not just about finding the next meal or building a glorified hut.

The project offers you a sandbox overflowing with possibilities. You can play the hero by collecting the remaining fragments of humanity. Or you can go into the shadows, becoming the king (or queen) of scavengers, who has a taste for the most exquisite things of the apocalypse - for example, clean water that does not glow green. The world reacts to what is happening in real time, adapting to your decisions. It’s like a choose-your-own-adventure story, only the stakes are a little higher than being locked in a creepy attic. But here’s the catch: there is no one right way to play City 20. Want to build a utopia powered by rainwater and communal gardens?

Forward. Fancy yourself a post-apocalyptic warlord with a flair for the dramatic? Kill yourself (just avoid irradiated pigeons - trust me). This level of freedom makes every playthrough feel unique, a fresh take on a familiar genre. So if you’re tired of the same old survival simulator routine, this title could be your oasis in the desert. Just remember that every action has a reaction, and in this twisted world, your choices can mean the difference between a prosperous community and… well, a slightly less prosperous community populated by feral pigeons. Just like that.
