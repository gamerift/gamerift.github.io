Game Description#

Emergency 5 is a continuation of the cult rescue simulator, which you can play for free online with a friend as a pirate. An online Internet connection is available, allowing you to play with anyone from anywhere in the world. The sequel improves the engine and makes character or environment animations more realistic. Fire in Emergency 5 spreads smoothly between buildings, setting cars, garbage, and more on fire.

Alone or using free online play, you can explore the realistic streets of Munich, Hamburg and Berlin. An advanced infrastructure system that simulates real life is presented. Cities consist of ports, train stations, cable cars, etc. Emergency incidents can happen anywhere in the location. Multiplayer allows you to team up with a partner to quickly go to save lives, be it putting out a fire or pulling a person out of the snowy foot of a mountain.

Competitive mode available. It is represented by a set of different tasks where players compete on the speed of completing operations. With each subsequent task the difficulty of the levels increases. It is possible to use a firefighting drone, which is able to quickly analyze the situation and report the extent of the disaster. A map editor is introduced, and a wide range of modding tools is included, allowing you to create your own unique maps and scenarios.
