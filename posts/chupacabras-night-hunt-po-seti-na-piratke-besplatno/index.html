Game Description#

Chupacabras Night Hunt is a multiplayer action game with an asymmetrical type of gameplay that allows you to play pirate online for free with a friend or players from all over the world. You can fight each other over the Internet online. The network game is designed for a game of 5 participants. Four players take on the role of hunters, while the fifth player controls the Chupacabra.

The events of Chupacabras Night Hunt take place in a mysterious forest. There are various abandoned buildings here that must be explored to find useful items, keys and supplies for weapons. There are two types of locations: foggy and night. All of them limit visibility, thereby keeping players in constant suspense. It is very important to listen to your surroundings and constantly interact with your partners. The possibility of free online play offers the opportunity to feel like the prey or appear as a powerful hunter capable of tearing a person to pieces.

The gameplay for each side is very different. Factions have side quests. For example, playing as hunters requires you to look for goats lost in the forest, while playing as a Chupacabra requires you to eat them to become stronger. By feeding on flesh, new abilities are unlocked that help you deal with your enemies even more brutally. People also have the opportunity to develop and upgrade in order to more effectively resist the bloodthirsty monster.
