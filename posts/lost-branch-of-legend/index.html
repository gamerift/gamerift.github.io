Game Description#

Touhou: Lost Branch of Legend will transfer you to the fantastic world of Genskoo. Create your deck using mana of different colors, and then fight with famous characters from the Touhou universe. In this Roguelike builder, there is a constant increase in mana from scattered exhibits, which makes each passage of the game unique.

The game can boast of more than 500 unique bullet rains, which accurately recreate the frantic charm of Gennosoko. Do not worry, a convenient barrier will protect you from danger! Take control of the Hakury Raima, the girl of the Hakury Sanctuary, known for appearing at any time when problems arise. If you find a lunar treasure, you can increase donations and attract new believers in the sanctuary! Reima copes with both the attack and the defense, which makes it a universal choice for beginners.

Marisa Kirisame, a magician living in the forest of magic, and a notorious treasure hunter, enters into a fight. The thirst for treasures makes her participation in this incident quite natural. Marisa uses black and red mana, which allows her to cook potions, charge powerful attacks and cool enemies, demonstrating dazzling magic. Sakuya Izaiai, the main maid of the scarlet devil, also goes on stage. Despite his elegant appearance, Sakuya surprisingly willingly get his hands in order to quickly resolve incidents. Manipulations with white and blue mana allow her to throw knives, control time and leave opponents surrounded by a flurry of silver blades.

Even the cirrus, the mischievous fairy from the foggy lake, appears with her friends, seeking inspiration for its drawings. He uses the blue and green mana, which allows her to freeze the enemies, call on the allies and demonstrate the power of the “strongest fairy!” Lost Branch of Legend offers five playable characters, each of which has two options for a starting deck. Players will be able to collect more than 160 exhibits, 500 cards and fight with more than 55 enemies, including 23 recognizable faces from Genskoo. In the game 37 events, each adventure promises to be new and exciting.
