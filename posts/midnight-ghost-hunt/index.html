Game Description#

Midnight Ghost Hunt is a creepy version of hide and seek in which players will have to catch ghosts or, on the contrary, drive away people who disturbed the peace of the spirits. 4v4 confrontation takes place between friends or random users. The main task of the hunters is to catch all the evil spirits with the help of their equipment, and the ghosts are to kill all living things by any means.

A team of paranormal seekers travels around the world in search of interesting and unusual phenomena. They have encountered real evil more than once, so they know how to deal with it. But this matter turned out to be especially difficult, because their opponents turned out to be quite intelligent and cunning. To eliminate all squad members, they use their telepathic abilities after midnight, scattering objects and throwing them at their enemies. They are able to hide in furniture, fly and pass through walls, so the task will definitely not be an easy one. Armament of Midnight Ghost Hunt hunters: Radars - allow you to detect a spirit in the vicinity, squeak when it is nearby and can disrupt their plans to attack from behind. Trap - can be installed anywhere, can slam shut if a spirit gets into it and kills immediately his Vacuum - sucks up the remains of a restless soul and thereby sends it to the next world; Spectral gun - smashes the enemy to smithereens, but very heavy and clumsy

Users will also have other devices at their disposal such as salt, sledgehammers, harpoons, guns and others. Often weapons can be found directly on the map, speeding up the hunting process. There are also special passive skills that allow you to restore health, increase movement speed, etc.
