Game Description#

Paranormal Cleanup is a cooperative horror with a first -person view. You can play it together, three or four via the Internet. There is also a single mode. The characters are controlled through the mouse and the keyboard. In addition, there is support for gamepads, but only partial. This project is distinguished by its concept, gameplay and atmosphere from other indie chorrorists. The players are assigned to the roles of employees of the cleaning company. They were hired for cleaning in an old house with giving. Users can communicate with each other through text and voice chat. In this game, tense moments alternate with comedic episodes.

In the Title called paranormal cleaning there is reiglabia. It is achieved at the expense of the random. Before the start of each new match, the location of objects changes. In addition, there is a system of random events and a dynamic change in complexity. For a successful passage of the map, you need to clean the mansion and do not fall in the ghosts. Special artificial intelligence is responsible for their behavior. The heroes have a scoop and a brush in their hands. They know how to use flashlights, remove locations, interact with interactive objects, as well as raise and throw bags with garbage. Each action of these cleaners can defeat the supernatural inhabitants of the estate. The gameplay is accompanied by immersive and voluminous sound accompaniment.
