Game Description#

Gunner, Heat, PC! - A tank simulator aimed at realistic battles between military equipment from the Cold War. The gameplay focuses on realistic behavior of each tank. Each type of combat transport has unique management features, and is also represented by individual characteristics. Gunner gameplay, Heat, PC! It is distinguished by a high level of complexity.

The players have such legendary tank models as T-55A or M-60A3. By managing your tank, it is necessary to destroy enemy objects and perform many different tasks. A special emphasis is on realism. The environment affects the maneuverability of the tank. Poor weather conditions overlap a normal review, etc.

The ballistics is also based on realism. The resulting damage depends on the caliber of the barrel and the projectile. Each tank has its own strength level. There is more maneuverable transport and slow, but hardy to damage. There is both a single mode, and a multiplayer is presented. A single and joint campaign consist of a set of various tasks. In cooperative missions, goals are set, which can be achieved through constant interaction.
