Game Description#

Another Crab’s Treasure is an exciting underwater adventure created by AGGRO CRAB. You take on the role of a hermit crab named Creel, who goes on a dangerous treasure hunt to get back his stolen shell. In this unique underwater kingdom, on the verge of destruction, garbage is not only part of life, but also a valuable resource used to create fashionable outfits and weapons. However, with the garbage comes a mysterious danger - “slurry” - which threatens to destroy the entire ocean.

In the game you will use the trash at hand as protection from huge enemies and discover dark secrets hidden by the polluted ocean. Navigate through kelp forests, coral reefs, sandcastle cities, and even the pitch black of deep-sea canyons to reclaim your precious shell. There are many “mind techniques” - powerful attacks adopted from the inhabitants of the seas, such as the terrible “sea worm” or the amazing power of the “mantis shrimp”. You can use them to defeat enemies and advance further in the story.
