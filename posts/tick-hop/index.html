Game Description#

Tick ​​Hop is a 2D platformer that requires the accuracy of movements, where a gamer assumes the role of the keeper of time. The journey includes the passage of the stages, each of which consists of temporary fragments. The main goal is to solve riddles, opening the aspects of the past to restore the future. The reverse reference system in this project requires quick actions to move through chronological spaces.

After the disaster, the central chronometer was destroyed, time was distorted, mixing the facets of the past, present and future. In Tick Hop, the keeper moves through the locations from fragments, restoring the watch. Each level contains puzzles and mechanisms that depend on manipulations over time, requiring decisions under pressure. The salvation of native lands requires the assembly of time, launching a chronometer and restoration of order. The platformer contains more than 100 locations, each with its obstacles and interactive elements.

The contention mechanism determines the pace; The tasks end before the limit. Temporary restrictions affect the environment, creating situations requiring quick adaptation. Various collected items, such as extending the course of time or its cancellation, help in overcoming the tests. The built -in level editor provides tools for creating your own stages that can be shared with others. The time frame, the color palette of backgrounds, as well as the exact location of the elements of gameplay are configured. The achievement system fixes the milestones when fulfilling the conditions.
