Game Description#

Sea of ​​Thieves offers to become a corsair for free as a pirate, commanding your own crew of sea robbers. A personal ship is available, which you can control alone or with a friend. Sea of ​​Thieves allows you to play online both in versus mode and in co-op, sailing the seas and oceans with a partner. Through the Internet, online players can travel through the open world and land on islands in search of secrets and treasures.

Online play gives you the opportunity to gather your own squad of thugs, setting off on an eventful journey across the numerous continents of the open world. There are many tombs and treasuries on display. The most desperate can join forces with Jack Sparrow himself and get to know his personal team. Controlling the ship is a real simulator. The possibility of free online play allows each member of the squad to take on a specific role: captain, navigator, sailor, etc. You need to patch holes, load guns, cook food and much more. There is a special activity for each participant.

Having set out on the open sea, you must enter into confrontation with other sailors. The ship is equipped with buckshot, nipples, cannonballs and other ammunition. Each type is used for specific tactical purposes. Some knock down masts, depriving the enemy of maneuverability, others pierce the hull, others kill crew members, etc. After the enemy’s ship is destroyed, you are allowed to collect all the valuable loot from it. It can be sold, and some of the materials are needed for crafting and pumping. The resources are boards, cores and bananas.

The main gameplay is aimed at social interaction between players. Three sides of the conflict are represented: the Trade Union, Sea of ​​Thieves and the Order of Arcs. Each faction has its own unique characteristics. The tasks have minimal variability and largely consist of the standard find-pick-bring scheme. When a valuable artifact is captured, it must be handed over to the company that assigned the task. After every fifth level, upgrade contracts become available. Level 50 allows the character to gain the status of a legendary hero.
