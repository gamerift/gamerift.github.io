Game Description#

Underward is a cooperative horror in which gamers will work together in a deadly underground hospital. Here, up to four users take on the roles of investigators who are instructed to extract the monsters of Test-Subject from the depths of this abandoned object. The task is clear: to convey dangerous capsules to the elevator and escape, but the path is far from simple. Users will encounter unpredictable enemies, traps and dangerous environmental factors, making their way through labyrinths. The key element is cooperation, as some areas require command work for access, and the transfer of heavy capsules with a test -object is a task for several gamers.

As explored, the noise becomes an important factor in survival. Some enemies are attracted by sounds, so moving carefully and hide when necessary is vital. If the enemy notices you, you need to quickly think and use fast reflexes. Throwing objects or the use of the surroundings will help to fight off the pursuer. Among this chaos, communication with teammates is crucial.

If the gamer has fallen, the team may try to save him, but time goes on, and the risk of losing valuable progress is very high. The mechanic encourages users to adapt to various situations, always be on the alert, plunging deeper into the unknown. In a single game, Underward is becoming an even more intense test. In the absence of a team, gamers should rely only on secrecy and strategy in order to avoid detection.
