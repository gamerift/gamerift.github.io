Game Description#

Rainy Day Racer sends drivers to the wet streets of the Scottish city, where strong rains are constantly pouring. The main occupation is the races for a while, with the main goal - to overcome the ring routes for the shortest period. Movement in urban conditions requires control over the machine with an ongoing bad weather. Each attempt is a single competition with a stopwatch, aimed exclusively to establish a personal record or ahead of other riders in the leaders table.

Such a system puts at the forefront of accurate driving and memorizing the track. The gameplay in the Rainy Day Racer focuses on the arcade style. Transport management is responsive, and clashes with the environment are not damaged, allowing you to maintain the gained speed. Drift is a key technique that is facilitated by the system that helps the gamer to maintain control in corners. For the competitive mode, online ratings are provided that track the best results.

You can also upload data about the races of other people to compete with their ghostly copies on the highway, getting a visual comparison of skill. A choice for such events is available 3 cars, including a van and more familiar cars. Competitions are held on 3 unique tracks, and two of them have one general segment of the path. The achievement of a fairly high position in the list of leaders opens up a special encouragement in the game. A model girl with a sign appears on the screen, which displays the player’s name, noting his record achievement.
