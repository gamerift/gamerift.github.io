Game Description#

UNDER NIGHT IN-BIRTH Exe:Late[cl-r] provides the opportunity for free online play with a friend or online. This exciting game allows you to play online as a pirate and enjoy all the benefits of free online play. You will immerse yourself in the world of Wormwood Night, choosing one of 20 unique characters with their own fighting styles and motives. The intuitive 2D combat system provides satisfaction for both button mashing enthusiasts and those who prefer a strategic approach, while offering devastating combos and easy controls in online multiplayer play.

As the ageless guardian Lynn, players search for the legendary “Sword of Destiny”, a beacon of hope amidst the encroaching darkness. Her path crosses with Hyde, a young man burdened with a mysterious connection to the desired blade. Together, they navigate a treacherous landscape, facing monstrous Hollows that feed on the energy of the Hollow Night, and Reborns that covet its power for their own twisted ends.

While unraveling the mystery behind the sudden increase in both forces, Lynn stumbles upon a nefarious plot orchestrated by the mysterious “Paradox”. This shadowy figure, revealed to be the leader of the underground organization Amnesia, threatens to plunge Nightworm into irreversible chaos. Lynn, driven by unwavering determination, vows to stop Paradox’s machinations before they plunge the world into darkness.

Unaware of this, Hyde follows on her heels, driven by an insatiable curiosity about the mysterious warrior and the forces she opposes. As their destinies intertwine, Hollow Night’s plot unfolds, drawing players into a web of intrigue, power struggles, and the looming threat of the ever-expanding Void, an entity of unfathomable power bent on consuming everyone.

Whether you want to pull off bone-crushing combos, master complex timing mechanics, or simply enjoy the thrilling story woven through the tapestry of combat, UNDER NIGHT IN-BIRTH Exe:Late[cl-r] invites you to step into the ring and create your own legend in the dangerous shadows Night emptiness.
