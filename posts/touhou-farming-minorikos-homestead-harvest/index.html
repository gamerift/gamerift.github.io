Game Description#

Touhou Farming ~ Minoriko’s Homestead Harvest challenges the harvest goddess Minoriko Aki to increase the number of her followers. In the world of Gensokyo, there is high competition among deities, so the heroine strives to provide food for the growing Human Settlement. Her main goal is to accumulate faith, which serves as a measure of popularity, by growing various crops, including a special sweet potato. All of these activities are carried out in conjunction with the Moriya Shrine to take advantage of the changes in the region.

The gameplay of Touhou Farming ~ Minoriko’s Homestead Harvest combines farming, interacting with characters, and exploring the world. The player will manage resources to plant and harvest crops that help increase Minoriko’s influence. Outside the estate, the heroine can visit the resort town of Yasaka to communicate with other gods and youkai from the mythology of this project. Also available for exploration are the surroundings of Youkai Mountain, travel through which can lead to battles with local inhabitants.

Two separate game modes offer different approaches to time and resource management. The casual option removes daily limits, allowing you to move through the story at your own pace. In contrast, Serious Mode introduces a daily timer and sets a deadline of 2 in-game years to complete the Faith accumulation task. This option also adds management challenges that require more careful planning to succeed under given conditions.
