Game Description#

Spaceflux is an arena shooter that you can play online with a friend for free on a pirate, fighting in fast-paced battles both locally in split-screen mode and online online. Fights take place in stylish fractal worlds. It is noteworthy that the environment is completely interactive and subject to destruction. The matches stand out for their super-fast combat. Online play allows you to test your strength on 6 bright and unique maps. Each world is represented by its own special style.

By participating in competitions and winning matches, the hero accumulates experience, which allows him to improve his skills. The possibility of free online play offers you to fight in different modes, including mirror mode - a completely reverse map. A special feature of the Spaceflux gameplay are the fractal worlds themselves, where the levels are endless loops, as well as a vertical loop into which you can fall and not get out until the end of the match. Horizontal looping allows the map to repeat itself endlessly in all directions. The gameplay is based on physics and geometry. Any object on the location is available for destruction or interaction. The overall gameplay is that of a classic competitive first-person shooter.
