Game Description#

The whole world in the game Primordia is mired in devastation and chaos after nuclear apocalypse. Everything around is gloomy, gray and lifeless, like the whole planet as a whole. In the computer application, topics of religion, freedom, justice and laws are raised. The protagonist Horatio The Human Blessed is a mechanic-engineer, a leading hermit lifestyle. The robot wears a black cloak with a dense hood, and his only friend of Crispin, a small flying saucer, constantly funny all around, sometimes bending the stick.

They live on the Uniic spacecraft, which has long been abandoned. However, the characters decide to repair and try to launch it into the sky. Everything would be nothing, but an unknown thief abducts the power source of the ship, and Horatio with his faithful ally does not have to do anything, except how to go in search of a power supply. Crispin during the trip will tell the player some features of Primordia, and will also help with solving puzzles and quests during the passage.

Studying the city, the gamer will come across various creatures with which you can chat. The player himself chooses what to answer from the presented options. But it is worth approaching the choice responsibly, because from any action, even a simple dialogue, the final scene can change. Several plot turns are available to choose from. The main antagonist of the game is the female artificial intelligence Metromind. It is not one of the key characters, but II can definitely be called a colorful hero.
