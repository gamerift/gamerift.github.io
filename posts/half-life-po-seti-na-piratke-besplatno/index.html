Game Description#

The pirateHalf-Life offers the opportunity to play online with a friend for free. You can both compete on multiplayer maps and go through the story campaign online on the Internet. The plot centers on the silent theoretical physicist Gordon Freeman, who finds himself in a whirlpool of fantastic events in the underground research complex Black Mesa. The ability to play online for free in Half-Live allows you and your partner to complete all 19 missions of the adventure, including Anomalous Materials, Diversion Well, Lambda Core, Nihilanth and others.

The gameplay is a classic first-person shooter. All locations are linear. The Half-Life campaign is a set of missions connected by a common storyline. You will not only have to fight numerous enemies, but also solve various puzzles. On the maps there are first aid kits and batteries that replenish players’ supplies. You can move boxes, pull levers, interact with various panels and terminals. The weapons used are a crowbar and various firearms. Online play allows you to ride a trolley or train together.

Jump puzzles involve jumping on platforms, adjusting the direction and distance of the jump. The heroes wear special protective suits. There are sections with different gravity. The enemies are such aliens from the world of Zen as headcrabs, zombified people, vortigaunts, houndeyes, barnacles, bullsquids, tentacles, gargantuas, snarks, ichthyosaurs, leeches, alien foot soldiers, controllers, Gonarch and Nihilanth. The allies are HECU unit fighters and black operatives.
