Game Description#

Demolish and Build 3 is a first-person simulator that puts you in the driver’s seat of a demolition company. You will complete orders and lead your team to victory using a variety of tools and heavy equipment. This game will test your skills as you manage your business and complete every project with precision and efficiency. A completely new realistic model of destruction will make you marvel at what you see more than once, since each fragment is physically simulated. Carefully place explosives to destroy entire buildings and watch how different parts of the structures interact with each other. We’ve achieved the perfect balance between realism, performance and fun, so you can enjoy the thrilling destruction without any lag.

Starting with a small plot of land and a modest hut, you will build your empire with numerous parking lots and offices. Hire workers, purchase new tools and vehicles, buy land and control your finances. But don’t forget to manage your workers too, otherwise you will end up in a disaster of destruction. Detailed vehicle models and sounds immerse you in the game, and you can control each one from inside or outside the cockpit. Use excavators with shears or jackhammers to get the job done properly. Whether you’re tearing down an old building or building a new one, Demolish & Build 3 has everything you need to become a master of your craft.
