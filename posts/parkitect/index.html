Game Description#

The game is a simulator where the construction of the amusement park will be the main task. Parkitect surpasses the rest of the games of this genre due to tactical management, the economic component and entertainment, which the player can only dream of. Initially, you are given a small plot of land and a few resources. Your task, as a manager of this park, invite as many people as possible. The value of the park is determined not by the number and “steep” attractions, but by a competently made gamification, because of which people will not be able to leave the park until they try everything!

A separate innovation was the slides and the ability to modernize any attraction. I want to make a slide longer and higher? You can configure this in the editor before putting it. The Ferris wheel is not red enough, and do you want it to be seen at night and it lured even the most fearful? Correct it!

The plot campaigns are designed to earn a certain amount that the player will carry with him from the mission to the mission. If water ends in the pool, add. If the relief does not allow you to put a number of kiosks - change it. About 100 world attractions that can be changed as you want. Various jewelry will allow you to create a unique park from which people will not want to leave. The gradual increase in the level of complexity will be noticeable not only in the finances limited at the beginning, but in the preferences of the guests. The further you went in the plot campaign, the harder it is even to please the most poor child who brought the last dollar to you!
