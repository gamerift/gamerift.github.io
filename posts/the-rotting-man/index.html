Game Description#

The Rotting Man will allow you to get used to the role of a student of the university who has arranged for the part-time job as a clerk in the Japanese Internet cafe. The narrative begins with the performance of a character of routine duties, including the maintenance of the booths and assistance to visitors in a closed space. Gradually, an ordinary working environment turns into a stage for a series of strange and inexplicable incidents that violate the usual routine.

These alarming events intensify, turning the usual one -looking work into a terrible test for a protagonist who masters new duties. The gameplay in The Rotting Man takes place in the first person, with an emphasis on the study of the nearest environment in the format of the walking simulator. Promotion in the plot includes navigation by cafes and conversations with a variety of characters who often visit this institution. Some interactions and discoveries encounter the main character with cruel incidents and naturalistic, bloody scenes that emphasize the elements of the psychological horror.

The decisions made during these clashes and the collected information become an integral part of the further development of events for the clerk. The storyline of this project is non -linear; It branches depending on the decisions made throughout the game. Each action contributes to the accumulative effect that directly forms the final events associated with the employment of the protagonist. This system of causes and consequences leads to 2 different endings, where each becomes a direct result of a particular game path in an unfolding story.
