Game Description#

InStacklands you have to build a village, completing various levels in the style of a deck-building battler. Users will collect food, build buildings and fight monsters on a special field using ability cards. It will be possible to use combinations of cards. For example, when you move a “village dweller” to a “berry bush”, “berries” will appear, which the city dweller will consume as food and get a chance to survive. The main task for players will be to think through a strategy, playing cards in the correct order in order to expand the village, trying to bring it to prosperity.

Users in Stacklands need to earn coins, which will allow them to acquire new abilities or character cards. In the game you will have to expand your deck in order to get more options for developing your settlement. It will be possible to focus on cooking, agriculture, construction and other areas of activity. It is necessary to keep the population well-fed so that the townspeople do not die of hunger at the end of each turn of the “Moon”. During the course of the game, users can expect more than 100 cards to collect, 25+ ideas to search for, 50+ quests, 7 deck-building kits and a single-player campaign, designed for 3-5 hours of real-time gameplay. Gamers will also engage in battles with fantasy creatures such as goblins, wild bears, evil rats, etc. He will go out to upgrade his residents, giving them weapons and improving their combat abilities.
