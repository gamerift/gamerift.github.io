Game Description#

The pirate version ofWarhammer Underworlds Online is free to play online, competing with a friend or other players from around the world. You will have to fight online online, controlling troops and fighting for the glory of the kingdom of the Age of Sigmar. Events take place in the Warcraft universe. The possibility of free online play offers intense PvP matches.

Warhammer Underworlds Online’s mechanics are built around large-scale battles using dice. Gathering your own army, you need to defeat the enemy army. The action takes place in the Mirror City of Shadowspire. The two factions are locked in a holy war, trying to survive in harsh conditions. Each side of the conflict has its own unique units and they are represented by unique strategic mechanics. A single player campaign, represented by various missions, and online play are available.

The army is assembled using a deck. Success depends on the fame points that players receive for performing various actions. The deck consists of two types of cards: universal and tactical. The first category includes 20 cards that represent gambits and upgrades. 12 cards determine the player’s tactical abilities. Multiplayer allows you to take part in rated and unrated matches. For winning, experience points are awarded, skins, bonuses and much more are unlocked. A variety of challenges are available for particularly experienced players.
