Game Description#

El Matador is a game inspired by the mechanics of the famous Max Payne series. “El Matador” is a third-person shooter where the protagonist, Victor Corbet, is an agent of the Drug Enforcement Administration (DEA). He uses his “bullet time” time dilation skills to perform acrobatic jumps in all directions for spectacular, and most importantly, effective extermination of bandits. Also similar to the project from Remedy Interactive will be the fact that Victor uses dual weapons for both hands.

The plot will tell the story of the DEA’s fight against a drug cartel operating in Colombia. The name of this group is La Valedora, they are one of the largest drug dealers around the world. But everything starts with sad events - Victor’s brother is killed, and the protagonist finds out about this only when he returns from the training camp. The assassin was ordered by gang members from Colombia, which gives rise to a desire for revenge in the main character, so, just in time, Corbet regards the offer to finish the job in place of his brother, who worked for the DEA, as a chance to destroy the cartel from the inside and take revenge on the killer.

In story missions, the player will be accompanied by characters controlled by artificial intelligence; they are needed to provide fire support and cover the rear of the alter ego. This will be especially useful in locations in the jungle, because there the enemy can hide anywhere, and help will definitely not be superfluous. The levels presented in the story campaign are as linear as possible and do not allow you to take a step to the side and do not allow you to miss any meeting with allied NPCs or clashes with numerous enemies.
