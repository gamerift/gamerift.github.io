Game Description#

Milk inside a bag of milk inside a bag of milk is a short visual novel with a deep message and unusual execution, made by the efforts of one developer, one of the indie creators. The visual design of the single-player campaign, which is the only game mode, is made using only two colors - purple and black. For completing a short story, which will take about 15 minutes, the developer has prepared three rewards, implementing a small list of achievements.

The gameplay consists of carefully studying the slides and reading the text presented on them. Some frame changes are accompanied by musical effects. The plot will tell about the difficulties that ordinary, everyday tasks can cause for some people. At the center of the events shown in the game is a young girl who cannot buy milk. The gamer will have to help her and try not to disappoint herself during the short meeting. The creator of the project put into the message the life experience he gained in real life, calling his brainchild an artistic manipulation.
