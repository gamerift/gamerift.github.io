Game Description#

Wolfenstein Youngblood allows you to play online for free with a friend as a pirate. Cooperative playthrough is available online online. The first-person shooter allows you to take control of the daughters of William Blaskowitz, the hero of the original Wolfenstein parts. Each of the heroines has a different character and abilities. There is a solo mode, in which the role of the second player is played by artificial intelligence. The online game is aimed at dynamic shooting using an interactive environment.

Simplified RPG mechanics available. Role-playing elements are focused on leveling up, leveling up and upgrading weapons. All guns can be modified and their color changed. Cannons are divided into three types: light (leicht), medium (gewehr) and heavy (schwer). Unlike previous games in the series, the levels of Wolfenstein Youngblood have become larger and more diverse, built on the principle of Dishonored. The possibility of free online play allows you to complete tasks in different ways, offering to use the verticality of locations, hidden paths, etc.

Both main and secondary tasks are presented. Many quests are completed in a random order, and you can also repeat them several times to improve your results or earn experience points. The plot directly continues the second part of the restart. The story begins 19 years after BJ’s adventures. Jess and Sophie travel to Paris to rescue their father from captivity. The daughters continue William’s work and fight the Nazis who won World War II. The world is on the verge of the threat of the Fourth Reich. The heroines have to find Laboratory X, where they need to destroy the Doomsday Machine.
