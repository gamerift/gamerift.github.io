Game Description#

Night Slashhers: Remake is a revival of the classic Beat ‘Em Up genre on horror, carrying players to the world where the supernatural reigns. From the very beginning, gamers are faced with a nightmare environment inhabited by zombies and other monstrous creatures. The action takes place in seven separate stages, each of which consists of many sections requiring the user to move forward, moving from left to right. Each stage turns into the battlefield, where the screen freezes, until all enemies are destroyed, which leads to tense fights with bosses at the end of each level. In Night Slashhers: Remake Team Work plays a decisive role.

Up to four gamers can unite in groups, each of which will take the task of surviving under the onslaught of supernatural creatures. This cooperative aspect turns the game into social experience, where the coordination of actions with friends is as important as the development of management. Players choose their heroes from a diverse list, each of which brings unique abilities and combat styles to the game. Mechanics supports a variety of combat techniques, from the execution of accurate combo to attacks from air and special techniques. A processed control scheme and battle mechanics here improve gameplay. The project includes modern achievements, but at the same time pays tribute to its arcade roots. Thanks to improved management, users can more effectively hit enemies and build the strategy of their attacks.
