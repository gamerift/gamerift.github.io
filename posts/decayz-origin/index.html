Game Description#

Decayz Origin is the story of a desperate struggle for life in a world that was destroyed by a viral disaster. In the center of the story is a man whose only goal is to save his loved one from a deadly infection, which makes him go to the very heart of the epidemic. The journey of the protagonist runs through the ruins of civilization, where terrifying mutations and traces of the former tragedy await at every step. The atmosphere of hopelessness is intensified thanks to a constant lack of resources, forcing to carefully plan each next step in this hostile environment.

Survival in harsh conditions requires a hero not only accuracy, but also ingenuity. The gameplay is built on the study of various locations, from abandoned villages to secret laboratories, each of which stores its secrets and dangers. The solution of puzzles integrated surrounded is becoming the key to promotion on the plot and disclosure of the truth about the origin of the virus. The Decayz Origin project proposes to create ammunition and medicines from the materials found, as well as improve weapons to confront more powerful opponents.

A special emotional depth of adventure is given by a satellite - a cat that accompanies the hero in his wanderings. This small companion becomes a symbol of hope and a reminder of humanity in a world absorbed in chaos. Interaction with it not only adds a unique element to the gameplay, but also emphasizes the main idea: even in the dark times there is a reason to fight. This Title offers a deep immersion in history about losses and the will to life.
