Game Description#

The player in Tainted Grail: The Fall of Avalon will have to unravel the secrets of the guards of menhirs, threatening the main character of the main character. The role -playing adventure will occur in an open world based on rethought classical legends about King Arthur. The user will be able to design his own deck of various cards with unique features and use.

The plot begins with the death of Avalon. The knights of the round table in confusion, and King Arthur has long been gone. In a remote country, menhirs live - keepers, the only ones who allows people to survive in dangerous territories. The central character lives in a small village. The head of the settlement chose the best and trusted inhabitants, who were sent on an epic journey. Their task is to get to Camelot, find the Grail and help dying land.

The gamer takes control over the gang of original characters. Ordinary farmers and workers went too late on a deadly trip. They do not have sufficient preparation and equipment. Nevertheless, they have a certain chance of success. The user is able to move freely around the world, exploring different locations and entering into battles. As they move, wards increase the level, pump their abilities and acquire better equipment. They will also find new people and take them to their detachment.
