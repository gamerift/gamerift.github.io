Game Description#

Seacret 1 is a sandbox in the 17th century Caribbean, where players are engaged in trade, battles or management. The economy here functions in real time, and the prices and volumes of goods in each individual city are constantly fluctuating. The journey begins with one modest tartan, but over time you can expand your flotilla to three vessels, including fast slices or more impressive galleons.

Strategic planning of commercial routes is becoming a key basis for the accumulation of wealth, vital for conducting operations in an extensive open world. In Seacret 1, sea battles are held in 3-on-3 format, the outcome of which directly depends on the tactical decisions taken. Captains use standard cannon nuclei to destroy the buildings, buckshot for the extermination of enemy crews and chain nickens to destroy masts.

Ship maneuvering requires close attention to the direction of the wind, which forces them to move with complex zigzags. A successful clash in the sea may end with boarding, turning into a furious hand -to -hand combat. The morale of the team must be constantly maintained by provisions, beer and women’s society, otherwise the displeased crew will inevitably raise a riot. In addition to marine activities, the player can assume the role of the governor, effectively leading the city through taxation and the construction of automated production buildings. On land, the characters call visits to fierce fights on 1-on-1 swords, which fully use the Ragdoll-physics for the comic effect.
