Game Description#

Test Drive Unlimited Solar Crown – restart of a series of arcade racing games. You can feel the vibration of hundreds of horses under the hood with modern graphics and physics. You can choose from top-notch hypercars from legendary manufacturers such as Koenigsegg, Porsche, Dodge, Ferrari, Lamborghini or Bugatti. It is worth understanding that luxury cars will require experience and skills, so you need to open them gradually. Each vehicle can be modified and customized to suit your preferences. You can change both the internal indicators and the appearance of the car. In addition, you can customize the main character in detail. The character must match his car, so you can choose from prestigious costumes and accessories.

The actions of “Test Drive Unlimited” take place on an island, recreated with the slightest detail from a real analogue on a 1:1 scale. Here users will have the opportunity to participate in the “Solar Crown” championship, a tournament with a unique set of rules. It is his concept that will allow the player to constantly develop and find something new from race to race. Under the wheels there may be perfect asphalt or bumpy gravel. The main emphasis in the physics of transport behavior is precisely on the reaction to changing surfaces, which will provide a unique experience in each environment.
