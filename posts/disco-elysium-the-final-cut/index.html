Game Description#

Disco Elysium is an RPG in which a detective must use his skills to conduct a unique investigation. The player will have to interrogate well-developed characters, investigate murders, and, if desired, take bribes. You can become either the savior of the city or the worst disaster for all humanity. Game Features: Unique branched dialogue system. Wide freedom of choice both in the story narrative and in additional tasks. Well-developed characters with their own stories and personalities. Wide range of clothes, equipment and skills. It is up to the player to decide whether to go through the game in the way that is considered correct or to immerse themselves in role-playing.

The game provides unprecedented freedom of choice. The detective can either use his own eloquence - intimidate and persuade, or resort to more effective and visual methods. But this is not limited to violence alone, because you can sing karaoke, write poetry, tear the dance floor to shreds, or you can even learn the meaning of life. All 24 skills are completely different, allowing you to create different combinations to suit your own playstyle. Or you can work out in detail the image for role-playing. Fortunately, an extensive wardrobe of 80 pieces of clothing also helps with this. And let’s not forget about the tools - pistols, flashlights, a tape recorder and even several different psychoactive drugs. And the cherry on the cake - 60 thoughts to ponder in the Thoughts Cabinet.

The dialogue system is very well designed and makes you believe that real people live in the game world, and not static bots. The characters can be helped, or they can be pitted against each other. You can even fall in love with them if you have the desire - the game’s dialogue system allows you to do whatever your heart desires. The same wide freedom of action is given in the gameplay itself. Firstly, there is nothing superfluous or forbidden here - death, taxes, disco and amorous affairs make up the classic list of a seasoned detective of the old school. The player can either investigate a mass murder or kick chairs and investigate small cases. Exploration, manipulation, bribery - it’s all available in a huge open world, from beaches to slums.
