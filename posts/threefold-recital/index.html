Game Description#

Threefold Recital invites gamers to move to bright eastern fantasy, where the overgrown city of Bluscles stands on the threshold of a new era. Once the city was warmed in the rays of the Golden Age, and now it is mired in political instability and tension. Title draws a picture of the world where ancient magic and modern technologies are intertwined, creating a unique mixture that drives the narrative forward. Bluezles is a city with many faces, and it seems that every corner is fraught with a riddle or conflict that is about to erupt.

In this turbulent atmosphere in Threefold Recital, the balance of power is always in question, and gamers should navigate in the complex web of relationships, each of which is more confusing the previous one. Title gives users the opportunity to control three main characters, each of whom has its own biography, as unique as the world in which they live. Triratna, a wolf that has turned into a monk has the ability to perceive and break the karmic lines.

This ability allows him to manipulate fate as others cannot. Thaitsin, who has turned into a priest, owns Taoist spells that give both offensive and defensive advantages in their journey. There is also a trance, a snake-artist, whose talent to change the appearance adds a fascinating strategy to the gameplay. Each character brings something important to the game, and their abilities often complement each other, creating the moments of synergy and solving problems.
