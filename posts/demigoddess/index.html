Game Description#

Demigoddess! - A step -by -step strategic game, with sessions calculated for completion for 1 hour. The procedural generation each time creates a unique world, changing the continents, the deployment of the enemy and the distribution of resources. The world consists of 64 regions, and to win the player needs to win over 32 of these territories. You can command armies of 20 different fractions, including over 450 species of soldiers and monsters. This basis provides a unique gameplay.

Separate areas contain up to 12 units on each side, leading to battles with the participation of up to 24 fighters. In Demigoddess! These conflicts are resolved automatically on the basis of preparation of detachments before a collision. The attack method of each warrior, whether it is weapons, magic or fire breathing, is installed in advance. The outcome depends on such parameters as the range and spontaneous properties, which makes the organization of troops and the use of more than 300 equipment of equipment in the most important part of the tactics. A similar approach attaches great importance to the pre -bear configuration.

The troops consist of fantasy races, including goblins, elves, orcs and beasts, as well as creatures from mythology, like dragons and griffins. More than 70 different heroes can act as allies or opponents, each with abilities that can change the course of the campaign. In this game, soldiers and monsters gain experience in battle, allowing them to increase the level, acquire skills and sometimes evolve into other forms. The economic system uses gold and mana for hiring forces and strengthening the army through a simplified mechanism.
