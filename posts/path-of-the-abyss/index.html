Game Description#

Path of the Abyss is a single -user project where you have to go to study the dungeons in the fantasy world. Users will test the gameplay mechanics of classical role-playing adventure with elements of Dungeon Crauler. The graphics are made in black and white and stylized under the drawings with a pen. Gamers will create characters and immerse themselves in the study of dark and confusing labyrinths, where dangerous monsters live.

Fights in Path of the Abyss are made in real time, where you will have to move in the first person and face the enemy. We will have to apply the skills and skills of the class that the hero has mastered. It will be possible to call on the help of allies, block attacks, impose protective buffs, or rely on magic spells. After the victory over the opponent, you will manage to choose a falling loot or study the room in which treasures can be.

Players will be able to collect different builds, choosing among the following classes: warrior, thief, clergyman and magician. Each hero will have 5 basic characteristics, as well as unique skills. We will have to find weapons and armor to strengthen the protagonists. You can wander around the map in free mode, if you wish, returning to the cleaned dungeons that are interconnected. Yuzers will have to solve the secrets of East Veltorden, who fell into decay due to the invasion of monsters.
