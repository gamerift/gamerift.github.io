Game Description#

F1 2010 is a racing simulator that focuses on online confrontations over the Internet. You can play Pirate for free online, competing with a friend or participating in various multiplayer championships. In F1 2010, single career mode and online play are available. A quick race mode is introduced where matches are created automatically. The free online play feature includes the Semi-Position competition. The races in this confrontation last 20 minutes.

The sprint challenges riders to complete 3 laps. The main goal is to take first place. The starting grid is determined randomly. Endurance allows you to set the duration of the race. Races in this mode are 20 percent longer than standard tracks. The weather changes randomly and affects the physics of movement. A special competition is the Online Grand Prix. Here you need to drive 7 laps. The championship begins with a 15-minute qualifying period, in which the starting grid of participants is determined.

The F1 2010 campaign begins with answering questions from journalists. The answers determine what the character will be like. This way you can set your name, nationality, nickname, etc. You can choose a team: HRT-Cosworth, Lotus-Cosworth or Virgin-Cosworth. There are two racing weekends available in the story campaign: Short and Long. The project includes all tracks and Formula 1 compounds of the 2010 season.

Teams and drivers are represented by Ferrari (Felipe Massa and Fernando Alonso), McLaren (Jenson Button and Lewis Hamilton), Red Bull (Sebastian Vettel and Mark Webber) and many others. Competitions take place on 19 unique tracks around the world, including Sakhir, Melbourne, Kuala Lumpur, Shanghai, Catalunya, etc. The gameplay is aimed at realism. The behavior of each car depends on individual characteristics and piloting skill.
