Game Description#

theHunter Call of the Wild can be played online with a friend, or as a pirate you can go on a free safari together using an online Internet connection. A hunter’s simulator that lets you hunt down game while exploring wild prairies and forests. The gameplay places a huge emphasis on realism and physics. A huge picturesque open world is available, inhabited by a wide variety of animals. The opportunity for free online play offers the opportunity to prove yourself as an experienced hunter.

Each participant is able to start a career as a hunter. TheHunter Call of the Wild online game allows you to both hunt animals together and organize a competition for the number of trophies obtained. Each creature has its own unique manner and habits. Some animals are very easy to scare away, while others, on the contrary, will go on the attack themselves. Assess your own strengths and capabilities. It is necessary to take into account the size of the animals, their weight and much more. Depending on the symmetry of the enemies, plumage, etc., you can find out the approximate cost of the trophy.

A wide arsenal of weapons is available, consisting of rifles, bows and other equipment. Each player selects his equipment and goes hunting, crossing the prairies on foot or by vehicle. It is very important to practice shooting, especially taking into account the strength of the wind, the trajectory of the bullet and many other factors. Receiving a monetary reward, you need to take new orders, open additional trunks, hunting kits and realistic generation of TruRACS horns.
