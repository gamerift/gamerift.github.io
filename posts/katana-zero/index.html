Game Description#

Katana ZERO is an extraordinary and dynamic platformer in the genre of science fiction and action, created in the best traditions of pixel graphics. The plot of the game tells us about a dystopian city, the main character lives in the third quarter, his name is Zero. He was severely wounded in the war, causing him to lose his memory. At night he has nightmares about past battles, and during the day he has to visit a psychotherapist. The doctor constantly listens to his ward, and then gives tasks - the purpose of which is to kill certain people.

According to the doctor, this is part of his therapy, which leads to recovery. The main character does not feel the pressure of his conscience and calmly continues to eliminate people, thinking that this is all part of the treatment. The gameplay of the game is created in the form of two-dimensional graphics. All levels consist of numerous roads with linear movement. Each mission gives you a choice of different weapons, as well as the ability to set traps that enemies will fall into while you hide behind various objects. There is excellent leveling up of the hero’s skills, which provide various advantages. All battles take place not only in open locations, but also in various public spaces.

Musical accompaniment creates a special mood. She well describes the complete serenity of the main character and his inner peace. The combat system in this project is completely unique; each enemy requires a special approach to destroy it. Traveling along various paths, the character leaves traces; they must be constantly covered up so as not to be detected. Go on this fascinating journey soon and find out the reason for Zero’s strange therapy and who he was before the disruptions in his memory began.
