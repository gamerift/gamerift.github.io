Game Description#

Rally Arcade Classics takes gamers into the heart of rally racing history, bringing classic arcade gaming to the world of motorsports. With 44 cars from four different decades - the 70s, 80s, 90s and 00s - you’ll get a look at the cars that helped shape the rally scene. There’s a wide variety of iconic cars here, each offering its own set of challenges and driving features. In addition to the impressive list of cars, there are 48 tracks, each designed for a different type of racing.

Thanks to the variety of conditions, users will always have a new challenge, regardless of the number of laps completed. The various modes in Rally Arcade Classics allow any player to challenge themselves. In Arcade mode, users will have to go through 16 unique challenges on four tracks. The competition intensifies as users compete against 14 other racers, each with their own tactics and skill level.

This mode is ideal for those who want to zip around the track quickly and feel the thrill without having to commit to long sessions. If you want something more traditional, then in Rally mode you can take on the challenge of classic rally competitions. Racing across four countries, gamers strive to win the gold cup while overcoming all sorts of obstacles, from tight turns to unpredictable terrain.
