Game Description#

Chickenhare and the treasure of Spiking Beard is an exciting adventure in which an unusual hero, half chicken and half hare, goes on an exciting journey with his loyal friends. He is accompanied by Abe the turtle and Meg the skunk, a martial arts master. The trio must outrun the villain named Pricklybeard and his minions in search of seven magical crystals. Their journey takes them through a variety of worlds, from the majestic halls of Featherbeard’s castle to the dark depths of the Demon Hole, where ingenious puzzles and hidden secrets await at every turn.

Each character makes a unique contribution to overcoming obstacles. Using his long ears for gliding, Little Rabbit is able to reach places otherwise inaccessible. Abe, being a strong turtle, breaks through fragile walls with his shell, opening new passages. Meg uses her fighting skills to defeat opponents and overcome obstacles. Success in the Chickenhare and the treasure of Spiking-Beard project depends on smart switching between heroes and using their combined skills to solve problems.

Edge of Adventure:

Explore colorful and diverse locations filled with secrets. Solving puzzles that require a combination of the abilities of all three heroes. Collect hidden treasures and collectibles around the world. Confront the treacherous Prickly Beard and his minions.
