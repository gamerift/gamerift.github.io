Game Description#

Age Of Empires (classic) is a classic part of the cult RTS, in which you can play online with a friend. On the pirate game you can participate in confrontations for free or play in co-op on the Internet online. There are 4 campaigns available: Rise of Egypt, Glory of Greece, Voices of Babylon and Empire of the Rising Sun Yamato. The first scenario consists of 12 chapters, and the other three include 8 missions. The Age of Empires allows you to take control of many factions, including Asia, Babylon, Egypt, Greece, Rome, Joseon, Persia, Macedonia and others.

The network game invites you to act together against a common enemy, or to participate in confrontations where everyone fights for himself. It is necessary to extract resources, construct buildings, hire troops, etc. The possibility of free online play allows each faction to explore different technology trees, including economic and military branches. An important mechanic of Age Of Empires (classic) is the transition to new eras. There are 4 of them in total. Each side starts from a primitive era, gradually developing to futuristic technologies.

All factions have their strengths and weaknesses. It is necessary to collect food, wood, stone and gold. Food is needed to create units. Wood is needed for the construction of buildings, ships, the construction of farms, and also for arming archers. Stones are required to build towers and walls. You can use diplomacy skills to conclude alliance pacts. It is allowed to declare war or remain neutral. Civilizations are divided into 5 styles. The multiplayer game is designed for 8 people.
