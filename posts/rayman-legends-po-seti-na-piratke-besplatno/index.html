Game Description#

PlayingRayman Legends online as a pirate with a friend, alone or with other players online is all the fun and mayhem imaginable! The gameplay is a rich platformer where you need to unlock characters, skins and various accessories, but not entirely for free, but by receiving victory points. The adventure directly continues the previous part of Rayman Origins. The online game Rayman Legends allows you to both compete against each other and take tests in co-op with up to 5 people.

The plot tells about Rayman and his best comrades - Globox and Teensy. While the heroes were resting, the nightmares of the Bubble Dreamer and the powers of the dark magician Murphy became so strong that they allowed them to kidnap 10 princesses, including Barbara and Teensy. The possibility of free online play invites you to team up to help out girls in trouble. Each princess in Rayman Legends represents her own unique world, referencing a famous fairy tale, such as Jack and the Beanstalk, etc. Heroes need to dodge spikes, collect lums, free babies, go through test rooms and solve puzzles. A huge number of version competitions, mini-games, etc. are presented.

40 remade levels from Origins are available. All stages are allowed to be completed in random order. There is a dynamic change of difficulty that increases the hardcore gameplay. At each level you will have to fight a unique boss, and you will also have to prove yourself in musical competitions under Eye of the Tiger. In total there are 6 chapters and 1 bonus stage, together consisting of 120 levels: Babies in Danger, The Story of a Toad, Fiesta of the Dead, etc.
