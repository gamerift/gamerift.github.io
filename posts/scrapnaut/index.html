Game Description#

Scrapnaut stands out against the background of other sandboxes with elements of survival with its concept. The hero has to fight for life in an open world using the surroundings of steampunk. He periodically fights steam robots and is engaged in the study of the surroundings. On the back, the protagonist wears an oxygen cylinder, so it is important to monitor the level of air. The appearance of the adventurer can be changed due to the change of clothing.

SCRAPNAUT has a traditional craft genre. To create unique items, you need to visit a specific biom and get the necessary resources in it. With the help of Kirka, the character mines minerals, and with an ax cuts trees. There is also a shovel that will come in handy when looking for treasures. The game allows weapons and armor, and then improve equipment. The tools and weapons have a margin of safety. There is an opportunity to acquire a large robot and use it instead of transport.

It was not without the construction of the base, where they allow to break the gardens and grow vegetables or fruits on them. The harvested harvest is used to cook food. There are many recipes for dishes. Periodically, mechanical enemies will be raided on the home of the protagonist. You can develop your camp, improve and repair, and also surround with fences. Wind generators allow you to use electricity.
