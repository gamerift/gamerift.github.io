Game Description#

Mists Of Noyah is a survival role-playing game that allows you to play online as a pirate for free with a friend, exploring dark fantasy locations together. Co-op play is available online or locally. You will need to fight dangerous monsters while collecting resources to build your own base. The online game is designed for participants to strengthen their fort during the day, and at nightfall they fight off various evil spirits.

The possibility of free online play in Mists Of Noyah invites you to go with your partner to explore a gloomy world, where you will need to find survivors and stock up on resources such as wood, iron, ore, plants and food. As the sun sets, the army of darkness attacks the fortress. With each subsequent night, the number of enemy troops not only increases, but also becomes stronger. Cooperative play is designed for five players. Each takes control of a unique character with individual abilities. Subsequently, all the hero’s skills are upgraded. Content and mechanics: A huge variety of main and secondary tasks; Leveling up characters with the help of talents and familiars; More than 10 unique biomes to explore; Dynamic day and night system; Many dungeons with different difficulties: Normal, Heroic, Mythic; Various equipment and weapons; Relics, perks and crafts: Blacksmithing, Cooking, Alchemy; Agriculture, construction, cooking and alchemy; Role-playing mechanics combined with tower defense elements; Battles with rare monsters and bosses.
