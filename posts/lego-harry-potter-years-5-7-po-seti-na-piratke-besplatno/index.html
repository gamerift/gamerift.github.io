Game Description#

LEGO Harry Potter Years 5-7 - an arcade game with the ability to play for free online with a friend, controlling the characters of the famous film franchise. Multiplayer is available locally and online, offering key scenes from the final three films in the franchise: Order of the Phoenix, Half-Blood Prince and Deathly Hallows. The visual side is made in the style of Lego toys.

The main action of Lego Harry Potter takes place in Hogwarts castle. In LEGO Harry Potter Years 5-7 you can go to Hogsmeade. The Hogwarts Express is available to travel to King’s Cross Station in London. Through it you can visit the Leaky Cauldron, Diagon Alley, the Royal Forest of Dean and many other levels. The possibility of free online play allows you to take control of one of the main characters of the series in order to jointly overcome all obstacles and defeat many enemies.

Gold blocks and red parcels are hidden in locations. You also need to find three hidden heroes and 4 parts of the coat of arms of the school of magicians. They allow you to unlock new items in the Extras section. There are a total of 200 franchise characters available, 100 of which are playable. Three characters are initially revealed: Harry Potter, Ron Weasley and Hermione Granger. The rest are bought at Madame Malkin’s shop. In battles you will need to use regular and elder wands. Hagrid and Malfoy use umbrellas and canes. The plot consists of 24 missions, including a bonus chapter. After completing the campaign, free movement through locations opens.
