Game Description#

On the pirated version of Sniper Elite V2 you can play for free online with a friend or take part in an online confrontation over the Internet. The gameplay is a third-person shooter with an emphasis on stealth mechanics. The gameplay can be described as a sniper simulator. The online game focuses on realistic physics: distance, bullet weight, wind speed and other parameters are taken into account. Heartbeat also plays an important role. In a calm state, the hero’s pulse reaches 60 beats per minute. During the action, it increases to 100, thereby affecting the accuracy of aiming.

Focus function available. For a moment, the protagonist can freeze and focus his sight on one point. You need to act as quickly as possible, since this mechanic quickly increases your heart rate. The main character of the adventure is Karl Fairbairn. He is an experienced sniper and master of sabotage. His tasks include secretly infiltrating enemy bases in order to assassinate important terrorist leaders. Free online play includes co-op play through the story campaign.

A special feature of the gameplay is the rapid camera and X-Ray, showing the flight of a bullet and the rupture of human organs by a projectile. An X-ray camera reveals the victim’s bones and entrails. The inventory includes rifles, machine guns, tripwires, dynamites and other auxiliary equipment. The events of Sniper Elite V2 take place in the last days of World War II in Berlin. The main target is the German general Hans von Eisenberg, by eliminating whom the war will finally end.
