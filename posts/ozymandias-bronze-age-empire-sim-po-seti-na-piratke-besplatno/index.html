Game Description#

InOzymandias Bronze Age Empire Sim you can play with a friend online on a pirate for free, working together online to lead the empire to prosperity. The mechanics represent a classic 4X strategy. The possibility of free online games allows you, together with a partner, to build huge cities, expand the borders of your own possessions and train troops to defend territories. By fulfilling your goals, you need to earn money and rewards that open up additional development paths. The victory conditions in Ozymandias Bronze Age Empire Sim are set randomly, thereby making game matches as unpredictable and varied as possible.

There is both an online game for several participants, as well as a single-player campaign and random maps. A total of 8 unique locations are available, reflecting the heyday of the Bronze Age. There are 52 empires to choose from. Each faction has its own set of units, fleet and various individual characteristics. Despite the turn-based style, in multiplayer all participants act simultaneously, which increases the dynamics of the gameplay several times. You can choose two modes of multiplayer parties: active and asynchronous. The turn timer depends on the choice. It can be either short or long. In the single-player campaign, the scenario is dedicated to the legendary female archaeologist Gertrude Bell. Artificial intelligence analyzes every action and adapts to the player’s style.
