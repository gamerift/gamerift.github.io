Game Description#

Mechanic Heroes is an online car repair game for four players. Play online with your friends, be it in local co-op or online pirate. Teamwork is a free-to-play online feature as you and your cute buddies race against a ticking clock to diagnose sputtering engines, replace dented fenders, and send happy customers roaring out of the garage. It doesn’t matter if you’re huddled around a beeping jalopy at your local co-op or playing online online, you’re about to become the best pirate team online.

Forget about clean showrooms and friendly customer service. This is Speedy Motors, where hero physics reigns. Need a wrench in the garage? Drop him! Are the nuts jammed? Tighten them! Time is money, and sometimes the quickest way to fix something is with a well-placed toolbox. Just don’t tell OSHA about it. But it’s not all muscle and chaos. As you complete 25 challenging levels, you’ll unlock a range of unique characters, each with their own special skills. Spike, a heavyset veteran, can jerk engines with ease. Wiz, a technician, can diagnose problems with just a glance. Let’s not forget about Zuma, the speed demon who, stuffed with caffeine, will make these machines purr like precise mechanisms. And when the grease is wiped off and the tools are put back in place, it’s time to resolve the main debate: who is the fastest driver on the team? Competitive racing pits your wrenching skills against your team’s need for speed. Remember, a well-oiled engine is only as good as the person behind the wheel. So fasten your seatbelts, mechanics, and get ready to prove your worth in the ultimate test of teamwork, greasy mud and glorious vehicular mayhem.
