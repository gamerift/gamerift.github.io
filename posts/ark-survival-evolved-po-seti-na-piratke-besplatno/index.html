Game Description#

ARK: Survival Evolved is an open-world survival game that you can play online with a friend. All network functions are available for free on the pirate ARK. Using the Internet online, you and your partner can explore the vast territories of the mysterious island, surviving among bloodthirsty dinosaurs and other creatures. You can switch between first and third person. You can move around the world both on foot and with the help of animals, if you can saddle them.

Firearms are used to fight enemies. By collecting resources, players build a base to protect themselves from other people or dinosaurs. The online game ARK: Survival Evolved is aimed at constant interaction between participants or bloody clan feuds. You can trade between settlements or players, tame dinosaurs, explore dungeons, various biomes, etc. A detailed underwater world and full-fledged hunting mechanics are presented. The possibility of free online play allows you to jointly explore more than 36 square kilometers of land and more than 12 squares of ocean.

The base is built using floors, walls, doors, windows and other elements. Allows you to craft weapons and tools. Any object is subject to improvement. For example, you can attach a flashlight or a silencer to a pistol. Openworld is represented by a variety of enemy types, including dinosaurs, ancient arthropods, reptiles, amphibians, mammals such as saber-toothed cats or mammoths, phantasmagorical ones: wyverns, golems, death worms and other types of creatures such as rock drakes or karkinos. Dinosaurs are represented by a variety of species, including Utahraptor, Trodon, Triceratops or Tyrannosaurus. Each of them has unique skills and performs a specific function.
