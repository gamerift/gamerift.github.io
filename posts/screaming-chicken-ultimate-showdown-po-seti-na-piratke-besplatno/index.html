Game Description#

The co-op arcade gameScreaming Chicken Ultimate Showdown can be played online for free, competing with a friend or other players. Locally or online, players control rabid chickens, running through completely destructible locations. Multiplayer allows you to customize your characters by choosing the craziest costumes for them. At the levels you can find a variety of weapons to fry other birds. The possibility of free online play offers you to fight in duel matches or take part in competitions in teams of 2 people. The multiplayer game includes different modes with individual rules and conditions.

A match is available against everyone, where you need to fight for yourself with absolutely all opponents on the level. Enemies can be blown up with bombs, thrown into lava, or a predatory plant can be forced to eat a chicken. Creative killing of opponents is rewarded with extra points. Screaming Chicken Ultimate Showdown features ninja chickens, Cthulhu chickens, and many other chicken monsters. A total of 40 types of weapons are available with unique characteristics that influence the tactics of fights. These include both small arms and various projectiles with a large damage radius. There are 130 levels for battles with individual architecture and biomes. You can create your own maps using the editor and customize various match conditions.
