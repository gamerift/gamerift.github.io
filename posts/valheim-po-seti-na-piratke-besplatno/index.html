Game Description#

Valheim is a first-person fantasy survival game that allows you to play online online, traveling through the Scandinavian lands of Scandinavia, with a friend or alone. All online functions in Valheim on Pirate are available for free. The gameplay is steeped in Viking culture and Scandinavian mythology. Together with your friends you will need to go through incredible adventures in which you will find out whether the gates of Valhalla will open for the heroes? Events take place in the 10th Viking world. Monsters and monsters live here. To achieve the favor of the gods, you have to prove your power in a variety of tests, going into dense forests, climbing high into the mountains, etc.

By collecting resources, you can engage in crafting and build fortresses. Multiplayer allows you to join forces with other players to build a majestic settlement together. Also, the possibility of free online play involves a battle on longships, exploring the nearest sea. The further you go deep into the mysterious northern lands, the more difficult the dangers you encounter along the way. Features of Valheim: extensive character customization; flexible system for constructing buildings and settlements; procedurally generated world; blacksmithing skills that allow you to create weapons, armor, etc.; complex combat system based on strikes and blocking; arena battles with powerful bosses; survival mechanics that require you to monitor your health and nutrition; the ability to sail the seas on ships.

As you progress through the main quest or explore the far corners of the map, you will need to not only fight powerful enemies, but also collect more valuable loot, dropped both from monsters and found in the deep caves of the world of Valheim: legendary weapons, strong armor, rare resources, etc. .d. - all this is hidden from human eyes and only a real warrior is able to find the true treasures of the Vikings.
