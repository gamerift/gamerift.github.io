Game Description#

Quilts & Cats of Calico is a unique, warm and cozy puzzle game. Here you will have the opportunity to work as a tailor and create a unique quilt from scraps. Combine colors and patterns on your scraps to not only score points, but also attract cute cats who will decide where to snooze on your creation. If you’re a board game fan, you’ll be happy to know that this is a digital version of the already beloved board game. Here you will find not only familiar mechanics, but also new rules and modes. You can play alone against the computer or with friends invited to an online game. You can also take part in weekly challenges and compete in rankings.

If you are looking for not only an exciting game, but also want to immerse yourself in an unusual world, then the story mode is definitely for you. Inspired by the work of Studio Ghibli, it depicts a tailor’s journey through a war-torn world. Here you can create blankets, improve your skills and help new friends. And the cats that live here are simply adorable! They will react to your actions and, with their behavior, immerse you in an atmosphere of comfort and warmth.
