Game Description#

Murder at Midnight is a casual game in which you can join up to 15 people online online to take part in various competitions as pirates. You can play for free online with a friend, trying to track down your prey as a hunter, or as prey you need to deceive the pursuer, confusing him. The gameplay is a loose interpretation of the Mafia.

Gameplay focuses on mechanics such as stealth, hunting, and discussion. Murder at Midnight’s multiplayer gameplay is a classic predator versus prey game. The game begins with a murder taking place at midnight. People are faced with the fact that they are trying to decide who is involved in the crime. Everyone can put forward their own conclusions and blame their suspect. The possibility of free online play allows a company of 4 to 15 people to join forces, tracking down the real killers.

Predators can be from 1 to 3 players. The number of criminals can be set at the very beginning of the game. The remaining participants become prey and can be killed during gameplay. The survivors have their own goals that they pursue during the game. At the end of the round there is a vote where players decide who will be accused of murder and called a predator. With each new game, the goals and roles change, increasing the replayability of the project.
