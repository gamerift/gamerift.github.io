Game Description#

Savage Lands is a sandbox with survival elements that allows you to play online for free with a friend on a pirate, exploring the frozen lands of Tarvkhas. Alone or online online, you need to overcome the cold and fight monsters. You will need to uncover the secret of the Wild Lands and enter into a struggle for survival, where every element of nature wants to kill the hero.

A huge fantasy world is available for exploration. Free online play in Savage Lands invites you to join forces to survive the harsh winter conditions together. By collecting resources, you need to create weapons, equipment and auxiliary items. There are a huge number of dungeons available with unique enemies, bosses and loot. The opponents are frost giants, rabid wolves, etc. Online play allows you to form a whole tribe to make survival easier.

You can customize your equipment, upgrade your skills, and much more. The mining and crafting system plays an important role. Using resources, unique weapons and armor are created to help you survive in the harsh northern world. Bases and fortifications are also created using the collected resources to help fight off monsters. Creatures are able to attack in groups. Special resources can be obtained from defeated enemies. At the beginning of the adventure, each participant creates his own unique character, endowing him not only with individual appearance parameters, but also with various talents.
