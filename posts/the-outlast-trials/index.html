Game Description#

The Outlast Trials - first-person horror game in the Outlast universe. The difference from the main series is that you can play both alone and in a company. At the center of the story is the Murkoff company, which is engaged in inhumane and sadistic experiments. You will play as one of these crazy organizations. You can join forces with up to 4 people and try to survive this nightmare together.

The action will take place during the Cold War. Scientists from the Markoff Corporation recruit people to test their brainwashing system and seize control of their minds. The Outlast Trials is a psychological horror game that tests your fortitude, endurance and sanity. And all this in the name of science, progress and… money!

The gameplay has absorbed all the best from the older game and has not undergone any special changes. The gameplay is primarily a walking simulator. You will hide and run away from monsters. Use cabinets or crawl under tables to hide from enemy sight. Instead of a video camera, the heroes will wear night vision goggles, which will allow them to see in the dark and be more mobile. However, due to the increased gameplay dynamics and stronger and smarter enemies, walking through the medical complex will not be an easy task. Let’s all get scared together!
