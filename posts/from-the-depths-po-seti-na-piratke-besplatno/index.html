Game Description#

On the pirateFrom the Depths you can play for free online with a friend or fight in intense online battles online with random players. Using more than 1000 blocks, you can create unique equipment or build both a naval and space fleet. The From the Depths online game features 8 unique factions. Everyone has their own individual characteristics and characteristics. The mechanics combine elements of strategy, simulation, construction and role-playing adventure.

There is a huge world available for exploration. This can be either the bottom of the ocean or outer space. The possibility of free online play invites you to engage in an intense duel with the enemy, using all available military resources. These can be powerful forts, flying fortresses, starships and anything your imagination can do. The editor includes more than a thousand different blocks and about 40 unique modules. There is an adventure mode and various multiplayer competitions. You can also pass various tests.

Weapons include cannons, lasers, mines, bombs, torpedoes, etc. Vehicles can consist of boosters, propellers, jet engines, wings, balls, etc. The main emphasis is on realism and physics. When constructing a structure or transport, it is necessary to comply with physical laws. influencing the behavior of the car, its controllability and functionality. It is important to obtain resources to construct ships, drones, planes, submarines, starships and much more. Electric motors, generators, lasers, drilling guns, turbochargers, etc. - everything requires resources and energy. You can join a team, exchanging resources, or engage in intense battles where everyone fights for themselves.
