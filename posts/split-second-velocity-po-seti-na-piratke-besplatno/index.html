Game Description#

Split Second: Velocity offers free online play for fast-paced, explosive races. Pirate players can compete both locally and online online. You can compete in splitscreen mode with a friend. Gameplay Split Second: Velocity is an arcade racing game with action elements. A single player campaign and online play are available, allowing you to compete in co-op or take part in multiplayer races.

The plot tells about a murderous and spectacular reality show. Race participants compete for the amusement of spectators. The races take place within the city scenery. The racers have one goal - to come first to the finish line and become the champion of the season. When participating in races, you need to ram the vehicles of your opponents. You can push them off the track, explode or knock down surrounding objects, slowing down or destroying the enemy. An interactive environment is presented that allows you to interact with various objects in locations. Gameplay and mechanics: Single races and the possibility of free online play; Dynamic chases with a huge amount of destruction; Timings that suggest destroying buildings and surrounding objects in time; Tactics and strategy of races in which you need to be cunning and shorten routes; Unique opponents with their own individual features and characteristics; A huge interactive city that acts as a weapon and an ally; Many different modes, including quick matches and tournaments.
