Game Description#

The dynamic shootouts in Bullet Runner show the adventures of a pixelated man who is forced to resist against a crowd of evil opponents. The game is a top-down action shooter where you have to fight with various opponents. The project is filled with fast and active gameplay, where users can test the speed of their reaction to current events. You’ll have to move around the map and shoot enemies using a wide arsenal of weapons. The character in Bullet Runner is able to run, dodge, slide and swing to maneuver around the map.

You have to deftly move between natural shelters, bypass opponents and shoot on the run, so as not to remain sandwiched by a horde of enemy fighters. Soldiers will attack back with rifles and other weapons, forcing the protagonist to dodge shots and constantly be on the move. Players will be faced with ordinary opponents and bosses of locations that are larger and stronger than their minions. Gamers get the opportunity to customize equipment in their own way, select items and ammunition, and formulate tactics for passing. In battle you will be able to try out pistols, shotguns, rifles, machine guns and plasma weapons. Gameplay mechanics are tailored for active battles, where it is impossible to stay in one place to survive.
