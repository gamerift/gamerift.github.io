Game Description#

Longstory 2 continues the established narrative with a large number of dates, developing friendly relations and difficult situations. The action takes place in the summer in front of a high school, immediately after the events of the previous part. During the party, in honor of Marseille’s return, a personality, known as Turkeyhawk, disappears home. This event gives the start of the main riddle, offering the player to find this character. The search for an individual who left a turkey costume in a closet becomes the main task before the start of the school year.

In LongStory 2, the disappearance investigation takes place against the backdrop of numerous rumors. Others are divided by their own theories about what happened, and some are looking for clues on the network. The dialogs are filled with speculations about what happened to the personality in the suit. You have to analyze these conversations to establish facts. The key question concerns the motives of the missing, including his desire to be found and the influence of the change of identity on your friendship.

What awaits you in this adventure:

Search for the missing character Turkeyhawk before starting to study. Interaction with the environment full of rumors and speculation. Organization of romantic dates and the development of relations. Making decisions affecting the course of investigation and personal life. Combining detective activities with the construction of love ties.

This game combines the search with the structure of the Simulator of the Date. You organize meetings and flirting, building your line of behavior in society. Romantic interests often intersect with detective responsibilities, creating comprehensive situations. The choice of replicas in the dialogs and the player’s actions determine the denouement of both the detective line and personal relationships. Promotion in the plot entirely depends on the decisions made during communication with the actors.
