Game Description#

Serious Sam HD: The First Encounter introduces the ability to play with a friend online, fighting online with a huge number of monsters. You can go through the campaign for free on a pirate with a partner or compete on special version maps. The re-release of Cool Sam HD: First Blood includes improved effects, textures, bonus content, etc.

The protagonist of the adventure, Sam Stone, fights against powerful demons and monsters from another dimension. Online play allows you to join forces with a legendary character to jointly rid the world of evil. Players have a huge variety of weapons to choose from, including a combat knife, heavy guns and much more. Free online play in Serious Sam HD: The First Encounter also features a Deathmatch mode. Here each fighter must prove his superiority.

The plot tells that a certain Egyptian artifact of the ancient civilization of Sirius allowed people to advance in development by studying advanced technologies. However, because of this, they stumbled upon a race of aliens with whom humanity became involved in a war. The key to Eternity may be what will help people win this confrontation. Sam decides to use the artifact to travel to Ancient Egypt and destroy the leader of the aliens, Mental. The single and co-op campaigns consist of 15 levels filled with total madness and action. You will have to confront such enemies as the Headless Kamikaze, Ukh-Zan and others. Multiplayer allows up to 16 players to play simultaneously.
