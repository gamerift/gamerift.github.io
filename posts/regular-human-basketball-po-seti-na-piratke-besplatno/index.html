Game Description#

Regular Human Basketball is a multiplayer arcade basketball game that allows you to play with a friend online on a pirate for free, climbing inside a human robot. On the Internet, online players gather into one basketball player, where each participant controls the motor, wheels, magnetic arms, etc. In other words, the possibility of a free online game is aimed at coordinated interaction between partners, where a mistake by one participant can lead to loss.

Regular Human Basketball’s gameplay is fun-oriented and perfect for parties. The team takes control of a giant mechanical basketball player. The online game encourages constant interaction with each other: shouting orders, flipping switches and shooting balls into the hoop. The second team tries to take the ball and throw it into the opponent’s hoop. You can invade enemy mechs and disable their engines to gain an advantage over your opponent.

It is possible to play in splitscreen mode. Co-op is designed for two people and allows you to play with a whole team of up to 10 players. The more members there are on a team, the more concentration required to interact. The matches are played to the soundtrack of rousing 80s hip-hop, so it is very important not to be distracted by dancing or waving your arms, setting your team up. Particular emphasis is placed on heavy physics. Post-apocalyptic devastation reigns all around, but this does not matter in the face of a three-point dunk.
