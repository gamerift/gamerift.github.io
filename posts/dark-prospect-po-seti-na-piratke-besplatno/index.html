Game Description#

Dark Prospect allows players to play online in a battle between human soldiers and hellish monsters. Available for free on pirates, this asymmetrical multiplayer game pits FPS players against a friend against an RTS overlord in a fight for humanity’s survival. Soldiers mine resources, strengthen their positions and create weapons to resist the demonic horde. Meanwhile, the overlord controls legions of creatures with unique abilities, casting spells and enhancing his minions to crush human resistance. Online network play on the Internet adds an additional element of tension to this confrontation.

The essence of the game comes down to protecting or destroying a plasma cannon - humanity’s last hope for sealing infernal portals. FPS players must work together, balancing resource gathering, base defense, and offense. The RTS player, whether human or artificial intelligence, orchestrates waves of attacks by strategically placing units and exploiting weaknesses in human defenses. This creates a dynamic battlefield where tactics and reflexes collide.

Dark Prospect offers plenty of content to keep players busy. Five different maps serve as the battlefield, each with their own characteristics and strategic considerations. Custom Game Options allow you to tailor the game to suit you, adjusting settings for both FPS and RTS. It doesn’t matter if you want a quick shootout or an all-out war, the game has it all covered. Don’t forget about bots - they will fill up your FPS team if you don’t have enough human comrades.

Communication is key in Dark Prospect, and the game doesn’t skimp on features. Voice chat lets you coordinate on the fly, while quick chat and text options ensure you’re never left without words. With support for up to 32 players via Steam, you can stage epic battles or take on someone else’s nightmare. It’s a hectic dance of bullets and brimstone, where every decision can tip the scales between victory and damnation.
