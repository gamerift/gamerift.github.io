Game Description#

Forza Horizon 5 is a racing game that focuses on multiplayer racing and free-roaming in an open world. This project includes many aspects from Forza’s previous games. More than 200 original cars from real-life companies are presented, with realistic driving and clutch physics. There are high-quality graphics, effects and other elements that completely immerse users in a believable environment.

While riding around the world, the player is able to collide with other gamers or ordinary cars controlled by artificial intelligence. There are tons of original races scattered throughout the area, such as drifting, off-road driving, Monster Truck racing, and more. Users are able to participate in the search to instantly be transported to the race track and compete with the racers. Among the gameplay features of Forza Horizon 5, there is a dynamic change of seasons that directly affects the gameplay. Cars can be driven in spring, autumn, summer and winter, which will affect grip, visibility and type of competition.

There are many places scattered around the world with side missions where players are able to set records. These are small jumps or drift areas where you will need to score the most points to get on the leaderboard. For example, when jumping on trampolines, you need to fly the maximum number of meters. Users can take a long time to select the right angle and place for acceleration to break records. The game supports 4K resolution and 60 frames per second. Gamers can use steering wheels, gamepads or joysticks to participate in races.
