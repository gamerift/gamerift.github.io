Game Description#

The story of Rise Eterna follows Lua as she attempts to uncover the secrets of her own origins. The gameplay combines tactical elements and RPG in a Japanese animated style. The user will assemble a squad of characters with special skills and characteristics. A squad can have up to 14 wards. The heroes will go through brutal battles, dungeons and boss encounters.

As the player progresses, he discovers a dark and mature story centered around the relationship between two lost souls. They go through all the obstacles together, trying to understand the meaning of their existence. There will be critical moments in the lives of Louis and Natila. They will encounter heroes and mercenaries who want to join their squad. There is a lot of hidden content scattered throughout the project. Reaching it requires solving puzzles, interacting with characters, and overcoming challenges. Additional story elements and aspects are found in each location. Rise Eterna’s main features:

Create your own army of 14 special warriors. Each one is unique to personalize and plays a specific role in the journey. The user is able to study the skills of mercenaries and characters, recruit them, train, equip and upgrade them, preparing them for the next battles. Crafting will help you use mined gems, created from several rare ingredients. Runes will strengthen your players and give them new skills.
