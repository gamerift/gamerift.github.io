Game Description#

RESIDENT EVIL 2 / BIOHAZARD RE:2 - a complete rework, allowing players to enjoy modern graphics, a new engine, enemies, weapons and a much more advanced game story. Follow the story of police officer Leon S. Kennedy and student Claire Redfield.

Due to an unknown outbreak of a virus, the entire city of Raccoon City was enveloped in monsters, people began to turn into strange creatures, and then kill the survivors. When completing tasks, you will be able to control only one character, while the second will be handled by artificial intelligence. There are missions where you can put yourself in the shoes of any hero, which will give you a better understanding of the story by watching it from different angles.

Your main enemy is William Birkin. It was he who created the new G-virus. After meeting with Hunk’s military group, who tried to kill William, he injects himself with the virus, mutates and survives the attack. Important innovations include an over-the-shoulder view and stunning graphics. Well-developed effects, reflections, shooting and a new-looking environment. Walk through creepy corridors, solve puzzles, pick up ammunition or items, fighting for survival among monsters.

As in the original game, gamers will explore locations and kill opponents, completing tasks along the way. As you shoot at monsters, you’ll leave behind realistic marks that give you a full sense of the weapon’s power. The main tasks of the characters are to survive and find Claire’s brother. The gameplay contains an excellent frightening atmosphere, which is created by high-quality sound, hordes of monsters and many dark locations.
