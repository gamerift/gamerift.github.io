Game Description#

Dino D-Day is a multiplayer shooter set in 1942. The gameplay allows you to play online as a pirate with a friend or compete online for free with players around the world. The story begins with Adolf Hitler succeeding in reviving the dinosaurs. He does everything he can to get them to attack Europe. The Nazi army becomes truly invincible. Key features of Dino D-Day: 7 types of dinosaurs: Velociraptor, Dilophosaurus, Desmatosuchus, Stygimolochus, Protoceratops Trigger, Tyrannosaurus Rex and Styracosaurus; 9 human characters; 26 types of weapons; phantasmagoric setting of World War II; multiplayer battles inspired by Team Fortress and Left 4 Dead.

The project is made in a retrofuturism setting, introducing elements of fantasy into historical realities. Multiplayer allows you to fight on a variety of maps from the Second World War. You can play for the Allied or Nazi team. There are 7 unique characters on the Alliance side. One of them is Trigger Protoceraptos, a dinosaur who acts on the side of the allies. The ability to play online for free allows you to take control of three human characters and 3 bloodthirsty lizards.

Each character has its own unique abilities and uses individual tactics in the fight against enemies. Desmatosuchus or Dilophosaurus allows you to instantly kill your opponents, committing bloodthirsty murders. Some events replicate famous historical battles, while allowing dinosaurs to appear on the battlefield.
