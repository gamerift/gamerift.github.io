Game Description#

Lushfoil Photography SIM is an incredibly beautiful and relaxing game in which the player can simply relax as much as he needs. She does not drive the user forward, allowing him to admire the views, relaxes the natural sounds of the noise of leaves, water or the creak of snow. If desired, you can completely turn off the musical accompaniment and plunges into the virtual world, so similar to the real one.

The main character is an experienced photographer who travels around the world to capture the beauty of nature, and then put them into printed products or sell them on a network for commercial goals. He simply loves his work and gives it all of himself and all his strength. Walking through the forest, he can appreciate beautiful angles and unusual wonders. Moreover, it is important for his profession not to follow standard tourist paths. Having come deep into the trees, you can notice flocks of animals, squirrels with lingerie and many unique phenomena. The main thing is to observe silence and caution so as not to frighten off the fragile beauty and capture it on the camera.

Lushfoil photography Sim was created on the basis of images of enthusiasts. The developers only remained to revive the materials received, so the player can enjoy the most detailed picture. He will need to perform a number of missions related to photographs of certain objects at some angles. The tasks are optional, but will definitely reward the user who will decide on their implementation.
