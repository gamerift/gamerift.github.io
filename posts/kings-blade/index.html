Game Description#

King’s Blade throws a small detachment of elite warriors to break through the hordes of the Bargian Empire. This Beat ‘Em Up with side scrolling immerses in a gloomy fantasy world created under the influence of the Konan-Cover and Arcadian Titles universe like Golden Axe and Streets of Rage. Players have to move around the enemy lands and participate in brawls, trying to break through to the capital of the enemy.

Promotion in the plot consists of constant battles in a series of levels, which are new areas for battles. The combat system in King’s Blade opens tactical scope due to 6 non -future heroes. The gameplay unfolds on 12 diverse locations that differ in layout and a set of opponents. The mechanics of the battle are built on a deep system of combo units that are unique to each character of special receptions and a set of magic spells. Moreover, fighters can select environmental objects and use them as an impromptu weapon against imperial soldiers, which brings an element of dynamics to every battle and allows us to adapt the battle style.

You can go on a trip alone or uniting with other warriors. The game supports a local cooperative mode for 4 gamers, allowing one team to confront the Empire’s forces on the general screen. If you do not succeed in collecting a full detachment of people, their partners under the control of AI will take up their free places. Such an opportunity helps to collect a full -fledged army to storm enemy positions, preparing for the upcoming resistance.
