Game Description#

Secrets of Grindea is an interesting adventure game in which you will need to take control of a hero and go on a long journey through a huge fantasy world in search of new adventures. The game begins with character generation. You will have to create it, change its appearance using an advanced editor, and then decide on the basic skills. After this, you can begin to explore the world around you. It is worth noting that the developers and the plot do not limit you in any way. You decide for yourself how to upgrade your hero, what skills he will learn, and what he will do in general in this universe.

The world here is unpredictable and vast, living by its own laws. As you move through it, you will meet many different characters, about whose lives you can learn more, and some of them will even have to help. Some will ask you to find a missing item, while others will need to bring the heads of evil monsters from the dungeon. And some will even try to kill you on the sly. There are a lot of variations, and no matter how many times the game starts again, there will be more and more problems, events and adventures.

You are given complete freedom, both in terms of leveling up the protagonist and in choosing decisions and actions. You can create a mage who uses fighting staves, or a tough warrior who does not use magic. Everything is possible here!
