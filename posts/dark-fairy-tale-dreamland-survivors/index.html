Game Description#

Dark Fairy Tale: Dreamland Survivors puts a player under the control of Pinocchio, a puppet who has trapped in a hostile and distorted space of nightmares. The character moves around this world by controlling one hand, while the system is automatically introduced to creatures from rethought fairy tales. The promotion depends on the collection and absorption of more than 100 different forbidden dreams of dreams, each of which modifies offensive and defensive capabilities.

The main goal is to survive under the onslaught of monstrous inhabitants and pave the way back to reality. Gamers create combat styles in Dark Fairy Tale: Dreamland Survivors, combining numerous talents and found objects. The protagonist owns three basic types of weapons, which form 6 unique attack options depending on the sequence of their use, each with 3 exclusive skills, which is 18 improvements in total.

Dream eggs serve as the main resource for setting up the character, affecting the gameplay; They can be activated for one set of bonuses or absorbed for another internal amplification, which provides tactical flexibility. These skills are combined with tarot abilities to gain additional advantages. The campaign of this project is structured around 22 stages of Tarot dreams, each of which offers special mechanics and types of enemies, which requires a strategic approach. For the successful passage of the test, a fragment of the mysterious force of the corresponding card is given.
