Game Description#

Friday The 13th: The Game is a horror with a third -person view, where the player will test the role of the victim or become a maniac. 7 people try to survive and escape from Jason Wurchis. The game takes place in a small town near the forest. You can hide in houses, hide in the foliage, but the inexorable killer will still overtake you. The main goal is to get out of the damned place by leading the engine of the car or calling the police.

Jason has a wide arsenal of opportunities that allow us to find and eliminate students. You can hunt with the help of special vision, imperceptibly sneak up, teleporting other people’s backs and kill in various ways. The incredible power of the lurkhis allows you to break the spine or break the skull to the victim. Filling the scale of rage, the killer becomes even more dangerous. The approximation of the maniac speaks of interference on the screen and characteristic terrible sounds. During the game, the appearance of the famous maniac from different films of the Friday 13 - second, third, sixth, seventh, eighth parts. Skins give passive abilities, for example, increasing resistance to stunning.

The victims must act together, united in the face of danger. There are several ways of salvation from a nightmare - call law enforcement agencies, fix the boat on the lake, start the car. For all finals, it is necessary to find objects hidden in the vicinity - a walkie -talkie, gasoline and other things. On Friday 13: The game each character has its strengths and weaknesses. Someone is stronger, and the other is running faster. You can also protect yourself from the maniac, temporarily confusing it with a blow to the head, shooting from a shotgun or throwing the fireworks under his feet.
