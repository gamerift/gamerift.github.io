Game Description#

Circle Empires Rivals is a strategy aimed at online cooperative play on the Internet, allowing you to play online with a friend. You can fight with other players because all multiplayer functions and modes are provided for free on the pirate site. You have to take control of a small kingdom consisting of circles. The location of each territory has individual properties and is represented by a unique external style. You have to fight the enemy army, hunt monsters and collect loot and resources to improve your army and upgrade your territories.

At any moment, best friends can turn into enemies and attack, so you should be on your guard. Network play allows you to unite against common enemies, fight in teams, etc. Locations are randomly generated, offering you to fight the opposing faction with hundreds of units. When defeating the enemy, you need to take his resources, investing them in the development of your army and state. It is very important to develop your own individual tactics and deceive your opponent in every possible way. Features of Circle Empires Rivals: possibility of free online play; co-op and single play; leveling up and increasing; expansion and conquest of territories; procedural generation of lands, plants and creatures; 250 types of units, buildings, resources, etc.; 25 heroes and leaders; 40 powerful bosses; customizable difficulty levels.
