Game Description#

On the pirateCook-Out you can play online for free with a friend or a whole group, cooking unusual and exotic dishes online over the Internet. The free online play option invites participants to travel to a cook’s hut in a fairytale forest. The online game Cook-Out: A Sandwich Tale brings together 4 people to cook a variety of dishes together. Many hungry magical creatures come to the establishment. They need to be provided with first class service. A huge number of different modes and challenges are presented. Various little animals act as cooks. These can be monkeys, pigs, lizards, dogs, cats and many other animals to choose from. You can set the style of the form and its color.

You have to not only cook, but also fight off angry and aggressive visitors. For example, jesters constantly hide some of the orders, and thieves are always ready to steal important ingredients, without which it is simply impossible to prepare a dish. You can customize your bosses and upgrade them in every possible way using the Do it your way function. There is a mode called House Party. Various skins, outfits and other cosmetics are gradually unlocked. It is possible to play alone with the help of a computer partner. Hardcore challenges are available for the most desperate and experienced team of chefs.
