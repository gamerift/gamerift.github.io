Game Description#

Dark Hours is a cooperative horror game for survival created by Piece of Cake. Here users are sent to a robbery, which sharply turns into the unknown. Locked in an auction house, they face a demonic being, which violates their plans. What began as a mission to theft of valuable things quickly turns into a battle for survival. The atmosphere changes when gamers understand that now their goal is to get out alive. In Title, up to four gamers can take part, which contributes to the development of the spirit work spirit.

Each participant must work together to explore the dark corners of the auction house. Puzzles are scattered throughout space, demanding joint work and creative thinking. Equipment management is crucial; Users must develop a strategy for their actions in order to find a way to security. With the onset of the night, the acuteness of the situation increases, increasing the significance of each decision made. Processedly generated cards in Dark Hours play an important role in gameplay.

Each session differs from another, which allows gamers to face new tasks every time they plunge into the game. Such diversity prevents monotony and makes users be involved in the process, trying to find out what is hidden around the next angle. Thanks to the combination of familiar settings and unpredictable elements, the situation remains fresh, prompting players to constantly adapt their strategies.
