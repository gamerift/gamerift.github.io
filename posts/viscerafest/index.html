Game Description#

Viscerafest is a dynamic shooter made in retro style. The game takes you into a sci-fi world where you have to fight alien invaders and various creepy monsters. The arsenal of weapons consists of various futuristic guns. Allows you to take the barrel in your hand to destroy enemies, shooting Macedonian style. Events take place in 3796.

The main character of Viscerafest is named Caroline. She is a mercenary and a psychopath rolled into one. Her wish is to marry the nice guy Athens Fetter. Wedding rings are very expensive, so she has no choice but to take on the case of the warlock Kromune. There is a very high price on his head. He uses the services of USC (United Science Conglomerate) and Dr. Mortis to protect his life. The campaign consists of 7 levels filled with non-stop action. There are 9 types of enemies in total.

Each enemy has its own unique movement and attack patterns. You have to rush through various corridors, complete tasks, look for key cards, solve puzzles, etc. The action takes place on space stations and in remote places of the galaxy. Soul Hunters pose a huge threat. Bunker Buster or Pung Cannon shotguns are effective against them. Ammunition is limited and requires constant collection of supplies, health points and armor. Cypher keys provide access to secret levels and modifications.
