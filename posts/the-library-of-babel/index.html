Game Description#

The Library of Babel is a stealth platformer, the actions of which are transferred to the world of the future in the jungle of futuristic Babylon. The main character is Louis - a detective investing in the murder case. He has to solve the secret why Babylon’s library was closed many years ago. What did the creators try to hide there? The plot line is inspired by the work of Horhe Borges. Humanity died out 20 thousand years ago. The world is inhabited by reasonable robots. Once, secret letters of the past are found, which fundamentally changes the idea of ​​the past of mankind.

It is ahead to fight with various cybersinity, exploring the long -abandoned and destroyed temples of people. It will be necessary to overcome a huge number of obstacles and solve puzzles. The events of The Library of Babel are developing among huge vegetation, since most of the planet has long died out and thicket with jungle. Huge emphasis is on stealth. The enemies are strong, so you need to try to go around them unnoticed so as not to enter into direct confrontation. For secrecy and pacifism, the game generously rewards with bonuses. The plot is served in the form of a graphic short story with the ability to choose a replica of a protagonist. Louis has an inventory where he adds useful and quest objects. They can be used during dialogues with characters, turning the situation in their direction and achieving the necessary goal of the development of events.
