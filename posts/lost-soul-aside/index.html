Game Description#

Lost Soul Aside is a role -playing action inspired by Ninja Gaiden and Final Fantasy XV. The game has a worked out combat mechanics and provides an opportunity to explore a huge open world inhabited by various phantasmagoric creatures. The visual style is inspired by the “final box” and all lovers of modern JRPG-games will appeal.

The game is based on a complex combat system, a characteristic series about deadly ninjo. You can conduct powerful combinations, dodge opponents and apply crushing counterattacks. The plot of the game starts 10 years after the Great War ended. Mysterious creatures began to penetrate our world. The protagonist of Lost Soul Aside is a caser who ended up at the epicenter of the battle and set off in search of magic crystals. The character does not pursue a global goal to save the world, but first of all longs to help himself.

Huge fantasy-world contains countless biomes and points of interest. During the passage, you will get acquainted with many charismatic heroes and fight on your way dozens of various monsters that differ not only in appearance, but also with unique tactics. Each specific adversary will need to look for an individual approach, including the type of weapon and a combination of shock. Initially, the game was developed by one person named Yan Bin on Unreal Engine 4, but later the project acquired his own studio and team.
