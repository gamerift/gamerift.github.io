Game Description#

Breach & Clear: Deadline is an isometric zombie survival game aimed at playing online with a friend, fighting in co-op mode over the Internet online. Free on the pirate Breach & Clear: Deadline, you need to join a group of survivors to fight an army of infected monsters. The virus is gradually spreading and with every passing minute the zombies are becoming more aggressive. An online game allows you to assemble a team of daredevils who are ready to sacrifice themselves in the name of saving humanity.

There are 4 elite special forces to choose from. Each hero in Breach & Clear: Deadline has their own unique abilities and fights with individual weapons. The fighters fire in real time, but tactical mechanics are also presented. All characters have unique tactical properties. The possibility of free online play allows you to experience team control over the characters, planning battles and interacting with each other in every possible way.

It is important to learn to act together and discuss tactics in advance. You have to fight back against an army of monsters, so any mistake can be fatal. A wide range of weapons, enemies, scenarios, quests and more are available. Players need to explore the huge city of Harbor City. It consists of several districts generated randomly. The gameplay features are RPG mechanics. There are many side quests, experience for completing tasks, increasing tactical characteristics and abilities, as well as unlocking rare weapons, equipment, supplies, etc.
