Game Description#

Just Another Night Shift pits you online and pits you against a friend in an intense game of cat and mouse. Think you have the reflexes of a seasoned night guard? Or perhaps the cunning of a master thief? This casual 3D indie pirate game will let you experience both sides of the coin. Play this exciting online game for free, online.

Each round drops you into a luxurious mansion with one goal: as a thief, steal a valuable figurine before the guard catches you. However, the job of a security guard is not all sleep and donuts. You also need to make sure that the fish are fed, the cheese is protected from hungry mice, and the exotic plant is watered - otherwise the game will be over for you.

The thief is not completely unarmed. His agility allows him to move around the mansion with ease, and his special ability to hide makes him a shadow in the corners. But a vigilant security guard with a well-trained dog and a network of cameras can make the statue seem sweeter. Luckily, both sides have access to shops - a black market for the thief and a security shop for the guard. Invest in useful items to give yourself an edge, but remember that strategy is just as important as a full wallet.

So grab a friend, pick a side, and see who comes out on top in Just Another Night Shift. It’s time to work the night shift of your life!
