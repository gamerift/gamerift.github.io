Game Description#

On the pirateEresys you can play online with a friend in co-op for free. Up to 4 people can gather online online to face monsters inspired by Lovecraft’s stories as cultists. The Eresys network game is built on constant interaction between participants. You need to explore a mysterious forest, fight enemies and solve puzzles. To successfully complete the stage, you will need to perform an occult ritual.

The main goal is to drive all the demons back to hell by sealing the portal in the level. You will need to look for resources to be sacrificed on the altar. Every second, dark entities take over the island, so you need to act as quickly as possible. The possibility of free online play offers both acting together and sharing responsibilities, trying to complete all the goals as quickly as possible. Artificial intelligence adapts to the actions of the players and always tries to find a way to kill the heroes.

Some game elements are randomly generated. For example, the location of objects, victims, books, caves and much more changes. You can rest in the cave. Scattered throughout the island are pages of a ritual book in which you can find a way to exorcise demons. The blood of the victims is needed to fill special vials. Oil lamps are needed to illuminate the path and scare away enemies. Banishing rituals can be performed on altars. The stage is considered completed when the occult ritual occurs at all altars in the location.
