Game Description#

Start Over is a multiplayer survival simulator set on a desert island, providing an immersive experience. You have to build a base, make the necessary equipment and defend against waves of enemies. The game features a realistic and dynamic environment where you need to adapt to constantly changing weather conditions, hunt animals, collect resources for survival and engage in trading items to earn money. As you explore the island, you can discover hidden secrets such as secret caves, ancient ruins, and even military camps.

To enhance the gameplay, there is a wide selection of tools, weapons and items that allow you to customize both your character and base. Whether you prefer simple shelters or complex fortresses, bows and arrows or weapons and explosives, basic clothing or armor and accessories, the possibilities for personalization are endless. One of the distinctive features of Start Over is multiplayer, which allows you to team up and create a community. This mode allows you to exchange resources, provide mutual assistance in completing tasks, and fight opponents together. Communication with other players is carried out through voice or text chat, which further expands the possibilities of co-op.

However, not all island residents are friendly. Gamers will often encounter hostile enemies and raiders who will relentlessly attack both them and their bases. To defend against these threats, you need to use weapons and traps strategically. For added flexibility, the game also allows you to change the difficulty of the waves to suit individual preferences - from peaceful to hard mode.
