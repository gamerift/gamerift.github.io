Game Description#

Worms Armageddon is a 2D turn-based strategy game that offers the opportunity to compete online or fight with a friend locally. Up to 8 people can join up to 8 people for free. Players can be divided into teams or play in one faction. Worms Armageddon allows you to make moves one by one, thinking through each of your actions.

The network game offers two teams to fight in various locations. The environment is interactive and fully deformable. Worms Armageddon characters can jump, crawl, teleport, etc. Each participant is armed with a unique weapon. The possibility of free online play also allows the worms to use a huge number of gadgets and devices. For example, a parachute, rope, etc. The faction that destroys all enemy fighters wins. All worms have a health bar. When injured, it gradually decreases and tends to zero. If you fall into the water, the character instantly dies.

Training is available, deathmatches are presented that increase the rank of the team, etc. There is character customization. you can choose flags, fanfare, special weapons and even a grave option. Gasoline barrels can be blown up, causing enormous damage to those around you. Heroes have the right to use artillery and carry out airstrikes. Weapons include bazookas, shotguns, grenade launchers, etc. Weapons can randomly fall at a certain point on the map. Sometimes superweapons can be found in blue boxes.
