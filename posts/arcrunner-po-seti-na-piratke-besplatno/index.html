Game Description#

ArcRunner takes place on a Titan-class space station - the Ark. The artificial intelligence was struck by the Metavirus, which infected the ship’s Core. The company sends its best operatives to reboot the central computer. For free, you can play the pirate ArcRunner online with a friend or cooperate in a team of up to three people. Over the Internet, online partners will have to fight against the rebel machines while exploring the station. Levels are generated randomly. You will need to go through an intense and difficult path, consisting of 7 stages, starting from the City and ending with Eden Heights.

Free online play allows you to play as one of three android heroes with their own unique tactical abilities. Soldier, ninja and hacker are available. Depending on the choice of character, the style of play changes. You can be a martial artist, a fast assassin, or an experienced burglar. The network game is aimed at interaction between partners. Heroes fight using unique weapons: an energy hammer, a katana or electricity.

Enemies and bosses drop upgrades or upgraded weapons. New weapons can also be found in chests hidden throughout the levels. The characteristics of guns can be increased. The barrels are subject to pumping using various modifications. Nanite allows you to pump up and receive meta improvements in a cryochamber. Using an autodoc, you can add 4 additions to your equipment, affecting strength, weapon effects, movement speed, and much more. Add-ons can be improved fivefold. All protagonists have their own individual extras.
