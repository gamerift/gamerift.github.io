Game Description#

Choo Choo Survivor is an exciting adventure in the world, teeming with a zombie, where you have to take the train and break through the endless waves of undead. Chu Chu Survaivor is a unique mixture of Bullet Hell and Horde Survival genres, where you can configure your weapons and abilities in accordance with your game style. You can choose one of different trains, each with their own characteristics and weapons, and improve them as they move.

But be careful, zombies are not so easy to kill. They come in different shapes and sizes, and some of them are even bosses that challenge your skills. You will need to competently use your abilities, such as the Exp magnet, a critical blow, an air blow and an orbital blow that can be opened by performing achievements. Choo Choo Survivor is a fun and fascinating game that will entertain you for hours. You can try a free demo here, on Steam, and make sure that. Do not miss the chance to become the best survivor on the train! Among the features you can highlight the following:

Several locomotives for unlocking, each with their own statistics and weapons. A lot of funny meta-progressions and significant achievements of Steam for unlocking. Developers continue to add new interesting materials.
