Game Description#

On the piratedGTA 3 you can play for free online with a friend or online against other players from all over the world over the Internet. The huge interactive city of Liberty City is available for exploration. You are allowed to steal vehicles, run away from the police, fight gangs, complete numerous missions and move freely around the metropolis. The GTA 3 network game is aimed at co-op and player confrontation.

Participants can get into any type of vehicle, including cars, trucks and motorcycles, and simply fight among themselves. Grand Theft Auto 3 has migrated to a full-fledged three-dimensional space. Now the gameplay is a full-fledged third-person action game. To complete missions, you need to go to telephone booths or visit crime bosses who issue various orders to a hero named Claude. The possibility of free online play completely transfers all the mechanics from the single-player mode.

GTA 3 characters can jump, fight hand-to-hand and shoot weapons. If you fall into the water, instant death occurs. You can kill passers-by and break the law, but in this case the hunt for the hero begins on the part of law enforcement agencies. Depending on the level of violations, the police are represented by patrols, police helicopters, special forces, FBI agents and the US Army.

Every vehicle can be stolen. A total of 54 types of vehicles are presented, including 4 boats and one airplane. You can also drive an ambulance, a police car, a fire truck and a tank. Depending on the area, it is controlled by different groups: Devils, Triads, Yardies, Hoods, Yakuza, Mafia and Colombian Cartel. The story campaign features characters such as Salvatore Leone, Donald Love, Phil Cassidy, Catalina and Toni Cipriani.
