Game Description#

Poly Plaza Ultimate sends a player to an open city to purchase property and rent rent. The key task is to manage an economic enterprise through the sale of resources and the commission of strategic purchases to increase capital. The main goal is the accumulation of wealth in the worldwater, filled with non-game characters, parked cars and a dynamic change of day and night. Such an environment serves as a background for all commercial undertakings.

Promotion in the simulator Poly Plaza Ultimate is tied to a comprehensive system for creating objects. The method of obtaining clothes is completely redesigned: now it must be created using intra -game materials. This innovation applies to many other objects, since the project contains a large number of new recipes. The gameplay proposes to collect components in the surrounding world for the production of tools, equipment and personal items necessary for doing business.

This version of the game includes all previously released loaded content. Additions expand the list of available classes and assets, adding a collection of new vehicles for traveling around the city. Moreover, additional collectible objects appeared in the world, offering tasks that go beyond the simple accumulation of capital. All these elements together form a more complete title about entrepreneurship with advanced capabilities.
