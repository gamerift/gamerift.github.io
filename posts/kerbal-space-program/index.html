Game Description#

In the space simulatorKerbal Space Program the player will lead the Kerbal race exploring the vastness of space. Be the one who guides them towards a brighter future, inventing new technologies, constructing many new mechanisms and helping in the exploration of the galaxy.

Start your journey in one of three modes. In “Career” you will need to monitor the education and training of your charges, as well as help in modernizing their devices. In the “Science” mode, the user will be able to conduct various experiments and study new devices for conquering outer space. In a creative game, the gamer will be able to do whatever he wants, gradually understanding the mechanics and more than hundreds of available parts and mechanisms.

Based on the laws of physics, it is necessary to assemble space rockets in order to launch them into orbit, and then travel on them in space. Create spaceships from many parts, each of which has a special function. The player will be able to take control, fully controlling the flight process.

Create interplanetary bases, visit unique moons and planets, and assemble orbital stations with your own hands. Establish communication between the planets and the Center. Build bases on uncharted planets, train and select astronauts to write their names in history. Mine resources in your own or another galaxy, create new technologies and conquer the entire space!
