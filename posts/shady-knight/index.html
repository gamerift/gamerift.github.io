Game Description#

Shady Knight is a first-person hand-to-hand combat game. The environment is interactive and contains a huge amount of dynamic combat. The gameplay combines elements of platforming. You will need to go through many towers and brutally tear apart your enemies. You use a sword with which you kill your enemies. You can also kick opponents onto spikes, shoot enemies with fire, and throw swords at their bodies to pin them to walls. You can use the environment to throw objects at enemies.

Having knocked down an enemy, you must act quickly, since the adversaries are stunned for a short time. When jumping from high points, you can use an enhanced sword strike Slam Slash - an effective way to instantly kill an enemy. There is also a blow that can throw NPCs into the air. Traps that are located in locations can be used for your own purposes.

Fire can be transferred from one body to another. Fragile structures can also be thrown onto opponents. Take advantage of all the possibilities that Shady Knight gameplay provides. The game has a minimalistic visual style and contains scenes of violence, cruelty and blood.
