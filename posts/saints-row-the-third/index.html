Game Description#

The Third Street Saints are a large and large group in the universe of the gameSaints Row: The Third. The third-person shooter with its crazy plots gave the game’s heroes their own brand of sneakers, a carbonated drink and a logo, and now they have to fight the world’s largest crime syndicate, whose people are scattered around the world and do their work in the “shadows”. Since the main characters have long captured Stillwater, the Syndicate demands offerings from them in the form of tribute for the criminal leadership. The Saints are not a criminal group, but a collection of friends fighting for the same idea, which is why they refuse to pay.

Throughout the game we will kill, cut off hands and fly around Stillwater in the person of the Boss. Metropolis contains the criminal Syndicate, because they managed to seize power in the city. The plot tells us how many criminals united and killed, divided the spoils, and after that one of the leaders, analogue of 246 in the Triad, decided to create his own association and seize power. The entire police and every military man in Steelport has been bribed and put on a sharp financial needle. We have to not only beat the crap out of criminals, but also return the city to its past freedom, which no one knows about.

The abilities of the heroes remained at the same level of pumping - we also get points for completing tasks, we also pump up heroes, make weapons or buy them in stores. The map remained open, but the scale increased. Saints Row: The Third has become a game with an age limit, because we will defeat a gang of Mexican dancers with a rubber sex toy, drop a tank on a gang of wielding dancers, and call for air support.

There are several dozen key characters in Saints Row 3, some of them are leaders in criminal gangs. As soon as the Boss decided to ignite a war between the factions, the rest of the gangs arrived in Steelport, deciding that they could seize power in the city. We will have to fight with everyone. The game has positive and negative endings.
