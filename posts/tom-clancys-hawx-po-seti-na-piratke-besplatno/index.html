Game Description#

The flight simulator**Tom Clancy’s H.A.W.X. **can be played online with a friend or completed single missions in solo mode. A multiplayer mode is available for free on the pirate site, allowing you to play over the Internet online or on a local network. The abbreviation stands for High Altitude Warfare eXperimental Squadron. Free online play includes co-op play through the story campaign.

There are 54 aircraft to choose from. Each vehicle in Tom Clancy’s HAWX has individual characteristics. Most of the planes open up as you progress. You must accumulate experience points and complete secondary tasks to gain access to special types of transport. Single and multiplayer games are taken to the near future. Since the 2012 Reykjavik Accords, military companies have played a huge role in national defense.

Many elements are carried over from previous games in the series. The action takes place between EndWar and Advanced Warfighter. Contains many references to other parts of the series. For example, there are Easter eggs for Splinter Cell. In some episodes, the Hawks cover a squad of Ghosts, etc. Missions take place in Mexico, Central Africa, Afghanistan, Brazil and other countries. The storyline consists of 19 missions. The tasks are that you need to destroy all rebel forces, gain a certain height, get to a specified point, etc. The complexity of the missions increases with each new chapter.
