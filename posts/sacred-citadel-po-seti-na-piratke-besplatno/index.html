Game Description#

Sacred Citadel is a spin-off of the series of the same name, made in beat-em-up style. On the pirate Sacred Citadel, you can play online for free with a friend, going through the story campaign in co-op. You can walk through it on one screen and connect via the Internet online. Events take place in the world of Ancaria. It is necessary to join the fight against the Ash Empire, fighting the Grimmock orcs, seraphs and other minions for.

Network play allows three players to join forces. There are a total of 4 characters to choose from. Each hero in Sacred Citadel represents a unique class and has individual skills. While traveling around the world, you can ride animals, get into combat vehicles and learn new abilities. You can also loot treasuries to find jewelry or powerful weapons. The heroes' abilities are combined, resulting in a unique protagonist build. The possibility of free online play includes interaction between participants: you will need to share resources, fight enemies together, cover your partner, solve puzzles together, etc.

Heroes have 4 unique parameters that can be developed as they progress. The world consists of several unique cities. Each one contains charismatic heroes, new quests, enemies and much more. The characters are warrior, mage and other classes. By choosing one of the protagonists, the style of passage changes. One deals strong blows in close combat, the second is effective at long range due to fireballs, etc.
