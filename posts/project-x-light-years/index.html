Game Description#

In the Project X: Light Years, the user will have to pilot a modern starship through enemy territories 30 years after the initial battle. There is navigation on various locations, including asteroid fields and outposts in deep space, where it will have to be opposed by the waves of alien opponents. The main task is to collect objects that improve the firepower, speed and protection of the ship.

This Title is an official continuation of the series that arose on the Amiga platform and is based on the mechanics of the sid-chilling shooter, requiring high accuracy for survival. Project X gameplay: Light Years encounters pilots with many enemies with unique behaviors. Contractions over time become more complicated, moving from maneuverable fighters to colossal bosses, requiring special strategies to break through their defense. Local coop gives the second user to join, opening the opportunity for coordinated attacks.

During the missions, you can get 35 different strengthening, introducing unpredictable elements to the process and allowing the vessel to configure the vessel for a specific task. The structure of the game is built on a linear passage of a series of levels. Several complexity modes are provided, including an adaptive system that corrects the call depending on the actions of the pilot. The results are monitored in the attack mode for glasses with global record tables.

Key aspects of the galactic war:

Dynamic battles with hordes of enemies and giant bosses. A system of improvement of the ship with 35 random bonuses. The possibility of passing the mission together in the local coop. A variety of levels: from asteroid fields to enemy bases. Competitive mode with record tables and many secrets.
