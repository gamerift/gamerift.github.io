Game Description#

TOEM is an adventure puzzle game made in black and white style. Go fancy. expedition, revealing the secrets of the magical world of TOEM. The visual style is made entirely in a hand-drawn monochrome manner. Meet funny characters and help solve their problems. Travel the world and take different photos.

Puzzles revolve around exploring the environment and taking pictures. By taking a photo, you can find the answer to your question, thereby helping local animals with pressing problems. Visit a magical forest, meeting colorful and interesting animals and anthropomorphic creatures. Carefully explore the environment, which is fraught with many secrets and mysteries. Don’t be fooled by the cuteness of the surrounding characters. They are not that simple!

Every character in TOEM hides, conceals, or keeps something back. As a young researcher and photographer, in the role of the main character you will have to uncover the mysteries of this place and bring everyone to light. Is this place as utopian as it wants to seem? The answers to these questions must be answered by a talented naturalist. What is hidden from view, but can be seen by the lens? The solution is inside the game.
