Game Description#

Resident Evil Village continues the adventures of Ethan from the Seven. The Roman numeral hidden in the title on the posters hints that this is not a spin-off, but the eighth part of the series. This is already the tenth project in the main line. Winters was able to escape from the cannibal mansion and save his wife Mia. They got away from the American outback and decided to live an ordinary life. However, a few years later, Ethan finds himself in the wilderness again, only this time in a snowy area. His arrival in the mystical village was facilitated by Chris Redfield himself, the legendary RE character.

After experimenting with remakes, the creators returned the first-person view to the series. Previously, locations served only as a backdrop, albeit a well-made one, for stories about confronting the monsters of the Umbrella corporation. In Resident Evil Village, the surroundings are so detailed that the village can be regarded as a full-fledged character. The protagonist of Resident Evil 8 will face the past, present and future of a mysterious settlement located near the castle. In addition to monsters, he will also meet normal people. The Duke, for example. You can ask rumors and buy items from this polite and courteous inhabitant of the Village.

Melee weapons now break during use. An exception was made only for the main character’s knife. Classic firearms include a pistol and a shotgun. Much emphasis is placed on exploring locations, finding useful things and keys, solving puzzles, and inventory management. Opponents here can use weapons and wear armor.
