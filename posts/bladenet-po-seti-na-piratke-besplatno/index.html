Game Description#

BLADENET is a multiplayer third-person shooter that allows you to play online with a friend, playing on the same team, or participate as a pirate in massive battles, competing for free in classic deathmatch and other modes online online. The setting is made in a futuristic manner and is aimed at cybernization, hacking, etc. Online play makes it possible not only to take part in shootouts, but also to steal important data in order to successfully escape from the crime scene. If you manage to remain anonymous, you can get more victory points.

The gameplay is presented from a first-person perspective. You can take dual pistols and go into direct confrontation, or try to act more delicately, going through a stealth mission. The possibility of free online play offers the feeling of being a real spy, using various advanced technologies, gadgets and disguises. It is important to be able to blend in with the crowd by hiding in crowded places. When the identity is revealed, it is necessary to enter into direct confrontation with living people. Any incorrect behavior arouses interest from the crowd, so you need to be as careful as possible.

Events develop in the digital world called BLADENET. Inside it there is a simulation of life and all the information about any person is stored. However, a group of hackers decides to sabotage and steal important data. The crowd is programmed to monitor any deviations from social norms, so it is very important not to give yourself away in any way. Bloodnet has the status of a virtual state with its own rules, laws and principles. Once in this world, getting out of it is not so easy.
