Game Description#

Roady Life is a truck driver simulator that offers the opportunity to play online as a pirate with a friend for free, driving different trucks and delivering valuable cargo online online. The network game is aimed at a competitive element, where each participant will have to get ahead of his competitor in order to get more money to buy a new vehicle or upgrade an existing truck.

The gameplay allows you to create your own transport company, dealing with logistics and hiring employees. You can also drive the truck yourself to deliver the cargo safe and sound in the shortest possible time. The possibility of free online play allows up to four people to team up, creating their own long-range team or arranging a confrontation between companies. Having opened your own business, you will need to be prepared for difficulties: making a schedule, searching for customers, pumping or repairing vehicles, etc. Features and content of Roady Life: single adventure or playing with friends; purchasing new trucks, upgrading and maintaining them; many types of cargo: bulk, liquids, passengers, etc.; time management and numerous economic issues; huge number of contracts, goals and orders; budget management and expense planning; investing in developments and improvements; purchasing goods, starting your own production and much more.
