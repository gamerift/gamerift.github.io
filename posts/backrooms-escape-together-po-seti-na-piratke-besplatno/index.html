Game Description#

The horror gameBackrooms: Escape Together can be played online with a friend or group on a pirate for free, trying to escape from the entity lurking in the shadows. Having teamed up with your teammates online, you need to constantly interact with each other, performing various tasks at the levels. Free online play allows one player to take on the role of the monster. His task is to hunt for survivors. The action takes place in the back rooms, which are a confusing labyrinth. Several unique locations are available with their own unique features, tasks and items.

A team of survivors must constantly keep in touch with each other and share tasks to successfully achieve the goal. The rooms and locations of objects are generated randomly, thereby greatly increasing the dynamics of the gameplay. The network game Backrooms: Escape Together is an asymmetrical multiplayer horror game in 1 versus 4 mode. Each hero has his own set of features that must be applied in a given situation. Success depends on the coherence of all team members. Individually, the characters are weak and cannot do anything against the monster. The creature becomes stronger every minute, so the speed and attentiveness of each individual participant is important. The environment must be used for your own purposes. You can hide behind shelters or in special storage rooms to hide from your pursuer.
