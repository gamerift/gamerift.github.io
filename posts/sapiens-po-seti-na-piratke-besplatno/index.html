Game Description#

Sapiens takes players to a primitive world where you can play online with a friend to build a civilization from the ground up. In this colony simulator, available for free even on a pirate site, you will lead your tribe through millennia of progress. You will start with basic tools and end with complex technologies. The game’s huge procedurally generated map, available online online, dwarfs the size of the Earth, offering a variety of conditions for settlement. Each location has unique resources and obstacles, making your initial choices critical to long-term success in multiplayer.

As you develop your settlement, you will guide individual tribe members to specialize in different tasks. They will hone their skills in areas such as farming, construction and research, becoming increasingly valuable assets over time. You will need to balance their needs with their mood while supporting the growth of families in your community. The game emphasizes resource management and exploration, with players going on expeditions to discover new materials and innovations.

Multiplayer adds a cooperative dimension to Sapiens. Team up with friends to realize ambitious projects, such as sprawling cities or intricate trade networks. There is no player-versus-player combat, instead the focus is on cooperation and shared achievements. You can also interact with AI-controlled tribes, each of which specializes in different crafts, opening up opportunities for mutually beneficial exchange.
