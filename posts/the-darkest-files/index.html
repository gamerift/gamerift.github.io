Game Description#

The Darkest Files unfolds in post -war Germany in 1956, in the period marked by the consequences of World War II and a complex process of searching justice. The user rises in place Esther Katz, a young prosecutor who joins the special unit of Fritz Bauer, who is instructed to investigate war crimes committed many years ago.

In this role, the gamer will have to find hidden evidence, to navigate in a society that does not want to recognize his past, and to confront criminals, who are now occupied by leading posts. The Darkest Files offers a historical background with an emphasis on the personal and public struggle at this turning point in history. Throughout the Title, the gamers are faced with the need to make complex decisions, trying to collect the evidence and testimony of witnesses.

The narrative unfolds from several points of view, and Esther’s eye offers to look at various versions of events presented by witnesses. Some evidence may be truthful, others - distorted or deliberately misleading. The investigation process is full of difficulties, since time is a decisive factor, and silence often becomes a formidable obstacle to establishing truth. The investigation is non -linear, each solution has consequences that may affect the outcome. Users must use their skills to break through the walls of silence and expose the lie, trying to restore justice for those who have avoided it for many years.
