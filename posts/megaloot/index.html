Game Description#

Megaloot occupies a unique niche in the world of role-playing games, requiring gamers to pay meticulous attention to inventory management. Unraveling a mysterious world in which a colossal structure has the ability to replicate objects from countless realities, the title sets the stage for an odyssey filled with strategic choices and tactical delights. Users will find more than 100 different items, each of which boasts its own characteristics. Combine them cleverly to create a dominant build - the possibilities are mind-boggling.

There are 23 stats presented here - from the little-known “Toxicity” stat to the more familiar “Strength” and “Wisdom”. Strategically combine them with loot and unique deck cards to create your playstyle. The tests take place on 99 floors of an endless tower. Each level offers new opportunities to hone your strategy and tactics. And here the heart of the gameplay stops - mastery of inventory becomes paramount.

Strategic skill, combined with a deck of cards and loot, is the key to constructing buildings that will help you cope with the challenges that spiral upward. Climb a tower and you’ll find yourself in constant motion, adapting and evolving. Experimentation and discovery are woven into the very fabric of the game, offering many paths to triumph. With a laser focus on strategic inventory management and a huge variety of buildings on offer, Megaloot brings a breath of fresh air to the RPG genre, keeping users glued to the screen until the very end.
