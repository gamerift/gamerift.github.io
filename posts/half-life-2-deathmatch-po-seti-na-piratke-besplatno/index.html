Game Description#

Half-Life 2: Deathmatch is a competitive shooter that you can play online online, fighting with a friend or random players. All three confrontation modes are available for free on the pirate site. The environment and physics are completely borrowed from the original Half-Life 2. The possibility of free online play includes both a standard deathmatch and a team game Combine vs. resistance. Network game Half-Life 2: Deathmatch includes: Deathmatch - a classic death match, where each participant fights for himself; Team Deathmatch - similar to the previous mode, but aimed at group competitions; Puzzle - a puzzle where all participants start on the same Rebel team, choosing your model from the list in the process.

By winning a deathmatch, points are awarded that determine the champion. After death there is an instant respawn. Having died, the character starts with a set of standard characteristics and a default weapon. In team matches, you must split into Rebels and Alliance. Each faction has its own unique heroes from Half-Life 2. The team whose player scores the maximum number of victory points wins.

A special feature of the puzzle mode is that everyone starts without guns. You can take the gravity gun. The cards offer a variety of tests. They are often divided into two types: single and cooperative. The tasks consist of not dying, passing difficult obstacles, building bridges, etc. By successfully completing quests, victory points are awarded. The faction whose member scores the most points wins.
