Game Description#

In the center of Xiaomei and the Flame Dragon’s Fist is the story of Xiao May, a martial artist who is looking for his older sister of Xiao In. The narrative was built in the conflict of two schools: the heroine owns a fist of the dragon fiery, while her sister suffered the dark art of a fist of the murderer of a dark dragon. Xiao in disappeared, succumbing to the temptation of forbidden power, which made her sister go on a dangerous journey for her salvation.

The path of the fire dragon: the main features of Xiaomei and the Flame Dragon’s Fist:

The plot about the salvation of the sister and the confrontation of styles. A fighting combining basic blows and special techniques. Classic levels with consistent waves of enemies. Stressed battles with bosses in the final of each location.

The gameplay offers to move along two -dimensional locations, sequentially cleaning them from the advancing groups of enemies. The fighting is based on direct clashes, where the protagonist uses standard strokes with arms and legs. To defeat numerous opponents, special skills are also provided, causing increased damage.

Promotion to the next stage opens only after the complete elimination of all threats on the current screen. The final of each level is marked by a battle with a unique and powerful boss that checks the player’s skills. This title organically combines the personal drama of the sisters with a continuous arcade action, immersing the militant about the eastern martial arts into the atmosphere. Passing requires a quick reaction and gradual development of combat techniques to successfully overcome more and more complex tests.
