Game Description#

Pizza Tower stands out with its pixel graphics and animation. The art imitates old cartoon series from the end of the last century. The project also boasts an energetic soundtrack. It is a 2D side-scrolling platformer. This title is the spiritual successor to the video games from the Wario Land series. In it you can walk left and right, run, accelerate, jump, make dashes, break some obstacles, ride mounts, and turn into monsters. It is possible to hit opponents and parry their attacks. The gameplay consists not only of battles with enemies and overcoming obstacles. Here you can also study the stages. There are also boss battles. To gain access to them you need to collect the filling and earn money. There is a small TV in the upper right corner of the screen. It shows the character’s changing facial expressions.

The Pizza Tower is an object that the hero wants to destroy. In the story, Peppino Spaghetti tries to save his restaurant. Besides this balding Italian, they sometimes let a guy named Gustavo drive the place. According to the plot, a pizza-faced villain wants to blow up the protagonist’s establishment from his hideout using a laser. To prevent this from happening, the chef must go to the enemy’s lair, go upstairs and deal with the antagonist. The building consists of 5 floors, each of which is divided into many different levels with hidden areas. Locations can be completed quickly to get as many points as possible, or explored at your own pace.
