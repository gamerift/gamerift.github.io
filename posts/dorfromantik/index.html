Game Description#

Dorfromantik - strategy in low-poly style. The game encourages you not to think about military strategies and operations. enjoy peaceful buildings and development of your lands. Game mechanics of Dorfromantic combine classic RTS and puzzle. Create a village, developing and expanding, building new buildings on hexagonal tiles.

Explore a procedurally generated world filled with diverse and colorful biomes. Complete tasks to fill locations with new life and create truly unique worlds. Immerse yourself in a quiet and peaceful atmosphere, taking your mind off the hustle and bustle of everyday life. Develop your settlement and discover how wonderful country life can be. Features of Dorfromantik: creation of endless landscapes; a unique combination of strategy and puzzles; landscapes of villages and wildlife; leaderboards and records; random generation of game processes.

The game starts with a few hexagonal tiles, gradually installing new buildings on them and expanding the territory. Combine a variety of types of landscapes: forests, mountains, rivers, lakes, etc. Gradually, special objects with which quests are associated will become available to you. For example, place a windmill with 6 grain fields, install a locomotive so that it can go along 10 tracks, or place a deer in a forest that should consist of 50 trees. By completing tasks, you gain access to new tiles and biomes.
