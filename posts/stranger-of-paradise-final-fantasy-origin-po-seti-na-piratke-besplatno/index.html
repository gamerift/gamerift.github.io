Game Description#

Stranger of Paradise Final Fantasy Origin is a spinoff of the cult series Final Fantasy, which you can play for free online as a pirate with a friend, exploring the vast expanses of a fictional world called Cornelia, fighting together online online against the army of Darkness. The main character of the story is called Jack. The online game allows you to become a partner of the protagonist in order to jointly fight back the villain Garland.

There can be up to three heroes in a party. All team members act as Warriors of Light. Darkness is approaching the protagonists' native lands, and only Jack and his team are able to stop the apocalypse. The main focus of Stranger of Paradise Final Fantasy Origin is on the complex combat system. Free online play allows characters to interact to unleash powerful attacks. All heroes can be customized according to your own preferences. There are 8 types of weapons available, both melee and ranged.

There is a system of professions. You can simultaneously select two types of activities for your character, switching between them in real time. Available skills include Fencer, Duelist, Fist Fighter, Mage, Lancer, Swordsman, Ronin and Marauder. There is also a skill tree that allows you to upgrade additional abilities. There are a huge number of main and secondary quests available. They can be taken from local residents if you select the appropriate dialogue line. The heroes will visit places such as the Temple of Chaos, the ruins of Ronk, the Floating Continent, the Crystal Tower and many other locations.
