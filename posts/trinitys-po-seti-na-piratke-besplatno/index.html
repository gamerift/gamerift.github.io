Game Description#

TrinityS is a fantasy adventure that allows you to play online as a pirate for free, going through the multiplayer Boss Rush together with a friend. Co-op mode is available online or locally. Free online play allows you to team up with up to three people to take on numerous enemies and powerful bosses.

A feature of the TrinityS gameplay are the powerful leaders, each of whom has a unique attack pattern. You will need to find an individual approach to each type of enemy. A network game is based on teamwork and cooperation between participants. Positioning and ability to manage the character’s abilities play a key role. The trinity is represented by 3 unique heroes: Gray is a knight who covers his partners with shields; Emil is a magician who defeats opponents with powerful spells; Bell is a priest who can heal allied units.

All protagonists have their own unique skills. Heroes can be customized between battles to create your own custom unit build. Each participant plays a role in the adventure. You will need to become a real and united team to fight back against the powerful monster. To increase characteristics and abilities, it is necessary to use passive enhancements and upgrade the skills of the protagonists. In single player mode, you will need to go through the campaign with two partners controlled by artificial intelligence.
