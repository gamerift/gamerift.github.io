Game Description#

Tavern Talk throws you into the very heart of the fantasy kingdom, but not as a powerful warrior or clever robber. Here you will take into your hands the reins of the reign of a cozy tavern Wayfarer’s Inn. But this is not an ordinary bartender simulator. The “Traveler” tavern is filled with a whisper and secrets of adventure seekers - your regulars. This is not just thirsty souls who want to drink a mug of ale. They are the life power of the “taverns”. Each tired traveler who looked at your door brings with him a story, rumor, a fragment of the unfolding history of the world.

Your work is to listen and learn from them juicy pieces of information. You are a bartender, yes, but you are also a rumor and a weaving narrative. The tavern becomes your central point. This is not just a place where drinks are served, but also a knot for a web of stories that connect the Tavern of Conversations. Every tired warrior and gossip-bard, which crossed the threshold of your institution, contributes. It is here, among tanks and flickering light of candles, decisions are made. What rumors do you decide to spread? What tasks will you push your regulars to?

Remember that in the “tavern” the fate of the world can depend on the stories heard and the drinks presented. So put on your metaphorical hat of the listener and get ready to master the power of information. Tavern Talk invites you to enter the world where the action is not all. Here, the weight of whispering hearing can be as significant as the swing of the hero’s sword. Each of your solution, every story heard can change the course of the life of the adventurer’s life, and maybe the world itself.
