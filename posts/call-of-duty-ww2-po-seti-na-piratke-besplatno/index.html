Game Description#

The pirateCall of Duty WW2 features many multiplayer modes that you can play online for free with a friend or random players from around the world. The network game is available online online. You can arm yourself with authentic guns and equipment. The action takes place during the Second World War. The possibility of free online play offers you to fight in Division mode. Here each player creates his own character and chooses a class. The social zone is called Headquarters, and there is also a story-based co-op mode called War.

The gameplay is a tactical first-person shooter. In battles you can get 10 prestige. There are a total of 55 levels available. You can choose 6 divisions: Infantry, Landing, Mechanized Corps, Mountain Rifleman, Expeditionary Forces and Partisans. The single-player campaign in Call of Duty: WWII allows you to complete missions such as D-Day, Tank Armada Breakthrough, Citadel, etc. Call of Duty: World War II allows you to take part in many of the famous battles of the mid-20th century. Events are developing in Germany, France and Belgium.

On the battlefields, you can perform heroic deeds, such as Enemy Surrender, Combat or Medical Rescue. Allied forces are fighting alongside the hero. You can give them orders, building unique tactics.
