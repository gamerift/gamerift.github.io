Game Description#

Splatter - Zombiecalypse Now takes players into a dark, nightmare world where multiplayer turns into a real test of survival. Here you can play online with a friend, fighting countless hordes of zombies. Even on a pirate site, you can play the game online for free, exploring 16 exciting levels filled with a noir atmosphere and voiced dialogues.

The streets require adaptation, and the game offers an innovative light-based combat system. Zombies shrink in light, creating tactical opportunities for players to manipulate their flashlights while managing limited resources. Each encounter offers new survival lessons, from optimal weapon selection to critical battlefield positioning. The environment itself becomes a weapon: walls crumble, obstacles crumble, occasionally revealing hidden caches of supplies.

Money earned from successfully completing missions goes into an extensive weapon upgrade system that transforms basic firearms into specialized weapons of destruction. The single-player campaign branches out into numerous side quests, each adding new layers to the apocalyptic saga. Once the story concludes, Survival mode kicks in, challenging players to test their mettle against endless hordes. Local co-op mode supports up to four players, turning zombie hunting into a social experience where any control scheme from keyboard to controller is at play, giving everyone a chance to survive.
