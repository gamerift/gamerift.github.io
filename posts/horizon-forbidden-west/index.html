Game Description#

Horizon Forbidden West – continuation of the adventures of the hunter Aloy, from the Nora tribe. In this part, the heroine finds herself on the coast of the former United States, where she will encounter new obstacles and deadly machines. You will have to use your bow and your ward’s abilities to confront your opponents and find out the secret of the islands. Watching Aloy from a third person, the user navigates the open world, completing main and side quests.

The forbidden west hides new and more dangerous secrets. The gamer will encounter giant machines and meet tribes in a post-apocalyptic environment. The world is on the brink of destruction, and humanity is on the brink of choice. No one knows the reason for the appearance of the formidable machines, and the main character sets off alone to unravel the mysterious events. She will meet old friends and make a truce with many aggressive factions.

The vast area includes locations such as flooded cities, American mountains, dense forests, beaches and archipelagos of islands. In each clash, the user will be able to make tactical and strategic decisions, come up with their own plan of attack and identify the weak points of their opponents. Gradually, the heroine gets to new and better equipment, upgrades her abilities and learns more about the weaknesses of her opponents. Swimming underwater is available, where there will be many hidden objects and elements. In this part, the opponents became more formidable and stronger.
