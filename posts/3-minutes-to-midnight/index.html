Game Description#

3 Minutes to Midnight is a casual indie adventure with all kinds of puzzles, filled with satire and parodies of modern society, humor (sometimes dark and rude) and unpredictable plot twists. This project uses a side view, which allows movement in two dimensions. The backgrounds are detailed and made in three-dimensional style. Control is available using a controller from any manufacturer for maximum convenience.

The story campaign tells the story of a strange night explosion somewhere in Mexico that changed the life of a young girl named Betty Anderson. After waking up, she discovered that she remembered practically nothing from her past. Restoring the facts of her life, she will simultaneously have to save human civilization from a conspiracy that could lead to the destruction of the entire planet. Helping her in this difficult task will be the second protagonist of the game “3 Minutes to Midnight” - a police major named Eliza Barrett. Events take place in different territories: a secret government base, the White House, a city in the province, a farm, a church, and so on.

Each of the 40 characters involved in the story has its own backstory and personality. Interaction with them leads to a variety of dialogues, from which you need to highlight important information and move forward in the plot. The puzzles found in each location will require the player’s utmost concentration and ingenuity to interact with the outside world.
