Game Description#

Dead Rising 3 is an open-world zombie action game that you can play online with a friend or compete with random players online. The gameplay includes elements of crafting and survival, allowing you to explore an entire city consisting of four districts for free as a pirate. Each area contains unique enemies and a different set of quests. It is necessary to destroy zombies using a variety of available means, including melee weapons and self-crafted tools. The possibility of free online play allows you to combine weapons in every possible way, turning an ordinary stick into a deadly saw or a deadly bat with spikes.

Online play allows you to use a special workbench to craft unique weapons. Transport is available and can also be upgraded. The main character of the adventure is auto mechanic Nick Ramos. The hero has a tattoo of 12 on his neck. He is one of 50 artificially infected orphans. But the antagonist Carlito Keys gave him immunity to the virus. The main mystery of the game is connected with this character, because he finds the name Liz Jackson on the list of orphans, which may be his real name. Characters of Dead Rising 3: Dick - a truck driver and a character for a cooperative game; Annie - Nick’s girlfriend and an illegal immigrant; Rhonda - the owner of a workshop in Inlton; General Hemlock - a military man and the main villain of the adventure; Diego - a friend of the protagonist with a 49 tattoo.

The plot also features characters such as Harry, Red, Marian, Isabella Keyes and others. The events take place in the city of Los Perdidos, California. There was an outbreak of a zombie virus, as a result of which all residents turned into bloodthirsty creatures. The plot starts 72 hours after the epidemic. The entire city was filled with an army of the living dead. The hero will have to break through entire hordes of the undead, using all available means.
