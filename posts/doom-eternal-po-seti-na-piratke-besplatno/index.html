Game Description#

DOOM Eternal is a first-person shooter that allows you to play as a pirate for free online with a friend. Online internet connection available. The free online game feature includes multiplayer competition, where players not only fight demons, but also fight each other using a huge variety of weapons. The main plot gives you the opportunity to become the Executioner of Doom in order to close the portal to hell, where demons come from.

The gameplay is based on classic FPS mechanics: there is hardcore platforming, collecting first aid kits, armor, ammo, collectibles, etc. Between missions you can explore the hub location and modify the guns. By collecting power-ups, you can unlock various rewards. There are 4 difficulty levels to choose from. Almost all weapons have several firing modes. Equipment includes 2 types of grenades, a flamethrower and a chainsaw, which allows you to one-shot any monsters.

Multiplayer DOOM Eternal gives you the opportunity to take control of a marine or powerful demons such as Marauder, Mancubus, Pain Elemental, etc. Each demon has 4 unique abilities. Monsters can resurrect partners. The goal of both sides is to destroy the opposing team. The match lasts until three rounds are won by one of the factions.

The hero is represented by health and armor parameters. Replenishment must be found at locations. You can find 9 runes that enhance various passive skills. Allows you to use the powerful finishing move Bloody Strike. There are three types in total, which you can switch between. Demons are able to jump, fly, have enormous strength and increased health.
