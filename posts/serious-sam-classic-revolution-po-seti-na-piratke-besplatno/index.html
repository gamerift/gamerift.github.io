Game Description#

Serious Sam Classic Revolution is a dynamic shooter designed to play online online, competing with different people around the world, or to fight on a pirate ship for free with a friend, participating in cooperative or competitive modes. The gameplay stands out for its total madness and brutality. Cyberpunk style intersects with black magic and a psi-setting. At the center of the story is a hero named Sam. The possibility of free online play is aimed at multiplayer confrontations between participants around the world.

The project is a collection of two classic parts of the series, created by fans of the series. Serious Sam Classic Revolution features a single player campaign, survival mode and multiplayer. New enemies, maps, bosses, and a lot of other content were added that have never been seen in the franchise. Serious Sam travels through Ancient Egypt, explores the temples of the Mayan civilization, the Persian Empire and medieval Europe. The main enemy is Mental. He is obsessed with the idea of ​​destroying the world of the living.

You will have to fight with a headless kamikaze, a Sirian werewolf, Clear’s skeleton and three powerful generals. Weapons include a double shotgun, a minigun, a sniper rifle, a plasma machine gun and a Warped minelayer. There are 100 secrets hidden around the world. An extensive editor is available as a bonus. Online play includes competitions such as Team Deathmatch, Capture-the-Flag, Control Zone and Deathmatch and Scorematch.
