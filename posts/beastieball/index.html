Game Description#

Beastieball unites two seemingly unrelated genre - taming monsters and sport - into a single holistic experience. In this game, users take on the role of a coach who is not like a coach or manager, directing the team of animals through various biomes in order to gain, train and ultimately take part in a step -by -step volleyball league. Paying special attention to strategies, research and teamwork, Advencura is a new look both at the monsters gathering genre and the world of sports.

It is not only about catching creatures or a set of glasses, but also about the formation of strong ties and understanding how to achieve the best results in your team. Exploring the open world, gamers are faced with various biomes, each of which offers its own set of challenges and capabilities. These biomas are teeming with animals - creatures with special abilities and characteristics, which are an integral part of the tactical volleyball gameplay of the game.

To recruit animals is only the first step, the real work begins when the time comes to form commands. Users can assemble a detachment up to five Beasties, of which two go on the field, and three are waiting in stock. The tactical nature of the game is manifested during matches where gamers can make up to three moves per move. These moves may include a pass, a change of position or the arrangement of animals for attack. The main difficulty is to choose the right moment to use each stroke, since time is crucial.
