Game Description#

The multi -user game Grey Hack makes it possible to feel like a professional hacker. The player is accessible to complete freedom of action as a procedurally generated world web network. At the beginning of the game, only a few programs are available, including File Explorer, Text Editor and Terminal. As a hacker skill grows, many new utilities will appear and a wide range of opportunities will open.

It is noteworthy that the Grey Hack terminal is based on the real UNIX teams and observes all fundamental values. This is the main tool for hacking and conducting hacker attacks. It is necessary to explore the network and create your own reputation as the most famous world hacker. At each session, the player will make changes to other people’s computers. Even if the user is not online, his PC is available for other hackers who may try to hack it.

Each perfect action affects not only a particular person, but also on the entire network. You can unite groups or act only in their interests. You can how to inflict damage, or try to do a beneficentest and help to catch criminals and other programmers. You can act in the shade all the time and create dirty tricks by the wrong hands. There is an opportunity to play offline mode in which all actions are preserved and do not change in any way if you are outside the network. All background functions are preserved only on the hard drive of the computer.
