Game Description#

Towa and the Guardians of the Sacred Tree begins with how the priestess of the goods collects its associates to protect the village of Shinju from the advancing evil. She has to lead the guards against the forces of the Magatz - the essence that threatens the distant mystical world. The main task is to restore peace, exploring dangerous lands, whose splendor hides numerous threats. The adventure covers several temporary lines, during which the detachment is fighting for the future of his house. Each expedition weakens the influence of the Magatsu and allows you to learn more about the world around us.

The battles at Towa and the Guardians of the Sacred Tree are based on the interaction of two characters. The gamer directly controls the Tovoy, fighting Kagur’s staff, and chooses one of 8 guards to his partners. Each ally owns a special set of swords known as Tsurugi and has unique abilities. Such mechanics allows you to form different combat styles depending on the satellite chosen for assignment. To strip the dungeons and victory over the minions of the Magatz, it is necessary to combine their skills.

Between the sorties, the player returns to the village of Shinju, developing as the plot progresses. Here you can establish relations with residents, learning their customs and personal stories. The settlement serves as the center for the constant strengthening of the detachment. It is proposed to hone the skills in the Dodzo, in the forge - to create new swords, as well as call for powerful Grace to gain advantages in future campaigns. These classes closely associate the dynamic game process with the narrative and development of heroes.
