Game Description#

Hidden Cats in Istanbul offers the player detailed, unpainted scenes created based on the historical city. The central task is to search for cats hidden throughout the location. If each animal is detected by clicking, parts of the initially monochrome landscape are stained in different colors, gradually appearing a complete picture. This key mechanics combines the solution of puzzles with a gradual artistic manifestation of various city attractions and streets. The gameplay boils down to careful observation and research of places drawn.

The main level in Hidden Cats in Istanbul contains 200 cats, but more specific tasks are to be carried out to promote. Detection of 12 special pets opens access to bonus locations and gives useful tips for further searches. Gamers can also find 20 certain citizens providing radar signals to help in the search for the most secretive creatures. These additional stages differ in size and number of hidden kittens, offering unique tests outside the main map.

In addition to the main task, this game offers a system of collecting, tied to the search for rare creatures. The find of such unique animals is rewarded with collection cards adding another aspect to the research process. This approach motivates to carefully study each area for collecting a complete set and obtaining achievements. The entire gameplay is designed for one person and has simple management, facilitating the interaction with the environment. The atmosphere of the project contributes to a calm and concentrated search for objects.
