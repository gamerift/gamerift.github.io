Game Description#

For Evelyn 2 - Shards of Creation puts a player in the world of auras, a kingdom encountered with war and dark magic. Months after the events of the previous part, the order of observers is the last defense of the region. Being a new member of this order, your decisions directly affect the outcome of the ongoing conflict. Each action forms the future of the entire kingdom, determining whether bloodshed or darkness will absorb these lands. This game contains moral dilemmas and branched ways based on the choice made.

The path to For Evelyn 2 - Shards of Creation runs through tactical clashes and the study of locations. The gameplay proposes to fight opponents using a combination of close combat attacks, long -range weapons, magic and secretive maneuvers. Each enemy reacts with special behavior, demanding a unique approach to overcome it. In addition to fighting, the world contains confusing puzzles and secret routes for detection. Bosses with distinctive mechanics protect artifacts and abilities, challenging the preparation of a gamer and its ability to manage resources.

Interaction with the inhabitants of the world abandones new challenges. The trip includes meetings with characters with unique motives and secrets, as well as establishing ties and negotiations with fractions. Replicas in dialogs affect promotion on tasks, personal relationships and the ending of history. This project also allows you to collect materials from the environment to create equipment and strengthen magical abilities, adapting the equipment to new threats arising during the plot.
