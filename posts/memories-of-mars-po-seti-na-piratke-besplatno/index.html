Game Description#

Memories of Mars allows you to survive together with a friend on a pirate ship for free, exploring the surface of Mars over the Internet. You can play online or go through a solo adventure. The possibility of free online play is built on joint survival, cooperation or competition for valuable resources. In Memories of Mars you will need to create weapons and equipment using 3D printing. The collected resources allow you to build your own base. The online game takes you to the surface of the Red Planet, where you will need to explore abandoned mines and craters. The main goal is to leave this mysterious place. The storyline tells how the colonists left the surface of Mars for an unknown reason.

It is necessary to fight off enemies and overcome dangers. In addition to ordinary opponents, a battle with natural disasters awaits. More than 16 square kilometers of Mars are available for exploration. 120 objects are presented, including cities, mines, factories, etc. You can improve skills to become stronger. There are about one hundred different items available for collection. You will need to fight both with security bots, drones, and other players. You will also need to engage in battle with giant machines, robots and military vehicles. There are a variety of events for both single player and multiplayer, allowing all participants to gather in certain areas. You can complete unique tasks from a system called Emergency Mode.
