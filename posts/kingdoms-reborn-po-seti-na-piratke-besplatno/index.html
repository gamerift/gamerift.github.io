Game Description#

Kingdoms Reborn is a city-planning simulator that allows you to play pirates online with a friend, or connect online for free to compete with players around the world. Maps and events are randomly generated. All settlers are living units, minding their own business or performing specified functions. The online game offers to turn a small settlement into a whole prosperous kingdom. By teaming up with friends and using the opportunity of free online play, you can exchange resources, trade and even confront each other with the help of entire armies of warriors.

The gameplay of Kingdoms Reborn is presented in real time. The gameplay is based on the mechanics of Anno and Civilization. You need to collect resources, develop an extensive technology tree, improve your settlement, and much more. When managing people, it is necessary to give them shelter, food and assign them to different positions. Rest plays a special role, since fatigue directly affects the morale of the settlers. By collaborating with a neighboring city, you can trade, fight and steal.

An extensive randomly generated map is presented. In total, it can host 7 unique biomes, where you will need to collect individual types of resources. All areas are represented by a variety of raw materials, flora and fauna. One of the participants may completely control a particular type of resource, so here it is necessary to decide how best to approach the issue: through diplomacy or through the sword. Having collected a sufficient amount of materials and resources, you can increase the era, moving to a more technological period. There are many tasks, rules, interaction quests and much more available.
