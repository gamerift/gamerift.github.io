Game Description#

Children of Morta is a role-playing game that gives you the opportunity to prove yourself as a hero by teaming up with a friend on a pirate to play online both in challenge mode and through the story campaign. The game is available for free online and via local connection. Multiplayer allows you to choose one of 7 unique heroes from the Bergson family to guide them through a dark fantasy world full of pain and suffering. They will stop at nothing to save those they love dearly.

The protagonists fight against the evil itself, descending into procedurally generated caves and dungeons, as well as visiting dark forests and swamps. You will have to confront a horde of monsters and various undead. Hack-n-slash mechanics combined with a roguelike provide unlimited variations of situations, and the possibility of free online play increases the replayability of the project, allowing participants to interact with each other in every possible way, combining their abilities and capabilities. By developing skills and pumping up levels, you don’t have to be afraid of losing progress.

Death in Children of Morta is not permanent, so if one or another character dies, you need to start the journey from the camp, where you can buy new weapons, stock up on equipment, or change the hero altogether. The plot tells about love, hope, longing, doubt and loss. All human, ephemeral and philosophical topics are raised. You have to fight powerful bosses. By teaming up to 4 players, you can together confront the giants, looking for their weaknesses. Each family member is unique and fights using unique tactics:

Father John is a mighty warrior with a sword and shield; Linda is a well-aimed archer; Kevin is a lightning-fast fighter with daggers; Lucy is a fire magician; Mark is a martial arts master; Joey crushes enemies with a hammer; Apan is a connoisseur and healer.
