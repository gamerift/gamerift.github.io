Game Description#

The events of Bridge Constructor Medieval take players to the distant past, when knights and castles existed in the world. The gameplay is based on physics, where you will need to build bridges to supply your lands with food and goods. Build structures that can withstand enormous weight, or, on the contrary, will not allow the enemy army to pass through, eager to capture your fortress.

The campaign consists of 40 episodes. Your structures will destroy the enemy army. Opponents will be able to attack them with cannons and other weapons, trying to cut you off from receiving useful resources. To protect your army and carts with valuable cargo, strengthen bridges and provide them with a roof over their heads.

Roofs are an important and additional element of protection that can temporarily delay the enemy. The materials are wood, stones, ropes, etc., each of which has its own physical properties. Siege mode is a constant defense against the enemy horde. An additional mode allows you to build structures in such a way that the enemy army can fall into the abyss. The gameplay of Bridge Constructor Medieval allows you to use all your skills as a designer and engineer. Place clever traps to tell your enemies: you will not pass!
