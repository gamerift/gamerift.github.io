Game Description#

WRC 5 FIA World Rally Championship is a racing simulator designed to be played online with a friend, participating together in various competitions. On the pirate platform you can take part in world tournaments for free. On the Internet online, each player takes on a specific role: one acts as a driver, and the other becomes a navigator. The possibility of free online play allows you to interact with your partner in every possible way. Only teamwork will help you overcome all obstacles and be the first to reach the finish line!

Also, online play allows you to compete with friends for the title of the best rally driver. The gameplay focuses on realism. The race depends on many factors: surface, terrain, speed, weather, time of day, etc. There are 13 types of rallies available all over the planet. Before heading into Career mode, you can go through Rally School. Many gameplay aspects in WRC 5 FIA World Rally Championship have been improved compared to the previous installment. For example, during acceleration and braking, the center of mass shifts. The traction system, slippage and many other parameters have been updated.

A well-developed damage system is presented. Components and parts may fail. Car repairs must be done between races. A career is represented by an FIA license. All cars, teams, drivers and tracks of the tournament are available. Some championships may consist of 6 races in a row. The path of the best racer begins with the junior league, gradually moving to the very top. With the funds received you need to buy cars or upgrade your car.
