Game Description#

The first-person puzzle gamePortal 2 received an official announcement only in the spring of 2011, after several postponements. This project is a direct continuation of the original part, which received the highest reviews from critics and gamers around the world. The game was developed by the legendary company Valve, which needs no introduction.

The game’s plot continues the line begun in the first part of the “Portal” series. A girl named Chell defeated a powerful artificial intelligence that went out of control and destroyed the entire staff of the Aperture Science research complex. The girl managed to stop the raging cybernetic creature, but due to negligence, GLaDOS was restored by her. This time, she will again have to face the same trials that almost destroyed her the first time.

Movement around the scientific complex will be carried out using a special hand-held device that opens small portals - passages between space. Artificial intelligence will put a spoke in the wheels of the main character, in every possible way preventing her from achieving her goal - to stop GLaDOS forever. Whether she will succeed in her plans this time, or whether the AI ​​will now turn out to be triumphant, depends on the actions of the player himself.

Valve director Gabe Newell called Portal 2 the best game of the studio’s existence. Despite the fact that the graphics have not stepped far ahead in comparison with the original part, excellent physics does its job, presenting the player with an interactive semi-open world. According to the creators, the first part was just a test run, and the sequel is already a full-fledged game. Also, Portal 2 is a game for two with a built-in level and character editor. Forget about reality when playing as Chell.
