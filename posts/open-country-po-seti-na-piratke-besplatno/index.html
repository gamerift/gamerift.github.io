Game Description#

Open Country is an adventure simulator in the open world, which you can play online for free as a pirate with a friend or random players by connecting online via the Internet. Gameplay allows you to explore the wild or hone your hunter skills. It is necessary to use available means and numerous tools to achieve maximum harmony with nature. Online play allows participants to become real masters of the forest.

Leaving city life, you will need to go alone or with a group to conquer the wild nature. The possibility of free online play allows you to team up with a whole squad to explore the endless locations that Open Country offers to enjoy. Wolves, bears and other wild animals pose a particular danger. They must be avoided, as they are always eager to feast on defenseless tourists.

Playing as a ranger, you need to listen to the advice of the experienced forester Gary, who lives at the local Snowridge hotel. He will always help you on your travels, but in return he will demand fresh game. Going to explore the local forests, players will have to survive in harsh and wild conditions. While hunting and tracking game, you can cook meat in camp. To do this you will need to collect resources and build a shelter. All weapons and equipment in the game can be upgraded. There are many tasks available that are given by residents of the nearest village.
