Game Description#

In the epic gameStar Wars Jedi: Fallen Order you will find amazing adventures, new challenges, modern graphics and high-quality laser sword battles! Immerse yourself in this saga of the Jedi and intergalactic war, in which you will play one of the key roles. The story takes place after Episode III, 5 years after the ominous Order 66. You will take on the role of a young Jedi Padawan who barely managed to escape from a trap created by the Sith. Most of the masters and wielders of the Power of Light have been destroyed, and you need to fight a more powerful enemy.

Recover the pieces of the past to reassemble the Jedi Order, while increasing your lightsaber mastery and the power of your Force. Be one step ahead of the Empire and its cruel inquisitors. Improve your skills, fight enemies and watch cinematic battles, taking direct part in them. You will find familiar weapons, characters, locations and planets that were present in previous parts of video games and films. Players will encounter realistic battles and a well-thought-out combat system. Parry with swords, deflect enemy laser shots, use evasion and the Force to instantly hit your opponents. Master many new skills to become one of the most powerful Jedi.

This is a new story in which you will learn the art of the Jedi and try to assemble the Order. Travel with a droid, the restless pilot of the spaceship Stinging Mantis, and a former Jedi Knight. A unique adventure awaits you, where you decide where to go and what tasks to complete first. Don’t hesitate, because the Empire is always close to you.
