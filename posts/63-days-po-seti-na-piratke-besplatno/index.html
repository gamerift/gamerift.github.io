Game Description#

63 Days takes players into the heart of the Warsaw Uprising during World War II, where you can play online with a friend. You will take part in the events that unfold in a besieged city, and your every decision will influence the outcome. The ability to play pirate for free allows more players to experience dramatic moments together. The game supports network play over the Internet online, which makes it available for playing together with friends.

The odds are stacked against you, but that’s what makes victory so sweet. Together with a squad of resistance fighters, you will plan missions, gather information and carry out daring raids. Each character has unique skills - from marksmanship to the ability to handle explosives. It’s up to you to use these talents wisely and keep your team alive.

63 Days doesn’t deal with the harsh realities of war. You must make difficult moral choices and witness the devastating impact of conflict on civilians. But amidst the chaos, there are moments of hope and camaraderie. The bonds formed between resistance fighters become a lifeline in their darkest hours.

As you progress, you’ll unlock new weapons and tactics, giving you more options to complete missions in your own way. Will you go into battle with a weapon or will you keep a low profile? The choice is yours, but remember - one wrong step can spell disaster for the entire resistance. 63 Days is a thrilling blend of strategy and action that challenges players to rewrite history against impossible odds.
