Game Description#

Starlink: Battle for Atlas is an adventure game in which you become a space pilot to rid the Atlas system of the villains Grax and the Forgotten Legion. You will lead your Starlink squad and take a stand against despotism to save the entire galaxy from powerful invaders. There are four ships to choose from to participate in the competition: Zenith, Neptune, Pulse, Lance.

The game provides a choice of six pilots, including Mason, Judge, Chase, Hunter, Levi, Razor, who have their own skills and characteristics. Each shuttle has individual characteristics and, combined with the character’s skills, you can select your own tactics and way of playing.

There are 12 types of guns in total: Flamethrower, Ice Squall, Chopper, Levitator, Vulcan, Vacuum Cannon, Iron Fist, Frost Ray, Destroyer, Ultra Machine Gun, Shock Wave and Gauss Cannon. Install any weapon on your space car and fight your rivals. The world of Atlas is represented by various planets through which you will navigate. Each planet contains unique landscapes and biomes.

Your method of play will be unique, because by combining the skills of drivers, shuttles and weapons, you will create your own individual hero build. Ships in Starlink: Battle for Atlas can be modified as you progress, gaining experience points. Bonuses will allow you to unlock new abilities and skills.
