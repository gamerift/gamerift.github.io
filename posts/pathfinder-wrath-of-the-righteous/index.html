Game Description#

Pathfinder: Wrath of the Righteous - RPG about the fight against demons. Here the player will have to become a legendary warrior, and at the same time understand the true price of good and evil. The story will begin with a fault in the abyss. Crowds of terrible demons escaped from their imprisonment and now they want to enslave this world. The main character and his team of brave fighters with evil will be opposed to them. In the fight against wickedness, the hero can become anyone. A noble paladin or a cruel necromancer is to solve only a player.

In “Pathfinder: Wrath of the Righteous” the character will come out to create absolutely unique. 25 classes and 12 races give thousands of possible combinations of spells and other abilities. For destruction of evil spirits, 2 game modes are available. The first passes in real time, with the ability to put pauses, and the second - step by step. The user will either command his detachment from the outside, giving instructions and strategically planning everything. Either he will become a shoulder to the shoulder with his brothers, and from the first person it will turn demons into dust.

It will be difficult to fight with crowds of enemies himself, so up to 10 partners can come to the aid of the hero. True, first he will have to earn their friendship, fulfilling their instructions. Each decision made will condemn its consequences, which will ultimately lead to one of the guides of the hero. You can become a radiant angel and a powerful dragon or a deceptive tricster. The character will also affect the character’s strength. Having reasonably uniting their weapons with pumped abilities, it will be possible to create a deadly warrior according to the truth.
