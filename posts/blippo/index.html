Game Description#

Blippo+ is a non -cable television simulator with living actors. The main gameplay is to switch the channels for viewing transmissions broadcasting to the entire alien world. The broadcast is filled with soap operas, sitcoms, news releases, weather forecasts and all kinds of talk shows. Each broadcast creates a separate universe stylized for pirate TV and public stations of the 1980s, which is supplemented by a surreal supply with interference on the screen.

Blippo+:

Soap operas involving cloned actors. Sitcomas about the everyday life of aliens. News releases with intergalactic reports. Tok show with crazy scientists and leading Victorin. Weather forecasts for different parts of the planet.

The main plot unfolds between different shows, and the player collects it in parts. Viewing the gears opens access to data packets that update electronic television gig with new episodes and messages for history promotion. So the event of a planetary scale is gradually revealed, left behind the scenes. More information about local culture can be gleaned on Femtofax video text forums, while cloned actors, scientists and leading quiz appear in the show themselves.

The project creates a psychedelic and often comedic atmosphere thanks to the format with living actors and a kind of scenario. Content hides a philosophical subtext behind a layer of underground humor. The role of the player is to be an observer collecting a fragmented story from the consumed media content. Gamers also have access to a wide set of television settings for personalizing visual broadcasting parameters, which allows you to change the perception of the game.
