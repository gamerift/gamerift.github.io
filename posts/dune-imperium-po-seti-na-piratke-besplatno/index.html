Game Description#

Dune: Imperium offers free online play and allows online play. This award-winning strategy board game puts you in the thick of the action in the famous Dune universe. Explore the dangerous territories of Arrakis, where melange is mined, a valuable resource known throughout the galaxy.

The game provides the opportunity to fight for free with a friend or against artificial intelligence, as well as play as a pirate. You can challenge other players online or test your strategic abilities against AI. Achieve new achievements to show off your skills, or dive into challenging scenarios that require thoughtful thinking. For a variety of gameplay, take part in battles for tokens in Skirmish mode.

Will you dominate through military might or create political alliances? Will you focus on economic power or use subtle intrigue? Dune: Imperium offers a unique combination of deck building mechanics and worker placement. Each round presents you with new challenges and forces you to make difficult decisions. The fate of the Imperium will depend on your choice.

As the leader of one of the Great Houses, you must secure your place on Arrakis. Will you earn the Emperor’s favor or form an alliance with the secret Bene Gesserit? Perhaps influence the Dimensional Guild to gain economic advantage, or side with Fremen’s ferocious warriors.

Each round opens up new possibilities and forces you to adapt your strategy. Collect a powerful deck of cards, expand your influence and manage your resources carefully. The path to victory is not clear cut. Will you be able to surpass your rivals and take control of the Imperium? The choice is yours.
