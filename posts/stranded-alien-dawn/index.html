Game Description#

Stranded: Alien Dawn is an open world survival simulator. In this case, an alien. According to the plot, a group of people on a rescue shuttle is evacuated from a burning starship and landed on the first planet that fall. The player controls the team of surviving crew members of the space ship. He needs to take care of his wards, providing them with warmth, light, food, medicines, weapons, entertainment. The detachment must also be protected from diseases, monsters, extreme weather conditions and other dangerous factors. To get food, you can do agriculture or go hunting. In addition, it is necessary to monitor the psychological state of representatives of mankind and increase their level of happiness.

Each member of the detachment is a unique character with his personality, background, skills, positive and negative features. You can improve the ability of virtual men. SAD Game has high reigree. Each new passage differs from the previous one due to procedural generation. The plot events are created randomly. In addition to them, there are various challenging crises. In this video game, according to the tradition, resources can be obtained for the genre. Throwing trees and other familiar activities is not limited. Cosmic garbage periodically falls from heaven to Earth, which contains a lot of useful materials. There is an opportunity to build, decorate and improve the base. It can be equipped with different protection systems to repulse the local aggressive fauna. The user can also explore technology to expand their capabilities.
