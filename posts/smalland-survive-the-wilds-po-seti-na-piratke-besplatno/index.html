Game Description#

Smalland: Survive the Wilds is a survival game in which you, together with a friend or other survivors, will explore Smallworld - a world inhabited by grasshoppers, spiders, beetles and other insects. You can play online in co-op, or compete with each other for free on pirates. In a team online or alone, once in the Tiny World, you will need to look for useful resources to create weapons, equipment and resources to build a base. You can tame animals for protection or ride on horseback, covering long distances on the map. Multiplayer Smalland: Survive the Wilds allows you to share resources, build fortifications together, and much more.

Due to the tiny scale of the characters, puddles represent entire lakes, trees stretch into the sky, and road cracks are an analogue of canyons. The familiar world seems completely different if you look at it from a different perspective. The possibility of free online play allows you to join a team of up to 9 people. You can fight riding a grasshopper, dragonfly or spider. To build a shelter you will need leaves, branches, stones, pieces of iron and any resources that can be found throughout the map. Armor and weapons are created from the same materials. For example, feathers can be used to create wings that allow you to glide from trees and hills. You can craft a hook to quickly climb onto high platforms. There is a system of changing weather and seasons. The base is attacked by ants, wasps, beetles, cockroaches and other insects.
