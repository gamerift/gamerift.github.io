Game Description#

Ravenswatch is a hardcore adventure in which you will need to explore a world captured by a terrible Nightmare. The gameplay is an isometric action role-playing game with procedurally generated locations. Battles are distinguished by their increased complexity and take place in real time. The gameplay is aimed at the player’s skill and creating a unique character build. Depending on the choice of the protagonist, weapons, equipment and a set of auxiliary items, the strategy of fights completely changes.

The action takes place in a fictional world called Riveri. On his way, the hero encounters a huge number of ordinary monsters and strong bosses. All enemies, including regular mobs and leaders, are represented by individual attacks. Each enemy will need to look for unique tactics. Even an ordinary monster poses a threat. The Ravenswatch universe is inspired by Norse mythology, folklore tales such as the Tale of the Three Little Pigs, and Eastern classics such as the Arabian Nights.

At the beginning of the passage, you can choose one of several heroes. All protagonists have unique traits and fight using individual tactics, which radically changes the passage for one or another protagonist. Locations are generated every new launch of the game. The location of quest and auxiliary items, enemies, bosses, quests, random events and a lot of other content are changing.
