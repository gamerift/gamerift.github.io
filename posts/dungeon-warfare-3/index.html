Game Description#

Dungeon Warfare 3 offers the player to construct a deadly obstacle strip, laying out the landscape tiles and cutting out dangerous paths for the advancing adventurers. The main activity is the deployment of an expanded arsenal of traps and cunning mechanisms intended for inflicting injuries, wounds and disorientation of enemy detachments. You can directly form a battlefield, erecting walls or swarms and thereby directing opponents to strategically arranged protective structures.

Key elements of dominance in Dungeon Warfare 3:

Formation of the battlefield by erecting walls and the creation of abysses. Placement of deadly traps and ingenious mechanisms. The direction of flows in the most dangerous areas. Creating unique possessions configured for maximum damage.

Successful defense brings experience points used to constant to improve the torture tools and discover powerful abilities. Progress encourages experiments with combinations of various devices and manually created by a landscape. The advanced rune system allows you to configure the complexity and rewards for each fight. The game introduces the expanded composition of opponents pose unique threats and requiring dynamic strategic solutions.

This project in the Tower Defense genre offers a choice of scenarios created by hand and procedurally generated dungeons that ensure reiglable. The endless regime brings defensive configurations to the limit in battles with ruthless waves, allowing gamers to compete for high points. The central task requires cunning and creativity to create effective defense against a constantly growing threat.
