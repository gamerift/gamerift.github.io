Game Description#

On the piratedCall of Duty: Black Ops 3 for free, you can fight with players from all over the world or play online with a friend, participating in a variety of multiplayer modes. Over the Internet, online players can participate in ranked matches and fight in zombie mode. A jet booster is available, allowing you to run along walls or dash in the air. In addition to the Pick 10 system, various specialties are available in the form of a unique character with their own abilities. There are 9 of them in total, including variations of skills and equipment. Using the Gunsmith system, you can customize the hero, and Paintshop allows you to paint your equipment.

The possibility of free online play in Call of Duty: Black Ops 3 invites you to test your strength in zombie mode. a new system for gaining experience is introduced here, many unique items are available for unlocking, as well as gubblegams - perks in the form of candies that temporarily strengthen the hero. Weapon sets allow you to customize your character by choosing appearance and equipment. The story campaign supports co-op with up to 4 participants. Each player chooses their gender, appearance and equipment. A difficult mode called Realism is available. After completing the game, the additional Nightmares campaign opens, where the opponents are replaced with zombies.

There are a number of both conventional and unique weapons available to choose from, including the Thompson, PPSh-41, AK-74u, Nova-6, Locus, Ice Staff and many others. The action takes place in the future, namely in 2065. Humanity has achieved technological progress in the form of human cybernetization. Now the limbs are replaced with prosthetics that give enormous strength. A threat has overtaken the world in the form of a direct neural interface - a device with which the human mind can surpass the cybernetic one.
