Game Description#

The Mind Over Magic offers the player to become the master of the magic school located over a dangerous underground maze. You have to design, build and manage all aspects of your school, from classes to dormitories, from gardens to laboratories. You also have to train your students with various magical skills, such as lost lasso, production of potions, growing plants and necromancy.

But your school is not a safe refuge. The deadly fog slowly crawls out of the depths of the underground school, threatening to absorb everything in its path. You must prepare your students for a meeting with horrors hiding below, such as monstrous creatures and ancient traps. You will also have to deal with the consequences of death, since the spirits of the fallen haunt your school and offer their help in various ways.

Mind Over Magic will allow you to reveal your creative potential and imagination. You can configure every detail of your school and your students, choosing from many magical disciplines and styles. You can also create magic sticks and potions to improve the abilities of your students and call on stronger recruits. You can practice dark magic to grow servants that will help you in performing various tasks.
