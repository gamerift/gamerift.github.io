Game Description#

Keep Talking and Nobody Explodes is a cooperative sapper simulator. You are locked up with your friends. No one knows where the explosives are, but instructions are issued on how to disarm them. They must verbally explain what elements the bomb consists of and ensure that the player, acting as a sapper, can defuse it.

Each round will be quite intense. Everything depends only on the correct and competent relationship between the participants. Only through mutual understanding, including the ability to listen and speak, can a bomb be quickly dismantled. The game is ideal for small companies and parties. You can always take the place of both the sapper and the experts, periodically changing positions for more variety and fun. Choose who best identifies ports and knows how to count batteries. Features of Keep Talking and Nobody Explodes: Challenging puzzles that will test friendships;

Procedurally generated explosives provide new quests and challenges;

You only need one copy to play the game, because… The Sapper may invite you to become a Connoisseur;

Free mode is enabled, allowing you to create your own bomb;

support for virtual reality helmets.
