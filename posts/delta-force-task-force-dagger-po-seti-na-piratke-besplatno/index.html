Game Description#

InDelta Force: Task Force Dagger you can play online as a pirate, fighting for free in multiplayer modes with a friend or random participants. Over the Internet, online players take on the role of Delta Force fighters to go to Afghanistan to resolve the military conflict. The online game is aimed at interaction and cooperation between squad members.

Before starting the mission, you should listen to the briefing and select equipment. Participants are given various conditions that must be strictly followed, otherwise the mission will fail. The locations in Delta Force: Task Force Dagger are huge and open. Free online play and a wide selection of equipment give you unlimited tactical options, allowing your teammates to carefully plan their attack.

The goals in Delta Squad: Operation Dagger are set to be very different: destroy all enemies, undermine all enemy equipment, free hostages, capture the terrorist leader, engage in reconnaissance and much more. Each participant chooses a fighter from different units. These could be NATO or Commonwealth soldiers. Delta Force also includes members of the Green Berets, Navy SEALs, CIA Special Task Force, SAS, SASR, JTF-2, US Army Rangers, Marine Corps and Military Pararescue.

Single mode and version multiplayer are presented. A map editor is also available, allowing you to create your own levels and set various goals. The campaign consists of 25 unique missions, the action of which develops in the foothills of Kabul, Kandahar airport, Mazar-i-Sharif headquarters, etc. The arsenal consists of 30 different guns, ranging from machine guns to sniper rifles. There are 10 interactive maps available for battles.
