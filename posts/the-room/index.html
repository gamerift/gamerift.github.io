Game Description#

The Room is an indie project in the mystical puzzle genre, which spawned a series of 4 sequels. Look at where it all began and what has changed in the future.

The gameplay consists of exploring the secrets of a box of secrets. You need to show remarkable restraint and have a keen eye to find hidden objects on the box, for example, keys, cabinet handles, nails and other small parts, and small secret drawers to gradually unravel the puzzles found inside the compartments.

To move towards a complete solution and completion of the game, you need to correctly manipulate and move the pieces of an improvised puzzle, rotate the fully three-dimensional structure and select the correct digital combinations by finding not the most obvious clues. All this gives a pleasant feeling when unlocking a new episode, of which there will be 4 pieces.

A special feature will be a kind of magical eyepiece that gives superhuman capabilities to those who look at the world through it. Its owner is your protagonist. With the help of a magical artifact, you have the ability to look through objects and essence, to notice hidden facts that are inaccessible in our dimension with our human eyes. The plot will be revealed by finding notes left by a certain “A.S.”, the insane creator of this puzzle, claiming that mysticism is associated with the ethereal zero element. The musical accompaniment and dark design of The Room will build up the atmosphere to complicate the gamer’s task.
