Game Description#

The Drifter is a criminal n-clic-triller, telling about the resurrection of the hero Mick Carter a couple of moments to death. The story of the protagonist is very dramatic and difficult. He never managed to linger for a long time in one place and at one work. His life put him in a position when he had to return to his hometown.

Here he also failed to find peace, because he becomes a victim of a maniac. By some miracle, Mick returned to life. He was given a chance to find out who it was and why his goal was Mick. The visual style of the quest is inspired by the works of Stephen King, John Carpenter ribbons and thrillers of the 70-80s. The game has many scenes of violence and blood. History shows life in slums and landfills and raises the topics of drug abuse and alcohol.

The player along with the Carter needs to unravel the mysterious ball of events, full of intrigues and unexpected turns. Despite the fact that the game is made in a two -dimensional style, the main feature of The Drifter is the possibility of a viewing of 360 degrees. The gameplay is based on expeling, collecting and unraveling in many puzzles. The developers try not to overload the player with the complexity of the puzzles in order to maintain the dynamics and high pace of the narrative.
