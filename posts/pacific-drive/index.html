Game Description#

Pacific Drive is an atmospheric car survival adventure in the Olympic Exclusion Zone. Here you will not find the usual racing tracks, but there will be supernatural dangers waiting for you that will make you very tense. The only chance of salvation is your car, which will have to be strengthened and improved throughout the game. You will have to look for resources in abandoned laboratories, set up a base in the garage, research new spare parts and construct new stations. Collect valuable materials and explore an unpredictable and hostile world.

Be prepared to battle the unpredictable dangers that lurk in every foray in the Zone. Try not to slow down and keep tools at hand - because your car can break down at any moment. Storms, moving piles of scrap metal and mysterious figures in the dark - each new race is fraught with its own otherworldly dangers. You will have to collect resources, create equipment for the machine and change its design to suit your needs. And don’t forget about the appearance - your station wagon should look not only powerful, but also stylish. The task is not easy, but if you can uncover the secrets of the Olympic Exclusion Zone, then emerging victorious from this game will be incredibly exciting. Dive into the harsh world of survival on wheels and test your skills to the limit!
