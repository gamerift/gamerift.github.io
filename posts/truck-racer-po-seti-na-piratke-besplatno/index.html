Game Description#

Truck Racer not only gives you the opportunity to play online with a friend on a pirate, but also gives you a lot of emotions from what is happening on the screen. Here, finding yourself behind the wheel of a massive truck, the user is immersed in the atmosphere of dynamic races on tracks in a variety of settings. In total, there are 48 types of tracks on 10 different landscapes, which is why you have to constantly adapt to circumstances in order to beat your opponents. And given the presence of different modes, online competitions on the Internet can turn into a real fight.

Truck Racer features 6 categories of trucks, each of which offers different configurations to try out. Some stand out due to better maneuverability and small dimensions, while others, on the contrary, can push opponents out of the way, taking up a large space. As for the modes themselves, there is a Championship, Time Attack, Elimination and Quick Race. Each of them contains not only standard sessions, but also special events (13 in total). The online game creates a leaderboard, where each participant can improve their position by regularly participating in events. The possibility of free online play allows you not only to participate in one race, but also to do it with a split-screen function. So, everyone can feel the drive and atmosphere of what is happening to a greater extent than if it were in standard mode. As for realism and destruction effects, they are present here in large quantities. Any impact or collision with foreign objects may leave a dent in the case. However, this will not affect handling in any way, so even a completely wrecked truck can be accelerated to the desired performance.
