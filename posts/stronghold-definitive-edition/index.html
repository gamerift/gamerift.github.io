Game Description#

Stronghold: Definitive Edition brings medieval England to life in a strategic and addictive game. In this classic real-time strategy, gamers will have to restore a destroyed kingdom, unite disparate forces and fight the insidious villains - the Rat, the Pig, the Snake and the Wolf. The story takes place on a war-torn frontier where the king has been betrayed and the kingdom is on the brink of collapse. The user will have to go through a series of missions - from lifting sieges and capturing enemy castles to building grandiose fortresses and defending against continuous assaults. Your strategic skill will be tested to the limit as you manage resources, protect citizens, raise armies, and wage all-out war.

Stronghold: Definitive Edition doesn’t just return to the past, it improves on it. The developers have updated the visual effects, modernized the gameplay, implemented support for a multiplayer session for 8 participants, as well as a new campaign. With updated artwork and a remastered soundtrack, the game captures the essence of its 20-year legacy while introducing new content and challenges. A notable addition is a 14-mission campaign in which you must rescue Sir Longarm’s captured relatives. In addition, there is also a historical “Castle Trail” with 10 unique siege scenarios, allowing you to relive important moments in history. With Steam Workshop support, gamers can create, share and enjoy community content, further extending the life of classics. Whether you’re a series veteran or new to the world of medieval strategy games, Stronghold: Definitive Edition offers a fun and challenging experience that will keep you coming back for more. Your kingdom is waiting for you, sire!
