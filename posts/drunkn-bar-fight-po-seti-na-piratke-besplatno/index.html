Game Description#

InDrunkn Bar Fight you can play online with a friend, fighting for free on a pirate in various pubs and bars. On the Internet online you can gather a whole crowd of drunken men to clean up the faces of your opponents. The online game offers you to plunge into real madness and fun. The basis of a fighting game is humor, noise, total stupidity and incredible cruelty of people under strong drinks. The free online game Drunkn Bar Fight is available in a cooperative game for up to 4 people. There is only one limitation in fights: the imagination of the player himself. The gameplay exploits the typical stereotype of when one person hits another in a bar, thereby starting a mass fight, when everyone beats absolutely everyone they see in front of them. The whole point is in fights and destruction.

The environment stands out for its complete interactivity. You can grab a table, chair, bottle or glass to throw into the jug for your enemy. By grabbing your opponent by the scruff of the neck, you can slam his head on the table or throw him to the floor as hard as you can. You need to cause as much harm as possible to the drunken wrestler. Break a skull on a glass display case? Why not! No hospitals, emergency rooms, police or anything like that. Just total chaos, immorality and inhumanity. The gameplay allows you to let off steam and create real mayhem. Each character and surrounding object has its own physics, which creates a lot of hilarious and comical situations.
