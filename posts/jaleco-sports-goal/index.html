Game Description#

Jaleco Sports: Goal! It contains versions of a football tit for NES and SNES, representing a collection of historical console projects. This simulator opens access to 24 international teams to participate in several disciplines, such as comradely meetings, cup tournaments and a penal series. The gamer has to resist the computer opponent in solitary mode, or a living opponent in the framework of a local multiplayer.

The system of monthly tables of leaders to track achievements and comparing the results with other participants is also implemented. Before entering the field, the player determines the tactical scheme, choosing one of 4 accessible defensive formations and setting up the general style of command behavior. Gameplay Jaleco Sports: Goal! Allows you to personally manage the goalkeeper during the reflection of the blows, making decisions at responsible moments. The arsenal of the actions of the players includes passes, ball selection, lock, head blows and an acrobatic blow through yourself in a fall.

Some attacks send the ball along the curved trajectory, and the viewing radar helps to evaluate the location of athletes on the field to make strategic decisions. This game provides an opportunity to instantly switch between versions for NES and SNES, including all their regional publications. The functionality is supplemented by the mechanics of rewinding time, giving a chance to correct the error or to outplay the unsuccessful episode. To help in mastering the product itself, complete original instructions and saved images of boxes are integrated.
