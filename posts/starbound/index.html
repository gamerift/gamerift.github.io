Game Description#

Starbound is a 2D side-scrolling indie RPG adventure with graphics that reference the classic 8-bit titles of the last century. The project has become widespread thanks to the ability to play with a friend in co-op mode on different platforms, as well as a well-developed system of achievements and rewards that motivate you to improve your own skills and collect all kinds of items for crafting and collecting. The surrounding world is presented in the form of open territories, where there are no prohibitions on colonizing unknown planets or performing secondary tasks; you can even not touch the story campaign at all. 7 races live in the vastness of space: Apexes are creatures similar to monkeys. They have the best technology at their disposal. Avians are birds that were deprived of their wings many centuries ago. The weapon was inherited from an ancient powerful civilization. Floranes are plants and are not averse to eating their own brothers. They have mastered the technologies of all races through reverse engineering. Hylotls are fish with unique diplomatic abilities. People look no different from real ones. A single government has been established on Earth, which has eliminated the need for division into countries. Glitches are robots that have remained in the Middle Ages in their technological development. But they have a tremendous speed of construction. Novakids are children of solar energy, have a rather short memory and do not like to write down references to themselves, which is why there is very little information.

The story starts with the creation of a protagonist. You can create a representative of any race, there is the possibility of slight customization. Then a huge universe opens up before the player, full of colorful characters, quests, assignments and dungeons to clear and gain experience for leveling up. In dangerous areas you can stumble upon monsters suitable for taming. They will make excellent companions and can help in battle.

Thanks to a crafting system of more than 2,000 items, gamers are given the chance to equip many planets and become an intergalactic owner of lands. The possibilities for creating your own nook are truly enormous. You can build an inconspicuous treehouse, or you can build a giant Gothic castle, as well as many other options. And everything is limited only by the amount of resources that can be replenished by completing procedurally generated tasks.
