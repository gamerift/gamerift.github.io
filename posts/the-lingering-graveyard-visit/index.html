Game Description#

The Lingering Graveyard Visit introduces the class, an 11-year-old boy who accompanies his mother to the burial place of his late father. This game is part of an episodic series that adapts stories and legends from Indonesia. A visit to the graveyard acquires a different measurement after mentioning the sacrificial ritual to search for wealth. They say that the ceremony requires the offering of the soul of a small child.

The path of the crew begins as a regular visit, but it can end with its participation in this rite. During the events, The Lingering Graveyard Visit will meet a local guy named Tomi, who knows a lot about the village. Tomi speaks of rumors circulating around the rite to obtain treasures. Alarmed by this information, the crew begins to think that his presence in the cemetery is directly related to the ceremony.

He connects a potential ritual with his mother’s monetary problems after the death of his father, which makes him doubt her motives. The gameplay consists of a plot-oriented first-person study. As a walking simulator, the main interaction is to manage the movement of the EDU on the territory of the Indonesian necropolis. The plot moves as the player studies the environment and listens to conversations in order to understand the fullness of the situation. The main game task is to pass the linear story in order to reveal the truth buried in the past of this place.
