Game Description#

InBatman: Arkham Knight - a third-person action game, we eliminate this problem, and the Scarecrow himself too. After the events of the last part of Batman, Gotham City finally has a normal, friendly environment. Fights, robberies and murders continue, but if the protagonist does not hear about them, it means that everything is covered up by the police, or these are just rumors. Suddenly the Scarecrow appears and says that during the last incident he only used 100 grams of his drug.

According to the plot, we move around Gotham a lot at the very beginning, but the main events open up before us in Chinatown, where the scarecrow, as it turns out, has a small hideout where he makes his toxin. Having received the latest development from a military motorcade, we set off to study it in order to prevent the consequences of a new explosion. The developers left us key characters who influence the plot and complement it. James Gordon is a police commissioner who helps Batman. Catwoman, who at first cannot get along with the main character, and later becomes his lover. The antagonists were also left behind.

Joker. The king of the underworld, as always, the main villain. A scarecrow who controls people’s fears and is well versed in human psychology. Harley Quinn, Two-Face, Penguin and many others. The game is full of plot twists no worse than a movie. The developers have retained most of the privileges from the previous part. Surely they left us gadgets and other fancy things like a grappling hook, a zip line launcher and a batarang. Detective vision has undergone some changes: it works in a specific location, and is not applicable within the city if we go on a free trip. And the quests themselves sometimes force us to go into the open world of a beautiful city. The combat system has changed a lot in Batman: Arkham Knight. Now, in addition to counter-strikes, allies can unite in a “combat team” and perform super techniques before their eyes. New costumes, a controlled Batmobile in which you can perform stunts - all this remains in place.
