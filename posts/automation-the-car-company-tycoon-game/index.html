Game Description#

Automation - The Car Company Tycoon Game is the simulator of the owner of the car concerns. A good product if you like to disassemble and redo cars in your own way. To design a car starting from the engine and ending with the suspension, body, as well as other parts. In this game there is a company where we will be the head of the campaign for the production of machines and will be part of the market from the end of World War II and now on our days.

The game has a free mode, where you are given the design of spare parts and the cars themselves, and about thirty scenarios with different levels of complexity. In the first task, you are given an engine that is used on 95 octane fuel, you need to redesign it under 80 octan and minimize changes in the engine component. Then you have to encounter more hardcore plots in which you have to design the engine for a powerful tank at the request, or remake it under a civil mode of transport.

Automation drags on with his head if you start to understand everything, but they will help you understand the principles of mechanical engineering. Inside the game you are invited to go through a tutorial with a video and voice acting, for a better understanding, a mechanic of the game. Also in the game there is statistics after starting a new engine. This game is great for lovers of upgrade and Dawngrady cars, and it will not make you get bored by various ways of passing will make you think about how to do the best.
