Game Description#

The development of routes in Fly Corp allows you to feel the work of real airports located throughout the globe. Players get the opportunity to create transport routes in the airspace, setting up the uninterrupted transportation of passengers who want to fly from one city to another. The task of the user will be to establish the right nodes and the implementation of flights that can most profitably and effectively transport the inhabitants of the planet to the necessary destination.

Crisis in Fly Corp makes it sense to act meaningfully, thinking over it with transfers or refueling in advance so that the planes get on time and without problems to the landing stripes. The analysis and construction of strategic pathways in the project comes to the fore, immersing the players into a full -fledged simulation of the work of the dispatcher and the control lines. When calculating the distance, it is necessary to take into account the capacity on board the flying vessel, because different aircraft can gain a limited number of customers. By coordination in air spaces, gamers will have:

Test a variety of gameplay, depending on territorial work. In Europe, there will be many knots and it is easier to fly to small distances. Open the world map to take long distances that require careful preparation. To develop tactics and plan financial resources that quickly end. To participate in free modes or tests where resources are limited and when overloading the airport, the user suffers defeat.
