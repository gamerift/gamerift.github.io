Game Description#

HordeCore is a dynamic scrolling shooter that you can play online with a friend for free on a pirate. By joining a team online or locally over the Internet, you have to destroy hordes of bloodthirsty zombies. The game takes place in a post-apocalypse. Multiplayer and single player campaign available. One player becomes the leader of a group of survivors. It is necessary to explore locations for useful supplies.

Defeating hordes of monsters requires leveling up your characters. You can make various devices and weapons, as well as craft protective equipment. The possibility of free online play allows you to destroy, survive, explore and craft useful items together with your partners. The main emphasis of the gameplay is on interaction between survivors and strengthening your own base. The mechanics combine elements of action, RPG and CCG. Card elements are optional and serve to diversify the gameplay. Gameplay and content of HordeCore: single or cooperative survival in the post-apocalyptic world; a huge number of zombies and other types of monsters; mining rare materials and making powerful guns; assembling your own team of unique characters available for customization; building and protecting your own base by creating defensive tools; replenishing the team with survivors found in the vast expanses of scorched lands; over 60 collectible cards to collect; strengthening the deck by combining cards.
