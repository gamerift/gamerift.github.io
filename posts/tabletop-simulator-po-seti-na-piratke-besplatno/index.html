Game Description#

Tabletop Simulator is a board game simulator that allows you to play online for free with a friend to create your own unique wargame project in the form of chess or cards. Internet connection online or local play available. The mechanics are inspired by tabletop games such as Dungeons & Dragons, Warhammer and Magic: The Gathering. On a pirate, each player chooses the plot and concept for his adventure. The set of assets and presets consists of 18 games that allow you to create a project with the mechanics of go, chess, backgammon, solitaire, cards or dominoes. Multiplayer allows you to create tables, figures, dungeons, enemies and much more. Hinges, physics, and various gameplay elements, including rules and interaction mechanics, are also adjusted.

There are many tiles, environmental details, furniture, figurines, chests and monsters from which you can assemble a location at your discretion. Many functions are available for the Master leading the party. The free-to-play Tabletop Simulator features are truly endless, allowing you to import your own skins, textures, 3D models, etc. to greatly expand your existing library of assets. A multiplayer competition can accommodate up to 10 participants at a time. Gameplay features: creating a tabletop sandbox in any genre; multi-user connection for up to 10 people; writing your own script and rulebook; you can use the Lua programming language; gameplay that completely imitates real actions: shaking dice, rotating the table, voice chat for discussion, etc.; tournament tables and recording of personal achievements; a board for every taste: mahjong, pachisi, poker, Chinese checkers, etc.
