Game Description#

Nitroplus Blasterz Heroines Infinite Duel is an anime-style arcade fighting game that allows you to play online with a friend on a pirate for free, controlling unique fighters with various special abilities. Each heroine has her own set of moves and performs spectacular super strikes. The network game offers you to fight both online and locally. The project is a crossover of several well-known arcade titles popular in Japan.

A special feature of the gameplay is that the main character is first selected, and then two auxiliary heroes are assigned who should help the players during the fight. The battles in Nitroplus Blasterz Heroines Infinite Duel are distinguished by their speed and dynamics. Even a beginner in the genre can master combinations, but in order to master the abilities perfectly, you will need to observe timings and thoroughly study your opponent’s patterns.

The heroines are characters from such universes as SUPER SONICO, SENRAN KAGUR, Arcana Heart, etc. We are talking about such charismatic protagonists as Saber, Saya, Sonico, Ruyli, Homura and others. All girls are proficient in several types of strikes, know how to jump and block. Free online play includes leaderboards that allow you to track your personal achievements as well as those of other players around the world.
