Game Description#

The Backrooms: Survival is a horror game with the ability to play online for free as a pirate with a friend or random players, exploring randomly generated locations. On the Internet online you need to take part in the confrontation. Objects, surrounding objects and much more are also generated randomly. The levels also require you to fight with different entities, trying to maintain thirst, hunger and sanity. Online play is available for up to six people at the same time.

The gameplay is first-person horror with roguelike elements. The possibility of free online play in The Backrooms Survival allows you to divide into teams or participate in fights where everyone fights for themselves. Battles take place in gloomy utility rooms, where you need to find useful items and try to get to the exit. The gameplay contains survival elements. It is important to maintain sanity and vitality, so food and food are scattered throughout the levels.

Each level is represented by unique events, items, goals, etc. The layout of the rooms changes with each new launch. There is a mechanic of permanent death. Having died, the character is eliminated from the battle until the end of the match. At the beginning, you are offered to create your own character or choose an already created hero using random. However, after the death of a unit, you will need to create a new hero. The graphic design is inspired by the console adventures of the 1990s. There is a leaderboard and a unique obituary system that tracks failures and deaths. Here you can find the character’s name, see his age, profession and cause of death.
