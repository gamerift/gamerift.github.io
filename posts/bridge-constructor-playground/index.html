Game Description#

Bridge Constructor Playground is a bridge construction simulator that gives complete creative freedom. Build a wide variety of structures that connect the banks and allow cars to cover distances from point A to point B, significantly saving time. The Bridge Constructor Playground campaign consists of 32 varied levels in which you build complex structures through valleys, canals and rivers.

After you have built an object, it needs to pass a stress test. It lets you know whether your structure can withstand a huge number of cars and trucks. Fasteners are constructed using physical properties. The fastening material, angle, force, pressure, etc. are taken into account. The gameplay makes it possible to build bridges with an unlimited budget and a wide variety of materials. You are constrained only by your own imagination.

Your work is rated on a five-star scale. To receive the maximum score, you will need to meet a number of different conditions. In total, you can build 5 types of superstructures, including the city, canyon, beach, mountains and hills. An overview map is available, which makes it possible to unlock new levels and worlds. Contains a complete manual that explains the principle of the game as much as possible. Structures are constructed from four types of materials: wood, steel, steel cable and concrete piles. Each building element has its own percentage and color visualization of loads. Two types of transport cross the bridges: cars and trucks.
