Game Description#

World War Z VR is a single -user first -person shooter that places people in the world captured by the undead. It has to withstand the enormous clusters of zombies in cruel combat clashes. This action adapts the basis of its predecessor, a title with a base of players exceeding 25 million people. Survival depends on the movement along the territories infected by the dead and the use of firearms to eliminate the hordes of advancing opponents.

The main task is to withstand the ruthless onslaught of these masses, moving from one fight to another. Before the start of each mission, it is necessary to choose a specific operative and collect an individual set of weapons and skills. Such a system provides variability of approaches to tactical situations in processed city locations. The World War Z VR project offers 3 separate levels of complexity that change the intensity of the onslaught from the zombie Roos.

The combination of the chosen character, equipment and the level of complexity turns each passage into a unique tactical experience, and in this a pre -selected team of the Allies helps in battles in the middle of the collapsed civilization. The main gameplay is focused on survival in a swarm - the so -called crushing waves of zombies, flooding the city landscapes. The battles are constantly happening and include scenes with blood, where the player divides and eliminates the masses of infected. Promotion on hostile locations and the successful completion of tasks is rewarded with experience points.
