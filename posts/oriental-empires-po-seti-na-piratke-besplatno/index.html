Game Description#

Oriental Empires is a global turn-based strategy game in which you can compete with other players online. On the pirate site you can take part in all multiplayer modes aimed at competition or cooperation for free. You can play online with a friend or random players from all over the world. Oriental Empires takes place in ancient China. The task is to develop civilization and expand the borders of your possessions.

It is necessary to create a personal army and send it into battle, participating in full-scale battles on interactive maps. You will have to start your journey from a small settlement, gradually increasing to the state of a metropolis or expanding to an entire powerful state. A network game or a single-player campaign is built on the fact that it is necessary to develop technology, culture, religion and other sectors of the lives of citizens. The maps consist of mountains, plains, forests and deserts. The possibility of free online play allows you to both cooperate with other participants and enter into confrontation.

There are 24 factions to choose from. Each side of the conflict has its own unique and distinctive characteristics. An army consists of infantry, cavalry, chariot warriors, artillery, naval forces, etc. You can conduct trade and market relations with your neighbors, creating markets and ports. The skill tree allows you to develop in different areas, including culture, philosophy, religion, etc. There are many indicators that determine the development of the country: Leaders, Sieges, Riots, Uprisings, etc. Having taken the place of the emperor, it is necessary to sign laws and decrees that affect the further prosperity of the state.
