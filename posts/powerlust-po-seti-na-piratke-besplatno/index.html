Game Description#

Powerlust provides the opportunity to play online and with a friend, even pirated and for free. This network game is available to play online online, where you can create a unique character without being tied to classic archetypes. Do you want to become a magician who wields fire and sword, or an archer who controls the powers of the dead? Paverlust gives freedom of choice.

The game boasts a huge arsenal of spells, weapons, abilities and skills. Experiment and create unique characters, constantly improving your strategy to defeat your enemies. Don’t just defeat your enemies, but also deceive them. Mastery of evasion and targeted attacks are the key to survival.

Powerlust isn’t just about fighting. Explore a vast open world, travel between cities, find hidden treasures, and even help quirky NPCs (who knows, maybe you’ll find a few lost teeth along the way). This is a one man project, a labor of love over several years. There are no corporate overlords here, just the developer putting his heart into the game. Want to customize your experience even more? Powerlust offers multiple camera modes, from isometric to first-person, and lets you customize your visuals with fun filters.
