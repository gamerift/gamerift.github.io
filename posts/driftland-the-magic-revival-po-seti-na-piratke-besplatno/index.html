Game Description#

Driftland: The Magic Reviva provides online play and you can enjoy online play for free with a friend, even on a pirate. This game takes you back to an era when the world was shattered by a great magical disaster. A powerful spell holds the fragments of the planet together, maintaining a fragile balance. As old magic fades, new magicians strive to gain control of the resurgent forces. Will you be able to restore Driftland to its former glory or will you witness its final fall?

As the Mage Master, you’ll lead from your own castle, using unique abilities to explore a vast, ever-changing world. Weave floating lands together to create your kingdom in the sky. Forget about taking care of every soldier - set tasks and use your warriors, archers and magicians. Tame dragons and other flying creatures to create fearsome aerial fleets. Each battle takes place on the same map - scout, strategize and conquer.

Will you be able to unite a fractured world, or will Driftland succumb to the echoes of a magical cataclysm? Dive into the single-player campaign, form alliances in multiplayer, or test your mettle against other mages. Will you be a benevolent ruler, uniting the lands and promoting their development? Or a formidable conqueror, leaving behind smoking ruins? The choice is yours in Driftland: Magic Reborn.
