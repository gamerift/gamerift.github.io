Game Description#

Binky’s Trash Service lays a waste collection route that runs through a series of villainous shelters. The key task at each level is to find and clean all the garbage containers, and then get to the exit. Performing a mission requires verified movements surrounded by a complete platform obstacles. Success depends on the methodical stripping of locations and accurate control of the actions of the hero.

The gameplay in Binky’s Trash Service is built on three buttons responsible for a limited set of movements. This system allows you to make jerks, jump from walls and make a somersault back to move in hard -to -reach areas. Such abilities are necessary for the study of 15 unique maps, which are compact, saturated areas. Effective passage will require masterful ownership of techniques to lay the optimal path to each Baku.

Mechanics for high -speed cleaning of the villainous bases:

Acrobatic platforming with an emphasis on accuracy of movements. 15 unique areas with thematic obstacles. Complete leveling of the level from garbage as the main task. The system of high -speed testing for shakes of skills.

The locations are decorated in different styles, among them is a giant tweezers, a corporate office and peace in the spirit of the operating system of the 1990s. Each place is offered by unique interactive elements: conveyor tapes, portals and cursors that can influence the hero. The project contains frequent conservation points, a simplified mode, as well as 3 available and 5 characters opened. This game offers to hone the routes to perfection.
