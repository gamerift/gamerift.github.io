Game Description#

In Strange Antiquets, the gamer will become the keeper of the bench of occult objects. The establishment is located in the town of Andermir, located on the border between the worlds. The proximity to the unknown affects local residents looking for mysterious artifacts to solve their unusual problems. The central task is to help these clients, responding to their specific and sometimes alarming requests generated by life in such a strange place.

The gameplay Strange Antiquits is built around the identification of artifacts and inventory. It is necessary to study subjects using deduction and illustrated reference books for their proper cataloging. When interacting with powerful relics, decisions made directly affect the development of the plot. The choice can both help the visitor in his difficulty, and bring a curse on him, and the townspeople remember actions, changing their attitude and forming the ending of history.

What is to be done by the Guardian of Antiquities:

The study and cataloging of mysterious relics using ancient folios. Interaction with the inhabitants of Andermir, whose fate depends on decisions made. The study of the gloomy environs of the town and the disclosure of the secrets of the ancient building. Care for a cat Jupiter, creating comfort in the middle of occult puzzles.

In addition to working behind the counter, this game proposes to explore and investigate events in the vicinity. Periodically, you will have to leave the bench to check the rumors or search for relics, guided by the cards of Andermir’s cards drawn by hand. The old building itself stores its own secrets and puzzles associated with its long history. Casual concerns also include caring for Jupiter - a local cat, communication with which it brightens the mysterious atmosphere.
