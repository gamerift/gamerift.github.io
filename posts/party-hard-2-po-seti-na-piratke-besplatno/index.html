Game Description#

Party Hard 2 is a killer simulator that offers you to cooperate online with a friend over the Internet, or engage in a bloody confrontation. You can play online on a pirate for free, and a single-player mode is also available, represented by a set of various missions. The online game offers a more complex challenge with the participation of live players.

The sequel develops the ideas and mechanics of the original. There are many ways to kill partygoers. During the game, you can destroy a drug syndicate and save the planet from alien invaders. You need to act as inconspicuously as possible. The possibility of free online play allows you to enter into confrontation with each other, trying to kill as many nightclub visitors as possible.

Dozens of new traps and other types of weapons have been added. At the beginning of the passage, you can choose one of four characters. Some open as you progress. All heroes have unique skills and abilities. The mechanics of Party Hard 2 are represented by crafting. By collecting various items, you can craft bombs, traps, weapons, etc. A multi-kill is counted when several people are killed in a row.

The hero has a special ability called Party Vision. The skill allows you to see hidden objects and traps. You can kill in a variety of ways. You can bribe guards, get girls drunk, hire punks and much more. A wide range of tasks are available, completing which unlocks various unique items and bonuses. Local multiplayer available.
