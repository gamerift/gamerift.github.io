Game Description#

Honkai: Star Rail is a role -playing adventure in which you can play for one of the four characters. Heroes travel around the world with a team. Each member of the detachment is represented by a unique class and fights using individual abilities. An open world and various dungeons with strong bosses and valuable loot are available for research. The fighting is represented by a step -by -step system in the spirit of classic Japanese role -playing games. There are main and secondary quests, as well as many random events.

The plot starts from the spacecraft of star express. The characters March 7 and the tribute of Hen should deliver the ancient relics of Herthe, however, in their way there are smuggles from the legion of antimatter, who attack the shuttle. At this time, two hunters for stellarons arrive at the station - Kafka and a silver wolf. From this moment, the main actions of the adventure begins. The main characters of Honkai: Star Rail:

Khimko Murat - the head of the star express; Young Velt is a former leader of anti -anthropia; The tribute of Hen is a guard of shuttle and a mysterious guy with a mysterious past; March 7 - a girl who has long been in anabiosis; Cheetah Landau is the leader of the silver -haired guards in Belobog; Zel is one of the members of the wild fire brought up in the Belbog dungeon; Clara is an ordinary tramp that got into the detachment by accident; Asta is a girl from a very rich family working as a researcher; Locha is a local merchant; Kafka is a lover of stylish coats; Arlan is the head of the security department of Shattla Gerth.
