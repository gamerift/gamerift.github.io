Game Description#

Tiny Brains allows you to play online, teaming up to solve puzzles and interacting with a friend on the pirate board. Laboratory animals endowed with superpowers and intending to escape from the place in which they are being held come under control. The mad scientist is preparing to implement his plan, but the main characters do not want to be part of this action. Therefore, in order to pass all the prepared tests, they will have to work hard, and gamers, in turn, will have to coordinate their actions online.

Teamwork really matters in Tiny Brains. Due to simple controls, users quickly adapt and will be able to use the characters’ skills in accordance with current tasks. For example, a rabbit with a lack of attention may attract objects that are at different heights. In some locations, this skill will allow you to remove unnecessary objects from the path in order to move on. At the same time, the levels are built in such a way that they can be solved in several ways depending on the composition of the group. In this sense, online play creates variety, and you don’t have to worry that one or another segment will be identical to the previous one. Caves with traps, mechanized traps and other obstacles will show what challenges and difficult situations can be. The ability to play for free online allows casuals and hardcore fans to come together so that everyone can enjoy the process. In addition, the system keeps statistics, allowing you to track progress.
