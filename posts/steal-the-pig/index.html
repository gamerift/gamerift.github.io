Game Description#

Steal The Pig is a cooperative game in which 2 players take on the roles of thieves. The main task is to transfer the pig through a series of locations filled with obstacles. The animal can wake up and try to hide, demanding coordinated actions from the partners for further advancement. This project supports a single mode, the passage of together on the same device with a divided screen, as well as online lobby for joint sessions. Each format offers its own approach to the implementation of the central task.

In Steal The Pig, gamers will face a variety of traps and puzzles. These obstacles will require accurate actions and coordination between two abductors for successful overcoming. The game world operates on the basis of the physical system; Elements such as mass and inertia directly affect the interaction of objects and the movement of characters. Such a design develops the concept of cooperative platformers, while offering its own set of tests for thieves, solved together as they move through the environment.

In addition to the main goal, the Title map contains additional activity available for detection. The study of the environment can lead to the opening of various mini-games located in certain areas. In addition to these side activities, many cosmetic costumes are scattered around the world. You can collect these outfits for characters by searching for places away from the main route, which brings an additional measurement to the passage for zealous partners.
