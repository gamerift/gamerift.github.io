Game Description#

In this game, elves and other heroes gather in one flock. InDivinity: Original Sin 2 - Definitive Edition even Rivellon has undergone significant changes. The developers completely remade the entire game. No one knows where to start.

The graphics in the game became more like realistic ones for some other world. There is nothing superfluous on the screen, and procasts, magical effects and the death of other heroes look better. Items that enhance your character not only add stats or health, but also the greatness inherent in your character’s background.

By the way, at the very beginning of the game we learn that the Divine is already dead. To do this, we form a squad of 4 characters, among whom may be our friends, and go to the eternal PvP arena in the open world. The third-person role-playing Divinity with an amazing plot has several endings, but, as they say, getting to each one is almost impossible. Our main task is to become a god in this world. If the Divine owned the relics (and this is already a versatile plot), then we must take possession of the same objects. If he mastered spells, then we are obliged to do the same.

However, as we progress through Divinity: Original Sin 2 we will see new crafting, a new spell and combat system, so don’t think that the plot will come first. Since the game is multiplayer, PvP will become your favorite activity.

At the beginning of Divinity, we will be asked to create our own character without a special appearance editor. Human, dwarf, undead and 3 more classes. The choice will directly affect the passage of the entire plot. In addition, 12 schools of magical skill were added to the game, which means that more than 200 spells and combinations are available at any time in the game. Become a god in a turn-based third-person role-playing game where the key to victory is not skill, but intelligence and cunning!
