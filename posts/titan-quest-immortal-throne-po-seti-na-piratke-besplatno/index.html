Game Description#

Titan Quest Immortal Throne is an addon to the original adventure, introducing the School of Dreams, the fourth chapter, a storage chest, etc. into the game. You can also play pirated for free online with a friend, going through the story in co-op, or you can participate in online confrontations over the Internet. Network play is included, recipes, artifacts, a new hero, armor, weapons, characteristics for heroes and the ability to improve the protagonist to level 75 are added.

The plot begins with the murder of Typhon. The protagonist finds himself in Rhodes, where he will need to destroy Hades himself. To do this you have to go to the Ikian forest. Here Medea must help him. The possibility of free online play offers you to go through the scenario in the company of a partner. The role-playing system allows you to choose the gender of the hero, set the color of the tunic and come up with a name. By defeating enemies and completing quests, you can upgrade 5 parameters: life, energy, strength, intelligence and agility.

You can choose two masteries. The first opens already at the second level, and the second - from the eighth. Mastery greatly facilitates the passage. Titan Quest Immortal Throne introduces the 11th school - Dream Magic. There are 10 of them in the main game: Warfare, Defense Art, Shadow Craft, Earth Magic, etc. Schools determine the tactics and style of passing. The gameplay is represented by hack-n-slash mechanics.

You need to fight a huge variety of monsters. For killing enemies and completing tasks, experience points are awarded. The world consists of three large regions: Ancient Greece, Egypt and the East. The final location is Olympus. Enemies and the location of objects are generated randomly. The plot is based on the myths of the Greeks, Egyptians, Sumerians, Chinese, etc. The bosses on the way will be Nessus, Demon Bull and Scarab.
