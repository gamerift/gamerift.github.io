Game Description#

Seabed detective visual novel is revealed through the anime drawing and elements of Yuri, showing the relationship between two cute girls. Players will direct the development of the plot line with the adopted elections, observing the events. We’ll have to look at the world through the prism of the views of three characters that are faced with the problems of illusory dreams and the reality of adult life, which every mature person meets in one way or another.

The storyline in the game the sea bottom raises a variety of questions that relate to each hero of the story. One girl is immersed in reading, completely surrendering to the imaginary universe and escapism, which protects from reality problems, while the other suffers from amnesia and works on his own memory in order to restore lost fragments from a past life in pieces, collecting them like puzzles. The story is filled with romance and establishing relationships between girls, allowing you to shed light on the events in distant childhood, where everyone was friendly and carefree. To learn the universe of the game for the following protagonists:

Mizuno Satiko. A designer suffering from hallucinations about former love. Narasaki Hibika. A psychiatrist working on human memory and the possibilities of consciousness. Takako. A girl who has an amnesia, who has forgotten many fragments of her life.
