Game Description#

The driving simulator at breakneck speeds - Assetto Corsa Competizione - will show you the real competition of racers in the Blancpain GT Series championship. In the cars that are used in the championships, we will compete against professional racers who will be controlled by artificial intelligence, but at the same time their actions were recorded from the actual behavior of the car on the road when it is driven by a professional.

The developers warned players that the entire project is built on exceptional realism. The races, made in a variety of modes such as sprint, endurance, 24 hour races, are made exactly in accordance with what happens on the roads during the Blancpain GT Series. The studio also showed during the game’s release how much attention to detail was given. All cars are made with millimeter precision, steering wheels, fabrics, turn signals, headlights - everything looks exactly like in reality. The mechanics and physics of the game were worked out in the same way with the appearance.

Career mode takes us through the main events that took place at the championship. We’ll try ourselves in the role of a racer who starts the competition with the worst car, but gradually achieves improved skills, cash rewards and horsepower under the hood! In single player mode, you can play “career” and “championship” modes. A multi-level event upgrade system will allow you to quickly complete all championships, and benefit from your experience in multiplayer mode with real players.
