Game Description#

ShellShock Live - two-dimensional tank battles that you can play online with a friend, competing for free on pirates in different multiplayer modes. Confrontation or co-op is available both online online and on the general screen. You can create your own tank, choosing not only the color of the vehicle, but also equipping the combat vehicle with a unique type of weapon. Using the opportunity of free online play, you can unite in coalitions and compete against other teams, fighting for championship.

By winning competitions, experience points are awarded, which can be spent on increasing characteristics or unlocking new tanks, weapons, equipment, accessories, etc. The network game is designed for confrontation with up to 8 people, breaking into teams or participating in intense deathmatches, where everyone acts for himself. There are many interactive maps and weapon combinations that make each new game unique. Sometimes earthquakes and other natural disasters occur.

In total, more than 200 types of weapons are available, ranging from the Seagull to the Nuclear Bomb. ShellShock Live provides 4 competition modes: Deathmatch, Assassin, Points and Juggernaut. Allowed to work 100 ranks. Each one gives you a specific reward and opens up additional opportunities. By destroying enemies, you need to move up the tournament table, increasing your rating.
