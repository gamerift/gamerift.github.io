Game Description#

One Step From Eden gives you the opportunity to play online with a friend, free on a pirate, using all the available multiplayer functions. You can compete both online and locally. The gameplay combines elements of a card battler, a simplified roguelike, a visual novel and an action game. The network game is aimed at confrontation between two players, where each participant chooses his own path of hero development: goodness and mercy, or rage and destruction.

Each path provides its own unique bonuses and abilities, directly affecting the style of passage. Free online play also allows you to play the campaign in co-op. You can use various spells, fight enemies in hand-to-hand combat, attack with long-range weapons, etc. The enemies become stronger with each new stage. The locations contain powerful artifacts that enhance the abilities of the main characters. The plot revolves around a mysterious place called Eden, to which the game’s protagonists are heading. Mechanics and features of One Step From Eden: collecting a deck, where each card has its own characteristics; the use of strategic and tactical skills; more than 200 spells and 100 auxiliary items; 9 main adventure characters; procedurally generated worlds with several ways to pass; co-op adventure and full-fledged confrontation mode.
