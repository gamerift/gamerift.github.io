Game Description#

InShave and Stuff, gamers find themselves in a lively and practical simulator, where two roles come to the fore - a hairdresser and a tattoo artist. The title combines hairdressing and paint work, which promotes creativity and experimentation. Whether performing precision cutting tasks or plunging into the complex world of body art, users are immersed in a world of tools, techniques and client preferences. A constant flow of tasks and light humor provide the basis for an original approach to customization and self-expression.

One of the distinctive features of Shave and Stuff is the presence of several modes, each of which has its own flavor. In the hair salon, players will have to make the wishes of clients come true. Some people want a classic style, others are inclined towards more daring haircuts. In a tattoo parlor you can create complex designs. Gamers can stick to the client’s instructions or use their own instincts to experiment with different patterns and themes. For those who prefer free creativity, Creative mode allows you to explore without restrictions.

Here gamers can give free rein to their imagination, try out new techniques or hone their skills. Interaction plays an important role in shaping gameplay. The characters come to life with their varied demands, quirky views, and sometimes humorous reactions. Each client presents you with a new challenge, whether it’s a requirement for a haircut with a specific taper or inspiration for a detailed tattoo.
