Game Description#

In Bye Sweet Carole, Lana Benton begins to investigate the mysterious disappearance of her friend Carol Simmons. The tracks lead her to the gloomy Bunny Hall shelter, which holds many secrets. The action takes place in Britain at the beginning of the 20th century, during the birth of the women’s rights movement. The heroine will have to plunge into the abyss of the secrets of this institution in order to find answers and understand what fate befell Carol.

Lana’s search for the truth in Bye Sweet Carole opens the way to Corolla - a surreal parallel dimension. It is controlled by hostile creatures, including the owl of Decree and the formidable Mr. Keen. The gameplay requires the player to constantly move between realities to solve problems and overcome dangers. The main game mechanic is the heroine’s ability to take on the form of a rabbit, which opens up access to various abilities to bypass traps and save herself from tar monsters.

Four Pillars of a Mysterious Adventure:

Moving between the gloomy asylum and the kingdom of Corolla. Use the ability to transform into a rabbit to solve puzzles. Overcome danger through stealth and platforming. Interact with characters to reveal the secrets of the plot.

Progress through the plot is built on dialogues with colorful characters that reveal the connection between worlds. The entire title is an experiment, combining the aesthetics of a classic animated film with elements of intense survival horror. In this unique structure, platforming segments and puzzles are woven into a narrative of the search for truth.
