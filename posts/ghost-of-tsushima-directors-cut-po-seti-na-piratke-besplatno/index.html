Game Description#

Ghost of Tsushima DIRECTOR’S CUT for free will allow you to play online with a friend via the Internet online on a pirate. Explore new horizons in feudal Japan as you conquer the island of Tsushima with the relentless Mongol invasion, or become the legendary Ghost, a samurai warrior who defies tradition to save his home.

This open-world action-adventure game pits you against the odds. The Mongol Empire is rushing to the East, and the island of Tsushima is the last shield protecting mainland Japan. Jin Sakai, one of the last samurai, watches his world crumble under the onslaught of the Mongols. Faced with an overwhelming enemy, Jin must choose between his honor and the survival of his people.

DIRECTOR’S CUT is the full version of Ghost of Tsushima for PC. Unlocked frame rates and a wide range of graphics options will satisfy both hardcore PC gamers and casual gamers. Enjoy immersive battles on ultra-wide monitors, or go full throttle with triple-monitor support. Advanced technologies like DLSS 3 improve performance, and features like NVIDIA Reflex ensure smooth gameplay.

For a truly immersive experience, choose a DualSense controller and feel the tension with every katana swing thanks to haptic feedback. Prefer a more traditional setting? No problem. Fully customizable mouse and keyboard controls let you play the way you want. The choice is yours, samurai. Will you become a Tsushima legend?
