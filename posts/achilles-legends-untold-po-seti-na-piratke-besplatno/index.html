Game Description#

Achilles Legends Untold is an action-adventure game based on ancient Greek mythology, which you can play online with a friend for free on a pirate, fighting numerous enemies represented by various gods, be it Hades, Ares, Phobos, etc. . On the Internet online you can play as Achilles and one of his allies. Achilles Legends Untold’s free online play allows you to play through the story campaign with a partner.

The gameplay is inspired by old school hack and slash adventures and soul mechanics, so every enemy you encounter poses a serious danger. You need to look for an individual approach to ordinary monsters and bosses in battle, trying to find both their strengths and weaknesses. You will need to explore mysterious lands and descend into the dark dungeons of Hades. Online play makes the game a little easier. The gameplay is based on the players' skills. All skills can be upgraded by increasing the character’s characteristics.

While exploring the mystical lands, you need to collect various resources and equipment. From the collected items you can either craft new equipment or improve existing ones. Materials for forging must be brought to Hephaestus. The blacksmith is able to create armor that will help destroy even the most difficult and powerful enemies. Each enemy adapts to the player’s tactics, learning his fighting style. There are several types of attacks. You can dodge blows and carry out counterattacks.
