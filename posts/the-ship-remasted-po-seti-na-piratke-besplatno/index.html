Game Description#

The Ship: Remasted is a remake of the original 2006 game The Ship: Murder Party. On Pirate Ship: Remastered, you can play online with a friend, competing for free in a variety of modes online online. You can play alone with bots, in co-op, or fight with random players from all over the world. The action takes place on a cruise ship built in the 1920s. Participants receive an invitation to board from a certain Mr. X. The online game The Ship: Remasted is a bloody deathmatch where ship guests must survive a real massacre.

The characters are the powers that be, such as Thatcher, Castro, Blair, Bush, as well as two sworn enemies - Hillary Clinton and Donald Trump. Each character has its own unique features and characteristics. All heroes can use weapons and the environment to effectively kill their opponents. The possibility of a free online game invites you to show your imagination and creativity in killing political opponents.

The match starts with Mr. X nominating targets to kill. There is also a hunter on board. If you do not kill the victim within the allotted time, then the hunter player himself becomes the prey. You will need to track your target using a wide range of traps, weapons, etc. You need to use cunning and all your killer skills to satisfy the requests of the owner of the liner. Multiplayer allows up to 16 players to compete simultaneously. Local multiplayer allows up to 8 participants to compete on a dedicated server.
