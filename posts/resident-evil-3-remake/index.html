Game Description#

[s][/s]Resident Evil 3: Remake is positioned as a remake of the original Resident Evil 3: Nemesis, released back in 1999. The time gap between the reissue and the original is 20 years. During this time, Capcom managed to radically rethink the series and enrich it with various innovations both in plot and gameplay, since progress does not stand still.

If Resident Evil 2 in 2019 offered the player a slower and more intense gameplay, steeped in horror, then the new product in 2020 looks a little different. The game is focused more on action, shooting and battles with Nemesis, rather than on puzzles, thoughtful exploration of locations and horror. Horror in Resident Evil 3: Remake became not the basis of the game, but its secondary component. Plot-wise, the 2020 Resident Evil 3 demo remake is as close as possible to the original 30 years ago. The main role, as before, is played by the famous Jill Valentine, a former employee of the special forces S.T.A.R.S. The protagonist finds herself in Raccoon City during a pandemic of a virus that turns people into the walking living dead - into zombies, among which there are dangerous mutants, unlike anything else, and aggressive infected dogs.

However, all opponents are not as scary and dangerous as Nemesis (aka Nemesis) is a particularly tenacious mutant capable of using weapons and obeying commands. The order given to him was simple - to destroy everyone who managed to leave that damned mansion from the first part, in which it all began. In addition to Jill Valentine and Nemesis, in Resident Evil 3: Remake 2020 the player will meet other well-known characters: the seasoned warrior Mikhail, the cynical and self-centered Nikolai, as well as the kind-hearted and simply sincere guy Carlos. By the way, the player will control Carlos for a good third of the entire passage.
