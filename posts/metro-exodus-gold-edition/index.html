Game Description#

The events ofMetro Exodus - a first-person shooter with elements of stealth and horror - take place in the same areas as the previous parts. The Russian Federation and the Republic of Kazakhstan are still waging a fierce war for life inside, in underground territories, with representatives of hostile clans, factions and monsters who devour, rather than kill, their prey. Artyom leaves the Order after the battle for D6.

The life of people in the metro becomes more or less banal, the routine of murders and pressure turns into something simple and standard, so commonplace that the main character manages to marry Anna. At that time, Melnik did not deviate from his principles and prepared to restore the Order.

Artyom still has hope that someone outside of Moscow has also survived. The last time he climbed the Ostanski TV tower, he heard voices and negotiations of other clans. Precisely at that moment when he launched a rocket at the Black lair. No one from the Order, and in particular Melnikov and Artyom’s wife, approves of this idea, forcing him to forget and live his usual life, fighting off mutants and pumping up weapons.

The crafting system in Metro: Exodus is no better improved than the graphics, and the picture is made at the highest level of realism. Add a new sight to the weapon, change the receiver, clip, even the caliber can be changed. The types of bullets were also not limited to the family. New ones have been added. According to the plot, we continue to make forays into the tower in order to catch the same signal. Artyom does not lose hope, but at one moment he is attacked by a whole squad of Guardians. The hero is so seriously wounded that he is found by the Sparta squad.
