Game Description#

Combat Mission Cold War is a step -by -step strategy, the actions of which unfold during the Cold War of 1979-1982. Soviet troops and NATO allies went into the regime of a full -fledged armed conflict. The plot campaign covers 45 years of intense war. The script explores the topic “what if” and offers to become an eyewitness to the hypothetical greatest bloody war in history.

The game gives a look at the first days of the existence of the US National Training Center, when the States began to quickly move on to the concept of Airland Battle. The gameplay is a full -fledged sandbox, giving players the opportunity to experiment with tactics. The most different equipment is involved on the battlefield, including M-60A3, T-80, T-64, etc. tanks. In total, 3 unique campaigns are presented. 15 different scenarios are also available, offering to pass special tasks or set various challenges for players. Each faction has its own unique units and technology. All countries are fighting with the help of individual tactical features. On the battlefield, infantrymen, tanks and aircraft fight. You can request an air jacket or shelling at the positions of the enemy. Since the world enters the digital era, you can use many innovative equipment and equipment. 3 Combat Mission Cold War:

The NTC script - events are developing in the Mohava desert in 1982. Players need to lead the command of the company of American soldiers; The plot of the United States in West Germany - fighting occur in the Southern Strategic Corridor of Fulda, and the second division of the 28 infantry regiment is ahead of command; Soviet campaign - actions begin in the first 48 hours of a hypothetical war, and the main events take place on fictional battlefields such as Eterfield and Alspeld.
