Game Description#

Predator Hunting Grounds is a shooter that offers you to fight online online, playing in cooperative matches with a friend, or participating in confrontations with other players. You can play online using all the multiplayer modes available for free on the pirate. The gameplay is an asymmetrical action game, where one player controls the Predator, and the rest of the participants take on the role of ordinary special forces soldiers. There are only two options: be the hunter or become the prey.

People must act together. Together they stand against a monster with enormous power. The possibility of free online play offers the opportunity to show coordinated work in exploring the jungle. You must listen carefully to your surroundings, look between the trees and try not to separate from the group. The tactical network game fully reveals the strategic skills of your partners. Attentiveness and timely response to critical situations are the key to the teamâ€™s success. It is necessary to use powerful trunks and set traps in an attempt to catch the alien. You can switch from first to third person at any time.

Playing as the Predator, you can silently move around the location using its verticality. Allows you to climb trees, enter a state of invisibility, etc. The monster has various advanced technologies in its arsenal, including Plasma Caster and Combistick, which tear people to pieces. Predator Hunting Grounds allows you to join a group of up to 5 people. One player controls the monster, and the other four heroes try to get to the rescue helicopter, overcoming the wild jungle. The predator must prevent people from leaving his domain at any cost.
