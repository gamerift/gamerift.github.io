Game Description#

Ship Graveyard Simulator 2 is a fascinating and realistic first -person construction simulator. If you are ready to pick up a cutter and a hammer again, then it’s time to go to the new coast with the largest cemetery of ships in the world and start dismantling huge details of ships. In this continuation, you will find new, even larger vessels, new gaming mechanics, functions and improvements, as well as improved mechanics that you loved in the first part. But do not think that everything will be easy!

For more than ten kilometers on the coast, there are written off the vessels and thousands of tons of steel, and you have to collect them all. In the game Ship Graveyard Simulator 2, large fragments, modules and components of decommissioned ships for disassembling, including engines, pumps, control panels and much more will appear. You can also order new ships for disassembly and receive new tasks. But be careful - your work is one of the most dangerous in the world, and many dangers await you. Do not forget to put on a helmet and use available tools to disassemble fragments of ships to smaller parts and develop your business. Ship Graveyard Simulator 2 is a game for real professionals who are ready for challenges and dangers. But if you are ready for them, then the coast with the cemetery of ships is waiting for you!
