Game Description#

Nibiru can be played with two to four people. On the pirate site you can team up online online to participate in all multiplayer modes for free. Gameplay Nibiru is a first-person shooter in a futuristic setting. The heroes arrive on an unknown planet to undergo a series of difficult tests. A huge open world filled with dangers and obstacles is presented for exploration.

The continent contains many hostile creatures. Every enemy in Nibiru poses a serious threat. Using the opportunity of free online play, you need to join groups or participate in confrontation mode. Alone or together with partners, you need to collect resources, upgrade skills, fight enemies and much more. The online game is aimed at cooperative survival. The expedition is collecting data about unknown planets that humanity is trying to colonize.

The story begins at the moment when seven astronauts found themselves on the surface of an alien planet after a disaster. They need to find a way to leave the hostile continent. As it turns out, there were already colonists here and now we have to find out what caused their death. The map consists of 38 square kilometers covered with anomalous clouds, wild creatures and bloodthirsty monsters. You should be wary of the night as the creatures become more aggressive. There is a crafting system that allows you to create weapons and items. By destroying enemies, your skill increases and your weapon skills are upgraded. Each new biome contains a unique type of monster.
