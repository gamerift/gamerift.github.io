Game Description#

The Dead Await immerses users in gloomy adventures, where it is necessary to survive during the zombie apocalypse. The users will have to participate in cruel step-by-step battles with walking dead, gradually gathering their own deck with cards-abilities. It will be possible to configure and pump the characters in the detachment, engage in the study of the open world, to extract resources, trade with secondary NPS and manage its own caravan for survival.

Users in Death Drive will have to go to study the vast universe, traveling along the dying locations that are filled with opponents. We have to enjoy the role adventure, where you can form your own adventurers' detachment and choose the desired build for the passage of levels. During adventures, users need to perform different tasks and quests for other characters in order to subsequently acquire useful supplies.

For traveling around the world, the users will have to create their own hero, choosing the necessary parameters and skills (hacking locks, specialization in explosives, hand -to -hand combat master, etc.). It will also be possible to distribute orders for the team, buy or find new weapons or auxiliary equipment elements in order to increase the chances of successful return from the raid. The game has a craft of objects, which allows you to create bits with nails and other types of weapons.
