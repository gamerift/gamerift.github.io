Game Description#

The Midnight Walk invites on a shadow journey around the world where fire and darkness are found. Title is an adventure based on dark fantasy and manual work. In the role of Pogorelets, gamers unite with Potbo, a Fund-Fund with an inexhaustible flame in order to navigate in a world full of dangers. Along the way, users should outwit terrible monsters who seek to extinguish the light, adding a tense layer to an already terrible environment.

The game is built around five separate stories, each of which has its own set of characters, problems and changing tones. The gameplay is based on cooperation between the burner and the potter, where the strategic use of light becomes the main tool for the user for survival. The terrible monsters who roam the ground pose a constant threat, forcing gamers to be alert while they move through gloomy landscapes. The combination of surprise and fear here allows users to experience constant reverence and awe.

The world in The Midnight Walk is unique, each of its details is made of real clay and animated using the technique of stopmans. This approach gives the game organicism, as if it exists outside the digital world. The Stop-Motion style gives the environment almost tactile sensations, creating an atmosphere that seems simultaneously intimate and alarming. It is available both on the flat screen and in the VR version, which makes it affordable.
