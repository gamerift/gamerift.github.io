Game Description#

Delta Force 2 is a tactical first-person shooter that gives you the opportunity to play online for free on a pirate, going through a joint campaign with a friend locally or online online. The plot tells about the fighters of the special unit Delta Force. Operatives must identify the world’s most dangerous criminals and carry out top-secret operations. You can complete special single-player missions, which take place in different parts of the world.

A single player campaign and multiplayer game designed for co-op are available. Unlike the previous part, Delta Force 2 features two unique campaigns. The first is about biological threats. Alone or using the opportunity of free online play, you will need to save the world from disaster. The second chapter talks about nuclear terrorists. The heroes will have to find and neutralize all the warheads. The events of Delta Force 2 take place in Africa and Siberia.

Available to complete 20 unique missions. Players explore spacious locations. The levels are distinguished by interactivity, allowing you to interact with surrounding objects. Everyone can develop their own tactics, using individual weapons or landscape features. Before the start of the operation, you can customize the soldier, choosing weapons from a huge arsenal. Various auxiliary equipment is presented in the form of grenades, knives, etc. The weapons are authentic and fully correspond to their real prototypes.
