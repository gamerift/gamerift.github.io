Game Description#

Corporeal is a psychological thriller that immerses in the gloomy anti -utopian world in the role of Liam, a special operative known as a corporeal. This gift allows him to separate his mind from the physical body, turning into a ghostly essence to study the environment, collecting information and tactical planning.

The main plot rotates around the investigation of a series of mysterious events that force the hero to encounter not only external threats in the form of demonic creatures, but also with his own internal demons. The gameplay is built on constant switching between two states: a bodily shell for conducting tense fighting from the first person and the astral form for secretly penetration and solving puzzles.

Two facets of one reality in the gloomy future:

In physical uniform, Liam relies on firearms and reflexes in order to survive in cruel contractions with aggressive opponents who flooded the city ruins. In a spiritual state, the protagonist is able to pass through the walls, get into electronic devices and even master the bodies of enemies for a short time, using them for their own purposes. The investigation is a key element that requires a gamer of attentiveness to details and the use of astral ability to search for evidence in inaccessible places. The confrontation of demonic entities requires not only accuracy, but also an understanding of their nature, because many of them are the creatures of distorted human consciousness.

It is this interweaving that the mechanic makes Corporeal gameplay multifaceted, offering unique experience at the junction of the shooter, detective and horror. The narrative is revealed through cinematographic scenes, dialogs and a study of the environment, where each note or vision brings Liam closer to the unraveling of a large -scale conspiracy. The elaborate universe fraught with many secrets, and the atmosphere of oppressive and uncertainty makes you constantly be alert, because danger can be hidden not only in dark alleys, but also in the depths of its own mind.
