Game Description#

A terrible virus grows into a global problem inDying Light. Starting from the city of Harran in Latin America, the infection is decimating the entire world, turning those infected into the walking dead. The hero must survive in such circumstances and try to correct the situation. As an undercover operative, Kyle Crane receives his most important assignment. He needs to find a guy named Suleiman in order to take away from him an important file that he stole. This will help save the Earth from a terrible disaster. Setting out to carry out the task, Crane gets caught in a building by parachute, falling to Earth. Here a couple of bandits are waiting for him, from whom a pistol saves him. Suddenly zombies attack, and a guy and a girl help Kyle escape from them. The role-playing system is divided into levels with a separate development branch, this includes: Survival - you need to complete missions, helping other survivors to escape. Agility is climbing walls and performing tricks. Strength - exterminating enemies.

After a while, the protagonist comes to his senses and sees an unfamiliar, simple environment. Here he will master parkour skills and be able to craft items by finding a drawing and collecting the necessary elements. Having gone to the city, the hero will be able to climb vertical surfaces, remaining out of the reach of monsters. There are just whole crowds of them on the streets. To fight the enemy, the gamer will mainly use improvised means, for example, pipes, although there are also weapons here.

At dusk, the city becomes even more dangerous, because more terrible mutants come to Harran. Their location is marked on the mini-map. The experience gained for surviving during the day doubles at night, so you can try to bypass the enemy. In the town there are two opposing groups of people who are also waging war among themselves. Caution here will not be superfluous.
