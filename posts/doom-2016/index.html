Game Description#

DOOM 2016, unlike DOOM Ethernal, has less dynamics, but is more related to the horror genre. We can say that it began the evolution of the series towards speed and platforming, and it is considered closer in spirit to the series than its brother released in 2020. It was the first step towards a global rethinking of the franchise.

The game takes place inside the corridors of a giant research complex. During an unsuccessful experiment with a portal to Hell, the base of the United Aerospace Corporation found itself in the clutches of invaders - terrible creatures from the underworld. All employees of the complex were mercilessly killed or turned into lesser demons. The enemy’s goal is to reach the Earth and capture it, and the demons have all the means to accomplish the plan.

You will get into the role of a lone foot soldier known as the Doom Slayer or DoomGuy. He is humanity’s last hope for salvation. Will he be able to eradicate all the demonic infection? You will have to find the answer to this question yourself, and the protagonist’s incredible pocket arsenal, consisting of a dozen different guns, both futuristic and familiar from previous games in the franchise, will help you. Each weapon can be modified during the progression, and hand-to-hand combat techniques, thanks to the new mechanics, will allow you to replenish your health.
