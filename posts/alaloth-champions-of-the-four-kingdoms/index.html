Game Description#

Action RPGAlaloth: Champions of The Four Kingdoms takes the player into the magical Middle Ages. The story begins in the Valley of Storms - a place where four legendary peoples intersect: Humans, Elves, Dwarves and Orcs. It is here that the cruel god Alaloth is imprisoned. From the very kingdom of the dead, he poisons all kingdoms with his evil. Soon monsters, storms, diseases and mighty dragons will strike this world. Someone will have to stop the forces of evil and restore world peace. The main features of the world of Alaloth will be: A varied combat system that requires special skills and constant training; Several branches of character leveling, classes and possible abilities; A reputation that opens the way to new opportunities; 4 playable races with more than forty factions. Each with its own ideals and history; 12 available partners, ready to help the hero at any time;

The combat system in “Champions of The Four Kingdoms” is extremely hardcore, so sometimes it punishes even the slightest mistakes. Movements should be thought out in advance, taking into account the characteristics of opponents. To unlock new skills, the player must upgrade the hero, constantly improving his potential. And besides this, you can choose a special focus: weapons, faith or nature - each of them opens access to their own professions.

Graphically, the game is made in a classic style for the genre, with an amazing surrounding world. From the elven Forest of Goldwood to the heights of the Fangs of Kemot with the dwarves. From the deserted home of the orcs, the Wastes of God, to the prosperous capital of the people, Blessed Ederest. You are given complete freedom to explore various locations, and their secrets can drag on for many hours.
