Game Description#

Sworn immerses the players into a gloomy rethinking of the Arturian legend. This cooperative action in the Rogue-Like genre allows gamers to pass the test alone or with three friends. Once the noble Camelot was under the corrupt rule of the perverted Arthur and his knights. In the role of a knight with a hardened soul, you must explore the depths of the fallen kingdom and find a source of impending darkness.

Along the way, you will meet legendary figures from artouurian myths, both friends and enemies. Seek the advice to Merlin and Nimue, but be prepared to encounter distorted versions of the knights of the round table. To overcome these difficulties, players will have to conclude alliances with the lords of Fairy. These powerful creatures offer more than 200 unique blessings, allowing you to endow their characters with fire, wind or other mystical abilities. Sworn pays special attention to the variety of constructions.

Gamers can experiment with characters, weapons, abilities and blessings, creating thousands of unique approaches to battle. With each passage of the constantly changing Camelot’s dungeons, players will have the opportunity to hone their skills and improve builds. The path to the title of a worthy knight is long, but those who are decisively configured will have a final fight with a vicious arthur.
