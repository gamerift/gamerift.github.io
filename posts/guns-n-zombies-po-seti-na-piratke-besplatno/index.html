Game Description#

Guns N Zombies is an isometric zombie shooter. Fans of co-op can play online with a friend, and also on pirate. Co-op play is only available online. You can’t run together on the same computer here.

In Guns N Zombies you need to shoot crowds of enemies in limited space. You can also install defensive turrets. Network play also allows you to do this. Events take place in a world where an apocalypse has occurred. Humanity was practically destroyed. Many residents have turned into robotic zombies due to an artificially created nano-virus. The protagonists set off on a journey to find out the causes of the disaster. There are 2 heroes to choose from. The gameplay for each of them is different. The narrative is presented through pictures with text. Sometimes dialogues and monologues appear during gameplay.

Missions are divided mainly into 3 types. They ask you to protect someone, collect something, or simply deal with waves of opponents. There are optional side quests throughout the levels. For completing them, they give money as a reward. Currency is needed to upgrade a fighter and his equipment, including weapons. Here you can return to previously completed locations to earn more money. Thanks to a little randomness, visits to old zones and battles are slightly different from each other. There are 19 stages in total in the indie project. As you progress, new types of monsters appear. In addition to the story campaign, there is a survival mode. Both games have the option of free online play.
