Game Description#

Warhammer 40,000: Rogue Trader is an isometric role -playing game made based on the cult universe of a fighting hammer. In her setting, the Grimard is mixed with science fiction. This product offers to travel through space on a huge ship, visit different places, chat with colorful non -pains, pump, perform quests, participate in battles. All the battles here are step -by -step and tactical.

They are divided into 2 types. In the battles on starships, you can move your transport by squares, use the skills of crew members, give a volley from the left and right side, produce torpedoes, shoot a beam, activate tower guns. During fights on the planets, a detachment of 6 creatures must be controlled. They are able to move around the map, hide behind shelters, use the features of the landscape to their benefit, throw grenades, fight in close combat, use firearms, and use special abilities.

The game is also known under the slightly modified name Rogue Trader: Warhammer 40000. In it, the developers tried to adapt the rules from the desktop warhammer. The action of video game adaptation occurs in a dangerous and poorly exposed space area, which is called the kronus expanse. It is considered the outskirts of the Imperium and consists of many systems. The player is assigned the role of a free merchant. The plot here is non -linear. The user can remain faithful to the imperator God or join the enemies of mankind. He is even allowed to take Xenos to his team. You can also hire psikers and warriors. The decisions of the gamer affect not only the development of the common history and the fate of its wards, but also change life in the entire sector.
