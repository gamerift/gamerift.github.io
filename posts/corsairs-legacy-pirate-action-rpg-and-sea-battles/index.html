Game Description#

Corsaires Legacy - Pirate Action RPG and Sea Battles is a pirate adventure in the open world, which makes it possible to become a real corsar, exploring the seas and oceans, as well as fighting with numerous enemies both on land and at sea. Events develop on Barbados. Playing for an adventurer, it is required to become not only a thunderstorm of the seas, but also a whole tropical archipelago.

The events of Corsaires Legacy - Pirate Action RPG & Sea Batts are developing in 1689. The Odyssey of Captain Blad came to an end. The pirate became the governor of Jamaica. But history continues and the world awaits new heroes. This time, a black flag raises a certain Jack RECHEM. He never thought that he would become a pirate. But fate has developed in such a way that he has to become a smuggler. The hard is reduced with different people who draw a protagonist into a whirlpool of events.

A whole tropical archipelago consisting of cities and forts is available for research. You can explore the jungle, the places of sunken ships, various dungeons, etc. Other pirates, warriors of the Spanish delegation, etc. stand in the way of the hero. All pursue only one goal - to find hidden treasures. The game process Corsairs Legacy - Pirate Action RPG allows you to freely move between the islands, performing many quests and instructions. Proking abilities, you can develop a hero in several branches, creating a unique character of the character. The plot immerses in the mysterious world of pirates and refers to numerous classical works.
