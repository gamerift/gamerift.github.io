Game Description#

S.T.A.L.K.E.R.: Call of Prypiat - Enhanced Edition puts the player in the role of Major Degtyarev, an intelligence officer sent to the cover under the cover. Its task is to find out the causes of the failure of the military operation and the disappearance of the detachment. Events unfold in the Pripyat region, where everything is permeated with dangers: anomalies, radiation, hostile groups and an unstable situation.

In Stalker: Call of Prypiat - Enhanced Edition, each action has consequences. The gamer has to navigate not only in dialogs, but also in the behavior of others. Decisions directly affect the development of events. The shooter requires attentiveness, the ability to deal with disabilities and understanding how groups work inside the zone. When moving forward, the major encounters loners, mercenaries and military, during the conversations with which the true background of the incident gradually opens. This version received a series of technical improvements.

Interface elements are updated, bugs were eliminated, visual effects were processed. All this is done in order not to interfere with immersion in the atmosphere. The gameplay remained the same: the study is replaced by skirmishes, the allies can suddenly become enemies, the situation is constantly changing. It is easier to move in the afternoon, you have to be especially careful at night. The zone is unstable. Maps lose their relevance, familiar routes become fatal. Radiation fields move, stalkers disappear without a trace, access to supplies can disappear at any time. Moving deeper, the user faces fragments of the truth, behind which the main reason for the failure of the expedition is hidden - and it is deadly.
