Game Description#

Yomegami My Sweet Goddess is a story about the difficult fate of Hayato Narukawa, whose new life suddenly turns into a series of comical and awkward situations. A sudden kiss with Ichijiku Mikoto, the daughter of the head of a local crime syndicate, jeopardizes his deepest mission. Now the protagonist will have to find a common language not only with the mafia heiress, but also with the clumsy class leader, as well as with a naive girl with dog ears. Each of them brings even more confusion into his already complex everyday life, filled with supernatural events.

The reason for all the trials that befell the young man is his own past. Hayato is the reincarnation of a powerful demon, and to atone for past sins, he arrives in the city of Masayresin. This task was entrusted to him by a divine mother named Iris, who oversees his attempts to do good deeds. In the visual novel Yomegami: My Sweet Goddess, the plot development is directly dependent on the decisions made, directing the story along one of four unique routes, each of which focuses on one of the heroines and leads to its own ending.

Events take place in a peaceful human dimension, whose inhabitants are not even aware of the ongoing war in the divine kingdom. This hidden conflict becomes the backdrop for Hayato’s personal drama, as he is forced to balance redemption, romance, and maintaining the secret of his origins. The gameplay offers the ability to adjust the voice volume for each of the voiced heroines individually, which allows you to dive deeper into the atmosphere of this fantasy novel about love and divine intervention in earthly affairs.
