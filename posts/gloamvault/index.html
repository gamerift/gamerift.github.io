Game Description#

GloamVault combines a chaotic excitement of monsters and terrible depths of the dungeons. In the role of an resurrected evil wizard, you have to go through the procedurally generated dungeons, hunting more than 500 monsters. In this world, you are not just fighting - you fascinate creatures and replenish your growing army to go further into the fatal depths of the dungeons. Your party can have no more than five monsters, so you will have to choose wisely and use your dark forces to merge them, creating stronger and unpredictable minions.

GloamVault is based on Roguelike mechanics, offering a permadeite and step -by -step battle on a grid. The procedural generation guarantees that each launch will be new, which will allow you to be alert. Despite the fact that the game is not a traditional Roguelike, its randomization in combination with the lack of a conservation system gives it a familiar, heartbreaking sensation. The ability to charm any oncoming monster, provided that his health is not enough, adds an additional level of strategy. In addition, you will find upgrades and objects that will increase your chances of success or improve the stats of monsters.

This attractiveness lies in the system of unification of monsters. There is no limit to combinations that you can create, and the merger works best when monsters have similar abilities. With 25 different abilities with which you can play, the possibilities are unlimited. Each meeting is an opportunity to experiment, configure and see what powerful creations you can collect to confront them.
