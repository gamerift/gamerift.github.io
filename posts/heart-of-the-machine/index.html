Game Description#

Heart of the Machine outwardly resembles a town -planning Taikun. But in fact, the project is something like a god of God with elements of tactics and strategy in real time. Only here the Juzer is assigned the role of not a divine being, which owns magic, but artificial intelligence. This video game has a science fiction setting. Its action takes place in a distant and gloomy future in the territory of the metropolis-colonies. This huge settlement is under the control of autocracy. The user is the first reasonable and awakened AI who decided to act secretly. He appears not on an empty location, but on an already rebuilt area. The card is generated again with each new passage. It houses thousands of buildings and millions of virtual men.

Game of the heart of the car gives the gamer a certain freedom of action. He is free to decide how to do people, and whether to do anything with them at all. For example: a player can engage in a space program and just fly away from the planet, help the lower class to demolish the autocratic regime, overthrow the government and become a new good or bad leader, take control of the military control of the deadly arsenal and arrange a nuclear apocalypse. This title allows you to control the devices and equipment. This ability makes it possible to influence the world in every way. For example, you can blow up TVs, hack cars, overloading electric circuits, capturing furs and using these robots to create chaos. Fights can take place both indoors and on the streets. Buildings here are allowed to be destroyed.
