Game Description#

The Dungeon Of Naheulbeuk: The Amulet Of Chaos is a humorous adventure set in the Naheulbeuk Universe. John Lang wrote a comedy series based on role-playing game parodies and heroic adventures. The developers decided to create a game based on the author’s universe. In a dungeon set in a fantasy setting, clumsy characters try to get their hands on treasure.

The RPG has complex tactical elements, with a dose of humor, unexpected surprises and ridiculous encounters. The heroes, despite their different characters and multiple disagreements, will have to tolerate each other in order to complete the mission. A valuable treasure is hidden in the depths of the dungeon and the heroes go in search of it, going through a difficult path. The dungeon consists of different locations - from caves, huge quarters belonging to Lord Zangdar, a goblin park, and a tavern. In the process, you need to avoid traps and solve difficult puzzles.

The gamer has 7 characters to choose from, which complement the skills. Ranger, Dwarf, Elf, Mage, Thief and Ogre have their own skill tree, which improves their abilities and equipment. The player will be able to choose an additional companion from 3 possible ones to join the adventure. Battles take place turn-based, using mechanics between characters.

The game has a difficulty system with easy battles in story modes and difficult tactical decisions in nightmare modes. In the process you will have to fight with more than a hundred different enemies. When fighting epic boss battles, the user uses a strategic approach and objects located in the environment and amenable to destruction.
