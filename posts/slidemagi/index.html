Game Description#

Slidemagi is a duel between the two magicians on the 10x10 board, where units move by shifting the entire grid lines. In one move, the player performs one action: moves a row or applies a spell. This solution is central to the tactical pattern of battle. Following this, all controlled fighters on the field simultaneously attack in four main directions in the presence of goals.

This tactical game offers several modes for single and multiplayer competitions. In Slidemagi, gamers form a deck of 16 cards before the start of the battle. These cards open access to spells, calling for new creatures or creating defensive and attacking effects. 5 spells come from the mixed deck in the hand, which adds an element of randomness to tactics. The main task is to defeat the enemy magician in battle or exterminate all enemy forces in a separate mode with waves, allowing the use of various approaches.

The gameplay includes several tactical systems. By shifting the line, you can clamp the enemy’s detachments at the edge of the field and place a dangerous landscape under them. The dead creature leaves behind a grave that blocks its line in place, and the necromancer is able to use these points to resurrect the dead. Such mechanics allow you to launch chain reactions, for example, destroying the enemy and resurrecting the fallen ally in one move, which changes the situation on the board.
