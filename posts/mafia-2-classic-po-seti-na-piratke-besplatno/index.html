Game Description#

Mafia 2 (Classic) is a continuation of the cult action game in the open world, which players can play online online. You can play online for free with a friend on a pirate ship, exploring the city of Empire Bay together. The gameplay of Mafia 2 (Classic) is represented by the mechanics of a third-person shooter and a car simulator. You can freely move around the city and steal cars. The online game and single-player campaign tell the story of a hero named Vito Scaletto.

It is necessary to complete many different tasks, including participation in chases and shootouts, evading the police, communicating with other heroes and much more. The possibility of free online play in Mafia 2 allows you to complete tasks in co-op or participate in confrontations. There is a change of seasons. Each location is designed in two versions: winter and summer. All areas of the city are open to exploration from the very beginning of the adventure.

The metropolis is inspired by New York and other American cities. There are various landmarks that resemble real-life buildings or locations, such as the Empire State Building, City Hall, Central Park, etc. If you break the law, the cops will ask you to provide documents. They can issue fines, arrest and shoot to kill.

To escape persecution, you need to change clothes in stores, give bribes or kill police officers. Patrols are able to remember the car number. The weapons are various barrels, such as Thompson M1928, Colt 1911, MP40, M3 and many others. Vito uses master keys to pick locks. Characters such as Giuseppe or Harry can replenish their ammunition. Cannons also drop from killed enemies.
