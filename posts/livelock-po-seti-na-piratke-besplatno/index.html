Game Description#

Livelock is a top-down shooter aimed at allowing pirates to play online for free with a friend by connecting online via the Internet. Alone or using the free online play option, you will take part in a tense confrontation between machines and people. It is necessary to take control of one of the leading intellects in order to try to open the doors to Eden - the gate that allows humanity to be revived. The story centers on robots with human consciousness. The online game features three heroes: Hex, Vanguard and Catalyst.

You have to enter into a confrontation for survival. Humanity is under threat and only robotic organisms are able to fight for the continued existence of the human race. Livelock has a huge selection of weapons, knowledge, equipment, etc. that determine the style of play. You can play in a team of up to three people. Each hero is represented by an individual class, wields a unique weapon and has a personal character.

The action takes place in a post-apocalyptic world, where artificial intelligence has taken over all the resources on Earth. There are two game modes to choose from: campaign and survival. You can choose from several difficulty modes. The most hardcore is singularity. Enemies become much more aggressive, and the main character is less tenacious. Here, any mistake can be fatal and lead to disaster, including the death of not only the main character, but also all his partners. A real test for fans of the genre!
