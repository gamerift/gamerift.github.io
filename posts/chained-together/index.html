Game Description#

Chained Together throws you into the depths of hell so you can escape. But there is one peculiarity: you are chained to your comrades. That’s right, teamwork (or at least some serious coordination) is essential if you want to survive the scorching heat and treacherous platforms.

Play through the game alone or take up to three friends for a chaotic co-op adventure. Each world presents new challenges, testing your ability to navigate the chain. The chain itself is a physical object that can get caught on platforms for a reason, so be prepared for some hilarious (or frustrating) tangles.

The difficulty can be adjusted to your liking. Beginner mode offers a safety net, allowing you to teleport back after a nasty fall. The normal mode is a direct path to success: make a mistake and start over. Feeling like an adventurer? Lava mode increases the pressure, forcing you to quickly climb before the fiery depths consume you.

Think you’ve already mastered escaping from hell? Leaderboards will allow you to compare your best times with others, giving you bragging rights (assuming you can climb fast enough, of course). So, are you ready to test your teamwork (or lack thereof) in the fiery underworld?
