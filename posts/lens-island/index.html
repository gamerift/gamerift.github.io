Game Description#

Len’s Island is an open world adventure, which is the construction of a base with the possibility of a pharmacy, craft, research and battles. Each player is able to build his own house of dreams, care for crops and simply explore the secrets of the island. If you manage to find a sword, then you can go to more dangerous adventures, fighting with numerous enemies guarding the treasures.

Once in the vastness of the far continent, it is necessary to arrange a new life, having only a small backpack of tools. Visiting the dark caves, exploring the corners of the mystical island, you will need to reveal all the secrets of this place and meet face to face with dangers. The game process Len’s Island is divided into two components: peaceful construction with the development of agriculture and intense battles with the study of dungeons.

When engaged in craft and building a house, it is necessary to show all your creativity and completely surrender to creativity. A huge number of materials are available for a choice. When traveling around the world, it is required to open new types of resources, materials, tools, weapons, etc. Caring for the crop, you can grow exotic species of vegetables or other plants. By selling food to the villagers, gold is earned, which is spent on improvements and upgrades. There are many hiding places and hidden places in the world where only the most curious travelers can get. The plot rotates around an ancient sword. It is believed that he has long been lost, but the hero does not lose hope of finding this powerful artifact.
