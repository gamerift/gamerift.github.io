Game Description#

Age of Fear: Total is a comprehensive package that combines the titles in the series into a single whole. It brings together past releases, future updates and downloadable content - all designed to provide a rich experience. Whether you’re a longtime fan or a newcomer, the set offers a unique opportunity to delve into the series' expansive universe. Over 100 hours of gameplay promise a world filled with different characters, factions and challenges. In short, there is a lot to explore here.

One of the features of Age of Fear: Total is its movement system, which eschews the traditional grid format. Instead of limiting units to hexagonal or square tiles, this system allows for more fluid and flexible positioning. This makes battles dynamic and opens up new opportunities for thinking through tactics. Whether you’re commanding a giant squad or a small squad, positioning becomes more important as size and terrain play a more significant role.

The focus on movement and positioning allows users to approach each battle from a new perspective, making each encounter unique. The title also offers a wide selection of heroes and units, each of which has its own abilities and development potential. The choice of characters is huge: heroes from different factions and with different origins. The inclusion of minor factions such as Giants, Foot Soldiers and Elementals expands the variety of heroes.
