Game Description#

Bad Guys at School is a bully simulator that allows you to play online with a friend or other players, causing a commotion at school. By joining forces online, you can cooperate as schoolchildren or teachers. It is necessary to organize competitions, using for free to the maximum all the possibilities provided on the pirate site. Having rebelled against the school system, there are many different missions available to complete to destroy boring regulations. Taking on the role of a principal or teacher requires all means necessary to stop violence in the school.

You can play alone, but the online game Bad Guys at School allows you to fully unleash the gameplay potential of the project. Divided into two teams, you need to use the opportunity of a free online game to demonstrate your creativity in destroying an educational institution. At locations you can collect various items, earn money, buy tools or weapons. The maps are fully interactive. You can interact with almost any objects, including numerous vehicles.

Playing as teachers or students, you need to complete a number of assigned tasks. The team that more successfully copes with the goals, or effectively suppresses the potential of the opposing faction, not only wins, but also receives the highest score, which allows you to pump up and increase the level. By increasing your skills, new abilities and opportunities open up. With their help, playing becomes even more interesting and crazier. In what other game can you roll over a schoolchild, or launch a teacher into space, tied to a rocket?
