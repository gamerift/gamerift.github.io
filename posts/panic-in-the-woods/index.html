Game Description#

Panic In The Woods is a survival and loot horror game that immerses gamers in a chilling woodland environment. The game can be played solo or in co-op with up to four gamers, playing as boy scouts lost in a haunted forest. The goal is simple: survive the relentless pursuit of the monstrous creature. Communication and strategy are of utmost importance. Users use walkie-talkies to coordinate their movements and exchange important information. The forest is a treasure trove of items, including traps, sonar and flare guns, which can be used to evade or distract the monster.

Noise is the monster’s Achilles heel, so users must move carefully and use their resources wisely to avoid detection. Each playthrough of Panic In The Woods is a unique experience as key objects and tools are randomly scattered across the map. Thanks to this, no two sessions are the same, and the gameplay remains fresh and interesting. Gamers will have to explore the forest, find the necessary items and perform a demonic ritual to drive out the monster.

The cooperative nature promotes teamwork, as users must work together to rescue captured comrades and outwit the monster. This is an exciting and atmospheric survival horror adventure game that rewards teamwork, strategy and quick thinking. The relentless pursuit of the monster creates a tense and exciting experience, making every decision feel decisive.
