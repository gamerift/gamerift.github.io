Game Description#

On the pirateRe:Legend you can build a farm online for free or play online with a friend, going on adventures together to fight numerous enemies. The possibility of free online play allows you to build, craft, fish, care for pets and much more. Online play and single player mode are available.

Re:Legend takes place on a remote island. The main character Vokka was washed ashore in a shipwreck. He doesn’t remember his past. However, he has to start a new life on an unknown continent. When meeting with local residents, you need to carry out their various instructions and collect useful resources. You can use the collected items to build your own farm.

Players can engage in farming by growing vegetables and fruits. You have to take care of creatures called magnuses. All pets differ in both appearance and abilities. The entire continent of Ethiopia is available for exploration, consisting of plains, lakes, mountains, forests, etc. By expanding the boundaries of the village, you can improve buildings to protect yourself from attacks from wild animals and monsters.

Within the village, you can make friends with local residents by organizing festivals and various celebrations. Playing in multiplayer mode with friends, you can create your own factions and coalitions. The heroes' skills are upgraded the more often they are used. There is a huge variety of skills available for upgrading. The hero is not limited by classes and weapons.
