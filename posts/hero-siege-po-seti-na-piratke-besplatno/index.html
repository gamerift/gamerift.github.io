Game Description#

Hero Siege is a roguelike with hack-n-slash elements that can be played online on a pirate site for free. You will have to explore the generated locations alone or online. Monsters, loot, bosses, and many other elements are also randomized. The free online game allows you to team up with up to four people to descend into dark dungeons and fight powerful opponents.

The single player campaign and online play consist of 7 acts. There are 15 character classes to choose from. All heroes have a personal tree of abilities, skills, an individual voice and stand out with a bright design. The multiplayer mode is represented by a leaderboard that tracks the achievements of players from all over the world. The leaderboard consists of two options: regular and super levels.

As players progress, they will encounter 5 powerful bosses, which are also randomly generated. There are a total of 30 leaders available, but only five of them will be encountered during clearing the dungeons. The rarer the enemy is, the more valuable the loot drops after killing him. Items are divided into several types: ordinary, magical, rare, legendary, mythical or satanic. You can use runes, the strength of which is rated on a scale from 1 to 5. You can have up to 5 runes in your inventory at the same time. Hero Siege content includes: cooperative mode for up to 4 people; 170 unique relics; 80 types of enemies and 30 types of bosses; 100 types of hats and helmets; 7 dungeons for clearing; 15 classes with their own skills; many random events; 6 levels complexity.
