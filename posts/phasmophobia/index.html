Game Description#

Phasmophobia is a cooperative horror game designed for four players. The plot is built around paranormal phenomena, where a group of researchers is trying to prove with the help of special equipment that spirits and ghosts exist. You have to collect as much evidence as possible to find out what type of evil entity you are dealing with. Having studied the strengths and weaknesses of otherworldly forces, begin to expel demons. The ghost hunt has begun!

Equipment in Phasmophobia includes CCTV cameras, motion sensors, electromagnetic fields, thermometers and night vision devices, etc. Each individual player has their own special set of items: lanterns, EMF readings, ghost book, crucifix, stained sticks or sacred herbs. Playing together with friends allows you to help each other, coordinating actions and guiding your partners along the trail of the spirit. Phasmophobia is designed for co-op and in every possible way encourages interaction between participants. You can play both over a local network and over the Internet with players around the world.

Some types of paranormal activity are only called upon after the required actions have been completed. Some spirits can hear. You will need to use certain phrases to communicate with the ghost. Speak directly into the headset or use the in-game Ouija board. At the same time, be prepared that the terrible truth about what is happening within the walls of the building will appear before you.

After studying the mechanics of the Phasmophobia pirate, you can create a complete action guide. Before you start playing, distribute responsibilities among yourself and develop a strategy for catching ghosts. The passage can take a different amount of time, depending on the teamâ€™s teamwork and the correct order of actions.

The atmosphere of the game is inspired by classic films about paranormal activities and spirits living in various ancient buildings and estates. Throughout the game you will be haunted by a creepy and depressing atmosphere of suspense. During the campaign you will meet 10 different types of ghosts, each of which has its own characteristics. Each subsequent investigation will be different from the previous one and will require a new strategy and tactics from you.
