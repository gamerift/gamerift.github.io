Game Description#

Age of reforging: The Frelands is a cruel world of the Middle Ages in an alternative world where magic exists, and the gods are quite real and if they are enough to pray and make generous gifts, they can even bless the asking person. There reigns tyranny and lawlessness, and survival is being built in trade. The more successful the seller, the easier he lives, the rest was terribly unlucky.

Actions take place in Freland, this is a special region, which consists of several cities-states, one is dirty than the second. Slavery here is quite normal, therefore, a person is regarded as one of the types of goods. The main character is one of the gladiators. They are used in mortal battles simply to entertain the nobility and business in the form of bets. The stronger and more successful fighter, the higher its cost will be, but it matters only for the owner. Then the protagonist decides not to endure such humiliation and actively raises the uprising. He will have to go a difficult path from a beginner to a leader, blessed by the goddess of fate.

Age of Reforming: The Frelands is very variable. The user will have a choice of more than 600 types of weapons. There are both the most classic variants of the sword and arrows, as well as magic spells, staff and other combinations. The world around was completely discovered, it can be explored alone or the company of friends, each of which will have its own unique hero. In total, 20 areas with NPS are available and side and main quests.
