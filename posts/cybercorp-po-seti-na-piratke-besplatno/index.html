Game Description#

CyberCorp allows for online play, immersing players in a brutal, fast-paced isometric world where loot is the main goal. In this online cyberpunk game, players can join the ranks of fighters for free and fight with a friend or online online. Using the latest Synth, which is a remote-controlled combat projectile, you can enjoy the game even as a pirate. This unique top-down co-op shooter is a breath of fresh air for action game fans.

The heart of the game is the aggressive, fast-paced playstyle. It’s all about combining moves and creating awesome gear-based combos. Whether you’re a melee brawler, a skill-focused tactician, or a long-range sniper, CyberCorp has you covered. A flexible progression system allows players to customize their equipment with card-based weapon and armor upgrades. This is a real paradise for those who like to play around, allowing you to create kits as unique as the cyberpunk city itself.

Speaking of the city, it is a huge playground with slums, factories, laboratories and luxury luxury complexes. Players can dive into story missions alone or team up with friends for multiplayer mayhem. The game keeps you on your toes, offering a variety of tasks - from protecting objects and hacking security systems to capturing control points and discovering hidden goodies. And let’s not forget about the loot! CyberCorp is designed for everyone, offering difficulty levels that will satisfy both casual players and challengers.
