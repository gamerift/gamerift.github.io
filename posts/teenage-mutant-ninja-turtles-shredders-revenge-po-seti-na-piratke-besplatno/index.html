Game Description#

Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles - Shredder’s Revenge - beat-em-up with the ability to play online with a friend, fighting for free on a pirate with Shredder and mercenaries from the Foot clan. The gameplay is inspired by old-school Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles projects and is a dynamic side-scroller with platforming elements. Single mode and online play available. Partners can fight with opponents both online and through the campaign locally.

You can play as Leonardo, Donatello, Michelangelo, Raphael, April, Splinter and Casey O’Neil. Each character is represented by its own unique type of weapon and special attacks. The combat system consists of regular attacks and simple combinations of gifts. The possibility of free online play allows you to join a team of up to four people. Almost all levels in Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles - Shredder’s Revenge are locations from the original 1987 animated series.

The pixelated visual style references classic 90s beat-em-ups. The gameplay combines old-school side-scroller mechanics with modern co-op action elements. Together with the turtles you will need to go through 16 levels: the streets of Manhattan and Coney Island, visit skyscrapers, the depths of sewers, etc. There are secrets and collectibles to collect to earn bonus points. You must fight with Triceratons, ordinary mercenaries and stone soldiers from Dimension X. There is a huge amount of humor, many references to old-school projects and cartoons about the legendary turtles. Kawabanga!
