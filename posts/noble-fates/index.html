Game Description#

The fantasy world of Noble Fates immerses you in adventures taking place in a fairy-tale kingdom. A group of gods oversee the plane of human material existence while mortals vie for control over territories and new lands. The main character is Contra, who is a demigod and capable of influencing people and other inhabitants of the planet. It will be possible to manage entire colonies of settlers in order to achieve prosperity for the future great civilization.

The main task in Noble Fates is to maintain the well-being of one’s own faction by developing the future kingdom. The game focuses on life simulation, where you will have to observe the constructed strategy for the development of society and culture. It is necessary to erect the first structures and fortress walls in order to protect in the future from attacks by aggressive monsters or demons who want to seize power into their clutches.

Players will be engaged in the construction of farms, mines, barracks and other buildings aimed at supporting the lives of settlers and providing primary requirements. You have to get food by hunting or craft new tools in order to increase productivity in the extraction of ore or stone, which are necessary for the construction of new structures. We will have to distribute resources in different areas, maintaining balance and economic stability.

There is the possibility of trading and concluding agreements with traveling merchants or travelers in order to strengthen relations with neighboring states and increase reputation. You can purchase magical goods on the market that can increase the strength or endurance of your own people. It is possible to rule as a tyrant and fight with your neighbors, or behave as a diplomatic god who tries to achieve a peaceful resolution to conflicts with opponents.
