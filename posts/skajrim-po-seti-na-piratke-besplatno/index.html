Game Description#

In Skyrim you can play for free as a pirate online with a friend, traveling together through the northern lands via the Internet online. Free online play also includes versus. You can upgrade your character and attack other players, demonstrating your strength and dragon cry. The Elder Scrolls 5: Skyrim features an open world, a huge number of NPCs, dungeons, cities and a variety of different activities.

Each player can become anyone: a blacksmith, a craftsman, an alchemist, a thief, an assassin, etc. You will take on the role of the Dragonborn, who must stop the dragon named Alduin. The Elder Scrolls V: Skyrim Together online game allows you to join forces with fellow brave souls to prevent an impending disaster. The map consists of 9 major cities: Solitude, Riften, Morthal, Dawnstar, Windhelm, Winterhold, Whiterun, Falkreath and Markarth. Heroes can cook food and potions, enchant items, develop a farm, mine ore, or learn blacksmithing.

Skills grow as you use them. The role-playing component is represented by the absence of classes. You can develop all your talents at the same time. There are 280 of them in total. They are presented in the form of constellations, where players select the corresponding perks. As you level up, you can upgrade the hero’s three main parameters: health, strength, and magic. The hero’s inventory is not endless, so it is important to sell unnecessary items from merchants. During the passage, you can join one of the guilds: Companions, College of Winterhold, Thieves Guild, Dark Brotherhood, Imperial Legion, Stormcloaks, College of Bards, Blades, Greybeards, Dawnguard and Vampire Clan of Castle Volkihar.
