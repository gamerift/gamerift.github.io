Game Description#

Selfloss is an atmospheric adventure set in a fantasy setting. The plot centers on a lonely old man, and the game itself is inspired by ancient Russian and Icelandic legends. Travel through the isometric world using your magic staff. Fight against numerous mythical creatures from Slavic fairy tales and myths. Help everyone who needs it. Pass many tests to perform a secret self-floss ritual that can heal a wounded soul. The story of Selfloss starts from the moment when the hero hears a cry for help made by a wounded animal. Having gone to help the animal, a series of the most incredible events begins.

Almost all of the game’s mechanics were built around the concept of a magic staff. This ancient artifact helps to perform many different actions: fight, heal, etc. The history of Selfloss is also closely connected with this magical and mysterious object. When you control the hero, you also control the wand. An ancient artifact is always close to its owner. Controlling the old man, the staff always follows instructions and performs various actions. Generate a powerful light beam with it or use the artifact as a shield. Explore the world of Selfoss on foot, or take a boat to the neighboring islands. The game’s visual style is inspired by post-impressionism using simultaneous contrast and minimalist design.
