Game Description#

The events ofCompany of Heroes 2 unfold before us in snowy Norilsk, where one of the largest Stalinist Gulags is located. NKVD officer Churkin comes here, who understands that he will not survive the next Stalinist purge of officers. In the Gulag he finds his comrade from the front, who is serving his sentence and preparing to be shot. Isakovich is the name of the character, he wrote a tome with the real actions of the commanders of the Red Army, for which he was sentenced to death.

Churkin decides to find out the whole truth that happened in the war, and Isakovich begins to tell, explaining each event in a non-linear sequence one after another in the form of a battle on a strategic map. It shows the real actions of people during the Siege of Leningrad, the actions of Soviet commanders and officers, as well as the involvement of traitors in the first ruling hand of the country. Also in the game, the Battle of Berlin, the feat of the soldiers who captured the Tiger tank for study, and many other facts that are difficult to learn about in history books are revealed in full. On the strategic field, we will be able to hire more than 20 infantry units and 10 units of heavy equipment, about 10 guns and 20 elements of vehicles and transportation points. Our base is where we started the game. Entire squads of soldiers will be led by officers who report to you as long as the mission does not go beyond the story campaign. We will also be able to play for the leaders of the Verkhmat and feel what it was like for the Germans in that war. Company of Heroes 2 has a well-thought-out system of cold, snow and ice. Difficult, snow-covered roads will be difficult for the infantry, and in the severe frost in the open field our soldiers will begin to freeze.
