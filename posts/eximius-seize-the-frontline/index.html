Game Description#

EXIMIUS: Seize the Frontline is a first -person shooter with real -time strategy elements. The multiplayer game consists in battles of 5 by 5, where 4 people act as officers on the front line and one commander acting as a support and giving orders.

Cards for battles are represented by various cities in the world of post -apocalypse. When playing for the commander, you need to build a base, train your fighters and help allied organizations. When playing for the officer, you will need to use gross power, destroying the enemy with various types of firearms, collecting resources for the base and improving their abilities and equipment.

Each officer has his own skills and only competent tactical interaction will lead your team to victory. One class of fighters acts better in long distances and uses shelters, the other prefers a close confrontation and tries to “tank”. The main goal of Eximius: Seize the Frontline is to keep the control points. If possible, you can try to destroy the enemy base, which will lead to an instant victory.

Two warring organizations participate in the conflict: Axeron Corporation and Global Security Forces (GSF). The interface will allow you to instantly switch between RTS and FPS modes. The commander can appoint a technique for both AI and provide control of one of the officers. As there are more resources, you can buy new equipment, equipment for the army, study various technologies, etc. The top of the development is nanocostom. With its help, the characteristics increase several times and give fighters special abilities.
