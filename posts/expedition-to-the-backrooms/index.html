Game Description#

Expedition to the BackRooms puts the player in the place of the ASYNC research team member. This group is sent to study the place of the alleged anomaly, and the search is brought into a parallel measurement from the interconnected rooms and corridors. This world consists of the same type of buildings with a yellow tint, and the lighting is provided by the roar of ceiling fluorescent lamps. Gamers move through this labyrinth -like structure to detect artifacts and tips. These finds help to learn more about the past and the device of this measurement.

To advance in the Expedition to the Backrooms, it is necessary to interact with the entities that the corridors inhabit. Some of these creatures are passive, while others are hostile and will begin to be pursuit of detection. Studying the habits of each creature is a key mechanics for survival. The game includes a voice chat system, thanks to which the sound from a microphone can be heard by the nearest opponents. The study of locations can be carried out together with other players in network sessions, which makes cooperation an important element for navigation surrounded.

The gameplay is also focused on interacting with the environment and solving problems. It is possible to manipulate objects in the world to overcome obstacles and open access to new areas. Many locations contain puzzles, for the solution of which you need to find objects or perform actions in a certain sequence. The disclosure of secrets is associated with the performance of these tasks and the collection of information. As the research team moves in deep, the testing and layout of the environment change, requiring new approaches to passage.
