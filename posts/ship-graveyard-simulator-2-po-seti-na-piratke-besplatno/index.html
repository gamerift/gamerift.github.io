Game Description#

InShip Graveyard Simulator 2 you can play online for free with a friend on a pirate directly on the Internet online. Another round of recycling and rescue awaits you in this multiplayer game. Get ready to light your trusty torch and pick up your hammer—a new job awaits. This time you will go to the world’s largest ship graveyard - a vast coastline littered with the wrecks of countless ships. Hundreds of thousands of tons of steel are just waiting to be noticed, so roll up your sleeves and get to work.

Whether you’re a seasoned boat builder or a complete newbie, Ship Graveyard Simulator 2 will welcome you with open arms (and hopefully a sturdy pair of gloves). The main gameplay - dismantling ships - remains, but has been significantly updated. New colossal ships await you that surpass anything you have seen before. These behemoths will require a whole new approach - think giant cranes and massive containers to cope with the sheer scale of these rusty giants.

Speaking of giants, you won’t stop at recycling everything that washes up on shore. The game now has a ship ordering system, allowing you to choose your next target from the many colossal ships waiting in the wings. Each ship has its own unique set of materials to collect, from valuable steel to precious copper. Remember that every scrap has value, so don’t underestimate the little things.

But don’t let the promises of riches fool you - shipbreaking is a dangerous business. Gas leaks, dangerous electricity, toxic fumes are just some of the occupational hazards you will have to face. Don’t forget about the ever-present danger of falling pieces of the ship - a helmet is, of course, a must-have accessory. So, are you ready to brave the danger and win the big bucks? Ship Graveyard Simulator 2 game is waiting for you.
