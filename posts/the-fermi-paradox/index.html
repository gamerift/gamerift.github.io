Game Description#

The Fermi Paradox is a fantastic setting strategy. The gameplay is focused on choosing a plot, which will affect the player’s decisions, the place of travel and presented civilizations. It is necessary to conduct races and types of creatures through tens of centuries, helping them to study the expanses of the universe, establish contacts with other living organisms and create harmony.

Being the ruler of an alien civilization, you will need to direct them to prosperity, help survive, collect radio signals from long stars and make contact with new races. Gradually, there are more opportunities for traveling through the galaxy and creating advanced technologies. The Galactic GOD mode proposes to become a galactic gardener and manage 10 races simultaneously. The passage is based in the elections, since each solution changes the course of history. The consequences have been reflected for millennia. The main functions of The Fermi Paradox:

The ability to take control over unique alien races. It is necessary to manage comedle deep -sea creatures, elegant intelligent creatures, smart plants, hairless mammals and others. 400+ events in which civilization develops. The main actions promote history and offer new options for elections and further continuation. Original places where wards live. Many of them will be on unusual planets, inside starships, ships and other areas that create different difficulties.
