Game Description#

Liftoff: Micro Drones is a drone simulator. The game is dedicated to FPV thrones and FPV quadrocopters. This is the name of the model equipped with a camera, allowing their owners to observe the flight with first -person view. For those who prefer to watch actions from the outside, there is an opportunity to change the camera angle. This video game contains 6 really existing UAVs from well -known manufacturers, some of which are allowed to modify. So users who own drones in real life can use this program as a simulator. Gamers allow you to delve into the settings to bring the physics of the behavior of a particular apparatus and their sensations from control over the device to what they experienced in reality. The players' management scheme is allowed to change for themselves.

This project is part of a small series. In Liftoff: Micro Drones, the developers finalized the engine. The creators improved graphics and worked on optimization. This continuation also has less loading screens than predecessors. In addition, developers redrawn the interface. And this sequel differs from other similar games in that it is not focused on freestyle or races, but on aerial photography indoors. A choice of several cards, such as a living private house, warehouse and other places, is given. There are a large number of narrow passages through the locations, through which you can fly.
