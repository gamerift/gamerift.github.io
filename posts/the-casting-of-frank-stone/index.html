Game Description#

The Casting of Frank Stone is an interactive, dramatic horror video game that promises to delve into the dark and twisted depths of a small town haunted by its past. Developed by Supermassive Games and set within the Dead by Daylight universe, the adventure will put gamers in the shoes of a group of young friends and uncover the chilling truth about the infamous Frank Stone. A shadow hangs over Cedar Hills, a town forever scarred by the brutal deeds of Frank Stone.

The game’s description hints at the profound impact of Stone’s actions, suggesting that his legacy has carried through generations, leaving a lasting scar on society. By exploring the city’s hidden secrets, users will unravel a story that transcends time and reveals the chilling consequences of Stone’s terrible deeds. The interactive nature of The Casting of Frank Stone allows gamers to shape the narrative, with their choices determining the fate of the characters and the outcome of the story.

The project promises an exciting and entertaining experience filled with suspense, horror and unexpected twists. As users navigate the treacherous trails of the Cedar Hills, users will encounter a range of intriguing characters, each with their own unique motivations and secrets. The plot is sure to keep users on the edge of their seats as they try to piece together the dark mystery of Frank Stone’s past.
