Game Description#

DRIFT21 is a car simulator in which players can compete online online, tuning the car and assembling the car of their dreams. DRIFT21 allows you to play pirates for free online with a friend or enter into confrontations with other participants from all over the world. Everyone can change parts of their car, increasing its power and controllability. The competition takes place in Japan on the Ebisu circuit. The online game provides a choice of 20 licensed cars, including the Mazda MX5, Subaru BRZ, BMW E46 M3 and Nissan 350Z.

All cars are subject to complete customization, from appearance to technical part. The possibility of free online play includes customizing a car in your own garage using about 1,900 different parts. Once the car is assembled, you can go to competitions in 7 single-player modes or take part in multiplayer championships for up to 6 people. Races take place on tracks such as Driftland, Minami, Touge, Koszalin, Stadium and others. The project allows each participant to become a true drift legend.

The vehicle’s handling is affected by the engine, suspension, wheels, etc. The better you configure your car and the more competently connect the spare parts together, the better the car will perform on the road. Visual customization is based on changing the body, color, vinyls and many other parameters that create a unique appearance of the vehicle. Features 2 training tracks, single race, career mode, quick race, sandbox, free skating, time trial, sprint, circuit competition, tandems, garage customization, tests for mechanics and tuning. All modes include unique mechanics and present players with different goals.
