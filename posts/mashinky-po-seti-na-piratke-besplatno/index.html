Game Description#

On the pirateMashinky you can play online with a friend for free, building railway routes together. On the Internet online you can both compete and cooperate to build a railway industry. Gameplay Mashinky is a simulator with management elements. Single play, online play and classic isometric mode are available.

The gameplay is inspired by board games. A set of restrictions and rules applies. The ability to play freely online allows you to work together to build paths, deal with economic issues, buy new vehicles, plan routes, transport passengers and much more. By delivering goods to their destination, funds are credited. They must be spent on pumping, purchasing new equipment or improving existing ones. You will need to move around the territory using special cells. Logistics is a real puzzle in which you will need to correctly calculate routes and make sure that trains do not collide.

You can exit construction mode at any time to freely explore locations. You can board the train and travel the full route as a driver or passenger. 7 eras are presented. They offer unique types of vehicles and facilities. Depending on the era, the main resources are different materials: coal, firewood, electricity, etc. You will need to go all the way from a novice entrepreneur to the owner of an entire railway empire.
