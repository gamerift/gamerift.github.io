Game Description#

Monaco What’s Yours Is Mine is a cooperative heist game that allows you to compete online, play with bots or with a friend. You can gather your own team of thieves to play pirates online against a team of opponents. While one opposing party is trying to hack all the devices in the location in order to get treasures and loot, the second group is trying to track down the criminals. Events take place on a huge liner called Monaco. The network game is represented by 8 heroes: Locksmith - a blue collar and infiltration expert; Observer - a leader who hears everything around; Pickpocket - a thief with a monkey on his shoulder; Cleaner - a psychopath and the embodiment of Jack the Ripper; Mole - stupid but massive a Persian who can dig tunnels;Gentleman - disguises himself as others;Hacker - hacks doors and devices;Red - an insidious manipulator.

Using the free online game feature, you can team up with up to four players. Monaco What’s Yours Is Mine allows you to compete on a shared screen on one PC. Cross platform is also supported. The gameplay is focused on tactics. It is necessary to apply logic, think strategically and try in every possible way to deceive your opponent. The map has schematic symbols, as well as captions of the most important objects. The radius of visibility of characters is always shown. There are many modes presented, including co-op, vsus, challenge set, single missions and much more.
