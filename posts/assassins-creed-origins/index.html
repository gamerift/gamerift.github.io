Game Description#

The story of the third-person slasher is fully explored inAssassin’s Creed Origins. Only in this part we are told and described in detail how the brotherhood of Assassins was actually created, why the entire caste of killers hid for several hundred years, and how the principles of the brotherhood were formed.

The plot begins with the fact that we kill one of the pharaohs who own an entire pyramid, hundreds of subjects and countless hired killers. Kills in Assassin’s Creed Origins have been made more colorful since they added X-Ray. Now the hero does not have eagle vision, but has a full-fledged eagle, which will explore the area, mark opponents and interesting places for the hero. The time of day is also under our control. Not available only in difficulty mode - “nightmare”.

The first assassin was Bayek. Contract killings and assistance to Cleopatra, who was at war with her brother Ptolemy the Thirteenth, began with him. The game, as usual, takes place in the present and past. If in real time the Order is trying to restore the Forerunner and capture the entire planet. As usual, we are missing one piece of Eden. To get it, the assassins again plunge into the past in order to restore balance in the world and, accordingly, take possession of it.

The gameplay has undergone a small number of changes. In addition to the added X-Ray and the eagle flying at our command, the basis of the game still remained hidden assassinations and helping allied assassins in hostilities with other classes and factions. By the way, at the beginning of the brotherhood, our heroes were hunted because we represented the greatest danger on the planet. The game reveals the plot with dignity, shows new RPG content and clearly demonstrates the theory of success in creating something - they always try to break you if there is potential.
