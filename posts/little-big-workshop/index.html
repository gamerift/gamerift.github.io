Game Description#

Little Big Workshop is a meditation game with calm, measured gameplay. The passage begins very strangely, someone left a game with figures of little human workers on his desktop. And this someone begins to fantasize that these little men come to life, and work is in full swing in their toy workshop. It is the fantasies of the mysterious protagonist that gamers will have to watch.

The strategy will not give the player time to rest, because orders will immediately begin to arrive, and the virtual phone will be ringing off the hook with constant calls to it. First, you will need to purchase a workbench, distribute craftsmen across all compartments, and monitor the execution of the work. All orders should be completed efficiently and on time so as not to spoil the relationship with the employer. You also need to not focus on the same product, but monitor its demand in the market and the cost of materials. Competitors will also constantly interfere, luring away customers and taking advantageous offers.

The relaxation room in Little Big Workshop deserves special attention. It’s also worth pumping up to maintain the mood of your employees. However, it is important not to overdo it and keep an eye on all employees. Sometimes you will come across “personnel” who do not carry out orders, but sit in the break room all day long. Such underemployed workers will only bring losses.
