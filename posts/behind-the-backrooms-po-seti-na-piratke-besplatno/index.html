Game Description#

Behind the Backrooms immerses players in a world of eerie exploration and gut-wrenching horror, where you can play online with a friend as a pirate. In this online co-op game, teams of up to four battle in a labyrinth of utility rooms that defy logic and common sense. By joining forces, you and your friends will be knee-deep in free puzzles that will make your brain do somersaults. The game’s levels, available online online, are steeped in dungeon lore and are constantly expanding with regular content additions, so there’s always a new nightmare waiting around the corner.

But hold on to your hats, friends—it’s only going to get weirder. Hidden within these already confusing levels are entrances to sub-levels where things really go awry. And if you thought this was as weird as it gets, think again. Within these sublevels are portals to the negative levels, a concept that will have you spinning faster than a roller-skating dervish. It’s a Russian nesting doll of horror, each layer peeling away to reveal new secrets and fears.

The game is not just about running and screaming, although there is plenty of that here. This is a test of teamwork, ingenuity and nerves of steel. You’ll have to marshal all your strength to crack the codes, outsmart everyone lurking in the shadows, and find a way to get out before you lose your mind. Behind the Backrooms isn’t just a game, it’s a one-way ticket to the Twilight Zone, complete with multiplayer mayhem.
