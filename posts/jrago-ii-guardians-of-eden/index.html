Game Description#

Jrago 2 Guardians of Eden offers players to unite in a company up to 4 people for battle with a revived darkness. Heroes form a detachment of guards in order to exterminate demonic forces and return the light to their world. The gameplay combines two -dimensional platforming and elements of the role action on a spacious card. This project, which is a direct continuation of the Jrago The Demon Hunter, expands the basic Hack and Slash mechanics within the framework of a large metroid that focuses on joint actions.

The study in Jrago 2 Guardians of Eden is to move along a huge, interconnected world. Players find new skills and magical spells that open access to previously inaccessible areas. In this game, 9 unique heroes are available, offering special skills for various combat situations. During the adventure, it is necessary to collect a variety of weapons and armor, which gives tangible advantages for the development of characters in the fight against growing threats in complexity.

The cooperative gameplay allows you to create strategic unions against numerous opponents. Title setting draws inspiration from dark fantasy and medieval aesthetics, creating a gloomy Gothic atmosphere for the events. The main goal remains the destruction of many demons in direct, swift fights. The action itself combines arcade features with Hack and Slash clashes, and the magic systems complement physical attacks, offering the guards additional tactical opportunities.
