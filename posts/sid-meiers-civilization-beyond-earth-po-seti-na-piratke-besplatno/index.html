Game Description#

On the pirateSid Meier’s Civilization Beyond Earth you can play online for free with a friend or compete for world domination with other players online online. Sid Meier’s Civilization: Beyond Earth is a global 4X strategy represented by 8 factions: American Resettlement Corporation, Brazil, Franco-Iberia, Cavitan Protectorate, Panzian Cooperative, Polyastria, People’s African and Slavic Unions. Multiplayer allows you to take control of one of the parties to establish dominance in the galaxy.

There is a hexagonal map to explore. Players create cities and develop eras. It is necessary to appoint leaders, study technologies and make improvements. At the beginning of the match, you will have to make certain choices, for example, to fund one of the expeditions or choose colonists for research. The very first level is presented with the technologies of our time, but as the gameplay gradually develops it gains more futuristic features. The ability to play free online in Sid Meier’s Civilization Beyond Earth allows you to demonstrate your superiority in capturing outer space.

People are forced to leave their home planet as a result of a catastrophe called the Great Mistake. An important resource is the energy that fuels all buildings. Units need to stay healthy and fed, so it is important to build botanical gardens. The technology tree is represented by the Web system, which allows you to develop in any direction without any restrictions. There is a parameter of culture points required for the development of a colony. They are divided into 4 branches: Strength, Prosperity, Knowledge and Industry. There are 6 types of main resources: oil, geothermal source, titanium, xenomass, anti-gravel, firaxite.

Units are divided into three types: combat, non-combat and orbital. Each has its own unique abilities and characteristics. The latter include various satellites and drones. The Perfection system influences the progress of civilization, and Harmony is aimed at caring for the environment with the possibility of creating the Flower of the Mind. Righteousness honors the traditions of the Old Earth, and the philosophy of Transcendence completely rejects any connection with the human past. On different planets you can find a new alien life form. There are 5 ways to win: Transcendence, Promised Land, Liberation, Contact and Dominance.
