Game Description#

Tale of Immortal, an open-world sandbox deeply rooted in Chinese mythology and cultivation, invites players into an immersive experience where the path to immortality is paved through strategic choices and the conquest of mythical monsters from the Classic of Mountains and Seasons. Your journey as a novice Qi Refinement Stage cultivator begins in Yukong Village. A world filled with unpredictable monsters, mystical kingdoms and treasures waiting in the wings will open before you. The path of cultivation is not just a solitary journey; it requires a deep understanding of the craft and the ability to overcome danger and adversity.

The NPC world in Tale of Immortal is fully realized, with each character leading their own independent lives. However, your choices may intersect with theirs, offering a dynamic and unpredictable narrative. Whether you cooperate with others, take revenge alone, or become everyone’s enemy, the power of the game is in your hands. Mastering martial arts, teaching NPCs the Taoist mind, participating in auctions, and running a cult, Tale of Immortal offers a variety of activities. The possibilities are vast: from exciting adventures to fulfilling promises, inciting jealousy or discovering long-forgotten treasures.

As you progress in the game, sect management gameplay appears, allowing you to experience the full spectrum of sect life. As you rise from a humble disciple to a powerful sect master, you can return a fallen sect to the pinnacle of glory, or join a huge sect to rise through the ranks. To succeed in this world, you need to hone your skills and become a real warrior. Cultivate a powerful mindset, delve into martial and spiritual skills, and study unique guides to improve your abilities. The world is filled with treasures, rare monsters, and challenges like the Tournament of Immortals and the Master Ranking that will put your school to the ultimate test.
