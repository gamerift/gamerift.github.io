Game Description#

No Captain Allowed! is an action roguelike that allows you to fight both against artificial intelligence and to play a pirate game online for free with a friend. Allows up to 4 people to fight in interactive arenas using an online Internet connection or a local network. You will have to operate various special equipment while in a state of war. Every decision made has its own significance for the world. A huge tree of skills is available for leveling up, with the help of which an individual character build is built.

You need to fight robots, aliens, space slime, medieval soldiers, etc. The universe is under threat, so it is necessary to overcome each of them. Free online play allows participants to make their own decisions and face the consequences. In No Captain Allowed! There are many quests to complete, earning resources and money. It is necessary to strengthen the defense in order to fight enemies, gaining a tactical advantage. Features and mechanics: shooter with puzzle elements that allows you to act as a team; players vote and make decisions on where to move next and what actions to take; many random situations, enemies and quests; a branching skill tree that creates an individual hero build; a huge selection of weapons: lasers, missiles, flamethrowers, etc.; various factions: slime Suluz, robots Verikira, etc.; elements of a bagel, allowing you to generate a huge variety of situations and scenes.
