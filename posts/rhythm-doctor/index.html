Game Description#

Rhythm Doctor is an indie game in which you need to treat patients to the rhythm of music. The music in the game has healing properties. Each patient has unusual problems based on music theory: polyrhythms, hemiols, atonality, syncopation, etc. Rhythm Doctor helps you learn music theory through gameplay. The game contains: more than 20 levels with their own stories; each chapter is a rhythmic concept; many doctors and unique patients; single and cooperative mode; about 50 different visual effects and backgrounds.

The gameplay is represented by music, where you need to hit the beat in time by pressing certain keys at the right time. The better the player copes with the task, the better the patient begins to feel. Each patient has a medical card where you can study the medical history in detail. There is an opportunity to create your own levels. An extensive editor allows you to add your own compositions, create unique rhythms, beats and effects. For experienced players, the “Night Shift” mode is available, which has increased difficulty, speed and rhythms.
