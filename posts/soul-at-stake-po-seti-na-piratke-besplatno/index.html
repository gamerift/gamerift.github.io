Game Description#

Soul at Stake is a survival game with the ability to play for free on a pirate online with a friend or random participants, avoiding a ghost in enclosed spaces. The cooperative is available online online. The project is imbued with oriental themes and myths. The online game Soul at Stake features asymmetrical multiplayer 4 vs 1. Urban legend says that if you survive the night in an old mansion, you can get incredibly rich. However, you need to put your soul on the line.

The ghost of Xi’E is hunting the survivors. He is not capable of killing, but does everything to turn people into bloodthirsty monsters. He uses the greed and depravity of humans for his own purposes. Several types of demons are represented. There are several unique maps available for the game. The opportunity for free online play brings together a team of four daredevils (or crazy people) who must survive throughout the night and avoid the monster lurking in the corridors of the ancient monastery.

Both parties must move quietly around the location. It is necessary to set traps and deceive the enemy in every possible way. Each level, be it an abandoned train station, a hundred-year-old palace, etc., has its own unique architecture and objects for interaction. Success depends on the coordinated work of all participants. Everyone determines for themselves what to do in a given situation. Come to the aid of a wounded comrade or flee to try to get a monetary reward yourself.
