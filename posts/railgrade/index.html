Game Description#

Railgrade gameplay combines elements of strategy and construction. The plot covers the times of the great construction of railways around the world in order to connect fast transportation between foreign cities with valuable industry. The main character will save the future of mankind, since he is the last employee of the earth colony. Megacorporations asked him to fulfill a difficult mission and save everything that remained on the planet after the disaster that occurred due to errors in the calculations of management.

Gamer receives dozens of trains, an extensive list of tools and capabilities into its own hands to restore the industry of a resource colony. A single campaign includes 30+ levels with gradually complicating mechanics and elements. The player will have to help restore the economic prosperity of cities and organizations for all continents. The use of tools occurs using intuitive actions. The user is able to configure his trains using different engines and types of cargo. The combination between goods, trailers, engines and other elements will help create original compositions.

Strategic placement of rails is the path to the success and speed of transportation. Many obstacles can be destroyed due to dynamite, and bridges are created over the abysses. The system of thorough input management and output will help control the flow of production. Narrow places can harm trains and destroy the valuable load. Profit allows you to invest in the city, improve life or increase the company’s revolutions. The user will be able to experience his abilities in additional “contract” missions, and each of them has new and complicated goals.
