Game Description#

The narrative inMonomyth takes you to dark and gloomy times, immersing you in a dark fantasy atmosphere. Strong natural forces in the form of storms devastate the surface of the earth, which is why humanity has to go underground. The world is plunging into a period of chaos and hopelessness, making it increasingly difficult for ordinary people to survive; strife and civil strife begin, followed by the decline of civilization and culture of the human race.

Set as a protagonist driven by his goals in Monomyth, players will have to explore vast underground spaces and engage in battles with a large number of monsters inhabiting the gloomy locations. You will have to carefully examine the zones and elements of the environment with which you can interact and advance the plot by completing incoming tasks. The main task for the hero is to search for a certain ancient artifact, which is a divine gift that can help in these troubled hours.

Together with the expedition, the squad goes into the abandoned cellars of the destroyed fortress to find a sacred relic and help save the protagonist’s brother. While exploring the surroundings, you have to move carefully, without attracting the attention of lurking evil creatures, ready to attack at any second. Objects in the game are interactive, allowing you to pick them up and examine them in more detail. During battles, you will have to monitor the character’s condition and perform precise strikes and blocks to defeat opponents.
