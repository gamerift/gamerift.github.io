Game Description#

In Breakwaters, the player will reconnolate the procedurally generated world with monstrous ocean depths and huge titans that change the method of interaction with water. Water is replaced by powerful crystals obtained in the mine. The user is able to delve into its bowels to collect rare objects, build obstacles to restrain the ocean or block waves, and help people after destruction.

Titans, creatures of an extinct submarine race, wander around the world, causing cataclysms. The key task is to stay alive, using what will be collected on land, trade with the vessels floating by and travel through the light through deadly storms to make amazing discoveries. You can create buildings interacting with the modeling of the ocean on a global scale. It will be possible to easily redo the gorge into the lake, open the gates or flood the lowlands. Functions Breakwaters:

Cooperative regime and solitary campaign. A huge oceans simulator, creating a special interactive open world. It will be necessary to collect crystals and turn them into objects, displacing and deforming the ocean, opening numerous resources below. It is necessary to defeat the titans the size of the island and fight with golems growth of the house. Objects are generated in accordance with the chosen style of the game, whether it be a farmer, researcher or adventurer.
