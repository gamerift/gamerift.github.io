Game Description#

Terminus: Zombie Survivors is a gloomy journey that takes gamers to the setting of the zombie apocalypse. It is ahead to survive among dangerous mutants, making their way through a variety of locations. The main task for the hero will be the search for the place of terminus, the last stronghold of mankind. In the game there are elements of Rowlaik that create random situations on the map. It will come out to compete with starvation, cold temperature and aggressive opponents.

In the process of passing the terminus: zombies of a surveyor, users will move in procedurally generated locations, mining a variety of resources for survival. The gameplay is built on a step -by -step system, where it will be able to move, strike, as well as look for objects hidden on the map. Before starting the trip, users will choose a protagonist that differ among themselves unique combat abilities.

Users will find on the map auxiliary elements of equipment, weapons, medications, food, clothes, tools, electrical appliances, etc. Gamers are forced to track the state of the character, trying to support him in good physical and mental form. You will also have to join the battles with mutated monsters that they can infect a hero with a bit. At the death of a protagonist, players need to start passing from the first level.
