Game Description#

TheDuel on Board format is suitable for playing online with a friend on pirate. In this project, users take on the role of pirates who fight not only for an extra glass of beer, but also for the opportunity to become a new authority in these waters. Here you can play not only online online, but also on one computer, when two users use the same keyboard. The characters are located on the deck of the ship, which will become the main battlefield.

The rules in Duel on Board are simple. You must either be the first to get to the beer mug, which is in the center, or throw your opponent off the ship so that he does not do the same to the gamer. The main weapon will be a pistol, whose projectiles can be reflected using a shield. So, by placing a block, you can reflect the bullet at the one who fired it, and if he does not react in time, he will receive a lot of damage. To make the online game more interesting, you can choose one of the random events. List of random events and their features in Duel on Board: Blizzard - fired shells slow down users, and several consecutive hits can completely turn a pirate into an icicle. Alien invasion - flying saucers will fly over the deck, and so that the gamer is not kidnapped, you need to dodge the rays. Haunted seas - the ghosts of pirates who were once a threat to the locals will enter the battle water.Storm - the arena is struck by thunder and lightning strikes. In order not to lose the round, you need to watch the game and avoid direct hits.

The possibility of free online play allows you to make the gameplay more dynamic than when fighting with bots. The user selects the crew member he will control. Each unit has its own set of characteristics, so you can try out all the available options to choose the most suitable candidates for future battles.
