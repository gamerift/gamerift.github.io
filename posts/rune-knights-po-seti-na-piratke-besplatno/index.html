Game Description#

Rune Knights can be played online with a friend, clearing dungeons in co-op. A network game is available for free on the pirate site, allowing you to connect online via the Internet to go explore dark locations with your partners. Before starting the journey, each player creates his own character, giving him external attributes and distributing characteristics. Procedurally generated locations will be explored. There are 8 character classes in total, which can be upgraded to level 30.

Heroes of Rune Knights fight with one-handed or two-handed swords, hammers, staves, shields, bows, etc. The adventure has no plot. Free online play allows up to four players to join forces. Partners can both explore the various corridors of the gloomy world and fight in an endless arena, demonstrating their skills and strength. Allows you to develop 8 ability branches containing over 120 talents. Each hero has more than 90 individual skills that determine the style of play.

By collecting resources, you can craft weapons, tools and useful items. By killing enemies, you accumulate experience points, allowing you to unlock new abilities. Found items can be improved or processed into special modifications. A total of 15 unique objects are available for collection, which are subject to experimentation or combination. You will have to fight with 20 types of ordinary enemies. Also featured are 8 elite enemy units and 5 powerful bosses. Trade mechanics allow you to exchange items or sell unnecessary junk.
