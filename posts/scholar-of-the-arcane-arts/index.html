Game Description#

Scholar of the Arcane Arts is devoted to the use of spells interacting with each other to confront the invasion. The universe flooded five plans of magic, forcing the player to look for new forces. Knowledge is gained through the collection of magic scrolls hidden in different parts of the world. For survival, it is necessary to solve puzzles, explore the secret dungeons and destroy monsters, stereing of powerful artifacts. The character will have to find out how to combine various spells to overcome difficulties.

In Scholar of the Arcane Arts, a deep understanding of the principles of magic is the key to success. The gamer is available for the development of five schools: hydrandery, pyromania, geomancy, necromancy and aeromancy. These directions can be combined and improved by creating over 10,000 unique combinations. The central task is to select a spell for counterattacks, neutralization or direct attack on opponents. The result depends on the tactically correct use of the collected arsenal.

The path runs through 3 vast, procedurally created world, full of dangers. The study of locations will solve confusing puzzles and clean the dungeons in search of powerful spells. Each game world is teeming with dangerous enemies, nests of the creatures and fortified logs of monsters. These territories are under the control of formidable opponents that surpass the hero in speed and strength. Ultimately, the journey will lead to direct clashes with frightening demigods that control the captured lands.
