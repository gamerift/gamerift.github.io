Game Description#

Garden of Witches will tell about the strength - a girl who makes his way through a dangerous magical world to return order. Its goal is to find the escaped witches who violated agreements and abandoned the garden. The place is rapidly destroyed, but the strength is not going to allow Leni to destroy everything that she built. Each promotion requires actions, choice and readiness for a fight.

In Garden of Witches, progress depends on overcoming threats and obtaining new skills. As the forces advance, it opens up the abilities necessary for survival and movement forward. Fights arise suddenly, and each time you have to quickly adapt. The procedural levels are not repeated, and not a single attempt will look like the previous one. This action requires constant control over the situation and accurate calculation.

As the returns of the witches deposit, the territory becomes more hostile. The enemies are intensified, the allies are in no hurry to help. To return the violators back, you have to act hard. Each witch is a separate test that makes you use everything that has accumulated over the past races. Between the sorties you can return to the shelter to prepare and improve abilities. Failures here do not zero progress. The acquired forces are preserved, the structure of the passage remains. Each next attempt is a continuation, not the beginning from scratch. This Title pushes the gamer not to give up and go to the end. For the sake of maintaining the garden, there are no steps back, but only forward.
