Game Description#

A FISTFUL of Yankes offers the player to lead a detachment of government soldiers sent to the American West in 1865 to combat desperate criminals and robber barons. On this post -war frontier, life and death solve weapons. The key task of the unit is to establish order by tracking the robbers attacking the settlers. However, this mission draws the fighters into a much larger conflict with serious consequences, going beyond initial orders.

Law and order under the muzzle of a revolver:

Management of a detachment in tactical shootings using shelters and special abilities. A reasonable disposal of resources and fighters for survival in harsh conditions. The passage of a cinematic campaign filled with unexpected plot turns and intrigues. Immersion in the atmosphere of a classic western with elements of strategy in real time.

As a real -time strategy, the a Fistful of Yankees title model tactical shootings in lawless territories devoid of formal duels. Combat scenarios require players to find shelters for their wards, create narrow passages to contain the enemy’s advance and apply special skills to win the hostile forces.

The game also includes a system of management of units in the group and the prestige earned by their actions. The wrong order of these resources can lead to the complete destruction of the group on the battlefield. The plot campaign of the project is conducted by a detachment of arid wastelands to dense northern forests as the tasks set. This promotion leads to a trace of cruel intent organized by more dangerous criminals than ordinary thugs. History unfolds through cinematographic skirmishes, representing an insidious intrigue with a structure inspired by western comics.
