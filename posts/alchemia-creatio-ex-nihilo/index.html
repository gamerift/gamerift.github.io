Game Description#

Alchemia: Creatio Ex Nihilo combines several genres. This is a head-headed game, a step-by-step strategy and a simplified corgorate. The action takes place in the fantasy world, where magic is adjacent to alchemy. The hero travels through the forgotten kingdom, finds artifacts (available 30 species), collects materials, opens recipes (there are 40 varieties here), crafts weapons from them and fights with its help with different monsters. The user can choose a route for his ward on a fork. There are different events at the points of interest - he is visiting the store before the fight with opponents. In total, there are 15 possible options for events.

The name of the project can be translated as alchemy into Russian: creating from nothing. The gameplay resembles partially Slay The Spire and other diabetes-dekebilders. The fighting works on the principle of KKI, only here the character receives not cards in the hands, but reagents. From them he collects various bombs, grenades, potions and other deadly or auxiliary objects. They can be applied to the main character or to his rivals. The more often the protagonist creates the same thing, the faster the skill scale grows. After filling it, an improved version of a particular recipe opens. The ingredients for craft must be manually dragged into one of 4 cells. The video game encourages experiments in building and craft. According to the developers, in their creation there are 10 quintillions of combinations. In Indie game there are 35 types of opponents. Each enemy needs to look for their approach.
