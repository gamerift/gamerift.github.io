Game Description#

A puzzle game where in the first part you manipulated a magic clock mechanism. In the second part of the game, they were given the opportunity to walk around different rooms, solving various riddles. The Room Two - Follows a mysterious path with the help of letters from a scientist known as “AS”. The hero is trapped in a pocket dimension. He must find the author of the notes. The mysterious scientist was the first to connect the magical properties of the boxes with the zero element.

After the tutorial level, there are five chapters, each located in different locations, with different topics. The highlight of the game is the sea level. In it you solve puzzles between a ship, a treasure chest and a puzzle box.

Gameplay includes scenes of finding small switches, hidden parts, keyholes and matching keys, strange patterns, hieroglyphs, and objects that can be flipped, pulled, or pushed. The view camera is implemented better than in the first part. You can see through panels, see alternate versions of objects. Thanks to the magic clock, additional dimensions open up.

The transitions from one chapter to the next include well-crafted horror moments. On the way you come across creeping tentacles, mystical visions and eerie sounds. After solving a puzzle in one room, the player is moved to the next location, which may be on the opposite side of the world.
