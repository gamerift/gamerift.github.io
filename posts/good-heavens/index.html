Game Description#

Good HEAVENS! It takes place in the Severe Kingdom of Xant-Rar, a bizarre afterlife, where souls live on floating islands. This songwriter with the open world allows gamers to pave their own path in the world, crowded with bizarre capabilities. Imagine: you have lived a good life, have left the earthly path and got into Xant -Rar - a place intended for the fulfillment of all your desires throughout eternity. Sounds like paradise, right? But this is not so.

Its creator, a clumsy mighty rara, has a mischievous vein. He grabs souls from our world and throws them into his bizarre creation, often leading to deplorable results. This is where you enter. You, a gamer, choked on a chewing gum (seriously?), Find yourself resurrected in this absurd afterlife. Having accused the powerful RAR in your distress, you become an unlikely hero who is instructed to save others from their meaningless and frankly miserable existence.

In Good Heavens! There are procedurally generated floating islands, each of which is a unique environment with its resources, races and creatures that can be found. It doesn’t matter who you consider yourself - a farmer, a warrior, a cook or a merchant - the game is designed for a variety of styles. Explore, build and improve your base and character, plunging into this world. The dynamic class system and a modeled ecosystem make the game ideal for network cooperation, so take friends and get ready for joint chaos.
