Game Description#

Bratz Rhythm and Style offers players to go on a world tour with Claus, Yasmin, Sasha and Jade. The main task is to earn a reputation on the catwalks around the world. The studio has to create unique images for the chosen heroine using tools for designing outfits, hairstyles and makeup. These branded styles are demonstrated in competitions and performances. The main trip consists of traveling around the iconic cities to participate in local events and the formation of unique identity for the team.

The gameplay in Bratz Rhythm and Style is being built on a visit to 6 different capitals of fashion, including London, Milan and Tokyo. In each location, gamers compete in the Mini-IGR series, checking their skill in fashion shows, dancing and fitness. These rhythmic trials take place to the music from the Bratz universe. Throughout the tour, the self -proclaimed Queen of the Berdin style and twins tweaded the self -proclaimed Queen of the style. To promote the plot, you will have to defeat them in stylish battles, showing an excellent taste and honed dance movements.

The Title is designed for a single passage and a local multiplayer. Up to four people can unite for a joint or competitive game in the separated screen mode. Regardless of the selected format, the action takes place in the three -dimensional world of the universe. The main mechanics are focused on self -expression through visual setting and gameplay, tied to performances. Success requires a combination of a creative approach in the design studio and a sense of rhythm during testing on stage.
