Game Description#

In Saints Row 2022, you can play online as a pirate for free with a friend, exploring a large open world together over the Internet online. The action takes place in Miami, namely in the city of Santo Ileso. Three groups are fighting among themselves: the Maidari Panthers, the Idols and the Marshalls. Each faction has its own unique traits. For example, Maidari prefer close confrontations, Idols are lovers of nightlife, and Marshalls are responsible for security and try in every possible way to control the metropolis.

The online game Saints Row 2022 allows you to take control of the so-called bosses, who are new to Santo Ileso. You will need to recruit people into your gang and confront all three warring factions. The possibility of a free online game invites each participant to create his own unique character, specifying his gender, external parameters, character traits, etc. The main characters of the adventure are such squad members as Nina - a professional mechanic, Kevi - a charismatic DJ and Eli - a brainy businessman.

The city is completely accessible for exploration. It is represented by 9 different areas, including Rancho Providenzo - a country backwater, Eldorado - a rich entertainment area, Monte Vista - a calm suburb, etc. You can open your own illegal business to earn more money, get into any transport, quickly covering vast distances, buy various weapons, clothes and much more. A sophisticated character editor is available with a choice of skin color, hair and other appearance attributes.
