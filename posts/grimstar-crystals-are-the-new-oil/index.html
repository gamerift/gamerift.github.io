Game Description#

Grimstar: Crystals Are the New Oil! - This is an exciting strategy in real time, in which you will go to study the distant world called Grimstar, where you will find a new type of natural energy resources. But not everything is so rosy and sunny - because of your brother’s sudden death, you have to fulfill his contract with the corporation for which he worked, and you must take his place on an alien planet. With each mission passed, you will open valuable improvements, new units and buildings, as well as increase your level to become a more effective resource mining. But the point is not only in the extraction of crystals - there are also alien artifacts that need to be collected, and enemy hordes, from which you need to fight off your armies. As you pass the game, various planets will meet you, each with its unique vegetation and characteristics.

But this is not all - in the game there are also heroes who can be called to help, they are aligned and become stronger after each mission, giving you an advantage over the enemy. It depends on you how effectively you will manage your armies, using as few resources as possible, and will be a winner in your desire to make your company successful. So what are you waiting for? Explore other people’s planets and show the universe who is the master in it, in Grimstar: Crystals are the New Oil!
