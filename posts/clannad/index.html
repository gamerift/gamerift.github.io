Game Description#

CLANNAD is a visual novel in anime style. The story follows a second grade student who begins a new school year at Hikarizaka School. On his way to school, he meets a girl, Nagisa. It turns out that she is constantly alone and has no friends at all. Depending on the actions you take, the course of the story changes. By helping a girl make friends, you build good relationships with some characters, but at the same time spoil them with others. 6 main characters of CLANNAD: Tomoya Okazaki - the main character of the game, who lives with his alcoholic father; Nagisa Furukawa - the protagonist’s friend, a lonely girl with poor health; Kyo Fujibayashi - the class president who is secretly in love with Tomoya; Kotomi Ichinose - a student , who has the status of a local genius; Tomoyo Sakagami is a strong girl, which is why many call her cruel; Fuko Ibuki is a kind and sympathetic student who is always happy to help.

The events of the game are divided into two parts: School life and After school. Each part consists of 8 episodes. Your choices influence the narrative and have a direct impact on the fate of the characters. At the end of the story, you can expect both good and bad endings to the story arcs of both the main characters and secondary ones.

For completing the arc, players are awarded a special bonus, the Sphere of Light. To get the only canonical ending to the story, you need to collect 13 of these spheres. The main difference between this game and other studio projects is that there are absolutely no intimate connections between the characters. Various pieces of art and concept art are included as bonus materials.
