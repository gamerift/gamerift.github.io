Game Description#

The gameThe Elder Scrolls Online offers you to go through a number of locations for several warring factions, playing both alone and in real time with other players. The plot tells how Molag Bal, known as the Prince of Violence, decides to take over the entire kingdom of Tamriel and enslave ordinary citizens. The gamer will have to face the forces of evil and defeat him.

Throughout the game world of The Elder Scrolls Online, there are several factions available, each of which is endowed with certain properties. For example, the Aldmeri Dominion includes races such as the Khajiit, Altmer and Bosmer, the Eponheart Pact includes the Nords, Dunmer and Argonians, and the Daggerfall Covenant includes orcs, Bretons and Redguards.

The main storyline is designed for solo playthrough, but there are a number of dungeons and similar missions that are available for cooperative exploration. In total, there are 3 types of dungeons available in the game, among which there are single, group and public, which in turn are divided into veteran and standard. In addition, gamers can improve in one of 7 professions, master blacksmithing, cooking, alchemy, jewelry, sewing or magic crafts and woodworking.

In addition, you can join a specific guild or create your own faction. The most popular character classes are Thieves, Mages and Warriors. During the battle, there are 5 main skills available that can be selected before the fight and 1 separate skill, which is the strongest. The weapons are designed for different fighting styles, and are suitable for both close and long-range damage; among the ammunition there are swords and maples, staves for sorcery and bows. Added armor of different levels, from light to the most protected.
