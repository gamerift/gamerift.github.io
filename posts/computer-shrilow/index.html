Game Description#

In the Computer Shrilow, a familiar computer screen appears in front of the gamer, in the center of which is now a green dog. Its appearance coincides with the origin of the whole economy that functions inside the system. The task is to interact with this digital space to find out what exactly captured the machine and its functions. The linear plot directs the passage, introducing to the characters and events explaining the strange circumstances of the incident.

Gameplay in the Computer Shrilow is built on clicks to receive currency and buy improvements for automation of the process. In addition to this main cycle, the project has a variety of mini-games that provide alternative ways of developing and earnings of resources. Gamers visit domestic stores to purchase objects such as clothes and phones, or control operations on objects like mines. The training system introduces these mechanics that complement each other as they pass.

Key features of digital madness:

Click for earnings and automate income. Participate in mini-games and visit internal stores. Interact with the characters, revealing the linear plot. Take pets in a world full of psychedelic elements.

The plot is revealed through communication with the characters found inside the computer, including personalities such as John Stonener inhabiting the digital world. This gameplay includes psychedelic elements that reflect the unusual transformation of the medium of a personal computer into a captured space. You can also start pets and take care of them, which adds another level of interaction to a solitary campaign of this game.
