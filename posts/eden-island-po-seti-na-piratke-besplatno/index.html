Game Description#

SurvivalistEden Island offers to explore the Island of Eden on the Internet online, or alone. You can play pirate for free online with a friend or random players, surviving together on a tropical island. The enemies are natural disasters and wild animals. Online play is also represented by the ability of Eden Island to fight among themselves. Everyone chooses their own path: become a peace-loving farmer or hunt other survivors.

You will need to explore vast territories, trying to find useful items. The map is represented by land and ocean. There are useful resources in the sea, but getting them is not so easy. The possibility of free online play allows you to join a tribe to build a huge house and base, or carry out raids on the resources of other players.

Mechanics include gathering, building, hunting, growing crops, cutting trees, and trading. The further you move deeper into the wild jungle, the more dangerous enemies you can meet on your way. Wild kangaroos, powerful pandas and other large animals are capable of attacking. Construction involves many different types of walls, roofs, furniture, etc. There is an opportunity to build an entire town. Farming includes a wide variety of crops, and the ocean is home to many species of fish.

The natives living on the island are able to exchange or buy goods. Mineral resources are of particular value. There is a dynamic system for changing weather conditions and time of day. Weather has a direct impact on gameplay. Just like the night, as there are more enemies and they attack the camp much more intensely.
