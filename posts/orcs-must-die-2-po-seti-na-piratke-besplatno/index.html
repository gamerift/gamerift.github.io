Game Description#

Orcs Must Die 2 is a third-person cooperative action game aimed at playing online with a friend. LAN connection and online Internet connection are available. Allows you to go through the campaign together on a pirate, using all network functions for free. There are two main characters - a battle mage and an agile sorceress. Each hero has his own skills and abilities.

The protagonists will have to defend their fortress and guard the mines, preventing orcs and other monsters from entering human possessions. To protect themselves from monsters, heroes use various weapons, spells, traps, amulets and much more. Multiplayer allows you to play through the story campaign together and demonstrate your skills in the endless survival mode. Key features of Orcs Must Die 2: possibility of free online play; each character fights using unique tactics; classic mode, containing 10 levels from the first part; 50 types of weapons, traps, etc.; 255 types of upgrades; choice difficulties, including Nightmare.

Gradually, you need to open up access to new equipment, various means of defense, and much more. The protagonists also upgrade their skills and improve their skills. In total, you have to fight with 20 different monsters, where each enemy poses a serious threat: trolls, elementals, ghouls and many others. Some opponents migrated from the previous part.
