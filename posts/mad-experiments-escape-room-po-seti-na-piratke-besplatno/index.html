Game Description#

You can play online inMad Experiments Escape Room either alone, with a friend, or in a group of several participants. Having connected online via the Internet, players find themselves in an quest room from which they need to find a way out. The pirated Mad Experiments Escape Room has a Supporter Pack available for free. In order to leave the premises, only 60 minutes of real time are given, so you need to act as quickly as possible. There is no time to hesitate!

The possibility of free online play allows a team of daredevils to complete 3 unique levels: Laboratory in the estate, Amnesia and Lost Childhood. Each location is represented by individual rooms, puzzles, conditions and artistic performance. You need to quickly explore the rooms for quest items. Almost all objects in the premises are interactive, which allows you to interact with them: you can move, rotate, transfer, etc. The more coordinated and well-played the team is, the greater the chances of solving all the puzzles, finding the necessary keys and getting out, taking a high position in the world leaderboards.

The plot centers on Professor Cheshire and his assistant Hildegard, who invite everyone to participate in one very strange and unusual experiment. Scientists have prepared a series of tests and tests to help them analyze the human brain. Throughout all three chapters, Hildegard helps and guides the players in every possible way, and in the last chapter, it is into her past that the test participants find themselves. Multiplayer includes: co-op for up to 6 players; cooperative learning and interaction with the environment; a huge number of riddles, puzzles and puzzles; participation in a strange experiment that requires a high level of deduction; 3 different levels with individual conditions and mechanics.
