Game Description#

The gameplay ofSpace Engineers is that you will build space stations, ships, and begin to study neighboring planets and passing asteroids, where you will find many useful resources. Control is carried out from the perspective of a person, a ship, a base, a flying station, and you can change the camera view from both the third and first person.

When you create a world, the game automatically generates a random sequence of planets on which you can find valuable resources. However, the structure of Space Engineers helps you get comfortable with the gameplay, creating simplified conditions for contact with other planets, civilizations and technologies.

There are 3 types of ships in the game: small, large and station ships. While researching new technologies, you will have to build all kinds of space fleets to send small spaceships to the surface of other planets; in large ones, send entire expeditions to study and collect resources; stations will be needed to create a colony or industrial complex on the basis of another planet or asteroid. In fact, the game is a sandbox in which you need to create ships, but a lot depends on the mode.

Creative mode. You have an endless amount of resources. You can create entire stations, ships, blocks instantly, without waiting for construction to be completed. You can also copy ships; Survival. You are thrown into outer space, the structure of which is built by artificial intelligence. You don’t have resources, so the first step is to provide yourself with them. A feature of the regime was electricity, which tends to run out. There will be living people at your initial base, so you will have to monitor your oxygen and life levels.

With new updates, the developers have added a Blueprint system to create unique buildings in outer space.
