Game Description#

Danger in Pompeii places a player in a historical city on his last day. The goal is to decipher the message from Mercury and an attempt to survive during the eruption of Vesuvius. Promotion in the plot is ensured by navigation through the streets and interaction with the population. The central part of the experience is helping the city’s residents in solving their problems before the start of the cataclysm. The narrative develops as a gamer helps others and works on a plan for salvation from disaster.

Riddles on the eve of the disaster:

Deciphering the mysterious message from Mercury. Investigation of an attempt on a famous actor. Search for missing sacred chickens. Help to the townspeople to save from eruption.

The gameplay in Danger in Pompeii is focused on puzzles offered by the inhabitants. In this Title, an attempt on the actor Claudia will have to be investigated to establish the guilty. Other tasks include the search for the missing sacred chickens and the discovery of components for the recipe for bread in Pistinum. The implementation of these tests allows you to collect history in parts. Each revealed secret contributes to the common storyline about the fate of the city and the impending volcanic event.

The world of this project is a reconstruction of Pompey according to historical documents open to research. You can visit temples on the forum, go on stage of the Bolshoi Theater or join the audience in the amphitheater. The city is inhabited by more than 60 characters with its backstory and related side tasks. In total, this game has 9 main quests and 28 cognitive materials from the experts of the Le Rose Institute, describing in detail the daily life of Roman citizens of that era.
