Game Description#

Chosen War puts the player in the role of the chosen one who is instructed to gather and novels the army in the archipelago. It is necessary to move around the fogs covered in fog and enter into battle with enemy forces using the tactical placement of detachments. Each adventure is built around the study of the world and provisions. The ultimate goal is a collision with the supreme priest on the final location. Routes, the arrangement of opponents and events are generated procedurally, ensuring the uniqueness of each new sortie.

In Chosen War, success on the battlefield is rewarded with gifts from a deity named God Spaghetti. Awards include amplifiers for detachments, and obtaining a third upgrade for one type of unit leads to a qualitative transformation of its abilities. Gamers can spend gold coins on a new set of amplifiers. This currency is also key in interaction with a cunning merchant. This character offers various objects, new recruits and skills available for study in exchange for gold on special trading locations.

The gameplay offers many strategic possibilities in addition to direct clashes. On some lands, the altar of miracles allows you to resurrect the fallen warriors for a fee, but they return to the system weakened. You can also break prisons guarded by local inhabitants, and free the captives, replenishing them their ranks. The movement between the land arrays occurs on the boat, which consumes supplies, or through teleportation between special sugar-triangular islands that do not deplete the diet. Some territories store treasures under the protection of garrisons, and to obtain values ​​it is necessary to overcome these detachments.
