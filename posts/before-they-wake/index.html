Game Description#

Before They Wake takes place in the summer of 1984 and tells the story of Alfred, who is confined to a wheelchair. Trapped in an orphanage overrun by sound-sensitive mutants, Alfred navigates a path of survival and escape. His wheelchair squeaks with every movement, adding to the stress as he navigates the dangerous environment. The mechanics boil down to controlling the noise level so as not to attract the attention of mutants.

Before They Wake’s gameplay emphasizes stealth and strategy. Gamers must carefully plan their movements, balancing the need to escape with the risk of making noise. The layout of the shelter and the unpredictable behavior of the mutants create difficult conditions. It uses a third-person perspective, allowing users to fully immerse themselves in Alfred’s struggle for survival. The narrative unfolds through Alfred’s journey, revealing the dark history of the asylum and the origins of mutants.

The plot is full of suspense and mystery, forcing users to unravel the secrets hidden within the walls of the shelter. The atmosphere is designed to evoke a sense of dread and urgency, forcing players to think quickly and act decisively. Overall, the title is a unique combination of horror and strategy, forcing players to navigate a dangerous environment while dealing with the constant threat of sound-sensitive mutants.
