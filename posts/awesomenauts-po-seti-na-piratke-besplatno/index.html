Game Description#

Awesomenauts is a two-dimensional MOBA that allows you to play together with a friend online, fighting for free on a pirate in confrontations both on one device and online online. You can play cooperatively or participate in competitions. The possibility of free online play allows you to take part in dynamic 3 on 3 multiplayer matches. The plot revolves around an interstellar conflict in which robots fight among themselves. The average duration of a mast is 30 minutes. Key mechanics and features: cartoon style of the 80s; many classes, modifications, creeps, turrets and everything that is characteristic of the MOBA genre are available; control of one hero with unique skills and battle tactics; the ability to fight on one screen with friends; hundreds of unique items to collect, various power-ups, etc.; many planets and space stations to explore.

The action moves to 3587. Each side of the conflict wants to gain influence in the vastness of the galaxy. Awesomenauts are mercenaries representing the interests of the state. Each robot has unique weapons, skills and tactics. Multiplayer, single-player and split-screen battles are available. The characters are a gangster, a casanova - a chameleon, an octopus, an admiral, a military man, etc. Each hero has his own backstory. Before the start of the fight, you can customize your character by choosing equipment, fighting style, and much more. While fighting enemies, you need to collect items, upgrade skills, and much more.
