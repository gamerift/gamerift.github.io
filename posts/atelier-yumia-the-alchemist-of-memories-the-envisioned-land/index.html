Game Description#

**Atelier yumia: the alchemist of memorials & the envisioned Land ** offers users to plunge into the world formed by the remnants of the lost empire. In the center of the plot is Yumi Lissfeldt, a young alchemist who goes in search of secrets of the cataclysm, who once destroyed a powerful civilization. During a trip to the unknown continent, gamers will engage in various types of activities, such as collecting materials, the synthesis of objects and battle with enemies. In this vast world, synthesis plays a key role.

Gamers at Atelier Yumia: The Alchemist of Memories & The Envisioed Land must manipulate mana ingredients to create objects that help in battle, research and construction of the base. These items are not just a sight: they can become a weapon, for example, a sword or spear, and be used in real time, where time and strategy are crucial. The combat system offers a unique highlight - two types of attack ranges, between which users can switch to vary their skills and an approach to battles.

Traveling around the open world, users will be able to build their own bases, furnishing them with furniture and other elements in accordance with their needs. The craft is not limited only by combat instruments: it also applies to the creation of practical objects for research, making every step in the journey useful. Thanks to these custom options, gamers can adapt to the difficulties that they face, plunging into the secrets of alchemy.
