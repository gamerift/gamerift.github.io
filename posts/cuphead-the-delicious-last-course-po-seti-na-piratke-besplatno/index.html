Game Description#

InCuphead The Delicious Last Course players can team up online to take on a variety of bosses together. On the pirate Cuphead The Delicious Last Course, you can play online with a friend for free, controlling Cuphead and Mug. A new character has appeared in the addon - the Golden Cup. Network play allows you to take control of this hero. The Golden Chalice initially has all its abilities open, so it will help beginners in completing the campaign.

The gameplay of Cuphead stands out due to its hardcore difficulty. Mechanics is an old school platformer. You will need to fight exclusively with bosses. There are no ordinary enemies. Between battles with the leaders, you can move around the map, choosing the order of fights. Each monster comes with a unique design and attack patterns. All opponents have several phases, which radically change the tactics of battles. The possibility of free online play is aimed at cooperative interaction between participants. You and your partner will need to defeat a series of powerful opponents.

All characters are charismatic and memorable heroes. One of them is chef Solonkin. The events of the add-on take place on the new Ink Island. Several additional types of weapons have also been added, which are designed to diversify the gameplay. With the Golden Cup you can fight not only with new unique creatures, but also go through the main campaign of the original adventure.
