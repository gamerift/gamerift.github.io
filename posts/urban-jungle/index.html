Game Description#

Urban Jungle gives users the opportunity to turn ordinary residential premises into bright vegetable sanctuaries, without straining in a gameplay. The project invites enthusiasts to experiment with the placement of plants in compact apartments, creating personal green corners without typical care worries that accompany gardening in the real world.

Users use the main mechanics of strategic decoration, determining the optimal position for each plant variety in order to maximize the level of comfort in digital living space. The action of Urban Jungle takes place at various stages of the life of the protagonist, revealing the thin elements of the narrative through environmental clues, and not through explicit techniques of presentation. The Title includes light elements of the puzzle that make the gamers critically comprehend their choice of decor, without limiting them in time and not providing pressure.

Each plant finds its ideal place by trial and errors, and successful arrangements open additional varieties to further improve the aesthetic attractiveness of the apartment. The presence of a cat companion adds another level of interaction, prompting users to take breaks in decoration in order to chat with his virtual pet. The video game is designed for lovers of calm titles, in which the main thing is relaxation, not competition or achievement of the goal. The isometric perspective provides a complete overview of the space, allowing you to accurately arrange objects using simple control “Indicate and click”.
