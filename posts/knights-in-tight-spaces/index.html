Game Description#

Knights in Tight Spaces takes place in a medieval fantasy setting. Your main task is to control the environment and assemble a squad to face various bandits and supernatural threats. The mechanic is based on deck building, where strategic choice and card synergy play a crucial role in defeating your enemies. You will need to carefully plan each move, taking into account the environment, team composition and available resources.

The title forces users to adapt to new situations with each level, offering a dynamic approach to each battle. At its core, Knights in Tight Spaces is about mastering the intricacies of tactical combat. Gamers will have to build the best deck from over 300 available cards, each with unique abilities and effects. Choosing the right combination of cards can turn the tide of battle, making every decision when building your deck important.

In addition, there are various character classes and equipment, which further expands your tactical options. This flexibility allows you to use different strategies as you progress through levels. One aspect that stands out is the need to carefully select and manage your team. Not all allies are created equal, and sometimes you will have to weigh the pros and cons of adding a particular character to your party.
