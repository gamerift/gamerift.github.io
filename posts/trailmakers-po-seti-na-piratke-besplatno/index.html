Game Description#

Trailmakers combines construction, sandbox and racing. You can play Pirate by yourself or online with a friend. Co-op and PVP are available online. The main feature of the indie project is the ability to assemble airplanes, cars, helicopters, and submarines from blocks reminiscent of Lego sets. Humanoid combat robots are also allowed here.

Trailmakers has an open world consisting of two large maps. And the possibility of free online play makes it more lively and intense. There are many ways to entertain yourself. You can build your own transport or choose a ready-made design from other users, take part in rallies, organize races with friends, try yourself in passing tests, and fight for a place on the leaderboard. There is also a small campaign, which is an extended training.

You can also take part in combat races with shooting or assemble an aircraft to explore the area on it. The title allows you to soar into the sky, sink to the bottom of the ocean, or go into space. Trips by water and land are also available. A network game with free entry and exit contains all the modes that are available for a single player. A large number of details gives scope for creativity and experimentation. The range of bricks available even allows you to recreate vehicles from movies or other video games. The physics engine takes into account the aerodynamic characteristics and other parameters of the vehicle.
