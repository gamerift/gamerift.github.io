Game Description#

Journey Of Life is a role-playing adventure that you can play with a friend online or explore the open world alone. For free on pirates, each player can connect with a partner via the Internet online to go on the journey of a lifetime. You will play as an engineer who has lost his entire fortune at one point. He was forced to leave his home to start his life from scratch. Network play gives you the opportunity to wander around a huge open world with your friends, interacting with each other.

The gameplay includes mechanics of survival, city building simulator, action, etc. Having started the journey empty-handed, you gradually have to collect various resources with which you can craft weapons or build your own base. Gradually, it will be necessary to grow from a small village to a thriving settlement. The city consists of stations that process materials and various components, residential buildings, etc. Resources are wood, copper and ore. Also scattered around the world are ancient runes that enhance the hero’s abilities. Journey Of Life mechanics: possibility of free online play; PvP and PvE content; protecting the village from attack; character gender selection: male or female; hero customization; dynamic system for changing weather conditions; ore smelting, casting, construction, hunting, etc.; many production stations: fiber breaker, looms, mills and many others; exploration of a huge open world.
