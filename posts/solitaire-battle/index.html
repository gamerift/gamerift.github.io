Game Description#

Solitaire Battle combines the mechanics of the card game with the progression of the Autobatler’s Horned. Players are faced with opponents where key interaction is a party in the solitaire of three peaks. Each card taken from the field or from the deck is converted into an offensive or defensive action, creating automated combat sequences. The main task is to strategically disassemble the layout to win enemies, while forming a set of random pool of tools that contribute to powerful synergies and encouraging new combinations in each passage.

Solitaire Battle represents 14 unique heroes available for choice or appearing as opponents on the battlefield. The choice of the character determines the fundamental strategy; The dwarf can focus on the stunning of the rivals, digging up the cards, while the knight works on filling the field with shields for protection. After each battle, the project opens access to the store to improve the deck with new weapons, skills and passive items of 60 available options.

Such an assembly is necessary for the confrontation of 25 bosses using their own methods for the counter -action of the player’s moves and the application of status effects. The standard race covers 12 rounds and ends with a fight with the final boss. Victory brings treasures that open access to new characters and 4 complexity levels. After successful passage, gamers can go into an endless format, constantly increasing the test until the defeat. This cycle unfolds in 10 different margins for contractions and offers 75 unique treasures for collecting and further progress.
