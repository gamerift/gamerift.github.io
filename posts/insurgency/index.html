Game Description#

Insurgency is a first-person shooter, in which the developers focused on playing in cooperative or multiplayer mode, about the Iraq War. The protagonist is a member of the US PMC, and he is fighting against Iraqi insurgents. The player chooses one side at the beginning of the match, and after that a brutal fight begins between the two factions. The basis of the game is a confrontation between two teams, which include 16 people. The fight takes place on one of the maps covered in the events of the Iraq War.

The game contains more than 20 weapon models, around which points are farmed in tasks or matches. The more battle points you accumulate, the better equipment or modification you buy. To kill an enemy with a regular assault rifle, you will need 3-4 shots. One hit to the head is fatal. There are a total of 12 cards in the game. These are zones in Iraq, Somalia and Afghanistan. 8 multiplayer modes, which perfectly demonstrate the graphics and audio component of the game, supporting 5.1 and 7.1 sound. In each battle, the player will be able to communicate with his team using voice chat, and also show the attack symbol on the map. Game Modes:

Push. Capture three control points; Battle from two fires. The enemy or you have captured 2 points out of 3. You must take 2 and prevent the 3rd from being captured; Cache. Each team, in addition to 3 neutral points, has a place into which the enemy cannot be allowed; Occupation. Simple capture of a point and holding it for a certain time; Escort. You, together with your team, must escort one person to a certain point. Task: do not let them die; Cache protection. You or the enemy will have access to the cache. It is important not to allow the enemy to destroy it or, conversely, to destroy it.

These and other multiplayer and co-op game modes bring the first-person shooter experience of Insurgency to life in the most realistic way possible. Weapons, graphics, sound, maps, events - all this was created by developers who prefer truth in games rather than fiction!
