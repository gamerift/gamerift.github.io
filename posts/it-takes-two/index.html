Game Description#

The co-op adventureIt Takes Two follows two central characters: Cody and May. They decided to get a divorce, which is why they found themselves in a surreal environment, turning into dolls. Now they have to go through hundreds of obstacles and dangers while exploring a new environment. A story about the eternal problems of relationships and how the main characters will reunite after overcoming a ton of obstacles on their own path.

The story about the main characters begins with how a “crack” appears in their relationship. They decided to leave, but a magical event brought them to the fairy-tale continent. Along the way, they will encounter unexpected and humorous obstacles, as well as real threats in the form of robber squirrels. The gameplay is designed for co-op play, where 2 participants control Cody and May. To overcome numerous obstacles you will need to interact with each other. During the passage, users will ride a sled on a magical snow globe, or play the role of a DJ at a dynamic party.

People who lived in the ordinary world will encounter fabulous and fantastic events that constantly occur in the new world. It Takes Two features a split-screen feature for players who want to experience the campaign on one monitor. The ability to play online helps you go through the storyline with a friend, even if he is on the other side of the globe. As they travel through the area, they will encounter rampaging vacuum cleaners, suave relationship gurus, and other rivals. There will also be friendly characters who will tell you more about the environment. The narrative is fully interactive, users are able to interact with almost any object, press levers, plates or grab objects.
