Game Description#

A new part of the real-time strategy Europa Universalis 4, the multiplayer mode of which allows up to 32 people to play on one server. Based on the previous parts, we can say that there are no more spies in the game, since they upset the overall balance of the game. The magistrate system has also changed greatly as the city’s development has undergone significant changes. The trading system has improved the most. Europe Universalis 4 represents the creation and management of a state at a time when the main goal of any nation was to seize neighboring territories to facilitate the structure of its own economy. No matter which faction you choose, the goal will remain world domination or the overthrow of a specific enemy that the game points to.

This time we start from the late Middle Ages, and the decade lasts until the Napoleonic Wars. In order to gain the upper hand, we will upgrade in the field of diplomacy, trade, economics, military craft, religion and the research of new technologies. For each institution, a small society is created separately, which will carry one or another message to the masses - to the people. The economy improves and stabilizes the production of resources, religion makes people believe that all actions of the state, especially aggressive ones, are the dictates of fate or God. In Europa Universalis IV, as the developers say, the goal is developed by the player himself. There are a series of achievements, such as the unification of the European Commonwealth or Italy, but the basis of the game is the capture of the entire world.
