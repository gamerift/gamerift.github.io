Game Description#

In Sword Art Online Fatal Bullet you can play online with a friend, exploring a fantasy world for free as a pirate. The gameplay is a mixture of action RPG and third person shooter. All multiplayer functions are available on pirate online. The story is based on the lore of the Gun Gale Online novels. The network game Sword Art Online Fatal Bullet allows you to create your own unique character, setting his appearance parameters and distributing skills. The arsenal of weapons includes a wide variety of guns with their own special characteristics, with the help of which individual battle tactics are developed. There are other types of weapons. For example, Kirito’s photon sword is available.

By going through a scenario in co-op or simply exploring the world and fighting enemies, you can upgrade your character, increasing his characteristics. The free online play option also includes a versus mode. A special feature of the gameplay is the grappling hook, with which you can quickly move around the location. The Bullet Line system has been introduced to help absorb damage.

Co-op is designed for 4 players. You can fight in versus battles, dividing into teams of 2 people. Factions are divided into Alpha and Bravo. There are also additional mobs on the field that are controlled by the computer. In the story part, you can communicate with many characters in the series and complete various quests.
