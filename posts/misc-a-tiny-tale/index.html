Game Description#

Three -dimensional MISC platformer. A Tiny Tale tells about the adventures of two incredibly cute robots that explore the futuristic world and the search for golden screws. The protagonists of Bag Boy and Buddy always dreamed of adventures and when beautiful shiny wrappers, bolts and screws began to suddenly fall from the sky, they decided to hit the road and find out what is happening in the world of robots. The characters look very funny as they are collected literally in the garage with improvised materials and have a small size - they are no more than insects.

Traveling to the MISC universe. A Tiny Tale Gamer is obliged to collect brilliant details, as only the number of these objects needs to unlock access to the next level. Also, on locations, you can find customizers for the Persians to make the piece of iron even more beautiful. In addition to studying the virtual space, the gamer also has to solve numerous puzzles, using the abilities of each of the protagonists alternately.

Also, with almost every resident of the measurement, you can enter into a dialogue and listen to its entertaining story. Some will completely give out a secondary quest, for which generous bonuses rely on. During the narrative, merchants will also meet - they need rare objects that they are ready to exchange for the most valuable resources for the user, necessary for further advancement.
