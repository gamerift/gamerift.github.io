Game Description#

Tank Squad transfers players during the Second World War, to the most intense territories, whose fate will affect the outcome of the clash. The user will visit the role of captain of the tank detachment, taking part in the iconic battles of that time. Scenarios will include historical battles for a single -user or cooperative passage of three participants at the same time.

In the multiplayer, each gamer is able to control their own team, or unite among themselves and become a separate crew member. The campaign consists of many missions with different tasks, locations and goals for victory. Before starting the next tasks, the user is able to develop tactics and come up with a plan of action. For this, the possibility of using infantry, air support, artillery and other units is given. To go through the final battle, the entire crew is required, so you need to minimize the loss.

Running tanks are the main mechanics of the Tank Squad project. Each explosion or serious hit can harm the mechanisms and performance of military transport. Sometimes you need to replenish tank ammunition and reload machine guns with the main artillery. In tense battles, a random bullet can get a member of the crew, injure it or kill it. At special points, you can refuel fuel, cooling liquid or oil.
