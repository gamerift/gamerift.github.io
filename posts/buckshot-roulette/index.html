Game Description#

Two enter, one leaves. That’s the motto of Buckshot Roulette, a horror game that takes the classic game of Russian Roulette and turns it into a high-stakes shotgun game. Immerse yourself in the dark atmosphere of an underground nightclub, where metal railings beat to the beat of forgotten drum kits and the stakes couldn’t be higher. This is not Russian roulette. This is where you swap the revolver for a 12 gauge shotgun, because shotguns are just better, right?

The gameplay is dense and intense, lasting about 15-20 minutes. You fight the Dealer, three rounds each, winner takes all. No tricks, just pure luck and nerves of steel. If you’re feeling lucky, turn up the tension with Double or Nothing. Face to face with the dealer - a ghost who mirrors your choice. No illusions, no sleight of hand. Only skill and the sparkle of madness in his eyes. Can you beat him at his own game or will his trigger become a lullaby for your executioner?

How long can you push your luck before the buckshot claims its prize? And for those who crave even more thrills, a new batch of items has been added to help shake you up. Buckshot Roulette is not for the faint of heart. This is a game where every decision carries the weight of a potential shotgun blast. So, are you ready to roll the dice on your life? Just remember that luck is never guaranteed. Enter the arena, go head to head with the Dealer and see if you can walk away with the prize. Or not. The choice and consequences are yours.
