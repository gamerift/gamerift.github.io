Game Description#

Zombie action PURGE Three vs Blood features a cooperative mode for up to 3 players who will need to confront an army of the living dead. On the pirate PURGE Three vs Blood, you can play online for free with a friend, with random players, or fight alone. By joining forces online, participants choose weapons and equipment that determine the battle strategy. Multiplayer includes ranks, a variety of maps, numerous guns and charismatic heroes. The goal is to hold off the onslaught of enemies for as long as possible. When earning points, they must be exchanged for improved weapons, traps or skills. The possibility of free online play is represented by several modes, including cooperative and competitive matches.

You need to constantly interact with each other and be ready to come to the aid of your partner at any time. When a squad member is seriously injured, a loud heartbeat can be heard. You are given a limited amount of time during which you need to help your teammate. If you do not have time to provide assistance, the player is eliminated from the game until the next match. As soon as participants reach a certain level, they are assigned a new rank. It opens up access to new skills and equipment. There are several difficulty levels: easy, medium and hard. There is a dynamic system for changing weather conditions. Precipitation directly affects the gameplay and can either help or vice versa - make the resistance more hardcore.
