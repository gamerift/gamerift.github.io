Game Description#

On the pirate gameSons of the Forest, you can cooperate online with a friend or other players to explore the expanses of the mysterious forest. You can play online for free with partners or survive alone. The story follows a group of people who are sent to a remote island to find a missing billionaire. The possibility of a free online game poses one task for participants - to survive without falling into the hands of bloodthirsty cannibals.

The gameplay is based on the interaction of survivors. Requires collecting resources. to build fortifications to help protect against attacks from aggressive creatures. Online play Sons of the Forest allows you to exchange supplies, help out partners and much more. A huge selection of different weapons is available: pistols, axes, clubs and various auxiliary items. To start a fire you will need to rub sticks until a more stable source of fire appears.

Each item of inventory has its own special purpose. For example, an ax is needed to cut down trees. Gameplay changes depending on the weather and season. Meat is stored longer in winter. However, with the onset of winter there is always a shortage of resources and provisions, so it is important to stock up on everything you need in advance. When going down into the mines, it is important to have a flashlight with you to light the way. As night falls, the enemies become more aggressive and they begin to hunt in a whole group.
