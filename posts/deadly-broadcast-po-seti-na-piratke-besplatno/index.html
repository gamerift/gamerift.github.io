Game Description#

InDeadly Broadcast you can play pirate online for free with a friend or cooperate online online in a team of up to four people to survive in one crazy and bloody game. Free online play features include both co-op and versus modes. As a streamer, you need to run a survival stream where people are locked in dark rooms where they have to face a paranormal entity.

At the beginning of the match, you can choose one of 8 heroes. All characters have a unique set of skills. The online game Deadly Broadcast offers a race for the number of subscribers. The longer the hero stays in the deadly arena, the more viewers come to his channel. By earning money you can buy clothes and accessories for your hero. At levels you need to fight mutants and zombies using a variety of weapons. There are various puzzles. It is important to destroy the spirit that controls the hospital and the demon guarding the prison. Monsters have escaped into our world from an open Pandora’s box.

Servants of demons are killed with knives, axes and other bladed weapons. In total, there are 16 ordinary types of enemies. You need to run away from bosses or hide in closets. You can buy time. stunning the enemy with the camera flash. To destroy key monsters, you need to find clues in the levels. After a solution is found, you will need to find quest items in the locations. There may be 10 different paranormal phenomena present in the levels.
