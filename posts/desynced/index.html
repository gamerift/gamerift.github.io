Game Description#

Desynced is a three -dimensional real -time strategy. The indie project also has elements of survival and a colony control simulator. The Title is made in a science fiction setting. It has support for user modifications. According to the plot, the hero travels along with artificial intelligence and colonizers between distant planets. The protagonist plants bots to the surface, and he remains in orbit and leads mechanical creatures from there. After completing the tasks (collection of resources, research of the territory, battle with the local aggressive fauna, the construction of the base and so on), he flies to the next celestial body, where the entire cycle is repeated. The main character suffers from regular strange dreams and an oppressive feeling of the onset of a certain threat.

The video game allows you to maintain game progress and continue the session from anywhere. Cards are generated randomly. Day and night are changing to them. The plot campaign can be held not only alone, but also with friends. Also here they allow users to fight among themselves. Cooperative mode and PVP-multiplayer are available only via the Internet. In addition, there is a set of tests here. This RTS against the background of other representatives of the genre is distinguished by the ability to customize units through the replacement of parts. You can give them complex orders and automate their behavior. Castomization of buildings is also available. Players can install and replace the components, as well as reassign the functions of buildings.
