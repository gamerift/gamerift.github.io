Game Description#

DEATH IN UNISON is a co-op survival adventure game set in an isolated rehabilitation institute on Corton Island. Here gamers will have to survive in the fight against some of the most dangerous criminals in the world, many of whom have supernatural powers. Users must perform their prison duties alone or with a partner, relying primarily on radio communications to navigate the treacherous environment.

Gameplay in DEATH IN UNISON revolves around completing administrative tasks and protecting yourself from hostile prisoners. Every day there are power outages in the prison, making the task even more difficult. Gamers must infiltrate the prison’s online systems and solve puzzles to uncover the truth about the prison’s origins. There is a special emphasis on teamwork and strategic planning, as players must coordinate their actions to achieve success.

In addition to the co-op mode, the title also features a single player mode, in which gamers take on the role of an administrator working alone in an office. The single-player mode retains the core elements of cooperative gameplay, requiring users to cope with their responsibilities and defend themselves from prisoners. The setting and mechanics create a tense atmosphere, forcing players to remain vigilant and resourceful in the face of constant danger.
