Game Description#

Roadcraft is a simulator in which gamers take on the role of the head of a company engaged in the restoration of territories affected by natural disasters. The main task is to use various types of construction equipment for clearing blockages, restoring roads and repairing damaged infrastructure.

The Title offers a detailed approach to the problems that arise in the elimination of the consequences of natural disasters, offering tasks such as replacing faulty equipment and deploying resources columns to create the necessary materials for reconstruction. Paying special attention to realistic physics, Roadcraft includes objects such as sand, wood and asphalt, each of which has its own properties that affect their interaction with the environment.

Users should take into account the size and mass of each object, maneuvering with heavy equipment to change the terrain, which allows you to easily move vehicles and clear the ways. Attention to physical details applies to the unique behavior of each machine, whether it be a bulldozer, a heavy conveyor or a crane. The user can use these machines to perform specific tasks - from clearing obstacles to transportation of equipment and lifting heavy containers. Title gives an extensive campaign in which gamers will unlock more than 40 vehicles as they pass. In addition, users can configure their cars using personal colors and logos.
