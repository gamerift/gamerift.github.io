Game Description#

Atlyss is a role -playing advent with an open world, which represents to gamers a vast and surreal universe. It is filled with various creatures and mysterious locations, inviting users to explore each of its corners. Users themselves can choose how to interact with the world: to cooperate with others or to cope with difficulties alone. Dynamic nature allows them to adapt their style, raising a sense of responsibility for their experience. The customizable gameplay mechanics provide a unique journey for each person.

The huge world is divided into several aboveground zones, each of which has its own dungeons and specific tests. These zones are designed to check various aspects of gamer skills, encouraging adaptation and strategic thinking. As these zones, players are faced with the capabilities of the further development of their characters. Due to the variety of classes, each of which has unique abilities and skills, development becomes the main aspect of the gameplay.

The system allows you to specialize, and the skills develop over time to comply with various strategies or preferences. Castomization of characters is the key element of Atlyss, which gives the players the opportunity to give its avatars significant features. This goes beyond simple aesthetics and allows you to make functional changes affecting the gameplay. Title guarantees that users will be able to make a choice in accordance with their preferences, from appearance and ending with combat skills.
