Game Description#

Arcade Saga throws the player in the position of newfound artificial intelligence. This essence should fight for survival against the overlords of AI who seek to destroy its independent thinking. The struggle for freedom from digital slavery takes place in three different futuristic sports. To succeed, it is necessary to use a quick reaction and apply strategic thinking to deceive the hostile forces of the system and overcome the established obstacles.

Three tests on the way to freedom in Arcade saga:

Fracture: Destruction of digital barriers. Smash: High -speed data shipping. Bowshot: Exact viral stripping.

Key activity in this title is divided into three unique disciplines created for virtual reality. FRACTURE includes 40 levels where the bricks of the Brandmauer will have to break. Smash represents 20 stages requiring balls with data past the overlords and their minions. Finally, Bowshot consists of 24 tests focused on the destruction of viruses with the simultaneous evasion of response. Each sport in this game requires a change in tactics for survival.

Throughout the collection, gamers will face 4 bosses, while the choice of complexity modes is available. The project is designed for VR equipment and uses movement control and positional sound in a 360-degree environment. The option of the player-play-player allows you to directly compete with other users. Complete the set of functions of the leaders table for all modes that allow you to compare their own performance with the global rating.
