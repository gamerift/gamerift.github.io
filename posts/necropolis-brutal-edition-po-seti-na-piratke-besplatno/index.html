Game Description#

NECROPOLIS: Brutal Edition is an action role-playing game that allows you to play online with a friend, cooperating online over the Internet to overcome a huge number of obstacles, fight strong enemies, obtaining weapons, armor, potions, scrolls, etc. NECROPOLIS: Brutal Edition features several updates, including the new character The Brute and the unique location The Black Forest.

The gameplay is similar to the Souls series projects. The possibility of free online play allows you to fight with strong opponents in co-op, or demonstrate your strength in PvP battles. The only important thing is that even the most ordinary enemy poses a serious threat. Also in the updated edition, artificial intelligence has been significantly improved and the complexity of traps and puzzles has increased.

Multiplayer allows you to explore procedural dungeons with a partner or in a team of up to four people, engaging in intense battles with enemies and collecting resources. The accumulated resources allow you to engage in crafting and upgrade the protagonist’s abilities. The gameplay contains elements of survival. Each enemy is represented by unique attack patterns. You need to fight monsters such as hemiters, changelings, storekeepers and other evil spirits. Procedural generation, each new launch of the campaign updates and randomizes the content, increasing the degree of replayability of the project.
