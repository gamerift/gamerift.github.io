Game Description#

DEATH IN UNISON invites players to play online and immerses players in the dark atmosphere of the rehabilitation institute on Corton Island - a hidden prison in the vast waters of the Pacific Ocean. This online game allows two players to fight the most dangerous psychopaths with unexplained otherworldly abilities. You and a friend can take the place of prison officers, ensuring security and stability in the institution, while at the same time fending off prisoners. The game is available for free on the pirate market, and you can play online online, enjoying the exciting cooperative gameplay.

Communication is key in this exciting adventure. The partners find themselves separated by concrete walls and steel doors, and rely solely on crackling walkie-talkies to coordinate their actions. It’s a test of teamwork that will have you screaming “Watch out six!” more times than you can count. But don’t be discouraged if you’re flying alone - the game easily adapts to single-player mode, allowing lone wolves to cope with difficulties.

As if dealing with supernaturally gifted criminals isn’t enough, the prison’s electrical system seems to have a mind of its own. Power outages are common, turning routine tasks into heartbreaking ordeals. One minute you’re filling out paperwork, the next you’re running for a flashlight as alarms sound and shadows move in the darkness. Talk about a hell of a 9 to 5 job!
