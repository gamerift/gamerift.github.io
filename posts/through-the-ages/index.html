Game Description#

Through The Ages is a port of a cult board game adapted to personal computers. Compared to the original party in the digital version, they take much less time. Instead of 4-8 hours, a maximum of 1 hour takes the game session, since the computer is engaged in all calculations.

According to its concept, Through The Ages resembles the Titles of the Civilization series. Only here development occurs using various cards. The user begins with a small tribe. He has to spend his people through 4 eras. Gradually, the nation will move from new technological centuries. As in the Civil, there is construction, resources, diplomacy, military conflicts, great people and miracles of the world. To win here it is necessary to accumulate cultural glasses.

When transferring this step -by -step strategy to a PC, the developers have changed some rules. You can play under a new edition or return everything as it was in the source. In case of hooks in a multiplayer, each participant is given a fixed amount of time to think and walk. In addition to the network game, there is a training mode, single tests and custom games with bots that can challenge. The complexity of artificial intelligence can be configured. The gameplay is divided into 2 phases: politicians and actions. First, various global events are played out, giving the peoples bonuses or fines. And then the user is engaged in the usual things for TBS - hires workers or fighters, erects buildings, improves buildings or units, conducts research. Well, he buys new cards. After that, the move is transmitted to the next user.
