Game Description#

One finger death punch is an arcade fighting game in the style of beat’em up, in which you can feel like a real martial arts master. In this game we have to fight off crowds of enemies that have surrounded the main character and are advancing from opposite sides. The game design is made in 2D, and all the characters are drawn people, which adds simplicity to the game, but at the same time makes it unusual.

Control in the game is carried out by clicking on the left and right mouse buttons when the enemy comes within the radius of your strikes. By gaining combos through continuous combat and avoiding damage, you gain more points and receive various bonuses, for example: switch to Jedi mode and start destroying enemies with a lightsaber until you miss a few blows and it is knocked out of your hands . Sooner or later he will undoubtedly be eliminated, as at this point the pace of the game begins to accelerate. Initially, the dynamics of the game are comfortable enough for the player to feel like a real kung fu master, fighting off weak and strong enemies, but with each new level it begins to grow.

The game has several game modes, namely: passing levels and survival. During the passage, a large map opens in front of the player, on which he will be able to overcome more than 230 levels, which differ in design, pace of play, and in some of them you will have battles with strong enemies and bosses. The battle with them is different from the battle with ordinary opponents, who die from single blows from the player. To win the battle against strong enemies, you need to use combos that the player will see on his screen. As you complete levels, you will unlock various skills that will help you in a difficult and unequal battle with your opponents.

Survival mode - in it the player is asked to carry out an endless battle with the advancing enemy until the last drop of blood in order to score the maximum number of points for combos and a series of kills. In each mode, you can choose a difficulty level, which will determine the speed of the game and the number of enemies, and at the survival difficulty level, something truly unexpected awaits you… During the battle, a huge black cat will crawl out to the left or right of the screen, which will block your view and simply interfere with the fight, so you need to drive it away with the mouse cursor, how do you like this turn?

If you want to let off steam after a hard day and relax, then one finger death punch is perfect for this. Spending hours in this fighting game will not let you get bored, as the changing dynamics of the game, musical accompaniment, combat skills and hundreds of different strikes will not allow your attention to wander.
