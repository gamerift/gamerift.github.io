Game Description#

InSalt, an open-world survival game, you can play as a pirate for free online with a friend, exploring the expanses of a huge open world in online co-op. It consists of many islands and ocean. Having built a ship, you need to explore the open world, complete quests, trade various goods, visit ancient ruins and engage in battles with other sailors.

The adventure begins on a small island. The online game Salt gives each hero a small amount of resources and a few people for a small ship. From this moment on, each participant must go his own way. Each player can visit uninhabited continents, explore ancient dungeons and ruins, enter into confrontation with other corsairs and improve their ship. Traveling across continents, the opportunity for free online play offers the opportunity to enter into alliances with other participants, or be a lone wolf, fighting with players or random pirates.

The world is filled with charismatic characters, hundreds of rare things, secrets, knowledge, etc. There is a main quest that anyone can follow. During its passage you will encounter cruel corsairs, cultists, merchants, etc. Allows you to customize your ship. Role-playing mechanics make it possible to upgrade both the ship and the character. You can equip yourself with hundreds of types of weapons and armor. The map is generated randomly. There are mechanics of hunting and fishing, and you will also have to search for treasures.
