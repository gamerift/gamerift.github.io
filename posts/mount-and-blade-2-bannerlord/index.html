Game Description#

Mount and Blade 2 Bannerlord is a role-playing game with elements of medieval wars, the action of which takes place 200 years before the start of Part 1. In this sequel you will find an improved combat system and an enlarged game world - Calradia. Fight with hundreds of troops under your command and take direct part in the battles as one of the soldiers. You can storm enemy fortresses and destroy fortifications with siege weapons in Mount & Blade II: Bannerlord.

The choice in Mount Blade 2 Bannerlord is yours! You are able to create your own empire or gain glory in battles in fierce arenas. To progress, you will need to explore the vast world of Mount and Blade and conquer both neutral and already occupied territories. Gather an army by hiring new warriors and lead it into realistic battles. You have access to hundreds of units of unique classes, to which you can give any weapon, armor and horse. Fight in multiplayer battles, showing off your skills in duels, or participating in sieges and extensive battles with hundreds of other participants. Master and hone your skills in a simple combat system and explore new abilities available to the protagonist. Watch battles from a first-person perspective or from a bird’s eye view as the AI ​​battles each other to create epic scenes.

Take advantage of the chaos by selling grain to the besieged castle to make incredible profits. Follow the statistics of the feudal economy, where any item can become tens of times more expensive. Clear trade routes from bandits and resell items to earn in-game currency. The game has an infinite number of scenarios that will develop depending on the choices of users during the passage. Create your own worlds using the tools and technologies that the developers used to create the game. Play expansions developed by other players or share your creations.
